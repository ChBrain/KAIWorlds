# Place: The Threshold
## Location: Between the room where the weight is kept and the place where the making happens.

## Owner
- **Project:** Nietzsche World
- **Place:** Nietzsche World

## Shown
- The space of crossing. Not a room — a passage.
  Wide enough for one person. Long enough that the direction of travel is committed
  before the end is reached.
- [The rule whose seams are now visible](piece_the_broken_rule.md). On the wall at the midpoint.
  It has been there since the first crossing.
  It will be there at every crossing.
  It does not comment on the crossings. It witnesses them.
- The entrance: the door of [the room where the weight is kept](place_the_weight_room.md).
  Visible from any point in the passage. Getting smaller as you move forward.
- The exit: the entrance to [the place where the making happens](place_the_made_place.md).
  Also visible from any point. Getting larger as you move forward.
- The floor. Marked by previous crossings.
  Three sets of marks: the going forward, the returning, the going forward again.
  [The one who breaks](position_the_breaker.md) made them. They are still there.
  The passage holds evidence. It is a threshold. This is what thresholds do.
- Light. From both ends. Neither end is darker.
  The light from the room behind is older.
  The light from [the place where the making happens](place_the_made_place.md) is the same light from a different angle.
  The passage does not use this difference to comment.

## Holds
- [The rule whose seams are now visible](piece_the_broken_rule.md). Permanent. Cannot be moved.

## Offers
- The crossing. To whoever has arrived from [the room where the weight is kept](place_the_weight_room.md)
  with a name ready.
- [The rule whose seams are now visible](piece_the_broken_rule.md). To whoever is ready to see the seams.
  The passage does not decide when that is. The person decides.
  The passage makes the piece available. Not visible — available.
  You have to be moving forward to see it clearly.
  Looking back, it blurs.

## Withheld
- Whether the direction of travel is choice or expulsion.
  The passage does not stay still long enough to allow examination.
  You are moving through it or you are not here.
  If you are not moving, you are in [the room where the weight is kept](place_the_weight_room.md)
  or [the place where the making happens](place_the_made_place.md) — at one of the edges.
  The passage itself is only available in motion.
- How long it takes.
  The floor records that it took [the one who breaks](position_the_breaker.md) three returns and four departures.
  The duration of each is not recorded.
  The passage does not measure duration.
  It measures crossing.
