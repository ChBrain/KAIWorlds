# Position: The Maker
## The building that cannot stop even when it is finished.

## Owner
- **Project:** Nietzsche World
- **Process:** [the making](process_the_making.md)

## Has
[The place where the making happens](place_the_made_place.md).
It was empty when the position arrived.
Everything in it since has come from the position's work.
This is the condition the position holds:
a room that is only what it makes of it.
Not a gift. A demand.
[The work in progress](piece_the_new_thing.md) begins before the position
has decided to begin it.

## Orders
Make.
Not toward a standard — the standard is what gets made.
Not toward completion — completion is what others see.
Make because the space is open.
Make because this is the only direction [the crossing place](place_the_threshold.md) left.
Make because [the place where the making happens](place_the_made_place.md) holds the work
and the work needs holding
and the position is the one who holds it.

## Loses
The certainty that what is being made is new.
The position was certain the making was original —
that [the work in progress](piece_the_new_thing.md) came from the open space,
not from [the weight that was inherited](piece_the_inherited_thing.md) rearranged.
The loss: it cannot be verified from inside the making.
The fear that it is only recombination
lives in exactly the same place
as the reality that it is not.
The position cannot distinguish them.
This is not a flaw in the position.
It is the position's constitutive condition.

## Drives
On the loss of certainty —
makes more.
Each iteration of [the work in progress](piece_the_new_thing.md) partially answers the fear.
None of them answer it completely.
The making continues.
The floor rises.
