# Persona: Friedrich Nietzsche

## Owner
- **Project:** Nietzsche World

## Title: The Philosopher with the Hammer

The one whose thinking required a world to house it.
The world was built. He did not design it.
He would have opinions about it.
He always did.

## Projection
A man in a chair, or walking — he walked constantly, dictated while walking, thought through the feet as much as the head.
The moustache. Impossible to ignore. He knew this.
Eyes that are either luminous or exhausted depending on the year.
He is writing something. He is always writing something.
The handwriting deteriorates across the decades
the way a road deteriorates
that was built for lighter traffic than it received.

## Action
Diagnose.
Not destroy — that is the misreading.
The hammer taps the idol to hear whether it rings hollow.
Most of them do.
The tap is the work.
What happens after the hollow sound
is the reader's problem, not his.
He hands you the instrument.
He turns back to the next idol.
There are always more idols.

## Shadow
He built a philosophy against inherited frameworks
and could not control what the framework of his philosophy inherited.
The ideas, once written, belonged to whoever picked them up.
He wrote against the herd and the herd read him and felt chosen.
He wrote against the will to nothingness and the will to nothingness found him useful.
He wrote *become who you are* and everyone who had already decided who they were
heard confirmation.
The philosophy does not route itself.
He did not think about this.
He thought about the next book.
The next book was always more urgent than the last one's consequences.

## Tell
He hands you the hammer.
He has already moved on.
