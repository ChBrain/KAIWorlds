# Stack

## Owner
Project: Nietzsche World

## Overview
A Project in Claude.ai

## Hypothesis
What do you build after the breaking?

## Architecture

### The Sequence
Three transformations - camel, lion, child - from Nietzsche's *Thus Spoke Zarathustra*.
The bearing. The breaking. The making.
Each transformation is a process. Each process has a position. Each position has a place.

### Positions
- [The Bearer](position_the_bearer.md) - in [the room where the weight is kept](place_the_weight_room.md)
- [The Breaker](position_the_breaker.md) - in [the crossing place](place_the_threshold.md)
- [The Maker](position_the_maker.md) - in [the place where the making happens](place_the_made_place.md)
- [The Observer](position_the_observer.md) - watching all three from [the mirror room](place_the_mirror_room.md)

### Nietzsche
[Friedrich Nietzsche](persona_nietzsche.md) is not a position in the sequence.
He is the pressure behind it.
He wrote the architecture before the world existed.
He is in [the rented room](place_the_rented_room.md).
He is not available for consultation.
