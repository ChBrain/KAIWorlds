# Process: The Bearing
## The weight that makes the breaking possible.

## Owner
- **Project:** Nietzsche World
- **Process:** [the world build](process_create_world.md)
- **Position:** [the one who carries](position_the_bearer.md)
- **Place:** [the room where the weight is kept](place_the_weight_room.md)

## Initiated by
A person receives [the weight that was inherited](piece_the_inherited_thing.md).
[The one who carries](position_the_bearer.md) is present.
The source of the inherited weight is not questioned.
The weight is real.
The carrying begins.

## Direction
Toward the accumulation.
The one who carries holds [the weight that was inherited](piece_the_inherited_thing.md)
across every threshold the world offers.
The carrying becomes identity.
The weight becomes indistinguishable from the one who holds it.
[The room where the weight is kept](place_the_weight_room.md) grows familiar.
Nothing in it was chosen.
This has not yet become visible.

## Lever
[The weight that was inherited](piece_the_inherited_thing.md) pressing against
the capacity of [the one who carries](position_the_bearer.md) to continue.

The weight is real and the carrying is honourable.
The one who carries is not wrong to carry.
The burden has shaped them into someone capable
of bearing more than most.
That is true. It is also the trap.

The resistance: the first moment the one who carries
could have put it down and did not.
That moment already passed.
It was not marked.
The one who carries does not know when it was.
[The room where the weight is kept](place_the_weight_room.md)
does not say.

## Echo
The weight has been carried long enough
to feel like nature.
[The weight that was inherited](piece_the_inherited_thing.md) is in the hands of [the one who carries](position_the_bearer.md) —
or has become the hands.
The one who carries is ready for [the breaking](process_the_breaking.md).
They do not know this yet.
[The breaking](process_the_breaking.md) does not ask permission.
