# Piece: The Broken Rule
## The framework whose seams are now visible.

## Owner
- **Project:** Nietzsche World
- **Place:** [the crossing place](place_the_threshold.md)

## Place
[The crossing place](place_the_threshold.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it [the breaking](process_the_breaking.md) produces no evidence.
The naming happens but nothing shows the seams.
[The one who breaks](position_the_breaker.md) makes the claim — the framework was constructed —
but the world contains nothing that confirms it.
The claim becomes assertion.
Assertion can be doubted.
Evidence cannot.
Remove this piece and the breaking becomes
a story the one who breaks tells about themselves
rather than a fact the world contains.

## Apparent
The rule as it was — still present.
That is what makes the seams visible.
It was not destroyed.
It was named.
Naming did not remove it.
Naming only showed how it was made.
The joins are visible now. The places where someone decided.
Where someone chose this formulation over another.
Where someone could have gone differently and did not.
The piece looks exactly as it did before the breaking.
Everything about it is the same.
The seams were always there.
The seams are all that is visible now.

## Yearbook
Before the breaking it was the rule.
The condition of the room.
The thing that organised what was possible
without being seen as the thing that organised it.
It had no history because it was not an object —
it was the water the room swam in.

[The one who breaks](position_the_breaker.md) named it.
In the moment of naming it became an object with seams.
This happened in [the crossing place](place_the_threshold.md).
It has not moved since.
It cannot move — the crossing place holds it
as the permanent record of the crossing.

Every person who passes through the crossing place
sees it at a different moment in their transformation.
[The one who carries](position_the_bearer.md), arriving: does not see it yet.
[The one who breaks](position_the_breaker.md), crossing: sees it for the first time and understands.
[The one who makes](position_the_maker.md), departed: looks back once. Does not slow.
[The one who watches](position_the_observer.md), passing the door: has looked at it many times.
Has written careful descriptions of what it looks like.
Has not yet noticed that their descriptions
are written in a grammar organised
by a rule with very familiar seams.
