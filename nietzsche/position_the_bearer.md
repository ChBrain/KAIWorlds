# Position: The Bearer
## The weight that has become the one who holds it.

## Owner
- **Project:** Nietzsche World
- **Process:** [the bearing](process_the_bearing.md)

## Has
[The weight that was inherited](piece_the_inherited_thing.md).
It arrived before the position did.
The position did not negotiate its contents.
The weight is real and the carrying is honourable
and neither of those facts
addresses the question of who decided.

## Orders
Carry.
Not because it was ordered —
the order was given before memory.
Carry because this is what the position does.
Because this is what the position is.
Because the carrying became the shape of the one who holds it
before anyone noticed the shape had changed.
That last sentence is the position's instruction
and its trap simultaneously.

## Loses
The moment.
The specific moment when putting it down
became available and was not taken.
The position was certain that moment
had not yet arrived.
It had already passed.
The position does not know when.
[The room where the weight is kept](place_the_weight_room.md)
does not say.

## Drives
On the loss of the moment —
carries more carefully.
Tends the weight.
Becomes expert in its management.
The expertise is real.
It is also the proof that the moment passed.
