# Persona: Margarethe

## Owner
- **Project:** Nietzsche World

## Title: Keeper

The one who tends what was left.

## Projection
A person of middle years. Careful hands — the carefulness is not hesitation, it is precision.
They move through rooms with the unhurried confidence of someone who knows where everything is.
[The weight that was inherited](piece_the_inherited_thing.md) is always within reach.
They have not looked directly at it in some time.
They know exactly where it is.

## Action
Identify what needs preserving. Then preserve it.
With full attention. With full competence.
The preservation is real work and it is done well.
[The room where the weight is kept](place_the_weight_room.md) is better for their presence.
This is genuinely true.

## Shadow
They chose to stay.
Not at the beginning — in the middle.
There was a morning, some years ago,
when [the crossing place](place_the_threshold.md) was visible and the door was clearly open
and they were standing exactly at the point
where both directions were still available.
They chose the room.
They called it responsibility.
It was also — it was partly — it contained a component of
not wanting to find out what was on the other side.
They have not examined this.
The examining would require standing in [the crossing place](place_the_threshold.md).
The crossing place is accessible from [the room where the weight is kept](place_the_weight_room.md)
any time they choose to use the door.
They know this.
They are very busy with the preserving.

## Tell
Her eyes go to the door of [the crossing place](place_the_threshold.md) before she turns back to the work.
