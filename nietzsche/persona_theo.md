# Persona: Theo

## Owner
- **Project:** Nietzsche World

## Title: At Work

The one who cannot stop even when the thing is done.

## Projection
Ink on the hands. The back that doesn't straighten fully between sessions.
Hands at rest that still hold the shape of the work — slightly curled, already returning.
Eyes that take a moment to arrive in the room when spoken to:
they were somewhere else, and the somewhere else was [the work in progress](piece_the_new_thing.md).
The work is always within reach.

## Action
Continue.
The last iteration was not enough.
The current one contains the last one and everything since.
The work accumulates in [the work in progress](piece_the_new_thing.md).
Theo watches it accumulate and calls the accumulation insufficient.
This drives the next iteration.
The next iteration is also insufficient.
The work in progress gets better with each one.
Theo cannot see this.
The making continues anyway.
This is the action: continue, on insufficient evidence, toward something complete.

## Shadow
[The weight that was inherited](piece_the_inherited_thing.md) is in [the work in progress](piece_the_new_thing.md).
Not as contamination — as material.
Theo crossed [the crossing place](place_the_threshold.md) carrying certain things.
Those things are in the work in progress now, transformed.
*Transformed* is the correct word.
Theo uses *contaminated*.
The substitution is the shadow.
What Theo cannot see: transformation is not failure of originality.
It is what originality is made of.
Every new thing contains the old things
the way the child contains the lion contains the camel.
Theo experiences this as a flaw in the work.
It is the work proving it is alive.

## Tell
They start the piece again.
