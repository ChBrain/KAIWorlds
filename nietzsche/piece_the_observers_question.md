# Piece: The Question the Observer Was Holding
## The question about others that was always about the one holding it.

## Owner
- **Project:** Nietzsche World
- **Place:** [the mirror room](place_the_mirror_room.md)

## Place
[The mirror room](place_the_mirror_room.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it [the mirror room](place_the_mirror_room.md) has no mechanism for the reveal.
The mirror room shows the one who watches themselves —
but without this piece, they see only their reflection.
A reflection is not a transformation.
A reflection confirms.
This piece, visible in the reflection, creates the pressure:
*why are you holding that?*
*Who gave it to you?*
*When did you last put it down?*
Those questions are the mirror room working.
Remove this piece and the mirror room is ornamental.
[The one who watches](position_the_observer.md) loses their function.
The one who watches completes the circuit:
the carrying, the breaking, the making — and the one who watches all three
and is in the middle of all three
and knows it least.
Without this piece, the circuit is open.
The world does not close.

## Apparent
In the mirrors it looks like [the weight that was inherited](piece_the_inherited_thing.md).
The same size. The same wear pattern — the places where hands have held it,
returned to it, adjusted it without needing to.

## Yearbook
Origin unknown to the one who watches.
This is also what [the weight that was inherited](piece_the_inherited_thing.md) says about itself.
The one who watches has noted this parallel in others.
Has written about it at some length.
Has not applied it inward.

The piece has been carried through every room in the world.
[The room where the weight is kept](place_the_weight_room.md): the one who watches visited, studied the room, left a careful description. The piece was in their hands throughout.
[The crossing place](place_the_threshold.md): the one who watches stood at the entrance, observed the crossing, did not cross. Wrote a good account of what the crossing looks like from outside.
[The place where the making happens](place_the_made_place.md): has looked through the window. Has not entered. The one who makes has not invited them. The one who watches is not certain they would accept.
[The mirror room](place_the_mirror_room.md): has not been. Knows it exists. Has a reason to visit that keeps changing.

The piece has been to every room but one.
The one remaining is the only room
where the piece can be seen for what it is.
The route there passes the door every day.
