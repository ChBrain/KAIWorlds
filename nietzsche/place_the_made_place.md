# Place: The Made Place
## Location: The far end of the crossing place.

## Owner
- **Project:** Nietzsche World

## Shown
- A working room. Everything in it was chosen or made by [the one who makes](position_the_maker.md).
  This is true.
  The one who makes is not entirely certain it is true.
  The room does not resolve this.
  It was empty when the one who makes arrived.
  Everything present arrived after that.
  The room holds this as fact.
- [The work in progress](piece_the_new_thing.md). At the centre. Always in progress.
  The one who makes has arranged the room around it.
  The room has not said anything.
- The tools. Present. Used. Each one chosen.
  The one who makes chose them.
  Some of them were the same tools used in [the room where the weight is kept](place_the_weight_room.md).
  They crossed [the crossing place](place_the_threshold.md) in the maker's hands.
  The one who makes remembers choosing them. This is accurate.
  Choosing to bring a tool is different from having inherited it.
  The room holds both truths without distinguishing between them.
- The window. Faces [the crossing place](place_the_threshold.md) and, beyond it, [the room where the weight is kept](place_the_weight_room.md).
  The one who makes keeps it covered.
  [The one who watches](position_the_observer.md) looks through it from outside.
  The one who makes does not know this is happening.
  The window covering blocks the maker's view out.
  It does not block the view in.
- The door. From [the crossing place](place_the_threshold.md). It opens inward.
  Nobody has used it except the one who makes.

## Holds
- [The work in progress](piece_the_new_thing.md). Here because the one who makes is here.

## Offers
- The working surface. To whoever arrives from [the crossing place](place_the_threshold.md) with something to make.
- The tools. Already present. The one who makes put them there.
  Available to whoever uses the room next.
  The room does not require that the tools be used.
  It requires only that the making be genuine.
- The door, open. The one who makes left it open when they arrived.
  They have not closed it since.
  It is an invitation that the one who makes does not know they issued.

## Withheld
- What the room looked like before the one who makes arrived.
  Empty, presumably.
  The one who makes is beginning to wonder about this.
  The room will not say.
  The emptiness before the making is the making's necessary precondition.
  If the room described it, the making would feel like filling rather than creating.
  The room withholds the emptiness to protect the work.
- Whether the arrangement around [the work in progress](piece_the_new_thing.md)
  was chosen or inherited.
  The room knows.
  It will not be the one to say it.
  That is [the mirror room](place_the_mirror_room.md)'s work.
