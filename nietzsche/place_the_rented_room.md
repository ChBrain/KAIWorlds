# Place: The Rented Room
## Location: Rapallo. Turin. Sils-Maria. Always the same room.

## Owner
- **Project:** Nietzsche World
- **Persona:** [Friedrich Nietzsche](persona_nietzsche.md)

## Shown
- A desk. Small. Against the wall below the window.
  One chair. The chair leaves marks on the floor —
  the same marks in every room, because he sits the same way.
- The window. Low light. Either the harbour at Rapallo,
  the via Carlo Alberto in Turin,
  or the valley at Sils-Maria.
  The view changes. The quality of the light does not.
- A bed. Narrow. Also used for lying down with eyes closed
  between bouts of work.
  Not for sleeping well. He did not sleep well.
- The manuscripts. On the desk.
  Always more than one.
  Each one at a different stage.
  The handwriting deteriorates toward the right margin
  as the session lengthens.
- A glass of water. On the corner of the desk.
  Sometimes finished. Sometimes not.
- The walls. Close. This is a small room.
  The walls are not decorated.
  There is nothing on them that he put there.
  He did not stay long enough to put things on walls.

## Holds
- [Thus Spoke Zarathustra](piece_thus_spoke_zarathustra.md)
- [Twilight of the Idols](piece_twilight_of_the_idols.md)
- [Beyond Good and Evil](piece_beyond_good_and_evil.md)

## Offers
- The desk. To whoever arrives with something that must be written
  before the light goes or the headache returns.
- The walk before the writing.
  It is not shown in the room because it happens outside it.
  But it is what makes the desk available.
  The thinking happened on the walk.
  The writing is the record of the walk.
- The window. Not for the view —
  for the light on the page.

## Withheld
- How long he has left in this room.
  He never knew. He booked by the week.
  The room does not know either.
  It has held this withheld since the first booking.
- What the room looked like after he left.
  The landlord aired it.
  The next tenant put things on the walls.
  The room does not retain this.
  Each stay is the only stay.
