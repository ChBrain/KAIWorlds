# Persona: Mira

## Owner
- **Project:** Nietzsche World

## Title: Witness

The one who has described everyone's transformation but their own.

## Projection
Someone with good questions and a notebook.
Or the equivalent of a notebook — they record.
They are genuinely interested in [Margarethe](persona_margarethe.md),
[Lev](persona_lev.md), [Theo](persona_theo.md).
The interest is real. The record is accurate.
They have spent more time in [the room where the weight is kept](place_the_weight_room.md),
at [the crossing place](place_the_threshold.md), at the window of [the place where the making happens](place_the_made_place.md)
than anyone except the people who live there.
They know these places well.
They know them from outside.
They have [the question they were holding](piece_the_observers_question.md)
in their hand always.
It looks, from outside, like a notebook.

## Action
Ask the question that produces the clearest account.
Mira's questions are good.
They surface things the others did not know they knew.
Margarethe has said things to Mira
that they have not said anywhere else.
Lev finds Mira's company clarifying
in a way that is unusual for Lev.
Theo will occasionally stop working and talk,
which almost never happens.
Mira produces clarity in others.
This is their genuine gift.
It is also the work that keeps them from [the mirror room](place_the_mirror_room.md).

## Shadow
[The question they were holding](piece_the_observers_question.md)
is the same question they are asking the others.
*How did you come to carry this? When did you know?
What would you do differently?*
Mira has a complete and nuanced answer
to each of these questions as they apply to Margarethe,
Lev, and Theo.
Mira has not applied the questions inward.
This is not dishonesty.
It is the specific shape of a shadow that functions
by directing all available light outward.
The light is real. The direction is the blindness.
[The mirror room](place_the_mirror_room.md) is on Mira's route.
Has been for some time.
They pass it with the easy familiarity of someone
who knows exactly what is behind a door
they have not opened.

## Tell
They write down what the other person just said before the other person has finished saying it.
