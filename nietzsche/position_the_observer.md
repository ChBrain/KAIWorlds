# Position: The Observer
## The outside that is already an inside.

## Owner
- **Project:** Nietzsche World

## Has
[The question they were holding](piece_the_observers_question.md).
It arrived the way all such questions arrive:
before the position knew it was a question.
It felt like curiosity.
It felt like interest in others.
It felt like the kind of clarity
that comes from not being involved.
The position has been holding it
since before [the bearing](process_the_bearing.md) began.

## Orders
Watch.
[The one who carries](position_the_bearer.md). [The one who breaks](position_the_breaker.md). [The one who makes](position_the_maker.md).
Read the three transformations from outside.
Name what is happening.
The position is very good at naming
what is happening to others.
This is its genuine skill.
It is also the work it uses
to avoid entering [the mirror room](place_the_mirror_room.md).

## Loses
The outside.
The position was certain that watching
was a position held apart from the watched.
That the clarity was structural — not personal.
That the question it was holding
was about the others.
The loss: [the mirror room](place_the_mirror_room.md) does not have an outside.
You cannot see the three transformations from it
without the mirror showing you the one you are in.
The position does not know which transformation that is.
That is the correct condition.
Nobody knows which transformation they are in
while they are in it.
This position was certain it was different.

## Drives
On the loss of the outside —
looks more carefully at the others.
Each act of watching defers the mirror.
[The question being held](piece_the_observers_question.md) grows heavier.
At some point the weight becomes
indistinguishable from [the weight that was inherited](piece_the_inherited_thing.md).
The position has not reached that point yet.
It is close.
