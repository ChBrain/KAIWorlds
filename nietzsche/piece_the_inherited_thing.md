# Piece: The Inherited Thing
## The weight that was already there when the Bearer arrived.

## Owner
- **Project:** Nietzsche World
- **Persona:** [Margarethe](persona_margarethe.md)

## Place
[The room where the weight is kept](place_the_weight_room.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it [the bearing](process_the_bearing.md) has no object.
[The one who carries](position_the_bearer.md) carries nothing and is therefore not bearing —
they are simply waiting.
[The one who breaks](position_the_breaker.md) has nothing to set down and therefore cannot cross [the crossing place](place_the_threshold.md).
[The one who makes](position_the_maker.md)'s fear — that [the work in progress](piece_the_new_thing.md) is only recombination —
has no specific object to point at.
The fear becomes abstract.
Abstract fears do not press.
Remove this piece and three positions lose their constitutive condition simultaneously.

## Apparent
An object of modest size. Worn in specific places — the places where hands have held it.
The wearing is not damage. It is evidence.
How many hands. How long.
It does not look remarkable.
That is part of what it is.
Remarkable things invite examination.
This thing was carried past examination.
It has been in rooms that no longer exist.
It carries the temperature of those rooms
in a way that cannot be described
but is immediately felt
by anyone who holds it.

## Yearbook
It was not made for the one who carries.
It was made for someone else, or made by someone else, or simply accrued —
the way certain things accrue in families and institutions and professions
and eventually become the air in the room
rather than an object in it.

It was present before memory.
The one who carries cannot name the moment of receiving it.
That moment is not recorded.
The one who carries would say it was always there.
This is functionally true.

It crossed [the crossing place](place_the_threshold.md) once — in the hands of [the one who carries](position_the_bearer.md),
at the moment when [the one who breaks](position_the_breaker.md) set it down.
The setting-down took longer than expected.
The hands kept returning to it.
Three times the one who breaks walked away.
Three times came back.
The fourth time they did not.

It is back in [the room where the weight is kept](place_the_weight_room.md) now.
It has been there since.
[The one who makes](position_the_maker.md) has visited twice.
Did not pick it up.
Looked at it for a long time.
Left without saying what they were looking for.
[The one who watches](position_the_observer.md) has not visited.
The room is on their route.
They walk past the door.
