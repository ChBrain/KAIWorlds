# Place: The Mirror Room
## Location: Adjacent to all three rooms. Entered from nowhere in particular.

## Owner
- **Project:** Nietzsche World
- **Place:** Nietzsche World

## Shown
- Mirrors. On every surface that could hold a mirror.
  Not facing each other — facing inward, toward the centre,
  toward whoever is standing in the room.
- The reflections of [the room where the weight is kept](place_the_weight_room.md),
  [the crossing place](place_the_threshold.md), and [the place where the making happens](place_the_made_place.md),
  visible in the mirrors behind whoever is standing here.
  The three rooms are always behind the person in this room.
  Looking at yourself, you see them.
  Turning to look at them directly: you see yourself.
  This is how the room works.
  This is the only way it works.
- [The question the observer was holding](piece_the_observers_question.md),
  visible in the reflection.
  In the mirrors it looks like [the weight that was inherited](piece_the_inherited_thing.md).
  From outside the room this resemblance is not apparent.
  The room does not import the resemblance.
  The resemblance is already there.
  The room only provides the angle from which it becomes visible.
- One chair. At the centre. Facing the largest mirror.
  The chair is sized for whoever needs to sit down.
  It is always the right size.
  Nobody has sat in it yet.
  It has not been empty long.

## Holds
- [The question the observer was holding](piece_the_observers_question.md).
  Visible here. Nowhere else with this clarity.

## Offers
- The angle. The only angle from which the carrying, the breaking, and the making
  are visible simultaneously, in reflection, behind the person looking.
  The room does not force the looking. It makes it available.
- The chair. To whoever arrives and needs to sit down
  because what the mirrors are showing requires sitting down.
- The question: *which transformation are you in?*
  The room does not ask this aloud.
  It arranges the mirrors so the person asks it of themselves.
  This is the only way the question has force.
  Asked by a room, it is furniture.
  Asked by the person who needed to ask it: it is the Hypothesis.

## Withheld
- The answer to which transformation they are in.
  The room shows the three rooms.
  The room shows the person.
  The room shows [the question the observer was holding](piece_the_observers_question.md)
  in the person's reflection.
  The room does not point.
  Pointing would be answering.
  This room does not answer.
  It is not that kind of room.
- The door in.
  There is a door. [The one who watches](position_the_observer.md) knows it is there.
  Has passed it on the route that passes all the other rooms.
  The door is not hidden.
  It is not locked.
  The room withholds the moment the one who watches opens it.
  Not forever. Not much longer now.
