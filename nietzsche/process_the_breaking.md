# Process: The Breaking
## The naming that cannot be unnamed.

## Owner
- **Project:** Nietzsche World
- **Process:** [the world build](process_create_world.md)
- **Position:** [the one who breaks](position_the_breaker.md)
- **Place:** [the crossing place](place_the_threshold.md)

## Initiated by
[The bearing](process_the_bearing.md) has run long enough.
[The one who breaks](position_the_breaker.md) arrives at [the crossing place](place_the_threshold.md).
[The weight that was inherited](piece_the_inherited_thing.md) is still in their hands.
The first question arrives: *who put this here?*
The question cannot be unasked.

## Direction
Toward the naming.
The one who breaks names the framework as external —
as received, not chosen, not inevitable.
The naming is the work.
Not the decision that follows.
Not the freedom that is claimed.
The naming itself.
[The rule whose seams are now visible](piece_the_broken_rule.md) becomes visible the moment the name is spoken.
Its seams show.
[The crossing place](place_the_threshold.md) cannot be held.
The one who breaks moves.

## Lever
The act of naming pressing against
what the name unmakes.

The one who breaks has the strength
to see the framework clearly —
to say: this was constructed,
I did not construct it,
I was handed it and I carried it.
That is true and the saying of it is an act of power.

The resistance: the naming also names the namer.
The one who declares the framework external
has declared themselves the one who sees it.
The one who sees it is responsible for what comes next.
The freedom is real.
The one who breaks called it freedom.
They have not yet noticed it is also weight.
[The crossing place](place_the_threshold.md) does not say this.
[The rule whose seams are now visible](piece_the_broken_rule.md) sits there with its seams showing
and does not say it either.

## Echo
[The rule whose seams are now visible](piece_the_broken_rule.md) is visible.
Its constructedness cannot be unseen.
[The weight that was inherited](piece_the_inherited_thing.md) has been set down —
or is in the process of being set down,
which is not the same thing,
and takes longer.
[The crossing place](place_the_threshold.md) has expelled the one who breaks forward.
[The making](process_the_making.md) is the only remaining direction.
The one who breaks believes they chose it.
