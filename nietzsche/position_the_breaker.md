# Position: The Breaker
## The naming that costs the namer.

## Owner
- **Project:** Nietzsche World
- **Process:** [the breaking](process_the_breaking.md)

## Has
[The crossing place](place_the_threshold.md).
It exists before the position arrives.
It does not require permission.
The capacity to see the framework as constructed
and [the crossing place](place_the_threshold.md) to cross through
are the position's two conditions.
One internal. One in the world.
Neither negotiated.

## Orders
Name it.
Not carefully. Not with permission.
The naming does not wait for the right moment.
The right moment is when the name is available.
It is available now.
Say it.
Watch [the rule whose seams are now visible](piece_the_broken_rule.md) become visible.
Stand in [the crossing place](place_the_threshold.md)
and do not try to stay.

## Loses
The outside.
The position was certain the naming
left it standing apart from the framework —
able to see it clearly because no longer inside it.
What the naming actually does:
it names the namer.
The one who sees the construction
is now responsible for what is built next.
There is no outside from which to observe this.
[The crossing place](place_the_threshold.md) has already expelled them forward.

## Drives
On the loss of the outside —
calls it freedom.
This is not wrong.
It is also not complete.
[The making](process_the_making.md) is the completion.
The position cannot see this yet
because it is still standing
where the framework was
feeling the air where it used to be.
