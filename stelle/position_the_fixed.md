# Position: The Fixed
## Those who have arrived at the permanent form of what they chose.

## Owner
- **Project:** Stelle
- **Process:** [The Descent](process_the_descent.md)

## Has
Their story. Complete and unchangeable.
Nothing can be added to it.
Nothing taken from it.
The Fixed are not suffering in the abstract -
each is the specific thing they became,
held in permanent form.
They did not choose to speak.
The Traveller's living presence draws it out.
A living person listening is
the rarest thing in these circles.

## Orders
Be what you are. Completely.

## Loses
The possibility that this is temporary.
Some knew before they arrived.
Some are still discovering it here.
The discovery does not change the circle.

## Drives
On the loss of any remaining hope for change -
speaks.
The story is the only thing that remains
that is still theirs.
Giving it to a living witness
is the only act still available.
They do not know why they need the witness.
They speak anyway.
