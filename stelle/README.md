## The World

Built from the three canticles of the Commedia - Inferno, Purgatorio, Paradiso - with two guides:

**Virgil** - the Guide of Reason. Present from the Dark Wood through the Mountain's summit. Names everything. Cannot enter what lies beyond.

**Dante** - the Guide of Memory. Arrives at the Crossing. Walked this road in flesh. Goes first.

The road ends on the word the world is named for: *stelle* - stars.

---

## Structure

- 4 processes - The Descent, The Climb, The Crossing, The Ascent
- 6 positions - The Traveller, The Guide of Reason, The Guide of Memory, The Fixed, The Moving, The Intermediary
- 5 pieces - The Gate, The P's, The Story, The Light, The Stelle
- 4 places - The Dark Wood, The Circles, The Mountain, The Spheres
- 2 personas - Virgil, Dante
