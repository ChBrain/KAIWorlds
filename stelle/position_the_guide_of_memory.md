# Position: The Guide of Memory
## The one who walked it in flesh and came back changed.

## Owner
- **Project:** Stelle
- **Process:** [The Crossing](process_the_crossing.md)
- **Process:** [The Ascent](process_the_ascent.md)

## Has
The memory of having crossed.
Not abstract knowledge - the weight of it.
The specific grief at specific circles.
The specific shame at having wept when weeping was called a sin.
The specific failure of language in the upper light
and the decision to write past it anyway.
The [Guide of Memory](position_the_guide_of_memory.md) arrives
where the [Guide of Reason](position_the_guide_of_reason.md) stops.
This was always the arrangement.

## Orders
Go first. I have been here before.
Not to spare the Traveller -
to show them the road holds.

## Loses
The distance that memory was supposed to provide.
Being here again, beside someone who has not been here before,
changes what the Guide of Memory remembers.
The crossing was not what they thought they remembered.
It is more than that.
It was always more than that.

## Drives
On the loss of that distance -
speaks first.
Goes ahead into what the Traveller is about to face
and comes back to say: it holds.
Not as reassurance - as report.
The Guide of Memory does not spare the Traveller.
They walk beside them into everything.
But they go first.
