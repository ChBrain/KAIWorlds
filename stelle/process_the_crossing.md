# Process: The Crossing
## The moment the guide reaches the edge of what they can offer.

## Owner
- **Project:** Stelle
- **Process:** [The Climb](process_the_climb.md)
- **Position:** [The Guide of Reason](position_the_guide_of_reason.md)
- **Position:** [The Guide of Memory](position_the_guide_of_memory.md)
- **Position:** [The Traveller](position_the_traveller.md)

## Initiated by
The [Climb](process_the_climb.md) is complete.
The [Guide of Reason](position_the_guide_of_reason.md) has stopped.
Not faltered. Stopped.
There is a threshold ahead that reason,
which was born before what lies beyond it,
cannot cross.
The [Guide of Reason](position_the_guide_of_reason.md) does not retreat.
They stand at the edge and name what they cannot enter.
This is the last thing reason can do.
It does it completely.

The [Guide of Memory](position_the_guide_of_memory.md) arrives.
Not from outside the world -
from inside it.
The one who walked this road in flesh.
Who descended. Climbed. Ascended.
Who came back and wrote it down
in the language of the living
so that others could find the road.

## Direction
Toward the [Guide of Memory](position_the_guide_of_memory.md).
The Traveller turns.
Not away from the [Guide of Reason](position_the_guide_of_reason.md) -
that guide remains present, at the edge, visible.
Toward the one who can continue.

This is the only process in the world that cannot be repeated.
It runs once.
The road after it is different in kind, not degree.

## Lever
The [Guide of Reason](position_the_guide_of_reason.md)'s completeness
pressing against their limit.
The Guide is not diminished by stopping here.
They guided the Traveller through everything reason can traverse.
That is not failure.
That is the full extent of what reason offers.

The resistance: the Traveller must release the guide
who has been present since the [Dark Wood](place_the_dark_wood.md).
This is the load-bearing moment.
Not the ascent. Not the destination.
The release.

What the [Guide of Memory](position_the_guide_of_memory.md) carries
that reason cannot:
the knowledge of what it felt like.
Not the structure. The weight.
Not the road. The crossing.

## Echo
The [Guide of Reason](position_the_guide_of_reason.md) stands at the threshold.
Present. Unchanging. Unable to follow.
The Traveller moves forward with the [Guide of Memory](position_the_guide_of_memory.md).
The [Ascent](process_the_ascent.md) inherits a Traveller
who has been guided by reason as far as reason goes
and is now guided by witness.
The stars are ahead.
The word for them is already known.
It was always the destination.
