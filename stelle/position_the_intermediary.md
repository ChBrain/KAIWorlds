# Position: The Intermediary
## The one whose gaze holds what the Traveller cannot yet bear directly.

## Owner
- **Project:** Stelle
- **Process:** [The Ascent](process_the_ascent.md)
- **Place:** [The Spheres](place_the_spheres.md)

## Has
The capacity to hold the [Light](piece_the_light.md) directly
and return it in a form the [Traveller](position_the_traveller.md) can receive.
Not less than the Light - the same Light,
mediated through a presence that loves the Traveller
and is loved by them.
The Intermediary does not dim the Light.
They train the Traveller's eyes toward it.

## Orders
Look at me. Not yet at what I see.

## Loses
The role of Intermediary
as the Traveller's capacity grows.
Each sphere the Traveller can hold more directly.
Each sphere the Intermediary's necessity decreases.
They know this is how it is supposed to go.
They guide the Traveller toward not needing them.
This is what it means to love someone well.

## Drives
On the loss of necessity -
turns toward the final Light alongside the Traveller
rather than between them and it.
No longer intermediary.
Witness beside witness.
Both looking at the same thing.
This is what the Intermediary was always moving toward.
