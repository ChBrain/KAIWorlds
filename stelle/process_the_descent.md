# Process: The Descent
## The road that only goes deeper.

## Owner
- **Project:** Stelle
- **Position:** [The Traveller](position_the_traveller.md)
- **Position:** [The Guide of Reason](position_the_guide_of_reason.md)
- **Position:** [The Fixed](position_the_fixed.md)

## Initiated by
The [Traveller](position_the_traveller.md) has entered the [Dark Wood](place_the_dark_wood.md).
The straight way is lost.
The [Guide of Reason](position_the_guide_of_reason.md) is present.
The [Gate](piece_the_gate.md) has been read.
The Traveller has passed through it.
The Descent begins the moment passage is chosen.
It cannot be initiated twice.

## Direction
Downward. Inward.
Circle by circle, the road narrows.
The [Guide of Reason](position_the_guide_of_reason.md) names what is seen.
Names do not change what is there.
The Traveller moves through each circle
carrying what they brought through the [Gate](piece_the_gate.md).
Nothing is dropped here.
Weight accumulates.

## Lever
The [Traveller](position_the_traveller.md)'s need to understand
pressing against the impossibility of intervening.
The [Guide of Reason](position_the_guide_of_reason.md) explains.
Explanation is not relief.
The dead speak when the Traveller is present.
They speak because someone living is listening.
This is the only thing that changes in the circles.
Everything else is permanent.

The resistance: the Traveller cannot help what they see.
They can only witness.
Witnessing without the capacity to act
is the structural condition of Inferno.
The thing the Traveller loses here is specific:
the belief that understanding what they see
is the same as being changed by it.
The circles disprove this belief systematically.
Understanding is complete long before the Descent ends.
The Traveller is still being changed.
The [Guide of Reason](position_the_guide_of_reason.md) knows this.
The Traveller learns it.

## Echo
The Traveller has reached the bottom.
The bottom is not the worst thing they saw.
The worst thing they saw was somewhere in the middle
and they did not know it was the worst until they had passed it.
The [Guide of Reason](position_the_guide_of_reason.md) is still present.
Still able to move.
The [Climb](process_the_climb.md) inherits a Traveller
who has seen everything the living are capable of
and come out the other side.
Upward is now possible.
It was not possible before this.
