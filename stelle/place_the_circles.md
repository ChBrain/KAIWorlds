# Place: The Circles
## The permanent expression of what was chosen.

## Owner
- **Project:** Stelle
- **Process:** [The Descent](process_the_descent.md)
- **Position:** [The Fixed](position_the_fixed.md)

## Shown
Stone. Narrowing. Each circle deeper and tighter than the last.
The air changes as the descent continues -
warmer near the top, cold at the absolute bottom.
The [Fixed](position_the_fixed.md) are present in their circles.
Each circle holds one kind of misdirected love,
arranged from least severe at the top
to most severe at the bottom.
The [Guide of Reason](position_the_guide_of_reason.md) moves without obstacle.
The Traveller moves beside him.
The Fixed do not reach toward them.
They speak when the Traveller is close enough to hear.

## Holds
- [The Story](piece_the_story.md) - inside every Fixed, given when the Traveller is present.
- [The Gate](piece_the_gate.md) - at the entry above, behind the Traveller once they have passed through.

## Offers
The Traveller can listen.
The Fixed will speak their story if approached.
The Traveller can ask.
The Fixed will answer what they can.
The Traveller can weep.
The Guide of Reason will note it
and may rebuke it
and the Traveller will weep anyway.
This is not weakness.
The circles permit all of it.

## Withheld
Whether witnessing changes anything for the Fixed.
The circles do not confirm or deny it.
The Fixed speak as if it matters.
The architecture of Inferno does not say.
The Traveller carries the stories out.
What happens to the stories after that
is outside the circles.
The circles do not reach that far.
