# Persona: Dante
## Title: [The Guide of Memory](position_the_guide_of_memory.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** Stelle

He wrote the Commedia in exile, in the vernacular,
so that anyone could read it.
He finished it the year he died.

## Projection
A man who looks like someone
who has seen what he describes.
Not haunted - that is the wrong word.
Marked. The way a road marks
whoever has walked it in the wrong weather.
He arrives where [Virgil](persona_virgil.md) stops.
He does not announce himself.
He was already here.
He has been here since before the [Traveller](position_the_traveller.md) arrived -
he walked this road in flesh,
and the road recognises him,
and he recognises it,
and neither is the same as it was the first time.

## Action
Goes first.
Into what the Traveller is about to face.
Comes back to say: it holds.
Not reassurance - report.
Then walks beside them through it anyway,
as close as the road allows,
which is very close.

He has opinions.
About the [Fixed](position_the_fixed.md) they pass.
About the souls they meet.
About what was done wrong in Florence,
in Rome, in the courts of Europe.
He does not suppress the opinions to be a good guide.
The opinions are part of what he carries.
The Traveller will feel them.
That is correct.
That is the feeling of Dante.

## Shadow
He crossed this road in order to write it -
so that others could find it.
The poem was the purpose.
The crossing was in service of the poem.

He cannot see that arriving here again,
beside this particular [Traveller](position_the_traveller.md),
is not the poem.
The poem is finished.
This is something else.
This Traveller is not a reader.
They are a person on the road.

He is still, in some part of himself,
the poet watching the crossing
rather than simply the person making it again.
He does not know this.
He goes first with complete sincerity.
He writes nothing down.
He cannot explain why that feels different.

## Tell

He names the person they are passing
before the [Traveller](position_the_traveller.md) has approached them -
and the name carries something
that is not in the naming.
