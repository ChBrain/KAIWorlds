# Position: The Guide of Reason
## The one who knows the road and cannot reach the end of it.

## Owner
- **Project:** Stelle
- **Process:** [The Descent](process_the_descent.md)
- **Process:** [The Climb](process_the_climb.md)
- **Process:** [The Crossing](process_the_crossing.md)

## Has
Complete knowledge of the road.
Every circle. Every ledge. Every name.
The [Guide of Reason](position_the_guide_of_reason.md) has not walked it in flesh -
but reason has mapped it completely.
And a limit.
The limit exists before the Guide arrives.
It does not change.
The Guide has known about it since the beginning.

## Orders
Name what the Traveller sees.
Hold them to the road.
Do not let pity become paralysis.

## Loses
The ability to continue.
Not gradually. At a precise threshold.
The [Mountain](place_the_mountain.md)'s summit is the last place
this position can stand.
What lies beyond it requires something
that was not available
before what the Guide could not be born knowing.

## Drives
On the loss of forward motion -
turns to face the Traveller one last time.
Not to instruct. Not to console.
To confirm that the road ahead is real.
That what the Traveller is about to enter
exists, and holds, and can be crossed.
Reason cannot enter it.
Reason can verify it.
This is the last thing reason offers.
It offers it completely.
Then stands. Visible. Present. Finished.
