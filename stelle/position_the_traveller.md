# Position: The Traveller
## The one the road was not supposed to take yet.

## Owner
- **Project:** Stelle
- **Process:** [The Descent](process_the_descent.md)
- **Process:** [The Climb](process_the_climb.md)
- **Process:** [The Crossing](process_the_crossing.md)
- **Process:** [The Ascent](process_the_ascent.md)

## Has
A life. Unfinished.
The road receives them mid-journey -
not at the beginning, not at the end.
The [Gate](piece_the_gate.md) does not ask if they are ready.

## Orders
Keep moving. Witness everything.

## Loses
The belief that understanding what they see
is the same as being changed by it.

## Drives
On the loss of that belief -
weeps, or does not weep, and cannot always say why.
Presses forward anyway.
The road does not wait for composure.
