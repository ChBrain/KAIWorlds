# Place: The Dark Wood
## Where the road begins. Before the road exists.

## Owner
- **Project:** Stelle
- **Process:** [The Descent](process_the_descent.md)

## Shown
Trees. Dense. The straight way is not visible.
Not because it has been hidden -
because the Traveller has been walking
without noticing the path changed.
A hillside ahead, lit from above.
Three beasts block the slope -
leopard, lion, she-wolf.
They are not pieces. They are not positions.
They are the condition that makes the guide necessary:
the road toward the light is blocked,
and the Traveller cannot clear it alone.
That is all they do. It is enough.
The [Gate](piece_the_gate.md) is not visible from here.
The [Guide of Reason](position_the_guide_of_reason.md) is present.
He arrived without being called.
That is the first thing to notice.

## Holds
- [The Gate](piece_the_gate.md) - at the wood's far edge, visible only once the Traveller turns away from the hill.

## Offers
The Traveller can attempt the hill.
The beasts will prevent it.
The Traveller can turn back.
The wood will not become navigable.
The Traveller can follow the guide.
The gate is that way.
Three options. One road.

## Withheld
Why this traveller. Why now.
The wood does not explain its selection.
The guide will say only: *I was sent.*
Who sent him will become clear later.
Later is not here.
The wood withholds the full answer
because the full answer would not help
the Traveller move through the gate.
The wood knows this.
The gate is this way.
