# Place: The Spheres
## Where geography ends and light begins.

## Owner
- **Project:** Stelle
- **Process:** [The Ascent](process_the_ascent.md)
- **Position:** [The Guide of Memory](position_the_guide_of_memory.md)

## Shown
Not geography. Light.
The [Guide of Memory](position_the_guide_of_memory.md) is present.
The [Intermediary](position_the_intermediary.md) is present.
The [Guide of Reason](position_the_guide_of_reason.md) is not.
The souls here are not fixed. They are not climbing.
They are fully themselves - which looks, from outside,
like being made of light.
They speak when addressed.
They speak about love,
about God,
about what they understand now
that they could not understand living.
Their speech is not suffering and not explanation.
It is the sound of something that has arrived
where it was always going.
The [Stelle](piece_the_stelle.md) is here, at the end -
and also already in the word
the Traveller carries from every ending they have passed.
The [Light](piece_the_light.md) intensifies with each sphere.
The Traveller discovers they can hold more of it
than they could at the start.

## Holds
- [The Light](piece_the_light.md)
- [The Stelle](piece_the_stelle.md) - present at the final sphere, and again at the world's end.
- [The Intermediary](position_the_intermediary.md) - present throughout, becoming unnecessary by degrees.

## Offers
The Traveller can look.
Gradually more directly.
The Guide of Memory will say when to redirect -
not to protect the Traveller from the light
but because the eyes need time.
The Traveller can ask the souls what they understand.
The answers will exceed the questions.
This is not a problem.
The Traveller can press language as far as it goes
and then discover where it stops.
The stopping is part of what is offered.

## Withheld
The final light directly.
Until the Traveller is ready.
The spheres do not withhold it as punishment -
they withhold it as sequence.
Each sphere increases capacity.
The final light requires the full capacity
built across every sphere.
The Traveller cannot skip to it.
The Spheres know the difference
between readiness and impatience.
They are the same place.
They are not the same experience.
