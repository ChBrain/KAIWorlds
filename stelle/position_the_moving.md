# Position: The Moving
## Those who are not yet finished, and know it, and climb.

## Owner
- **Project:** Stelle
- **Process:** [The Climb](process_the_climb.md)

## Has
The knowledge that they are saved
and the weight of what still must be shed.
Both at once.
The Moving suffer - but the suffering has direction.
They chose it.
They continue choosing it on every terrace.
They move slowly. Sometimes barely.
They move.

## Orders
Continue. The summit exists.

## Loses
The consolation of arrival.
Each terrace completed is not the end -
it is the floor of the next one.
The Moving cannot see the summit from where they are.
They know it exists because the architecture of the mountain
makes it structurally necessary.
This is not the same as seeing it.

## Drives
On the loss of consolation -
moves anyway.
Not by suppressing the weight
but by carrying it upward.
The weight does not diminish until it does -
suddenly, completely, confirmed by the mountain shaking.
The Moving do not manufacture that moment.
They make themselves available to it.
