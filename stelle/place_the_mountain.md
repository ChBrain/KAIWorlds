# Place: The Mountain
## The only place in the afterlife where things change.

## Owner
- **Project:** Stelle
- **Process:** [The Climb](process_the_climb.md)
- **Process:** [The Crossing](process_the_crossing.md)
- **Position:** [The Moving](position_the_moving.md)

## Shown
An island in the southern hemisphere.
The mountain rises from the shore.
Stars visible from the first moment -
after the circles where no stars were.
The [Moving](position_the_moving.md) are on the terraces.
Some carry weights. Some have eyes sewn shut.
Some run. Some crawl. All are going up.
Slowly. With difficulty. Willingly.
Angels at the gates between terraces.
The [Guide of Reason](position_the_guide_of_reason.md) moves freely here -
this is still the territory of reason.
The summit is not visible from the lower terraces.
The mountain is too tall.
The Traveller's forehead carries the [P's](piece_the_ps.md)
from the gate of Purgatory onward.

## Holds
- [The P's](piece_the_ps.md) - on the Traveller's forehead, removed terrace by terrace.

## Offers
The Traveller can speak with the Moving.
Unlike the Fixed, the Moving speak of what they are becoming,
not only of what they were.
The Traveller can climb.
Each terrace is a question the mountain asks.
The Traveller can stall.
The mountain permits it.
The mountain does not hurry.
The mountain does not need to.

## Withheld
When consent will arrive.
The mountain knows when a terrace is cleared -
the earthquake confirms it, the shout goes up -
but the Traveller does not know in advance.
The moment cannot be manufactured.
The mountain withholds the schedule deliberately.
Knowing the schedule would make the consent transactional.
Transactional consent does not clear the terrace.
The mountain has been here longer than anyone climbing it.
It knows the difference.
