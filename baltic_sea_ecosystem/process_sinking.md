# Process: The Sinking
## The descent that feeds the floor and starves the water.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Ecosystem
**Persona:** The Phytoplankton

## Initiates
The bloom peaks. Nutrients are exhausted.
The cells die or aggregate.
Gravity is the only force left acting on them.

## Direction
Downward. Through the thermocline.
Through the oxygen-rich surface water.
Into the cold dark below.
Onto the seafloor.

## Lever
The dead biomass itself.
It is the food and the poison simultaneously —
food for the benthos and the bacteria,
poison for the oxygen as it decomposes.
Remove the sinking biomass and the benthic
system starves. Leave it and the oxygen
below the thermocline is consumed.

## Echo
Positions assigned:
The Phytoplankton → The Fallen.
The Bacteria → The Decomposer.
The Benthos → The Receiver.
The Oxygen → The Consumed.
The thermocline seals what follows.
