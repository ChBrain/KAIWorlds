# Position: The Grazer
## What the web requires to keep production in check.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Ecosystem
**Position:** The Annual Cycle

## Has
The requirement for consumption of primary production.
It exists as soon as The Producer is active.
The bloom requires a limit.
Something must set it.

## Orders
Graze. Before the bloom crashes on its own terms.
The timing is the technique.
Too early and there is nothing to graze.
Too late and the bloom has already sunk.
The position requires precision the Grazer
cannot always deliver.

## Loses
The bloom. It was there.
Then it wasn't — either grazed down
or sunk before the Grazer could reach it.
Either way, the food supply ends.
The Grazer did not decide when.

## Drives
On the loss of the bloom — descends.
Follows the sinking organic matter.
Eats what The Producer left behind
on the way to the floor.
