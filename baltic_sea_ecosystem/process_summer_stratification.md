# Process: The Summer Stratification
## The seal that cannot be opened until autumn.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Ecosystem
**Persona:** The Oxygen

## Initiates
Surface water warms. It becomes lighter than the cold water below.
The thermocline forms. Warm above. Cold below.
No exchange between them.
The Sinking continues below it.
The decomposition continues below it.
The oxygen below it is being consumed
and cannot be replaced.

## Direction
Toward depletion. The oxygen below the thermocline
is drawn down by bacterial decomposition
of the sinking bloom material.
The dead zone expands from the bottom up.
August. The worst month.
Getting worse each decade.

## Lever
The thermocline.
It is not a wall. It is a density gradient —
warm light water floating on cold heavy water.
Warming deepens it. Strengthens it.
A stronger thermocline means less exchange.
Less exchange means less oxygen reaching the bottom.
The warming makes the lever stronger every year.

## Echo
The dead zone.
Fish have moved to shallower water.
Benthos in the affected zone: dead or absent.
The Oxygen held The Consumed until it couldn't.
What was there is no longer there.
The instruments have recorded it.
It will run again next year.
Longer.
