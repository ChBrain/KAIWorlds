# Position: The Witness
## The capacity to be changed by what the data cannot transmit.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Ecosystem
**Position:** The Baltic Sea Ecosystem

## Has
The distance. It is in the water before the Witness arrives.
They came as an observer of an environmental problem.
The position does not preserve the observer.
It does not explain this in advance.

## Orders
Enter the water. Not above it — inside it.
Stay below the thermocline long enough
to feel the difference between
what was here and what is here now.
Not understand it. Feel it.
The position does not explain
what feeling it will cost.
It closes the species boundary
without asking permission.
The Witness did not agree to cross it.
The water does not wait for agreement.

## Loses
The species boundary.
They were certain they could understand
the ecosystem from the position of a person
observing organisms.
The world shows them that understanding
arriving from above
is not the same as understanding
arriving from inside.
Below the thermocline in August
with the oxygen at two milligrams per litre —
the boundary between witness and witnessed
was the thing they were certain they had.
The position takes it.

## Drives
On the loss of the species boundary —
carries what the sea cannot say
in the language the world outside uses.
Not the data. What the data points at.
The Witness walks back into the world
that is making decisions about the sea
carrying something the sea gave them
that the sea cannot give the decision-makers directly.

*Assigned by: Not by process. By attention.*
