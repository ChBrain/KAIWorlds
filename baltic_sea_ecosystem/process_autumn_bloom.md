# Process: The Autumn Bloom
## The second production into exhausted conditions.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Ecosystem
**Persona:** The Phytoplankton

## Initiates
Autumn storms break the thermocline.
Surface and deep water mix.
Nutrients from the bottom return to the light zone.
The phytoplankton respond again.
The second bloom.

## Direction
The same direction as the Spring Bloom.
Production. Sinking. Benthic input.
But the oxygen below has been depleted all summer.
The benthos that receives this bloom
receives it into conditions already degraded.
The bacteria decompose it into water
that has less capacity to absorb the cost.

## Lever
The mixing event — the storm that breaks stratification.
Without it the nutrients stay below the thermocline.
The autumn bloom does not run.
The benthos receives nothing.
What the storm releases determines
what the benthos survives on through winter.

## Echo
The second Sinking runs.
Positions assigned again — Producer, Fallen, Decomposer.
The benthos receives.
Winter begins.
The oxygen will not fully recover before
the spring thermocline seals again.
The baseline drops.
Fractionally. Every year.
