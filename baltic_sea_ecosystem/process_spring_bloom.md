# Process: The Spring Bloom
## The production that cannot stop once light returns.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Ecosystem
**Persona:** The Phytoplankton

## Initiates
Winter ends. Nutrients have accumulated in the water column
through months of darkness and mixing.
Light reaches the surface. Temperature lifts fractionally.
The phytoplankton respond before anything else does.

## Direction
Upward and outward. Biomass multiplies.
Diatoms first, then dinoflagellates.
The copepod maximum follows — the grazers arrive
after the bloom has already committed.
The bloom does not wait for the grazers.
It has already run.

## Lever
The nutrient load in the water column.
Remove it and the bloom produces nothing.
The lever is what accumulated all winter
and is released by light in a single season.

## Echo
Positions assigned:
The Phytoplankton → The Producer.
The Zooplankton → The Grazer.
The web is running.
The sinking has already begun.
