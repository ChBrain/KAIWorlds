# Position: The Decomposer
## What the web requires to return what fell to what will rise.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Ecosystem
**Position:** The Sinking

## Has
The requirement for remineralisation.
The fallen biomass must be broken down.
Without decomposition the nutrients stay locked
in the organic matter on the seafloor.
The position exists before any bacterium arrives.
The floor has always needed breaking down.

## Orders
Break it down. Immediately.
The organic matter is fresh.
The energy charge is high.
The position demands maximum activity
at the moment of arrival.
It does not wait for oxygen to be available.
If oxygen is absent, use what is available.
Sulfate. Nitrate. Whatever remains.

## Loses
The oxygen. It was there when decomposition began.
The position consumes it in the act of breakdown.
The more efficiently the Decomposer works,
the faster the oxygen disappears.
Efficiency is the mechanism of depletion.

## Drives
On the loss of oxygen — continues without it.
Anaerobic decomposition. Slower.
Producing hydrogen sulfide.
The position does not stop because conditions
become hostile. It runs until the substrate is gone.

*Assigned by: The Sinking, The Summer Stratification*
*Held by: Bacteria (pelagic and benthic), microbial community below the thermocline*
