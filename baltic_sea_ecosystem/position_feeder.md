# Position: The Feeder
## What the web requires to move energy between trophic levels.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Ecosystem
**Position:** The Food Web

## Has
The energy waiting to move between trophic levels.
It is in the web before any organism arrives to carry it.
It will not move without a body to move through.
The position is the obligation to carry it.

## Orders
Eat. Be eaten.
Both at once, always.
The position does not assign one without the other.
Tori and uke simultaneously.
The Feeder did not negotiate which direction it faces.

## Loses
The certainty of what it is.
Every organism that holds The Feeder
discovers it is both predator and prey.
The position holds them in both directions
until one direction fails.

## Drives
On the loss of certainty —
the trophic transfer continues.
What is eaten feeds what eats it.
The chain does not stop because
one link is uncertain about its position.
It stops when a link is absent.
Not uncertain. Absent.

*Assigned by: The Food Web (all processes)*
*Held by: Every organism in the pelagic and benthic web simultaneously*
