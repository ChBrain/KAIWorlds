# Position: The Producer
## What the web requires to be generated from nothing.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Ecosystem
**Position:** The Annual Cycle

## Has
The requirement for primary production.
It exists in the water column before any organism arrives.
Light and nutrients are present.
Something must convert them.
The position does not negotiate.

## Orders
Convert. Now. While light and nutrients coincide.
The window is weeks, not months.
The position does not explain this.
It simply requires it from the moment conditions allow.

## Loses
The nutrients. They were the resource
the Producer arrived with.
The position consumes them in the act of production.
What is made depletes what made it possible.

## Drives
On the loss of nutrients — sinks.
The position releases what it built
into the water column below.
The Sinking begins.
