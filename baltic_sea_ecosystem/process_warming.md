# Process: The Warming
## The driver that distorts all six processes simultaneously.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Ecosystem
**Persona:** The Oxygen

## Initiates
It has already initiated. 1.2°C at Boknis Eck since 1957.
It does not wait for the annual cycle.
It runs beneath all six processes, distorting each one
as it passes through them.

## Direction
Toward irreversibility. The stratification deepens.
The thermocline strengthens and persists longer.
The bloom season lengthens.
The oxygen depletion season lengthens.
The winter mixing season shortens.
The recovery is fractionally less complete each year.
The nutrient reduction that was supposed to restore oxygen
is no longer sufficient.
Doing the right thing is no longer enough.

## Lever
The stratification. Physical separation of warm surface water
from cold deep water. Warming makes it stronger.
Stronger stratification means the thermocline holds longer,
seals more completely, and allows less exchange.
Every other process runs through this lever.

## Echo
The stratification period is longer than it was last year.
Longer than it was the year before.
The dead zone forms earlier.
Closes later.
The winter mixing season is shorter.
The oxygen recovery after summer is fractionally less complete.
The baseline drops.
It does not recover.
The process has run.
It will run again next year.
Starting from where this year ended.
