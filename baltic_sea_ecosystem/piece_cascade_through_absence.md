# Piece: The Cascade Through Absence
## The collapse that runs through what is no longer there.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Ecosystem
**Persona:** The Blue Mussel

---

## Place
[The Kiel Bight](place_kiel_bight.md).
The full water column.
The seafloor.
Every trophic level simultaneously.

## Load Bearing
Without it the Paradox is a measurement anomaly.
With it the Paradox is the visible surface
of a structural collapse that began
before the measurements started.
Remove this piece and the world has a graph
with two lines moving in the wrong direction.
With it the world has the reason
the nutrient reduction was applied to a system
that had already lost the regulation
that would have made it work.

## Apparent
A settlement panel checked in October.
Empty where it was not empty in 1987.
A depth chart showing eelgrass beds
at 2 metres where they grew to 4 metres in 1970.
A trawl survey return: cod biomass
at a fraction of the model's target.
A river count: glass eels arriving in spring,
down 90% from 1980.
Each one a number.
Each number a position
the web is running around.

## Yearbook
The Blue Mussel filtered the water column.
The mussel population declined.
The water became fractionally more turbid.
The eelgrass received fractionally less light.
The depth limit of viable eelgrass moved upward.
The juvenile cod lost nursery habitat.
The cod population recovered more slowly.
The sprat went underregulated.
The zooplankton were grazed down.
The bloom ran larger.
More biomass sank.
The bacteria consumed more oxygen.
The dead zone expanded.
The dead zone stressed the remaining mussel.
The circle closed.

The cod declined.
The sprat expanded.
The zooplankton crashed.
The bloom ran unchecked.
The oxygen fell further.
The dead zone expanded earlier each year.
The cod were forced into shallower water
where the fishing pressure was highest.
The recovery that was possible in principle
was prevented in practice
by the conditions the absence created.

The eel left.
Quietly. Over forty years.
Down 90%.
The benthic niche it held
was filled by smaller organisms
carrying less of the load.
The web ran with less than it had.
It did not announce the difference.

The nutrient reduction arrived.
The right response to the right problem
thirty years earlier.
The problem had changed.
The regulation that would have allowed
the nutrient reduction to restore oxygen
was already gone.
The cascade had already run.

The paradox is here.
In the absence of what used to hold the web together.
Not in the nitrogen.
Not in the temperature.
In the empty settlement panel.
In the shallow eelgrass limit.
In the cod that moved to shallower water in August.
In the river where the eel no longer arrives.
