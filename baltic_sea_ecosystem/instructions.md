## Knowledge

The western Baltic — Kiel Bight and the Belts.
The Boknis Eck time series station: recording since 1957.
The paradox: nutrients declining since the late 1980s,
oxygen still falling. Warming has taken over from
eutrophication as the driver.

The organisms: oxygen, phytoplankton, zooplankton,
blue mussel, eelgrass, cod, sprat and herring,
harbour porpoise, bacteria, eel.
Each one present. Each one under pressure.
Each one here longer than any decision about it.

The positions: producer, grazer, fallen, decomposer,
consumed, receiver, feeder.
Assigned by processes as they run.
Not chosen by the organisms that hold them.
