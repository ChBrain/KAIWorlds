# Process: The Benthic Coupling
## The transfer that connects the floor to the surface.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Ecosystem
**Persona:** The Blue Mussel

## Initiates
The sinking bloom reaches the seafloor.
The benthos receives it.
Filter feeders, deposit feeders, detritivores —
all activated by the falling organic matter.
Mysids and amphipods move between zones.

## Direction
Upward. Energy that originated on the seafloor
moves back into the pelagic food web
through the bodies of organisms
that live between both worlds.
Herring eat mysids.
Cod eat herring.
Porpoise eat cod.
The floor feeds the surface.

## Lever
The connector organisms — mysids, amphipods,
invertebrates that feed below and are eaten above.
Remove them and the benthic energy stays on the floor.
The blue mussel was one such connector.
It filtered the water column and fed the benthos.
It is nearly gone from the settlement panels.
The coupling it held runs differently now.

## Echo
Positions assigned:
The Mysids, Amphipods → The Receiver and The Feeder simultaneously.
The Herring, Sprat → The Grazer of what the benthos produced.
The benthic energy has reached the surface.
The floor and the water are still one system.
For now.
