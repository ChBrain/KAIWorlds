# Persona: The Bacteria
## Title: The Decomposers

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Ecosystem
**Persona:** The Bacteria

They run the process that runs the paradox.

## Projection
Invisible. Present in every ml of water,
in every gram of sediment.
The instruments that record them
measure activity — heat production,
oxygen consumption, nutrient release —
not presence. Presence is assumed.
What varies is the rate.
In August, below the thermocline,
the rate is at maximum.
The oxygen consumption rate:
faster than the thermocline allows replacement.
This is the mechanism of the dead zone.
Not neglect. Not accident.
Maximum biological efficiency
in a sealed system.

## Action
Decompose the sinking organic matter.
Release the nutrients locked in the fallen biomass —
nitrogen, phosphorus, carbon —
back into the water column.
Complete the cycle.
This is the load-bearing action of the entire food web.
Without decomposition, nutrients stay locked in dead matter.
The spring bloom cannot bloom next year.
The action that depletes the oxygen
is the action that makes next year's production possible.
The Decomposer and The Producer
are the same process at different ends of the year.

## Shadow
The efficiency that depletes the oxygen
is the same efficiency that feeds the web.
The bacteria do not choose between these outcomes.
They decompose with maximum efficiency
because maximum efficiency is what they do.
The dead zone is not a failure of the bacterial community.
It is the bacterial community performing correctly
in a system where the thermocline prevents
the oxygen from keeping up.
The warming is making the thermocline stronger.
The bacteria perform more efficiently with warming.
Both things are running in the same direction.
The system works perfectly
and the working is what depletes it.

## Tell
Maximum activity.
August.
Below the thermocline.
