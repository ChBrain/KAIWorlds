# Position: The Receiver
## What the web requires to hold what falls and pass it upward.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Ecosystem
**Position:** The Benthic Coupling

## Has
The accumulated organic matter on the seafloor.
Decades of blooms sedimented and waiting.
The position holds the floor's energy reserve
before any benthic organism arrives to use it.

## Orders
Take what falls. Process it.
Make it available to the pelagic system
through the bodies of organisms
that move between floor and water column.
The position is the hinge
between what sinks and what rises.
It does not explain this.
It simply requires occupancy.

## Loses
The accumulated reserve.
When the dead zone expands,
the reserve is consumed by anaerobic bacteria
rather than passed upward by benthic organisms.
The position loses its function
without losing its requirement.

## Drives
On the loss of the accumulated reserve —
moves what remains upward.
Less than before.
The coupling runs on what is left.

*Assigned by: The Benthic Coupling*
*Held by: The Blue Mussel (near-absent), mysids, amphipods, benthic invertebrates, The Eelgrass (as sediment holder)*
