# Position: The Consumed
## What the web requires to be present and finds cannot be maintained.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Ecosystem
**Position:** The Summer Stratification

## Has
The requirement for aerobic life below the thermocline.
Every organism in the deep water needs it.
It is assumed to be present.
The position holds this assumption
before any organism arrives.

## Orders
Be present. That is all.
The position requires nothing more than presence.
It does not ask for production or movement or response.
Only: be there.
This is the demand the persona cannot meet.

## Loses
Itself. Gradually. Irreversibly within the season.
The Decomposer consumes it.
The thermocline prevents replacement.
The position requires presence.
The persona cannot maintain presence.
The position and the persona
are running in opposite directions
and the position does not care.

## Drives
On the loss of presence — holds the empty position.
The web required something here.
Nothing is here.
The position remains assigned.
Unoccupied.
The process does not reassign it.
It waits.

*Assigned by: The Summer Stratification, The Warming*
*Held by: The Oxygen*
*What holds it when the Oxygen cannot: nothing*
