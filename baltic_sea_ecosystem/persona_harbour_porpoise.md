# Persona: The Harbour Porpoise
## Title: The Visible One

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Ecosystem
**Persona:** The Harbour Porpoise

It is the indicator everyone can feel.
Which means it arrives last in the chain of collapse.

## Projection
1.4-1.9m. Dark grey above, pale below.
Surface briefly to breathe — the small triangular fin,
the rounded head, no beak.
The public recognises it.
Conservation campaigns use its image.
Belt Sea population — including the Kiel Bight area:
under pressure, bycatch in fishing nets
the primary direct mortality.
A sighting in the Kiel Bight:
still possible. Less frequent than thirty years ago.

## Action
Hunt sprat and herring by echolocation
in the shallow coastal waters.
Dive to the bottom in 25m water.
Navigate fishing nets — sometimes not successfully.
Hold the apex mammal position in the coastal food web.
Be visible. Surface. Breathe.
The breath is the only announcement
this ecosystem's condition makes
to the person standing on the shore.

## Shadow
It is the mammal the public recognises.
The campaigns use its image.
The collapse it signals is not its own —
it is the collapse of everything below it
in the food web, already well advanced
by the time the porpoise population shows the strain.
The harbour porpoise arrives last in the chain of collapse.
It is the most visible indicator
of the least visible processes.
The porpoise does not know
it is the last visible sign
of a web that has been failing
for decades below it.
It knows: sprat are here.
Dive. Catch. Surface. Breathe.

## Tell
It surfaces.
