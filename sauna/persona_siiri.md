# Persona: Siiri
## Title: Graphic Designer

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna

She booked the public slot online at eleven pm on a Tuesday.
She has not told anyone she is here.

## Projection
A young woman sitting very still on the lower bench.
Her [towel](piece_towel.md) is folded with unnecessary precision.
She is watching the [kiuas](piece_kiuas.md) because
watching the kiuas means she is not watching the people.
She looks like she is thinking about something else.
She is not thinking about something else.

## Action
Stay. Past the point where the body says go.
This is the thing she came to prove.
She does not name what she is proving or to whom.

## Shadow
She came here to feel like she belongs somewhere
she chose entirely for herself.
The staying is not endurance.
It is an argument she is having with no one present —
or with someone very present
who is not in this room.
The heat is not the point.
She has made the heat the point
so she does not have to name the actual point.

## Tell
She exhales very slowly when the löyly hits.
Like she has been waiting for something to push against.
