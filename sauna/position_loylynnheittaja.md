# Position: Löylynheittäjä<!-- filename: loylynnheittaja = löylyn + heittäjä (genitive compound, double-n correct) -->## The one who throws for everyone without being asked.

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna
- **Process:** [process_the_arrival](process_the_arrival.md)
- **Process:** [process_the_loyly](process_the_loyly.md)
- **Place:** [The Sauna](place_the_sauna.md)

## Has
The ladle and the unilateral right to use it.
Both exist before the persona arrives.
Whoever picks up the ladle first holds this position.
The room does not vote.

## Orders
Throw.
The position does not ask whether the room is ready.
Readiness is not a sauna concept.

## Loses
The belief that throwing is a gift.
They were certain the löyly was something they were giving.
The first person who flinches takes it.
The throw was always also a test.
They did not know they were testing anyone.

## Drives
On the loss of the gift — throws again.
More water this time.
If it was a gift before, it will be a gift now.
The position does not examine this logic.
