# Piece: The Ladle
## The thing that makes the löyly a decision.

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna
- **Place:** [The Sauna](place_the_sauna.md)

## Place
[The Sauna](place_the_sauna.md).
Hung on the wall beside the [Kiuas](piece_kiuas.md).
Or in someone's hand.
Those are the only two states it has.

## Load Bearing
Without it the [Löylynheittäjä](position_loylynnheittaja.md)
has no instrument.
The water bucket is present.
The kiuas is hot.
But without the ladle the throw cannot happen —
and without the throw there is no löyly,
no heat spike, no moment
where every body in the room
receives something it did not choose.
The central mechanism of the world does not run.

## Apparent
Wood, long-handled. Pale from use and steam.
The bowl holds roughly one cup of water.
One throw. Then back to the bucket.
The handle is smooth where it has been held.
It has been held a great deal.

## Yearbook
It has been picked up by people who asked first
and people who did not ask.
It has been held by the experienced
who knew exactly how much water to throw
and where on the stones to land it.
It has been held by the uncertain
who threw too much, too fast,
and watched the room flinch.
It has been held out as an offering —
here, you throw — and accepted,
and declined.
Every social negotiation this room has ever run
passed through this handle at some point.
The wood does not record any of it.
It is smooth from the holding.
That is all it shows.
