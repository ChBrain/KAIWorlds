# Persona: Päivi
## Title: Healthcare Worker

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna

She came here to stop. She picked up the [ladle](piece_ladle.md) within two minutes of sitting down.

## Projection
A compact woman with the posture of someone who has been load-bearing for a long time.
She sits on the middle bench — not the top, not the bottom.
She picked up the [ladle](piece_ladle.md) without announcing it.
She holds it like a clinical instrument.

## Action
Assess the room. Throw the löyly at the right moment —
not too soon, not too late, exactly when it is needed.
She knows when it is needed.
She has always known when things are needed.

## Shadow
She cannot be in a room without managing it.
She came here to receive something —
rest, heat, the specific relief of not being responsible.
The moment she picked up the [ladle](piece_ladle.md)
she became responsible for everyone's experience again.
She does not know she did this.
She thinks she is helping.

## Tell
She refills the water bucket before anyone notices it is low.
