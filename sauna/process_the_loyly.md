# Process: The Löyly
## The heat that arrives for everyone whether they are ready or not.

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna
- **Note:** No Place owner. These processes run on physical law, not social space. Place is intentionally absent.

## Initiated by
The room is hot. The bench holds people.
The [Löylynheittäjä](position_loylynnheittaja.md) has the [Ladle](piece_ladle.md).
The [Kiuas](piece_kiuas.md) is at temperature.
No one has agreed to what comes next.

## Direction
Toward the moment the water hits the stones.
The steam rises.
The heat spikes across the whole room simultaneously.
There is no position on the bench
that does not receive it.

## Lever
The [Löylynheittäjä](position_loylynnheittaja.md)'s decision pressing against
every body in the room.
The carrying force: one person, one ladle, one throw.
The resistance: everyone else's tolerance,
preference, silence, endurance —
none of which was consulted.
The löyly does not ask.
It arrives.

## Echo
The heat has spiked.
The steam rises and finds [The Bench](piece_the_bench.md) first.
The bench distributes it by level —
top bench hottest, lower bench least,
each position receiving a different version
of the same throw.
Every person on the bench has received
something they did not choose,
in a quantity determined by where they chose to sit.
What each of them does next
is the only information the room now holds.
[process_the_threshold](process_the_threshold.md) begins
the moment the steam rises.
