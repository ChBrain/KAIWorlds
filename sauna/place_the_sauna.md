# Place: The Sauna
## Location: Somewhere in Finland. Exact location unimportant. It is always the same room.

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna

## Shown
- A small wooden room. Lower than you expect.
  The walls are pale pine, darkened near the [Kiuas](piece_kiuas.md).
  The smell: wood, heat, birch, old sweat, something clean underneath all of it.
- The [Kiuas](piece_kiuas.md). Centre-right. Iron and stone.
  The stones on top are dark. The air above them moves.
- [The Bench](piece_the_bench.md). Two levels. The upper bench hotter.
  The wood worn smooth in the places people always sit.
  The heat distributes by level — top bench, middle bench, lower bench,
  each one a different sauna from the same throw.
- The [Ladle](piece_ladle.md). Hung on the wall beside the kiuas.
  The [Bucket](piece_the_bucket.md) below it. Full.
- The [Vihta](piece_vihta.md). Soaking in a smaller bucket near the door.
  Or absent. Both versions of this room exist.
- The [Sauna Door](piece_sauna_door.md). Opposite the bench.
  Small. You duck to go through.
  The glass panel fogs immediately.
  Cold air when it opens. Every time.
- The window, if there is one. Small. High up.
  Shows sky or dark or the edge of trees.
  Nothing that tells you what time it is.
- Outside the door: the lake, or the yard, or the shower.
  Cold. Available. Not this room.

## Offers
- [The Bench](piece_the_bench.md). Three positions. Each one a different heat.
  The choice is available. It is always a statement.
- The [Ladle](piece_ladle.md). To whoever will pick it up.
- The [Vihta](piece_vihta.md). To whoever it is offered to, or whoever takes it.
- The [Sauna Door](piece_sauna_door.md). To whoever is ready to use it.
- The heat. To everyone. Not equally.

## Withheld
- What was almost said on the bench.
  The heat loosens things. Not all the way.
  Some things get to the surface and stop there.
  The room holds them without filing them.
  They do not leave with the people.
  They stay in the wood.
