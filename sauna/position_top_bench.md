# Position: Top Bench
## The hottest seat. Taken, not assigned.

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna
- **Process:** [process_the_arrival](process_the_arrival.md)
- **Place:** [The Sauna](place_the_sauna.md)

## Has
The highest heat in the room.
The top bench exists before any persona arrives.
It is not offered. It is not withheld.
It is simply there, and hotter than everything below it,
and whoever sits in it
holds this position whether they intended to or not.

## Orders
Stay.
The position does not ask why you came up here.
It only measures whether you remain.

## Loses
The privacy of the choice.
They were certain sitting here was personal —
endurance, habit, preference, a small private test.
The room watches.
Everyone below has an opinion about how long they last.
The choice was never private.
The bench made it public the moment they sat down.

## Drives
On the loss of privacy — stays longer than is comfortable.
The discomfort is now the point.
It was not the point when they sat down.
