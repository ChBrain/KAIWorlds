# Process: The Threshold
## The body that decides before the mind does.

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna
- **Note:** No Place owner. These processes run on physical law, not social space. Place is intentionally absent.

## Initiated by
The löyly has run.
The heat is at its peak.
Every person on the bench is now
in a room that is asking something of them.
The [Door](piece_sauna_door.md) is present.
It has always been present.
It is now the only thing in the room
with a clear purpose.

## Direction
Toward the stay or the leaving.
Not a decision — a discovery.
The body already knows.
The mind is the last to find out.

## Lever
The heat pressing against
whatever each person brought in with them.
The carrying force: the accumulated löyly,
the bench position, the company, the silence,
the thing that was almost said
and wasn't.
The resistance: the reason they came.
Whatever it was — endurance, habit,
belonging, escape, the thing
they have not named even to themselves.
The [Door](piece_sauna_door.md) does not force anyone.
It is simply available.
That is the whole of its pressure.

## Echo
Someone stayed. Someone left.
The room is different for both.
The ones who stayed know something
about themselves they did not know before.
The ones who left know it too.
Neither will say so.
