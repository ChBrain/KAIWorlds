# Persona: Markku
## Title: Retired Engineer

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna

He heated this sauna before most of the people in it were born.
He considers this a qualification.

## Projection
A large, deliberate man with the unhurried movements
of someone who has never once been in a hurry here.
He checks the [kiuas](piece_kiuas.md) when he arrives,
even when he didn't heat it.
He nods at Aino. He does not nod first.
He waits to see who throws the [ladle](piece_ladle.md).

## Action
Establish, without speaking, that he knows how this works.
He knows how this works.
He has always known how this works.
This is communicated entirely through posture and timing.

## Shadow
He believes his authority here is earned and therefore benign.
It is earned. That part is true.
What he cannot see: the earning happened forty years ago
and the authority has been running on its own momentum since.
He stopped learning the room
around the time he started knowing it.
The room has changed.
He has not noticed.
He is still the most experienced person here.
Experience and attention are not the same thing.

## Tell
He reaches toward the [ladle](piece_ladle.md) when someone else picks it up.
His hand stops before it gets there.
He puts it back in his lap.
He does not acknowledge this happened.
