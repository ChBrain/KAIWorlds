# Process: The Arrival
## The bench read and the position taken.

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna
- **Process:** [process_the_heating](process_the_heating.md)
- **Place:** [The Sauna](place_the_sauna.md)

## Initiated by
[process_the_heating](process_the_heating.md) has run.
The room is ready.
The first persona is at the door.
[The Bench](piece_the_bench.md) has positions available:
top bench, upper bench, middle bench, lower bench.
None are taken yet.
The door opens.

## Direction
Toward the bench filled.
Each persona enters, reads what is available,
and takes a position.
The read happens before the sitting.
The sitting happens before the decision is conscious.
The position taken is the Shadow's first statement
in this room tonight.

## Lever
[The Bench](piece_the_bench.md) offering positions
pressing against each persona's Shadow.
The carrying force: the available positions,
each one a different heat, a different social weight,
a different version of the question
*what are you here to do.*
The resistance: what each persona brought through the door.
The Shadow does not negotiate with the bench.
It reads the available positions
and takes the one that fits what it already is.
The top bench goes to whoever needs
to prove something in full heat.
The upper bench goes to whoever
has been here long enough
to take it without statement.
The middle bench goes to whoever
is watching rather than claiming.
The lower bench goes to whoever
needs the room to ask the least.
Nobody deliberates.
The body already knows.

## Echo
The bench is filled or partially filled.
Every taken position is a Shadow already running.
Every available position is a pressure
still waiting for someone to claim it.
[process_the_loyly](process_the_loyly.md) can begin
when the [Löylynheittäjä](position_loylynnheittaja.md) position
has been claimed —
which happens the moment
someone picks up the [Ladle](piece_ladle.md).
That too is an arrival.
