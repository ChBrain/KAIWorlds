# Persona: Jere
## Title: Student

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna

He has not been in a sauna since he was eight years old.
That was with his father.
His father is not here.

## Projection
A young man sitting on the lowest bench
with the careful posture of someone
trying not to take up too much space.
His [towel](piece_towel.md) is slightly too small —
he grabbed the first one he found.
He is very warm. He thought he would be warm.
He did not know it would feel like this.

## Action
Hold still. Do not show that this is difficult.
He is not sure whether the difficulty is the heat
or the silence
or the fact that he does not know
what is expected of him
or whether he is doing it wrong.

## Shadow
He thinks he is struggling with the heat.
He is not struggling with the heat.
He is struggling with being in a room
where he does not know the rules
and everyone else seems to.
The heat is the least of it.
He will leave because of the heat.
He will tell himself it was the heat.
It will not have been the heat.

## Tell
He looks at the [sauna door](piece_sauna_door.md).
Then at his hands.
Then at the door again.
