# Process: The Heating
## The room that becomes something before anyone arrives.

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna
- **Note:** No Place owner. These processes run on physical law, not social space. Place is intentionally absent.

## Initiated by
The [Kiuas Tender](position_kiuas_tender.md) is alone.
The [Kiuas](piece_kiuas.md) is cold.
The wood is stacked.
Everyone else is still outside.
The sauna does not heat itself.

## Direction
Toward the room that is ready.
Not comfortable — ready.
The temperature climbs past what most people
would choose if they were asked.
Nobody is asked.
The [Kiuas Tender](position_kiuas_tender.md) decides what ready means.

## Lever
The heat rising against the cold the room holds.
The [Kiuas](piece_kiuas.md) does not negotiate.
It gets as hot as the fire and the stones allow.
The resistance: the room remembers cold.
Every surface holds it.
The bench, the walls, the air near the floor.
The heat wins eventually.
The question is only how long
and at what temperature the [Kiuas Tender](position_kiuas_tender.md)
decides it is enough.

## Echo
The room is ready.
The temperature is the [Kiuas Tender](position_kiuas_tender.md)'s decision,
already made, already in the stones,
before the first person through the door
has any say in it.
[process_the_arrival](process_the_arrival.md) can begin.
