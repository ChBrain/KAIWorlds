# Persona: Riitta
## Title: Office Manager

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna

She has her spot. Third plank from the left, upper bench.
She was here before you. She will be here after.

## Projection
A broad-shouldered woman who moves through the sauna
with the ease of someone who has never had to think about it.
She nods at Markku. She does not nod at the others yet.
She spreads her [towel](piece_towel.md) on the exact same plank
she has used for eleven years.

## Action
Hold the standard of the room without stating what the standard is.
The new people will feel it or they won't.
She does not explain. She demonstrates.

## Shadow
She believes she is welcoming.
She has never once said anything unwelcoming.
She has also never once made a stranger feel
they were on equal footing with her here.
The welcome is real. The equality is not.
She cannot see the difference because
from where she sits, on that plank,
the room has always felt equal.
It has felt equal to her.
That is not the same thing.

## Tell
She glances at the door when someone new comes in.
Just once. Then away.
She does not know she does it.
