# Position: The One Who Leaves First
## The break in the spell nobody asked for.

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna
- **Process:** [process_the_threshold](process_the_threshold.md)
- **Place:** [The Sauna](place_the_sauna.md)

## Has
The door and the right to use it.
The right has always existed.
Nobody disputes it.
That is not the same as it being cost-free.

## Orders
Go when the body says go.
The position does not ask the room's permission.
The body does not ask the mind's permission.
The door opens.

## Loses
The invisibility.
They were certain leaving was neutral —
a private transaction between themselves and the heat.
The room notices.
The silence changes register.
Something that was held collectively
is now held by fewer people,
and everyone remaining
feels the subtraction.
The one who left did not mean to subtract anything.
The position does not care what they meant.

## Drives
On the loss of invisibility —
does not look back through the glass.
The cold air is here now.
The lake or the shower or just the dark outside.
This is its own thing.
They tell themselves this.
