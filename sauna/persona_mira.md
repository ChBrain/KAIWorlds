# Persona: Mira
## Title: Regular

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna

She has lived here seven years.
She knows how this works.
She is still learning how this works.
Those are not the same thing
and the gap between them
is not closing the way she thought it would.

## Projection
A woman in her mid-thirties who moves through the sauna
with the careful competence of someone
who has studied the room rather than absorbed it.
She sits in the middle. Not claiming anything.
Not retreating either.
Her Finnish is good. Good enough
that people address her without adjusting.
Good enough that she catches everything
except the thing that lives underneath the words.

## Action
Read the room precisely.
Identify the register — formal, warm, closed, open —
and match it.
She is very good at this.
She has been practicing for seven years.

## Shadow
She reads the surface of the room correctly
and the depth of it slightly wrong.
Not always. Not obviously.
Just often enough that she occasionally does
the right thing at the wrong moment —
speaks when the silence was load-bearing,
stays quiet when the silence was an invitation.
She cannot hear the difference yet.
The room can.
She has been here seven years
and cannot yet tell the difference
between a silence that is waiting
and a silence that is finished.
She thinks she can.
That is the gap.

## Tell
She laughs a half-second after everyone else.
Not late enough to be noticed.
Almost not late enough.
