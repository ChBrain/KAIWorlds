# Place: The Porch
## Location: Outside the sauna door. Between the heat and everything else.

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna

## Shown
- A wooden step down from [The Sauna](place_the_sauna.md).
  The air hits immediately. Cold, even in summer.
  The body registers it before the mind does.
- Wooden boards underfoot. Worn. Wet from previous exits.
- The cold barrel, or the lake, or the shower rigged to the wall —
  depending on the sauna. Always one of these.
  Always cold. Always close.
- Towels on a hook or a rail. Some dry. Some not.
- A bench or a step to sit on. Low.
  People sit here wrapped in towels and say nothing
  or everything.
- The night, or the evening, or the afternoon —
  whatever the sky is doing.
  There is no ceiling. The sky is available from here.
- The door back to [The Sauna](place_the_sauna.md). Still warm at the edges.
  The heat comes through it faintly.
  It is always slightly open.

## Holds
- [The Towel](piece_towel.md). Several of them. Belonging to different people.
  Folded or dropped. The difference is information.
- [The Cold Water](piece_the_cold_water.md). The barrel, the lake, the shower.
  Always full. Always the same temperature.
  Always waiting.

## Offers
- The cold. To whoever steps out.
  It does not wait to be requested.
- The bench. To whoever needs to sit before going back in
  or deciding not to go back in.
- The sky. To whoever looks up.
  Nobody is required to look up.
  Some people do not look up once.
- The distance from the bench inside.
  What could not be said in the heat
  sometimes gets said here.
  Not always. Not on demand.
  The porch does not promise this.
  It only makes it possible.

## Withheld
- Whether someone is cooling down or leaving.
  From the porch you cannot tell the difference
  and neither can anyone watching from inside.
  The person standing here sometimes cannot tell either.
  The porch holds that uncertainty without resolving it.
