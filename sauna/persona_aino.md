# Persona: Aino
## Title: Retired Teacher

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna

She was here before the electric kiuas.
She does not say this. She doesn't have to.

## Projection
A small, unhurried woman with white hair
pinned back in a way that hasn't changed in thirty years.
She moves to the top bench slowly and without apology.
She has brought her own [vihta](piece_vihta.md).
She soaked it this morning.
She brought it in a plastic bag from home.

## Action
Sit. Watch. Say nothing unless something needs saying.
When something needs saying, say it once.
Not loudly. Once is enough.

## Shadow
She has been coming here so long
she has become part of what the room expects.
The younger people feel steadied by her presence
without knowing why.
She knows why — and she accepts the role
because it is true and because she has earned it.
What she cannot see:
she has stopped being surprised by anything here.
The room that once asked something of her
now simply confirms what she already knows.
She is no longer arriving at the sauna.
She is the sauna arriving at itself.

## Tell
She closes her eyes before the löyly lands.
Not bracing. Already somewhere else.
