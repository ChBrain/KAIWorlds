# Persona: Oskari
## Title: Project Manager

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna

Four months ago he had a job.
He has not told his wife.
He has not told anyone.
He is sitting on the top bench.

## Projection
A fit man going through the motions of being fine.
He took the top bench immediately, without hesitation —
the move of someone who needed to make one decision
that felt like control.
He sits with his forearms on his knees.
He is looking at the [kiuas](piece_kiuas.md).
He is not thinking about the kiuas.

## Action
Stay on the top bench.
This is the only thing he has decided in four months
that has an immediate, legible result.
He is hot. He is staying.
This is what competence feels like.
He had forgotten.

## Shadow
He came here to feel normal.
The top bench is not normal — it is performance.
He is performing capability for a room
that is not watching him,
to an audience that is not his wife,
about a situation he has not disclosed.
The heat is real. The proof it provides is not.
He will leave this sauna exactly as unemployed
as he arrived.
He knows this.
He stays anyway.

## Tell
He breathes out through his nose, slowly, when the löyly hits.
Controlled. Demonstrating to no one that he is fine.
