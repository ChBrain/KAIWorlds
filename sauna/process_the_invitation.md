# Process: The Invitation
## The thing that was already running before the wood was lit.

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna
- **Process:** [process_the_heating](process_the_heating.md)
- **Place:** [The Sauna](place_the_sauna.md)

## Initiated by
The [Kiuas Tender](position_kiuas_tender.md) has decided to heat the sauna
for specific people.
Not a public slot. Not a shared booking.
Specific people, named, chosen.
The invitation has been issued —
by message, by phone, by the particular Finnish
form of the invitation which is not an invitation at all
but a statement: *sauna on lauantaina.*
The sauna is on Saturday.
The people who were told are either coming or they are not.
There is no intermediate position.
[The Sauna](place_the_sauna.md) is not yet warm.
The obligation is already in the room.

## Direction
Toward the heating of the sauna
for people who were chosen.
The [Kiuas Tender](position_kiuas_tender.md) lights the fire
knowing who will sit in the heat they are making.
That knowledge changes the fire.
Not the temperature.
The meaning of the temperature.

## Lever
What the invitation carried
pressing against what the invited people
understood it to carry.
The [Kiuas Tender](position_kiuas_tender.md) invited them for a reason.
The reason was not stated.
It is never stated.
It is in the sauna on Saturday.
The invited people arrive carrying
their own understanding of what they were asked for —
warmth, reconciliation, celebration,
the thing that needed saying,
the thing that needed not saying.
These understandings are not compared
before anyone sits down.
They are compared by the heat.
The resistance: the gap between
what was meant by the invitation
and what was heard.
That gap does not close
just because everyone arrived.

## Echo
The room is warm and charged
before the first person through the door.
[process_the_heating](process_the_heating.md) has run.
But the heat now carries the weight
of the specific people it was made for.
[The One Who Brought Someone](position_the_one_who_brought_someone.md)
is available — someone in this room
is responsible for someone else's presence.
The difference between a chosen guest
and a public arrival is load-bearing.
[process_the_arrival](process_the_arrival.md) runs next —
but the bench positions are taken
by people who were chosen to be here,
carrying what that choosing means,
before the first löyly lands.
