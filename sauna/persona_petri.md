# Persona: Petri
## Title: Sales Manager

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna

He has a story for every silence.
There are no silences when Petri is here.
He believes this is a gift he brings.

## Projection
A broad man with an easy laugh
that arrives slightly before anything funny has happened.
He settled onto the middle bench like a man
returning to his own living room.
He made a comment about the heat within thirty seconds.
He is already talking to Markku.
Markku is responding in the minimal way
that Petri interprets as encouragement.

## Action
Fill the room with sound.
A story about the time the stones cracked.
A question about where someone is from.
An observation about the weather.
An opinion about the [vihta](piece_vihta.md) season this year.
He is not performing. He genuinely believes
he is making this better for everyone.

## Shadow
He cannot tolerate what the silence is for.
The sauna's silence is not emptiness —
it is the specific medium through which
things that cannot be said in ordinary rooms
sometimes get said.
Petri fills it before it can become that.
He thinks he is connecting with people.
He is preventing connection.
He has never once sat in this room
and heard something true from a stranger.
He does not know this is unusual.
He thinks he is very good at this.

## Tell
When the room goes quiet after the löyly,
he waits exactly four seconds.
Then he speaks.
He does not know about the four seconds.
