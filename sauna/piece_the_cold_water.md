# Piece: The Cold Water
## The thing that receives what the bench found.

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna
- **Place:** [The Porch](place_the_porch.md)

## Place
[The Porch](place_the_porch.md).
The barrel, the lake, the shower rigged to the wall.
Always cold. Always close.
Always the same temperature regardless
of who goes in.

## Load Bearing
Without it the session has no counter.
The bench reveals. The cold water receives.
Remove it and what the heat found
has nowhere to go —
it stays on the bench, accumulating,
building past the point of use
into something else entirely.
The cold water is not the end of the session.
It is what makes the next round possible.
It is also, occasionally, the end of the session.
The body decides which.

## Apparent
A barrel. Dark wood, iron bands.
Full. The surface still.
Or a lake — black, enormous, indifferent.
Or a shower nozzle on a wooden wall,
the water running cold from a pipe
that has never been warm.
All three are the same piece.
The cold is the constant.
The container changes.
When you go in:
total. Simultaneous. No position.
The top bench and the lower bench
receive exactly the same cold.
This is the only moment in the session
when that is true.

## Yearbook
It has received people in triumph —
the specific cold-water shout of a body
that endured the top bench
and earned this.
It has received people in relief —
the quiet entry of someone
who needed the bench to stop
asking something of them.
It has received people mid-cry,
the cold arriving at exactly the wrong moment
and exactly the right one simultaneously.
It has received Oskari's shaking hands
and didn't ask why they were shaking.
It has received first-timers who gasped
and regulars who barely flinched
and one person who stood in front of it
for a full minute before going in
and came out having decided something
they had been not-deciding for months.
The cold water does not distinguish between them.
It receives everyone at the same temperature.
What each person brings to it
is entirely their own.
What they carry out is different
from what they carried in.
The cold water does not track this.
It is already still again.
