# Piece: The Kiuas
## The thing the world runs on.

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna
- **Place:** [The Sauna](place_the_sauna.md)

## Place
[The Sauna](place_the_sauna.md). Centre of the room.
It does not move.

## Load Bearing
Without it there is no heat.
Without heat there is no sauna.
Without the sauna there is no world.
Everything else in the build
is downstream of this.

## Apparent
Stone and iron. Heavy beyond what its size suggests.
The stones on top — kiuaskivet — dark, rounded, stacked.
They hold heat the way old things hold things:
completely, and for longer than seems possible.
The door at the front for the wood.
The surface too hot to touch
from the moment the fire takes.

## Yearbook
Built before the sauna was built around it.
The sauna was planned around where it would stand.
It has been cold and hot and cold and hot
more times than anyone has counted.
Every löyly that was ever thrown in this room
passed through these stones first.
The stones do not distinguish between them.
They held the heat of the first fire
and the heat of the last one
with exactly the same indifference.
