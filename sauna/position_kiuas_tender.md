# Position: Kiuas Tender
## The one who owns the temperature before anyone arrives.

## Owner
- **Project:** Sauna
- **Process:** [process_the_heating](process_the_heating.md)
- **Place:** [The Sauna](place_the_sauna.md)

## Has
The fire and the time before the others come.
The kiuas is cold. The wood is here.
The decision about what temperature the room will be
belongs entirely to this position.
No one else is present to contest it.

## Orders
Heat it properly.
The position does not specify what properly means.
That is the whole of its authority.

## Loses
The neutrality.
They were certain they were just heating the sauna —
a practical task, no statement made.
The temperature is a statement.
Everyone who enters the room
enters the decision the Kiuas Tender already made.
They did not know they had opinions until someone complained.

## Drives
On the loss of neutrality — tends the fire more carefully.
The next sauna will be exactly right.
They are still the only one who decides what right means.
