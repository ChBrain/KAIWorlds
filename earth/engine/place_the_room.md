# Place: The Room

## Owner
- **Project:** Earth

## Shown
A meeting room. Table. Chairs. A screen at one end showing [The Data](piece_the_data.md). [Knower](position_knower.md) is present. [Institution](position_institution.md) is present. [Actor](position_actor.md) may or may not be present -- the room does not require them. The room has no windows.

## Holds
- [The Data](piece_the_data.md) -- displayed, summarised, projected onto the wall. Present in full. Available to everyone in the room.

## Offers
The appearance of proximity to the problem. Everyone in this room is working on [Earth](place_earth.md). The room offers the feeling of contact with the Place without the Place being present. It offers consensus -- the people in the room can agree. It offers documentation -- what was agreed can be recorded. It offers legitimacy -- decisions made here carry institutional weight.

## Withheld
The planet. [Amplifies Heat](process_amplifies_heat.md) does not pause for the meeting. [Crosses Threshold](process_crosses_threshold.md) does not wait for the report to be filed. The room withholds consequence -- nothing that happens here lands on [Earth](place_earth.md) at the speed the processes are running. The distance between the screen and the Place it represents is the room's constitutive feature. It cannot be removed without destroying the room.
