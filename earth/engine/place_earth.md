# Place: Earth

## Owner
- **Project:** Earth

## Shown
The planet. Atmosphere, ocean, ice, soil, forest, current. Present tense: warming. The ice sheets are retreating. The permafrost is softening at the edges. The AMOC is slowing in water that cannot be seen from the surface. The Amazon is releasing more carbon than it absorbs in its southern sections. Everything that happens in this world happens to this Place or because of it.

## Holds
- [The Threshold](piece_the_threshold.md) -- embedded in the system.
- [The Act](piece_the_act.md) -- received.
- [The Data](piece_the_data.md) -- produced here, measured here, read elsewhere.

## Offers
The measurement. Temperature, concentration, species count, sea level. The instruments work. The readings are available. What lands here determines whether any of the human processes worked.

## Withheld
The answer. Whether [The Threshold](piece_the_threshold.md) has been crossed. Whether [The Act](piece_the_act.md) mattered. Whether the cascade is already running or still stoppable. [Earth](place_earth.md) processes on timescales that do not fit inside a human life or a political cycle or a news cycle. The consequence is always already running. The confirmation always arrives after the moment when it could have changed the decision.
