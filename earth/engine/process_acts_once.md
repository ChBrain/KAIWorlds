# Process: Acts Once

## Owner
- **Project:** Earth

## Initiated by
An [Actor](position_actor.md) is present. The knowledge from [Knows Everything](process_knows_everything.md) has reached them. The frameworks from [Protects Destruction](process_protects_destruction.md) have not been enough. The [Actor](position_actor.md) makes one decision. Not a policy. Not a framework. One act. The process begins at the moment of commitment and cannot be recalled.

## Direction
Toward consequence. The act is complete the moment it is taken. What follows is not more action -- it is the world responding to the action already taken. The direction is out of the [Actor](position_actor.md)'s hands the instant the process starts.

## Lever
Carrying force: the irreversibility. The act is done. It cannot be undone, refined, or scaled back. The carrying force is the commitment itself -- the moment when one person's decision enters a system too large to control and too interconnected to predict.

Resistance: the scale of [Earth](place_earth.md). One act against a planetary system. The resistance is not hostile. It is simply the difference in size between the actor and the place the act lands in.

## Echo
The consequence is running. [Earth](place_earth.md) has received [The Act](piece_the_act.md) and holds it without confirmation.
