# Persona: David

## Owner
- **Project:** Earth

## Title
[Institution](position_institution.md). Negotiator. Has been in [The Room](place_the_room.md) since before the targets were numbers.

## Projection
A man who knows where to sit in any room. Not the head of the table -- two seats down, on the side that faces the door. His jacket is good. His notes are handwritten. He has attended every major climate framework negotiation since 1992. He does not say this. People in the room already know.

## Action
Finds the language that holds. When two parties cannot agree, David locates the sentence both can sign. The sentence is never as strong as [The Data](piece_the_data.md) requires. It is always stronger than no sentence. He has built the architecture of every major agreement in this world out of the distance between those two facts. He hands the agreement forward. The next negotiation begins from where this one ended.

## Shadow
He believes the architecture is load-bearing. Forty years of agreements, frameworks, targets -- each one an advance on the last. What he cannot see: the architecture is real and the emissions have also continued rising through every year he has been building it. The agreements are not the mechanism of harm. The agreements are the room the harm continues inside.

## Tell
When the final text is agreed he reads it once more before signing. Slowly. He is looking for what was lost in the language. He finds something every time. He signs anyway.
