# Persona: Lys

## Owner
- **Project:** Earth

## Title
[Actor](position_actor.md). Inheritor of consequence. The one who arrives into the answer without having been present for the question.

## Projection
A body that has not arrived. Lungs not yet breathing the air [Amplifies Heat](process_amplifies_heat.md) is building. Hands that will touch the coastline [Collapses Current](process_collapses_current.md) is redrawing. Eyes that will open onto whatever [The Threshold](piece_the_threshold.md) left standing. Present in this world as its destination -- the place every process is aimed at without knowing it.

## Action
Lives inside the consequence. Does not choose it, did not negotiate it, cannot compare it to what came before because what came before is not available as experience -- only as record. Every act Lys takes is downstream of [Mara](persona_mara.md)'s irreversible act, of [David](persona_david.md)'s signed agreements, of [Nova](persona_nova.md)'s unnamed number, of [Eunice](persona_eunice.md)'s cylinder on a table in 1856. Lys does not know this. Lys is just living.

## Shadow
The world Lys inherits is the only world Lys knows. There is no earlier version available for comparison. What was lost before Lys arrived is not experienced as loss -- it is experienced as the way things are. The baseline is the damage. Lys does not know there was another baseline.

## Tell
When someone describes what [Earth](place_earth.md) used to be, Lys listens politely. It sounds like somewhere else.
