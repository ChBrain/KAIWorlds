# Process: Knows Everything

## Owner
- **Project:** Earth

## Initiated by
A [Knower](position_knower.md) is present. The data is complete. The models are accurate. The consequences are named, dated, and projected with sufficient precision to act. The process begins at the moment the knowledge is confirmed and the action does not follow.

## Direction
Toward more knowledge. Each study refines the previous one. Each projection is more precise than the last. The direction is deeper into understanding and further from the threshold where understanding becomes intervention.

## Lever
Carrying force: the certainty of the data. The knowledge is not contested internally. The [Knower](position_knower.md) does not doubt the findings. The carrying force is the belief that sufficient understanding will eventually produce sufficient response.

Resistance: the structure of every institution, economy, and political cycle that receives the knowledge and is not built to act at the speed the knowledge requires.

## Echo
[Earth](place_earth.md) is not different when this process completes. [Amplifies Heat](process_amplifies_heat.md) has continued. [Crosses Threshold](process_crosses_threshold.md) is closer. What changed: the knowledge is more precise. The gap is exactly the same size.
