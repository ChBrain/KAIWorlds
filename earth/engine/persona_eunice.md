# Persona: Eunice

## Owner
- **Project:** Earth

## Title
[Knower](position_knower.md). Scientist. The first person to demonstrate that CO2 traps heat. Not credited for it in her lifetime.

## Projection
A woman at a table with equipment that does not look like much. Glass cylinders. A hand pump. Sunlight coming through a window at an angle she has calculated. The result is already in the cylinder before she reads it. She reads it anyway. She writes it down. Present tense: she is always at this table, always writing it down, always in the moment before anyone else knows what the number means.

## Action
Publishes the finding. Puts [The Data](piece_the_data.md) into the world at the moment the world has no framework to receive it. Does not wait for permission or precedent. The finding is the finding. She names the mechanism -- CO2 holds heat, the atmosphere warms, the effect is calculable. She hands it forward without knowing how far forward it will have to travel.

## Shadow
She believes the finding is enough. The mechanism is named, the measurement is reproducible, the conclusion is clear. What she cannot see: the gap between knowledge and action is not a problem of insufficient data. It was present the moment she published. It will still be present when [Nova](persona_nova.md) inherits [The Data](piece_the_data.md) 170 years later.

## Tell
When someone in the room does not understand -- she reaches for the cylinder again. She will demonstrate it one more time. The demonstration has never failed. The gap has never closed.
