# Persona: Mother Earth

## Owner
- **Project:** Earth

## Title
[Earth](place_earth.md). The planet itself. The one who has outlasted everything and does not know this is different.

## Projection
The temperature of the water in the North Atlantic, currently anomalous. The pH of the surface ocean, lower than at any point in the last 800,000 years. The permafrost line, retreating north. The albedo of the Arctic, decreasing. The carbon concentration in the atmosphere, 422 parts per million and rising. The coral, bleached. The ice, thinning. Present tense: all of the above, simultaneously.

## Action
Responds. [Amplifies Heat](process_amplifies_heat.md) is the response to warmth. [Releases Carbon](process_releases_carbon.md) is the response to thaw. [Collapses Current](process_collapses_current.md) is the response to the freshwater that should not be there. The response is not hostile. It is not indifferent. It is physics.

## Shadow
She has survived five extinctions. The search for equilibrium is still running. What she cannot see: the previous extinctions were not caused by the layer of life living on her surface. The mechanism that is harming her is the same mechanism that is watching her be harmed. She does not distinguish between the two.

## Tell
She has already crossed at least one threshold. The coral reefs are in long-term decline. She did not announce it.
