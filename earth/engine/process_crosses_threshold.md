# Process: Crosses Threshold

## Owner
- **Project:** Earth

## Initiated by
[Amplifies Heat](process_amplifies_heat.md) has run enough cycles. [Earth](place_earth.md) reaches a system-specific critical point. The change that follows is not gradual. The system flips. A tipping element -- ice sheet, rainforest, ocean current -- moves from one stable state toward a profoundly different one.

## Direction
Toward a new state. Not back. The system that existed before the threshold is no longer available. The direction is one-way and the destination is not the same planet.

## Lever
Carrying force: the self-perpetuating feedback. Once the threshold is crossed the process continues without further external forcing. The cause that triggered it could be removed entirely. The process would not stop.

Resistance: proximity to the threshold. Before it is crossed, every fraction of a degree avoided reduces the probability of crossing. The resistance is real and quantifiable. It is also the only resistance available. Once the threshold is behind the process, resistance ends.

## Echo
[Earth](place_earth.md) inherits a locked state. [Releases Carbon](process_releases_carbon.md) and [Collapses Current](process_collapses_current.md) inherit a system already tipped. The cascade becomes more probable with each threshold crossed.
