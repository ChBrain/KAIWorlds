# Process: Amplifies Heat

## Owner
- **Project:** Earth

## Initiated by
[Earth](place_earth.md) has warmed enough to begin melting surface ice. The threshold does not announce itself. Ice retreats. Darker ground and ocean surface beneath it are exposed. The condition that makes this process inevitable is already present.

## Direction
Toward more heat. Each degree of warming removes more ice. Each removal of ice exposes more surface. Each exposed surface absorbs more radiation. The loop closes. Heat produces the condition that produces more heat.

## Lever
Carrying force: the ice-albedo feedback. Bright ice reflects solar radiation back into space. Dark ocean and rock absorb it. Remove the ice and the planet's own surface becomes the engine of its warming.

Resistance: the remaining ice. While it lasts, it reflects. While it reflects, it slows the process. The resistance is structural, not intentional. It diminishes with every cycle.

## Echo
[Crosses Threshold](process_crosses_threshold.md) inherits a warmer planet with less reflective surface than before.
