# Piece: The Data

## Owner
- **Project:** Earth
- **Process:** [Knows Everything](process_knows_everything.md)

## Place
Where [Knower](position_knower.md) is.

## Load Bearing
Remove it and [Knows Everything](process_knows_everything.md) has no carrying force. [Protects Destruction](process_protects_destruction.md) loses its justification -- the institution needs the data to prove it is working. [Acts Once](process_acts_once.md) loses its origin -- the [Actor](position_actor.md) acts because the data made the cost of not acting visible. Without this piece the world runs on assumption, not on knowledge.

## Apparent
Numbers. Graphs. Projections in degrees and decades. Published in journals, presented in rooms, summarised in reports that summarise previous reports. Present tense: accumulating. Each new study sits on top of the last. The pile does not decrease.

## Yearbook
First measured in the 19th century -- Eunice Newton Foote, 1856, demonstrating that CO2 traps heat. Keeling Curve began 1958, measuring atmospheric CO2 without interruption since. IPCC established 1988. Each assessment more precise, more urgent, more comprehensive than the last. The data has been sufficient to act on for decades. It is still being produced.
