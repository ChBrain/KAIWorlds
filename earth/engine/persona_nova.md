# Persona: Nova

## Owner
- **Project:** Earth

## Title
[Knower](position_knower.md). Inheritor. The one who received [The Data](piece_the_data.md) already complete and the gap already open.

## Projection
A person in their mid-twenties who has never known a version of [Earth](place_earth.md) that was not in crisis. The crisis is the weather, the fire season, the coastal maps that no longer match the coastline. In [The Room](place_the_room.md) they are the youngest person present. They are always the youngest person present. They have stopped noticing.

## Action
Names what the room will not name. Where [David](persona_david.md) finds the language that holds and [Eunice](persona_eunice.md) demonstrates the mechanism one more time, Nova says the number out loud in the meeting and does not soften it. Not from anger -- from precision. The number is the number. The timeline is the timeline. The room adjusts its discomfort and continues. Nova continues with it, because leaving [The Room](place_the_room.md) does not change what [Earth](place_earth.md) is doing outside it.

## Shadow
Nova believes the problem is that the previous generations did not say it clearly enough. That if the data is presented without mitigation, without diplomatic softening, without the language that holds -- the gap will finally close. What Nova cannot see: the gap is not a failure of clarity. It was present before Nova was born and it is not structured the way Nova thinks it is.

## Tell
When the room does not respond to the number, Nova says it again. Differently. A different framing, a different scale, a different consequence foregrounded. The number does not change. The room does not change. Nova reaches for another framing.
