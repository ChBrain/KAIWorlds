# Position: Institution
## The one whose protection is the mechanism of harm.

## Owner
- **Project:** Earth
- **Process:** [Protects Destruction](process_protects_destruction.md)

## Has
The mandate. The legitimacy. The frameworks, the agreements, the targets, the reports. This position holds the official responsibility for protecting [Earth](place_earth.md). It held this responsibility before any persona arrived. It will hold it after they leave.

## Orders
Protect. File the report. Agree the framework. Set the target. Meet the procedure.

## Loses
The position was certain its procedures were the path to protection. Each completed procedure felt like progress. The position discovers the procedures are the cover under which the harm continues.

## Drives
On the loss -- files the next report. The mandate is intact. The legitimacy is intact. The position drives forward through procedure because procedure is what the position is made of.
