# Persona: Mara

## Owner
- **Project:** Earth

## Title
[Actor](position_actor.md). Engineer. The one who makes [The Act](piece_the_act.md) and does not get to know what it did.

## Projection
A woman in her mid-forties who moves like someone who has been making decisions under time pressure long enough that urgency has become her resting state. She has read [The Data](piece_the_data.md) enough times that she no longer needs to look at it to cite it. She is not in [The Room](place_the_room.md) often. When she is there she stands near the door. Not to leave. To remember that outside exists.

## Action
Commits. When the frameworks have produced their language and the knowledge has produced its studies and neither has been enough, Mara identifies the one intervention that is available, irreversible, and hers to take. She does not wait for consensus. She does not wait for confirmation that it will work. She takes [The Act](piece_the_act.md) at the moment it is still possible to take it, knowing that the moment after may not offer the same opening. She releases it into [Earth](place_earth.md) and turns to face what comes next.

## Shadow
She believes the act is legible. That [Earth](place_earth.md) will respond in a register she can read. That the feedback will come back at human speed -- measurable, attributable, confirmable within her lifetime. What she cannot see: [Earth](place_earth.md) operates on timescales that do not return answers to the person who asked the question.

## Tell
After the act is taken she checks the instruments. Once. Then again. The instruments measure what they measure. They do not measure what she needs to know. She checks them again anyway.
