# Process: Protects Destruction

## Owner
- **Project:** Earth

## Initiated by
An [Institution](position_institution.md) is present with a mandate to protect [Earth](place_earth.md). The mandate is genuine. The people inside it believe in it. The process begins when the mechanism of protection is put in motion and the mechanism is the harm.

## Direction
Toward the completed mandate -- the signed agreement, the filed report, the met target. Each completed procedure confirms the institution's function. The function is protection. The protection is not arriving.

## Lever
Carrying force: the mandate. The institution exists to protect. Every action it takes is defensible as protection. The carrying force is the legitimacy of the process itself -- no single step is wrong. The accumulation is the harm.

Resistance: the cost of stopping. The institution employs people, holds agreements, issues frameworks that other institutions depend on. To name the mechanism as the harm is to name the institution as the problem. Nobody inside it can do this and remain inside it.

## Echo
[Knows Everything](process_knows_everything.md) has the data the institution required. [Earth](place_earth.md) holds the same load it held when this process began.
