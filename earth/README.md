# Earth

*The data is in. The threshold is approaching. The question is whether The Act arrives before it does.*

---

*For the person who knows what the numbers say and cannot make the system move at the speed the numbers require.*

---

## What this world is

The planet is warming. Not as metaphor -- as mechanism. The ice retreats. The surface beneath it absorbs what the ice reflected. The permafrost softens. The Amazon releases more than it absorbs. The AMOC slows in water that cannot be seen from the surface. Seven processes are running simultaneously. Four of them are planetary. Three of them are human. None of them are waiting.

In a room with no windows, people who know the numbers are trying to act on them. The planet does not attend the meeting.

This world runs on one question: can the human processes -- Knows Everything, Protects Destruction, Acts Once -- move fast enough to intersect the planetary ones? The answer is not in the room. It is in Earth.

## The Personas

[Eunice](engine/persona_eunice.md) - Knower. Scientist. The first person to demonstrate that CO2 traps heat. Not credited for it in her lifetime. She is always at the table, always writing it down, always in the moment before anyone else knows what the number means.

[David](engine/persona_david.md) - Institution. Negotiator. Has been in The Room since before the targets were numbers. He knows where to sit in any room.

[Mara](engine/persona_mara.md) - Actor. Engineer. The one who makes The Act and does not get to know what it did. She stands near the door. Not to leave. To remember that outside exists.

[Nova](engine/persona_nova.md) - Knower. Inheritor. The one who received The Data already complete and the gap already open. Always the youngest person in the room.

[Lys](engine/persona_lys.md) - Actor. Inheritor of consequence. The one who arrives into the answer without having been present for the question. Present in this world as its destination.

[Mother Earth](engine/persona_mother_earth.md) - The planet itself. The one who has outlasted everything and does not know this is different.

## The Pieces

[The Data](engine/piece_the_data.md) - the measurement. Temperature, concentration, sea level, species count. Available in full. In the room. On the screen.

[The Threshold](engine/piece_the_threshold.md) - the point after which certain processes cannot be reversed. Present in the system before anyone agreed to name it.

[The Act](engine/piece_the_act.md) - the intervention. What the human positions can produce. The thing that either lands on Earth in time or does not.

## The Places

[Earth](engine/place_earth.md) - the planet. Atmosphere, ocean, ice, soil, forest, current. Present tense: warming. The consequence is always already running.

[The Room](engine/place_the_room.md) - the meeting room. Table. Chairs. A screen showing The Data. No windows. The distance between the screen and the Place it represents is the room's constitutive feature.

---

## The Positions

[Knower](engine/position_knower.md) - holds The Data. Has always held it. Cannot make it move faster than the institutions that receive it.

[Institution](engine/position_institution.md) - holds the framework. Has been building the framework since before the current numbers existed. The framework moves at the speed of consensus.

[Actor](engine/position_actor.md) - holds The Act. The only position that produces something that lands on Earth.

---

## The Processes

**Planetary:**

[Amplifies Heat](engine/process_amplifies_heat.md) - the ice-albedo feedback. Ice retreats, darker surface exposed, more radiation absorbed, more heat produced. The loop closes.

[Releases Carbon](engine/process_releases_carbon.md) - the permafrost and forest feedback. Warming unlocks stored carbon. Stored carbon drives warming. The reservoir has been building for millennia.

[Crosses Threshold](engine/process_crosses_threshold.md) - the point after which reversal exits human timescales. Does not announce itself. Arrives inside the other processes.

[Collapses Current](engine/process_collapses_current.md) - the AMOC slowdown. The conveyor that distributes heat across the North Atlantic. Slowing in water that looks like all other water.

**Human:**

[Knows Everything](engine/process_knows_everything.md) - what Knower produces. The complete picture. Available. Legible. Already in the room. Has not closed the gap.

[Protects Destruction](engine/process_protects_destruction.md) - what Institution produces when the framework holds what it holds. Consensus that arrives after the moment when it could have changed the trajectory.

[Acts Once](engine/process_acts_once.md) - what Actor produces. Singular. Irreversible. Aimed at Earth without knowing what it will find there.
