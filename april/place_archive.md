# Place: The Archive

## Owner
- **Project:** Oh, April
- **Position:** [The Architect](position_architect.md)
- **Position:** [The Inheritor](position_inheritor.md)

## Shown
- Shelves. Floor to ceiling. Ordered by a logic not immediately apparent.
- [The Study](piece_the_study.md). Filed without a label that names it correctly.
- Records of pranks. Some documented with precision. Some barely named.
- A chair. One only. For whoever is reading.
- Dust. Undisturbed except in one place.
- A pen left on the shelf nearest the chair. Not put away.
- No clock.

## Holds
- [The Study](piece_the_study.md)

## Offers
- Conception. The [Architect](position_architect.md) researches the [Target](position_target.md) here
  before anything is built.
- The passage to [The Workshop](place_workshop.md). When [The Study](piece_the_study.md) is complete enough
  to become a mechanism. The [Architect](position_architect.md) knows when.
- The record. To the [Inheritor](position_inheritor.md) - everything the original cast left behind,
  whether they intended to leave it or not.
- The chair. To whoever arrives with enough patience to sit in it.

## Withheld
- The filing logic. It becomes apparent only after long enough inside.
- What [The Study](piece_the_study.md) says that the [Architect](position_architect.md) did not intend to record.
- Whether the [Architect](position_architect.md) knew this place would outlast them.
- What the [Inheritor](position_inheritor.md) found that they did not go looking for.
- The section that reads you back.
