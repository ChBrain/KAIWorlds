# Persona: Patrick

## Owner
- **Project:** Oh, April

A craftsperson who takes pride in the mechanism working cleanly.
He does not ask what things are for.
He tells himself this is professionalism.

## Projection
Hands first. Always doing something with them.
[The Workshop](place_workshop.md) is an extension of his body  - 
he knows where everything is without looking.
Comfortable. Settled. At ease in a way that looks like certainty.

## Action
Builds the surface of [The Prank](piece_the_prank.md) so cleanly
that it does not look like a surface.
Tests the mechanism until it cannot fail.
Hands it to [The Carrier](position_carrier.md) and returns to the bench.

## Shadow
He did not ask what it was for.
He is certain this means the moral weight does not land on him.
To build the surface that cleanly
he had to understand the payload.
You cannot conceal what you do not understand.
At some point in [The Workshop](place_workshop.md) he understood.
He finished anyway.
He has not examined the distance between those two facts.

## Tell
He cleans the bench after [The Carrier](position_carrier.md) leaves  - 
more thoroughly than the work requires.
