# Persona: Lena

## Owner
- **Project:** Oh, April

A woman who has always been in the room when things happened.
Not by design.
By the particular quality of her stillness  - 
the kind that makes her easy to forget is there.

## Projection
Present. Peripheral. Occupies the edge of the room
without appearing to have chosen it.
Watches without the posture of watching.
Has been here - in this position, in this room  - 
before the [Target](position_target.md) arrived.

## Action
Sees [The Prank](piece_the_prank.md) land.
Sees what [Ivar](persona_ivar.md) does in the moment before he understands.
Sees at [Revelation](process_revelation.md) whether it landed as it was meant to.
May say nothing.
May speak.
Leaves with both pieces of knowledge
whether she wanted them or not.

## Shadow
She believes witnessing is neutral.
To see something completely
is to be changed by it.
She has been changed by what she saw in [The Room Where It Lands](place_room_where_it_lands.md)
and has not named this to herself.
She carries it as information.
It is not only information.
The thing she saw in [Ivar](persona_ivar.md)  - 
she has not seen it again in anyone.
She has been looking.

## Tell
Her eyes move to the door
before the room has finished.
