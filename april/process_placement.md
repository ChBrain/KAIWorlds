# Process: Placement

## Owner
- **Project:** Oh, April
- **Position:** [The Carrier](position_carrier.md)
- **Place:** [The Street](place_street.md)

## Initiates
[The Prank](piece_the_prank.md) leaves [The Workshop](place_workshop.md).
The [Carrier](position_carrier.md) receives it.
They are not told everything.
They are told enough.
[The Route](place_route.md) opens.
[The Street](place_street.md) waits.

## Direction
Toward the moment the prank stops being the [Carrier](position_carrier.md)'s problem.
The handoff point.
[The Letter](piece_the_letter.md) delivered to a named person who will pass it on.
[The Errand](piece_the_errand.md) begun toward a destination that does not exist.
Where it becomes everyone's reality.

## Lever
**Carrying force** - the [Carrier](position_carrier.md)'s delivery.
Unhesitating. Natural. Unremarkable.
The prank lands softly because the [Carrier](position_carrier.md) does not know they are carrying it.
Or almost does not know.

**Resistance** - [The Route](place_route.md).
The space where refusal is still possible.
Where the [Carrier](position_carrier.md) could open [The Letter](piece_the_letter.md).
Could stop.
Could ask the question they are not ready to ask.
They do not.
They arrive at [The Street](place_street.md).

## Echo
[The Prank](piece_the_prank.md) is in position.
[The Street](place_street.md) holds it.
The [Witness](position_witness.md) is already present.
The [Target](position_target.md) is not yet.
[Detonation](process_detonation.md) inherits it.
