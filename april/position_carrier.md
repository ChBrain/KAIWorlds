# Position: The Carrier

## Owner
- **Project:** Oh, April
- **Process:** [Placement](process_placement.md)
- **Place:** [The Route](place_route.md)

## Has
An important letter.
Or something that looks like one.
Handed over with enough trust
to make refusal feel like an accusation.
[The Route](place_route.md) from here to there.

## Orders
Deliver it.
Don't open it.
The position does not explain why.

## Loses
The excuse of not knowing.
They were certain they were simply carrying something.
Somewhere on [The Route](place_route.md)
the weight of it becomes specific.
Not what is inside - what it means
that they are the one carrying it.
They have been here before.

## Drives
On the loss of not-knowing - delivers it anyway.
Arrives at [The Street](place_street.md).
Hands it over.
Does not ask the question [The Route](place_route.md) offered them.
The question will wait.
It has waited before.
