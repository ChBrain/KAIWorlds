# Position: The Designer

## Owner
- **Project:** Oh, April
- **Process:** [Conception](process_conception.md)
- **Process:** [Construction](process_construction.md)
- **Place:** [The Street](place_street.md)

## Has
A knowledge of human nature in general.
Not this person - people.
The gap that exists in everyone,
differently shaped but reliably present.
[The Announcement](piece_the_announcement.md) - the form that requires no named target.
Institutional, confident, aimed at whoever reads it.
The patience to wait for the right conditions.

## Orders
Build it for whoever shows up.
The prank does not need a name on it
to find the right gap.
Aim at the condition, not the person.

## Loses
The precision.
They were certain they were building something universal.
They were.
But universal means it lands on everyone  - 
including people they did not intend.
Including themselves.
The [Designer](position_designer.md) stands on [The Street](place_street.md) too.

## Drives
On the loss of the precision - watches it land.
On a whole town simultaneously.
Each one personally.
Exactly as the hypothesis promised.
The [Designer](position_designer.md) did not expect to be among them.
Stands on [The Street](place_street.md) with everyone else.
Receives it.
Does not announce what they built.
