# Persona: Oliver

## Owner
- **Project:** Oh, April

A man who does not think small.
He waited years for the right weather.
He is still waiting for the right weather.
He is always waiting for the right weather.

## Projection
Large. Patient. Cheerful in a way that suggests deep reserves.
Moves slowly. Plans at a scale that makes other people uncomfortable.
Keeps notes on conditions - weather, timing, readiness  - 
in a system no one else can read.

## Action
Builds [The Prank](piece_the_prank.md) for whoever shows up.
Aims at the condition, not the person.
Waits until the conditions are exactly right.
Executes without hesitation.
Writes APRIL FOOL in letters large enough to be seen from above.

## Shadow
He believes he is aiming at everyone equally.
[The Designer](position_designer.md) stands on [The Street](place_street.md) too.
[The Announcement](piece_the_announcement.md) lands on him the same as the rest.
He did not design for himself because he did not think he was among the targets.
He has always been among the targets.
This is the gap in him that no study has named.

## Tell
He announces the reveal before checking
whether he is standing in the blast radius.
