## Load Bearing
Without it the world has observation but no event.
[The Study](piece_the_study.md) remains private.
The gap between the [Target](position_target.md)'s self-image and what is visible from outside
is never made visible to the [Target](position_target.md).
Nothing is revealed.
Nothing is inherited.
The hypothesis goes untested.

## Apparent
A surface. Innocent-looking.
Dressed as something that requires no suspicion  - 
a letter, a fish, an announcement, an errand.
The payload not visible from outside.
The gap between surface and payload
engineered with precision.

## Yearbook
It has been a spaghetti harvest on a respected news programme.
It has been a paper fish on a back in a Paris street.
It has been an important letter passed from hand to hand
until the last person opened it and read: *send the fool further.*
It has been seventy tyres burning in a volcano crater
with the words APRIL FOOL spray-painted in the snow.
It has been a clock going digital.
A bell being sold.
A left-handed burger.
A Gmail account launched on the wrong day.
Each time it looked like something else.
Each time it found the gap.
Each time the gap was already there.
The prank did not create it.
The prank only made it visible.
It is patient.
It has been waiting for you specifically.
You did not know that until now.
