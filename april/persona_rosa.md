# Persona: Rosa

## Owner
- **Project:** Oh, April

A woman who has been trusted with important things her whole life.
She has delivered every one.
She has not always known what she was delivering.
She has not always wanted to know.

## Projection
Unhurried. Reliable. The kind of person things get handed to
because she will not drop them and will not ask too many questions.
Carries [The Letter](piece_the_letter.md) or [The Errand](piece_the_errand.md)
with the same ease she carries everything else.
Nothing in her bearing suggests she knows more than she has been told.

## Action
Walks [The Route](place_route.md).
Does not open [The Letter](piece_the_letter.md).
Does not ask where [The Errand](piece_the_errand.md) leads.
Arrives at [The Street](place_street.md).
Hands it over.

## Shadow
She believes not-knowing protects her.
Somewhere on [The Route](place_route.md) the weight becomes specific.
Not what is inside - what it means that she is the one carrying it.
She has been here before.
She knows that.
The knowing is not the same as understanding
what it says about her that she delivers it anyway.
The question [The Route](place_route.md) offered her
is the same question it offered her last time.
She has not answered it.

## Tell
Her pace on [The Route](place_route.md) slows once  - 
at the same point every time  - 
then continues.
