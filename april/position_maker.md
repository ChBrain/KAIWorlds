# Position: The Maker

## Owner
- **Project:** Oh, April
- **Process:** [Construction](process_construction.md)
- **Place:** [The Workshop](place_workshop.md)

## Has
The craft.
[The Workshop](place_workshop.md) stocked and ready.
The knowledge of how to make a surface
that does not show what it carries.
This exists before any specific prank arrives.

## Orders
Make it work.
Not what it means - whether it works.
The mechanism must be clean.
A prank that fails in delivery
reveals only the [Maker](position_maker.md).

## Loses
The innocence of not knowing.
They were certain they were building the surface only.
The craft requires understanding the payload
well enough to hide it properly.
You cannot conceal what you do not understand.
At some point in [The Workshop](place_workshop.md) they understood.
They did not stop.

## Drives
On the loss of not-knowing - finishes it.
The mechanism is clean.
The surface is innocent.
Leaves [The Workshop](place_workshop.md).
Is not present for what follows.
Not at [The Street](place_street.md).
Not in [The Room Where It Lands](place_room_where_it_lands.md).
Not at [The Archive](place_archive.md) when the [Inheritor](position_inheritor.md) arrives.
The [Maker](position_maker.md) built the thing that did all of it.
Sleeps well.
Or almost.
