# Persona: Ivar

## Owner
- **Project:** Oh, April

A man who knows himself well.
He has given this considerable thought.
The gap between what he believes about himself
and what is visible from outside
is the only thing the world required of him.
He provided it without being asked.

## Projection
Composed. Considered. Measured in speech and movement.
Carries his self-image with the ease of long practice.
Does not appear to be performing it.
Has been performing it so long
he can no longer locate the seam.

## Action
Enters [The Room Where It Lands](place_room_where_it_lands.md).
Receives [The Prank](piece_the_prank.md).
Does something in the moment before he understands.
That thing is the truest data point
[The Study](piece_the_study.md) was built to capture.

## Shadow
He believes the gap is not visible.
That certainty is the gap.
He has curated his self-image with such care
that he has mistaken the curation for the self.
[The Prank](piece_the_prank.md) did not create the gap.
It only made it visible.
The gap was always there.
[Aida](persona_aida.md) saw it before she built anything.

## Tell
He laughs - exactly one beat too late.
