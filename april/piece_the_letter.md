# Piece: The Letter

## Owner
- **Project:** Oh, April
- **Position:** [The Carrier](position_carrier.md)

## Place
Where [The Carrier](position_carrier.md) is.

## Load Bearing
Without it the prank has no chain.
It detonates once, in one place, for one person.
The Letter makes the prank self-perpetuating  - 
each recipient becomes a new [Carrier](position_carrier.md)
without being told they have accepted the position.
Remove it and [Echo](process_echo.md) loses its primary mechanism.

## Apparent
Sealed. Addressed to a named person.
Marked important or urgent or confidential
in a way that discourages opening.
Carried at arm's length.
Heavier than it looks.

## Yearbook
In Ireland it was passed from door to door
until the last person opened it
and found four words: *send the fool further.*
The fool was not the final recipient.
The fool was every person who carried it
without asking what was inside.
The chain has never fully stopped.
Every sealed envelope handed to someone
with the instruction *don't open this, just deliver it*
is a link in the same chain.
The [Carrier](position_carrier.md) who delivers it has been here before.
They know that.
They deliver it anyway.
The letter is still travelling.
You may have carried it yourself.
You did not open it.
That was the correct choice.
Or it was the choice the letter required.
The letter does not distinguish.
