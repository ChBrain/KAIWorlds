## Owner

## Place
[The Archive](place_archive.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it [Conception](process_conception.md) produces curiosity, not knowledge.
[Construction](process_construction.md) builds a generic mechanism.
The prank lands on a type, not a person.
The hypothesis weakens from *the prank knows more about you*
to *the prank knows something about people in general.*
The precision collapses.

## Apparent
Notes. Observations. Patterns noticed and recorded.
What the subject does when they think no one is watching.
What they reach for first.
What they flinch from.
What they cannot help but show.
Private. Handwritten or held in memory.
Never shown to the subject.

## Yearbook
It began as attention.
The [Architect](position_architect.md) noticed something
and could not stop noticing.
Called it research.
Called it preparation.
Did not call it love, though it bore that shape.
Grew until it knew the [Target](position_target.md)
more completely than the [Target](position_target.md) knew themselves.
At some point it became a mirror
the [Architect](position_architect.md) had not intended to build.
The [Inheritor](position_inheritor.md) will find it in [The Archive](place_archive.md)
and read it without knowing what it is.
They will think they are reading about the [Target](position_target.md).
They will be reading about the [Architect](position_architect.md).
They will also be reading about themselves.
This is not an accident.
This is what a study does
when it is thorough enough.
