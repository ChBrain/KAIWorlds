# Piece: The Fish

## Owner
- **Project:** Oh, April
- **Place:** [The Street](place_street.md)

## Place
[The Street](place_street.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it the [Witness](position_witness.md) position has no object.
The social dimension of the prank collapses  - 
the crowd that knows before the [Target](position_target.md) does,
the shared knowledge that creates the [Witness](position_witness.md),
the gap between what is visible to everyone
and what the [Target](position_target.md) believes about themselves.
Remove it and detonation becomes private.
The Street becomes unnecessary.

## Apparent
Paper. Cut in the shape of a fish.
Small enough to attach without being noticed.
Pinned or taped to a back, a coat, a bag.
Visible from behind.
Invisible to its wearer.

## Yearbook
In France it has been attached to backs
on the first of April since the sixteenth century.
*Poisson d'avril* - April fish  - 
named for the young fish easily caught,
the gullible one, the one who does not see the hook.
It has been drawn on postcards.
Printed in newspapers as a clue.
Held up behind someone's head in a photograph
they will not see until later.
The mark that the crowd shares before the [Target](position_target.md) knows.
Everyone on [The Street](place_street.md) is briefly in on it together.
That is the prank inside the prank:
the [Witness](position_witness.md) is created simultaneously with the [Target](position_target.md).
A community assembled around a shared secret
that lasts exactly as long as it takes
for the [Target](position_target.md) to reach up and feel it.
You have been the crowd.
You may have been the fish.
You did not feel it until someone told you.
