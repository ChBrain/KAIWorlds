# Process: Echo

## Owner
- **Project:** Oh, April
- **Position:** [The Inheritor](position_inheritor.md)
- **Place:** [The Room Where It Lands](place_room_where_it_lands.md)
- **Place:** [The Archive](place_archive.md)

## Initiates
Time has passed.
The original cast is gone - dead, dispersed, silent.
The [Inheritor](position_inheritor.md) finds [The Prank](piece_the_prank.md).
In [The Archive](place_archive.md). In [The Room Where It Lands](place_room_where_it_lands.md).
In a letter that kept being passed forward.
They did not go looking for it.
It found them.

## Direction
Toward understanding that has no one left to confirm it.
The [Inheritor](position_inheritor.md) reads [The Study](piece_the_study.md) without knowing that is what they are reading.
They stand where the [Target](position_target.md) stood.
The prank lands on them sideways.

## Lever
**Carrying force** - [The Prank](piece_the_prank.md)'s patience.
It was built well enough to wait.
It does not need the [Architect](position_architect.md) present to work.
It does not need anyone present who was there the first time.

**Resistance** - the absence of context.
The [Inheritor](position_inheritor.md) cannot ask what it meant.
Cannot verify what they think they understand.
Must decide alone whether to carry it or set it down.

## Echo
The [Inheritor](position_inheritor.md) knows something about the original cast
that none of the original cast knew about themselves.
It is a lonely thing to know.
The floor rises.
Or the [Inheritor](position_inheritor.md) walks away.
Both are permitted.
The prank does not mind.
It has already done its work.
