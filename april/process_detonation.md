- **Position:** [The Witness](position_witness.md)

## Initiates
The [Target](position_target.md) arrives.
[The Prank](piece_the_prank.md) is already there.
The room - or [The Street](place_street.md) - holds both simultaneously.
The [Witness](position_witness.md) is present.
Nothing is announced.

## Direction
Toward the involuntary thing.
What the [Target](position_target.md) does in the moment before they understand.
That is the truest data point [The Study](piece_the_study.md) was built to capture.

## Lever
**Carrying force** - [The Prank](piece_the_prank.md)'s precision.
When the [Architect](position_architect.md) built it: around one person, private, precise.
One [Target](position_target.md) in [The Room Where It Lands](place_room_where_it_lands.md).
When the [Designer](position_designer.md) built it: around a condition, public, simultaneous.
Every [Target](position_target.md) on [The Street](place_street.md) at once.
Either way it finds the gap.

**Resistance** - the [Target](position_target.md)'s self-image.
What they believe about themselves meeting what the prank knows about them.
The gap between those two things is where the detonation lives.

## Echo
The [Target](position_target.md) has revealed something they did not choose to reveal.
The [Witness](position_witness.md) has seen it.
The [Target](position_target.md) may not know either of these things yet.
[Revelation](process_revelation.md) inherits it.
