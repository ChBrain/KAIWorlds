# Position: The Witness

## Owner
- **Project:** Oh, April
- **Process:** [Detonation](process_detonation.md)
- **Process:** [Revelation](process_revelation.md)
- **Place:** [The Street](place_street.md)
- **Place:** [The Room Where It Lands](place_room_where_it_lands.md)

## Has
A clear line of sight.
Present before the [Target](position_target.md) arrives.
Not the one the prank was aimed at.
Therefore the one who sees it land cleanly
without being inside it.

## Orders
See it.
The position does not ask permission.
The [Witness](position_witness.md) was simply there.

## Loses
The option of comfortable silence.
They were certain they could watch
without being changed by what they saw.
At detonation they saw something
in the [Target](position_target.md) that the [Target](position_target.md) did not know was visible.
At revelation they saw whether it landed
as it was meant to.
Both pieces of knowledge
now belong to the [Witness](position_witness.md)
whether they wanted them or not.

## Drives
On the loss of comfortable silence - carries it.
May say nothing in the room
because the room made silence the only reasonable option.
May speak anyway.
Either way leaves with it.
Still thinks about it.
The thing they saw - they have not seen it again
in anyone.
