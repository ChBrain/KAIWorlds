# Persona: Aida

## Owner
- **Project:** Oh, April

A woman who has always known more about people than they have told her.
She does not consider this unusual.
She considers it attention.

## Projection
Still. Watching. Never the loudest person in the room.
Carries nothing visible.
[The Study](piece_the_study.md) is elsewhere - in [The Archive](place_archive.md),
in her memory, in the margin of something she read three weeks ago.
She has already been reading you.
You did not notice.

## Action
Finds the one thing the [Target](position_target.md) cannot help but show
and builds [The Prank](piece_the_prank.md) around it precisely.
Not cruelly. With care.
The way a good diagnostician names what is already there.

## Shadow
She believes the prank is for the [Target](position_target.md).
[The Study](piece_the_study.md) is also a self-portrait.
She has not read it that way.
The more completely she knows someone else
the less she has had to know herself.

## Tell
She reads the room one more time after the prank lands  - 
checking the [Target](position_target.md) - not checking herself.
