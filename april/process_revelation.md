# Process: Revelation

## Owner
- **Project:** Oh, April
- **Position:** [The Architect](position_architect.md)
- **Position:** [The Target](position_target.md)
- **Position:** [The Witness](position_witness.md)
- **Place:** [The Room Where It Lands](place_room_where_it_lands.md)

## Initiates
The detonation has run.
Someone says: *April Fool.*
Or nobody says it and everyone knows anyway.
The [Architect](position_architect.md) may or may not be present.
The room holds what happened regardless.

## Direction
Toward understanding.
Not forgiveness. Not resolution.
Understanding of what just happened  - 
and what it revealed about the one it happened to.
Sometimes what it revealed about the one who built it.

## Lever
**Carrying force** - the exposed gap.
The [Target](position_target.md) now knows what the prank knew.
Or begins to.
The [Architect](position_architect.md) now knows what the prank found  - 
which is not always what the [Architect](position_architect.md) expected to find.
The knowledge is irreversible for all of them.

**Resistance** - the [Target](position_target.md)'s right to not understand.
Some [Target](position_target.md)s refuse the revelation entirely.
Call it a joke. Laugh. Move on.
The prank does not mind.
It already did its work.

## Echo
Something is understood in the room that was not understood before.
The [Witness](position_witness.md) carries what was seen at detonation
and now whether it landed as it was meant to.
The [Architect](position_architect.md) carries what was confirmed - and what surprised them.
The [Target](position_target.md) carries what they choose to carry.
The room holds the remainder.
[Echo](process_echo.md) inherits it - years later, or never.
