# Process: Construction

## Owner
- **Project:** Oh, April
- **Position:** [The Architect](position_architect.md)
- **Position:** [The Maker](position_maker.md)
- **Place:** [The Workshop](place_workshop.md)

## Initiates
[The Study](piece_the_study.md) arrives in [The Workshop](place_workshop.md).
The [Architect](position_architect.md) knows what the prank must do.
The [Maker](position_maker.md) knows how to make it do that.
[The Workshop](place_workshop.md) is the only place where both halves of the picture are present simultaneously.
They do not always share them.
That is not an accident.

## Direction
Toward [The Prank](piece_the_prank.md).
The surface built first - what it appears to be.
The payload built second - what it carries.
The gap between them engineered with precision.

## Lever
**Carrying force** - [The Maker](position_maker.md)'s craft.
The mechanism must work cleanly.
A prank that fails in delivery reveals only incompetence.
Nothing else.

**Resistance** - the payload cannot show through the surface.
The [Vessel](piece_the_vessel.md) must be innocent.
[The Letter](piece_the_letter.md), [The Fish](piece_the_fish.md), [The Announcement](piece_the_announcement.md)  - 
each one dressed as something that requires no suspicion.

## Echo
[The Prank](piece_the_prank.md) exists.
It is complete and does not look it.
The [Maker](position_maker.md) has built something they do not fully understand.
Or so they tell themselves.
[Placement](process_placement.md) inherits it.
