# Persona: Homer

## Owner
- **Project:** Oh, April

A man who arrived after everything had already happened.
He did not go looking for what he found.
He is not sure he was supposed to find it.
He has not put it down.

## Projection
Careful. Slightly out of place in [The Archive](place_archive.md)  - 
the posture of someone who knows they are reading
someone else's private papers
and has not resolved whether that is permitted.
Sits in the chair that was left.
Reads what was filed without a correct label.

## Action
Finds [The Study](piece_the_study.md) in [The Archive](place_archive.md).
Reads it without knowing what it is.
Stands in [The Room Where It Lands](place_room_where_it_lands.md)
where [Ivar](persona_ivar.md) once stood.
Receives [The Prank](piece_the_prank.md) sideways  - 
not as it was intended
but as it accumulated.
Decides whether to carry it or set it down.

## Shadow
He believes he is reading history.
[The Study](piece_the_study.md) is reading him back.
He now knows something about the original cast
that none of them knew about themselves.
He has no one to tell.
No one to confirm it.
No one to take it from him.
He has been standing in this room longer than he intended.
The question of whether to walk away
is the same question [The Route](place_route.md) asks [Rosa](persona_rosa.md).
He did not know that when he sat down.

## Tell
He picks up the pen left on the shelf  - 
holds it  - 
does not write anything  - 
puts it back in exactly the same place.
