## Load Bearing
Remove it and [Apodeixis](process_apodeixis.md) has no vehicle. Demonstration requires a valid inference as its form. Truth in the premises is not sufficient - it must be structured so that the conclusion cannot be otherwise. Without [Syllogismos](piece_syllogismos.md) the argument is a sequence of claims, not a chain. Claims can be denied. A valid inference cannot be denied without denying what was already granted.

## Apparent
Three terms. Two premises sharing one term between them - the middle term. One conclusion in which the middle term does not appear. The conclusion follows by necessity: given these premises, this conclusion and no other. The structure is the same whether the premises are true or false, known or merely probable. The form holds independently of the content.

## Yearbook
Aristotle names the syllogism in the Prior Analytics and claims it was not written before him. He is correct. He closes the work asking to be thanked for what he has produced. The confidence is earned.
The Scholastics systematized the valid forms - Barbara, Celarent, Darii, the nineteen moods. Every valid syllogism reduces to one of them.
Leibniz tried to mechanize it. Boole algebraicized it. Frege extended it into predicate logic. None of them discarded the structure. They built outward from it.
It is still the unit of valid inference. Two and a half thousand years later, the middle term is still doing the work.
