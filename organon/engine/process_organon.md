The chain does not reverse. Where it breaks, it breaks. [Apodeixis](process_apodeixis.md) either fires or it does not.

## Lever

Remove any link and what remains is assertion wearing the shape of proof.

Resistance: the [Phainomenon Syllogismos](piece_phainomenon_syllogismos.md) - the argument that mimics the chain without running it. Present throughout. Indistinguishable from the outside until the [Elenchus](piece_elenchus.md) is applied. The instrument exists because this resistance exists.
