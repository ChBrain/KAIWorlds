# Place: The Trap

## Owner
- **Project:** Organon

## Shown
Looks like [The Lyceum](place_the_lyceum.md). The terms appear fixed. The propositions appear established. The chain appears to run. The [Sophist](position_sophist.md) is present - though not always visible as such. The [Questioner](position_questioner.md) and [Answerer](position_answerer.md) believe they are in a genuine exchange. The [Phainomenon Syllogismos](piece_phainomenon_syllogismos.md) is being built around them.

## Holds
- [Phainomenon Syllogismos](piece_phainomenon_syllogismos.md) - the structural content of this place. The Trap is built from it.

## Offers
The appearance of demonstration. The [Answerer](position_answerer.md) can be led to a conclusion that appears to follow by necessity. The [Questioner](position_questioner.md) can be made to believe they have produced a genuine argument. Both can leave the exchange believing knowledge was produced. What this place makes available: the effect of [Apodeixis](piece_apodeixis.md) without [Apodeixis](piece_apodeixis.md).

## Withheld
The break. The Trap does not reveal where the [Phainomenon Syllogismos](piece_phainomenon_syllogismos.md) fails. The equivocal term, the unestablished premise, the conclusion that does not follow - all are present and all are hidden inside the apparent structure of the chain. The break surfaces only under the [Elenchus](piece_elenchus.md). This place withholds the [Elenchus](piece_elenchus.md) by making it seem unnecessary - the argument appears to have already succeeded.
