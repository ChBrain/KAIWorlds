# Process: Horos

## Owner
- **Project:** Organon
- **Process:** [Organon](process_organon.md)

## Initiated by
[Organon](process_organon.md) has begun. A [Questioner](position_questioner.md) and [Answerer](position_answerer.md) are present. A claim is in dispute. The terms of the dispute have not yet been fixed. Before anything can be said that holds, what is being spoken about must be named precisely.

## Direction
Toward terms that are univocal - carrying one meaning, the same meaning, for both [Questioner](position_questioner.md) and [Answerer](position_answerer.md). Not toward agreement. Toward shared reference. The argument cannot begin until the things being argued about have been fixed.

## Lever
Carrying force: the [Kategoriai](piece_kategoriai.md) - the ten-fold classification of everything that exists. They provide the ground on which terms are placed. A term without a category cannot be fixed. A term that is fixed can be used in a [Protasis](process_protasis.md).

Resistance: equivocation - the same word carrying different meanings for [Questioner](position_questioner.md) and [Answerer](position_answerer.md) without either noticing. The chain that begins on an equivocal term does not break visibly. It produces a conclusion that appears to follow and does not. The [Phainomenon Syllogismos](piece_phainomenon_syllogismos.md) often begins here.

## Echo
[Protasis](process_protasis.md) inherits terms that are fixed. The [Questioner](position_questioner.md) and [Answerer](position_answerer.md) are now speaking about the same things. What changed: the argument has a ground. The equivocation, if present, is now load-bearing.
