## Place
*Where [Aristotle](persona_aristotle.md) is.*

## Load Bearing
Remove it and [Horos](process_horos.md) has no ground to fix terms on. A term that cannot be placed in a category cannot be made univocal. An argument that begins on an unfixed term does not break visibly - it produces conclusions that appear to follow and do not. The [Kategoriai](piece_kategoriai.md) are the precondition for the chain running at all.

## Apparent
Ten classes. Listed in order: substance, quantity, quality, relation, place, time, position, state, action, passion. Everything that can be said to exist falls into one. A term is placed here before it enters a proposition. The placement is not derived from prior argument. It is seen directly or it is not seen.

## Yearbook
Aristotle lists them in the Categories without deriving them - the first and foundational work of the Organon, the ground the whole instrument stands on.
The Scholastics inherited them as the structure of reality itself. Thomas Aquinas built his metaphysics on them. The list held as the grammar of philosophical argument for two thousand years.
Kant read them and built his own table of categories - twelve, derived from the structure of judgment, not listed from observation. His explicit target: Aristotle's method of compilation. He did not think listing was sufficient. He was right. He was also standing on the same ground.
The list is still the first thing named in introductory logic. It has never been replaced. It has only been argued with.
