# Position: Questioner
## The one who advances the argument.

## Owner
- **Project:** Organon
- **Scope:** Universal
- **Process:** [Horos](process_horos.md)
- **Process:** [Protasis](process_protasis.md)
- **Process:** [Syllogismos](process_syllogismos.md)

## Has
The thesis to be established. The [Questioner](position_questioner.md) holds a conclusion before the argument begins and constructs the path toward it. The path runs through premises the [Answerer](position_answerer.md) cannot reasonably deny. What this position brings is not the conclusion - the conclusion is what it is building toward. What it brings is the ability to see which premises, once conceded, make the conclusion unavoidable.

## Orders
Ask. The position does not assert - it leads. Every move is a question. The [Answerer](position_answerer.md) must concede or deny. The chain advances on concession.

## Loses
The advantage of knowing. The position arrived certain that holding the destination was the advantage - that seeing the conclusion in advance was the thing that made the chain possible. The chain reveals that the knowledge of the destination is the constraint: it cannot be used directly. What the [Questioner](position_questioner.md) was certain gave it the upper hand is the thing it must conceal in order to build the chain at all.

## Drives
On the loss - asks the next question. The position advances through what the [Answerer](position_answerer.md) has already granted. It does not argue. It accumulates concessions until the conclusion follows by necessity and cannot be denied without contradicting what has already been given.
