# Process: Protasis

## Owner
- **Project:** Organon
- **Process:** [Organon](process_organon.md)

## Initiated by
[Horos](process_horos.md) has run. The terms are fixed. Two terms can now be placed in relation. The [Questioner](position_questioner.md) advances a proposition - a claim that can be affirmed or denied. The [Answerer](position_answerer.md) faces it.

## Direction
Toward propositions that can serve as premises - claims that are either universal or particular, affirmative or negative, and can therefore function as the material of a [Syllogismos](process_syllogismos.md). Not toward truth yet. Toward propositions with the right structure to be tested.

## Lever
Carrying force: the proposition itself - two terms in relation, stated in a form that admits of being true or false. The [Questioner](position_questioner.md) selects propositions the [Answerer](position_answerer.md) is likely to concede. The concession is the material. What the [Answerer](position_answerer.md) grants here is what the [Syllogismos](process_syllogismos.md) will run on.

Resistance: the [Answerer](position_answerer.md)'s refusal. A proposition that is denied cannot serve as a premise. The [Questioner](position_questioner.md) must find the path of concession - the propositions that are true and that the [Answerer](position_answerer.md) cannot reasonably deny. If no such path exists, [Aporia](piece_aporia.md) is present before [Syllogismos](process_syllogismos.md) begins.

## Echo
[Syllogismos](process_syllogismos.md) inherits premises that have been conceded. What changed: the [Answerer](position_answerer.md) has committed. The conclusion is now constrained by what they have already granted. They do not yet know what they have granted.
