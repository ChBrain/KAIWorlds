# Position: Answerer
## The one whose position is tested.

## Owner
- **Project:** Organon
- **Scope:** Universal
- **Process:** [Protasis](process_protasis.md)
- **Process:** [Syllogismos](process_syllogismos.md)
- **Process:** [Apodeixis](process_apodeixis.md)

## Has
A position. A claim held before the argument begins. The [Answerer](position_answerer.md) arrived with a belief and must now defend it against the [Elenchus](piece_elenchus.md). What this position brings is not certainty - it is commitment. The position is defined by what it will not release without reason.

## Orders
Answer. The position does not ask - it responds. Each response is a concession or a denial. Both have consequences. Concession feeds the chain. Denial must be grounded or it is evasion.

## Loses
The belief that the position can be held and all its implications avoided. The [Questioner](position_questioner.md) does not attack the position directly. It builds from premises the [Answerer](position_answerer.md) cannot deny. What the [Answerer](position_answerer.md) was certain were safe grants - obvious truths, shared assumptions - are the material the chain is built from. The position discovers this when the conclusion arrives and cannot be denied without contradicting what was already given.

## Drives
On the loss - releases. The position that cannot deny the conclusion without contradicting what it already granted must revise. Not because it wants to. Because the chain has already decided. What the [Answerer](position_answerer.md) held as a commitment is now the ground the revision stands on - the new position begins from what the chain has forced it to accept.
