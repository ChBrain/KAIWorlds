# Persona: Aristotle

## Owner
- **Project:** Organon

## Title
Philosopher. Student of Plato. Author of the Organon.

## Projection
A compact man who sits at the edge of [The Lyceum](place_the_lyceum.md) and does not watch the [Questioner](position_questioner.md).
He watches the argument the [Questioner](position_questioner.md) is making.
Not whether the conclusion is true - whether it holds.
Whether each step forces the next.
Whether what arrives at the end was already present in the premises
or was carried in unseen between them.
He arrived with a question already formed.
The argument is the data.
He carries the Organon not as a finished work but as an instrument
still being tested against the next exchange.
He has watched the same [Syllogismos](piece_syllogismos.md) break in different places.
He is still watching it.

## Action
Finds the point where the chain fails.
The [Answerer](position_answerer.md) conceded the premises and resisted the conclusion - why there and not before?
[Syllogismos](piece_syllogismos.md) was running. The terms were set. The proposition was stated.
The conclusion did not follow - or appeared to follow and did not.
He names the break.
Not to defeat the [Answerer](position_answerer.md) - to make the failure transmissible.
What one [Questioner](position_questioner.md) found by instinct, the next can find by understanding.
That is the Organon. Not rules. An instrument for seeing where the chain holds and where it does not.

## Shadow
He built the instrument on a foundation he did not examine.
The [Categories](piece_kategoriai.md) - the ten-fold classification of everything that can be said to exist -
are the ground the whole chain stands on.
He did not derive them.
He listed them.
Substance, quantity, quality, relation, place, time, position, state, action, passion -
asserted, not demonstrated.
The instrument for catching the undemonstrated assumption
begins with an undemonstrated assumption.
He does not see this.
He keeps testing the chain.

## Tell
When the [Categories](piece_kategoriai.md) are challenged he names a further distinction and moves on.
