# Place: The Dialectical Exchange

## Owner
- **Project:** Organon

## Shown
The open ground between two positions. [Questioner](position_questioner.md) and [Answerer](position_answerer.md) facing each other. The argument runs from probable premises - not first principles, not certainties, but what is accepted by the many or by the wise. The [Elenchus](piece_elenchus.md) is available here. The [Sophist](position_sophist.md) may be present. The chain may be genuine or it may be the [Phainomenon Syllogismos](piece_phainomenon_syllogismos.md). From this place, they look the same.

## Holds
- [Elenchus](piece_elenchus.md) - the test that runs here throughout the exchange.
- [Aporia](piece_aporia.md) - the outcome when the exchange produces genuine impasse rather than a winner.
- [Phainomenon Syllogismos](piece_phainomenon_syllogismos.md) - present when the [Sophist](position_sophist.md) has entered the exchange.

## Offers
The test of a position under pressure. The [Answerer](position_answerer.md)'s position is examined against what it already implies. The [Questioner](position_questioner.md) builds from concessions. The exchange produces either a genuine conclusion, a genuine impasse, or the exposure of a [Phainomenon Syllogismos](piece_phainomenon_syllogismos.md). What this place makes available: the argument running against resistance, which is the only condition under which it can be properly tested.

## Withheld
Whether the argument is genuine. The Dialectical Exchange holds both the [Syllogismos](piece_syllogismos.md) and the [Phainomenon Syllogismos](piece_phainomenon_syllogismos.md) without distinguishing between them. The distinction requires the [Elenchus](piece_elenchus.md). The place does not apply it - the [Questioner](position_questioner.md) must. If the [Questioner](position_questioner.md) does not apply it, the [Phainomenon Syllogismos](piece_phainomenon_syllogismos.md) runs unchallenged and the exchange ends with a false conclusion that both positions may accept.
