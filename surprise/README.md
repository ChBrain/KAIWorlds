# Surprise

*The kitchen. One hour before the party.*

A world about what people do when the thing they owe arrives due all at once. Five personas, two places, one piece, five processes, five positions. The Forgetter is at the counter. The Fixer is already working. The Rememberer knew yesterday. The Witness is in the doorway - neither room, both rooms. The Birthday Person is in the Hallway with their coat still on.

The cake exists. It is slightly uneven on the left side. There is one candle too many. Nobody corrected this.

The world does not resolve. The party arrives.

---

**How to use**

1. Create a new AI project - Claude, Grok, Copilot, and Gemini all work
2. Upload all `.md` files from this folder
3. Start a conversation - bring what you have

The first scene will go somewhere the files did not predict.
