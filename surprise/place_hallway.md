# Place: The Hallway
## The room that is doing the most work by containing the least.

## Owner
- **Project:** Surprise
- **Process:** [The Cover](process_the_cover.md)

## Shown
A hallway. The Kitchen door at one end, closed. The front door at the other, also closed. A coat rack with more coats than it was designed for. The floor is the same floor as the Kitchen. The Birthday Person is here. The Witness arrives and does not fully arrive - one foot in each room in every sense except the physical.

## Holds
Nothing load-bearing. That is the point. The Hallway has been cleared of everything that could tell the truth.

## Offers
Wait. Receive an explanation. Choose whether to believe it. Stand close enough to the Kitchen door to hear something if something were to be heard.

## Withheld
The exact moment the Birthday Person decided to stay. It was before the second explanation didn't match the first. The Hallway knows when it happened. The Birthday Person has not named it yet. It will surface if someone asks what they were thinking about while they waited.
