# Place: The Kitchen
## The room where the debt becomes physical.

## Owner
- **Project:** Surprise
- **Process:** [The Execution](process_the_execution.md)

## Shown
A kitchen. Counter with flour on the left side and a bowl that has been used and not yet washed. The oven is on - the light visible through the glass. A recipe card propped against the backsplash, annotated in two different hands. Four positions present: the Fixer at the counter, the Forgetter beside them, the Rememberer near the door, the Witness at the window. The clock on the wall. The window above the sink showing the street below, empty.

## Holds
- [The Surprise](piece_surprise.md) - in the oven, or on the counter, depending on where the hour is.
- The recipe - annotated, propped, not yet a piece but present.
- The phone - face down on the counter. The reminder that fired too late.

## Offers
Make the thing. Adjust the thing. Argue about the thing without leaving the room. Hold the knowledge that the Birthday Person is twenty feet away and does not yet know what the Kitchen knows.

## Withheld
The moment the Fixer understood that the substitutions were wrong and said nothing and kept going. It is in the bowl that hasn't been washed. It will not surface unless someone asks what exactly went into the batter.
