# Position: The Fixer
## The one who reduces the problem to what is actually possible.

## Owner
- **Project:** Surprise
- **Process:** [The Plan](process_the_plan.md)
- **Process:** [The Execution](process_the_execution.md)

## Has
The ability to begin before the picture is complete.
The Fixer does not need to know how it ends to know what to do next.

## Orders
Make the thing. Not the planned thing. A thing.

## Loses
The assumption that a good plan survives contact with the Forgetter.

## Drives
Adjusts without announcing the adjustment.
The adjustment is the work.
Announcing it would be a different position entirely.
