## Owner

- **Position:** [The Fixer](position_fixer.md)

## Initiated by

## Direction

Not the planned version.
A version. The Fixer does not mourn the planned version.
That is what makes the Fixer the Fixer.

**Resistance** - the Forgetter holds a position in [The Execution](process_the_execution.md) that the Plan has not budgeted for.
The Plan assumed fewer cooks.

## Echo

It is not a good plan.
It is the only plan.
[The Execution](process_the_execution.md) and [The Cover](process_the_cover.md) begin simultaneously.
