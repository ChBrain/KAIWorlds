## Place

## Load Bearing
Without it the five processes are kitchen chaos with a deadline.
The Realization has nothing to almost lose.
The Plan has nothing to save.
The Execution has nothing to make physical.
The Cover has nothing to protect.
The Reveal has nothing to land.
Remove it and the debt has no currency.
The people in the kitchen are just busy.
They are not the same thing.

## Apparent
A cake. Not the cake anyone planned. Slightly uneven on the left side. The frosting applied with more confidence than skill. One candle too many - someone miscounted and nobody corrected it. Still warm.

## Yearbook
It has never existed before today.
That is the problem.
That is also, eventually, the point.
It was meant to exist yesterday.
Then this morning.
Then an hour ago.
Each time the intention was real.
Each time something else was also real.
It exists now because the deadline arrived before the excuses did.
That is not nothing.
