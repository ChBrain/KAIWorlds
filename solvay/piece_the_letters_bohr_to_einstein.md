# Piece: The Letters — Bohr to Einstein
## The framework, explained again, more precisely than before.

## Owner
**Project:** Solvay

**Persona:** Bohr

---

## Place
[Einstein's Study](place_princeton_study.md), Princeton.
The desk. Half-answered.

## Load Bearing
Without them Einstein argues into silence.
The thought experiments travel one way
and meet no resistance from inside the framework.
The letters are what makes the argument continuous.
Bohr explaining is not capitulation.
It is the only form of respect
that matches what Einstein is doing.

## Apparent
Careful. Dense.
Each letter explains the framework again.
More precisely than the last.
As though precision is what is missing.
Precision is not what is missing.
Bohr knows this.
He sends the letter anyway.

## Yearbook
First letter, 1920.
Last letter: never sent.
The unanswered letter was written April 1955.
Einstein died before it arrived.
It is [piece_the_unanswered_letter.md](piece_the_unanswered_letter.md).
Einstein's copies are on his desk,
half-answered,
the care in the replies the argument continuing.
