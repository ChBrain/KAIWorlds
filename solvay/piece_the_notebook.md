# Piece: The Notebook
## The thing that holds what cannot be said to either of them.

## Owner
**Project:** Solvay

**Persona:** Ehrenfest

---

## Place
[Ehrenfest's Study](place_leiden_study.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it Ehrenfest has no private interior.
He is only ever the hinge between the other two.
With it there is a place where the weight goes —
not down, not away, but into the notebook,
which is also not away.
The notebook travels with him.
It does not empty.

## Apparent
Small. Brown cover. Well used.
The pen lives inside the cover, clipped.
He uncaps it often.
He does not always write.

## Yearbook
Brussels, 1927. Uncapped. Capped.
The argument between Einstein and Bohr named in full
on a page that was never sent to either of them.
Solvay, 1930. Three pages.
The last entry is undated.
It is not finished.
