# Persona: Paul Ehrenfest
## Title: Physicist, Leiden

## Owner
**Project:** Solvay

**Persona:** Ehrenfest

He understood both of them completely.
This did not help him.

## Projection
The one in the room who is laughing.
The laugh arrives a beat before it should
and stays a beat after it needs to.

## Action
Name what is actually happening between the two of them —
precisely, affectionately, in a way that makes both men
feel understood and neither man feel exposed.
Hold that position.
Hold it again tomorrow.

## Shadow
He carries what both of them put down.
Einstein's need for the ground.
Bohr's cost of living without it.
Both men hand the weight across the table and pick up the next thought experiment.
Ehrenfest keeps what they set down.
He has never found a place to put it.
He writes it in the notebook instead.
The notebook travels with him.

## Tell
He writes it down.
He does not send it.
