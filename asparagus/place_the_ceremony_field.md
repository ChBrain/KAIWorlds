## Holds

- [piece_the_photograph](piece_the_photograph.md): produced here each season.

## Offers
- The opening of the season - officially.
- The representation: a face, a crown, a regional claim. Available to whoever the ceremony names.
- The photograph's composition: fixed. Available to whoever is inside the frame.

## Withheld
- The morning. The ceremony is at midday or late morning. The stalks in the photograph were cut at dawn. The field that is visible in the background was full of workers four hours ago. This is not hidden. It is simply before the frame opens.
