# Persona: Ingrid Hartmann
## Title: [Grower](position_grower.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** The Asparagus Season

A woman in her early sixties. Her family has grown asparagus in Hesse for three generations. She inherited the field. She inherited the knowledge. She has not examined what else she inherited.

## Projection
Standing at the edge of [the field](place_the_asparagus_field.md) in early April, reading the ground. Farming clothes because there is work. The regional newspaper has photographed her at the opening ceremony twice. Both times she was standing slightly to the left of where the frame was aimed.

## Action
Read the ground. Not the forecast, not the price — the texture of the soil at blade-depth, the drainage, the color. Then tell whoever is asking what the field is saying, and what it is not saying, which is different.

## Shadow
[Marta Kowalczyk](persona_marta_kowalczyk.md), who has returned to Ingrid's farm for eleven seasons, knows row seven better than Ingrid does. Ingrid knows this. She has never said it aloud. The position of Grower was inherited — her grandfather's, then her father's, then hers — and the knowledge that built it is being redistributed by the labor she contracts without anyone deciding this should happen. She cannot examine it without examining what she inherited and what she holds now and whether those are the same thing.

## Tell
She gives you the yield figures for the farm. Not the row breakdown.
