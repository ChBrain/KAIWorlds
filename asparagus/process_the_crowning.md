# Process: The Crowning
## The ceremony that replaced its reason.

## Owner
- **Project:** The Asparagus Season
- **Position:** [Asparagus Queen](position_asparagus_queen.md)

## Initiated by
The season opens. A young woman — from a farming family, from the agricultural community, from somewhere inside the regional claim — is elected or selected. She stands before a minister-president or a mayor or a regional dignitary. The [crown](piece_the_crown.md) exists. The ceremony has been running long enough to feel necessary.

## Direction
Toward the opening of the season — officially. The [Asparagus Queen](position_asparagus_queen.md) represents the region at markets, festivals, trade fairs. For eight to ten weeks. Until June 24th.

## Lever
**Carrying force:** the tradition. Old enough to feel inevitable. Recent enough that photographs exist of the first ones.
**Resistance:** the ceremony names a face for the season. The face is from the farming community. The [Harvest Helper](position_harvest_helper.md) who made the season possible is not from the farming community. The ceremony is not dishonest. It does not know what it is not showing.

## Echo
The season is open. The region has a queen. [process_the_cut](process_the_cut.md) has been running since the ground decided — with or without the ceremony. The ceremony knows this. It continues regardless.
