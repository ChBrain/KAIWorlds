# Persona: Marta Kowalczyk
## Title: [Harvest Helper](position_harvest_helper.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** The Asparagus Season

A woman in her mid-forties from a village outside Wrocław. She has come to the same farm in Hesse for eleven seasons. She knows this farm. The world does not follow her onto the coach.

## Projection
Bent at the waist at dawn, knife at ankle-height, moving across [the field](place_the_asparagus_field.md). She knows where row seven drains slow and where row twelve comes up first. She is not in [the photograph](piece_the_photograph.md).

## Action
Move over the ground slowly, reading it. Cut the stalk before light reaches it. Straighten. Move to the next. Do this from dawn to midday. Know which rows are ready before the [Grower](position_grower.md) checks.

## Shadow
She has come to believe that the knowledge is sufficient — that to understand this field this specifically, to return year after year and find it still legible, is its own form of belonging. She believes this because the alternative is that eleven seasons of physical knowledge and geographical loyalty add up to a contract position in a photograph she's not in. She has not examined the belief. She books the coach for next April.

## Tell
She names the rows when she talks about the farm. She does not say her name first.
