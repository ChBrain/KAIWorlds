# Process: The End
## June 24th.

## Owner
- **Project:** The Asparagus Season

## Initiated by
The calendar reaches June 24th. Not the ground temperature. Not the plant's condition. Not the market price. The calendar. Johannistag — the feast of John the Baptist. The agronomic reason is real: the plants need the summer to rebuild energy. The theological reason is almost entirely forgotten. The date is not.

## Direction
Toward the last cut. The [Harvest Helper](position_harvest_helper.md) straightens for the last time. The lorry moves. The [Asparagus Queen](position_asparagus_queen.md)'s term ends. The [Grower](position_grower.md) lets the ferns grow tall. Every restaurant crosses asparagus off the menu on the same day.

## Lever
**Carrying force:** the date. Fixed. Inarguable. Known by every farmer, every restaurant, every person who has bought asparagus in Germany.
**Resistance:** the reason for the date has been forgotten. The saint is gone. The date remains. The date needs no reason now. It is the reason. Remove the saint and nothing changes. Remove the date — and the season has no wall to press against.

## Echo
The season is over. The [Harvest Helper](position_harvest_helper.md) leaves. The [Asparagus Queen](position_asparagus_queen.md)'s [crown](piece_the_crown.md) is returned. The field grows uncut through summer. The reason is gone. The date survives.
