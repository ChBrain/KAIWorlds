# Piece: The Photograph
## What the season looks like from outside.

## Owner
- **Project:** The Asparagus Season

## Place
Everywhere the season is represented publicly. The ceremony. The festival. The market stall.

## Load Bearing
Without it the gap between who is named and who is present has no form. [process_the_crowning](process_the_crowning.md) and [process_the_cut](process_the_cut.md) run simultaneously. The photograph shows one of them. Remove it and the concealment has no surface.

## Apparent
A photograph. Regional minister-president. [Asparagus Queen](position_asparagus_queen.md) in her crown. First stalks of the season held upright, white, unblemished. The field may be visible in the background. The [Harvest Helpers](position_harvest_helper.md) who cut those stalks this morning are not in it.

## Yearbook
The photograph appears each year on the same day the season opens. The faces change - new minister-president, new queen, new stalks. The composition does not change. The [Harvest Helper](position_harvest_helper.md) is not in the composition. This has been true since the tradition began. It is not a decision anyone made. It is the shape of the frame.
