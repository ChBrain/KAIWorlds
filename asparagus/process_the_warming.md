# Process: The Warming
## The ground decides.

## Owner
- **Project:** The Asparagus Season

## Initiated by
The winter has ended - or ended enough. The soil temperature at 10cm depth reaches the threshold the plant requires. No ceremony has authorized this. No calendar has been consulted. The ground decides.

## Direction
Toward the first stalk. Underground. The [Grower](position_grower.md) watches the field - not the calendar. The calendar is approximate. The ground is exact.

## Lever
**Carrying force:** the plant, the accumulated winter, the slow heat moving down through sandy loam.
**Resistance:** every other force that wants to set the date - the market, the ceremony, the contract that says the [Harvest Helper](position_harvest_helper.md) arrives on a fixed Monday. The ground does not negotiate. The plant does not wait for agreement. The stalk breaks the surface on the day the stalk breaks the surface.

## Echo
The field is ready. The [Grower](position_grower.md) knows. Nobody else needs to know yet. [process_the_cut](process_the_cut.md) can begin.
