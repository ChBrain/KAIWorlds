# Stack

## Owner
Project: Asparagus

## Context
The German asparagus season. Opens in April when the ground is ready. Ends June 24th - the feast day of St. John the Baptist, who is no longer the reason. The date outlived its reason. The ceremony replaced its origin. Five personas. None of them know the others are in it.

The cut and the crowning run simultaneously without mutual acknowledgment. This is the world's central mechanism.

**Ingrid Hartmann** - Grower. Inherited knowledge being redistributed without anyone deciding.
