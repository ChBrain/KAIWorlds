# Position: Harvest Helper
## The knowledge without a name.

## Owner
- **Project:** The Asparagus Season
- **Process:** [process_the_cut](process_the_cut.md)

## Has
The knife. The body bent at the waist. The ability to read the slight rise in the soil above a ready stalk. This knowledge is physical. It arrived through repetition. It lives in the hands and the back. It does not appear in documentation.

## Orders
Cut the stalk before it reaches light.

## Loses
The legibility. They were certain the knowledge - specific, accumulated, irreplaceable - made them visible to the world that benefited from it. The knowledge is real. The world that benefits from it does not look at them. The photographs are taken elsewhere.

## Drives
On the loss of legibility - returns to the field the following season anyway. The same farm, in some cases. The same rows. The knowledge does not require recognition to persist.
