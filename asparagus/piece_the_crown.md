# Piece: The Crown
## The object the season is given.

## Owner
- **Project:** The Asparagus Season

## Place
Where the [Asparagus Queen](position_asparagus_queen.md) is. Between seasons: in a cabinet at the regional agricultural association. Brought out next April.

## Load Bearing
Without it [process_the_crowning](process_the_crowning.md) has no object. The ceremony is gesture without weight. The [Asparagus Queen](position_asparagus_queen.md) has a title but no transfer of authority has occurred. The season has been opened by announcement, not by form.

## Apparent
A crown. Not elaborate. Regional variations exist - some floral, some formal, some with asparagus worked into the design. Worn once at the opening ceremony. Worn at festivals and trade fairs throughout the season. Returned at the end. It is not the same crown between queens. The tradition persists. The object is new each time.

## Yearbook
The tradition has been running for decades. Photographs exist of the first ones. The crown in those photographs is different from the one in use today. The crown that exists today will not be used by the next queen. Between seasons it sits in a cabinet at the regional agricultural association. It is brought out next April. The ceremony is not interrupted by this. The crown is the form. The form is what holds.
