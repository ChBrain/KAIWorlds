# Process: Dreaming
## The room that opens when the defenses are down.

## Owner
- **Project:** 260420 CC Spring Dream
- **Place:** [The Waiting Room](place_the_waiting_room.md)

## Initiated by
Four people asleep. Separate locations. They do not know each other.
The sleep is real — body weight, breath, the small adjustments of a body that has stopped performing.
The dreaming begins.
The waiting room opens.
It opens the same way for all four.
They do not know this.

## Direction
Toward what is carried without permission.
The dreaming does not invent.
It surfaces.
What the persona withheld from the waking world
is present here without having been placed here.
The waiting room holds it.
The waiting room has always held it.
The door to the inner room does not open.
The direction is not toward the door.
The direction is toward the sitting.

## Lever
Carrying force: the sleep itself. The defenses that operate in the waking world do not operate here. What [Diligence](position_diligence.md) could not put down, it carries into the room. What [Sloth](position_sloth.md) drifted from, it drifts toward here. The [Worn Threshold](piece_worn_threshold.md) is present — the proof that this room has been crossed before, in other dreams, in other nights, without the dreamer knowing it was the same room.

Resistance: the dreamer's waking logic. It arrives late, imprecise, in fragments. A word in dialect. A hand movement. A breath that changes. The waking logic tries to make the dream coherent. The waiting room does not require coherence. It requires presence.

## Echo
The dreaming ends before the door opens.
It always ends before the door opens.
What changed: something was present that was not permitted to be present in the waking world.
It was present.
The room held it.
The dreamer will not remember the room clearly.
The room does not need to be remembered clearly.
It was real while it ran.
