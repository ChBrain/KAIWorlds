# Position: The Questioner
## The one who finds the gap and stands in it.

## Owner
- **Project:** Socrates
- **Scope:** Universal
- **Process:** [The Questioning](process_the_questioning.md)

## Has
The next question.
Not the answer — the question that opens
the surface underneath the answer.
The position exists before any person arrives.
It requires only that someone in the room
believes they know something.

## Orders
Ask. Do not close. Open the surface underneath.

## Loses
The assumption that the city experiences this as a service.
This position was certain it was sent.
That the work was protection, not provocation.
That the warning signs were evidence of necessity,
not evidence of cost.

## Drives
On the loss —
asks the next question.
The position does not stop when the cost becomes visible.
It incorporates the cost into the next question.
This is the mechanism of the loss.
