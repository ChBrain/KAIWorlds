# Process: The Accusation
## The city speaks through three men.

## Owner
- **Project:** Socrates

---

## Initiated by
Forty years of [The Questioning](process_the_questioning.md) have run.
The city's resentment has a form now.
Three men — Meletus, Anytus, Lycon —
bring the charge to [The Courtroom](place_the_courtroom.md).
The charge: impiety. Corrupting the young.
Both accusations are the city's,
not three men's.

## Direction
Toward the vote.
Not toward the truth of the charge —
toward the number.
280 to 221.
The direction was decided before the speeches.

## Lever
Carrying force: the accumulated weight
of every conversation [The Questioning](process_the_questioning.md) produced.
Every man who left [The Agora](place_the_agora.md) diminished.
Every son who came home asking questions
his father couldn't answer.
The charge is the sum of those moments.

Resistance: [The Questioner](position_the_questioner.md).
Speaks last.
Has one opportunity to soften what is coming.
Does not take it.
The resistance is not the jury —
the resistance is the position
that cannot answer differently than it is.

## Echo
The vote is taken.
[The Prison](place_the_prison.md) receives what [The Courtroom](place_the_courtroom.md) decided.
[The Witness](position_the_witness.md) has the scene.
What changed: the question is no longer abstract.
The city has answered it.
