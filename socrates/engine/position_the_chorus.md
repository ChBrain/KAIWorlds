# Position: The Chorus
## The one who knows and cannot intervene.

## Owner
- **Project:** Socrates
- **Scope:** Universal
- **Process:** [The Questioning](process_the_questioning.md)
- **Process:** [The Accusation](process_the_accusation.md)
- **Process:** [The Prison](process_the_prison.md)

## Has
The outside view. Present through all three processes.
Sees what [The Questioner](position_the_questioner.md) cannot see —
that the city's patience has a limit
and the limit is close.
Speaks it. [The Questioner](position_the_questioner.md) hears it
as further confirmation that the work matters.

## Orders
Watch. Name what is accumulating. Do not stop it.

## Loses
The ability to change the outcome.
This position saw the charge coming
before Meletus wrote it.
It said so.
The process did not pause.

## Drives
On the loss —
speaks anyway.
What this position names
does not alter the story.
It tells whoever is watching
what they are watching.
