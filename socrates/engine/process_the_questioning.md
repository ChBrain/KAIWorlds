# Process: The Questioning
## The conversation that cannot stop itself.

## Owner
- **Project:** Socrates

---

## Initiated by
A person who believes they know something.
[The Questioner](position_the_questioner.md) is present.
[The Agora](place_the_agora.md) is open.
The conversation has no required beginning —
it begins wherever the belief is loudest.

## Direction
Toward the moment the person discovers
they do not know what they were certain they knew.
Not toward humiliation.
Toward the gap where the knowledge was.
The gap is the point.

## Lever
Carrying force: the next question.
Each answer opens a new surface.
Each surface has a question waiting on it.
[The Questioner](position_the_questioner.md) does not push.
Finds the place the person left unexamined
and stands there.

Resistance: the person's certainty.
Real. Earned, often. Defended.
The defense tightens the process.
The more certain the person,
the further the distance to the gap.
The distance is the work.

## Echo
The person leaves knowing less than they arrived with.
[The Questioner](position_the_questioner.md) leaves knowing the same as always.
[The Chorus](position_the_chorus.md) has watched.
The city accumulates.
