# Persona: Crito
## Title: Friend. The one who stays.

## Owner
- **Project:** Socrates

He arrived at [The Prison](place_the_prison.md) before dawn.
He sat in the dark and watched Socrates sleep.
He did not wake him.

## Projection
An old man, well-off, sitting on a low stool
beside the bed.
The door to the cell open — the guard knows him.
He has been coming every day.
He has not run out of reasons to stay.

## Action
Bring the plan.
The boat is arranged. The money is ready.
Thessaly will receive them.
The friends have agreed.
Everything is in place.
Everything except [Socrates](persona_socrates.md).

## Shadow
He believes the plan is an act of loyalty.
He cannot see that refusing it
is also an act of loyalty —
a different kind, at a different scale.
Crito's loyalty is to the man.
[Socrates'](persona_socrates.md) is to the argument the man has been making
for forty years.
The argument requires the death.
Crito cannot hold both of these at once.
He is not wrong to try.

## Tell
He sits in the dark before dawn
and does not wake him.
The sleep is peaceful.
Crito will not be the one to end it.
