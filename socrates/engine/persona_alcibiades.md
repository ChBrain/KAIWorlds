# Persona: Alcibiades
## Title: Student. General. The name no one says at the trial.

## Owner
- **Project:** Socrates

He is not at the trial.
He is already dead — killed in exile, seven years before.
He is present anyway.
The charge of corrupting the young
has his face on it.

## Projection
A man remembered in the room he no longer occupies.
The seat in [The Agora](place_the_agora.md) where he used to sit
still organizes the space around it.
He listened longer than anyone
and understood more
and did less with it than anyone.
Everyone present knew this.
He knew it first.

## Action
Use what the philosophy gave him
for what the philosophy said not to use it for.
Power. Persuasion. The advantage in the room.
He took the questioning and made it a tool.
[Socrates](persona_socrates.md) taught him to find the gap
in other people's certainty.
He used the gap.

## Shadow
He loved Socrates.
Not the ideas — the man.
He said so, at a dinner, drunk,
in front of everyone.
He said: I know what I should do
and I cannot make myself do it
and he is the only person
who makes me feel the cost of that.
He said it and then did everything he had named
as the thing he should not do.

## Tell
He shows up in Plato's Symposium —
late, drunk, uninvited —
and gives the truest account of Socrates
in the entire corpus.
He was not supposed to be there.
He could not stay away.
