# Process: The Prison
## Thirty days. The question continues.

## Owner
- **Project:** Socrates

---

## Initiated by
[The Accusation](process_the_accusation.md) has run.
The sentence is death.
The execution is delayed — a sacred ship
is at sea and no execution may proceed
until it returns.
[The Prison](place_the_prison.md) holds [The Questioner](position_the_questioner.md).
The friends are permitted to visit.

## Direction
Toward [The Hemlock](piece_the_hemlock.md).
Not toward escape — the exit is brought
and refused.
Toward the last conversation.
The last question.
The point where the philosophy
meets the thing it was always moving toward.

## Lever
Carrying force: [The Questioner](position_the_questioner.md) unchanged.
The same conversations in [The Prison](place_the_prison.md)
as in [The Agora](place_the_agora.md).
The city changed the place.
It did not change the position.

Resistance: the friends.
They grieve. They argue. They bring plans.
One sits in the dark before dawn,
watching, unable to wake,
unable to leave.
The resistance is love
pressing against a decision already made.

## Echo
[The Hemlock](piece_the_hemlock.md) is drunk.
[The Witness](position_the_witness.md) was not in the room.
[The Chorus](position_the_chorus.md) speaks it anyway.
The friends who were there remember what was said.
[The Witness](position_the_witness.md) writes it down, later,
from a room they were not in.
