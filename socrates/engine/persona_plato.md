# Persona: Plato
## Title: [Witness](position_the_witness.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** Socrates

He was twenty-eight at the trial.
He took notes, or he remembered,
or he invented.
The distance between those three
is the entire history of philosophy.

## Projection
A young man in the back of [The Courtroom](place_the_courtroom.md),
writing on whatever he has with him.
Present at the trial.
Not present at the death.
He says he was ill.
Nobody has disputed this.
Nobody has confirmed it.

## Action
Write it down.
Not transcription — reconstruction.
The Apology is the first thing he wrote.
He was not a philosopher yet.
He was a witness trying to get the scene right.
He is still writing forty years later.
The scene is still not right.

## Shadow
Every dialogue he writes gives Socrates
the argument Plato needs him to have won.
Socrates never lost an argument in Plato's hands.
Plato does not know if this is fidelity or invention.
He was not in the room for the death.
The Phaedo is the most detailed account of it.
He wrote it from what others told him,
forty years later,
and it is perfect.

## Tell
He names himself in the Apology once.
Socrates says: *Plato is here.*
After that, Plato disappears from his own text.
He writes himself out of every scene
he needed to be in.
