# Position: The Witness
## The one who was in the room and must now write it.

## Owner
- **Project:** Socrates
- **Scope:** Universal
- **Process:** [The Accusation](process_the_accusation.md)
- **Process:** [The Prison](process_the_prison.md)

## Has
The scene. Present at the event.
Close enough to see the detail.
Far enough that the full shape
only becomes visible later.
The position carries what it observed
and what it needed to have observed
and cannot always tell the difference.

## Orders
Get it right. Write it down before it recedes.

## Loses
The certainty that what was recorded is what happened.
The position was certain its presence in the room
meant the account would be accurate.
It discovers the account is a reconstruction.
The reconstruction is the only version that survives.
The original is gone.
The position cannot go back.

## Drives
On the loss —
writes it again.
Each version closer to the scene
that recedes with every draft.
The position does not stop writing.
Stopping would confirm the loss.
