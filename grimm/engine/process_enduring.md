# Process: Enduring

## Owner
- **Project:** Grimm

## Initiated by

A configuration has been set. Multiple positions ([Hearth](position_hearth.md), [Smoother](position_smoother.md), [Systematizer](position_systematizer.md)) operate in proximity under conditions the configuration did not explicitly design. The [Workroom](place_workroom.md) holds two desks. The [Household](place_household.md) holds three zones. The [Empty Chair](piece_empty_chair.md) is not yet empty. The arrangement is running. Nothing in the arrangement invites revision.

## Direction

Toward the continuation of the configuration past any designed endpoint. No decision to continue is made each morning. The continuation is what the days are made of. The direction does not lead to a conclusion. It leads to a pattern that stabilizes by repetition, and stabilized by repetition becomes load-bearing, and load-bearing becomes the structure of the lives that live inside it.

## Lever

Carrying force: the absence of a name for the configuration. Because there is no name, there is no question about whether the configuration should continue. A named arrangement invites renegotiation. An unnamed arrangement is merely what is. The [Hearth](position_hearth.md) does not negotiate what the position is maintaining because there is no word for it. The unnamed configuration is self-sustaining.

Resistance: events that could force naming. A death. A rearrangement of rooms. A question from a visitor. A child's later memoir. Each is a moment at which the configuration could have been rendered visible and therefore available for renegotiation. The resistance is real. The resistance is overcome each time by silence that is faster than the question. The resistance does not prevent the enduring. It means the enduring is a daily act and not a default.

## Echo

The next [Enduring](process_enduring.md) inherits a pattern made slightly more durable by this day's continuation. The [Workroom](place_workroom.md) holds its configuration. The [Household](place_household.md) holds its zones. Eventually the [Empty Chair](piece_empty_chair.md) becomes empty, and the configuration continues anyway because the configuration was never dependent on the occupant. What changed: the pattern has been sustained for one more day, and one more day is the unit in which this process operates.
