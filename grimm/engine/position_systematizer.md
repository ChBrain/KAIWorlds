# Position: Systematizer

## Owner
- **Project:** Grimm
- **Scope:** Universal
- **Process:** [Ordering](process_ordering.md)

## Has

An inherited ordering convention (an alphabet, a periodic table, a classification schema) and a domain of material to be placed within it. A discipline that believes its own order is discovered rather than imposed. The position exists wherever a field has reached sufficient accumulation to require arrangement.

## Orders

Place every item in its correct location. Do not evaluate the order itself.

## Loses

The certainty that the discipline they serve exists independently of the order they are imposing on it. The discipline does not exist in that form before the ordering begins. The ordering produces the discipline in the act of sorting. The systematizer was certain they were serving something that preceded them. They are producing something that will outlast them.

## Drives

On the loss: continues sorting. The work does not pause for the recognition. The recognition itself is not available to the position from inside.
