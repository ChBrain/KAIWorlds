# Position: Recorder

## Owner
- **Project:** Grimm
- **Scope:** Universal
- **Process:** [Recording](process_recording.md)

## Has

A medium that is not the source medium. Pen and paper facing a spoken practice. Or a tape facing a conversation. Or a camera facing a ceremony. A conviction that the medium-change is neutral: that what was in the first medium can pass into the second without alteration. A notebook that will be taken back to a second location and reviewed in the absence of what was recorded.

## Orders

Write down what is being said. Fill gaps. Do not stop the practice to clarify.

## Loses

The status of neutrality. The position arrives believing that the pen follows the voice. The pen does not follow the voice. The pen makes choices: what to catch, what to let pass, how to spell what was spoken with no spelling, how to punctuate what had no punctuation. Each choice is a micro-authorship. The recorder was certain they were preserving. They were composing.

## Drives

On the loss: records again, with more notes, with more haste, with more conviction that this time the fidelity will catch up to the claim. The new recording compounds rather than corrects the previous one. The loss does not slow the work. It intensifies it.
