# Position: Source

## Owner
- **Project:** Grimm
- **Scope:** Universal
- **Process:** [Recording](process_recording.md)

## Has

A living practice and a recorder who has arrived to convert the practice into text. The practice predates the recorder by decades. The conversion is irreversible from the side of text, but the practice continues regardless, unaware that its recorded version will become its authoritative version in the century to come.

## Orders

Continue the practice in the ordinary way. Do not perform for the recorder.

## Loses

The authority over their own representation. The position arrives believing that their silence about the recorder's framing is a gift. The silence does not preserve the practice. The silence authorizes whatever frame the recorder imposes to spread outward into the public record. The source was certain they were giving something. They were ceding something.

## Drives

On the loss: tells another tale. The position does not interrupt. The position does not correct. The position tells, and the telling is absorbed into a frame that was constructed without the teller's consent and sustained by the teller's silence about it.
