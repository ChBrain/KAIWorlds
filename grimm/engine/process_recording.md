# Process: Recording

## Owner
- **Project:** Grimm

## Initiated by

A [Source](position_source.md) is running a living practice in its ordinary form. A [Recorder](position_recorder.md) arrives with pen, notebook, and the intention to preserve. The practice continues in the Recorder's presence, not performed for them, not slowed for them. The first mark on the page is not yet a distortion. The second mark begins the distortion.

## Direction

Toward a written record that is taken to preserve the practice. The record will be read as if it held what was spoken. The record will not hold what was spoken. The direction is one and only: what was in one medium must now appear in another, and the translation is understood by the [Recorder](position_recorder.md) as neutral at the moment of recording.

## Lever

Carrying force: the [Recorder](position_recorder.md)'s conviction that the pen follows the voice. The Recorder writes rapidly, fills gaps with plausible reconstructions, chooses a standard spelling for speech that had no spelling, chooses punctuation for speech that had no punctuation. Each choice is made in service of fidelity. Each choice is a departure from fidelity.

Resistance: the mismatch between media. Voice is continuous, recursive, situational; text is fixed, linear, decontextualized. The resistance is structural. No notebook can hold tempo, intonation, regional coloring, the hesitation before a word that was almost a different word. The resistance does not prevent the recording. It converts the recording from preservation into composition.

## Echo

[Smoothing](process_smoothing.md) inherits a text that the [Source](position_source.md) will no longer correct, a [Recorder](position_recorder.md) who still believes in their own fidelity, and a document that presents itself as transcript. The [Manuscript](piece_manuscript.md) has received its first entries. The [Card File](piece_card_file.md) has begun to accept citations extracted from the flow of reading. What changed: the practice has been converted to text, and the text will now become the memory of the practice.
