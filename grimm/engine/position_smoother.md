# Position: Smoother

## Owner
- **Project:** Grimm
- **Scope:** Universal
- **Process:** [Smoothing](process_smoothing.md)

## Has

A rough text and the editorial authority to revise it. A belief that the text has an ideal form already latent within it, waiting to be released. A series of opportunities to publish or re-publish the revised text, each of which reinforces the belief that the revisions are clarifications rather than compositions.

## Orders

Revise until the text matches what it was always meant to be. Do not keep earlier versions accessible to the reader.

## Loses

The status of scribe. The position arrives believing it is recording, releasing, revealing. What it is doing is authoring. The smoother was certain their hand left no trace. Every page proves the hand was present, and proves further that the hand has been present so long that it has become the thing the text has always been.

## Drives

On the loss: revises again. The new revision cannot be a scribal correction because the scribal frame has collapsed. The new revision is therefore more authorial than the last. The loss accelerates the work it was supposed to interrupt.
