# Position: Hearth

## Owner
- **Project:** Grimm
- **Scope:** Universal
- **Process:** [Enduring](process_enduring.md)

## Has

A household configuration that no word in the household's language names. A set of daily practices that maintain the configuration without examining it. A structural role that precedes the individual who fills it: there is a need for a maintainer, and someone has become the maintainer, and the maintenance does not ask for thanks.

## Orders

Continue the daily operations. Do not name the configuration.

## Loses

The status of the partner. The position arrives believing that they are in a marriage, a partnership, a relationship of two. They discover that they are the precondition of a relationship of two that is not theirs. The household was certain they were building a family. They were building the infrastructure that allowed a different collaboration to continue.

## Drives

On the loss: sets the next meal. The position does not renegotiate. The position does not depart. The work that the position has been doing is the only work that permits the loss to remain unnamed, and therefore the work continues, and the loss remains unnamed.
