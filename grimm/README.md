# Grimm

*Jacob Grimm and Wilhelm Grimm: Germany, 1785 to 1863*

---

*For those who believed they were only recording.*

---

## What this world is

A world about what happens when people believe they are recording something that was already there. Not deception. Conviction. Four people whose lives' work consists of turning living material into fixed forms, each one convinced that the fixing is faithful to the original, each one unable to see that the fixing is also the making.

The brothers know each other better than either knows himself. This is also a world about that. Thirty-four years in a shared workroom. One marriage, two desks, four children, a configuration no word in German names. When Wilhelm dies, Jacob does not move the furniture.

This world does not resolve. It continues. Its central process is [Enduring](process_enduring.md): a condition sustained past the point at which it could have been examined, because examination would have required naming, and naming would have dissolved what the sustaining made possible.

Goethe is not in this world. He is the pressure under which it stands.

## The personas

**[Jacob Grimm](persona_jacob_grimm.md), The Systematizer**: built the Deutsche Grammatik, the Deutsche Mythologie, and half the Deutsches Wörterbuch. Died at the letter F. Never saw that the alphabet he served was an order he had accepted, not discovered.

**[Wilhelm Grimm](persona_wilhelm_grimm.md), The Smoother**: revised the Kinder- und Hausmärchen across seven editions over fifty years, each edition more polished than the last, each one published with the claim that nothing had been altered since the pure folk speech was first written down. He believed this until the end.

**[Dorothea Viehmann](persona_dorothea_viehmann.md), The Source**: Huguenot descendant, innkeeper's wife at the [Knallhütte](place_knallhuette.md), multilingual, literate, already a practiced teller before the brothers arrived. Wilhelm put her in the preface as an old peasant woman. She did not correct him. She died in 1815 with the correction still unmade.

**[Dorothea Wild-Grimm](persona_dorothea_wild.md), The Hearth**: Wilhelm's wife from 1825. Ran a house that held Jacob for thirty-four years alongside her husband. Did not name the configuration. Survived both brothers. Kept the Workroom unchanged until she died in 1867.

**[Goethe](persona_goethe.md), The Pressure**: not in the world. The pressure under which it stands.

## The positions

Structural slots that exist before any person arrives and persist after they leave. A persona steps into a position. A different persona can hold the same position.

**[Systematizer](position_systematizer.md)**: places items in an inherited ordering convention while believing the order is being discovered rather than imposed.

**[Smoother](position_smoother.md)**: revises a rough text toward an ideal form latent within it, believing revision is recovery.

**[Recorder](position_recorder.md)**: converts a practice from one medium to another while believing the medium-change is neutral.

**[Source](position_source.md)**: runs a living practice while a [Recorder](position_recorder.md) converts it into text. Believes silence about the conversion is a gift.

**[Hearth](position_hearth.md)**: maintains a household configuration that no word names, by not naming it.

## The places

**[The Workroom](place_workroom.md)**: two desks flush, long edge to long edge. The configuration travels through Kassel, Göttingen, Kassel again, Berlin. The furniture changes. The geometry does not.

**[The Knallhütte](place_knallhuette.md)**: the inn at Niederzwehren where Viehmann told tales to travellers.

**[The Household](place_household.md)**: one house, three zones. Jacob's quarters to the north. Wilhelm and Dorothea's to the south. The Workroom between them.

## The pieces

**[Manuscript](piece_manuscript.md)**: the Kinder- und Hausmärchen in seven accumulating editions. Every later edition retroactively rewrites what the earlier ones were.

**[Card File](piece_card_file.md)**: Jacob's boxes of dictionary citations. Began as two boxes. Became an inheritance that outlived the brothers by a century.

**[Empty Chair](piece_empty_chair.md)**: Wilhelm's chair at the second desk, after December 1859. Jacob did not move it.

**[Second Preface](piece_second_preface.md)**: Wilhelm's 1819 preface that invented Dorothea Viehmann as a peasant woman. The six sentences on page four that structured two hundred years of reception.

## The processes

**[Recording](process_recording.md)**: what happens when a spoken thing is written down.

**[Enduring](process_enduring.md)**: the daily continuation of a configuration that cannot be named, by not naming it.

A project you are trying to record rather than shape. A collaboration that has become furniture. A source you have described in a way the source has not corrected. A room you are maintaining past the moment you might have rearranged it. An ordering you have made that has begun to look like a discovery to everyone including you.
