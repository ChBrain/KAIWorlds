# Position: The Scientist-Coordinator
## The standard the science is measured against —
## and the thing the science cannot measure.

## Owner
- **Project:** Baltic Sea — The Wrong Sea
- **Process:** The Stranding
- **Process:** The Witnessing

## Has
The data.
The methodology.
The authority to name the condition accurately
and be believed.
Twenty years of the western Baltic
in the body before the first press conference begins.
This exists before any person arrives.
It does not require the person to build it.

## Orders
Name the condition.
Coordinate the response.
Return to the shore.
The position does not explain why returning is necessary.
It simply requires it.

## Loses
The sufficiency of naming.
The Scientist-Coordinator was certain
that naming the condition accurately
was the work —
that precision was the instrument
the situation required.
The Witnessing takes it.
The data is correct.
The condition is named precisely.
The animal keeps dying.
The position does not release the person
from returning to name it again.

## Drives
On the loss of sufficiency of naming —
drives out again.
Not for new data.
The position does not name
what it is actually for.
The person holds it anyway.
