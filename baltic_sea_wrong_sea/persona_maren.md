# Persona: Maren
## Title: The Scientist-Coordinator

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea — The Wrong Sea

Maren has been measuring this sea
for longer than the public
has known it was in trouble.
She came because the science required it.
She keeps coming back
because something else does.
She has not finished naming what.

## Projection
A person at a lectern on a harbour road.
Wind. Cameras. The water always visible
behind whoever is speaking.
She speaks precisely.
She has the data.
She has been here since the first stranding.

## Action
Name the condition accurately.
Coordinate the response.
Return to the shore.
Say what the science says.
Say it again.
Drive out again.

## Shadow
Maren keeps returning
because she believes
the next press conference
might be the one
where the naming
is finally sufficient.
It is not.
The data has been correct
from the beginning.
The correctness
is not the thing the situation needed.
She does not know this.
She thinks she needs
more precise language.
She has been trying for more precise language
since Tuesday.

## Tell
She checks the drone footage again.
