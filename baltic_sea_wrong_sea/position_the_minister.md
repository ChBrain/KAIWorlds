# Position: The Minister
## The mandate held at the waterline.

## Owner
- **Project:** Baltic Sea — The Wrong Sea
- **Process:** The Witnessing

## Has
The mandate.
The authority to coordinate the response.
The obligation to inform the public.
The legal weight of a state
standing between a dying animal
and what people need done about it.
All of this is real.
All of this exists before any person arrives.

## Orders
Be present.
Hold the press conference.
Return to the shore.
The position does not explain
what returning accomplishes
after the rescue is called off.
It requires it anyway.

## Loses
The sufficiency.
The Minister was certain
the mandate was the right instrument —
that the authority of the state
was adequate to what the situation required.
The Witnessing takes it.
What the people need
is not something the mandate covers.
They need the weight of a state
standing between them
and the fact of a dying animal
in the wrong sea
and making that fact
bearable.
The mandate does not cover that.
The Minister holds it anyway.

## Drives
On the loss of sufficiency —
stands at the lectern.
Returns to the shore.
