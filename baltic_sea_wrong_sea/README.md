# Baltic Sea — The Wrong Sea

A world about a sperm whale in the wrong sea. Western Baltic coast, March–April 2026. A twelve-tonne animal in water that cannot sustain it — not hostile, just wrong. The wrong salinity. The wrong depth. The wrong five hundred kilometres from home.

The world runs the shore, not the science. Five people at the exclusion zone tape. Three processes running. The animal is in the water. The zone is held. Nobody at the shore minds their own business.
