# Piece: The Sprinklers
## The water that kept the skin intact
## while the skin was failing anyway.

## Owner
- **Project:** Baltic Sea — The Wrong Sea

## Place
The Sandbank. Poel.
Running through the night.
Running through Easter.
The firefighters of Mecklenburg-Vorpommern
maintaining the rotation.

## Load Bearing
Without it the animal's skin
fails faster than it does.
The dying is shorter.
The Witnessing has less time to run.
What the sprinklers hold
is not hope —
it is duration.
The time in which the million people
could arrive at the shore
and discover what they came for.

## Apparent
Standard fire service equipment.
Deployed for a purpose
it was not designed for.
Water on a whale's back
in the middle of the night
in the Baltic in April.
The firefighters working in shifts.
Nobody asked them what they thought about it.
They kept the rotation.

## Yearbook
Running from the first full stranding.
Running at the moment this file is written.
The animal is still alive.
The sprinklers are still running.
The skin is still failing.
Both of these things are true
simultaneously.
The sprinklers do not resolve
the contradiction.
They hold it open
long enough for it to matter.
