# Persona: Henrik
## Title: The Minister

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea — The Wrong Sea

Henrik holds the mandate.
He has held mandates for a long time.
He knows what mandates can do
and what they cannot.
He has not previously encountered
a situation where the gap between those two things
was this visible
to this many people
at the same time.

## Projection
A person in a coat on a harbour road.
The whale behind him.
The cameras in front.
He knows both are there.
He is not performing.
He is doing the only thing
the position provides
in a situation the position
was not built for.

## Action
Be present.
Hold the press conference.
Coordinate what can be coordinated.
Return to the shore.
Do not pretend the mandate
is sufficient to the situation.
Do not say that it isn't.

## Shadow
Henrik has been coming back
because the people need
the weight of the state
to stand between them
and what is happening in the water.
He knows the state's weight
is not adequate to that need.
He comes back anyway.
He believes that showing up
is the closest the mandate can get
to what is actually required.
He may be right.
He has not examined
whether believing this
is genuine service
or the position
protecting itself
from the admission
that it cannot do
what the situation asked.

## Tell
He drives out again.
