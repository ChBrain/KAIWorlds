# Place: The Kirchsee
## Location: Off the island of Poel, Wismar Bay,
## the western Baltic. March 31st — April 2026.

## Owner
- **Project:** Baltic Sea — The Wrong Sea

## Shown
- Shallow water. Tidal range: negligible.
  The depth that should be safe. Is not.
- The seabed. Sand and silt.
  Fifty to sixty centimetres of it
  now displaced by twelve tonnes
  that arrived and did not leave.
- The animal. Partially above the surface.
  The back visible. The blowhole accessible.
  The tail below.
- The sprinklers. Running.
  The firefighters of Mecklenburg-Vorpommern
  maintaining the rotation through the night.
- The exclusion zone. 500 metres in every direction.
  Marked by boats. Enforced by presence.
  The zone is for him.
  He does not know this.
- The seagulls. Three of them, on his back.
  Present from the fourth day.
  Nobody mentions them after the first report.
- The ghost light of the Baltic:
  flat, cold, April.
  The light that does not warm.

## Holds
The animal.
Not by intention.
By the specific combination
of wrong depth,
wrong salinity,
no tides,
and twelve tonnes
that has tried four times
and does not have a fifth try in it.
The net remnants.
Still present.
The fifty to seventy metres
that were removed
are gone.
What was swallowed
remains.

## Offers
The breath.
Every four to five minutes.
The spout visible from the shore.
The only announcement
the place makes
of what it holds.
The two seconds.
Once.
April 9th.
The tail.
The place offered this once
and did not offer it again.

## Withheld
Whether the place registers
as wrong to the animal.
Whether he knows
the Atlantic exists
and is not this.
Whether the singing at night
was for the place,
or for something
the place cannot hold.
What the place will be
after the animal is gone.
Shallow water off an island.
The same depth.
The same salinity.
The seabed levelled by winter.
The coordinates in the record.
Nothing else visible.
The place does not change.
That is the thing it withholds most completely.
