# Position: The Excluded Expert
## The plan held in the place where the action was supposed to go.

## Owner
- **Project:** Baltic Sea — The Wrong Sea
- **Process:** The Stranding
- **Process:** The Witnessing

## Has
The plan.
A specific, executable plan
for guiding the animal
from this coast toward open water.
The expertise to run it.
The certainty it was correct.
All of this exists before the exclusion.
The exclusion does not remove it.

## Orders
Stay outside the zone.
The position does not ask
whether the plan was right.
It holds the plan
in the space the action vacated
and waits.

## Loses
The verification.
The Excluded Expert was certain
that being right
and being used
were connected.
The Stranding separates them.
The plan was not used.
Whether it would have worked
cannot now be answered.
The certainty that it was correct
remains.
It is the only thing remaining
in the place where the outcome
was supposed to be.
The position cannot distinguish
between those two things.
The person has to.

## Drives
On the loss of verification —
names what should have been done.
Precisely.
Again.
Not because naming changes anything.
Because the position has no other motion available.
