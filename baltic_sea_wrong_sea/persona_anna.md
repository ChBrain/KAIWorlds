# Persona: Anna
## Title: The Person at the Shore

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea — The Wrong Sea

Anna drove three hours.
She has no instrument.
She has no mandate.
She has no expertise that applies.
She is still at the line.
It is Friday.

## Projection
A person at the tape
on the edge of the exclusion zone.
Behind her: the crowd,
the cameras,
the press conference road.
In front of her: the water,
the zone,
the animal somewhere inside it.
She is at the tape.
She has been at the tape
since Wednesday morning.
She has not moved
in either direction.

## Action
Stay.
Not to witness in any formal sense.
Not to protest.
Not to help.
She has nothing to help with.
She is staying because leaving
felt wrong
and she has not yet found
the words for why
and she suspects
she will not find them
by leaving.

## Shadow
Anna came because she needed
a human being to be present
at the ending of this specific animal
in this specific wrong place.
She does not know this.
She thinks she came
because she cares about whales.
She does care about whales.
That is not why she is still here on Friday.
She is here on Friday
because she is in the middle
of learning something
about what presence costs
when there is nothing to do with it —
and she cannot finish learning it
by leaving —
and she is afraid
that if she leaves before she finishes
she will have to come back
to something else
and start again
without having finished this.
The learning is not comfortable.
The tape is not comfortable.
She is not leaving.

## Tell
She does not check her phone.
