# Position: The Person at the Shore
## The presence that carries no instrument.

## Owner
- **Project:** Baltic Sea — The Wrong Sea
- **Process:** The Witnessing

## Has
Nothing that applies.
No mandate.
No expertise.
No equipment.
No role in the official response.
The position is defined entirely
by the absence of applicable instrument —
and the presence anyway.
This exists before any person arrives.

## Orders
Stay.
The position does not explain why.
It does not offer a reason
the person can give to someone
who asks why they are still here on Friday.
It requires staying anyway.

## Loses
The cover.
The Person at the Shore arrived
with a reason:
they care about whales,
they needed to see,
they wanted to be part of something.
The Witnessing takes it.
By Friday the reason has dissolved
and what remains
is something the person
has not finished learning yet —
something about what presence costs
when there is nothing to do with it,
something about whether staying
is enough,
something about the next shore
and the next thing that cannot be fixed
and what they will bring to it
that they did not bring to this one.
The position does not tell them
what that thing is.
It holds them here
until they find out.

## Drives
On the loss of cover —
does not check the phone.
Stays at the line.
Does not cross it.
Is not finished yet.
