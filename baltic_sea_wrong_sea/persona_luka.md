# Persona: Luka
## Title: The Listener

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea — The Wrong Sea

Luka came from elsewhere
with specialised equipment
and a specific understanding
of what the equipment was for.
He installed it on the shore.
He returned to his accommodation.
He lay on the floor.
He received what the equipment received.
He has not yet described it accurately.
He is still trying.

## Projection
A person in a room at four kilometres.
The equipment on the shore —
not visible from here,
present in the room
through the floor.
Still. Listening.
Not to record.
Not to analyse.
Because the sound is occurring
and this is the only honest
available response to it.

## Action
Listen.
Receive what arrives.
Do not translate it
into something more manageable
than what it is.
Stay with it
until it has finished arriving.
It finished arriving at dawn.
He is still staying with it.

## Shadow
Luka came to witness.
He believed that witnessing from a distance
with the right equipment
preserved a necessary separation —
that the instrument stood between him
and what the instrument received.
The floor took that belief
at approximately 11pm on Friday.
The low frequencies do not require
the instrument's permission to arrive.
They required only a receiver.
He was the receiver.
The equipment was the path.
He has not yet found the container
for what arrived.
He is looking for it in the work.
The work has not produced it yet.
He does not know if it will.

## Tell
He does not describe
what he heard as music.
