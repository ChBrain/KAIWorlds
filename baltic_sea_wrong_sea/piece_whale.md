# Piece: Whale

## Owner
- **Project:** Baltic Sea — The Wrong Sea

## Place
Everywhere the animal was.
Everywhere the record holds an animal
that came before.
Everywhere the next one is already swimming.

## Load Bearing
Without it the animal is a stranding.
A stranding is a sad event.
A statistic in the record.
With it: the animal is an individual
in a wrong place
that millions of people drove to stand beside.
Remove it: the Witnessing is about a species.
With it: the Witnessing is about this one.
That is the difference between
the 300,000 who die every year
without a press conference
and the one who dies with sprinklers
and an exclusion zone
and a musician at four kilometres.
The name does not save the animal.
It makes the question the world was built to house
unavoidable.
Without it the question can be deflected
into statistics.
With it: it cannot.

## Apparent
A word.
Given by the press, collectively,
after the beach where the animal first stranded.
Carried into every account.
Carried past the ending.
The name outlasts the animal.
That is what names do.
That is why they are given.

## Yearbook
Timmy. March 2026. The western Baltic.
Named before anyone knew how it ended.
Did not make it back.

Before Timmy: Bucki.
August 2008. The Baltic coast. Twelve metres.
Spotted near Warnemünde.
Spotted at a wedding on Rügen.
Then gone from the record.
The record does not say where.
Some of them find their way out again.
Bucki may have been one of them.
The record does not say.

Before Bucki: no names.
The stranding network was established in 1990.
Before 1990 they came
and the shore held no systematic account.
Numbers. Species. Coordinates.
Some of those returned too.
The record does not say which ones.

After Timmy: the next one.
No name yet.
Already swimming.
May find its way out.
May not.
The wrong sea does not predetermine
which ending runs.
Only the combination does —
the animal, the condition it arrived with,
the depth of the channel,
the direction it chose in the dark.
