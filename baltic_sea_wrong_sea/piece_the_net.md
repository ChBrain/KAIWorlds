# Piece: The Net
## The thing that was already there.

## Owner
- **Project:** Baltic Sea — The Wrong Sea

## Place
The animal's mouth.
Present before the first sighting.
Present at the last breath.

## Load Bearing
Without it the Entering has no prior condition.
The animal arrives weakened
or it arrives whole —
and those are different stories.
With the net: the trap was already assembled
before the Baltic was entered.
The Baltic did not build the trap.
The net did.
Remove it: the Stranding is an accident.
With it: the Stranding is the completion
of something that began
somewhere else,
in water nobody is watching,
before anyone gave the animal a name.

## Apparent
Ghost net.
Fifty to seventy metres of it
retrieved from the mouth.
Length unknown — what was swallowed
cannot be measured from outside.
Gillnet, most likely.
The kind that catches what it was not set for.
The kind that kills
300,000 animals per year
without a press conference.

## Yearbook
In the mouth before March 3rd.
Still there at the end.
The net outlasts the animal.
It will be in the next one too.
