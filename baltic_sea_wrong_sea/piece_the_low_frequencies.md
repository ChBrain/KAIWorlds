# Piece: The Low Frequencies
## What came through the floor at four kilometres.

## Owner
- **Project:** Baltic Sea — The Wrong Sea

## Place
The Room at Four Kilometres.
Friday night into Saturday dawn.

## Load Bearing
Without it the Listener position has no content.
The room at four kilometres is just a room.
The distance holds.
The separation is real.
With it: the distance collapses.
The instrument and the person
are revealed as the same thing.
Remove it: The Listener witnesses from outside.
With it: The Listener discovers
there is no outside.

## Apparent
Infrasound.
Below the threshold of hearing.
Above the threshold of feeling.
The body receives it
before the mind names it.
The animal sang from approximately 11pm
until dawn.
The sound did not require proximity.
It required only a receiver
and a building
and a floor
and a person
lying still enough
to notice.

## Yearbook
One night.
Friday into Saturday.
The animal alive.
The sound continuous.
The person at four kilometres
receiving it
without instrument sufficient
to contain what was received.
Still looking for the container.
The low frequencies
do not wait for the container
to be found.
They arrive anyway.
