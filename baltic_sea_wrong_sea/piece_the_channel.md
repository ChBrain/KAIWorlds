# Piece: The Channel
## The thing dug by hand — and then by machine.

## Owner
- **Project:** Baltic Sea — The Wrong Sea

## Place
The Sandbank. Timmendorfer Strand.
March 26th, 2026.
Gone by March 27th —
the animal swam through it overnight.

## Load Bearing
Without it the third stranding has no release.
The animal does not free itself that time.
There is no fourth stranding.
There is no Witnessing at Poel.
The world ends at Timmendorfer Strand
and the question it was built to house
is never asked at full weight.
With it: the animal is given
one more attempt.
It takes it.
It returns anyway.
Remove the channel:
different ending, smaller question.
With it: the full question.

## Apparent
A trench in sand.
Dug first by hand —
helpers on the beach with shovels —
then by excavator
brought from Lower Saxony.
One night's work.
The animal swam through it
in the dark
without anyone watching.
By morning it was gone.
By Saturday it was back.
The channel was not a rescue.
It was a door.
The animal chose to return.

## Yearbook
Dug once.
Used once.
Silted over by the tide that doesn't come.
What it proved:
that the animal could move
when the path was clear.
What it did not prove:
that a clear path was sufficient.
