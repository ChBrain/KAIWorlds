# Place: The Exclusion Zone
## Location: 500 metres from the animal.
## A radius. Not a wall.

## Owner
- **Project:** Baltic Sea — The Wrong Sea

## Shown
- Water on all sides.
  The zone is not marked on the water.
  It is understood.
- Boats at the perimeter.
  Present. Not crossing.
- The animal at the centre.
  Visible at distance.
  Breathing at intervals.
  The breath visible as a spout
  every four or five minutes.
  Then not.
  Then again.
- The people who are inside the zone
  by mandate: the experts,
  the ministry boats,
  the assessment teams.
  They approach once.
  They withdraw.
  The zone is re-established.
- The people who are outside the zone
  by everything except the line:
  the excluded, the press,
  the person who drove three hours.
  The line is real.
  The distance is not always
  the same as the line.

## Holds
The animal's right to die
without further intervention.
The formal acknowledgement
that presence had become harm
and that the most that could be given
was absence.
The zone holds that decision
in physical space.

## Does
Makes everyone outside it
aware that they have been placed
on the wrong side of something —
not by punishment
but by the simple fact
that the only gift remaining
is the one they cannot give
by staying.
The Excluded Expert stands at the line
and the zone asks:
*what would crossing accomplish?*
The Person at the Shore stands at the tape
and the zone asks:
*what is staying for?*
Neither question has an answer
the zone provides.
The zone only holds the line.
The people hold everything else.

## Withheld
Whether the animal knew the zone existed.
Whether the quiet it produced
reached the animal as quiet.
Whether it made any difference
to what happened inside it.
The zone does not hold these answers.
It holds the line
and leaves the rest
to whoever is standing at it.
