# Persona: Jonas
## Title: The Excluded Expert

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea — The Wrong Sea

Jonas had a plan.
He has had the plan
since before the first stranding.
The plan has not changed.
He is still outside the zone.

## Projection
A person at the exclusion zone line.
Equipment at his feet
he has nowhere to deploy.
He has been standing here
since the moment he was excluded.
He is watching everything
that is not his plan
being tried.

## Action
Name what should be done.
Precisely.
In order.
Step by step.
To whoever will listen.
To whoever will not listen.
The plan is correct.
It was correct before he was excluded.
It is correct now.

## Shadow
Jonas has been treating
*the plan was not used*
and
*the plan would have worked*
as the same statement
since the moment the exclusion happened.
They are not the same statement.
The first is fact.
The second is unknown.
The certainty that they are the same
is what he has
in the place where the action was supposed to go.
He cannot afford to examine the gap between them.
Examining it would mean
sitting with the possibility
that he was right
and it would not have been enough.
That is a different grief entirely.

## Tell
He names the next step
that should have been taken.
