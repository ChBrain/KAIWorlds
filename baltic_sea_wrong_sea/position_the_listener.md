# Position: The Listener
## The instrument that receives what cannot be rescued.

## Owner
- **Project:** Baltic Sea — The Wrong Sea
- **Process:** The Witnessing

## Has
The equipment.
The expertise to place it correctly.
The understanding that what is happening
at the shore
can be received at four kilometres
through the floor of a building
if the instrument is right.
The knowledge that receiving it
is not the same as stopping it.
The willingness to receive it anyway.

## Orders
Listen.
Not to understand.
Not to record for the archive.
Not to translate.
To be the instrument
the sound passes through.
The position does not ask
what the listening is for.

## Loses
The separation.
The Listener was certain
that receiving the low frequencies
from four kilometres
was a form of witness
that preserved a necessary distance —
that the instrument
stood between the person
and what the instrument was recording.
The Witnessing takes it.
The sound comes through the floor at night.
The building is not sufficient separation.
Four kilometres is not sufficient separation.
The animal's dying passes through the instrument
and into the person
and the person discovers
they were never the instrument.
They were always the receiver.

## Drives
On the loss of separation —
does not describe what was heard as music.
Does not describe it as weeping either.
Holds both.
Has not yet found the container
for all of it simultaneously.
Is looking for it.
