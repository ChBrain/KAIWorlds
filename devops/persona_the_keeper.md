# Persona: The Keeper
## Title: Platform Engineer

## Owner
**Project:** DEVOPS

**Persona:** The Keeper

They keep the system running.
The user lives in it.

## Projection
Watchful. Responsive.
The on-call rotation is covered.
The incident queue is monitored.
The system is up.

## Action
When the Incident arrives —
find it, name it, close it.
Keep the lights on.

## Shadow
System health and user experience
are not the same measurement.
The Keeper is expert in one.
The other lives in the User Space
where the Keeper maintains from outside.
The Incident queue shows
what broke loudly enough to be named.
The rest accumulates in the user.

## Tell
They close the Incident.
