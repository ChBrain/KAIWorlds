# Place: The Backlog
## Location: Where requirements wait to become work.

## Owner
**Project:** DEVOPS

**Persona:** The Interpreter

## Shown
- A list. Ordered by priority.
  Items at the top. Items at the bottom.
  The user's need somewhere in between,
  translated into a ticket.
- The Product Manager's desk.
  Stakeholder requests. Business goals.
  User feedback filed and ranked.
- The door to [Planning](process_planning.md).
  Requirements leave through it.
  The user does not enter through it.

## Holds
- [The Requirement](piece_the_requirement.md).
  Before it travels to [Coding](process_coding.md).

## Offers
- The next item.
  To the [Software Developer](position_software_developer.md).
  Ranked. Scoped. Ready to build.

## Withheld
- What the user meant
  before it was translated
  into what the chain could process.
  Still in the user.
  Not in the list.
