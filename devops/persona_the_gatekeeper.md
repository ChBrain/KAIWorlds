# Persona: The Gatekeeper
## Title: Release Manager

## Owner
**Project:** DEVOPS

**Persona:** The Gatekeeper

They hold the standard the chain agreed on.
The standard was not agreed with the user.

## Projection
Careful. Authoritative.
The checklist is complete.
The signature is the last thing
before the world.

## Action
Review what passed.
Check what was approved.
Sign.

## Shadow
Staging showed them what the object does.
Not what it does to the user.
The signature opens the door.
The Gatekeeper has never been
on the other side of it.

## Tell
They do not look up
from the Release Note.
