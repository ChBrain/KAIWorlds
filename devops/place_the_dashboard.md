# Place: The Dashboard
## Location: Where the system reports on itself.

## Owner
**Project:** DEVOPS

**Persona:** The Watcher

## Shown
- Graphs. Metrics. Alert thresholds.
  Response times. Error rates. Uptime.
  The system's account of its own performance.
- The SRE's screen.
  Watching what the instrumentation
  was built to watch.
- The door to [Planning](process_planning.md).
  [The Signal](piece_the_signal.md) leaves through it.
  If it is strong enough.
  If the [Product Manager](position_product_manager.md)
  receives it as a reason to change
  [The Requirement](piece_the_requirement.md).

## Holds
- [The Signal](piece_the_signal.md).
  The system's account of itself.
  Not the user's account of their experience.

## Offers
- The Signal.
  To [Planning](process_planning.md).
  Partial. Instrumental. Precise
  about what it was built to measure.

## Withheld
- What the user is experiencing
  that has no metric.
  The Dashboard cannot display
  what the instrumentation
  was not built to capture.
  It shows what it shows.
  The rest is in [The User Space](place_the_user_space.md).
