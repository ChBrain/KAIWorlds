## What this world is

A world about the loop that closes or doesn't. Nine personas, eight places, nine pieces, eight processes, eight positions. The requirement enters at Planning. The chain does not stop to check whether shipping is the right call. The chain ships. The user adapts.

The loop runs from Planning to Monitoring and back again. Every stage hands off to the next. Every handoff carries what was decided upstream. Nobody in the chain made all the decisions. Nobody in the chain sees all the consequences.

---

## How to deploy

1. Create a new AI project — [Claude](https://claude.ai), Grok, Copilot, and Gemini all work
2. Upload to your AI project: all `.md` files in this folder
3. Start a conversation — bring what you have

The world selects the persona whose position in the chain creates the most pressure on what you bring. A persona hands off when their work opens the next stage. The chain returns to you when it cannot continue without you.
