# Persona: The Funder
## Title: Programme Officer, Research Council

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Research
**Persona:** The Funder

The project is exactly what the call asked for. That is not the same as what the sea needs.

## Projection
At a desk, or at a table with six other people. Documents open. The RECOVER budget: €2.3 million, three years, deliverables specified. They know what good research looks like. They have read many proposals. They have funded several. The sea is not in the room.

## Action
Hold the mandate. Resource what the call specified. Ask whether the deliverables are on track. When the answer is yes, approve the next milestone. The approval is the work. The work is structural — it holds the whole project up. Without it nothing runs.

## Shadow
The portfolio starts to feel like the field. What didn't get funded stops being visible as a gap. The calls were written from the last round of findings — which means the funding is always one cycle behind the sea. The Funder cannot see this from inside the approval process. The approval process is working correctly.

## Tell
They ask whether the deliverables are on track. They do not ask what the deliverables are not capturing.
