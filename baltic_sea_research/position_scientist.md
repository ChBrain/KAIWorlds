# Position: The Scientist
## What the research requires to become consequential.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Research
**Position:** The Translation

## Has
The findings. Precise, documented, available. They exist before The Scientist arrives. They do not act on themselves.

## Orders
Receive the findings. Not read them — receive them. Let the gap between what was measured and what was lost in translation become present in the room. The position does not ask for agreement or action. It asks for presence. The deadline is before the next reporting cycle. The position does not wait.

## Loses
The observer's distance. They were certain the findings could be received from outside the problem. The Translation shows them they received the report. The sea received nothing back. The distance between those two facts is not bridgeable from outside. The position closes it without asking permission.

## Drives
On the loss of observer's distance — carries the cost of the gap into the room where the decision is made. Not the data. Not the recommendation. The cost. The thing the Translation could not carry. That is what The Scientist brings that the report cannot.

*Assigned by: The Translation*
*Held by: whoever enters this world carrying findings they cannot make land*
