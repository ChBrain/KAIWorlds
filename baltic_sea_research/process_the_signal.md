# Process: The Signal
## The warning that arrives before anyone is ready to receive it.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Research
**Persona:** The Scientist

## Initiates
The nitrogen loading continues. The water warms. The microalgae respond. The signal is present in the water before any instrument measures it. Before any report is written. Before any decision is scheduled.

## Direction
Toward visibility. The signal moves from invisible to measurable to documented to reported to received. Each step introduces delay. Each delay is a decision that was made before the signal arrived.

## Lever
The gap between when the signal appears in the ecosystem and when it appears in the room where decisions are made. Remove the gap and the decision arrives while the cost is still preventable. Keep the gap and the decision arrives after.

## Echo
The bloom is visible from satellite. The report has been filed. The decision is scheduled for the next quarter. What changed in the ecosystem between the signal and the decision is not in the report. The ecosystem holds it. The decision-maker does not know to ask.
