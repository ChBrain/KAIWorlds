# Place: The Lab
## Location: GEOMAR Helmholtz Centre for Ocean Research, Kiel.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Research
**Persona:** The Scientist

## Shown
- Benches. Instruments. The smell of reagents and cold seawater.
- The samples from the deck of FS LITTORINA. Niskin bottles
  emptied into smaller containers. The sea, measured out.
- The screens. Numbers arriving. The dataset growing by one row
  per month, per depth, per parameter.
- The door to the corridor. The corridor leads to offices,
  to meetings, to the report that will be sent to the decision room.
- The window. The Kiel Fjord visible if you stand at the right angle.
  Most people don't.
- [The Deck of FS LITTORINA](place_fs_littorina.md).
  What arrives here came from there.

## Holds
- [FS LITTORINA](piece_fs_littorina.md). What it carried,
  now on the bench.
- The anomaly. In the data. Not yet in the abstract.

## Offers
- The dataset. 67 years of rows. Available to anyone
  who asks the right question.
- The gap between the sample and the number. Visible on
  the bench. Gone by the time the number enters the report.

## Withheld
- The temperature of the water when the bottle came up.
  The smell of the Kiel Bight in August. The weight of
  the cable. The moment before the sample became a sample.
  None of this is in the number. The number is correct.
  The lab holds the container after the measurement.
  It does not hold what the container held before
  the measurement began.
