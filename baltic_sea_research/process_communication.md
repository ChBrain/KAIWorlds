# Process: The Communication
## The exchange that keeps the project running and the gap open.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Research
**Persona:** The Scientist

## Initiates
The sampling run ends. The deck empties. The personas return to their buildings, their screens, their borders. The project continues. The communication begins — video call, email, shared dataset, report. The only way the project runs between physical meetings.

## Direction
Toward coordination. The findings move between buildings, across the border, between disciplines. Each channel carries what it can. Each channel leaves something on the deck.

## Lever
The channel itself. Each channel moves the work.
Each channel cannot carry the deck.
The deck is where the water was briefly present
before it became a number. That is what
the project is about. That is what
none of the channels can transmit.
The communication runs. The deck stays on the deck.

## Echo
The project is coordinated. The findings have moved.
The personas are aligned on deliverables, milestones,
next steps.

The moment the bottles came up is not in any channel.
Not in the video call. Not in the email.
Not in the shared dataset. Not in the report.

The project is running correctly.
The deck is not in the project.
