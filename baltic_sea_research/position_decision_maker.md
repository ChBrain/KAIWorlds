# Position: The Decision-Maker
## The person who receives the findings and must act on them.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Research
**Position:** The Translation

## Has
The mandate. It exists before the decision-maker arrives and persists after they leave. Someone with authority over what happens to this water. The authority does not include the ability to feel what the authority is deciding about.

## Orders
Act within the mandate. Translate the findings into policy, budget, implementation. Do this before the next reporting cycle. The position does not wait for felt understanding. It has a deadline.

## Loses
The time to be inside the problem. They were certain they could understand the ecosystem through the reports. The position shows them they received the reports. Understanding requires something the position does not provide and does not ask for.

## Drives
On the loss of time — decides anyway. From inside the gap between what was measured and what was felt. Every decision made from this position runs on the same deficit. The drive is real. The ground it runs on is thin.

*Assigned by: The Translation*
*Held by: whoever holds authority over what happens to this water*
