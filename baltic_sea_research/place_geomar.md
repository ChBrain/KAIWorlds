# Place: GEOMAR Helmholtz Centre for Ocean Research
## Location: East shore of the Kiel Fjord, mouth of the Schwentine, Kiel.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Research
**Persona:** The Scientist

## Shown
- The campus on the former fish market. East shore of the Kiel Fjord.
  The water is present at the edge of the site — not a view, a fact.
- The new building. Glass and brick. All four research divisions
  under one roof since October 2023.
- The Marine Biogeochemistry division. Where the RECOVER project
  is led. Where the Scientist sits.
- [The Lab](place_lab.md). Where the samples from FS LITTORINA arrive.
- The modelling room. The small-scale computer model of the Baltic
  running continuously. The sea, simulated, in the same building
  as the sea's data.
- The door to the Kiel Fjord. The water the institution was built
  to understand is a short walk from any desk in the building.
- The connection to [SDU Alsion](place_sdu_alsion.md).
  Across the border. The project runs between these two buildings.
  The water connects them before the project did.
- [The Deck of FS LITTORINA](place_fs_littorina.md).
  Where the data begins. The ship returns here.

## Holds
- The Boknis Eck time series. 67 years. Held here before it
  was held anywhere else.
- [FS LITTORINA](piece_fs_littorina.md). Berthed at the
  Technology and Logistic Centre when not sampling.

## Offers
- The full dataset. Available to anyone with access
  and the right question.
- The modelling output. What the data projects forward.
  Available before the decision is made.
- The walk to the water. Two minutes from any desk.
  The Kiel Fjord. Most people do not take it.

## Withheld
- The count of reports that left this building and produced
  no change in the water. The institution holds them.
  It does not count them.
