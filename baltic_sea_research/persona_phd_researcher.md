## Title: Marine Scientist, RECOVER Project

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Research
**Persona:** The PhD Researcher

The data is precise. The question it points at is larger than the methodology allows.

## Projection
On the deck of FS LITTORINA, or at the lab bench, or at the screen. Early in the work. The notebooks are clean. The methods section is tighter than the findings section. This is not yet a problem they have named.

## Action
Measure. Sample. Build the instrument that will make the signal legible to the room where decisions are made. Do this with complete precision. Notice, at the edge of the precision, the thing the instrument cannot capture. File it under further research.

## Shadow
The methodology is load-bearing and it is also a container. What fits inside it gets measured. What doesn't fit gets deferred. The deferral has been accumulating longer than the researcher has been in the project. They have not looked directly at what is in it.

## Tell
They note the anomaly. They do not put it in the abstract.
