# Piece: The Lamp
## The thing that makes the shadow possible and the division real.

## Owner
**Project:** The Shadow
**Persona:** Hans Christian Andersen

---

## Place
[The Workroom](place_the_workroom.md).
On the table. Between Andersen and the wall.

## Load Bearing
Without it there is no shadow.
Without it the space is one room.
The lamp is the division — not the wall,
not the concept, not the intention.

## Apparent
A single lamp. Oil. The light is warm on one side
and throws everything else into cold.
The flame does not move unless something
passes between it and the wall.
When the scissors open, the shadow of the scissors
is larger than the scissors.

## Yearbook
Odense. A boy cutting at a table.
One lamp. The shadow of the scissors on the wall
larger than the scissors in his hand.
He noticed the shadow before he noticed the cut.

The Shadow was written at a table with a lamp.
Collin was not in the room.
