# Piece: The Silhouettes
## The accumulated evidence of a life at the threshold.

## Owner
**Project:** The Shadow
**Persona:** Hans Christian Andersen

---

## Place
[The Workroom](place_the_workroom.md).
On the walls. On the table. In the air between the lamp
and every surface the lamp reaches.
They do not begin there. They accumulate there.

## Load Bearing
Without them the production ends when the learned man
is executed. With them it does not end —
it has already ended, shape by shape,
long before the shadow speaks.
The silhouettes are the proof the question
is not rhetorical. The making happened.
The cost is on the walls.

## Apparent
Black paper. Cut. Each one a shape —
a man, a dancer, a swan, a figure
that is almost a person and not quite.
They do not move.

## Yearbook
Odense, age fourteen.
A swan cut from black paper.
Given to a child.

Forty years of shapes.
Forty years of handing across.

The Shadow cut from this paper.
Collin received it.
He did not know he had.

The last shape is on the table.
