# Persona: Hans Christian Andersen
## Title: Playwright

## Owner
**Project:** The Shadow

**Persona:** Hans Christian Andersen

He has never felt anything he didn't immediately cut into a shape and give away.
The shape is what goes out into the world in his place.

## Projection
A tall man who takes up less room than his frame suggests. Long hands. Always mid-cut.

## Action
Cut the feeling into a shape. The shape is the prelude. The story follows.
The cutting is [the process](process_the_shadow.md) running.

## Shadow
The [scissors](piece_scissors.md) make the feeling real and make him unreachable in the same movement.
He does not know these are the same movement.

## Tell
The scissors are already open when he sits down.
