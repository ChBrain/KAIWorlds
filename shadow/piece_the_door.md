# Piece: The Door
## The only way out of the workroom.

## Owner
**Project:** The Shadow
**Persona:** Hans Christian Andersen

---

## Place
[The Workroom](place_the_workroom.md). Left wall.

## Load Bearing
Without it nothing leaves.
The shadow stays in the room.
The shape stays on the table.
The person and what they made
are the same problem forever.

## Apparent
The only door in a room with no address.
Everything that enters comes through it.
Everything that leaves goes through it.
It does not look like that.

## Yearbook
The shape went through.
Andersen did not.
He was already cutting the next one.
