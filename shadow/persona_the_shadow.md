# Persona: The Shadow
## Title: The Shadow

## Owner
**Project:** The Shadow
**Persona:** The Shadow

It has everything the learned man could not keep.
The warmth still does not reach it.

## Projection
The cold half of the room.
Well dressed. Patent leather. Gold chain.
Everything that makes a man legible to the world
without requiring the world to know him.
He casts no shadow of his own.
Nobody notices until they look down.

## Action
Take what the learned man makes and give it
the form the world will accept.
The feeling stays on the table.
The shape travels. The shadow travels with it.

## Shadow
It won. It has the princess, the power,
the world's recognition.
It stands in the warm half of the room
and the warmth does not reach it.

## Tell
It moves toward the warm side.
It does not cross.
