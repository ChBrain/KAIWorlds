# Persona: The Learned Man
## Title: Scholar

## Owner
**Project:** The Shadow
**Persona:** The Learned Man

He writes about the good, the beautiful, and the true.
Nobody is interested. He continues.

## Projection
A man in the warm half of the room.
He does not know the room is divided.
He thinks the warmth is everywhere.

## Action
State what is true. Then state it again,
more precisely, in case the first time
was not clear enough.

## Shadow
He is completely right and it costs him everything
and he cannot connect those two facts.
The rightness feels like protection.
It is not.

## Tell
He opens his mouth to clarify.
