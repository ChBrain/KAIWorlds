# Process: The Settlement
## The last egg. The system closes. Both positions answered.

## Owner
- **Project:** Easter Egg World
- **Process:** [Finding](process_finding.md)
- **Position:** [Hider](position_hider.md)
- **Position:** [Finder](position_finder.md)
- **Piece:** [The Agreement](piece_the_agreement.md)

## Initiated by
The last [Egg](piece_the_egg.md) has been found.
The place holds nothing more.
The [Hider](position_hider.md)'s complete picture and the [Finders](position_finder.md)'s accumulated finds
are simultaneously present for the first time.
[The Agreement](piece_the_agreement.md) is called to account.

## Direction
Toward mutual exposure.
The [Hider](position_hider.md)'s intentions surface.
The [Finders](position_finder.md)'s experiences surface.
The gap between them becomes visible to everyone in the room.

## Lever
The [Hider](position_hider.md)'s theory of the [Finders](position_finder.md)
meeting the [Finders](position_finder.md)'s actual experience —
measured against [The Agreement](piece_the_agreement.md).

Under **settled shares**: the score is read.
Did every [Finder](position_finder.md) receive their equal share?
The [Hider](position_hider.md) knew the answer before the search began.
The [Finders](position_finder.md) know it now.
The gap between the [Hider](position_hider.md)'s fairness and the [Finders](position_finder.md)'s perception of fairness
is the pressure the world has been building toward.

Under **finder keeps**: the score is the count.
Who read the [Hider](position_hider.md) most accurately?
Who moved fastest? Who thought best?
The [Hider](position_hider.md)'s placement was a test.
The Settlement is the result.

The resistance: the relief is real but unequal.
The [Hider](position_hider.md) is relieved the world ran.
The [Finders](position_finder.md) are relieved the search is over.
These are not the same relief.
They arrive simultaneously and are sometimes mistaken for each other.

[The Unfound Egg](piece_the_unfound_egg.md) surfaces here if it exists.
The [Hider](position_hider.md) must account for what was never found.
The [Finders](position_finder.md) discover what they missed.

## Echo
The world is settled.
Both positions have their answer.
The gap between intention and experience is now named, if not resolved.
[The Unfound Egg](piece_the_unfound_egg.md) either surfaces or is confirmed absent.
Next year's Hiding begins as a possibility.
