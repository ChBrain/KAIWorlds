# Place: The House
## The intimate place. The Hider hides inside the Finder's own world.

## Owner
- **Project:** Easter Egg World

## Shown
- Rooms, each with their own logic: kitchen, living room, hallway, bedrooms.
- Furniture the [Finders](position_finder.md) use every day.
  Shelves, cushions, shoes, books, drawers.
- The objects that belong to specific [Finders](position_finder.md):
  a favourite toy, a coat pocket, a bedside table.
- Windows. Outside light entering. The Garden visible but not in play.
- Closed doors. Some rooms in, some rooms out —
  the boundary is architectural and decided by the [Hider](position_hider.md).
- [The Eggs](piece_the_egg.md), placed but not yet visible.
- The [Hider](position_hider.md), waiting. Possibly pretending to read something.

## Holds
- [The Egg](piece_the_egg.md)
- [The Unfound Egg](piece_the_unfound_egg.md)
- [The Agreement](piece_the_agreement.md)

## Offers
- Intimate terrain. Every hiding spot is inside a place the [Finder](position_finder.md) knows.
  The [Hider](position_hider.md) hides against that knowledge — or with it.
- Revelation of the relationship. A hiding spot chosen near a [Finder](position_finder.md)'s
  favourite object says something.
  The [Finder](position_finder.md) may notice. The [Hider](position_hider.md) knew they might.
- Contained search. The world is small and known.
  The [Finder](position_finder.md) can be systematic in a way the Garden does not allow.
- Rainy day operation. The House runs regardless of weather.
  The search does not depend on the season cooperating.

## Withheld
- Which rooms were included in the hiding and which were left out.
  The [Hider](position_hider.md) set the boundary. The [Finder](position_finder.md) may test the wrong edges.
- What the choice of hiding spot reveals about the [Hider](position_hider.md)'s
  knowledge of the [Finder](position_finder.md).
  This is present in the place. It only becomes legible at [The Settlement](process_the_settlement.md).
- The [Unfound Egg](piece_the_unfound_egg.md).
  The house holds it without comment.
  It may be found weeks later when something is moved.
  It will smell of chocolate or nothing, depending on the season.
