# Piece: The Agreement
## The contract the world runs on before anyone moves.

## Owner
- **Project:** Easter Egg World

## Place
Where the [Hider](position_hider.md) is, before [Hiding](process_hiding.md) begins.

## Load Bearing
Without it, [The Settlement](process_the_settlement.md) has no measure.
The found eggs cannot be evaluated as fair or earned.
The gap between the [Hider](position_hider.md)'s intention and the [Finder](position_finder.md)'s experience
becomes a dispute with no ground to stand on.
Remove it and the world does not run — it argues.

## Apparent
Two modes. One is in effect before the first egg is placed.

**Settled shares:** spoken aloud, usually by the [Hider](position_hider.md), before the Signal.
*Everyone gets the same.* Sometimes written on a small card.
Sometimes just understood. The children know which house they are in.

**Finder keeps:** also spoken. Shorter.
*What you find is yours.*
Sometimes accompanied by a starting signal —
a word, a whistle, a door opening.

In both cases: brief. Unambiguous.
The Agreement does not explain itself.

## Yearbook
Existed before this world had a name for it.
Every Easter hunt that ever ran carried one —
spoken or unspoken, fair or disputed.
The fights at The Settlement that had no resolution:
those were worlds that ran without one,
or with one that was heard differently by each position.
Travels from the [Hider](position_hider.md) into [Hiding](process_hiding.md),
through [The Search](process_the_search.md) and [Finding](process_finding.md),
and is called to account at [The Settlement](process_the_settlement.md).
Has never once been remembered the same way by all parties.
