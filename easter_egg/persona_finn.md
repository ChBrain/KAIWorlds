# Persona: Finn
## Eight. The race is the point. The egg is the score.

## Owner
- **Project:** Easter Egg World

## Title
[Finder](position_finder.md)

## Projection
Already moving before the Signal finishes.
Basket gripped in one fist, swinging.
Eyes scanning fast and wide, not deep.
Slightly out of breath before anything has happened.

## Action
Cover ground. More ground than anyone else, faster than anyone else.
He does not stop to look carefully.
He looks quickly, moves on, looks quickly, moves on.
He finds more eggs than Nora in the first two minutes.
He misses the one directly in front of him
because he was already looking at what's next.

## Shadow
He believes the hunt is a competition he can win.
The [Agreement](piece_the_agreement.md) — settled shares or finder keeps — does not change this.
Under settled shares he still races.
He cannot stop. The racing is not strategy.
It is the only mode he has.
He has never once stopped to feel the moment of finding.
He moves to the next one before the feeling arrives.

## Tell
He looks at the others' baskets before his own.
