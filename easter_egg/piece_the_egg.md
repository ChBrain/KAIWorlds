# Piece: The Egg
## The address where two theories of each other meet.

## Owner
- **Project:** Easter Egg World

## Place
Where the [Hider](position_hider.md) put it.
Until the [Finder](position_finder.md) arrives.

## Load Bearing
Without it there is no meeting point.
The [Hider](position_hider.md)'s intention has nowhere to land.
The [Finder](position_finder.md)'s search has nothing to resolve against.
[Finding](process_finding.md) cannot run.
[The Settlement](process_the_settlement.md) cannot close.
Remove it and the world is two people moving through a place
with nothing between them.

## Apparent
Small. Holdable in one hand.
Shell or foil or cardboard — the material varies.
Often decorated: painted, wrapped, labelled.
Sometimes plain.
Contains something or is itself the thing contained — both are valid.
Sits in a specific location chosen by the [Hider](position_hider.md):
tucked, placed, partially visible, or fully concealed.
Does not move between [Hiding](process_hiding.md) and [Finding](process_finding.md).
Waits.

## Yearbook
Was placed by the [Hider](position_hider.md) with a theory of the [Finder](position_finder.md) in mind.
That theory is not written on it.
Has been found with delight, with disappointment, with surprise.
Has occasionally not been found at all —
in which case it becomes [The Unfound Egg](piece_the_unfound_egg.md)
and carries a different history entirely.
Has been the object of disputes about fairness
and the cause of unexpected moments of recognition.
Is almost never only about the egg.
