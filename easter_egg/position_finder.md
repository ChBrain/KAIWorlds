# Position: Finder
## The one who reads the world the Hider made.

## Owner
- **Project:** Easter Egg World
- **Process:** [The Search](process_the_search.md)
- **Process:** [Finding](process_finding.md)
- **Process:** [The Settlement](process_the_settlement.md)

## Has
[The Agreement](piece_the_agreement.md) — received, not made.
A theory of the [Hider](position_hider.md).
The knowledge of what has been found so far.
No knowledge of what remains.

## Orders
Find what was hidden for you.

## Loses
The distance from their own childhood.

Children have none. The world is immediate, the finding is total, the joy arrives without management.
As they grow, the distance builds. It does not announce itself.
It arrives as restraint, as competence, as the knowledge of how things work.

The search collapses it.
Not because of the egg — because of the looking.
The state of not knowing, of moving through a world that is set against you,
of reaching without certainty —
it draws the [Finder](position_finder.md) back across the distance
to something they cannot name but recognise in their body.

A [Finder](position_finder.md) of four has no distance to cross. The search is just the world.
A [Finder](position_finder.md) of ten is already fighting the pull.
A [Finder](position_finder.md) of forty crosses back without expecting to.

[The Settlement](process_the_settlement.md) restores the distance.
The [Finder](position_finder.md) is grown again.
The world is the world again.
The [Unfound Egg](piece_the_unfound_egg.md) surfaces and must be accounted for.
The moment is over.
It was real.

## Drives
On the loss of the complete picture —
measures what was found against [The Agreement](piece_the_agreement.md).
Not against the world as it was.
Against the world as it was promised.
