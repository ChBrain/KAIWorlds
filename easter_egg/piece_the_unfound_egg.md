# Piece: The Unfound Egg
## The intention that outlasted the search.

## Owner
- **Project:** Easter Egg World
- **Piece:** [The Egg](piece_the_egg.md)

## Place
Still where the [Hider](position_hider.md) put it.
The world is no longer live.
Nobody is coming.

## Load Bearing
Without it, [The Settlement](process_the_settlement.md) has no remainder.
The [Hider](position_hider.md)'s complete picture has no gap.
The [Finder](position_finder.md)'s loss has no object.
Remove it and the world always closes cleanly —
which is not always true, and the architecture would be lying.
It is the piece that makes the world honest about its own incompleteness.

## Apparent
Identical to [The Egg](piece_the_egg.md) in every physical respect.
The difference is not visible.
It is still where it was placed.
It has not been touched since [Hiding](process_hiding.md).
If found after [The Settlement](process_the_settlement.md): usually by accident.
Sometimes weeks later.
Sometimes by someone not in the original search.

## Yearbook
Was placed with the same intention as every other [Egg](piece_the_egg.md).
The intention did not expire when the search ended.
It is still in the piece, unreceived.
Surfaces at [The Settlement](process_the_settlement.md) as an account the [Hider](position_hider.md) must give:
*I placed one here. Nobody found it.*
The [Finders](position_finder.md) discover what they moved past without seeing.
Has caused: laughter, mild guilt, renewed searching,
and at least once a significant argument about
whether the hiding was fair or the finding was careless.
The piece does not take sides.
It was just waiting.
