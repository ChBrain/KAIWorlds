# Process: The Search
## The live interval. The world is set. Nothing is found yet.

## Owner
- **Project:** Easter Egg World
- **Process:** [Hiding](process_hiding.md)
- **Position:** [Finder](position_finder.md)

## Initiated by
The Signal has sounded.
The [Finder](position_finder.md) is in the place.
The [Eggs](piece_the_egg.md) are placed but unknown.
[The Agreement](piece_the_agreement.md) is in effect.
The [Hider](position_hider.md) holds the complete picture and watches.

## Direction
Toward the first [Egg](piece_the_egg.md).
The [Finder](position_finder.md) reads the place
against their theory of the [Hider](position_hider.md).
The world is live. Everything is still possible.

## Lever
The [Finder](position_finder.md)'s attention meeting the place
the [Hider](position_hider.md) has set against them.

The carrying force: the [Finder](position_finder.md)'s theory of the [Hider](position_hider.md).
Where would *this* person hide something?
What do I know about them?
The search is not random — it is a reading.

The resistance: the place withholds.
Under **finder keeps**: other [Finders](position_finder.md) are in the same place,
running the same calculation faster or slower.
Attention splits. Speed becomes a variable.

Under **settled shares**: the [Finder](position_finder.md) may cooperate or may not.
[The Agreement](piece_the_agreement.md) guarantees the share.
It does not guarantee the effort.

[The Unfound Egg](piece_the_unfound_egg.md) exists as a permanent possibility
throughout this process.
The [Finder](position_finder.md) does not know which eggs exist.
Only the [Hider](position_hider.md) knows what has not been found.

## Echo
The first [Egg](piece_the_egg.md) is found.
[Finding](process_finding.md) runs.
