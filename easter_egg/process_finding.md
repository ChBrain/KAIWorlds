# Process: Finding
## One egg. Local relief. The search continues.

## Owner
- **Project:** Easter Egg World
- **Process:** [The Search](process_the_search.md)
- **Position:** [Finder](position_finder.md)
- **Piece:** [The Egg](piece_the_egg.md)

## Initiated by
The [Finder](position_finder.md) has located an [Egg](piece_the_egg.md).
The place has given something up.
[The Hider](position_hider.md) knows this has happened.
The search is not over.

## Direction
Toward the moment of contact —
the [Finder](position_finder.md) arriving at what the [Hider](position_hider.md) placed.
Two theories of each other, meeting at one object.

## Lever
The [Finder](position_finder.md)'s expectation meeting the [Egg](piece_the_egg.md) as found.

The carrying force: the excitement of location —
the [Finder](position_finder.md) was right about something.
The place, the [Hider](position_hider.md), the direction. Something worked.

The resistance: the gap.
What the [Hider](position_hider.md) placed is not always what the [Finder](position_finder.md) expected to find.
The [Egg](piece_the_egg.md) may delight. It may puzzle. It may disappoint.
The [Finder](position_finder.md) cannot ask the [Hider](position_hider.md) what was meant.
Not yet. The search is still live.

Relief is local. The full account is not yet settled.
Under **finder keeps**: the [Egg](piece_the_egg.md) is now held.
Under **settled shares**: the count shifts. Pressure redistributes across remaining [Finders](position_finder.md).

## Echo
One [Egg](piece_the_egg.md) is held.
The place holds fewer eggs than before.
The [Hider](position_hider.md)'s picture has one fewer unknown.

If eggs remain: [The Search](process_the_search.md) continues.
If this was the last egg: [The Settlement](process_the_settlement.md) runs.
