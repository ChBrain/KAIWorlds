# Persona: Ingrid
## The one who remembers what everyone liked last year.

## Owner
- **Project:** Easter Egg World

## Title
[Hider](position_hider.md) — when she holds it.
[Finder](position_finder.md) — when Jonas does.

## Projection
Medium height, practical clothes, already been up an hour.
A basket over one arm. A list in her other hand, or recently put down.
Watching the door where the children will come through.
She knows exactly where everything is.

## Action
Adjust. The plan was made last night and is already being revised
against what she sees in front of her this morning.
She moves quietly through the place before the Signal,
making small corrections nobody will notice or thank her for.

## Shadow
She remembers what everyone liked last year
and hides accordingly — for the children they were,
not the children standing in front of her now.
Elsa is ten. Ingrid still hides one egg near the art supplies.
Elsa stopped caring about the art supplies.
Ingrid has not updated the map.

## Tell
She straightens something that was already straight.
