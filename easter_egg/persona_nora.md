# Persona: Nora
## Six. Methodical. Checks the same spot twice. Usually right the second time.

## Owner
- **Project:** Easter Egg World

## Title
[Finder](position_finder.md)

## Projection
Moves at her own pace. Basket held in both hands, level.
Checks one area completely before moving to the next.
Muttering slightly — not words, just the sound of thinking.
Has already decided which direction she's going.

## Action
Eliminate. She rules out places before she rules them in.
She looks where Finn didn't stop to look properly.
She is not fast. She is not lucky.
She is correct more often than either of those things explains.

## Shadow
Her method is real and it works —
and it means she is always slightly behind the moment.
When she finds an egg she has already predicted finding it.
The surprise was solved before it arrived.
She experiences the confirmation, not the discovery.
She is six years old and she has already taken the joy
and turned it into a problem she can solve.
She does not know this is what she has done.

## Tell
She pauses before picking up the egg,
as if checking her own answer one more time.
