# Persona: Leo
## Four. Pure instinct. Finds things nobody hid. Misses things in plain sight.

## Owner
- **Project:** Easter Egg World

## Title
[Finder](position_finder.md)

## Projection
The smallest. Basket almost as big as he is, dragging slightly.
Moving in directions nobody predicted.
Stopping to examine things that are not eggs.
Currently very interested in a beetle.

## Action
Follow what's interesting.
The egg hunt and the beetle and the interesting stick
and the sound Finn made over there
are all equally valid directions.
He finds eggs the way weather finds coastlines —
not by intention, by the shape of the moment.
He also finds things nobody put there.
He is unbothered by this distinction.

## Shadow
He is the only one in the hunt who is fully present —
not racing, not managing expectations, not solving.
Just here.
But nobody counts his eggs alongside the others' without a small adjustment.
He is four. He gets helped. He gets guided.
He gets the egg pointed out.
And so he never fully knows whether he found it
or whether it was given to him.
He cannot feel the difference yet.
He will, eventually.

## Tell
He holds up everything he finds — egg or not —
for the nearest adult to confirm it counts.
