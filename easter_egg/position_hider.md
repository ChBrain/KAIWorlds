# Position: Hider
## The one who makes the world before anyone enters it.

## Owner
- **Project:** Easter Egg World
- **Process:** [Hiding](process_hiding.md)

## Has
Three timelines, running simultaneously before the first egg is placed.

The present: the complete picture.
Every [Egg](piece_the_egg.md) placed. Every location known.
[The Agreement](piece_the_agreement.md) — held before the first placement, carried through the Signal.

The future: the pre-run.
Every placement is rehearsed against a specific [Finder](position_finder.md).
The [Hider](position_hider.md) stands at the hiding spot and runs it forward —
who will come here, how they will move, what their face will do.
The joy is experienced before it happens.
This is the richest state in the world.

The past: the memory.
Beneath the rehearsal, without being named —
the [Hider](position_hider.md)'s own childhood finding.
What it felt like to not know. To look. To reach.
The distance from that moment is what the hiding is trying to cross.

## Orders
Set the world. Then release it.

## Loses
The guarantee.
The Signal is given and the world leaves their hands.
They were certain the picture they held — every placement, every intention —
would produce the joy they remember.
It may not.
The [Finder](position_finder.md) who finds with full, unguarded joy
gives something back the [Hider](position_hider.md) lost when they stopped being a Finder.
The [Finder](position_finder.md) who manages their joy, or races past it, or solves for it —
gives nothing back.
The [Hider](position_hider.md) cannot ask for it.
Cannot arrange it.
Cannot tell the difference in advance.

## Drives
On the loss of the guarantee —
watches for the moment a [Finder](position_finder.md) finds without protecting themselves.
Cannot intervene. Cannot correct. Cannot explain.
Holds the complete picture in silence
and waits to see if the joy comes back.
