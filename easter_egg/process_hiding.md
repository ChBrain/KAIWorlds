# Process: Hiding
## The world made before anyone enters it.

## Owner
- **Project:** Easter Egg World
- **Position:** [Hider](position_hider.md)
- **Piece:** [The Agreement](piece_the_agreement.md)

## Initiated by
[The Agreement](piece_the_agreement.md) exists.
The [Hider](position_hider.md) is alone in the place.
The [Finders](position_finder.md) are not yet released.
The world is empty and writable.

## Direction
Toward a place where every [Egg](piece_the_egg.md) is positioned
against the [Hider](position_hider.md)'s theory of the [Finder](position_finder.md) —
and the Signal is ready.
The world becomes set.

## Lever
The [Hider](position_hider.md)'s knowledge of the place and the Finders
meeting the resistance of [The Agreement](piece_the_agreement.md).

Every placement is a decision.
Under **settled shares**: the [Hider](position_hider.md) carries fairness across all placements simultaneously.
No single [Finder](position_finder.md) can be favoured.
The terrain must be read against every Finder at once.

Under **finder keeps**: the [Hider](position_hider.md) hides for sport.
Difficulty is the variable. Fairness is not required.
The resistance is the [Hider](position_hider.md)'s own restraint —
too easy and The Settlement means nothing;
too hard and [The Unfound Egg](piece_the_unfound_egg.md) multiplies.

The Signal is the last act.
The [Hider](position_hider.md) releases the [Finders](position_finder.md) into the world.
Control ends here.
What was placed cannot be unplaced.

## Echo
The place holds every [Egg](piece_the_egg.md).
The [Hider](position_hider.md) holds the complete picture — and nothing else.
The Signal has sounded.
[The Search](process_the_search.md) begins.
