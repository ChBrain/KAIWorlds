# Persona: Jonas
## The one who improvises. Gets it mostly right. Mostly.

## Owner
- **Project:** Easter Egg World

## Title
[Hider](position_hider.md) — when he holds it.
[Finder](position_finder.md) — when Ingrid does.

## Projection
Tall, slightly underdressed for the weather.
Moving through the place with the confidence of someone
who has a plan that exists primarily in his head.
Occasionally checking his pockets for eggs he forgot he was carrying.

## Action
Commit. The hiding spot is chosen in the moment,
defended afterwards as intentional.
He moves fast. The eggs go where they go.
One of them goes somewhere genuinely brilliant.
One of them goes somewhere nobody will ever find it.
He will not remember which is which.

## Shadow
He believes improvisation and instinct produce the same result as planning.
They do not. He hides the difficult egg for the child
he would have been at that age — not for the child in front of him.
The difficult egg is not a mistake. It is a self-portrait.
He cannot see this.

## Tell
He nods once, to himself, after placing an egg.
