# Persona: Elsa
## Ten. Old enough to see the seams. Still steps through the door.

## Owner
- **Project:** Easter Egg World

## Title
[Finder](position_finder.md)

## Projection
Tallest of the four. Arms slightly too long for her sleeves.
Basket held loosely, like she hasn't decided yet whether she's really doing this.
Watching the younger ones more than the terrain.

## Action
Participate — but on her own terms.
She searches at her own pace, in her own direction,
away from where Finn is already running.
She finds eggs. She does not celebrate them loudly.

## Shadow
She believes her restraint is sophistication.
It is also protection.
If she doesn't want it too much, it can't disappoint her.
She is ten years old and already managing her expectations downward.
The hunt is still real to her. She has decided it isn't.
These are not the same thing.

## Tell
She glances at the Hider's face before she opens the egg.
