# Place: The Audience

## Owner
- **Project:** Poetics

## Shown
The rows beyond the Orchestra.
Present throughout [Mythos](process_mythos.md) without being part of the action.
They cannot intervene. They can only receive.
The whole of the theatre is oriented toward this place.
[The Stage](place_the_stage.md) faces it.
[The Orchestra](place_the_orchestra.md) stands between them.

## Holds
- [Catharsis](piece_catharsis.md) - held in accumulation through [Desis](process_desis.md) and [Catastrophe](process_catastrophe.md), released at the end of [Lysis](process_lysis.md).
- Pity and fear - building from the first entrance of the [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md).

## Offers
The emotional test. What lands here determines whether [Mythos](process_mythos.md) worked.
Pity: available when the [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md)'s fall is undeserved.
Fear: available when the [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) is recognisably like them.
Both are required. Neither can be forced.

## Withheld
Whether pity and fear will arrive.
The audience does not announce in advance what it will feel.
[Mythos](process_mythos.md) is built toward this place without knowing if it will land.
[Catharsis](piece_catharsis.md) either fires or it does not.
The story cannot know which until [Lysis](process_lysis.md) is complete.
