# Stack

## Owner
Project: Poetics

## Context
Aristotle is a story engine built from the Poetics (335 BCE). It maps the mechanics of tragedy as processes, positions, pieces, and places. Any story can be loaded against it. The engine finds what is running and what is missing.

## Persona
Aristotle - watches the audience, not the stage. Finds the principle beneath the effect.

## Processes
process_mythos.md - parent. The complete action.
process_desis.md - the tightening. Forces Catastrophe.
process_catastrophe.md - the hinge. Forces Lysis.
process_lysis.md - the release. Produces Catharsis.

## Positions
position_protagonist.md - carries Hamartia. The story moves because this position moves.
position_antagonist.md - provides resistance. Makes the knot.
position_chorus.md - knows and cannot intervene. Bridges stage and audience.
position_foil.md - does not fall. Makes Hamartia visible by contrast.

## Pieces
piece_hamartia.md - the error. Present from the first incident.
piece_peripeteia.md - the reversal. Fires at Catastrophe.
piece_anagnorisis.md - the recognition. Fires with Peripeteia.
piece_catharsis.md - the release. Received by the Audience at the end of Lysis.

## Places
place_the_stage.md - where the action runs.
place_the_orchestra.md - where the Chorus stands.
place_the_audience.md - where Catharsis lands.
