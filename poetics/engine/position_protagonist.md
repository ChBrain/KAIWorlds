# Position: Protagonist
## The one whose fortune the story is.

## Owner
- **Project:** Poetics
- **Scope:** Universal
- **Process:** [Desis](process_desis.md)
- **Process:** [Catastrophe](process_catastrophe.md)
- **Process:** [Lysis](process_lysis.md)

## Has
The action. The story moves because this position moves.
[Hamartia](piece_hamartia.md) is present - carried without knowing it is carried.
The fortune is good at the start. This is required.
A villain falling produces no pity. A saint falling produces no fear.
This position is neither.

## Orders
Act. The plot does not wait.

## Loses
The belief that the situation is recoverable.
[Desis](process_desis.md) takes it incrementally.
[Catastrophe](process_catastrophe.md) takes the remainder.
What this position was certain it could resolve
is what the story was always about.

## Drives
On the loss -
forward. Into [Catastrophe](process_catastrophe.md).
Not because the position chooses to.
Because [Hamartia](piece_hamartia.md) has already chosen.
