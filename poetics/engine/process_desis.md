# Process: Desis

## Owner
- **Project:** Poetics
- **Process:** [Mythos](process_mythos.md)

## Initiated by
A [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) is in motion on [The Stage](place_the_stage.md). [Hamartia](piece_hamartia.md) is present - the error has been made, whether or not anyone knows it yet. The consequences have not yet arrived. The knot begins to tie.

## Direction
Toward the point where the knot cannot be tightened further. Every incident pulls the next one into necessity. The [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) acts. The action produces a consequence. The consequence produces the next action. The direction is one: forward, toward the moment nothing can be undone.

## Lever
Carrying force: [Hamartia](piece_hamartia.md) - the error already made, now working itself into the chain of consequences. Events follow by necessity and probability. Each one is the only thing that could follow from what came before.

Resistance: the [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md)'s belief that the situation is still recoverable. The actions taken in Desis are attempts to resolve. They tighten the knot instead. The resistance is genuine - the [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) is not wrong to try. The trying is what makes the knot.

## Echo
[Catastrophe](process_catastrophe.md) inherits a knot that cannot be undone. The [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) has arrived at the point where the situation veers - not by choice, but by the accumulated necessity of everything Desis produced. What changed: the [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) is no longer free.
