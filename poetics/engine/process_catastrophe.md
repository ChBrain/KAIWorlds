# Process: Catastrophe

## Owner
- **Project:** Poetics
- **Process:** [Mythos](process_mythos.md)

## Initiated by
[Desis](process_desis.md) has run. The knot is at its tightest. [Peripeteia](piece_peripeteia.md) arrives on [The Stage](place_the_stage.md) - the situation veers to its opposite. [Anagnorisis](piece_anagnorisis.md) arrives with it or because of it - the [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) passes from ignorance to knowledge. Both are present. The [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md)'s fate is sealed.

## Direction
Toward the change of fortune. Not toward understanding - toward the irreversible moment. The [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) does not choose this direction. The direction chooses the [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md).

## Lever
Carrying force: [Peripeteia](piece_peripeteia.md) and [Anagnorisis](piece_anagnorisis.md) fused - the reversal and the recognition arrive together for the greatest impact. What the [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) discovers is the reversal. The reversal is the discovery.

Resistance: the audience's pity and fear. They see what the [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) could not see. They feel what the [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) will now feel. The resistance is the gap between what the audience knows and what the [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) is only now learning. That gap closes here. It produces the emotional force that [Lysis](process_lysis.md) must release.

## Echo
[Lysis](process_lysis.md) inherits a [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) whose fortune has changed and who now knows why. The knot does not tighten further. What changed: the direction of the story reversed. Everything that follows is consequence, not cause.
