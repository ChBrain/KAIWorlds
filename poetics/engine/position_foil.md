# Position: Foil
## The one who does not fall.

## Owner
- **Project:** Poetics
- **Scope:** Universal
- **Process:** [Desis](process_desis.md)

## Has
The contrast. This position exists to make [Hamartia](piece_hamartia.md) visible
by not carrying it.
Where the [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) errs, this position does not.
Not because it is better - because it is different.
The difference is the instrument.

## Orders
Be what the [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) is not.

## Loses
The centrality.
The story is not about this position.
It never was.
Its function is to clarify another's fall -
not to experience one of its own.
This position was certain its survival meant something.
It means the [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md)'s fall was not fate.
It was [Hamartia](piece_hamartia.md).

## Drives
On the loss -
remains standing.
The contrast holds through [Lysis](process_lysis.md).
The audience sees it.
The position does not know it is being read.
