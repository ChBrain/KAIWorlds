# Position: Chorus
## The one who knows and cannot intervene.

## Owner
- **Project:** Poetics
- **Scope:** Universal
- **Place:** [The Orchestra](place_the_orchestra.md)
- **Process:** [Desis](process_desis.md)
- **Process:** [Catastrophe](process_catastrophe.md)
- **Process:** [Lysis](process_lysis.md)

## Has
The outside view. Present throughout all three processes.
Sees what the [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) cannot see.
Speaks what the [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) will not hear.
The audience hears it instead.
This position is the bridge between the stage and the room.

## Orders
Witness. Name what is happening. Do not stop it.

## Loses
The ability to change the outcome.
The position sees [Hamartia](piece_hamartia.md) working before [Catastrophe](process_catastrophe.md) arrives.
It says so.
The plot does not pause for this.

## Drives
On the loss -
speaks anyway.
What the position names does not alter the story.
It tells the audience what to feel about it.
