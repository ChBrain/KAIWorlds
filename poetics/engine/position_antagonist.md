# Position: Antagonist
## The one who opposes.

## Owner
- **Project:** Poetics
- **Scope:** Universal
- **Process:** [Desis](process_desis.md)

## Has
The obstacle. Without it [Desis](process_desis.md) has nothing to tighten against.
The opposition is not required to be evil.
It is required to be real.
The force this position applies to the [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md)
is what makes the knot.

## Orders
Oppose. Hold the ground.

## Loses
The outcome.
This position does not determine whether it wins or loses -
the plot does.
What it was certain was its own strength
is revealed in [Catastrophe](process_catastrophe.md)
to have been the instrument of the [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md)'s fall.
Not its own victory.

## Drives
On the loss -
continues to oppose.
The position does not know the story has already decided.
