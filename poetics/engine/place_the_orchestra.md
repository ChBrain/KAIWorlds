# Place: The Orchestra

## Owner
- **Project:** Poetics

## Shown
The circular space between the stage and the audience.
The [Chorus](position_chorus.md) occupies it throughout.
Neither on stage with the action nor in the audience receiving it.
The position between.

## Holds
- [Chorus](position_chorus.md) - present through all three processes.

## Offers
The view that is both inside and outside the action.
The [Chorus](position_chorus.md) sees [Hamartia](piece_hamartia.md) operating.
Names it. Comments on it. Cannot stop it.
What it offers the audience: the emotional frame.
Pity and fear arrive here before they are released as [Catharsis](piece_catharsis.md).

## Withheld
The ability to intervene.
The Orchestra is adjacent to the stage.
The distance is deliberate.
What the [Chorus](position_chorus.md) knows and what the [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) knows
never fully meet.
The gap between them is where the tragedy lives.
