# Place: The Stage

## Owner
- **Project:** Poetics

## Shown
The acting space. Raised. Defined.
[Protagonist](position_protagonist.md), [Antagonist](position_antagonist.md), [Foil](position_foil.md) enter and exit here.
The action is visible. The audience cannot intervene.
One location. The whole of [Mythos](process_mythos.md) occurs within it.

## Holds
- [Hamartia](piece_hamartia.md) - carried in by the [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) from the first entrance.
- [Peripeteia](piece_peripeteia.md) and [Anagnorisis](piece_anagnorisis.md) - both fire here at [Catastrophe](process_catastrophe.md).
- The action. Every incident from [Desis](process_desis.md) through [Lysis](process_lysis.md).

## Offers
The visibility of the action to the audience.
What happens here cannot be hidden.
The [Chorus](position_chorus.md) sees it from the Orchestra.
The audience sees it from beyond.
The [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) cannot see what both of them see.

## Withheld
The outcome. The stage holds the action without revealing where it leads.
Each incident follows necessarily from the last.
The destination is not visible until [Catastrophe](process_catastrophe.md) fires.
The [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) does not know this.
The audience suspects it.
Only [Lysis](process_lysis.md) confirms it.
