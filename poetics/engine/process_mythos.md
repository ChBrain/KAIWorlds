# Process: Mythos

## Owner
- **Project:** Poetics

## Initiated by
A serious, complete action of sufficient magnitude. A [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) is present. [Hamartia](piece_hamartia.md) is present. [The Stage](place_the_stage.md) is occupied. The story has a beginning that does not follow from anything prior. The first incident is the only moment in the process that is not caused by something inside the story.

## Direction
Toward a complete action with a beginning, middle, and end - ordered by cause and effect. Unity of action: every incident is connected to the one before it and the one after it by necessity and probability. The process does not tolerate the episodic.

## Lever
Carrying force: the chain of necessity. Each sub-process forces the next. [Desis](process_desis.md) forces [Catastrophe](process_catastrophe.md). [Catastrophe](process_catastrophe.md) forces [Lysis](process_lysis.md). Remove the chain and what remains is incident without consequence.

Resistance: the temptation to resolve by chance - the *deus ex machina*. A resolution that does not arise from the logic of what came before violates the process. The audience feels it. The process collapses into spectacle.

## Echo
A complete action. Pity and fear produced and released as [Catharsis](piece_catharsis.md) in the audience. What changed: the [Protagonist](position_protagonist.md) has moved from one state of fortune to its opposite. The movement was inevitable from the first incident. The audience now knows why.
