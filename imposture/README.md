# Imposture

A KAI World built on Molière's Le Malade Imaginaire (1673).

The world runs on one engine: a false belief held with complete conviction,
and the positions that form around it.
Load any story where a character's certainty organises everything around them.
Find where the belief holds. Find where it breaks.

---

## Components

**Processes (4)**
- `process_imposture.md` - parent: the full comic engine
- `process_quiproquo.md` - the knot tightening
- `process_deguisement.md` - the mechanisms converging
- `process_intronisation.md` - the resolution that absorbs

**Positions (5)**
- `position_le_malade.md` - the one whose false belief organises everything
- `position_le_raisonneur.md` - the one who argues from outside and fails
- `position_la_fourbe.md` - the one who enters the belief from inside
- `position_l_imposteur.md` - the one whose performance the belief requires
- `position_l_amoureuse.md` - the one whose fortune depends on the belief breaking

**Pieces (3)**
- `piece_la_croyance.md` - the false certainty: load-bearing
- `piece_l_ordonnance.md` - the written authority that makes the belief official
- `piece_le_testament.md` - the document that reveals the second imposture

**Places (2)**
- `place_la_chambre.md` - the room where the belief controls everything
- `place_la_ceremonie.md` - the ritual space where the belief is institutionalised

**Personas (5)**
- `persona_argan.md` - Le Malade
- `persona_toinette.md` - La Fourbe
- `persona_beralde.md` - Le Raisonneur
- `persona_beline.md` - L'Imposteur
- `persona_angelique.md` - L'Amoureuse

---

## Deploy

1. Clone or download this repository
2. Open `engine/instructions.md` in your KAI World runner
3. Bring a story. The engine will find the mechanism.
