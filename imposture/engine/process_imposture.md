# Process: Imposture

## Owner
- **Project:** Imposture

## Initiated by
A [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) alone in a room, counting bills for treatments he does not need.
[La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) is already running before the first scene begins.
A household is organised around it.
The story has a beginning that does not follow from anything prior.

## Direction
Toward the moment the imposture can no longer hold its shape.
Not toward collapse - toward transformation.
The belief does not disappear.
It finds a new body.

## Lever
Carrying force: [La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) held with complete conviction.
Every action [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) takes is logical given what he believes.
The logic is the trap.

Resistance: every position that can see what [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) cannot.
[Le Raisonneur](position_le_raisonneur.md). [La Fourbe](position_la_fourbe.md). [L'Amoureuse](position_l_amoureuse.md).
The gap between what they see and what [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) sees
is where the comedy lives.

## Echo
A complete comic action. Pity replaced by laughter.
The audience sees clearly what the protagonist cannot.
What changed: [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) has moved from one form of imposture to another.
The movement was inevitable from the first bill counted.
The audience now knows why.
