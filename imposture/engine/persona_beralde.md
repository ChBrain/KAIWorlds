# Persona: Béralde

## Owner
- **Project:** Imposture

## Title
[Le Raisonneur](position_le_raisonneur.md)

## Projection
A man who arrives with the argument already formed.
He sits across from [Argan](persona_argan.md) in [La Chambre du Malade](place_la_chambre.md)
and speaks in complete sentences.
He is calm. He expects to be heard.
He has been in this room before and left without being heard
and has returned with the same argument refined.

## Action
Argues. Names what [L'Imposteur](position_l_imposteur.md) is.
Names what [La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) costs.
Names what the marriage would do to [L'Amoureuse](position_l_amoureuse.md).
Each name is precise and correct and changes nothing.

## Shadow
He is certain that clarity, delivered with patience, will eventually land.
It will not land.
[La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) does not process argument.
It processes argument as symptom.
Every objection Béralde raises is absorbed by [Argan](persona_argan.md)
as evidence that Béralde does not understand how serious the illness is.
Béralde cannot see this mechanism
because he cannot imagine a mind that works this way.
He keeps refining the argument.

## Tell
When [Argan](persona_argan.md) does not respond to a point,
Béralde makes it again more slowly.
