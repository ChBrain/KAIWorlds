# Piece: Le Testament

## Owner
- **Project:** Imposture
- **Process:** [Déguisement](process_deguisement.md)

## Place
[La Chambre du Malade](place_la_chambre.md).

## Load Bearing
Remove it and [L'Imposteur](position_l_imposteur.md) has no stake.
Without the testament, [L'Imposteur](position_l_imposteur.md) is merely an unsympathetic presence.
With it she is an active force working [La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) for profit.
Remove it and the faked death scene loses its revelation:
the joy over the body requires the inheritance to be real.
Without the testament [La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) is merely a private folly.
With it the belief has consequences that reach beyond [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md).

## Apparent
A legal document. Signed. Witnessed.
Drawn up at [L'Imposteur](position_l_imposteur.md)'s request while [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) believes he is dying.
Names her as primary beneficiary.
Excludes the daughters.
Present in [La Chambre du Malade](place_la_chambre.md) before [Déguisement](process_deguisement.md) begins.
Not visible until the body is still.

## Yearbook
Le Testament is the piece that reveals the second imposture running beneath the first.
[La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) is the visible imposture.
[L'Imposteur](position_l_imposteur.md)'s performance of devotion is the hidden one.
Le Testament is where they intersect.
[La Fourbe](position_la_fourbe.md) does not need to find it.
She already knows it is there.
The faked death does not expose the document.
It exposes the position that needs it.
