# Persona: Béline

## Owner
- **Project:** Imposture

## Title
[L'Imposteur](position_l_imposteur.md)

## Projection
She enters softly.
Her voice drops when she speaks to [Argan](persona_argan.md).
She uses diminutives. Mon coeur. Mon fils. Pauvre petit mari.
She positions herself between [Argan](persona_argan.md) and the door
so that she is the last thing he sees when someone leaves.
[Le Testament](piece_le_testament.md) is already signed.
She does not mention it.

## Action
Performs devotion.
Each performance is calibrated to what [Argan](persona_argan.md) needs in the moment:
tenderness when he is frightened, agreement when he is angry,
concern when he is counting the bills.
The calibration is continuous and invisible.

## Shadow
She is certain the performance is sustainable
because [Argan](persona_argan.md) cannot see past it.
She has confused his blindness with her safety.
They are not the same thing.
[La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) protects her only while [Argan](persona_argan.md) is alive and convinced.
The faked death is not in her calculation.
She has no plan for the body that sits up.

## Tell
When [Argan](persona_argan.md) says something that requires a response she has not prepared,
she touches his hand before she speaks.
