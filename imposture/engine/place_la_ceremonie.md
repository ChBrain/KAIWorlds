# Place: La Cérémonie

## Owner
- **Project:** Imposture

## Shown
A ritual space assembled inside [La Chambre du Malade](place_la_chambre.md).
Robes. Latin. Doctors in procession.
The furniture of medicine made theatrical.
[L'Imposteur](position_l_imposteur.md) has exited.
What remains is the form without the function.
[Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) sits at the centre.
The chair is the same chair.

## Holds
- [La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) - present in its transformed state
- [L'Ordonnance](piece_l_ordonnance.md) - the vocabulary of this place, now spoken over [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) rather than handed to him

## Offers
The resolution that does not cure.
This place makes one thing available that no other place in the play offers:
the possibility of [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) becoming [L'Imposteur](position_l_imposteur.md).
Not recovery. Absorption.
[La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) finds its final form here.
[La Fourbe](position_la_fourbe.md) does not need to enter.
The ceremony runs without her.

## Withheld
The irony. This place will not name what it is doing.
The Latin continues. The robes are worn in earnest.
[Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) receives the title with complete sincerity.
The place withholds the joke from the one at its centre.
The audience holds it instead.
That gap is what [Intronisation](process_intronisation.md) runs on.
