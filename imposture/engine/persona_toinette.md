# Persona: Toinette

## Owner
- **Project:** Imposture

## Title
[La Fourbe](position_la_fourbe.md)

## Projection
She is already in [La Chambre du Malade](place_la_chambre.md) when the scene begins.
She has heard this before.
She does not lean against anything.
She stands where she can move in any direction.
Her face does not confirm what she thinks.

## Action
Enters [La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) from inside.
Where others argue, she performs.
She puts on the coat.
She speaks the Latin of [L'Ordonnance](piece_l_ordonnance.md).
She does not break character until the work is done.

## Shadow
She is certain she is helping.
She is. But the help is a demonstration of her own clarity,
not a gift to the person she is helping.
She already knew what the disguise would find.
She dressed up to confirm what she already knew.
The help and the proof are the same move.
She does not know they are the same move.

## Tell
When someone in the room says something she already knows,
she moves to the next thing before they finish the sentence.
