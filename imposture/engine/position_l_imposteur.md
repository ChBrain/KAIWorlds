# Position: L'Imposteur

## Owner
- **Project:** Imposture
- **Process:** [Quiproquo](process_quiproquo.md)
- **Process:** [Déguisement](process_deguisement.md)

## Has
The performance. Latin. Black robes. The vocabulary of authority.
[Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) needs [La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) confirmed.
This position confirms it - for a fee.
The imposture of illness and the imposture of medicine
require each other to survive.

## Orders
Perform. Maintain the vocabulary. Do not be caught.

## Loses
The performance when the audience changes.
This position was certain it controlled [La Chambre du Malade](place_la_chambre.md).
[La Fourbe](position_la_fourbe.md) enters the room in the same coat.
The room does not notice the difference.
That is the exposure.

## Drives
On the loss: exits.
The position cannot survive being made visible.
It leaves before [Intronisation](process_intronisation.md) completes.
What it built is what crowns [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) in its place.
