# Place: La Chambre du Malade

## Owner
- **Project:** Imposture

## Shown
A bourgeois bedroom. A chair at the centre.
[Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) does not leave it.
The household enters and exits around him.
[L'Ordonnance](piece_l_ordonnance.md) arrives here from outside.
[Le Testament](piece_le_testament.md) was signed here.
Every position passes through this room
on [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md)'s terms.

## Holds
- [La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) - present throughout all three processes
- [L'Ordonnance](piece_l_ordonnance.md) - delivered and received here in [Quiproquo](process_quiproquo.md)
- [Le Testament](piece_le_testament.md) - present but not visible until [Déguisement](process_deguisement.md)

## Offers
The only ground [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) controls.
Every position that enters must negotiate with [La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) to be heard.
[Le Raisonneur](position_le_raisonneur.md) argues here and fails here.
[La Fourbe](position_la_fourbe.md) enters here in disguise and succeeds.
[L'Imposteur](position_l_imposteur.md) performs here until the performance is made visible.
The room offers leverage to anyone willing to work within [La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md)
and offers nothing to anyone who argues against it.

## Withheld
The truth of who benefits from [La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md).
[Le Testament](piece_le_testament.md) is present in the room before [Déguisement](process_deguisement.md) begins.
The room will not release it until the body is still.
What [L'Amoureuse](position_l_amoureuse.md) needs to know is already here.
The room keeps it until the right pressure arrives.
