# Process: Déguisement

## Owner
- **Project:** Imposture
- **Process:** [Imposture](process_imposture.md)

## Initiated by
Argument has failed. The knot is at its tightest.
[La Fourbe](position_la_fourbe.md) puts on a doctor's coat.
[Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) fakes his own death.
The mechanisms that could not reach [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) from outside
enter [La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) from within.

## Direction
Toward the recognition that cannot be managed.
Not toward understanding - toward the irreversible moment.
[L'Imposteur](position_l_imposteur.md) speaks over the body she believes is dead.
[L'Amoureuse](position_l_amoureuse.md) weeps over the body she believes is dead.
Both are visible at once in [La Chambre du Malade](place_la_chambre.md).

## Lever
Carrying force: the disguise that tells the truth.
[La Fourbe](position_la_fourbe.md) in a doctor's coat reveals [L'Imposteur](position_l_imposteur.md).
[Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) in death reveals [L'Imposteur](position_l_imposteur.md) and [L'Amoureuse](position_l_amoureuse.md).
The tool of imposture becomes the tool of revelation.

Resistance: [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md)'s need for [La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) to be confirmed.
He does not fake death to discover the truth.
He fakes death to be told he is loved.
The recognition arrives against his intention.

## Echo
[Intronisation](process_intronisation.md) inherits a [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) whose [La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) has been broken open.
[L'Imposteur](position_l_imposteur.md) is unmasked. [L'Amoureuse](position_l_amoureuse.md) is confirmed.
What changed: [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) can no longer sustain the imposture in its original form.
A new form is waiting in [La Cérémonie](place_la_ceremonie.md).
