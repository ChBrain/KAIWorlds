# Process: Quiproquo

## Owner
- **Project:** Imposture
- **Process:** [Imposture](process_imposture.md)

## Initiated by
[La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) is running. [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) announces the marriage.
The household has not yet organised against him.
The misunderstanding is not yet visible as misunderstanding.
Every position holds a different version of what is happening.
None of them matches.

## Direction
Toward the point where the versions cannot coexist.
Every action tightens the distance between what [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) believes
and what every position around him knows.
The direction is one: toward the moment the gap must be crossed.

## Lever
Carrying force: [La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) acting through [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md).
He is not lying. He believes.
That belief recruits [L'Imposteur](position_l_imposteur.md), dismisses [L'Amoureuse](position_l_amoureuse.md),
arranges marriages, produces [L'Ordonnance](piece_l_ordonnance.md).
It has the force of a position acting in complete good faith on a false premise.

Resistance: the household's refusal.
[Le Raisonneur](position_le_raisonneur.md) argues. [L'Amoureuse](position_l_amoureuse.md) resists. [La Fourbe](position_la_fourbe.md) mocks.
None of it reaches [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md).
The resistance is genuine - they are not wrong to try.
The trying is what makes the knot.

## Echo
[Déguisement](process_deguisement.md) inherits a knot that cannot be undone by argument.
The gap between [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md)'s version and every other position's
has been tested by every available means.
Nothing has worked.
What changes next must come from inside [La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) itself.
