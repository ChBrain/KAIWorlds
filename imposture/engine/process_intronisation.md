# Process: Intronisation

## Owner
- **Project:** Imposture
- **Process:** [Imposture](process_imposture.md)

## Initiated by
[Déguisement](process_deguisement.md) has run. [La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) cannot hold its shape.
[Le Raisonneur](position_le_raisonneur.md) offers the solution: [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) need not find a doctor.
[Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) can become one.
[La Cérémonie](place_la_ceremonie.md) begins.

## Direction
Toward closure that does not resolve [La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) - that crowns it.
[Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) does not stop believing.
He becomes [La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md)'s physician.
The imposture is complete.

## Lever
Carrying force: the burlesque ceremony in [La Cérémonie](place_la_ceremonie.md).
Latin spoken over a man who knows no Latin.
A title conferred that means nothing and changes everything.
The ritual form of [L'Ordonnance](piece_l_ordonnance.md) now spoken over [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md)
rather than handed to him.

Resistance: the audience's knowledge.
They see exactly what is happening.
[Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) does not.
The laughter is the gap between his satisfaction and their clarity.
That gap does not close. It is the resolution.

## Echo
The comedy is whole. The imposture has not ended - it has been institutionalised.
What changed: [Le Malade](position_le_malade.md) is no longer at the mercy of [La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md).
He is its master. He is its proof.
Nothing was cured. Everything was resolved.
