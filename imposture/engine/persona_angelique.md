# Persona: Angélique

## Owner
- **Project:** Imposture

## Title
[L'Amoureuse](position_l_amoureuse.md)

## Projection
She is waiting when the scene begins.
Not passive - positioned.
She knows what [Argan](persona_argan.md) is about to say
before he says it
and has already decided she will not comply.
Her stillness is not calm. It is readiness.

## Action
Holds. Refuses the marriage.
Refuses without a mechanism to make the refusal stick.
The refusal is genuine and costs her something every time
and she makes it again.

## Shadow
She is certain her love for Cléante is visible to [Argan](persona_argan.md)
and that visibility will move him.
It is visible. It does not move him.
[La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) processes her love as disobedience.
Her father sees her feeling clearly
and uses it as the reason the marriage must happen quickly,
before she acts on it.
She cannot see that her transparency is the instrument of her constraint.

## Tell
When [Argan](persona_argan.md) speaks Cléante's name dismissively,
she goes still in a way that is different from her waiting stillness.
The waiting is forward. This is held.
