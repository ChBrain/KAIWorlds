# Persona: Argan

## Owner
- **Project:** Imposture

## Title
[Le Malade](position_le_malade.md)

## Projection
A man in a chair at the centre of [La Chambre du Malade](place_la_chambre.md).
He does not stand if he can avoid it.
When he stands it is to pursue someone who has made him angry
and he sits down again before he catches them.
He counts. Bills, treatments, days since the last purge.
The counting is continuous and precise.
[La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md) runs in him like a clock.

## Action
Arranges. The marriage, the doctors, the household schedule.
Every arrangement is a further proof that the illness is real.
A well man would not need to arrange so carefully.

## Shadow
He believes the arrangements are care.
They are control.
The household organised around the illness
is the illness organised around the household.
He cannot see that what he calls attention
is obedience he has extracted.
He is certain the love is genuine
because the compliance looks like love.

## Tell
When someone tries to leave [La Chambre du Malade](place_la_chambre.md) without his permission,
he reaches for the bell.
