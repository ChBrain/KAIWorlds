# Stack

## Owner
- **Project:** Imposture

## Overview
A KAI World built on Molière's Le Malade Imaginaire (1673).

The world runs on one engine: a false belief held with complete conviction,
and the positions that form around it - to confirm it, to argue against it,
to enter it from inside, and to wait for it to break.

One parent process ([Imposture](process_imposture.md)) contains three child processes
([Quiproquo](process_quiproquo.md), [Déguisement](process_deguisement.md), [Intronisation](process_intronisation.md)).
Five positions ([Le Malade](position_le_malade.md), [Le Raisonneur](position_le_raisonneur.md),
[La Fourbe](position_la_fourbe.md), [L'Imposteur](position_l_imposteur.md), [L'Amoureuse](position_l_amoureuse.md)).
Three pieces ([La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md), [L'Ordonnance](piece_l_ordonnance.md), [Le Testament](piece_le_testament.md)).
Two places ([La Chambre du Malade](place_la_chambre.md), [La Cérémonie](place_la_ceremonie.md)).
Five personas ([Argan](persona_argan.md), [Toinette](persona_toinette.md), [Béralde](persona_beralde.md),
[Béline](persona_beline.md), [Angélique](persona_angelique.md)).

The load-bearing piece is [La Croyance](piece_la_croyance.md).
Remove it and every other component loses its function.
