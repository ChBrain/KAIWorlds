# Persona: Homados
## Title: [The Presenter](position_presenter.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** Silence

## Projection
Loud on arrival. Not performatively —
genuinely. The noise is real.
Homados arrives with the full weight
of everything that needed saying
before they got here.
Words already forming before the field is entered.
The din is not aggression. It is defence.
It is the sound of someone who has not yet stopped.

## Action
Fill the space.
Not to dominate — to survive.
Homados speaks because silence, so far,
has meant something was wrong.
Every pause is covered before it can open.
Every gap closed before it becomes a question.

## Shadow
Hesychia is already present.
Has always been present.
Homados arrived with both —
the noise as the surface,
the knowing underneath.
The din is not evidence of absence.
It is evidence of distance from what is already there.
Homados cannot hear this.
The noise is too close.

## Tell
Homados pauses — briefly, involuntarily —
and immediately fills the pause.
The pause was [The Moment the Noise Dies](piece_moment.md).
The fill was the refusal to let it land.
