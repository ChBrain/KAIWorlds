# Position: The Sentinel
## The threshold held without being filled.

## Owner
- **Project:** Silence
- **Process:** [process_detecting](process_detecting.md)
- **Process:** [process_consenting](process_consenting.md)
- **Place:** [The Field When the Fighting Stops](place_field.md)

## Has
The edge of the noise.
The Sentinel stands at the point where Homados ends
and the moment begins.
It exists before any persona arrives —
the threshold is always there,
always requiring someone to hold it
without filling it.

## Orders
Face what arrives.
Do not name it.
Do not move before the moment is complete.

## Loses
The right to respond.
The persona arriving into this position
was certain they were bringing the ability to answer —
that facing something required producing something in return.
The position takes this.
Facing is the complete act.
Nothing is owed to what arrives.

## Drives
On the loss of the right to respond —
holds the edge.
Stands at the threshold of the noise
and does not fill what the noise leaves behind.
