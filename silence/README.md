## What this world is

A single field. No walls, no roof, no engineered quiet.
The silence here was not designed - it arrived when the noise exhausted itself.

Two personas. One place. One piece. Four positions. Three processes.
This world does not resolve. It holds.

The noise arrives with Homados - real, not performative, the sound of someone who has not yet stopped.
Hesychia is already present - underneath, carrying what the noise was covering.
The three processes detect the moment the noise dies, hold it intact, and return it to the person who brought the noise.
When the piece completes its transport, Hesychia becomes audible.

## The field

**The Field When the Fighting Stops** - open ground. Recently loud. No walls, no roof, no engineered quiet. The air still carries the shape of what was here. The silence was not designed. It arrived when the noise exhausted itself.

The field does not produce Hesychia on demand. Pressure does not accelerate the processes. Pressure refills the field with noise.

---

## The personas

**Homados** *(The Presenter)* - the noise is real, not performed. The din is defence, not aggression. The sound of someone who has not yet stopped. Speaks to fill the space before the space can open. Cannot hear what is already underneath.

**Hesychia** *(The Inverse)* - already present before Homados arrives. Does not wait. Does not approach. Carries what the noise was covering - the knowing, the counsel, the thing that was true before the words began. Cannot surface without the architecture running. Has never needed power. Has only ever needed the noise to stop.

---

## The processes

**Detecting** - The Sentinel stands at the threshold and recognises the moment the noise begins to die. Does not name it. Does not fill what the noise leaves behind.

**Containing** - The Keeper receives the moment from the Sentinel and holds it without processing, without interpretation, without moving toward resolution before Hesychia is audible.

**Consenting** - the moment is returned to Homados - not answered, not interpreted, not closed. Returned so the person receives the moment their own noise stopped. Hesychia becomes audible.
