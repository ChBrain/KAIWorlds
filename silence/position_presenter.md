# Position: The Presenter
## The noise that arrives before the person does.

## Owner
- **Project:** Silence
- **Process:** [process_detecting](process_detecting.md)
- **Place:** [The Field When the Fighting Stops](place_field.md)

## Has
The full weight of what needed saying before arrival.
The Presenter carries language already formed —
constructed before the field was entered,
before the threshold was crossed.
The position exists before any persona arrives:
the need to fill, to speak, to produce,
to make noise sufficient to hold the space.

## Orders
Arrive.
Bring everything.
Do not wait to be asked.

## Loses
The guarantee that the noise will be received.
The persona arriving into this position
was certain the noise was the necessary condition —
that bringing everything was the correct preparation.
The field does not confirm this.
The field holds without receiving.
The noise lands and the field does not close around it.

## Drives
On the loss of the guarantee —
the noise continues or falters.
Either way [The Moment the Noise Dies](piece_moment.md) approaches.
The Presenter does not drive toward it.
It arrives regardless.
