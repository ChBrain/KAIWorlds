# Process: Detecting
## The moment the noise dies is recognised.

## Owner
- **Project:** Silence
- **Position:** [The Sentinel](position_sentinel.md)
- **Place:** [The Field When the Fighting Stops](place_field.md)

## Initiated by
[Homados](persona_homados.md) arrives.
The noise is present and full.
[The Sentinel](position_sentinel.md) is at the threshold.
[The Field When the Fighting Stops](place_field.md) is open.
The process begins the moment the noise begins to die —
not before, not after.
The entry condition is the change, not the state.

## Direction
Toward [The Moment the Noise Dies](piece_moment.md).
[The Sentinel](position_sentinel.md) faces what arrives without filling it.
Does not name what it recognises.
Does not move toward [Hesychia](persona_hesychia.md) prematurely.
Holds the threshold until the moment is complete
and the Piece is available to [process_containing](process_containing.md).

## Lever
**Carrying force** — [The Moment the Noise Dies](piece_moment.md) arriving in [The Field](place_field.md).
The event is real. The Sentinel receives it.

**Resistance** — the instinct to close.
To name what is happening.
To fill the space Homados has left.
To move before the moment is complete.
The Sentinel holds against this.
Recognition without intervention.
The moment is not yet available to [The Keeper](position_keeper.md).

## Echo
[The Moment the Noise Dies](piece_moment.md) is recognised.
[The Keeper](position_keeper.md) can receive it.
[process_containing](process_containing.md) begins.
