# Piece: The Moment the Noise Dies
## The threshold between Homados and Hesychia — not silence, not noise, but the turn.

## Owner
- **Project:** Silence
- **Place:** [The Field When the Fighting Stops](place_field.md)

## Place
[The Field When the Fighting Stops](place_field.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it the three processes have nothing to transport.
[process_detecting](process_detecting.md) has no event to recognise.
[process_containing](process_containing.md) has nothing to hold.
[process_consenting](process_consenting.md) has nothing to return.
Remove it and the world produces gesture, not technique.

## Apparent
The last sound before the field goes quiet.
A breath cut short. A weapon lowered.
The specific texture of air after sustained noise —
not yet silent, no longer loud.
No duration. No length.
A threshold with a physical edge.

## Yearbook
Begins with [Homados](persona_homados.md) —
carried inside the noise,
invisible while the noise is full.

Arrives in [The Field When the Fighting Stops](place_field.md)
the moment the noise begins to die.
[process_detecting](process_detecting.md) receives it here.
[The Sentinel](position_sentinel.md) recognises it without naming it.

Passes to [The Keeper](position_keeper.md).
Held intact through [process_containing](process_containing.md).
Not interpreted. Not resolved.

Returns to [Homados](persona_homados.md) through [process_consenting](process_consenting.md).
The person receives the moment their own noise stopped.
[Hesychia](persona_hesychia.md) becomes audible.
The piece has completed its transport.
