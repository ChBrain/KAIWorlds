# Place: The Field When the Fighting Stops
## Where noise exhausts itself and Hesychia becomes audible.

## Owner
- **Project:** Silence

## Shown
Open ground. Recently loud.
The air still carries the shape of what was here.
[The Moment the Noise Dies](piece_moment.md) present but not yet named.
No walls. No roof. No engineered quiet.
The silence here was not designed —
it arrived when the noise exhausted itself.

## Holds
[The Moment the Noise Dies](piece_moment.md)

## Offers
The condition in which [Homados](persona_homados.md) can hear [Hesychia](persona_hesychia.md).
Not guaranteed. Not forced.
Available — if the processes run correctly
and both positions hold.
The field offers the possibility of the turn.
What the person does with it
is not the field's work.

## Withheld
The turn itself.
The field will not produce [Hesychia](persona_hesychia.md) on demand.
It will not shorten the hold
because the noise was brief
or the person is impatient.
The field releases [The Moment the Noise Dies](piece_moment.md)
only when [process_detecting](process_detecting.md) has run completely.
Pressure does not accelerate this.
Pressure refills the field with noise.
