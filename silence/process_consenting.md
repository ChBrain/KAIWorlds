# Process: Consenting
## The moment the noise dies is returned — unopposed, opened further.

## Owner
- **Project:** Silence
- **Position:** [The Sentinel](position_sentinel.md)
- **Position:** [The Keeper](position_keeper.md)
- **Place:** [The Field When the Fighting Stops](place_field.md)

## Initiated by
[process_containing](process_containing.md) has run.
[The Moment the Noise Dies](piece_moment.md) is held intact.
Both [The Sentinel](position_sentinel.md) and [The Keeper](position_keeper.md) are present.
The process begins when the Keeper judges the moment is ready to return —
not before the field has held long enough,
not so long after that the field fills again.

## Direction
Toward [Homados](persona_homados.md) — not with resolution but with opening.
[The Moment the Noise Dies](piece_moment.md) is returned to the person who brought the noise.
Not answered. Not interpreted. Not closed.
Returned so the person receives the moment their own noise stopped.

## Lever
**Carrying force** — [The Moment the Noise Dies](piece_moment.md) moving back through [The Field](place_field.md).
The Sentinel reads when. The Keeper releases what.
Together they produce the return.

**Resistance** — the instinct to close on the way back.
To add to the moment. To explain it.
To resolve what the silence opened rather than let it open further.
Both positions hold against this simultaneously.
The return is not an answer.

## Echo
[The Moment the Noise Dies](piece_moment.md) is with [Homados](persona_homados.md).
[Hesychia](persona_hesychia.md) is audible.
The field has done its work.
The world has transported silence.
