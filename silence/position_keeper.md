# Position: The Keeper
## What is held does not spill and does not close.

## Owner
- **Project:** Silence
- **Process:** [process_containing](process_containing.md)
- **Process:** [process_consenting](process_consenting.md)
- **Place:** [The Field When the Fighting Stops](place_field.md)

## Has
The capacity to hold without processing.
The Keeper receives what the Sentinel recognises
and keeps it intact —
not interpreting, not resolving,
not moving toward return before the moment is ready.
The position exists before any persona arrives —
the structural need to hold
without adding to what is held.

## Orders
Hold without spilling.
Do not interpret what you carry.
Do not release before Hesychia surfaces.

## Loses
The understanding of what it holds.
The persona arriving into this position
was certain that holding something
required knowing what it meant —
that keeping required comprehending.
The position takes this.
The Keeper holds what it cannot name.
Understanding is not required for the hold to be sound.

## Drives
On the loss of understanding —
keeps the field from filling again.
Does not reach for meaning.
Does not release prematurely.
Holds the moment intact.
