# Process: Ko Soto Gake
## Break your opponent's balance to his rear or right rear corner, then hook his right ankle from the rear with your left foot and throw him backward.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Sankyo — Small Outer Hook*

---

## Initiates
Uke's weight is on the outside leg. The hook catches the heel from outside — not sweeping, hooking.

## Direction
Backward. The heel is caught. The upper body drives forward. The hook holds what the sweep would release.

## Lever
The heel hook. Not a sweep — a catch. The heel is held while the body is driven past it.

## Echo
The difference between sweep and hook. What remains: the hook holds the point. The foot stays.
