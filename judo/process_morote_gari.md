# Process: Morote Gari
## Step in between your opponent's legs and wrap your arms around his legs just above his knees, and at the same time throw your shoulder into his chest as you reap his legs toward you to throw him directly to his rear.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Shinmeisho No Waza — Two-Hand Reap*

---

## Initiates
Tori shoots — both hands reach for both legs simultaneously. Total commitment forward.

## Direction
Down and through. Both legs taken at once. No single leg to defend.

## Lever
Both legs together. The double commitment that cannot be defended by defending one side.

## Echo
Both legs. What remains: when you take everything at once there is nothing left to stand on.
