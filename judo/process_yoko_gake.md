# Process: Yoko Gake
## Break your opponent's balance to his right side, then hook his right ankle from the rear with your left foot and fall onto your left side to throw him.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Gokyo — Side Prop*

---

## Initiates
Tori hooks the ankle from the side — the prop holds what the sweep would remove.

## Direction
Sideways. The hook holds. The upper body drives the opposite direction.

## Lever
The side ankle hook. Not swept — propped. The ankle is held in place while the body moves past it.

## Echo
The hook held. What remains: the hook and the fall arrived together.
