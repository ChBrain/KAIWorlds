# Process: O Soto Gari
## Break your opponent's balance to his rear or right rear corner to shift most of his weight onto his right heel, then reap his leg with your right leg.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Dai Ikkyo — Big Outer Reap*

---

## Initiates
Uke's weight is on the leg tori will reap. The commitment is there. The reaping leg swings back and through.

## Direction
Backward. The reap sweeps the supporting leg from outside. The upper body drives forward and down. Both forces meet at the fall.

## Lever
The supporting leg. The one that holds the weight. Remove it and the weight has nothing to stand on.

## Echo
The large outer reap. What remains: the weight was there. The technique went there.
