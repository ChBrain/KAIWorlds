# Process: Tomoe Nage
## Float your opponent's balance forward or toward his front corner, then drop backward while placing your right foot on his lower abdomen, pushing him up and throwing him forward over your head.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Sankyo — Circle Throw*

---

## Initiates
Tori sits down — falls backward deliberately. The foot is placed at uke's stomach. The pull brings uke over.

## Direction
Backward and over. Tori goes to the ground first. Uke follows the circle that tori's body describes.

## Lever
Tori's own fall. The foot at the stomach. The backward sacrifice creates the forward throw.

## Echo
You went to the ground first. They followed the circle. What remains: your back on the mat was the throw.
