# Process: Sumi Otoshi
## Step to the outside of your opponent's right foot and use the shifting moment of your body and the action of both hands to break his balance to his right rear corner and throw him in that direction.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Gokyo — Corner Drop*

---

## Initiates
Uke is moving. Tori pulls diagonally — into the corner of uke's stance where balance is weakest.

## Direction
Into the corner. The diagonal pull finds the narrowest base.

## Lever
The corner of the stance. Where the two lines of balance meet and both are weakest.

## Echo
Into the corner. What remains: every stance has a corner. That is where it falls.
