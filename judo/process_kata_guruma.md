# Process: Kata Guruma
## Break your opponent's balance to his front or right front corner, then lift him onto your shoulders across the back of your neck and throw him down.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Sankyo — Shoulder Wheel*

---

## Initiates
Tori reaches through and lifts — uke is taken off the ground completely. The shoulder becomes the axle.

## Direction
Over the shoulder. Uke is carried across tori's shoulders and thrown. The wheel turns on the shoulder.

## Lever
The shoulder as axle. The full weight of uke balanced on a single point.

## Echo
The wheel completes its turn. What remains: you went under. They went over.
