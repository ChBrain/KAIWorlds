# Process: O Soto Gaeshi
## When your opponent moves in for a right o-soto-gari or similar technique, before he can take your balance, reverse the situation to throw him with your own right o-soto-gari.

**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

## Initiates
Tori commits to O Soto Gari. The leg is in motion. The commitment is complete.

## Direction
The counter uses tori's own committed reaping leg. Uke steps through and reaps back.

## Lever
Tori's committed reaping leg. The counter runs on tori's own force.

## Echo
The reap reversed. What remains: the outer reap returned as an outer reap.
