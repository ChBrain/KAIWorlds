# Process: Hane Goshi
## Break your opponent's balance to his front or right front corner, then pull him close to your right side and throw him with the combined upward-springing action of your right hip and right leg.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Sankyo — Spring Hip Throw*

---

## Initiates
Tori enters. The leg bends and springs — the spring drives uke up and over. The hip throws but the leg springs first.

## Direction
Up from below. The spring creates upward force. The hip adds rotational force. The combination lifts and throws.

## Lever
The springing leg. Not the hip alone. The spring multiplies the force the hip provides.

## Echo
The spring released. What remains: the hip and the leg arrived together.
