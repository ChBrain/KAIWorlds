# Process: Tsubame Gaeshi
## When your opponent attempts a right de-ashi-harai, shift your weight to your left leg and withdraw your right foot by bending the knee, then throw him with a left de-ashi-harai.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Shinmeisho No Waza — Swallow Counter*

---

## Initiates
Tori's foot sweep is coming. Uke lifts the foot — the swallow turns in flight.

## Direction
The lifted foot becomes the sweeping foot. Tori's sweep misses. Uke's counter sweeps.

## Lever
The lifted foot turned sweep. The swallow's turn — direction reversal in a single motion.

## Echo
The swallow counter. What remains: the lifted foot, the same leg, one motion.
