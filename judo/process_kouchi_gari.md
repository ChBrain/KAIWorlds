# Process: Ko Uchi Gari
## Break your opponent's balance to his rear or right rear corner, then sweep his right ankle from the inside with the sole of your right foot to throw him backward.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Dai Nikyo — Small Inner Reap*

---

## Initiates
Uke steps. The small inner reap enters between the feet — smaller than the large inner reap, closer, faster.

## Direction
Inward. Small. The reap clips the inside of the ankle. The upper body drives the opposite direction.

## Lever
The inside ankle. Not the whole leg. The ankle. The smallest inside lever.

## Echo
The small version goes deeper than the large. What remains: small goes where large cannot.
