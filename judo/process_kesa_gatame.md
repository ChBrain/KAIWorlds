# Process: Kesa Gatame
## A pinning technique. Sit at your opponent's right side, grip his right arm with both arms against your body, then lean your weight onto his chest to hold him down.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Osaekomi Waza — Scarf Hold*

---

## Initiates
Uke is on the mat. Tori sits beside — the arm traps the neck, the body weight settles into the scarf position.

## Direction
Down and into the mat. The weight distributes across uke's upper body.

## Lever
The trapped arm and the neck together. The scarf closes both exits.

## Echo
The first hold. What remains: control on the ground begins beside, not above.
