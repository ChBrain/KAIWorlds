# Process: Kata
## The commitment that cannot be excused by not knowing.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo
**Persona:** Kano

---

## Initiates
Two personas. The roles assigned before they step on the mat. Tori knows. Uke knows. The technique is known. The outcome is known. The bow. The grip. Everything that follows has been decided. The process initiates anyway.

## Direction
Execute what is known. Precisely. Every part visible. The knowledge does not replace the commitment — it removes the excuse of not knowing. The technique runs exactly as trained. The body does what the body has learned. The mind watches.

## Lever
The precision. Remove it and kata becomes performance — the form without the pressure. The lever is the requirement that every part be exactly correct, not approximately correct. Approximately correct is not kata.

## Echo
The body knows. Not the mind — the body. What kata leaves behind is not understanding. It is the thing that runs before understanding arrives. The next time the technique is needed, the body is already moving.
