# Process: Shiai
## The thing that finds out what the training actually did.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo
**Persona:** Kano

---

## Initiates
Two personas. No assigned roles. No predetermined techniques. No known outcome. The bow. The grip. Everything trained for arrives at this moment and is either there or it is not. The process does not wait to find out which.

## Direction
What comes, comes. The roles are assigned by the technique as it arrives — not before, not by choice. The process runs on what was built in kata and randori. It does not build. It reveals.

## Lever
The unknown. Remove it — give either persona foreknowledge of what is coming — and shiai becomes randori. The lever is the irreducible uncertainty. The technique that arrives has not been announced. The role that finds you has not been negotiated. The process runs on that and nothing else.

## Echo
What the training did is now known. Not understood — known. The body either had it or it didn't. What shiai leaves behind cannot be unfound. The next kata runs differently. The next randori runs differently. The persona who stepped onto the mat for shiai does not step off the same mat.
