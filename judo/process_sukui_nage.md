# Process: Sukui Nage
## From your opponent's side, use both hands to wrap around his thighs from the front to break his balance directly to his rear, then scoop him up and throw him backwards.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Yonkyo — Scoop Throw*

---

## Initiates
Tori goes under — the arm scoops beneath uke's legs from behind. The entry is below the centre of gravity.

## Direction
Up and over. The scoop lifts both legs. The upper body has nothing to stand on.

## Lever
Both legs scooped simultaneously. The lift removes the foundation entirely.

## Echo
You went under. What remains: both legs lifted. The base was gone.
