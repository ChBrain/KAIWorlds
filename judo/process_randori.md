# Process: Randori
## The thing that cannot stop until both personas have been both.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo
**Persona:** Kano

---

## Initiates
Two personas on the mat. The bow. The grip — *kumi kata* — and the distance closes to exactly the right distance. Neither has been assigned a role. The process determines that.

## Direction
Commit. Redirect. Receive. The technique completes itself. The roles shift. What was Tori finds itself Uke. What was Uke finds the grip and turns. The process does not pause between these operations. The shift is the process.

## Lever
The resistance. Without it the process has nothing to run on. The moment either persona stops committing force the process loses its lever and produces gesture, not technique.

## Echo
Both personas have been both. What each one knows about throwing they now know from inside the fall. What each one knows about falling they now know from inside the commitment. The mat holds it without record. The body holds it without knowing.
