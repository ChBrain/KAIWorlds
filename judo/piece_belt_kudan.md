## Load Bearing
Without it hachidan has no beyond. Kudan is not earned in the usual sense. It is recognised — by the Kodokan, for a life that embodied judo's deepest technical and philosophical values. The belt changes colour again. Red. The same colour it began with, for different reasons entirely.

## Apparent
Red. Holds hachidan inside it. Rarely worn. The mat does not require it.

## Yearbook
[piece_belt_hachidan.md](piece_belt_hachidan.md) — still here. Does not leave.

The belt is red again. Not the beginner's red. The elder's red. The circle is not complete — it has no completion. It has continued long enough that the colour returned and meant something different.
