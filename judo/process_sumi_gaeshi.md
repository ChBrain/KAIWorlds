# Process: Sumi Gaeshi
## Break your opponent's balance to his front or right front corner, then fall backwards, catching the instep of your right foot under and behind his left knee or thigh to lift him up and throw him over your head.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Yonkyo — Corner Reversal*

---

## Initiates
Tori is being pushed into the corner. The pressure is there. The sacrifice uses the pressure as the initiating force.

## Direction
Backward into the corner — then over. The corner is not the end. It is the fulcrum.

## Lever
The corner itself. The pressure pushing tori into it. The sacrifice reverses what was being used against you.

## Echo
The corner reversal. What remains: nowhere left to go became the only place to go.
