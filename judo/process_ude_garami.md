# Process: Ude Garami
## A lock. From any one of several positions, use both arms to entangle one of your opponent's arms while twisting it to the outside or inside to control his elbow.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Kansetsu Waza — Arm Entanglement*

---

## Initiates
Uke's arm is bent. Tori creates the figure-four — the arm is wrapped against itself.

## Direction
The rotation closes. The arm is driven past the natural range of the shoulder joint.

## Lever
The arm used against itself. The figure-four means the arm locks the arm.

## Echo
Your arm against your shoulder. What remains: the joint is the limit. The limit is the technique.
