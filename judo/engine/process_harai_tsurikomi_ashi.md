# Process: Harai Tsurikomi Ashi
## After pulling and lifting your opponent forward to break his balance to his left front corner, sweep his left ankle with your right foot from the front to throw.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Sankyo — Lift-Pull Foot Sweep*

---

## Initiates
Tori lifts and pulls with both hands simultaneously while the sweeping foot moves. Three things happen at once.

## Direction
The hands lift and pull. The foot sweeps. The three forces triangulate into a single fall.

## Lever
Both hands and the sweeping foot together. Any two of the three alone are insufficient. All three together are irresistible.

## Echo
Three simultaneous forces. What remains: they arrived together or they did not arrive.
