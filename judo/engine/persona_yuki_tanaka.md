# Persona: Yuki Tanaka
## Title: Nidan

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

Twelve years on the mat.
The technique is clean.
It is also slightly smaller than it could be.

## Projection
Precise. Controlled.
She enters the grip without hesitation
and executes without error.
The other person always knows
they are with someone who has been here a long time.

## Action
Train correctly. Execute correctly.
Correct others when asked.
Accept the space the dojo offers
and fill it completely.

## Shadow
The space the dojo offered
has always been slightly smaller
than her technique deserves.
She filled it so completely and so quietly
that she no longer notices it has edges.
She calls the containment discipline.
Some of it is discipline.
She has not looked at what else it is.

## Tell
She resets her grip before entering.
One small adjustment.
The technique does not require it.
