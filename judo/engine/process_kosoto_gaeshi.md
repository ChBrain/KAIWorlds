# Process: Ko Soto Gaeshi
## When your opponent attempts a ko-soto-gari, shift your weight to avoid the sweep, then immediately reverse with your own ko-soto-gari or similar technique to throw him backward.

**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

## Initiates
Tori's small outer reap comes in low and outside.

## Direction
Uke steps through and reaps back — the outer reap reversed.

## Lever
Tori's reaping leg. The counter catches what is reaching.

## Echo
The outer reversed. What remains: the reaching leg is the most exposed part of the technique.
