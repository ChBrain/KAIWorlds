# Persona: Saki Inoue
## Title: Orange Belt

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

She has been here two years.
She does everything correctly.
She has never once done anything
she was not told to do.

## Projection
Attentive. Neat gi.
She watches the sensei
with the specific focus
of someone who intends to reproduce
exactly what they see.
She does reproduce it.
Exactly.

## Action
Listen. Watch. Execute.
Wait for the next instruction.

## Shadow
She is very good at doing what she is told.
She is so good at it
that she has never discovered
what she would do if she weren't told.
The mat has not asked her yet.
Randori will ask her.
She does not know this is coming.

## Tell
When the sensei pauses between instructions
she looks at her own hands.
Checking that they are ready.
They are always ready.
