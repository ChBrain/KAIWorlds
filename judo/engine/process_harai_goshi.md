# Process: Harai Goshi
## Break your opponent's balance to his front or right front corner, then pivot and pull him to your hips and sweep his right thigh with the back of your right thigh.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Dai Nikyo — Sweeping Hip Throw*

---

## Initiates
Tori enters — hip in, turning. The sweeping leg swings back and through uke's legs from behind.

## Direction
Over the hip and forward. The sweep clears the legs. The hip carries the body over.

## Lever
The sweeping leg and the hip together. The leg sweeps from behind. The hip throws from below.

## Echo
The sweep and the throw arrived together. What remains: one motion, not two.
