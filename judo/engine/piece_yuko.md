# Piece: Yuko
## The smallest score.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo
**Persona:** Kano

---

## Place
[The Competition Area](place_competition_area.md)

## Load Bearing
Without it the scoring system loses resolution at the margins.
The technique that landed on the side,
the upper back, the shoulder axis —
these are not nothing.
Yuko names them.

## Apparent
One point. The referee's arm extends to the side.
The technique landed — but not on the back.
The match continues.
The scoreboard moves by one.
Yuko does not accumulate into waza-ari.
It accumulates into a total.

## Yearbook
Removed from IJF competition in 2017.
Reinstated in 2025.
Its absence taught something:
without it, competitors stopped committing
to techniques that might only score small.
The scoring system
had removed the reward for trying.
Yuko came back.
