# Process: Hane Goshi Gaeshi
## When your opponent attempts a right hane-goshi, before he has a chance to apply the technique fully, either lift him up and reap his leg to your left with your right leg or hook your left leg around his lower left leg and reap it to your right to throw him onto his back.

**Project:** Judo

## Initiates
Tori springs into Hane Goshi — the spring commits the whole body.

## Direction
Uke steps through the spring and throws tori over their own springing motion.

## Lever
Tori's spring. The stored energy of the spring becomes the counter's force.

## Echo
The spring reversed. What remains: the stored energy turned.
