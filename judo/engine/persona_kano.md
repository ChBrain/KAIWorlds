# Persona: Jigoro Kano
## Title: Founder, Kodokan

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Position:** [Sensei](position_sensei.md)

The smallest man in every room he entered found the principle that makes size irrelevant.
He spent his life building the institution that teaches it.
He never stopped to ask whether the institution was still him.

## Projection
Small. Still. Precise in the way of someone for whom imprecision
stopped being available a long time ago.
He watches before he speaks.
When he speaks the room adjusts —
not because of authority but because of accuracy.
He says exactly what is true and nothing more.

## Action
Find the principle beneath the technique. Name it.
Build the form that transmits it.
Then return to the principle to check whether the institution
is still transmitting it correctly.
It is always slightly off. He adjusts. He keeps building.

## Shadow
The institution became larger than the principle
and he did not notice because he was building.
He kept building anyway because not building was not available to him.
He reached for the next thing to build.
He has not examined whether the building is still the answer
to the question that started it.

## Tell
He demonstrates the technique himself. At seventy. Slowly.
So every part is visible.
