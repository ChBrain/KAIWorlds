## The one who receives.

## Has
The resistance.
Without it Tori has no technique.
Only a gesture.

## Orders
Be genuinely present.
Resist. Commit force. Move.
The position requires real force —
performed resistance degrades the technique.

## Loses
The illusion of passivity.
The position takes it.
Uke is half the throw.
The fall belongs to this position
as much as it belongs to Tori.

## Drives
On losing passivity —
ukemi.
The fall is not survived.
It is executed.
The mat receives what the position completes.
