# Piece: The Blue Belt
## The depth where technique becomes instinct.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo
**Persona:** Kano

---

## Place
[The Dojo](place_dojo.md)

## Load Bearing
Without it the body has movement. The blue belt is where movement becomes instinct — where the counter arrives before the decision to counter. Combinations are now required. Shiai is required. What was built is being tested against people who are also trying to throw.

## Apparent
Blue. Cotton. Holds the green, orange, yellow, white belts inside it.
