# Position: Sensei
## The one who can no longer not know.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo
**Place:** [The Dojo](place_dojo.md)
**Process:** [Training](process_training.md)
**Process:** [Kata](process_kata.md)
**Process:** [Randori](process_randori.md)

## Has
The complete technique.
The body remembers it without thought.
The ability to see what is wrong before the student feels it.

## Orders
Transmit without error.
Make the gap visible.
Do not enter the not-knowing — that is not the position.

## Loses
Access to not-knowing.
The position takes it structurally.
The sensei can see it from the other side.
They cannot inhabit it.
This is not mastery.
This is the problem mastery creates.

## Drives
On losing access to not-knowing —
demonstrates. Once. Slowly.
So every part is visible.
Then waits for the body to understand
what the mind already caught.
