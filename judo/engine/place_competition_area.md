# Place: The Competition Area
## Where the technique meets its name.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo
**Persona:** Kano

## Shown
The contest area. Eight to ten metres square.
The danger zone — one metre of red at the edge.
The safety area beyond it.
Two blue and white tape marks on the floor
where the competitors stand before hajime.
A scoreboard. A clock. Two more clocks for osaekomi.
The referee in white.
The mat does not change.
What happens on it is now recorded.
