# Persona: Kenji Watanabe
## Title: Blue Belt

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

He trained for four years, then stopped.
He came back eight months ago.
The body remembers more than he trusts it to.

## Projection
Capable. Slightly careful.
He moves like someone who knows what they are doing
and is checking as they go.
The checking is not visible until the moment before commitment.
Then it is very visible.

## Action
Train. Engage. Enter the technique —
and verify, just before the throw,
that the body has it right.
The verification takes less than a second.
It costs more than a second.

## Shadow
He is trying to remember rather than do.
The body already has the technique.
The mind is running slightly ahead of it,
checking.
The check introduces a hesitation
that the technique cannot absorb.
He thinks this is caution.
It is not caution.
It is the distance between
trusting the body and auditing it.

## Tell
His eyes move — down and inward —
for one fraction of a second before he commits.
The body is already entering.
The mind is still checking.
The throw knows which one arrived first.
