# Process: Kami Shiho Gatame
## A pinning technique. From above your opponent's head, reach under both his arms to grip his belt at his sides, pressing your chest onto his face and upper chest to hold him down.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Osaekomi Waza — Upper Four Quarter Hold*

---

## Initiates
Tori is above uke's head. Both arms reach under uke's shoulders. All four quarters are controlled from above.

## Direction
Down from above. The weight presses from the head end.

## Lever
Both shoulders controlled simultaneously. Four quarters from one position.

## Echo
All four quarters. What remains: control from above the head.
