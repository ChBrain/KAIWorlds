# Process: Sasae Tsurikomi Ashi
## As you break your opponent's balance to his right front corner, block his supporting right leg by placing the sole of your foot just above his ankle, creating a pivot point, then lean back and twist to the left to throw him over it.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Dai Ikkyo — Propping and Drawing Ankle Throw*

---

## Initiates
Uke steps forward. Tori lifts and pulls simultaneously — the kuzushi is in both directions at once. Up and forward.

## Direction
Forward and over. The foot props the ankle. The hands lift and draw. The body arcs over the blocked foot.

## Lever
The ankle and the hands together. Neither alone is sufficient. The prop stops the foot. The pull takes the upper body. The two forces meet in the fall.

## Echo
Two simultaneous points of pressure. What remains: the arc they complete together.
