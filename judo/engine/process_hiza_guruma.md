# Process: Hiza Guruma
## Break your opponent's balance to his right front corner, then place the sole of your left foot on his right kneecap and use this as a fulcrum around which to throw him by pulling his upper body.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Dai Ikkyo — Knee Wheel*

---

## Initiates
Uke is moving. Tori pulls — lifting and drawing. The knee becomes the pivot point the moment tori's foot blocks it.

## Direction
Circular. The body rotates around the blocked knee. The wheel turns outward. The fall follows the arc.

## Lever
The knee. One point of contact that stops one part of the body while the rest continues moving. The wheel has its axle.

## Echo
The geometry of the circle. What remains: a single fixed point and a rotating force.
