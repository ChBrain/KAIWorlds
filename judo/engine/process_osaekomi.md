# Process: Osaekomi
## The hold that runs on a clock.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo
**Persona:** Kano

*Ne-waza — Hold Down*

---

## Initiates
Tori controls Uke on the ground.
The referee calls *osaekomi.*
The clock starts.
The hold either holds or it doesn't.
There is no partial credit for effort.

## Direction
Toward twenty seconds.
Five seconds: yuko.
Ten to fourteen seconds: waza-ari territory.
Twenty seconds: ippon.
The score rises with time held.
The hold must be maintained — not begun.

## Lever
The clock.
Remove it and osaekomi is just pressure with no consequence.
The clock is what makes the ground dangerous.

## Echo
The score — or the scramble that happened
when the hold broke.
Toketa: the referee calls it and the clock stops.
What was held is no longer held.
The mat resets.
The body remembers how close it was.
