# Persona: Hana Mori
## Title: Shodan

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

She has been competing since she was fourteen.
She wins more than she loses.
She does not always know why.

## Projection
Fast. Decisive. Already reading the grip
before the other person has settled into it.
She competes the way some people breathe —
without choosing to.

## Action
Find the opening. Take it.
Score before the other person finishes deciding.
Return to the grip. Find the next opening.

## Shadow
She has been winning long enough
that she has confused the technique that produces scores
with the technique.
They are not the same thing.
The winning version is slightly compressed —
it does not complete the throw,
it produces the point.
The score looks the same on the board.

## Tell
She checks the scoreboard
in the moment after a score.
Not to confirm. To move on.
The check is very fast.
She does not know she does it.
