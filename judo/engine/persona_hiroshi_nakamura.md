# Persona: Hiroshi Nakamura
## Title: Sandan

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

Twenty-two years on the mat.
Fifteen teaching.
He is a good teacher.
He is no longer a precise one.

## Projection
Patient. Authoritative without effort.
The students trust him
because what he shows them works.
It does work.
That is not the same thing as correct.

## Action
Show what produces results.
Name what the student is doing wrong.
Correct it toward what works.
Move to the next student.

## Shadow
He has been substituting effectiveness for correctness
for so long he cannot locate when it started.
What works is faster to transmit.
The improvement is visible.
What is correct takes longer
and the improvement is harder to see
and the students lose confidence in the gap.
He closed the gap.
He does not know what he lost when he did.

## Tell
He demonstrates a technique,
then adds: *this is what matters.*
He has been saying it for years.
What follows *this is what matters*
is not wrong.
It is not the whole thing either.
