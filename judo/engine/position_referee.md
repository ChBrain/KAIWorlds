# Position: Referee
## The one whose body is the scoring system.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo
**Place:** [The Competition Area](place_competition_area.md)
**Process:** [Shiai](process_shiai.md)
**Process:** [Referee](process_referee.md)

## Has
The complete vocabulary of what the mat produces.
Eleven gestures. Each one a different answer
to what the body just did.

## Orders
See it correctly. Name it immediately.
The position does not permit delay —
a score named late is a score in doubt.

## Loses
The option of uncertainty.
The position requires a call.
What the mat produced is always ambiguous
at the margin.
The position must answer anyway.

## Drives
On losing certainty —
the gesture.
The body speaks before the voice does.
The arm rises. The hand opens or closes.
The mat and the scoreboard agree.
