# Process: O Goshi
## Break your opponent's balance to his front or right front corner, enter deeply with your right hip to float him up, and twist to throw him over it.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Dai Ikkyo — Full Hip Throw*

---

## Initiates
Tori turns in completely. The hip is placed at uke's centre of gravity. The arm wraps. The entry is complete before the throw begins.

## Direction
Over and forward. Uke is lifted onto the hip and carried over. The hip is the wheel. Uke travels its arc.

## Lever
The full hip. Not floating — full contact. The entire hip against uke's centre. The largest lever available.

## Echo
The full hip throw. What remains: complete entry. Not partial.
