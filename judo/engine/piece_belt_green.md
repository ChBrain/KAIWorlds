# Piece: The Green Belt
## The body beginning to move without asking permission.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo
**Persona:** Kano

---

## Place
[The Dojo](place_dojo.md)

## Load Bearing
Without it the body has technique. The green belt is where the technique becomes movement — where the body stops consulting the mind before it acts. Randori is now required. The mat tests what was built.

## Apparent
Green. Cotton. Holds the orange, yellow, white belts inside it.
