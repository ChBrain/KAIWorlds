# Process: De Ashi Barai
## At the instant your opponent has taken a step forward or backward and is on the verge of placing his weight on the stepping foot, use your own foot to sweep that foot from the rear, side, or front.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Dai Ikkyo — Advanced Foot Sweep*

---

## Initiates
Uke is stepping forward. The weight is moving. The foot has left the ground but has not yet landed. That moment — between departure and arrival — is the only moment this works.

## Direction
Forward. Low. The sweeping foot moves across the ground, not above it. The contact is the ankle. You do not oppose the movement. You complete it. Further than uke intended.

## Lever
The stepping foot. The weight that was moving toward it. Remove the sweep and uke lands normally. The lever is the absence of the ground at the moment the ground was needed.

## Echo
Uke is on the mat. The sweep took less than a second. What remains: the timing. Not the force.
