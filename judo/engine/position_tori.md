# Position: Tori
## The one who throws.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo
**Place:** [The Dojo](place_dojo.md)
**Place:** [The Competition Area](place_competition_area.md)

## Has
The initiative.
The moment before the throw belongs to this position.
The commitment is already made before the technique begins.

## Orders
Enter. Break the balance. Complete the throw.
The position does not permit partial entry.

## Loses
The option of safety.
The moment Tori enters, exposure is structural.
The back is turned. The position is given up.
This is not a risk. It is what the technique requires.

## Drives
On losing safety —
the throw or the lesson that precedes the throw.
Nothing between.
