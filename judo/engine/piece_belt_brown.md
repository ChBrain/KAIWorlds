## Load Bearing
Without it black belt has no threshold. The brown belt holds all forty throws, all groundwork, all submissions. The body now has everything the system built. What remains is not more technique. It is what the technique reveals when tested against the full weight of another person who also has all of it.

## Apparent
Brown. Cotton. Holds every kyu belt before it inside it.
