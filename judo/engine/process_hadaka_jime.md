# Process: Hadaka Jime
## A strangling technique. From behind your opponent, place your right forearm across his throat and grip your own bicep with your right hand, then place your left hand behind his head and squeeze to apply pressure to the carotid arteries.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Shime Waza — Naked Strangle*

---

## Initiates
Tori is behind uke. The arm crosses the throat. No gi. Just the arm and the neck.

## Direction
The arm closes. The throat compresses. The blood or air stops.

## Lever
The arm across the throat. The most direct. No grip on clothing — just the body.

## Echo
The naked strangle. What remains: no grip on clothing. Just the arm and the throat.
