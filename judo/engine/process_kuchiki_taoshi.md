# Process: Kuchiki Taoshi
## Quickly grab the opponent's right leg, either from the inside with your right hand or from the outside with your left, lifting it up while simultaneously pushing him backwards to throw him down.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Shinmeisho No Waza — Single Leg Takedown*

---

## Initiates
Tori reaches for one leg — the most direct single-leg entry.

## Direction
Down and through one leg. The single leg removed. The other leg cannot hold.

## Lever
One leg. The simplest geometry. One leg taken and the other is insufficient.

## Echo
One leg is enough. What remains: one leg lifted, the other could not hold.
