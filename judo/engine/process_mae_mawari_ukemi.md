# Process: Mae Mawari Ukemi
## A rolling breakfall taken to protect the body when thrown to the front: roll diagonally across one shoulder and arm, coming up on the opposite side.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Ukemi — Forward Rolling Breakfall*

---

## Initiates
You are falling forward but rolling — the fall becomes a rotation. You come up on the other side.

## Direction
Forward and through. The roll continues past the fall. You arrive standing.

## Lever
The rotation itself. The roll converts the fall's energy into movement rather than impact.

## Echo
You rolled through the fall. You rose on the other side. What remains: the fall carried through to standing.
