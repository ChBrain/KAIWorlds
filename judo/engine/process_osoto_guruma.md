# Process: O Soto Guruma
## Break your opponent's balance to his rear or his rear corner, then pivot left to extend your right leg diagonally across the back of his knee and use your right thigh as a fulcrum over which to throw him.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Gokyo — Big Outer Wheel*

---

## Initiates
Tori's leg sweeps across both of uke's legs from outside — the outer wheel takes both legs together.

## Direction
Backward and over. Both legs swept from outside simultaneously.

## Lever
Both legs from outside. The outer wheel is wider than the outer reap.

## Echo
Both legs together. What remains: when one leg is defended, take both.
