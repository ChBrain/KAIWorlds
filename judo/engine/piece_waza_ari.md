## Load Bearing

Everything would be ippon or nothing —
which removes the information
that a technique was real but incomplete.

## Apparent
Half a point. Two fingers raised.
The technique had three of the four criteria.
The match continues.
The scoreboard has changed.
Everything that follows is different.

## Yearbook
*Waza-ari awasete ippon* —
two waza-ari combine into the win.
The accumulation is not just arithmetic.
It is pressure.
A competitor who holds a waza-ari
is not the same competitor they were before it.
Neither is the one who gave it.
