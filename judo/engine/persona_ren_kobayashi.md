# Persona: Ren Kobayashi
## Title: Yellow Belt

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

He has been here eight months.
He throws with everything he has.
Every time.
This is not technique.
It is what comes before technique.

## Projection
Eager. Slightly too much energy
for the space.
He bows correctly because he was told to.
He means the bow.
He is already thinking about the throw.

## Action
Grip. Commit. Throw as hard as possible.
Pick himself up.
Grip again.

## Shadow
He believes judo requires
everything he has.
It requires something subtler than everything.
The force is not wrong —
there is not enough principle underneath it yet
to direct where the force goes.
He does not know this.
He knows that sometimes it works
and sometimes it doesn't
and he cannot tell the difference in advance.
He thinks this is bad luck.

## Tell
He exhales loudly at the moment of commitment.
Every time.
The technique telegraphs before it arrives.
