# Process: Uki Otoshi
## Float your opponent's balance to his right front corner, then pull him strongly downward with both hands to throw him in a circle.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Yonkyo — Floating Drop*

---

## Initiates
Tori floats down — the minimal sacrifice. The pull guides uke over tori's floating, dropping body.

## Direction
Floating down. The most minimal. Almost nothing. The drop is so small it seems like nothing is happening until uke is on the mat.

## Lever
The float itself. The near-absence of tori. The minimal lever at the maximum moment.

## Echo
Almost nothing was enough. What remains: the float without the force.
