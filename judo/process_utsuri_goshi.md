# Process: Utsuri Goshi
## A counter throw against an attempted harai-goshi or other hip throw: bend your knees to block your opponent's attack, shifting your hips and wrapping his waist with your arm, thereby lifting him onto your hips and throwing him by twisting your body.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Yonkyo — Shifting Hip Throw*

---

## Initiates
Uke is mid-throw — committed to their own technique. Tori shifts the hip to receive and redirect.

## Direction
The shift reverses the direction. Uke's committed throw becomes tori's throw.

## Lever
The hip shift. The moment of uke's commitment is the moment of tori's opportunity.

## Echo
The counter that used the throw. What remains: their momentum was the technique.
