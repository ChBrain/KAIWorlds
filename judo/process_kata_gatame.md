# Process: Kata Gatame
## A pinning technique. Push your opponent's right arm against the right side of his neck with your right shoulder and head, then clamp your arms around his head and arm together to hold him down.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Osaekomi Waza — Shoulder Hold*

---

## Initiates
Uke's arm is trapped against their own neck. Tori's head pins it there.

## Direction
Down. The arm is used against the owner. The shoulder is pinned.

## Lever
Uke's own arm against their own neck. The hold uses uke's body against itself.

## Echo
Your arm against your neck. What remains: the arm that was free is now the hold.
