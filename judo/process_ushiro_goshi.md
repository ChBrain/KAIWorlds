# Process: Ushiro Goshi
## A counter throw against an attempted harai-goshi or other hip throw: shift out of the way and position yourself to wrap your arms around your opponent's waist from behind, then swing him up into the air to throw him onto his back.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Gokyo — Rear Hip Throw*

---

## Initiates
Uke is mid-throw. Tori catches them — wraps from behind, hip pressing into uke's back.

## Direction
Backward over tori's hip. The rear hip catches what the front throw started.

## Lever
Tori's rear hip against uke's back at the moment of uke's own commitment.

## Echo
The catch and reverse. What remains: their throw became the lift.
