# Process: Koshi Guruma
## Break your opponent's balance to his front or right front corner, then place your right hip solidly against his body and, continuing to grip his collar, draw him up and throw him over your hips with a large motion.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Dai Nikyo — Hip Wheel*

---

## Initiates
Tori enters. The arm goes around uke's neck — not the waist. The neck is the axle of this wheel.

## Direction
Over the hip. The neck is controlled. The body follows where the neck leads.

## Lever
The neck and the hip together. The neck controls direction. The hip provides the wheel.

## Echo
Control the head, the body follows. What remains: the neck as axle.
