# Process: Mae Ukemi
## A breakfall taken to protect the body when thrown to the front: turn the head to the side, slap both forearms and palms onto the mat simultaneously to absorb the impact.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Ukemi — Forward Breakfall*

---

## Initiates
You are falling forward. The ground is arriving. The choice is already made — fall or be fallen.

## Direction
Forward and down. The hands reach before the body arrives. The slap disperses the impact.

## Lever
The arms. They arrive first. They give the ground what it needs before it takes it.

## Echo
You fell forward. You rose. What remains: falling is a technique.
