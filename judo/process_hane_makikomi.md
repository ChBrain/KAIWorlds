# Process: Hane Makikomi
## After attempting a hane-goshi, release your right hand and place it on the outside as you twist to the left, thereby wrapping your opponent's body around you, then throw by taking him with you as you fall forward onto the mat.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Yonkyo — Springing Wraparound*

---

## Initiates
Tori enters and wraps — the arm traps uke's arm against tori's body. The spring and the wrap happen together.

## Direction
Around and down. The wrap closes. The spring adds upward force into the rotating fall.

## Lever
The trapped arm and the spring together. The wrap prevents escape. The spring drives the rotation.

## Echo
Wrapped and sprung. What remains: held in place, driven through.
