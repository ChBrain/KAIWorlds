# Process: O Uchi Gari
## Break your opponent's balance to his left back corner, causing his stance to open wide and his weight to shift mostly onto his left heel, then step in deeply with your right leg and use it to reap his left leg from the inside with a large motion to your right to throw him backwards.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Dai Ikkyo — Big Inner Reap*

---

## Initiates
Uke steps. Tori steps inside — between uke's legs. The reaping leg drives inward and through.

## Direction
Inward and backward. The leg reaps from inside. The upper body drives in the opposite direction. Inside and out simultaneously.

## Lever
The inner leg. The inside of the stance where balance is narrowest. The place uke cannot easily defend because it is behind their own body.

## Echo
The inner reap. What remains: the inside of the stance.
