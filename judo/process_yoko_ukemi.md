# Process: Yoko Ukemi
## A breakfall taken to protect the body when thrown to the side: turn into the fall, slap the mat with the lower arm extended at 45 degrees while keeping the head clear of the mat.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Ukemi — Sideways Breakfall*

---

## Initiates
You are falling to the side. The mat arrives at the side of the body.

## Direction
Sideways. The arm slaps hard. The whole side of the body lands simultaneously.

## Lever
The arm slap. The sound of the slap on the tatami is the technique working. The impact is dispersed into the slap.

## Echo
The slap on the mat. What remains: impact redirected.
