# Process: Ko Soto Gari
## Break your opponent's balance to his rear or right rear corner, then sweep his right ankle from the rear with the sole of your left foot to throw him backward.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Dai Nikyo — Small Outer Reap*

---

## Initiates
Uke steps. The small outer reap targets the heel — the smallest point of contact that carries the whole structure.

## Direction
Backward and outside. The heel is clipped. Small contact. Large consequence.

## Lever
The heel. The smallest lever. Faster than the large outer reap. Less visible. More precise.

## Echo
Small is faster. What remains: precision at a small point.
