# Process: Tsuri Goshi
## Reach over your opponent's right shoulder or under his arm with your right hand to grip the side or rear of his belt and pull him forward to break his balance, then lift him onto your hips and throw him over them.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Sankyo — Lifting Hip Throw*

---

## Initiates
Tori grips the belt — the centre of uke's body. The lift comes from below. The hip enters under uke's centre of gravity.

## Direction
Up and over. The belt grip lifts. The hip throws. Uke is lifted before they are thrown.

## Lever
The belt. The centre. Control the centre and you control everything attached to it.

## Echo
Grip the centre. What remains: the belt is where all forces converge.
