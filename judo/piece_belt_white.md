# Piece: The White Belt
## The threshold that holds both ends.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo
**Persona:** Kano

## Place
[The Dojo](place_dojo.md)

## Load Bearing
Without it no one enters. Without it mastery has nowhere to return to. The only piece that means the same thing twice — once when the body knows nothing, once when it knows everything.

## Apparent
White. Cotton. No marking.

## Yearbook
Kano made two belts. White and black. The whole system.

The white belt was the beginning. It remained the beginning.

In 1940 his nephew awarded him the 12th dan. The belt was white. After having learned everything — start again.

Every judoka who stepped onto the mat for the first time wore it. The mat was already there. The belt said: you have come to the door.
