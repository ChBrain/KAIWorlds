# Process: Okuri Ashi Harai
## Break your opponent's balance to his right side, and as he moves his right foot to the left, follow it in the direction it is moving with your left foot and sweep both of his legs with your whole leg to throw him on his back.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Dai Nikyo — Sliding Foot Sweep*

---

## Initiates
Uke is moving sideways. Both feet are close together — the moment between steps when neither foot is fully planted.

## Direction
Sideways. Both feet swept together. One sweep takes both because they are already close.

## Lever
Both feet. Neither fully planted. The moment of transition between steps is the moment of least stability.

## Echo
Catch them between steps. What remains: the step that did not land.
