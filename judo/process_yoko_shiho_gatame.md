# Process: Yoko Shiho Gatame
## A pinning technique. Lie perpendicular across your opponent's chest, controlling his near arm with your armpit while gripping his far lapel and his near leg, pressing your weight down to hold him.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Osaekomi Waza — Side Four Quarter Hold*

---

## Initiates
Tori is beside uke. One arm controls the head. One controls the hip. Four quarters from the side.

## Direction
Down from the side. The weight bridges across uke's body.

## Lever
Head and hip controlled simultaneously from the side. The bridge of tori's body.

## Echo
Head and hip together. What remains: both ends held.
