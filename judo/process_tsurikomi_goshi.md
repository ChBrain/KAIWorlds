# Process: Tsurikomi Goshi
## Use your grips on your opponent's collar and sleeve to break his balance to his front or right front corner, then drop your hips to the level of his thighs, then raise them again while pulling with both hands to throw him over your hips.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Dai Nikyo — Lifting-and-Pulling Hip Throw*

---

## Initiates
Tori lifts with the sleeve and pulls with the lapel simultaneously. The kuzushi is in both hands at once before the hip enters.

## Direction
Up and over. The hip enters after the kuzushi is established. The lift and pull have already broken the balance.

## Lever
Both hands working in opposite directions simultaneously. Lift and pull. The hip arrives into the space the hands created.

## Echo
The hands prepared the space. The hip completed it. What remains: kuzushi before entry.
