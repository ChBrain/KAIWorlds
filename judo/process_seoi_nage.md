# Process: Seoi Nage
## Break your opponent's balance to his front or right front corner, then pivot to the left while allowing the elbow of your right arm to bend and come under his right armpit, then load him onto your back and throw him over your right shoulder.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Dai Ikkyo — Shoulder Throw*

---

## Initiates
Tori turns their back to uke completely. The arm is trapped. The hips drop below uke's centre of gravity.

## Direction
Over the shoulder and forward. Uke is carried over tori's back. The turn creates the throw.

## Lever
The shoulder and the trapped arm together. The back is the ramp. The arm is what guides uke onto it.

## Echo
You turned your back. What remains: the back was the technique.
