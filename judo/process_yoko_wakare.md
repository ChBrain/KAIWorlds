# Process: Yoko Wakare
## Break your opponent's balance to his front or right front corner, then drop onto your back or left side while extending both legs out in front of him to throw him across your outstretched body.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Gokyo — Side Separation*

---

## Initiates
Tori falls to the side — the separation is tori stepping away from uke's space.

## Direction
Sideways. The separation creates the throw. Tori moves away; uke follows.

## Lever
The separation itself. Tori moving away creates a pull that uke cannot resist.

## Echo
You moved away. They followed. What remains: the separation was the throw.
