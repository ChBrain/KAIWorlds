# Process: Ura Nage
## Wrap both arms around your opponent's waist from his right side, pulling in close to him, then lift up and fall backward to throw him over your left shoulder.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Gokyo — Rear Throw*

---

## Initiates
Tori wraps from behind — both arms around uke from the rear. The throw goes backward.

## Direction
Backward. Tori falls back. Uke goes over tori's falling body.

## Lever
Both arms from behind. The full rear wrap.

## Echo
Behind and over. What remains: the throw came from behind. There was nothing to brace against.
