# Process: Harai Goshi Gaeshi
## When your opponent attacks with a right harai-goshi, hook your left leg around his lower left leg from behind and reap it to throw him.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Shinmeisho No Waza — Sweeping Hip Counter*

---

## Initiates
Tori enters for Harai Goshi — the hip turns in, the sweeping leg swings.

## Direction
Uke steps around the sweeping leg and throws tori over their own committed hip.

## Lever
Tori's committed hip entry. The counter uses the entry as the lever.

## Echo
The hip entry reversed. What remains: the hip was fully committed. That was enough.
