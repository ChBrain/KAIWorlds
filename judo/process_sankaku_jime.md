# Process: Sankaku Jime
## A strangling technique. From guard or a transitional position, entangle your opponent's head and one arm within a triangle formed by your legs and apply pressure to the carotid arteries by squeezing your thighs together.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Shime Waza — Triangular Strangle*

---

## Initiates
The legs form the triangle around the neck and one arm. Three points of pressure. No single escape direction.

## Direction
The triangle closes. Three sides simultaneously. Each side reinforces the others.

## Lever
The triangle itself. Three points. Geometry cannot be defeated by force — only by solving the shape.

## Echo
The triangle closed. What remains: three locked angles and no air.
