# Process: Ushiro Ukemi
## A breakfall taken to protect the body when thrown to the rear: tuck the chin, round the back, and slap the mat with both arms extended at 45 degrees to absorb the impact.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Ukemi — Backward Breakfall*

---

## Initiates
You are falling backward. The ground is behind you where you cannot see it.

## Direction
Backward and down. The chin tucks. The arms slap out to the sides. The back rounds.

## Lever
The chin tuck and the arm slap together. The chin protects the head. The arms distribute the impact.

## Echo
You fell backward into what you could not see. You rose. What remains: falling toward the unknown without bracing.
