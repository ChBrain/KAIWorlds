# Process: O Guruma
## Just as your opponent begins to step forward with his right foot, lead his movement to break his balance to his front right corner, turning left and stretching your right leg straight across his lower abdomen or upper thighs, then lift him by swinging your right leg up and back while twisting and pulling down with both hands to turn him over your leg.
