# Process: Tai Otoshi
## Break your opponent's balance to his right front corner, open your body and turn to step in front of his right foot with your right foot, then use the action of both hands to pull him down and throw him over your foot.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Dai Nikyo — Body Drop*

---

## Initiates
Tori pulls forward and steps across. The leg blocks outside — not reaping, blocking. The upper body drives forward and down.

## Direction
Forward and over. Tori drops their own body. The block is the fulcrum. Uke goes over it.

## Lever
Tori's own body. The drop. Tori goes down to make uke go over. The sacrifice of position is the technique.

## Echo
You dropped. They went over. What remains: you went down first.
