# Process: Uki Goshi
## Break your opponent's balance to his front or right front corner, then enter shallowly with your right hip while wrapping your right arm around his waist, and twist to the left to throw him over and around your hips without raising them or bending forward.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Dai Ikkyo — Floating Hip Throw*

---

## Initiates
Tori enters — turning in, hip sliding under uke's centre. The entry is the technique. Everything else follows from it.

## Direction
Over the hip. Uke floats — lifted slightly, turned, carried over. The hip is the fulcrum. The body of uke is the load.

## Lever
The hip. Not the arms. The arms guide. The hip throws. Remove the hip entry and there is no throw — only pulling.

## Echo
The first hip throw. What remains: the hip enters. Everything else follows.
