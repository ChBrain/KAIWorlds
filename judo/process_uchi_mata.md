# Process: Uchi Mata
## After opening the opponent's feet a little and breaking his balance to his front or right front corner, sweep his left inner thigh deeply from the inside using the back of your right thigh while twisting to the left.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Dai Nikyo — Inner Thigh Throw*

---

## Initiates
Tori enters. The leg drives up and through — between uke's thighs, from inside. The entry is deep. The commitment is total.

## Direction
Up and through. The inner thigh is the lever. The leg drives upward. The body follows.

## Lever
The inner thigh. The deepest inside position. Where the body is most vulnerable and least expecting.

## Echo
Between and through. What remains: the deepest entry on the mat.
