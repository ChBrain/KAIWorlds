# Process: Yama Arashi
## Using both hands to grip your opponent's right lapel and right sleeve, draw him forward to float his balance toward his right front corner, then use the back of your right leg to sweep his legs in an uplifting motion.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Habukareta Waza — Mountain Storm*

---

## Initiates
The grip is high — the collar, the sleeve. The entry is a turning storm.

## Direction
Over and through. The mountain storm turns everything.

## Lever
The high collar grip and the full turning entry. The storm is the combination.

## Echo
The mountain storm. What remains: the sweep and the pull arrived as one force. The body felt it as weather.
