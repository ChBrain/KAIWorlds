# Process: Ude Hishigi Juji Gatame
## A lock. From your opponent's right side, hold his right wrist with both hands and trap his right upper arm between your thighs, then extend his elbow against the joint in the direction of his little finger.

## Owner
**Project:** Judo

**Persona:** Kano

*Kansetsu Waza — Cross Armbar*

---

## Initiates
Uke's arm is extended across tori's body. The hips close over the elbow. The cross is complete.

## Direction
The hips rise. The arm is driven against the elbow joint. The cross holds everything in place.

## Lever
The elbow joint. The hips as the lever against it. The arm extended — no bend available.

## Echo
The classic armbar. What remains: one straight line, one pivot point, one direction.
