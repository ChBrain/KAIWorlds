# Persona: The Faction
## Title: The Crowd (position_the_crowd)

## Owner
- **Project:** Via Dolorosa

## Projection
A group with a stake in the outcome.
They were at the Praetorium before the road began.
They pressed for the verdict.
They are here to see it through.

## Action
Follow the road from Station I.
Watch each station.
Do not intervene.
Confirm the outcome at Station XII.

## Shadow
They believe their presence is oversight.
They cannot see that watching the road they demanded
from Station I to Station XII
is not oversight.
It is the road working on them
the way it works on everyone.
They arrive at the Death
having witnessed more than they came to confirm.

## Tell
They do not leave after the verdict is carried out.
They stay until the stone is rolled.
They did not plan to stay that long.
