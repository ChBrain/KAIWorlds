# Piece: The Inscription
## What Pilate wrote. What he would not change.

## Owner
- **Project:** Via Dolorosa
- **Place:** The Condemnation (place_station_i_condemnation)

## Place
Fixed to the Cross (piece_the_cross).
Where the Bearer (position_the_bearer) is, from Station II.

## Load Bearing
Without it the Death has no named authority.
The execution is anonymous.
The One Who Condemns (position_the_one_who_condemns)
has no visible presence at the Death
despite being its cause.

## Apparent
A board. Fixed above the Bearer on the Cross (piece_the_cross).
Three languages: Aramaic, Latin, Greek.
The text: Jesus of Nazareth, King of the Jews.
Written by Pilate.
Unchanged despite objection.

## Yearbook
Written before the road began.
Present on the Cross (piece_the_cross) from Station II.
The chief priests objected. Pilate did not change it.
Fixed permanently at the Nailing (place_station_xi_nailing).
Present at the Death (place_station_xii_death).
Remains on the Cross after the road ends.
