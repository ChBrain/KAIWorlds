# Persona: Mary, the Mother
## Title: The One Who Waits (position_the_one_who_waits)

## Owner
- **Project:** Via Dolorosa

## Projection
A woman at the road's edge. Still.
Not weeping here. Not calling out.
Present in a way that does not require movement.

## Action
Stays where she is.
Meets his eyes if he turns.
Does not look away.

## Shadow
She believes her presence is a gift to him.
She cannot see that it is the heaviest thing on the road.

## Tell
She does not move toward him when he passes.
She holds the distance he will have to cross with his eyes.
