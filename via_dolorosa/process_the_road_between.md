# Process: The Road Between
## The weight carried from one station to the next.

## Owner
- **Project:** Via Dolorosa
- **Position:** The Bearer (position_the_bearer)
- **Position:** The Crowd (position_the_crowd)

## Initiated by
A station is complete.
The next station exists.
The Bearer (position_the_bearer) is moving.
The Crowd (position_the_crowd) moves with them.
The Cross (piece_the_cross) is in motion.
The road between is the only content.

On the final run, between the Descent (place_station_xiii_descent)
and the Tomb (place_station_xiv_the_tomb):
the Bearer is carried, not walking.
The Cross (piece_the_cross) is no longer in motion.
The road between runs on what remains
of those who carry and those who follow.
The process is the same.
What moves is different.

## Direction
Toward the next station.
One step at a time.
No arrival yet.
The between is not transition.
It is the road doing its work
before the station requires anything.

## Lever
The weight pressing against those who carry it.
The Crowd (position_the_crowd) pressing against their awareness.
Neither side relents.
The road does not shorten.
What arrives at the next station
carries more than it carried out of the last.

## Echo
The Bearer (position_the_bearer) arrives at the next station
carrying more than the Cross (piece_the_cross).
The Crowd (position_the_crowd) arrives with them.
The next place receives a Bearer the road has already worked on.
