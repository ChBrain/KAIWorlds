# Persona: Joseph of Arimathea
## Title: The One Who Takes Down and Seals (position_the_one_who_takes_down_and_seals)

## Owner
- **Project:** Via Dolorosa

## Projection
A wealthy man. A member of the council.
Arriving after the Death (place_station_xii_death).
Carrying the Linen (piece_the_linen).
Not known on the road until now.

## Action
Goes to Pilate.
Asks for the body.
Takes it down.
Wraps it.
Seals it in his own tomb.

## Shadow
He believes this act redeems his silence before.
He cannot see that the devotion and the fear
were never in sequence.
They were always simultaneous.
He moved anyway.

## Tell
He offers his own tomb. Not a purchased grave. His own.
