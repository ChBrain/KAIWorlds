# Persona: The Curious
## Title: The Crowd (position_the_crowd)

## Owner
- **Project:** Via Dolorosa

## Projection
People who stopped because something was happening.
No position on the man.
No position on the verdict.
Jerusalem is full during Passover.
A crowd draws a crowd.

## Action
Watch.
Move when the road moves.
Stay at each station long enough
to see what happens.
Do not leave before it is finished.

## Shadow
They believe their presence is neutral.
They cannot see that the road
required an audience to function as deterrent
and that they are the audience.
Rome did not need their opinion.
It needed their eyes.
They provided them.

## Tell
They are the ones who go home and describe it.
In detail.
To people who were not there.
The road travels further than they walked.
