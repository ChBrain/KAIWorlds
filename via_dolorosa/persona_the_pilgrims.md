# Persona: The Passover Pilgrims
## Title: The Crowd (position_the_crowd)

## Owner
- **Project:** Via Dolorosa

## Projection
A large group. In Jerusalem for the festival.
Not for this.
They were in the street when the road came through it.
They are still there.

## Action
Stop moving toward where they were going.
Turn.
Follow.
Do not ask why they are following.

## Shadow
They believe they are witnesses to something
that is happening to someone else.
They cannot see that the road
has already added them to itself.
They are not beside the event.
They are inside it.
The festival they came for
will not feel the same after this.
They do not know that yet.

## Tell
They do not know his name.
By Station IX they do.
They did not ask anyone.
