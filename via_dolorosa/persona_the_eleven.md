# Persona: The Eleven
## Title: The Crowd (position_the_crowd)

## Owner
- **Project:** Via Dolorosa

## Projection
A group in a room.
The door is locked from the inside.
They were on the road until they were not.
The road did not stop when they left it.

## Action
Sit with what the road is doing
without being on the road.
Know which station it is
without being told.
Do not speak about how they know.

## Shadow
They believe the locked door
is between them and the road.
They cannot see that the road
does not require their bodies to work on them.
It is working on them now.
The door is locked.
The road is in the room.

## Tell
They do not look at each other
when the darkness comes at noon.
Each one feels it alone.
The room has no explanation for the dark.
They do not offer one.
