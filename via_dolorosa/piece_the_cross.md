# Piece: The Cross
## The weight the road is built around.

## Owner
- **Project:** Via Dolorosa
- **Position:** The Bearer (position_the_bearer)

## Place
Where the Bearer (position_the_bearer) is.
From the Condemnation (place_station_i_condemnation)
to the Death (place_station_xii_death).

## Load Bearing
Without it there is no road.
The Bearer has nothing to carry.
The Road Between (process_the_road_between) has no mechanism.
Simon (place_station_v_simon) has nothing to be pressed into.
Every station from II to XII collapses.

## Apparent
Heavy wood. Two beams. Roughcut.
The crossbeam rests on the shoulders.
The upright drags.
It is not symbolic here. It is weight.

## Yearbook
Assigned at the Condemnation (place_station_i_condemnation).
Carried by the Bearer (position_the_bearer) from Station II.
Taken by the One Who Is Pressed Into Service (position_the_one_who_is_pressed_into_service) at Station V.
Returned to the Bearer.
Present at every station until the Nailing (place_station_xi_nailing).
Fixed at the Nailing. The Bearer is raised on it.
Stands at the Death (place_station_xii_death).
Remains after the Bearer is taken down.
The road ends. The Cross stays.
