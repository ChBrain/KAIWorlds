# Persona: The Women of Jerusalem
## Title: The One Who Weeps (position_the_one_who_weeps)

## Owner
- **Project:** Via Dolorosa

## Projection
A group of women at the road's edge.
Audible. Visibly grieving.
They came to mourn publicly.
This is what mourning looks like here.

## Action
Weep for the Bearer (position_the_bearer).
Do not look away.
Receive what he says to them.

## Shadow
They believe their grief is an act of witness.
They cannot see that it is also shaped by
what mourning is supposed to look like
in this place and time.

## Tell
After he speaks, they weep the same way.
The shape of the grief does not change.
