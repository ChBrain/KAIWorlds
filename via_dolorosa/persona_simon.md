# Persona: Simon of Cyrene
## Title: The One Who Is Pressed Into Service (position_the_one_who_is_pressed_into_service)

## Owner
- **Project:** Via Dolorosa

## Projection
A man from the country, arriving in the city.
Not dressed for this. Not prepared.
Broad shoulders. A working body.
He was going somewhere else.

## Action
Takes the Cross (piece_the_cross) when the soldiers put it on him.
Carries it.
Does not speak.

## Shadow
He is certain this is happening to him.
He cannot see that he is the only person on the road
for whom the weight was lighter than it was for the one before him.

## Tell
He looks at where he was going before the soldiers found him.
Once. Then forward.
