# Persona: The Bearer
## Title: The Bearer (position_the_bearer)

## Owner
- **Project:** Via Dolorosa

## Projection
A man on a road.
Marked before the road began.
The Cross (piece_the_cross) on his shoulders.
Moving.
The Crowd (position_the_crowd) on both sides.
He does not look at them.

## Action
Keeps moving.
Falls.
Rises.
Keeps moving.
Does not ask to stop.
Does not ask for what is not offered.
Speaks to the Women (persona_the_women) at Station VIII.
Not for himself.
Speaks from the Cross (piece_the_cross) at Station XII.
Not for himself either.

## Shadow
He believes the road is what is being asked of him.
He cannot see that the road is also what he is.
The carrying is not the test.
The carrying is the person.
He does not know this.
He could not carry it if he did.

## Tell
When he falls, he rises without looking back
at the ground that held him.
