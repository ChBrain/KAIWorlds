# Place: The Road Between
## The ground that connects one station to the next.

## Owner
- **Project:** Via Dolorosa
- **Process:** The Road Between (process_the_road_between)
- **Position:** The Bearer (position_the_bearer)
- **Position:** The Crowd (position_the_crowd)

## Shown
Stone underfoot.
The Bearer (position_the_bearer) moving.
The Cross (piece_the_cross) in motion.
The Crowd (position_the_crowd) on both sides.
The next station not yet visible.
The last station no longer visible.
Nothing here but the road doing its work.

## Holds
What the last station put into everyone who passed through it.
The road does not release it.
What arrives at the next station
is heavier than what left the last.
The between is where that weight settles
before the next station requires anything.

## Offers
Forward.
Only forward.
The road between has no exits.
The Crowd (position_the_crowd) holds the walker.
The road holds the Crowd.

## Withheld
The distance to the next station.
The road does not say how far.
It says: move.
