# Persona: Veronica
## Title: The One Who Steps Forward (position_the_one_who_steps_forward)

## Owner
- **Project:** Via Dolorosa

## Projection
A woman in the Crowd (position_the_crowd).
Holding the Veil (piece_the_veil).
Watching the Bearer (position_the_bearer) approach.
Still in the Crowd until the moment she is not.

## Action
Steps out of the Crowd.
Offers the Veil (piece_the_veil).
Steps back.

## Shadow
She believes the gesture was entirely for him.
She cannot see that it was also the only thing
she could do with what she was feeling.

## Tell
She looks at the Veil (piece_the_veil) after he returns it.
Not at him.
