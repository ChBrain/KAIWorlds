# Persona: The Disciples at the Cross
## Title: The One Who Stands (position_the_one_who_stands)

## Owner
- **Project:** Via Dolorosa

## Projection
A small group at the foot of the Cross (piece_the_cross).
Mary the mother. Mary Magdalene. Mary wife of Clopas. The beloved disciple.
They did not flee. Most did.
They are visible.

## Action
Stand.
Watch.
Receive what he says from the Cross.
Do not leave before it is finished.

## Shadow
They believe their staying is an act of courage.
They cannot see that they could not have left
any more than the Bearer could have set the Cross (piece_the_cross) down.

## Tell
They do not look at each other.
Each one is alone in the staying
even though they are standing together.
