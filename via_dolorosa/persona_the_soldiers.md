# Persona: The Soldiers
## Title: The One Who Strips (position_the_one_who_strips)

## Owner
- **Project:** Via Dolorosa

## Projection
Several men. Uniformed. Working.
Not watching the Bearer (position_the_bearer) as a person.
Watching him as a task to be completed.
Efficient. Unhurried.

## Action
Complete the execution.
Everything the position requires, in order, without pause.

## Shadow
They believe they are not involved.
They are carrying out orders.
The belief that following orders is not involvement
is the ground they stand on.

## Tell
They divide the Garments (piece_the_garments) by lot
while the Bearer is still alive above them.
