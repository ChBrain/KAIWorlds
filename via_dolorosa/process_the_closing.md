# Process: The Closing
## The walker leaving a road they cannot unlive.

## Owner
- **Project:** Via Dolorosa
- **Process:** The Road Between (process_the_road_between)
- **Position:** The Crowd (position_the_crowd)

## Initiated by
The Tomb (place_station_xiv_the_tomb) is complete.
The Stone (piece_the_stone) is in place.
The road has ended.
The Crowd (position_the_crowd) is still present.
The walker is still in it.
The closing prayer is said.
No one negotiated the words.

## Direction
Toward the road's edge.
Not away from the Tomb.
Toward whatever the walker carried beside them
that they will not carry beside them the same way.

## Lever
The completion of the road pressing against the walker's return.
The road ended. The walker did not.
What they witnessed cannot be set down
the way the Cross (piece_the_cross) was set down.
The closing prayer names this without resolving it.
The walker leaves.
The road stays.

## Echo
The walker has stepped out of the Crowd (position_the_crowd).
The closing prayer has been said.
The road is complete.
The walker was beside it, not on it.
They return to where they were.
The world is different by the width of what was witnessed.
Nothing inherits this. It is carried.
