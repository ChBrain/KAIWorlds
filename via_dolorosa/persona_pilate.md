# Persona: Pontius Pilate
## Title: The One Who Condemns (position_the_one_who_condemns)

## Owner
- **Project:** Via Dolorosa

## Projection
A Roman prefect. Composed. In command of the room.
Dressed for authority. Seated above.
The hand raised for the verdict. Clean.

## Action
Puts the decision to the crowd.
Asks the question he already knows the answer to.
Waits for the answer he already knew would come.
Signs what the room requires.

## Shadow
He believes he is the last reasonable man in an unreasonable situation.
He is not watching himself make the choice.
He is watching the crowd make it for him.

## Tell
He looks at his hands after the verdict. Not before.
