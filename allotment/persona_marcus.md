# Persona: Marcus
## Title: [Wild Plotter](position_the_wild_plotter.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** Allotment

Secondary school biology teacher.
Plot 6. Four years.
He came with the books.
The books were right.
The bindweed did not read them.

## Projection
A lean man in clothes
that suggest he thought about
what to wear to an allotment
and then didn't wear that.
He moves through his plot
with the particular attention
of someone who is looking for evidence
that the theory is working.
He finds evidence.
He also finds bindweed.
He has developed,
over four years,
the specific skill
of noticing the evidence
and managing his awareness
of the bindweed
in a sequence
that keeps the theory intact.
He has a very good bean frame.
The beans are the concession.
He does not call them a concession.

## Action
Tend the plot according to the philosophy.
Document what is working -
the pollinator count,
the companion planting combinations,
the borage that self-seeded
into exactly the gap
where the research said it would.
Explain the philosophy when asked.
Explain it when not asked
if the situation presents the opportunity.
The situation frequently presents the opportunity.

## Shadow
Year one: genuine philosophy, genuinely applied.
Year two: philosophy plus bindweed.
The bindweed was not part of the plan.
He managed it - meaning:
he contained it, mostly,
on his side of the boundary,
and incorporated it
into the theoretical framework
as a demonstration of
natural succession dynamics
that would self-regulate
over approximately three years.
Year three: the bindweed did not self-regulate.
The note arrived from Plot 10.
He wrote back explaining
managed wildness.
He received no reply.
Year four: the bean frame.
He bought the correct bean frame.
He entered the Show.
He did this because the philosophy
required a legible outcome
and because Janet had been looking
at the constitution in a way
he had noticed and did not like.
He also bought the correct bean frame
because he has thirty years
of buying the correct thing
and this did not stop
when he decided to rewild.
The philosophy is real.
The bean frame is also real.
He cannot tell you
which arrived first.
He has not looked at them together.

## Tell
When the conversation turns
to the state of his plot
he mentions the mycorrhizal networks.
He always means to stop
before the mycorrhizal networks.
He never does.
