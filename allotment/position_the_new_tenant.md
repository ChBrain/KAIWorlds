# Position: The New Tenant
## The laminated plan meeting the plot.

## Owner
- **Project:** Allotment
- **Process:** [process_the_season_opening](process_the_season_opening.md)
- **Process:** [process_the_waiting_list](process_the_waiting_list.md)
- **Place:** [The Plot](place_the_plot.md)

## Has
The idea of the plot.
Refined across four years on the waiting list
into something considerably more perfect
than any plot could actually be.
The plan - laminated or otherwise -
exists before the persona arrives.
So does the enthusiasm.
So does the complete absence
of knowledge about what this specific soil
does in a wet June.
The position arrives fully equipped
with everything except experience.

## Orders
Begin.
The position does not wait
for the right moment
because the right moment
is always next weekend
and next weekend it will rain.
The seeds go in.
Some of them at the wrong depth.
Some of them at the wrong time.
Some of them in the wrong place entirely -
the Serious Cultivator on Plot 7
will notice this
and will mention it.
The position does not wait
for the mention to arrive.
It begins anyway.

## Loses
The plan.
Not all at once.
The plan loses first contact
with the plot
somewhere in the second week
of April.
They were certain the plan
was based on research.
It was.
The research was not about this plot.
The plan survives in modified form.
In significantly modified form.
The position does not permit
attachment to the original plan.
The plot has opinions.
The opinions were here first.

## Drives
On the loss of the plan -
improvises.
Asks someone.
Probably the wrong someone.
Gets advice that is either
excellent and unwelcome
or enthusiastic and incorrect.
Learns to tell the difference
by the end of the first season.
Returns for the second season
with a new plan.
Slightly less laminated.
Slightly more realistic.
The position is already working.
