# Process: The Tending
## The accumulation that happens while you are doing something else.

## Owner
- **Project:** Allotment
- **Place:** [The Plot](place_the_plot.md)

## Initiated by
The seeds are in the ground.
It is sometime between March and October.
The plot requires attention.
Not dramatic attention - continuous, small,
unglamorous attention.
Watering. Weeding. Checking.
Watering again.
The plot does not acknowledge
any of this.
The plot does not have a mechanism
for acknowledgement.
It grows or it doesn't
according to its own logic
which is related to but not identical with
what the person tending it intended.

## Direction
Toward the harvest -
or toward the discovery
that the harvest is not what was planned,
which is the more common direction.
The courgettes have taken over.
The carrots have forked.
The beans are extraordinary
or the beans have failed entirely
with no discernible reason for either.
The brassicas have been eaten.
Something has been eating them
that has never been caught
and may not be catchable.

## Lever
The person pressing against the plot
and the plot pressing back.
The carrying force:
the person - their intentions,
their schedule, their increasingly complicated
relationship with slugs,
the Saturdays they cannot come
because of other things,
the Saturdays they come
and find the other things were a mistake.
The resistance: the plot -
which grows on its own schedule,
responds to rain that came last Tuesday
rather than the watering that came Thursday,
and is entirely indifferent
to what was planned for it.
The tending produces something
in the person
that is not quite what they came for
and is harder to name
than the courgettes.

## Echo
The season has progressed.
The plot looks different
from how it looked in March.
The person looks different too -
incrementally, invisibly,
in the specific way
that sustained attention to something
that does not respond
changes the person giving the attention.
What was said on the path in April
runs in the soil in June.
The person who said it
does not know it arrived.
The person it arrived in
does not know where it came from.
The plot holds both of them
without filing the connection.
[process_the_show](process_the_show.md) approaches.
[process_the_boundary_dispute](process_the_boundary_dispute.md)
may have begun.
