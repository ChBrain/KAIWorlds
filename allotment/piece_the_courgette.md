# Piece: The Courgette
## The thing that wins whether you want it to or not.

## Owner
- **Project:** Allotment

## Place
[The Plot](place_the_plot.md).
Every plot on the site
where someone grew a courgette
and took their eye off it for a week.
Which is every plot on the site
where someone grew a courgette.

## Load Bearing
Without it the allotment
loses its universal leveller.
The Serious Cultivator's rows are straight.
The Wild Plotter's plot is managed chaos.
The New Tenant has a laminated plan.
None of this affects the courgette.
The courgette is indifferent
to experience, philosophy, and spreadsheets.
It grows.
It grows faster than expected.
It grows faster than is convenient.
It becomes a marrow while you are
on holiday for five days
and there is nothing to be done about it.
Remove the courgette and the allotment
loses the thing that reminds everyone
simultaneously
that the plot has its own agenda.

## Apparent
Starts as a small, promising seedling.
Becomes a medium courgette.
Becomes a large courgette
while you are doing something else.
Becomes a thing that requires
both hands to lift
and which nobody in the house
will agree to eat
because they are tired of courgette.
Is given to neighbours.
Neighbours are also tired of courgette.
Is left on the path
with a sign saying FREE.
Is gone by Sunday.
Someone was not tired of courgette.
Or they were and took it anyway
because it was free.

## Yearbook
Every plot on every allotment site
in Britain
in any year when courgettes were grown.
The year it won on Plot 11.
The year it won on Plot 11 again.
The year it will win on Plot 11 again.
The year someone on Plot 7
entered a courgette in the Show
in the Courgette category
and then also in the Marrow category
on the grounds that by the morning of the Show
it qualified for both.
The committee reviewed this.
The constitution did not cover it.
It has been covered now.
The courgette did not care about the ruling.
It continued growing.
