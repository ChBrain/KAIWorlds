# Position: The Child Who Was Brought
## Here because someone else decided. Receiving everything anyway.

## Owner
- **Project:** Allotment
- **Process:** [process_the_tending](process_the_tending.md)
- **Place:** [The Plot](place_the_plot.md)

## Has
The freedom from having chosen.
It exists before this persona arrived
and could not have been arranged -
it disappears the moment
the child puts their own name
on a waiting list.
The position holds it
only while it is still intact:
the state of being in a place
because someone else decided,
before the self has formed
opinions about where it should be.
The plot does not ask anything
of the child.
The child does not know
this is unusual.

## Orders
Nothing.
The position is the only one on this site
that issues no instruction.
It does not require tending,
or attending meetings,
or knowing where the boundary is,
or having a theory.
It requires only presence.
The presence does the rest
without being asked.

## Loses
The not-noticing.
They were certain the afternoon was
boring, or fine, or just a thing
that happened on Saturdays.
Then something arrived -
the smell of the turned earth,
the specific weight of the fork,
the old tree, the worm,
the strawberry eaten warm before
anyone said it was for the Show -
and the noticing happened
before the boredom finished.
The child does not know
they have lost the not-noticing.
They are too busy noticing.

## Drives
On the loss of not-noticing -
the body files it.
Not the mind. The body.
The mind will forget this Saturday
by Tuesday.
The body will not forget it
for forty years.
On an unremarkable Tuesday
four decades from now
it will put a name on a waiting list
and not be entirely sure why
and the reason will be here,
in this afternoon,
in the smell of the earth
and the weight of the fork
and the shade of the old tree
at four o'clock.
The position does not announce this.
It does not need to.
