# Piece: The Runner Bean
## Thirty-one years of correct technique, entered anonymously.

## Owner
- **Project:** Allotment
- **Persona:** [Derek](persona_derek.md)

## Place
[The Plot](place_the_plot.md).
Plot 3, the bed along the south wall.
Started inside in April.
Out in May.
On the frame by June.
The frame Derek made himself.
In 2003, and repaired twice since,
using the same method.

## Load Bearing
Without it the Show has no standard.
The runner bean category exists
because there are plots that grow runner beans
and some of them grow them correctly.
Derek's runner bean is the benchmark
against which the category
has been measured for eleven years.
Remove it and the Show
loses its resident certainty -
the thing that will probably win
before the entries are submitted.
The certainty is not comfortable for everyone.
It is structural.

## Apparent
Long. Straight. Even colour.
The particular deep green
of a bean that has been grown
in well-prepared soil
with correct spacing
and adequate water
applied at the right time.
Nothing flashy.
Correct. Consistently, demonstrably correct.
Placed on the show bench
on a clean white plate
with a label showing the plot number.
The plot number is known to everyone.

## Yearbook
Eleven consecutive wins
in the runner bean category.
Each one the result
of the same technique,
applied with the same attention,
to soil that has been improved
by thirty-one years of doing it right.
The technique is not secret.
Derek has offered it freely
to anyone who asked.
Two people have asked.
Neither of them has implemented it correctly.
This is not their fault.
The technique requires thirty-one years.
You cannot begin in the middle.
This year: a new entry from Plot 6.
The bean frame on Plot 6 is metal, expensive.
The beans are, from a distance, considerable.
Derek has looked at them once from the path.
He has not looked at them again.
He is aware of them at all times.
