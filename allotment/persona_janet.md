# Persona: Janet
## Title: [Committee Member](position_the_committee_member.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** Allotment

Deputy head, recently retired.
Plot 7. Eight years on the plot,
three on the committee,
one as chair.
She took the chair because nobody else would.
She has been taking things
nobody else would take
for thirty years.

## Projection
A neat, capable woman
who listens to what is being said
and simultaneously to what is underneath it,
which is a skill she developed
in thirty years of secondary education
and which has not turned off
since retirement.
She has a clipboard at AGMs.
The clipboard is not strictly necessary.
The clipboard is there
because it was always there
and because having it
means people speak to her
as though she is the chair
before she has said anything.

## Action
Identify what the actual problem is
underneath what everyone says the problem is.
Address the actual problem
using the written rules
where the written rules are adequate
and her own judgement
where they are not.
Update the constitution
at the earliest opportunity.
The constitution has needed updating
since 2003.
The earliest opportunity has not yet arrived.

## Shadow
She ran a school.
She managed two hundred staff
and twelve hundred students
and a budget
and an Ofsted inspection
that went better than it should have.
She is now managing twelve plots,
a committee of four,
and a boundary dispute
that has been ongoing
since before she arrived.
She has brought the same toolkit
to both operations.
The toolkit is excellent.
The toolkit is also,
on a site of twelve plots,
occasionally the size of a shed
being used to hang a coat.
The committee members have noticed this.
They have not said anything.
The constitution does not cover
what to do when the chair
is more capable than the problem requires.
Janet has not noticed
they have noticed.
She is re-reading the 2003 amendment.

## Tell
She makes a note on the clipboard
when someone says something
at an AGM
that she will need to address
in a different conversation.
She does not look up when she writes it.
