# Position: The Neighbouring Plot
## The person you did not choose and cannot avoid.

## Owner
- **Project:** Allotment
- **Process:** [process_the_tending](process_the_tending.md)
- **Process:** [process_the_boundary_dispute](process_the_boundary_dispute.md)
- **Place:** [The Plot](place_the_plot.md)

## Has
The adjacency.
It exists before either persona arrived
and will exist after both have gone.
The boundary between plots
is a fact of the site's layout,
not a choice.
The position does not permit
the selection of neighbours.
Whoever holds the adjacent plot
is the neighbour.
This is true regardless of
their approach to bindweed,
their bonfire timing,
their conversational habits,
or the question of whether
their shed is four inches
over the boundary line.

## Orders
Coexist.
The position does not specify
what coexistence requires
when the neighbour's runner beans
cast shade on your beds
from July onwards.
It does not specify
what coexistence requires
when the neighbour's
unsolicited advice
is delivered every Saturday
with apparent goodwill
and absolute certainty.
It does not specify
what coexistence requires
when the matter of 2019
has never been formally resolved
and both parties
tend their plots
every Saturday
without looking up.
The position requires coexistence.
The terms are negotiated continuously
and never finalised.

## Loses
The private plot.
They were certain the boundary
was a meaningful line -
their side, their decisions,
their approach to the question
of whether to let the borage
self-seed.
The bindweed takes it.
The boundary is a legal line.
It is not a horticultural one.
What grows in the neighbour's plot
does not stay in the neighbour's plot.
What the neighbour does on Saturday
is audible from the far end of this plot.
The private plot exists on the map.
In practice the two plots
are in continuous negotiation
whether or not either party
has agreed to negotiate.

## Drives
On the loss of the private plot -
tends the boundary edge
with particular attention.
The beds nearest the fence
are always the tidiest.
This is not noticed consciously.
It is noticed.
