# Process: The AGM
## The constitution of the site, as currently understood.

## Owner
- **Project:** Allotment
- **Place:** [The Committee Room](place_the_committee_room.md)

## Initiated by
It is November.
The season is over.
The notice has been posted
on the board by the gate -
fourteen days' notice,
as per the constitution,
section 4, subsection 2,
which was last amended in 2003
and which several people present
believe was amended incorrectly
at the time and have not stopped
believing this since.
There is an agenda.
The agenda has six items.
Item six is Any Other Business.
Item six will take forty minutes.

## Direction
Toward the minutes.
The AGM must produce minutes.
The minutes are the only thing
that is unambiguously produced
by an AGM.
Everything else - decisions,
resolutions, the matter of the chickens,
the amendment to the bonfire policy -
is produced in a form
that will be interpreted differently
by different attendees
on the drive home.
The minutes are Drumknott's work.
The minutes are accurate.
This does not prevent dispute.

## Lever
The constitution pressing against
the assembled membership.
The carrying force:
the agenda - the treasurer's report,
the proposal to repaint the gate,
the question of whether a plot holder's
composting arrangements comply
with the site rules as written
or merely as understood.
The resistance:
the assembled membership -
each of whom has come
with one thing they need the AGM to address
and four things they need it to not address
in the way it addressed them last year.
The chair attempts to hold the line.
The chair has been attempting to hold the line
for eleven years.
The chair is very tired.
The chair will stand again next year
because nobody else will do it.

## Echo
The minutes have been taken.
Drumknott has recorded what was said,
in the order it was said,
with no editorial comment.
The minutes will be disputed
at the next AGM
on the grounds that they do not capture
the spirit of what was decided.
The treasurer's report has been accepted.
The gate will be repainted
in a colour that was agreed upon
and which three people
already regret.
The chicken question has been deferred
to a working group
which will report back
at a date to be confirmed.
The working group has not yet been formed.
Item six took forty-seven minutes.
[process_the_winter_plot](process_the_winter_plot.md) is already running.
