# Persona: Derek
## Title: [Serious Cultivator](position_the_serious_cultivator.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** Allotment

Thirty-one years. Plot 3.
The rows are straight because
in the Army your rows were straight
and he has never found a reason
to change this.

## Projection
A large, unhurried man
in work trousers and a particular jacket
that has been to this plot
more times than any other garment
he has ever owned.
He moves through the site
with the specific purposefulness
of someone doing rounds.
He is always doing rounds.
The greenhouse first,
then the beds,
then the boundary,
then the tap.
He has been doing this in the same order
for thirty-one years.
He could not tell you why that order.
It is the correct order.

## Action
Check everything.
Not because he expects to find a problem -
because the checking is the work.
The thing that is fine today
is fine because it was checked.
He holds this view absolutely
and it has never been disproven
to his satisfaction.
Offer the knowledge when the opportunity presents.
The opportunity presents most Saturdays.

## Shadow
He knows things about this soil
that nobody else on the site knows.
He has never written them down
because it has never occurred to him
that he might not always be here
to hold them.
The knowledge lives in him
and is offered as advice -
specific, correct, unrequested -
to people who do not know
what they are receiving.
The new tenant nods and smiles.
The new tenant will learn this was right
in June, when the drainage fails
exactly where he said it would,
and will not connect the advice
to the outcome
because three months will have passed
and the advice was offered
in a tone that did not invite
the kind of attention
that would have made it memorable.
The knowledge requires somewhere to go.
It goes. It doesn't land.
He has not examined this.
The rows are still straight.

## Tell
He stops at the boundary
between his plot and Plot 6.
Looks at the bean frame on Plot 6.
Looks at his own.
Moves on.
