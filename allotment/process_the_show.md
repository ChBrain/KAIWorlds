# Process: The Show
## The harvest judged.

## Owner
- **Project:** Allotment
- **Place:** [The Show Bench](place_the_show_bench.md)

## Initiated by
It is August.
The show has been announced.
The categories have been posted -
same categories as last year,
same categories as every year,
with the addition of a new category
(Decorative Gourd)
that was proposed at last year's AGM
and which several people
consider a mistake.
The entries must be submitted
by ten in the morning.
The judging begins at eleven.
The results are announced at two.
Between eleven and two
nobody is allowed in the hall.
This rule is taken seriously.

## Direction
Toward the results.
The judge is from another site.
The judge does not know
the history of the runner bean category.
The judge does not know
that the person who has won
the runner bean category
for eleven consecutive years
is standing by the far wall
with an expression of
studied indifference
that has taken years to perfect.
The judge applies the criteria.
The criteria are: length, straightness,
colour, and - this is the contentious one -
what the schedule calls
*overall presentation.*

## Lever
What was grown pressing against
what was grown by the person
in the next plot.
The carrying force:
eleven months of effort -
the watering, the feeding,
the exact spacing,
the decision in June
that turned out to be correct
or didn't.
The resistance:
the judge, who has their own views
about *overall presentation,*
and a new entrant
who has entered the runner bean category
for the first time
and whose entry is -
and this must be acknowledged -
considerable.
The carrying force and the resistance
are both entirely serious.
The comedy is not in the bean.
The comedy is in what the bean means
to the person who grew it.

## Echo
The results have been announced.
The winner of the runner bean category
has been announced.
The runner-up has congratulated the winner
in terms that were entirely correct
and which will be replayed
in the car on the way home
for signs of insincerity.
The Decorative Gourd category
produced a winner
who appeared surprised by this.
The judge has been thanked
and has left the site
without knowing what they have done.
The results stand.
They will be discussed
until next August.
