# Piece: The Old Tree
## Here before the allotment. Here after.

## Owner
- **Project:** Allotment

## Place
The corner of the site
where the path bends around it.
It was not put there.
The path bends around it
because the path was put there
after the tree.
Every survey of this site
has noted it and worked around it.
The plots are arranged around the tree.
Nobody arranged this consciously.
It is simply the case.

## Load Bearing
Without it the site has no memory
older than its oldest plot holder.
With it the site sits inside something
that predates everyone on it
and will outlast everyone on it.
Remove the tree and the allotment
is a set of plots with an administration.
Keep it and the plots exist
inside a longer time
that does not consult the constitution
and does not enter the Show.
The tree is the reason
the site feels like something
rather than just a piece of land
that has been divided up.
Most plot holders cannot tell you why
they feel this.
They feel it anyway
when they walk past the tree.

## Apparent
Large. Older than the site.
Species disputed - oak, or possibly ash,
the older plot holders disagree
and neither will concede.
The bark is the bark of something
that has been bark for a long time.
The roots have reached
under at least two plots
and are, technically, in violation
of the tenancy agreements
of those plot holders.
Nobody has raised this
with the committee.
Nobody will raise this
with the committee.

## Yearbook
Was here when this was a field.
Was here when the field was surveyed.
Was here when the first plots were marked.
Was here for the first AGM,
the first boundary dispute,
the first note left on a gate,
the first winner of the runner bean category.
Will be here for the next boundary dispute.
Will be here for the next winner.
Does not know what a runner bean is.
Does not know what an allotment is.
Holds the site in its root system
without knowing it is holding anything.
Plot holders eat their lunch under it
in August when it is hot.
They do not think about this.
They simply go to where the shade is.
The shade has been there
longer than any of them
and will be there
when none of them are.
This is not something
they think about at lunch.
It is present anyway
in the quality of the shade.
