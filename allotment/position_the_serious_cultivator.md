# Position: The Serious Cultivator
## The knowledge the plot accumulated before anyone thought to ask for it.

## Owner
- **Project:** Allotment
- **Process:** [process_the_tending](process_the_tending.md)
- **Process:** [process_the_show](process_the_show.md)
- **Place:** [The Plot](place_the_plot.md)

## Has
The accumulated knowledge of this specific soil.
It exists before this persona arrived -
built across years of watching
what this particular patch of ground
does in a wet June,
what it does in a dry August,
where the drainage fails,
where the frost sits late.
The position knows things
that are not in any book
because they are specific to this plot
and this plot only.
The position has been here long enough
to have opinions about the weather
that have subsequently proven correct.

## Orders
Tend it correctly.
The position does not explain
what correctly means.
The position assumes this is known.
The position has always assumed this is known.
The assumption is the problem.

## Loses
The audience.
They were certain that the knowledge -
the straight rows, the labelled canes,
the yield that exceeds all neighbouring plots
by a margin that is not accidental -
would speak for itself.
The new people on the site
do not know what they are looking at.
They see a tidy plot.
They do not see thirty years.
The position has been speaking
to people who cannot hear the language
for longer than it has noticed.

## Drives
On the loss of audience -
offers advice.
Unsolicited.
Specific.
Almost always correct.
Almost always unwelcome.
Both of these things simultaneously.
The position cannot stop.
The knowledge requires somewhere to go.
