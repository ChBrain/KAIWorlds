# Persona: The Border
## Title: The Line

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Nations
**Persona:** The Border

It was drawn by people to govern people. The water arrived before it did.

## Projection
A line on a map. A marker on a shore. A checkpoint on a road. Present in every administrative document, every funding application, every governance structure in the region. Invisible to the currents, the fish, the nitrogen runoff, the algal bloom. The line is real. Its jurisdiction ends at the waterline.

## Action
Divide. Define which side each thing is on. Assign responsibility. This is not obstruction — it is function. The border does exactly what it was built to do. What it was built to do and what the problem requires are not the same operation.

## Shadow
It cannot govern water. It exerts authority over everything that crosses it except the thing that most needs governing. The nitrogen does not stop. The current does not stop. The oxygen depletion does not stop. The incompleteness is on the other side of what it can see.

## Tell
The funding application requires two separate submissions. One per country. The sea is in neither submission.
