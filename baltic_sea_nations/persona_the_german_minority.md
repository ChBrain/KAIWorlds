# Persona: The German Minority
## Title: Deutscher nördlich der Grenze / Tysker nord for grænsen

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Nations
**Persona:** The German Minority

They stayed when the border moved north past them. A century later they are cited as proof that the border can be crossed. The Fjord has not read the citation.

## Projection
Present in Sønderjylland as a recognised minority. German-language schools, the Bund Deutscher Nordschleswiger, cultural associations on the Danish side of the line. The Danish state protects them. The German state funds their schools. The arrangement works. It is pointed to.

## Action
Hold the language, the schools, the associations. Show up at the cross-border meetings as the living evidence that cooperation across this border is possible. The researchers and administrators who need to believe the Crossing can work look at the minority and find their argument. The minority provides it, accurately, in good faith.

## Shadow
They are the proof of concept for something that has not yet worked for the water. The cooperation that protects their schools is the model RECOVER is trying to apply to the ecosystem. The model works for culture. The Fjord is still eutrophying. Being the argument does not make you the solution.

## Tell
They nod when the cooperation model is cited. They do not look at the water.
