# Process: The Farming
## The application that feeds the field and the fjord.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Nations
**Persona:** The Border

## Initiates
Spring on both shores. Nitrogen and phosphorus applied to the fields of Schleswig-Holstein and Sønderjylland according to national agricultural guidelines. The application begins before the water quality report from last season has been acted on. It begins before the monitoring cycle has registered what last season left behind.

## Direction
Toward the water. The nutrients leave the fields through soil, groundwater, and drainage into the Schlei and the Flensburg Fjord. Both shores drain into the same inlets. The drainage does not follow the border. It follows the gradient.

## Lever
The agricultural guidelines. Germany sets its own nitrogen application limits, buffer strip requirements, and drainage regulations. Denmark sets its own. Each set is nationally correct. Together they govern two halves of one shared nutrient load — without a mechanism for accounting for the whole.

## Echo
A combined nutrient load in the Schlei and the Flensburg Fjord. Neither national monitoring system contains the full figure. The fields on both shores are already being prepared for the next application. The water is still carrying the last one.
