# Place: The Flensburg Fjord
## Location: Danish-German border. Flensburg at the western end, open to the Baltic at the east.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Nations
**Persona:** The Border

## Shown
- The fjord. 36 kilometres long. Narrow, deep in the middle channel, shallower on the shores.
- Flensburg at the western end — a German city in Schleswig-Holstein.
- Aabenraa to the north — a Danish city in Sønderjylland. The border crosses the fjord approximately halfway along its length.
- The water does not change at the crossing.
- The southern shore: Schleswig-Holstein. The Ministry for Energy Transition, Climate Protection, Environment and Nature has jurisdiction. Kreis Schleswig-Flensburg administers the immediate shore.
- The northern shore: Sønderjylland. Aabenraa Kommune has jurisdiction.
- The fish. They move between jurisdictions without paperwork.
- The nutrients from agriculture on both shores. They move between jurisdictions without paperwork.
- [The Border Marker](piece_the_border_marker.md). On the shore. Not in the water.
- [The Schlei](place_the_schlei.md) — connected at the Baltic end. What the Schlei carries arrives here.

## Withheld
