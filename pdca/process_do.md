# Process: Do
## The Hypothesis meeting the Gemba.

## Owner
**Project:** PDCA

**Persona:** The Operative

## Initiates
[The Hypothesis](piece_the_hypothesis.md) exists.
The [Gemba](place_the_gemba.md) is ready.
The Operative is in the room.
The Architect is not.
The gap between what was planned
and what the Gemba requires
is not in the room.
It is the room.

## Direction
Toward [The Result](piece_the_result.md).
Not toward the Hypothesis —
toward what actually happens
when the Hypothesis meets
the place it was built to improve.

## Lever
The contact.
The [Operative](position_the_operative.md) implements
what the Architect designed.
The Gemba responds.
The response is not always
what the Hypothesis predicted.
The Operative absorbs the difference.

## Echo
The Result exists.
The [Witness](position_the_witness.md) receives it.
[Check](process_check.md) can begin.
