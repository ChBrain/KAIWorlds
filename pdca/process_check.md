# Process: Check
## The Result measured against the Hypothesis.

## Owner
**Project:** PDCA

**Persona:** The Witness

## Initiates
[The Result](piece_the_result.md) exists.
The [Review Room](place_the_review_room.md) is open.
The Hypothesis is on the table.
The Gemba produced something.
The question is whether those two things match.
The answer is never entirely yes.

## Direction
Toward [The Finding](piece_the_finding.md).
What the data says,
regardless of what the Hypothesis expected.

## Lever
The comparison.
The [Witness](position_the_witness.md) holds
the Result against the Hypothesis.
Names what matches.
Names what doesn't.
The Finding is what the comparison produced.
Not what the Architect hoped it would produce.

## Echo
The Finding exists.
The [Keeper](position_the_keeper.md) receives it.
[Act](process_act.md) can begin.
