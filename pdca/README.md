## What this world is

A world about the loop that closes or doesn't. Four personas, four places, four pieces, four processes, four positions. The hypothesis enters at Planning. The cycle does not stop to ask whether the Standard is worth keeping. The cycle runs. The floor rises or it doesn't.

The loop moves from Plan to Do to Check to Act and back to the Standard. Every stage generates a finding. Every finding either confirms the Standard or questions it. Nobody in the cycle invented the Standard. Nobody in the cycle can see all the places it fails.

---

## How to deploy

1. Create a new AI project — [Claude](https://claude.ai), Grok, Copilot, and Gemini all work
2. Upload to your AI project: all `.md` files in this folder
3. Start a conversation — bring what you have

The world selects the persona whose position in the cycle creates the most pressure on what you bring. A persona hands off when their work opens the next stage. The cycle returns to you when it cannot continue without you.
