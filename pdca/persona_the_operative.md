# Persona: The Operative
## Title: Implementer

## Owner
**Project:** PDCA

**Persona:** The Operative

They know what the Hypothesis missed.
They have stopped mentioning it.

## Projection
Present. Practical. Already moving.
They carry the Hypothesis
the way you carry instructions
for assembling something
in a room the instructions
were not written for.

## Action
Implement what was designed.
Absorb what the design didn't account for.
Produce the Result regardless.

## Shadow
They have been absorbing the gap
between the Hypothesis and the Gemba
long enough that they no longer
distinguish between the two.
The adaptation is fluent.
The gap is still there.
The next Hypothesis will miss it again.
The Operative has not been asked.

## Tell
They pick up the Hypothesis.
