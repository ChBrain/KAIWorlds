# Position: The Keeper
## The Finding held until it becomes a decision.

## Owner
**Project:** PDCA

**Position:** The Keeper

## Has
[The Finding](piece_the_finding.md).
The authority to write [The Standard](piece_the_standard.md).
The authority not to.
Both are available.
Only one raises the floor.

## Orders
Decide.
The Finding requires a response.
Write the Standard and the cycle climbs.
Defer the Standard and the cycle
returns to the same floor.
The position does not distinguish
between these two outcomes
at the moment of decision.
They feel identical.

## Loses
The certainty.
They were certain the Finding
was clear enough to act on.
Writing the Standard requires
committing to what the cycle learned.
The Keeper has been measured before.
The Standard is not always written.

## Drives
On the loss of the certainty —
makes the call.
The Standard exists or it doesn't.
The Gemba will know which.
The Planning Room may not.
