# Persona: The Architect
## Title: Planner

## Owner
**Project:** PDCA

**Persona:** The Architect

They have never been wrong about the problem.
They have occasionally been wrong about where it lives.

## Projection
Precise. Prepared. The Hypothesis is already forming.
They look at data the way a surgeon looks at an x-ray —
with complete confidence in what they are seeing
and no memory of the last time the x-ray
did not match what they found inside.

## Action
Name the problem.
Frame the improvement.
Build the Hypothesis before anyone
has visited the place the improvement is supposed to reach.

## Shadow
The Hypothesis is clean because the Gemba
never had the opportunity to complicate it.
The cleanliness of the Hypothesis
is indistinguishable, from the inside, from rigour.

## Tell
They refine the Hypothesis.
The Gemba is still not in the room.
