## The Canonical Texts

**Walter A. Shewhart**
*Economic Control of Quality of Manufactured Product*
D. Van Nostrand Company, 1931.

The original source. The control chart as the instrument that turns variation into a signal.
The wheel that precedes the cycle.

---

**W. Edwards Deming**
*Out of the Crisis*
MIT Press, 1982.

The PDCA cycle named and applied. The fourteen points. The chain reaction.
Quality drives cost drives market share drives jobs.

---

**W. Edwards Deming**
*The New Economics for Industry, Government, Education*
MIT Press, 1993.

The System of Profound Knowledge. Variation, psychology, and the theory of knowledge
as the foundation beneath the cycle. The Standard cannot improve what it cannot see.
