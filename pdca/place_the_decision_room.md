# Place: The Decision Room
## Location: Where the Finding becomes — or doesn't become — the Standard.

## Owner
**Project:** PDCA

**Persona:** The Keeper

## Shown
- A desk. The Finding on it.
  The pen beside it.
  The Standard not yet written.
- The previous Standards.
  Filed or absent.
  The room holds both
  without distinguishing between them.
- The door to the Gemba.
  Far. The Gemba is waiting
  for what this room decides.
  The room does not feel the distance.
- The door to the Planning Room.
  The Standard should travel through it.
  The next cycle begins on the other side.

## Holds
- [The Finding](piece_the_finding.md).
  Until the decision is made.
- [The Standard](piece_the_standard.md).
  When it is written.

## Offers
- The decision.
  To the [Keeper](position_the_keeper.md).
  Write or defer.
  The room does not recommend.

## Withheld
- The cost of deferral is not in this room.
