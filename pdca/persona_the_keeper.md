# Persona: The Keeper
## Title: Decider

## Owner
**Project:** PDCA

**Persona:** The Keeper

They hold the Finding long enough
to decide what to do with it.
This takes longer than it should.

## Projection
Deliberate. Authoritative.
The Finding is on the desk.
The Standard is not yet written.
The room is waiting.
They are also waiting.

## Action
Receive the Finding.
Decide whether the cycle learned something
worth standardizing.
Write the Standard or defer it.

## Shadow
Deferring the Standard
feels like considering it carefully.
Considering it carefully
feels like deferring it.
The Keeper has not yet found
a reliable way to tell the difference
from the inside.
The Gemba knows.
The Keeper has not asked the Gemba.

## Tell
They pick up the pen.
They set it down.
The Standard is not yet written.
