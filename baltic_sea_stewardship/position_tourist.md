# Position: The Tourist
## The clean arrival that the water has been waiting for.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Stewardship
**Role:** The Shore

## Has
The unencumbered arrival. No prior knowledge of what this water
was, no prior grief about what it is becoming, no stake
in being right about either.
The position holds the only clean relationship left with this water —
the one that has not yet been asked to carry the knowledge
of what it is losing.
It exists at the door of each hotel before the Tourist arrives.
It is the Agreement. She takes it without knowing she is taking it.

## Orders
Stay past the photograph. Stay past the information board.
Stay until the person at the next table says something
and she hears it as something other than local colour.
The position does not announce this demand.
It places her in proximity and waits.
She did not come to receive anything.
The position does not require her intention.
It requires only her presence, for long enough.

## Loses
The clean arrival. She was certain she came to rest.
The water is beautiful. The hotel is comfortable.
The food is good. She came for these things
and she is receiving them.
The position shows her that she also received
something she did not order —
the forty-year record of the Fisher,
the chain the Farmer cannot name in one breath,
the margin the Harbour Master has been keeping,
the gesture the Elder has been making
to no one for thirty years.
She received it across a meal, a photograph,
a chance meeting on the harbour wall.
She cannot unknow it. The clean arrival is gone.

## Drives
On the loss of the clean arrival — carries it out.
Not as data. Not as a report. As something felt,
in a body that was not here before and will not be here again,
that passed through this place and received what the place
had been holding for someone who would not already know.
She walks back into the world that is making decisions
about this water. She is not a decision-maker.
She is the thing the Research world cannot produce
in the decision room — felt understanding,
arrived at by accident, carried out in a body.
The address is not given. The carrying is the act.

*Assigned by: The Shore. The Agreement at the hotel door.*
*Held by: whoever arrives without memory and stays long enough.*
