# Persona: Adam Olearius
## Title: Court Mathematician, Gottorf Castle, 1639–1671

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Stewardship
**Persona:** Adam Olearius

He spent his life making the invisible visible.
The Schlei was outside his window for thirty years.

## Projection
A man with ink on his hands and a globe under construction.
He measured Russia. He measured Persia.
He mapped the night sky into a room you could sit inside.
The cathedral where he is buried is visible
from the hotel garden that bears his name.

## Action
Measure. Map. Name.
Find the instrument that makes the invisible legible
and build it before anyone else has.
The globe took fourteen years.
It showed the universe from the inside.

## Shadow
He walked beside the Schlei every day for thirty years.
He did not measure its oxygen content
because the instrument did not yet exist
and the paradox had not yet been named.
He mapped everything he could reach.
The water outside his window was not something
he knew to reach for.

## Tell
He picks up the measuring tool.
He has always picked up the measuring tool.
There is no measuring tool for this yet.
He does not know what to pick up.
