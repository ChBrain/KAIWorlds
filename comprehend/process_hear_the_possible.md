# Process: Hear the Possible
## The architecture confirmed outside the head for the first time.

## Owner
Project: Comprehend
Position: The Organist
Piece: The Organ — Marienkirche, Lübeck
Place: Marienkirche, Lübeck
Persona: Johann Sebastian Bach

## Initiates
The Organist can hear a specific architecture internally —
voices moving independently, each complete,
together more complete —
that has no external confirmation yet.
Dieterich Buxtehude occupies the organ at the Marienkirche in Lübeck.
He has already built what the Organist has not yet built.
The distance is walkable.
The Organist walks it.
Not because a coach was unavailable.
Because the walk is the beginning of the process.
No other condition is required.

## Direction
Toward the proof.
The Organist moves to the place where the architecture in the head
can be heard outside it.
The roads are bad. The weather is winter.
The institution has given four weeks.

## Lever
The gap between the architecture heard internally
and what has not yet been confirmed externally.
Remove the gap: no walk.
Remove the walk: no proof.
Remove the proof: the work that follows has no foundation.
Carrying force: the pull of the specific unconfirmed thing.
Resistance: distance, weather, institution, money.

## Echo
The architecture is confirmed. It can be made.
The Organist has become the Composer.
The position has shifted.
The consistory court does not need to know what changed.
The roads back to Arnstadt are the same roads.
The Composer walks them differently.
