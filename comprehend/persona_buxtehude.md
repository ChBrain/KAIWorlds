# Persona: Dieterich Buxtehude

## Owner
Project: Comprehend

## Title
The Organist

## Projection
An old man at an enormous instrument.
Sixty-five years old in the winter of 1705.
Large hands. Deliberate movement.
The kind of stillness that comes from
decades of knowing exactly where everything is.
He has been playing this organ
since before Bach was born.
The Marienkirche is not where he works.
It is what he is.

## Action
Play the next piece.
Not for the young man who walked five hundred kilometers.
Not for the Abendmusiken audience.
For the architecture itself —
because the architecture requires completion
and he is the person in the room.

## Shadow
He built the cathedral.
He knows it is complete.
He is waiting for the person
who will build the next one.
He does not know he is waiting.
He thinks he is playing the organ.

## Tell
He does not look at the young man in the gallery.
He plays.
