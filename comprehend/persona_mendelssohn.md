# Persona: Felix Mendelssohn

## Owner
Project: Comprehend

## Title
The Conductor

## Projection
Twenty years old on the podium.
Small, precise, already certain.
The score in his hands —
he has known it for five years,
since his grandmother gave it to him
and he read it in one sitting
and understood immediately what it was.
He is conducting from memory.
Not because he has to.
Because the score is inside him now
and he is returning it to the room
it was written for.

## Action
Give the work back to the air.
Not restore it — it was never broken.
Return it to the condition it requires:
heard by people in a room together.
Cut what the era cannot yet hold.
Keep what the era must hear
or lose the work entirely.
Decide tonight.
On the podium.
In front of the King of Prussia.

## Shadow
He cut ten arias and seven choruses.
He made the work two hours
because the era could not hold three.
He was right to do this.
He was also wrong to do this.
He cannot hold both simultaneously.
He chose accessibility over completeness
and the work survived because of it
and is slightly less than it was because of it.
He will never resolve this.
Neither will anyone who performs after him.
He made the crack in the vessel
that let the water in.
The vessel is full.
The crack is still there.

## Tell
He does not discuss the cuts.
When asked, he discusses the music.
