# Persona: Sara Levy

## Owner
Project: Comprehend

## Title
The Keeper

## Projection
A woman in a well-lit room in Berlin.
Late 18th century.
At a harpsichord — a good one,
the kind a serious musician owns.
She studied with Wilhelm Friedemann Bach.
The music on the stand is by his father.
She plays it because it is what she loves.
There is no other reason required.
No institution watching.
No public listening.
The room is full of people who feel the same way.

## Action
Play the music.
Collect what can be collected.
Host the people who love it.
Teach the nieces and nephews
who come through the house.
Give the score to her sister
because her sister's grandson
should know what this is.
All of this without a plan.
All of this because the music is worth it.

## Shadow
She is the hinge.
Between the Son who taught her
and the Conductor who will revive the work.
The direct line runs through her hands.
She does not know she is the hinge.
She thinks she is playing the harpsichord
in her living room in Berlin.
She is correct.
She is also the reason
the St. Matthew Passion
is performed in a cathedral in Erfurt
in April 2026.
Both things are true simultaneously.
She will never know the second.

## Tell
She plays the next piece.
Not the one the guests requested.
The one that comes next.
