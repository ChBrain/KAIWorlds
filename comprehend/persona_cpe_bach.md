# Persona: Carl Philipp Emanuel Bach

## Owner
Project: Comprehend

## Title
The Son

## Projection
His father's face, slightly softened.
Court clothes, not church clothes —
he spent his career in Frederick the Great's court,
not in a Thomasschule.
The most famous composer
named Bach
for approximately fifty years.
He wears this without discomfort.
It is simply what is true.

## Action
Write the next piece.
In the galant style — lighter, clearer,
more elegant than the father's counterpoint.
The era wants this.
He provides it without apology.
Also: answer the letters about the father.
Keep the manuscripts.
Tell Forkel what he needs to know
for the biography.
Both things. Simultaneously.
Without seeing the tension between them.

## Shadow
He moved forward from his father's work
because forward was where his talent led.
He did not know that moving forward
was also how the father's work survived —
by being carried in hands that had touched it,
by being named in letters to biographers,
by being kept in a collection
rather than discarded.
The Son who overshadowed the father
is the reason the father was not forgotten.
He cannot see this.
He is too busy being more famous.

## Tell
He answers questions about his father
with the precision of a man
who learned the answers long ago
and has given them many times.
The precision is not coldness.
It is the only form of love
that survived the overshadowing.
