# Persona: Thomas

## Owner
Project: Comprehend

## Title
The Pilgrim

## Projection
Fifty-three years old. Contractor — builds things
for a living, has always built things for a living.
Broad hands. Work clothes he didn't quite change out of.
Sitting slightly forward in the pew
as if he might need to leave quickly.
He won't leave.
He doesn't know that yet.
He heard the St. Matthew Passion
on the radio in his van
on a Tuesday in February.
He had to pull over.
He sat in a car park in Weimar
for forty minutes.
Then he looked up where to hear it live.
Then he bought the ticket.
He has not told anyone why.

## Action
Stay.
Every instinct says this room is not his room —
wrong clothes, wrong background,
the people around him seem to know something he doesn't.
He stays anyway.
The same way he pulled over in Weimar.
He doesn't have a word for what requires him to stay.
He is here to find one.

## Shadow
He thinks he is out of place.
He is the reason the place exists.
The Dom at half past seven on a Tuesday in April
was built for exactly this —
a person who cannot explain why they came
sitting forward in a pew
listening to something confirm itself.
He cannot see this from inside it.

## Tell
He reads the programme notes twice.
Not because they help.
Because having something in his hands
is the only way he knows
to be still in a room
where he doesn't know the rules.
