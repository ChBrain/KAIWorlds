# Position: The Keeper

## Owner
Project: Comprehend

## Principle
Hold the work when the world is not looking.
Not as duty. As love, or conviction, or both.
The world's attention is not required.
The holding is.

## Carries
The manuscripts — physically.
The knowledge of what they are — partially.
The ignorance of how load-bearing the holding is — constitutively.
Zelter is the exception:
he knows exactly what he carries
and carries it anyway.

## Presses On
The Conductor — by ensuring the score
exists in a room when the Conductor
is ready to use it.
The Scholar — by preserving what the Scholar
will later name and publish.

## Pressed On By
The era — which has moved on
and does not ask the Keeper
to hold what it has forgotten.
The scale of the work —
which requires more than one Keeper
across seventy-nine years.

## Limit
The Keeper holds without recognition
during the holding.
Recognition arrives later
or not at all.
Sara Levy is remembered now
because Mendelssohn is remembered.
Not the other way around.
