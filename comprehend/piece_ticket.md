# Piece: The Ticket

## Owner
Project: Comprehend
Position: The Pilgrim

## Place
Where the Pilgrim is.

## Load Bearing
Remove it and Process 6 has no physical object.
The pull remains.
The decision to go remains.
Without the Ticket: the pull is private and leaves no mark.
With the Ticket: the pull becomes an act.
The act is recordable.
Some records are kept.

## Apparent
Paper or screen. A date, a time, a place.
Dom St. Marien, Erfurt.
19:30.
A name or a booking reference.
Sometimes a seat number.
Sometimes standing room.
Unremarkable until after the concert.
Then: either thrown away
or kept in a drawer
without fully knowing why.

## Yearbook
The Ticket has no single history.
It has twenty thousand histories per year
in Thuringia alone.
Each one begins the same way:
something pulled.
A decision was made.
A piece of paper changed hands.
Most Yearbooks end at the bin
outside the concert hall.
Some continue.
The ones that continue
are the ones this world is built on.
