# Place: Bach-Gesellschaft, Leipzig

## Owner
Project: Comprehend
Piece: The Complete Works — Bach-Gesellschaft Edition
Position: The Scholar

## Shown
An institution, not a building.
Founded 1850 in Leipzig.
The centenary of Bach's death.
A society of scholars with a single purpose:
collect, edit, publish every known work.
The building changes. The purpose does not.

## Holds
The Complete Works — 49 volumes, 1851–1899.
The scholarly apparatus that makes the work findable.
The standard text the Conductor uses.
The Neue Bach-Ausgabe — begun 1950, still ongoing.

## Offers
Findability.
The work made locatable by anyone
with a library card or a connection.
The infrastructure the Performer needs
before the Performer can perform.

## Withheld
Every work that did not survive
to be collected.
The cantatas lost before 1850.
The pieces wrapped in paper, used as kindling,
discarded without a Keeper present.
The Bach-Gesellschaft cannot publish
what no longer exists.
The edition is complete.
It is not exhaustive.
Nobody knows the difference.
