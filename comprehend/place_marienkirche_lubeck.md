## Shown

Four galleries arranged around it.
The Abendmusiken — Advent concerts,
Sunday evenings, the wonder of North Germany.

The proof that the architecture is possible.
The end of the walk.

## Offers
The confirmation.
One person, one instrument, one room,
voices moving independently,
each complete, together more complete.
The Organist hears it and knows.
Everything heard internally
made external for the first time.

The four months.

What the walk cost.
