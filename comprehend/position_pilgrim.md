# Position: The Pilgrim

## Owner
Project: Comprehend

## Principle
Go toward the thing.
Before you have words for what it is.
The words come after, if at all.

## Carries
The Ticket.
The half-remembered thing that pulled them here —
a recording, a film, a concert,
something heard on a Tuesday.
The inability to fully explain
to someone else why this was necessary.

## Presses On
The Keeper — without knowing it.
Some will keep the ticket.
The thread runs through them now.
They do not know this yet.

## Pressed On By
The Performer — whose sound
reaches the Pilgrim before anything else.
The work itself — which has been traveling
toward this room
since Lübeck, 1705.

## Limit
Cannot see the full arc of what they are part of.
The Organist couldn't either.
The Composer couldn't either.
This is not a failure of the Pilgrim.
It is the condition of everyone
who has ever been inside this world.
