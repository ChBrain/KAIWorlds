# Position: The Composer

## Owner
Project: Comprehend

## Principle
Turn the comprehended thing into structure that outlasts the body that comprehended it.

## Carries
The architecture heard in Process 1.
The weekly deadline of Process 2.
Both simultaneously. Neither cancels the other.

## Presses On
The Cantor — who occupies the same body
and requires the same hours
for completely different work.
The Son — who will inherit
exactly what the Composer produced
and nothing more.

## Pressed On By
The institution — which hired the Cantor
and received the Composer without knowing it.
The deadline — which forces completion
of work that requires infinity.

## Limit
Cannot see the full scale of what is being built
while building it.
The Composer works in weeks.
The cathedral takes centuries.
