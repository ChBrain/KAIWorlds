# place_the_casting_pit

## Owner
- Project: Schillers Glocke
- Place: [the foundry floor](place_the_foundry_floor.md)

## Shown
Below [the foundry floor](place_the_foundry_floor.md). A depression approximately one meter deep, accessible by two stone steps from the floor level. [the mold](piece_the_mold.md) sits here when present, upright, the entry channel aligned with the trough from the crucible above. Earth floor. Earth walls. The walls contain metal from previous casts. The smell is iron and soil.

## Holds
[the mold](piece_the_mold.md).

## Offers
[Mold Maker](position_mold_maker.md) can align [the mold](piece_the_mold.md) against the pit walls without additional bracing. The alignment holds through the pour.

## Withheld
The compression of previous molds in the earth floor. The pit retains the position and base diameter of every mold that has sat here. [Mold Maker](position_mold_maker.md) who reads the floor finds the ghosts of previous castings. The pit does not name them.
