# piece_the_mold

## Owner
- Project: Schillers Glocke
- Persona: [Ernst](persona_ernst.md)

## Place
[the casting pit](place_the_casting_pit.md). Until the opening.

## Load Bearing
Without it there is no pour. Remove [the mold](piece_the_mold.md) and the preparation has no object. The liquid metal has no form. [the bell](piece_the_bell.md) is never a bell.

## Apparent
Clay and dung, layered and dried. Approximately bell-shaped exterior. Two parts — cope and drag — fitted together and sealed. A channel at the top. A vent alongside it. Heavier than it looks.

## Yearbook
Built before [the preparation](process_the_preparation.md) begins. Dried over weeks. Carried to [the foundry floor](place_the_foundry_floor.md) and positioned in [the casting pit](place_the_casting_pit.md). Sealed the morning of the pour. Opened the day after the cooling was judged complete. Destroyed in the opening. The mold's entire history: built, waited, received the metal, broken. It does not outlast the bell.
