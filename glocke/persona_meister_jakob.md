# persona_meister_jakob

## Owner
- Project: Schillers Glocke

## Title
[Master Founder](position_master_founder.md)

## Projection
He moves around [the foundry floor](place_the_foundry_floor.md) without looking where he is going. He has never tripped on the steps to [the casting pit](place_the_casting_pit.md). His hands are always checking something — the temperature of [the mold](piece_the_mold.md)'s surface, the color of the metal, the seal on the channel joint.

## Action
Read the pour in the first three seconds. Name what he reads to [Apprentice](position_apprentice.md), immediately.

## Shadow
He has cast thirty-one bells. That knowledge is now faster than observation. He feels the failure before he sees it — and so sometimes he responds to a failure that hasn't happened yet. He corrects early. [Apprentice](position_apprentice.md) never sees the error. [Apprentice](position_apprentice.md) also never learns to read the pour.

## Tell
He takes over the task he assigned five minutes ago.
