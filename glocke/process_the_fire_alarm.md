# process_the_fire_alarm

## Owner
- Project: Schillers Glocke
- Position: [The One Who Transmits](position_the_one_who_transmits.md)

## Initiated by
Fire in the town. [The One Who Transmits](position_the_one_who_transmits.md) reaches [the bell tower](place_the_bell_tower.md).

## Direction
Toward the alarm reaching every part of [the town square](place_the_town_square.md) [the bell](piece_the_bell.md)'s voice can reach.

## Lever
Carrying force: the alarm — sustained, continuous, distinct.
Resistance: [The One Who Transmits](position_the_one_who_transmits.md)'s body — the pull must be regulated. An uneven ring does not carry.

## Echo
The alarm pattern has reached [the town square](place_the_town_square.md) and beyond. [the rope](piece_the_rope.md) is still only when the alarm is no longer needed.
