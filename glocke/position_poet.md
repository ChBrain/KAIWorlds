# position_poet

## Owner
- Project: Schillers Glocke
- Process: process_the_commission

## Has
The form before it has a subject.

## Orders
Find the form that can carry the weight without explaining it.

## Loses
The particular. The form that can hold everything a bell does cannot hold this bell, on this day, with these specific hands.

## Drives
On the loss of the particular — reads the finished poem aloud. It sounds right.
