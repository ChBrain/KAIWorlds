# piece_the_rope

## Owner
- Project: Schillers Glocke
- Place: place_the_bell_tower

## Place
place_the_bell_tower. Ground floor entrance hall.

## Load Bearing
Without it piece_the_bell cannot be rung by human hands. Remove piece_the_rope and the bell hangs complete and makes no sound. piece_the_rope is what makes piece_the_bell a bell.

## Apparent
Hemp. Thick enough to grip with both hands. The tail end knotted. The upper end rigged to the clapper mechanism. Long enough to reach the ground floor from the bell chamber. In use: the rope moves in a circle, not a straight pull.

## Yearbook
Rigged during process_the_hanging. First held in process_the_first_strike by position_bellringer. Worn gradually by use — the grip point darkens first, then thins. Replaced twice across fifty-one years of ringing. Cut at the top when piece_the_bell was pulled down at process_the_revolution. The cut end remained in place_the_bell_tower. The bell was gone. The cut end was not immediately removed.
