# piece_the_rope

## Owner
- Project: Schillers Glocke
- Place: [the bell tower](place_the_bell_tower.md)

## Place
[the bell tower](place_the_bell_tower.md). Ground floor entrance hall.

## Load Bearing
Without it [the bell](piece_the_bell.md) cannot be rung by human hands. Remove [the rope](piece_the_rope.md) and the bell hangs complete and makes no sound. [the rope](piece_the_rope.md) is what makes [the bell](piece_the_bell.md) a bell.

## Apparent
Hemp. Thick enough to grip with both hands. The tail end knotted. The upper end rigged to the clapper mechanism. Long enough to reach the ground floor from the bell chamber. In use: the rope moves in a circle, not a straight pull.

## Yearbook
Rigged during [the hanging](process_the_hanging.md). First held in [the first strike](process_the_first_strike.md) by [Bellringer](position_bellringer.md). Worn gradually by use — the grip point darkens first, then thins. Replaced twice across fifty-one years of ringing. Cut at the top when [the bell](piece_the_bell.md) was pulled down at [the revolution](process_the_revolution.md). The cut end remained in [the bell tower](place_the_bell_tower.md). The bell was gone. The cut end was not immediately removed.
