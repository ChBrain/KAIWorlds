# persona_anna

## Owner
- Project: Schillers Glocke

## Title
[Apprentice](position_apprentice.md)

## Projection
She is everywhere [Master Founder](position_master_founder.md) is not. She moves quickly, without noise. She reports in a flat voice.

## Action
See what is happening in the part of the pour she was assigned, and name it exactly as it is, in the fewest words possible, without deciding whether it matters.

## Shadow
She has learned to report without interpreting. She is very good at this now. But she has also learned not to name things that might be nothing. She withholds the probably-nothings. Some of them are not nothing.

## Tell
Her mouth opens. She takes a breath. The breath is audible. She does not speak. Her hand moves toward the thing she was looking at — not pointing, just moving toward it, the body already committed while the voice is still deciding.
