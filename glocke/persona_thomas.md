## Projection
A young man at the base of place_the_bell_tower. Holding piece_the_rope with both hands. Standing very straight. The straightness is not confidence — it is the shape he is making with his body to keep the other shape from being visible.

## Action
Wait for the signal. When it comes — pull.

## Shadow
The performance of readiness has replaced the readiness itself, and he knows it. He is afraid of the specific silence that will exist in the half-second between his pull and piece_the_bell's response — when the action will have been taken and the result will not yet be known and there will be nothing left to perform. He has been managing the rope grip and the posture and the breathing to avoid thinking about that half-second. He cannot avoid it. It is coming.

## Tell
He adjusts his grip. He adjusted it thirty seconds ago. The grip was correct both times.
