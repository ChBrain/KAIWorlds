# persona_jakob_younger

## Owner
- Project: Schillers Glocke

## Title
[Commissioner](position_commissioner.md) / [The One Who Unmakes](position_the_one_who_unmakes.md)

## Projection
A man in his forties in [the town square](place_the_town_square.md), watching [the bell](piece_the_bell.md) being lowered from [the bell tower](place_the_bell_tower.md). He gave the order. He is present.

## Action
Hold the order. He gave it. He does not revoke it.

## Shadow
He signed the commission document that authorized [the bell](piece_the_bell.md)'s removal. The commission document is the same form [Stadtrat Brenner](persona_brenner.md) used to commission the bell's casting. He used the same form because the town archive had no other form for civic commissions. He noticed this while signing. He did not mention it to anyone. He has not stopped thinking about it. He is not sure what it means. He is not sure it means anything.

## Tell
He looks at [the inscription](piece_the_inscription.md) as [the bell](piece_the_bell.md) is lowered past him. He has it memorized. He does not look away.
