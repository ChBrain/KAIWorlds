# persona_heinrich

## Owner
- Project: Schillers Glocke

## Title
[Master Founder](position_master_founder.md) / [The One Who Remains](position_the_one_who_remains.md)

## Projection
An old man standing at the edge of [the foundry floor](place_the_foundry_floor.md), away from everywhere he spent thirty years standing. His hands are in front of him. They have nothing to hold.

## Action
Watch [Meister Jakob](persona_meister_jakob.md) read the pour. Not to evaluate — to see whether the pour is being read the way he taught it to be read, or whether [Meister Jakob](persona_meister_jakob.md) has developed his own reading.

## Shadow
He passed the position to [Meister Jakob](persona_meister_jakob.md) twelve years ago. What he cannot see — what he has arranged his entire presence here not to know — is whether the passing was complete. Whether there is anything left of his craft that didn't transfer. He watches [Meister Jakob](persona_meister_jakob.md) read the pour and looks for the gap. He is not certain whether he is hoping to find one or hoping not to.

## Tell
He starts to speak. Stops. The pour is running correctly. He had no correction to make. He was going to speak anyway.
