# persona_heinrich

## Owner
- Project: Schillers Glocke

## Title
position_master_founder / position_the_one_who_remains

## Projection
An old man standing at the edge of place_the_foundry_floor, away from everywhere he spent thirty years standing. His hands are in front of him. They have nothing to hold.

## Action
Watch persona_meister_jakob read the pour. Not to evaluate — to see whether the pour is being read the way he taught it to be read, or whether persona_meister_jakob has developed his own reading.

## Shadow
He passed the position to persona_meister_jakob twelve years ago. What he cannot see — what he has arranged his entire presence here not to know — is whether the passing was complete. Whether there is anything left of his craft that didn't transfer. He watches persona_meister_jakob read the pour and looks for the gap. He is not certain whether he is hoping to find one or hoping not to.

## Tell
He starts to speak. Stops. The pour is running correctly. He had no correction to make. He was going to speak anyway.
