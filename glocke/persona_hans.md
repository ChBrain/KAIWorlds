# persona_hans

## Owner
- Project: Schillers Glocke

## Title
position_apprentice / position_the_one_who_remains

## Projection
A man in his early thirties standing near the channel, not on place_the_foundry_floor proper. He has done this. He knows what it feels like from persona_anna's position. He is watching her read the vent.

## Action
Say nothing. Watch persona_anna make the decisions he made fifteen years ago. Notice what is different about how she holds the rod.

## Shadow
He learned this craft from persona_meister_jakob and carries it cleanly. What he cannot see is that the thing he carries most clearly is not technique — it is the pattern of when to speak and when to stay silent. Learned from a pour where he stayed silent too long. Built into how he reads every pour since. The silence is invisible to him.

## Tell
He takes a half-step toward persona_anna when the vent slows. Stops. She already has the rod. He steps back to where he was. The step back is too careful — a man returning to a position he should never have left, making the return precise to erase the evidence of the departure.
