## Place
[the bell tower](place_the_bell_tower.md). Since [the hanging](process_the_hanging.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it seventeen processes run in sequence and produce nothing. Every persona in the world is shaped by an object that is not there.

## Apparent
At the moment of the opening. Bronze. Dark orange-brown at the surface, deepening toward red where the light from the foundry door catches the shoulder. The profile runs from the crown down through the shoulder, the waist, the sound bow, to the lip. The curve is acoustic. [the inscription](piece_the_inscription.md) at the shoulder: raised letters, sharp. *Vivos voco. Mortuos plango. Fulgura frango.* Weight: approximately 400 kg. No name.

## Yearbook
The copper and tin arrived as separate ingots. The ratio verified. The alloy loaded into the crucible together for the first time.

At [the pour](process_the_pour.md) the alloy became the bell. The two metals ran together into [the mold](piece_the_mold.md) and set. What emerged at [the opening](process_the_opening.md) was this object, this profile, this weight.

[the inscription](piece_the_inscription.md) emerged at the opening with the rest of the bell. Cast in the same pour, the same metal, the same moment.

The test strike was made at [the opening](process_the_opening.md). The tone was present.

At [the transit](process_the_transit.md) the bell moved through the town for the first time. [the inscription](piece_the_inscription.md) faced upward on the sledge and was readable from the street.

At [the hanging](process_the_hanging.md) the bell was seated in the yoke in [the bell tower](place_the_bell_tower.md). The yoke bolts were set. The clapper tie was cut.

At [the first strike](process_the_first_strike.md) the bell rang for the first time at full weight. The voice crossed [the town square](place_the_town_square.md).

The bell rang at every subsequent process — [the daily call](process_the_daily_call.md), [the wedding ring](process_the_wedding_ring.md), [the fire alarm](process_the_fire_alarm.md), [the death knell](process_the_death_knell.md) — for fifty-one years. The profile did not change. The weight did not change. [the inscription](piece_the_inscription.md) did not change.

At [the revolution](process_the_revolution.md) the yoke bolts were removed. The bell descended from [the bell tower](place_the_bell_tower.md). It had hung for fifty-one years.

At [the recasting](process_the_recasting.md) the shoulder was struck first. [the inscription](piece_the_inscription.md) was on the shoulder. The letters were gone in the first blow. The remaining bronze was fed into the furnace. The ratio changed. The bell became cannon metal.

The cannon was cast. It did not ring.
