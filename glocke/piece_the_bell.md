## Load Bearing
Without it seventeen processes run in sequence and produce nothing. Every persona in the world is shaped by an object that is not there.

## Apparent
At the moment of the opening. Bronze. Dark orange-brown at the surface, deepening toward red where the light from the foundry door catches the shoulder. The profile runs from the crown down through the shoulder, the waist, the sound bow, to the lip. The curve is acoustic. piece_the_inscription at the shoulder: raised letters, sharp. *Vivos voco. Mortuos plango. Fulgura frango.* Weight: approximately 400 kg. No name.

## Yearbook
The copper and tin arrived as separate ingots. The ratio verified. The alloy loaded into the crucible together for the first time.

At process_the_pour the alloy became the bell. The two metals ran together into piece_the_mold and set. What emerged at process_the_opening was this object, this profile, this weight.

piece_the_inscription emerged at the opening with the rest of the bell. Cast in the same pour, the same metal, the same moment.

The test strike was made at process_the_opening. The tone was present.

At process_the_transit the bell moved through the town for the first time. piece_the_inscription faced upward on the sledge and was readable from the street.

At process_the_hanging the bell was seated in the yoke in place_the_bell_tower. The yoke bolts were set. The clapper tie was cut.

At process_the_first_strike the bell rang for the first time at full weight. The voice crossed place_the_town_square.

The bell rang at every subsequent process — process_the_daily_call, process_the_wedding_ring, process_the_fire_alarm, process_the_death_knell — for fifty-one years. The profile did not change. The weight did not change. piece_the_inscription did not change.

At process_the_revolution the yoke bolts were removed. The bell descended from place_the_bell_tower. It had hung for fifty-one years.

At process_the_recasting the shoulder was struck first. piece_the_inscription was on the shoulder. The letters were gone in the first blow. The remaining bronze was fed into the furnace. The ratio changed. The bell became cannon metal.

The cannon was cast. It did not ring.
