# piece_the_alloy

## Owner
- Project: Schillers Glocke

## Place
The crucible. [the foundry floor](place_the_foundry_floor.md). Until the pour.

## Load Bearing
Without the correct ratio — approximately 78% copper and 22% tin — [the bell](piece_the_bell.md) cannot produce its tone. The alloy is the bell's voice before the bell exists.

## Apparent
In the crucible: liquid, the color of sunrise at temperature. The ratio is invisible in the liquid. It cannot be seen. It can only be trusted.

## Yearbook
Copper extracted from a seam by [Miner](position_miner.md). Tin from a separate seam. Both ores smelted by [Smelter](position_smelter.md), who called the moment each ran clean. Both transported as commodity to the foundry stores. Weighed and ratio-verified by [Master Founder](position_master_founder.md). Loaded into the crucible together for the first time. Brought to temperature. Poured at the moment when temperature, [the mold](piece_the_mold.md) readiness, and [the foundry floor](place_the_foundry_floor.md) clearance coincided. After the pour: the alloy is [the bell](piece_the_bell.md). It no longer exists separately.
