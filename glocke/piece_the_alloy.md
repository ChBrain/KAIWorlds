# piece_the_alloy

## Owner
- Project: Schillers Glocke

## Place
The crucible. place_the_foundry_floor. Until the pour.

## Load Bearing
Without the correct ratio — approximately 78% copper and 22% tin — piece_the_bell cannot produce its tone. The alloy is the bell's voice before the bell exists.

## Apparent
In the crucible: liquid, the color of sunrise at temperature. The ratio is invisible in the liquid. It cannot be seen. It can only be trusted.

## Yearbook
Copper extracted from a seam by position_miner. Tin from a separate seam. Both ores smelted by position_smelter, who called the moment each ran clean. Both transported as commodity to the foundry stores. Weighed and ratio-verified by position_master_founder. Loaded into the crucible together for the first time. Brought to temperature. Poured at the moment when temperature, piece_the_mold readiness, and place_the_foundry_floor clearance coincided. After the pour: the alloy is piece_the_bell. It no longer exists separately.
