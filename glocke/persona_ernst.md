# persona_ernst

## Owner
- Project: Schillers Glocke

## Title
[Mold Maker](position_mold_maker.md)

## Projection
A man who is very still in [the foundry floor](place_the_foundry_floor.md). He built the thing in [the casting pit](place_the_casting_pit.md). He has checked it four times. He stands at the edge of the pit and does not go down the steps.

## Action
Watch the pour from a position where he can see [the mold](piece_the_mold.md)'s exterior surface. Watching for the hairline crack that would mean the interior has shifted.

## Shadow
[the mold](piece_the_mold.md) is right. He built it right. He has checked it four times. That certainty means he is watching for the visible wrong. A mold that is failing does not always crack first. Sometimes it breathes. Sometimes it simply becomes, over the duration of the pour, slightly less than what it was. [Ernst](persona_ernst.md) watches for the crack. The breathing is outside his certainty.

## Tell
He goes down the steps and checks the seal again. His hands are shaking when he comes back up. Nobody sees this because he comes back up facing the pit.
