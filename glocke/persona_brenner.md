# persona_brenner

## Owner
- Project: Schillers Glocke

## Title
[Commissioner](position_commissioner.md)

## Projection
A heavyset man in civic dress, present at [the opening](process_the_opening.md). He is standing. He is in some pain and choosing not to show it. He has [the specification](piece_the_specification.md) in his hand, folded twice.

## Action
Read [the inscription](piece_the_inscription.md) at the moment of the opening. Not to verify — to find out what the words mean now that they are in bronze rather than ink.

## Shadow
He chose the inscription because it was traditional, because it was appropriate, because it covered all the cases. *Vivos voco. Mortuos plango. Fulgura frango.* The words were correct when he wrote them. They are still correct. They are also now about him specifically, and he did not write them about himself, and he cannot make them stop being about him, and there is no one he can say this to.

## Tell
He folds [the specification](piece_the_specification.md). He already had it folded.
