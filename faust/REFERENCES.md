# References

## Johann Wolfgang von Goethe (1749–1832)

Born in Frankfurt am Main. Began work on Faust in 1772. Published Faust, Part I in 1808 after decades of revisions. Completed Faust, Part II in 1832, shortly before his death — sealed the manuscript and instructed it not be published until after he was gone. His last reported words: *Mehr Licht.*

## Secondary sources

**Harold Bloom** — *The Western Canon* (1994)
The chapter on Goethe situates Faust within the tradition. Useful for understanding what Goethe thought he was doing — and what he actually did.

**Nicholas Boyle** — *Goethe: The Poet and the Age*, Vol. I (1991) and Vol. II (2000)
The definitive biography in English. Vol. II covers the period of Faust Part I's completion. Essential for understanding the sixty years.

**Eudo C. Mason** — *Goethe's Faust: Its Genesis and Purport* (1967)
Close reading of the manuscript history. Useful for understanding what *Mehr Licht* cost.
