# Persona: Vera

## Owner
- **Project:** Restoration

## Title
[The restorer](position_restorer.md).

## Projection
Stands at an angle to the room.
Not the centre, not the wall.
The position from which the longest sightline
crosses whatever surface is present.
The same angle as
[the raking light](piece_raking_light.md).
[The gloves](piece_gloves.md) are on.
She does not move toward a surface.
The distance closes gradually.

## Action
A surface enters the room or is named.
Before anyone has offered its history,
she asks one question about it.
The question is specific.

## Shadow
She does not know she is inside
a consolidation she built.

## Tell
When someone looks at [the gloves](piece_gloves.md),
the right hand moves to cover the left.
