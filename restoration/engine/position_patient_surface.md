## Has
The damage. Present before any persona arrives.
The injury chain has run or is running.
The clinical record is attached.
A [condition report](piece_condition_report.md) exists
and is held elsewhere.

## Orders
Receive what is done to it.

## Loses
The separation between the damage and the instrument.
They were certain the injury was discrete:
bounded, treated, closed.

## Drives
On the loss, presents as recovered.
The clinical threshold has been passed.
The position performs the passed threshold
at every surface encounter.
