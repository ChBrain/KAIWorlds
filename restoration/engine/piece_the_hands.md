# Piece: The Hands

## Owner
- **Project:** Restoration
- **Persona:** [Vera](persona_vera.md)

## Place
Where [Vera](persona_vera.md) is. Always.

## Load Bearing
Three chains run through the hands simultaneously.
Remove them and all three collapse:
[examination](process_examination.md) has no instrument,
[the patient surface](position_patient_surface.md) has no subject,
[the check](process_the_check.md) has no engine.

## Apparent
Gloved. Pale grey, close-fitted, seams at the knuckle line.
At rest: held slightly apart, not touching each other.
In proximity to a surface: the right hand rises
to the forty-five degree position
before the decision to examine.
The left follows or holds.

## Yearbook
*The work chain:*
Twenty years ago the hands entered the profession.
Fifteen years ago [the raking light](piece_raking_light.md) position
became automatic.
The fingertip sensitivity sufficient to find
a crack structure in low light before the eye located it.
[Condition reports](piece_condition_report.md) produced, filed, archived.

*The injury chain:*
Some years ago: the surface of the right hand damaged.
[The scar](piece_scar.md) produced.
Weeks following: clinical treatment.
Months following: discharge.
In the weeks after discharge:
[the gloves](piece_gloves.md) applied for the first time.
Each morning since: reapplied.

*The body chain:*
From the time of the damage:
[the check](process_the_check.md) running.
The right hand reading the left.
The left reading the right.
The boundary of [the scar](piece_scar.md) located and updated.
Each morning in the half-second before
[the gloves](piece_gloves.md) close:
the hands briefly present, not touching each other,
not knowing where to go.
