# Process: The Treatment

## Owner
- **Project:** Restoration
- **Process:** [process_the_accident](process_the_accident.md)
- **Process:** [process_the_recovery](process_the_recovery.md)
- **Place:** [place_the_treatment_room](place_the_treatment_room.md)

## Initiated by
[The accident](process_the_accident.md) has run.
[The hands](piece_the_hands.md) are in clinical care.
[The treating clinician](position_treating_clinician.md) is present.
[The patient surface](position_patient_surface.md) is on the table.

## Direction
Toward functional recovery.
The clinical measure: the hands work.
Sensation sufficient. Movement sufficient.

## Lever
Carrying force: the clinical protocol.
What is done to [the hands](piece_the_hands.md)
is determined by what the damage requires.
Resistance: the [condition report](piece_condition_report.md)
running in [the restorer](position_restorer.md)
against the clinical assessment.

## Echo
[The recovery](process_the_recovery.md) inherits
[the hands](piece_the_hands.md) past the clinical threshold
and a gap between what the clinical record holds
and what [the restorer](position_restorer.md) holds.
