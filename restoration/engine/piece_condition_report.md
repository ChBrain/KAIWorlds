## Load Bearing
Without it [intervention](process_intervention.md) has no instruction
and [consolidation](process_consolidation.md) has no record
of what was sealed and why.
Remove it and each process begins without knowing
what the previous process found.

## Apparent
A document on the desk at the side wall, filed and dated.
And held in [the restorer](position_restorer.md):
updated continuously, filed nowhere,
present in the hands that produced it.

## Yearbook
Two forms have run simultaneously from the beginning.
Twenty years ago: the written form learned first,
the body form already present and producing it.
The body form is distinct from the written form it produces.
What the written form cannot hold
remains in [the restorer](position_restorer.md).
