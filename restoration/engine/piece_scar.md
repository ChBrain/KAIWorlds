# Piece: The Scar

## Owner
- **Project:** Restoration
- **Persona:** [Vera](persona_vera.md)

## Place
[The hands](piece_the_hands.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it [the patient surface](position_patient_surface.md)
has no physical address and [the check](process_the_check.md)
has nothing to examine.

## Apparent
Tissue on the dorsal surface of the right hand,
from the base of the index finger
to the outer edge of the wrist.
Smooth at the centre.
Raised and firm at the boundary
where the affected tissue meets the unscarred skin.
The boundary is palpable before it is visible.

## Yearbook
Produced some years ago.
Treated in the weeks following.
Assessed as functionally recovered
at the close of [the treatment](process_the_treatment.md).
Present since, unchanged in physical extent.
The clinical record marks it closed.
