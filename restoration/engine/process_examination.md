# Process: Examination

## Owner
- **Project:** Restoration
- **Process:** [process_intervention](process_intervention.md)
- **Piece:** [piece_raking_light](piece_raking_light.md)

## Initiated by
A surface is present in [the studio](place_the_studio.md).
[The restorer](position_restorer.md) is present.
[The raking light](piece_raking_light.md) is positioned
or will be positioned.
The reading has begun.

## Direction
Toward the complete [condition report](piece_condition_report.md).
Every layer named. Every damage mapped.
Every previous intervention identified where possible.

## Lever
Carrying force: the trained reading in
[the restorer](position_restorer.md),
running before the decision to examine.
Resistance: the prepared surface.
Previous consolidation applied
so this reading cannot proceed.

## Echo
[Intervention](process_intervention.md) inherits
[the restorer](position_restorer.md)
who cannot unknow what the examination found
and a [condition report](piece_condition_report.md)
that is complete.
