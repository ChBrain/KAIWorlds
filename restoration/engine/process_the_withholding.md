# Process: The Withholding

## Owner
- **Project:** Restoration
- **Process:** [process_the_finding](process_the_finding.md)
- **Process:** [process_the_check](process_the_check.md)
- **Piece:** [piece_the_hands](piece_the_hands.md)

## Initiated by
[The finding](process_the_finding.md) has run.
The [condition report](piece_condition_report.md) is current.
No action is available.
[The gloves](piece_gloves.md) are applied
or about to be applied.

## Direction
Toward the next [check](process_the_check.md).

## Lever
Carrying force: [the gloves](piece_gloves.md).
Resistance: what [the hands](piece_the_hands.md) hold.
Each [check](process_the_check.md) adds to what is held.
The half-second before [the gloves](piece_gloves.md) close:
[the hands](piece_the_hands.md) briefly present,
not touching each other.
Then the gloves close.

## Echo
[The check](process_the_check.md) runs again.
[The gloves](piece_gloves.md) are on.
What is underneath is present.
