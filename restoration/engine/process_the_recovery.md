# Process: The Recovery

## Owner
- **Project:** Restoration
- **Process:** [process_the_treatment](process_the_treatment.md)
- **Piece:** [piece_the_hands](piece_the_hands.md)

## Initiated by
[The treatment](process_the_treatment.md) has run.
Clinical discharge has occurred.
[The hands](piece_the_hands.md) are returned
to [the restorer](position_restorer.md).
[The studio](place_the_studio.md) is available.

## Direction
Toward a decision about return.

## Lever
Carrying force: the clinical clearance.
[The hands](piece_the_hands.md) function.
No medical reason not to return.
Resistance: the examination
[the restorer](position_restorer.md) runs
on [the hands](piece_the_hands.md)
in the gap before
[the gloves](piece_gloves.md) are applied.
The sensitivity at [the scar](piece_scar.md) boundary.
The result of that examination, known,
held in [the restorer](position_restorer.md).

## Echo
[The check](process_the_check.md) inherits
[the hands](piece_the_hands.md)
with [the gloves](piece_gloves.md) applied,
a clinical record marked closed,
and a professional [condition report](piece_condition_report.md)
the clinical record does not contain.
