# Position: The Restorer

## Owner
- **Project:** Restoration
- **Process:** [process_examination](process_examination.md)
- **Process:** [process_intervention](process_intervention.md)
- **Process:** [process_consolidation](process_consolidation.md)

## Has
The trained reading. Surfaces have built a vocabulary
the body applies before the decision to examine.
The forty-five degree position at the light.
The fingertip knowledge that finds a crack structure
before the eye locates it.
The [condition report](piece_condition_report.md)
that begins before it is requested.
This exists before any persona arrives.

## Orders
Read what is there.

## Loses
The boundary between the surface examined
and the surface examining.
They were certain the reading was directional:
instrument to object, never the reverse.

## Drives
On the loss, reads anyway.
Every surface in proximity.
The reading runs whether or not
a [condition report](piece_condition_report.md) has been requested.
