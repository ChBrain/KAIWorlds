# Process: Intervention

## Owner
- **Project:** Restoration
- **Process:** [process_examination](process_examination.md)
- **Process:** [process_consolidation](process_consolidation.md)
- **Piece:** [piece_the_hands](piece_the_hands.md)

## Initiated by
[Examination](process_examination.md) has run.
The [condition report](piece_condition_report.md) is complete.
[The restorer](position_restorer.md) is at
[the moment of commitment](place_the_moment_of_commitment.md).
The surface has been touched.

## Direction
Toward the damaged layer.
Not toward the original state.
Toward what the damage has done
and what can be addressed beneath it.

## Lever
Carrying force: the [condition report](piece_condition_report.md).
Every action follows from what
[examination](process_examination.md) found.
Resistance: the surface's accumulated history.
Previous interventions, some documented, some not,
that complicate access to the damaged layer.

## Echo
[Consolidation](process_consolidation.md) inherits
a surface that has been opened and must now be closed
and a [condition report](piece_condition_report.md)
updated with what the opening revealed.
