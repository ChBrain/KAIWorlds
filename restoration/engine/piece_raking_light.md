# Piece: The Raking Light

## Owner
- **Project:** Restoration
- **Place:** [place_the_studio](place_the_studio.md)

## Place
[The studio](place_the_studio.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it [examination](process_examination.md) cannot find
what lies beneath a prepared surface.
At forty-five degrees every raised edge, every crack,
every seam casts a shadow long enough to read.
Remove it and the [condition report](piece_condition_report.md)
is incomplete.

## Apparent
A directed light source at forty-five degrees
to the surface being examined.
A long shadow at every irregularity.
Damage, repair lines, and seams present
that are not visible in frontal light.

## Yearbook
Present in the profession as long as
the profession has read surfaces.
Twenty years ago: learned as technique,
applied under instruction.
Fifteen years ago: the body found the position
without instruction.
The forty-five degrees became automatic.
