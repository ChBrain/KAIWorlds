# Process: The Accident

## Owner
- **Project:** Restoration
- **Process:** [process_the_treatment](process_the_treatment.md)
- **Place:** [place_the_studio](place_the_studio.md)
- **Piece:** [piece_the_hands](piece_the_hands.md)

## Initiated by
A corrosive agent in contact with the surface
of [the hands](piece_the_hands.md).
[The restorer](position_restorer.md) present.
The damage immediate and progressing.

## Direction
Toward [the treatment room](place_the_treatment_room.md).

## Lever
Carrying force: the damage, progressing
until treatment interrupts it.
Resistance: the trained reading
in [the restorer](position_restorer.md)
running on the injury in real time.
Depth, distribution, likely prognosis,
what this means for the work.
The reading produces
a [condition report](piece_condition_report.md).
It cannot be switched off.

## Echo
[The treatment](process_the_treatment.md) inherits
[the patient surface](position_patient_surface.md)
and a [condition report](piece_condition_report.md)
already complete before the clinical assessment begins.
