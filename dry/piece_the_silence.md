# Piece: The Silence

## Owner
- **Project:** DRY

## Place
Every place in this world.
It travels with whoever carries it.
It requires everyone.

## Load Bearing
Without it the world
is a different world -
one where everything is said.
That world exists.
This is not that world.
Remove The Silence and
[Process: Living With It](process_living_with_it.md)
loses its primary mechanism.
The room cannot hold together
without the agreement
nobody signed.
Without it breaking,
[Process: Recovered](process_recovered.md) cannot run.

## Apparent
Not an absence of sound.
An agreement.
Unwritten. Unspoken.
Known by everyone in the room
including [The One Who Doesn't Know Yet](position_the_one_who_doesnt_know_yet.md) -
who follows the rules
before knowing there are rules.

## Yearbook
Nobody made it.
It assembled itself
from the first time
something was not said.
Requires everyone to carry it.
If one person stops -
The Silence breaks.
[Process: Recovered](process_recovered.md) becomes possible.
[Process: Living With It](process_living_with_it.md)
must find a new shape
or cannot continue.
[Clara](persona_clara.md) carries it without being taught.
She learned the rules before she knew
there were rules.
Before she had words for what she was doing
she was already doing it.
That is how The Silence
ensures its own continuation.
It does not teach.
It is simply the shape of the house
and children learn the shape of the house
before they learn anything else.
