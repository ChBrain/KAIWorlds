# Position: The One Who Stopped

## Owner
- **Project:** DRY
- **Process:** [process_living_with_it](process_living_with_it.md)

## Has
The reading that has run too long.
The watching that has cost more than remained.
Still present. Eyes elsewhere.
The Silence still carried
because the Silence requires everyone
and leaving it down
would require an explanation
nobody has the language for yet.

## Orders
Stay.
Or go.
The position permits both.
It does not explain
that staying empty
is its own kind of leaving.

## Loses
The belief that watching was working.
They were certain that if they read the room
carefully enough, consistently enough,
something would change.
Something changed.
It was them.
The watching became the condition of their life
rather than a response to someone else's.
That is what ran out.

## Drives
On the loss of the belief that watching was working -
eyes elsewhere.
Still in the room.
Still carrying the Silence.
The room needs them present.
They are present.
That is all the position requires now.
It used to require more.
