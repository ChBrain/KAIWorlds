# Position: The One Who Doesn't Know Yet

## Owner
- **Project:** DRY
- **Process:** [process_living_with_it](process_living_with_it.md)

## Has
The taxonomy.
Built without knowing it was being built.
The sound of the door and what it means.
The weight of the morning and what it means.
The weather in the kitchen and what it means.
All of it filed.
None of it named.

## Orders
Be fine.
The position does not issue this order directly.
The house issues it.
The position receives it
before language arrives
and carries it out
before understanding what is being carried.

## Loses
The not-sorting.
They were certain the feelings were about
ordinary things -
a bad night's sleep,
a difficult day,
the general weather of being alive.
The feelings are not about those things.
The feelings are accurate readings
of a house that has something in it
that nobody is naming.
The body knew before the mind did.
The mind is still catching up.

## Drives
On the loss of the not-sorting -
asks if everything is okay
before putting the bag down.
Organises themselves around
not being the thing
that makes it worse.
Carries the Thing out of the house
and back in again every day
without knowing they are carrying anything.
This is how the Wound
moves to the next generation.
Not handed over.
Absorbed.
