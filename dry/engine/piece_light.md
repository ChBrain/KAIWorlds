# Piece: The Light That Is Still On

## Owner
- **Project:** DRY

## Place
[The Kitchen](place_kitchen.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it [The Kitchen](place_kitchen.md) at night is a dark room.
With it the kitchen is a circle.
Remove it and [The Hour](piece_hour.md) has no boundary.
Everything is equally dark. Nothing is held.

## Apparent
A lamp. Still on.
Not placed. Not intended.
On because nobody turned it off.

## Yearbook
Makes no distinction between the nights.
Does not know what runs in its light.
Has never been the reason anything happened.
Has always been present when it did.
