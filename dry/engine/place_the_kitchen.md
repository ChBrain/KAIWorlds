# Place: The Kitchen

## Owner
- **Project:** DRY
- **Position:** [The One Watching](position_the_one_watching.md)

## Shown
- A table. Chairs that have assigned themselves
  to people without anyone deciding.
- Morning light coming in at an angle
  that makes everything look like evidence.
- Surfaces that are clean or not clean.
  Both mean something in this house.
- Three cups. The third one smaller.
- The sounds of the house arriving here first.

## Holds
- [The Morning](piece_the_morning.md). Every morning.
  It lands here before anyone has spoken.
- [The Silence](piece_the_silence.md).
  At its most demanding here.
  The table requires something to be said
  or The Silence to be actively maintained.
  There is no neutral position at the table.

## Offers
- The first reading of the day.
  [The One Watching](position_the_one_watching.md) can read
  what kind of morning this is
  before anyone arrives.
- The surface.
  Clean or not clean.
  The night before, legible.
- The ordinary.
  A place to perform normalcy
  for [The One Who Doesn't Know Yet](position_the_one_who_doesnt_know_yet.md).
  The performance is not entirely false.

## Withheld
- What is not said over breakfast.
  It stays in the room after everyone leaves.
  The Kitchen holds it without comment.
  It accumulates here the same way
  [The Morning](piece_the_morning.md) accumulates -
  quietly, without announcement,
  until the weight of it
  changes the character of the room.
