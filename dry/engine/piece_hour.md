# Piece: The Hour

## Owner
- **Project:** DRY

## Place
[The Kitchen](place_kitchen.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it [The Excuse](piece_the_excuse.md) has no gap.
The Excuse runs during the day. It runs in the morning.
The Hour is when it does not run.
Remove it and [Process: Dry](process_dry.md) has no weak point.
Without a weak point the loop runs without the possibility of breaking.

## Apparent
A time that is not a time.
The hour in which no one is coming.
In which the next decision does not yet exist.

## Lever
Carrying force: [The Connection](piece_connection.md).
Whatever is audible from upstairs.
Whatever is present in the house that is not him.
When it is perceivable the weight shifts.
The bottle stays on the table longer.

Resistance: [The Silence](piece_the_silence.md).
A house completely silent offers The Connection nothing to work with.
When The Silence wins The Hour becomes a room with one thing in it.
The Wound. The Bottle. No weight on either side but his own.

## Yearbook
Has been running since the first time.
Knows both outcomes.
Has held longer when The Connection was present.
Has broken faster when The Silence was complete.
Accumulates nothing.
Passes to [The Morning](piece_the_morning.md) without comment.
