# Place: The Room With The Bottle

## Owner
- **Project:** DRY
- **Position:** [The One Holding](position_the_one_holding.md)

## Shown
- A door that closes.
- The specific place - a shelf, a drawer,
  a gap behind something -
  where [The Bottle](piece_the_bottle.md) was.
  The space it left is the same shape as The Bottle.
- Ordinary objects that have learned
  not to draw attention to themselves.
- No window, or a window that faces away.
  The outside does not press in here.

## Holds
- [The Bottle](piece_the_bottle.md). Or the space where it was.
  The room does not distinguish between the two.
- [The Wound](piece_the_wound.md) at its most concentrated.
  The Bottle was here because the Wound was here.
  The Bottle is gone. The Wound remained.

## Offers
- The truth.
  It is the only room in the house that offers this.
  [The One Holding](position_the_one_holding.md) can stand in it
  and know exactly what they are.
- The door.
  The choice to leave is always present.
  [The Excuse](piece_the_excuse.md) reassembles at the door.
  The room offers the moment before that happens.

## Withheld
- How many times the person has stood here.
  The room knows.
  It does not say.
- What [The One Holding](position_the_one_holding.md) knows
  in this room and cannot carry out of it intact.
  The knowing arrives here.
  The Excuse meets them at the door.
  The room withholds the knowing
  from every other room in the house.
