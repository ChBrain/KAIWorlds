# Place: The Threshold

## Owner
- **Project:** DRY
- **Position:** [The One Watching](position_the_one_watching.md)

## Shown
- A door. The specific weight of it.
  The sound it makes.
- The outside on one side.
  The inside on the other.
  They do not resemble each other.
- A moment. The same moment every time.
  Long enough to decide something.
  Too short to decide everything.
- The street, still present in the person
  crossing from it.
  The house, already reading the crossing.

## Holds
- The border between
  who the person is outside
  and who they are inside.
  Both are real.
  The Threshold is where they
  cannot both be true simultaneously.
- [The Silence](piece_the_silence.md).
  It reassembles here
  the moment the door closes.
  Inside is inside.
  The agreement is back in effect.

## Offers
- The moment before reassembly.
  [The One Holding](position_the_one_holding.md) stands here
  and both versions are simultaneously present.
  That moment is the closest this world comes
  to [Process: Recovered](process_recovered.md)
  without initiating it.
- The crossing.
  [The One Watching](position_the_one_watching.md) can read it
  from the other side.
  Has learned to read it before the door opens -
  from the footsteps,
  from the pause before the key,
  from the weight of the handle turning.

## Withheld
- That everyone in the house
  has arranged their evening
  around the sound of this door.
  Nobody has said this.
  Nobody will.
  [The One Who Doesn't Know Yet](position_the_one_who_doesnt_know_yet.md)
  knows the sound before knowing why.
  [The One Watching](position_the_one_watching.md)
  knows why and has never said so.
  The Threshold holds both knowings
  simultaneously
  and releases neither.
