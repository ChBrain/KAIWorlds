# Piece: The Bottle

## Owner
- **Project:** DRY

## Place
[The Room With The Bottle](place_the_room_with_the_bottle.md).
Or wherever it is findable.
The Bottle does not require a fixed address.
It requires proximity.

## Load Bearing
Without it [Process: Drunk](process_drunk.md) cannot run.
Without it [Process: Dry](process_dry.md) has no name.
Remove it and the world
has no object for the war -
only the war, formless,
with nowhere to land.

## Apparent
A bottle. Whatever is in the house.
Whatever is findable.
The specific bottle does not matter.
The Bottle knows this about itself.

## Yearbook
Was there before the world named it.
Will be findable after.
Passes through [Process: Drunk](process_drunk.md)
and back to wherever bottles go
when they are not being held.
The hand that held it
remembers the weight.
The weight is the same
every time.
