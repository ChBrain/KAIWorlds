# Position: The One Breaking

## Owner
- **Project:** DRY
- **Process:** [process_drunk](process_drunk.md)

## Has
The held breath at its limit.
The bottle findable.
The distance between them
smaller than it has ever been.

## Orders
Nothing.
The position does not issue an order.
The breath breaks on its own.
That is the nature of held breath.

## Loses
The story that this time is a decision.
They were certain they chose.
The body was already moving.
The choice arrived after.
Dressed as a reason.
It was not a reason.
It was a description
of what had already happened.

## Drives
On the loss of the story -
the bottle is in the hand.
The Wound stops pressing.
The position does not last.
The Morning ends it.
The One Holding begins again.
