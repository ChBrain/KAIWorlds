# Process: Living With It

## Owner
- **Project:** DRY

## Initiated by
The held breath belongs to someone else.
[The One Watching](position_the_one_watching.md) is present.
They know. Or suspect. Or have stopped asking.
All three are the same initiation.
[The Kitchen](place_the_kitchen.md) is the room where this becomes undeniable.

## Direction
Toward holding the room together.
Not the person - the room.
The room that [The One Holding](position_the_one_holding.md)
has been slowly taking apart.

## Lever
Carrying force: love.
Or what love becomes
after long enough.
They are not always the same thing
and [The One Watching](position_the_one_watching.md)
cannot always tell which one it is.

Resistance: [The One Holding](position_the_one_holding.md) is still the same person.
Putting [The Bottle](piece_the_bottle.md) down
did not put down
what the Bottle was carrying.
[The Silence](piece_the_silence.md) is the room feeling this
without being able to say it.

## Echo
Two possible Echoes.
The room breaks.
[The One Who Stopped](position_the_one_who_stopped.md) leaves.
Or the room holds.
The One Who Stopped stays -
present, empty, still carrying
[The Silence](piece_the_silence.md) because
the Silence requires everyone
and there is no one else.
Both Echoes are in this world.
The process does not choose.
