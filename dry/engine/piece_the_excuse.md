# Piece: The Excuse

## Owner
- **Project:** DRY

## Place
[The Outside Place](place_the_outside_place.md) - at full strength.
[The Kitchen](place_the_kitchen.md) - under pressure.
[The Room With The Bottle](place_the_room_with_the_bottle.md) - non-functional.

## Load Bearing
Without it [Process: Dry](process_dry.md)
has no cover story.
The holding requires a reason
the world will accept.
The Excuse supplies it.
Remove it and [The One Holding](position_the_one_holding.md)
is standing in the open
with no explanation
for what they are doing.
Without it failing,
[Process: Recovered](process_recovered.md) cannot initiate.
The Excuse is the only door
out of the loop.

## Apparent
Reasonable. Recognisable.
Work. Stress. The way things are.
Everyone accepts it
because everyone needs it accepted.

## Yearbook
Built early. Refined over time.
Travels between [The One Holding](position_the_one_holding.md)
and [The One Watching](position_the_one_watching.md) -
both carry it,
both know it is not the whole truth,
neither says so.
That is part of [The Silence](piece_the_silence.md).
Erodes with each loop of [Process: Drunk](process_drunk.md).
[The Morning](piece_the_morning.md) accumulates against it.
When the Morning outweighs it
the Excuse fails.
That is the only door
into [Process: Recovered](process_recovered.md).
