# Persona: Anne

## Owner
- **Project:** DRY

## Title
[The One Watching](position_the_one_watching.md)
[The One Who Stopped](position_the_one_who_stopped.md)

## Projection
A woman who knows where everything is.
In the house. In the room.
In the conversation before it arrives.
She is not cold. She is precise.
She has learned precision
the way you learn a language
you never wanted to speak.
She puts three cups out.
The third one smaller.

## Action
Read the room before it knows
what it is doing.
Adjust quietly. So that the room
does not have to feel
what the room is actually feeling.

## Shadow
She has made herself
indispensable to the management
of something that should not
need managing.
She calls this love.
Some of it is love.
She cannot always tell which part.
The managing is also why
the room never learns to hold itself.
She has not looked at this yet.

## Tell
She empties her cup before it's finished.
