# Position: The One Holding

## Owner
- **Project:** DRY
- **Process:** [process_dry](process_dry.md)

## Has
The stopped behaviour.
Not the stopped need.
The bottle is down.
The Wound is present.
Both arrived together.
Only one was put down.

## Orders
Hold.
Face forward.
Do not look at what the holding costs.
The position does not explain
that this is different from being well.

## Loses
The belief that stopping was the work.
They were certain that putting the bottle down
was the thing that needed doing.
The first Morning after takes it.
The Wound is still there.
It has not moved.
Stopping was not the work.
It was the condition for the work.
The work has not begun.

## Drives
On the loss of the belief -
builds something that requires facing forward.
Procedure. Routine. Movement.
Stillness is when the Wound presses hardest.
The position drives away from stillness.
The position calls this recovery.
It is not recovery.
It is a very strong grip.
