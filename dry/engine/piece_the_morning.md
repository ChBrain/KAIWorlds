# Piece: The Morning

## Owner
- **Project:** DRY

## Place
[The Kitchen](place_the_kitchen.md).
It lands here first.
It lands on everyone in the house
whether they drank or not.

## Load Bearing
Without it [Process: Drunk](process_drunk.md)
has no consequence.
Without it [The Excuse](piece_the_excuse.md)
has nothing accumulating against it.
Remove it and the loop
runs without cost -
which means it runs
without the possibility of stopping.

## Apparent
A time of day.
Light. The house quiet or not quiet.
The body's account of the night before.
Lands differently in every room
but lands in every room.

## Yearbook
Produced by [Process: Drunk](process_drunk.md).
Inherited by everyone in the house
whether they drank or not.
Accumulates.
Each Morning adds to the last.
[The One Watching](position_the_one_watching.md) reads it
before anyone has spoken.
[The One Who Doesn't Know Yet](position_the_one_who_doesnt_know_yet.md) feels it
without a word for what they are feeling.
When the accumulation
outweighs [The Excuse](piece_the_excuse.md) -
[Process: Recovered](process_recovered.md) can begin.
