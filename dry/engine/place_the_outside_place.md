# Place: The Outside Place

## Owner
- **Project:** DRY
- **Position:** [The One Holding](position_the_one_holding.md)

## Shown
- Other people. None of them
  carrying [The Silence](piece_the_silence.md) of this house.
- [The Excuse](piece_the_excuse.md) working perfectly.
  Accepted without question.
  Nobody here knows to question it.
- The person as they appear
  when [The Wound](piece_the_wound.md) is not pressing.
  Or appears not to be pressing.
- No accumulation.
  [The Morning](piece_the_morning.md) has not gathered here.
  Nobody has been present for enough of them.

## Holds
- The version of [The One Holding](position_the_one_holding.md)
  that the bottle was making possible.
  Out here something else makes it possible.
  The person does not always know what.
  That is load-bearing.

## Offers
- The performance of wellness.
  It is not entirely false.
  That is what makes it dangerous.
- Relief from [The Wound](piece_the_wound.md).
  Not resolution. Distance.
  The distance is real while it lasts.
- The Excuse at full strength.
  The only place in this world
  where it has never failed.

## Withheld
- That the Outside Place
  is also a version of [The Bottle](piece_the_bottle.md).
  Different mechanism. Same function.
  Same [Wound](piece_the_wound.md) underneath.
  [The One Holding](position_the_one_holding.md) has not looked at this yet.
  When they do -
  [Process: Recovered](process_recovered.md) becomes possible.
  The Outside Place withholds this
  for as long as it can.
  Which is: as long as it is working.
