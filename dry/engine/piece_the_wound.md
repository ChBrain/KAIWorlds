# Piece: The Wound

## Owner
- **Project:** DRY

## Place
Wherever [The One Holding](position_the_one_holding.md) stands.
It has no room of its own.
It travels.

## Load Bearing
Without it [The Bottle](piece_the_bottle.md) is just a bottle.
Without it [Process: Recovered](process_recovered.md)
has nothing to move toward.
Remove it and the loop
runs without an engine -
which means it runs forever
and nobody knows why.

## Apparent
Not visible.
Older than the bottle.
The bottle was standing in front of it.
That is all anyone knew for a long time.
That is all some people ever know.

## Yearbook
Arrived before the drinking did.
Will remain after the drinking stops.
Carried into [The Room With The Bottle](place_the_room_with_the_bottle.md)
every time [The One Holding](position_the_one_holding.md) stands in it.
The room holds it at its most concentrated.
The Excuse cannot function in that room.
In the next generation
it arrives as [The Thing The Child Carries](piece_the_thing_the_child_carries.md) -
unnamed, unfiled, already present.
The child does not know they are carrying it.
The name comes later.
The name is The Wound.
They are the same piece
at different points in time.
