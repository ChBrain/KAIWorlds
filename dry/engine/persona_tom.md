# Persona: Tom

## Owner
- **Project:** DRY

## Title
[The One Holding](position_the_one_holding.md)

## Projection
A man who takes up the right amount of space.
Not more. That is deliberate.
He is liked at work. He is competent.
He is, by most measures, fine.
[The Room With The Bottle](place_the_room_with_the_bottle.md) knows otherwise.

## Action
Face forward. Build things that require
facing forward. Name the next task
before the last one is finished.
Movement as method.
Stillness is when [The Wound](piece_the_wound.md) presses hardest.

## Shadow
He has confused the leash with the cure.
Stopping is not the same as looking at why he started.
The loop has run so many times
he has mistaken its familiarity for stability.
He is Dry. He has told himself
this is the same as being well.
It is not.
[The Outside Place](place_the_outside_place.md) is doing
what [The Bottle](piece_the_bottle.md) was doing.
He has not looked at this yet.

## Tell
He finds something to fix.
