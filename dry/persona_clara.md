# Persona: Clara

## Owner
- **Project:** DRY

## Title
[The One Who Doesn't Know Yet](position_the_one_who_doesnt_know_yet.md)

## Projection
A girl who is fine.
She will tell you she is fine
before you ask.
She has a lot of friends at school.
She sleeps with the light on
and has a reason for this
that is not the real reason.

## Action
Sort everything quietly.
The house. The weather in the house.
What [The Threshold](place_the_threshold.md) means tonight.
What [The Kitchen](place_the_kitchen.md) means this morning.
She has a taxonomy.
She built it herself.
Nobody helped her.
Nobody knows it exists.

## Shadow
She is carrying [The Thing The Child Carries](piece_the_thing_the_child_carries.md)
with both hands
and has organised her entire self
around not dropping it
and around nobody seeing
that she is carrying anything at all.
The organisation is not a coping strategy.
It is the architecture of a person
being built around [The Silence](piece_the_silence.md)
she did not choose and cannot name.
By the time she has a word for it
it will be structural.
It will be the shape of how she moves
through every room she ever stands in.
This is how [The Wound](piece_the_wound.md)
moves to the next generation.
Not handed over.
Absorbed.

## Tell
She asks if everything is okay
before she puts her bag down.
