# Position: The One Looking Left

## Owner
- **Project:** DRY
- **Process:** [process_recovered](process_recovered.md)

## Has
The loop behind them.
The Excuse gone thin.
The Outside Place seen for what it is.
Nothing left between themselves
and the Wound.
The question: why.
Finally: why.

## Orders
Look at it.
Not at the bottle.
At what the bottle was standing in front of.
The position does not promise
that looking will be sufficient.
It only requires that the looking happen.

## Loses
The forward direction.
They were certain that facing forward
was the same as moving forward.
The Wound takes that certainty.
You cannot face forward
and look left simultaneously.
The position requires turning.
Everything built to face forward
has to be left standing
while the person turns.

## Drives
On the loss of the forward direction -
stands in front of the Wound.
Without the Excuse.
Without the Outside Place.
Without the bottle.
With nothing between themselves and it.
That is the whole of the position.
What happens next is not a mechanism.
It is a life.
