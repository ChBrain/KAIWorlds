# Piece: The Thing The Child Carries

## Owner
- **Project:** DRY

## Place
[The Child's Room](place_the_childs_room.md).
It lives here because [The One Who Doesn't Know Yet](position_the_one_who_doesnt_know_yet.md) lives here.
It has no other address in this world.

## Load Bearing
Without it the world ends
with this generation.
Remove it and the second loop
does not run -
the world replicates once
and stops.

## Apparent
Unnamed. Unfiled.
A feeling without a word.
The weather in the house
that [The One Who Doesn't Know Yet](position_the_one_who_doesnt_know_yet.md) reads
before thought -
the way you know a floorboard is loose.

## Yearbook
Present before the child
has language for it.
Carried out of [The Child's Room](place_the_childs_room.md)
every morning.
Carried back in every evening.
Passes through [The Threshold](place_the_threshold.md)
in both directions
without ever being set down.
Carried out of this world
into the next.
In the next generation
it surfaces as [The Wound](piece_the_wound.md) -
older than the drinking,
already there when the drinking starts.
They are the same piece.
The name arrives later.
The child does not know this yet.
