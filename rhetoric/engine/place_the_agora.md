# Place: The Agora

## Owner
- **Project:** Rhetoric

## Shown
The speaking space. Open. The [Speaker](position_speaker.md) stands in it. The [Audience](position_audience.md) faces them. The occasion is public - a claim is being made before people who did not choose to believe it in advance. The whole of [Techne](process_techne.md) occurs within it.

## Holds
- [Logos](piece_logos.md) - the structure of the speech is built and delivered here.
- [Ethos](piece_ethos.md) - carried in by the Speaker from the first word, visible to the Audience from this place.
- [Kairos](piece_kairos.md) - the occasion that makes the speech possible or impossible is located here.

## Offers
The conditions for persuasion. The Speaker can speak. The Audience can hear. The claim can be stated, argued, and received. What happens in this place is witnessed - by the Audience, by the occasion itself.

## Withheld
Whether the Audience is persuadable. The Agora holds the Audience but does not reveal their prior convictions. The Speaker must read them from within the speech - from the reactions, the silence, the resistance. The place offers access. It does not offer certainty.
