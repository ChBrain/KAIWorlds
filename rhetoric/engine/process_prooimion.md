# Process: Prooimion

## Owner
- **Project:** Rhetoric
- **Process:** [Techne](process_techne.md)

## Initiated by
[Techne](process_techne.md) has begun. The Speaker is present. The Audience is assembled but not yet engaged. The occasion is known. The claim has not yet been stated.

## Direction
Toward a disposed Audience - one that is attentive, well-disposed toward the Speaker, and receptive to what follows. Not toward argument. Argument has not begun.

## Lever
Carrying force: the Speaker's opening move - the first establishment of [Ethos](piece_ethos.md) before it is named. Who is speaking. Why they are here. Why the Audience should continue to listen.

Resistance: the Audience's indifference. They did not come to be persuaded. They came to judge. The Prooimion must work against the weight of that prior posture without yet deploying the full means of persuasion.

## Echo
[Prothesis](process_prothesis.md) inherits an Audience that is listening. Not yet convinced. Not yet resistant. The ground has been prepared. What changed: the Audience has a reason to hear the claim.
