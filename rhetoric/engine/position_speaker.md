# Position: Speaker

## Owner
- **Project:** Rhetoric
- **Process:** [Techne](process_techne.md)
- **Process:** [Prooimion](process_prooimion.md)
- **Process:** [Prothesis](process_prothesis.md)
- **Process:** [Pistis](process_pistis.md)
- **Process:** [Epilogos](process_epilogos.md)

## Has
The claim. The occasion. The means - [Ethos](piece_ethos.md), [Pathos](piece_pathos.md), [Logos](piece_logos.md) - available to be deployed. The position exists before any persona steps into it. What it does not have: the outcome.

## Orders
See the available means of persuasion and deploy them fully.

## Loses
The certainty that the method is enough. The position holds the means. It cannot hold the Audience's response. What the persona was certain they were bringing - the power to convince - is not in the position. It never was.

## Drives
On the loss - speaks anyway. Deploys what is available. The position does not wait for certainty it cannot have.
