# Process: Epilogos

## Owner
- **Project:** Rhetoric
- **Process:** [Techne](process_techne.md)

## Initiated by
[Pistis](process_pistis.md) has run. The proof has been deployed. The Audience has been met with the full means of persuasion. The Speaker moves to close.

## Direction
Toward completion. The claim is restated. The emotional state of the Audience is addressed one final time. The speech does not end - it lands.

## Lever
Carrying force: summary and emotional appeal working together. Aristotle notes both are present in the conclusion: the recapitulation of what was proven, and a final movement of [Pathos](piece_pathos.md) directed at the Audience's capacity to decide.

Resistance: the Audience's remaining doubt. [Pistis](process_pistis.md) may not have fully closed it. Epilogos does not reopen the argument - it does not have time. It must work with what [Pistis](process_pistis.md) produced and use the close to consolidate it.

## Echo
[Techne](process_techne.md) is complete. The Audience moves - or does not. What changed: the occasion is finished. The Speaker has seen and deployed the available means. The Audience now holds what it will hold. The speech cannot be unsaid.
