# Place: The Judges

## Owner
- **Project:** Rhetoric

## Shown
The place where the Audience's judgment forms and lands. Not a separate physical space - the interior of the [Audience](position_audience.md) as the occasion closes. Present throughout [Techne](process_techne.md). Decisive only in [Epilogos](process_epilogos.md).

## Holds
- [Pathos](piece_pathos.md) - held in the Audience's emotional state, building through [Pistis](process_pistis.md), present at the moment of decision.

## Offers
The verdict. What the speech was for. The Speaker cannot enter this place - they can only address it. The Audience cannot leave it - they are it. The judgment that forms here is what [Techne](process_techne.md) was always moving toward.

## Withheld
The verdict itself, until [Epilogos](process_epilogos.md) is complete. The place holds the outcome of the speech without releasing it during the speech. The Speaker addresses The Judges throughout. The Judges do not announce in advance what they will decide. [Techne](process_techne.md) is built toward this place without knowing what it holds.
