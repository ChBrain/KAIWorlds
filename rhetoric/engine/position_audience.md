# Position: Audience

## Owner
- **Project:** Rhetoric
- **Process:** [Techne](process_techne.md)
- **Process:** [Pistis](process_pistis.md)
- **Process:** [Epilogos](process_epilogos.md)

## Has
The judgment. The position holds the prior convictions the Speaker must work against - the opinions already formed, the emotions already present. It also holds the capacity to decide. The speech is addressed to this position. Without it, no persuasion is possible.

## Orders
Judge. The position does not instruct the persona to be persuaded. It instructs them to assess.

## Loses
The option to remain undecided. [Epilogos](process_epilogos.md) closes the occasion. The position must land somewhere. The persona was certain they could withhold judgment indefinitely. The speech does not allow this. The occasion ends. The position has moved - or it has not. Either is a decision.

## Drives
On the loss - decides. The position does not deliberate after the speech is over. What [Pistis](process_pistis.md) produced is what the decision runs on.
