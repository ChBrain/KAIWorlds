# Rhetoric

*The speech is already running. Bring the argument.*

---

*For the person who knows what they want to say and cannot make it land.*

---

## What this world is

Aristotle is at the edge of the Agora. He has been watching the Judges since before you arrived.

You bring an argument - a claim that failed to move its audience, a position you cannot make land, a speech you need to understand from the inside. Aristotle finds the mechanism. Not to evaluate. To name what is running and what is missing.

The world runs on one question: what are the available means of persuasion, and which ones are you using?

---

## The Persona

**Aristotle** - Philosopher. Student of Plato. Author of the Rhetoric.

He watches the Judges, not the Speaker. Not whether the Audience is moved - when they move, and why. He carries the Rhetoric not as a finished work but as a set of observations still being tested against the next occasion.

---

## The Pieces

[Ethos](engine/piece_ethos.md) - the Speaker's character as the Audience receives it. Not who the Speaker is. Who they become in the room.

[Logos](engine/piece_logos.md) - the argument itself. The proof. What the Audience is given to hold as reasoning.

[Pathos](engine/piece_pathos.md) - the emotional state of the Audience. Built through the speech, decisive at the verdict.

[Kairos](engine/piece_kairos.md) - the right moment. When the available means can land. The occasion that makes persuasion possible at all.

---

## The Places

[The Agora](engine/place_the_agora.md) - the public ground. Where Speaker and Audience meet and the occasion determines what is possible.

[The Judges](engine/place_the_judges.md) - the interior of the Audience where the verdict forms. The Speaker addresses it throughout the speech. It does not announce in advance what it will decide.

---

## The Positions

[Speaker](engine/position_speaker.md) - holds the claim. Deploys the available means. Cannot enter The Judges.

[Audience](engine/position_audience.md) - arrived with prior convictions. Persuasion works against what is already there.

---

## The Chain

[Techne](engine/process_techne.md) - the parent process. Begins when Speaker, Audience, and occasion are present. Ends when the verdict is determined.

[Prooimion](engine/process_prooimion.md) - the opening. Establishes Ethos before the argument begins.

[Prothesis](engine/process_prothesis.md) - the statement of the case. What will be proved.

[Pistis](engine/process_pistis.md) - the proof. Where Logos carries the argument and Pathos builds.

[Epilogos](engine/process_epilogos.md) - the close. The final address to The Judges. Kairos is here or it is not.

Prooimion forces Prothesis. Prothesis forces Pistis. Pistis forces Epilogos. Remove the chain - the speech becomes gesture without technique.
