# Process: The Deciding
## The present moment running toward two futures simultaneously.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Timeline
**Persona:** The Scientist

## Initiates
The Now. The tourist is on the shore.
The Scientist has the record.
The branch point is where they are both standing —
one knowing it, one not yet.
Both futures are still possible.
The Deciding initiates not with a single decision
but with the accumulation of decisions
being made and not made
in every room that has authority
over what happens to this water:
the field, the policy office,
the fishing quota table, the RECOVER project,
the governance meeting that crosses the border,
the farmer who checks the tile outfall,
the scientist who pulls up the graph
and points at the trend line
without speaking first.
All of these are The Deciding.
It is running now.

## Direction
Toward two futures simultaneously.
Branch A: ecosystem-based management implemented.
The decisions made. The cod recovering.
The eelgrass returning.
The first joint measurement filed.
The RECOVER digital twin running.
The Naturpark and the monitoring station
speaking the same language.
Branch B: current trajectories continued.
The decisions not made —
not reversed, not fought against,
simply not made, in the way
that most decisions are not made:
by the continuation of what already exists.
Both directions are running from this moment.
The Deciding does not choose between them.
It runs toward both.
The choice is in the rooms, not in the process.

## Lever
The gap between the data and the decision.
Not the data. Not the decision.
The gap.
The Scientist has closed the data side completely —
sixty-seven years of monthly measurements,
the paradox named, the mechanism confirmed,
the branch point identified, Branch A modelled,
Branch B modelled.
The gap that remains is not scientific.
It is the distance between
what the Scientist knows
and what the decision-maker feels.
The RECOVER project is building the instrument
that could close this gap.
The instrument is not finished.
The gap is still running.
The Deciding is running through the gap.

## Echo
Two Echoes. Running simultaneously
from the same initiating moment.

Branch A: The Future — Branch A.
The eelgrass returning.
The Restorer who made the decision
and lives in its consequence.

Branch B: The Future — Branch B.
The closure sign.
The Inheritor who inherited the default
and considers it normal.

The branch point that produced both futures
is invisible in both of them.
In Branch A it is remembered
as the moment things almost went wrong.
In Branch B it is not remembered at all.
