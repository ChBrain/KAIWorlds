# Place: The Future — Branch B
## Location: The Kiel Bight. The Flensburg Fjord. ~2045.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Timeline
**Persona:** The Inheritor

## Shown
- The water. The same geography.
  The thermocline deeper than in 2026.
  The warming has continued.
  The dead zone forms in July now.
  It closes in October.
  Four months. The instruments record this
  as the new normal. The Inheritor
  does not know it used to be two months.
- The beach. The closure signs are part
  of the infrastructure — like the tide tables,
  like the weather forecast.
  Checked before visiting.
  Planned around. The Inheritor
  has a notification on her phone
  that tells her the water quality rating
  for her nearest beach.
  She checks it automatically.
  She has always checked it.
  She does not think of it as a sign
  that something is wrong.
  It is simply how beaches work.
- The eelgrass. At one metre depth
  where it was at one metre in 2026.
  No change. The retreat has stabilised —
  not recovered, stabilised.
  The shallower extent is now the extent.
  The Inheritor has never seen it deeper.
  To her it is simply: where the seagrass is.
- The cod. Absent from inshore waters.
  The inshore fishery has restructured
  around the species that tolerate warmer,
  lower-oxygen water.
  The Inheritor eats the fish that are here.
  She does not miss the fish that aren't.
  She has never eaten them.
- The monitoring. Still running.
  The Boknis Eck station: seventy-five years.
  The record shows the trajectory
  continued in the wrong direction.
  The scientists are still pointing
  at the trend line without speaking first.
  The room still does not receive it.

## Holds
- [The Closure Sign](piece_the_closure_sign.md).
  At the beach entrance.
  Before every August visit.

## Offers
- The water. Still swimmable in June.
  In September. In the windows
  between bloom events.
  The Inheritor swims in the windows.
  She considers this normal.
  She is not wrong.
  This is the water she has.
- The coast. Still beautiful.
  The Naturpark Schlei still maintained.
  The Gendarmestien still walkable.
  The hotels still open.
  The fishing tourism adapted —
  different species, different season,
  different expectation.
  Available.

## Withheld
- What the branch point was.
  The Inheritor does not know
  that in 2026 both futures were possible.
  She does not know about Branch A.
  She does not know the Scientist
  pointed at the trend line for twenty years.
  She does not know the RECOVER project
  existed and ran and closed
  and its recommendations were noted
  and filed and partially implemented
  and then the funding cycle ended.
  She does not know the branch point
  existed. She swims in the water
  that is available. She checks the sign.
  She does not grieve what she never had.
  That is the thing the branch point
  was trying to prevent:
  not the bad water,
  but the forgetting
  that the good water was possible.
  The Inheritor has forgotten
  because she never knew.
  The branch point is withheld from her
  completely and permanently.
  That is Branch B.
