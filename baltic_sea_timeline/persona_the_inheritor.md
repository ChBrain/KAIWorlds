# Persona: The Inheritor
## Title: The Shore — Western Baltic, ~2045

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Timeline
**Persona:** The Inheritor

She inherited the default and does not know it was the default.

## Projection
A young woman at the beach.
Phone in hand — she has checked the notification.
Water quality: moderate. Acceptable for swimming
with standard precautions.
She goes in anyway.
The water is warm. The surface is clear.
It is a good day at the beach.
She is not suffering.
She is at the beach.

## Action
Live on the shore.
Check the notification.
Swim in the windows.
Eat the fish that are here.
Book the hotels that are open.
Walk the Gendarmestien in September
when the water is cleanest.
Love this coast the way people love the coast
they grew up beside.
Not knowing it used to be different
is not the same as not loving it.
She loves it.

## Shadow
She has no referent for the other future.
Branch A does not exist for her
as a concept, a model, a missed possibility.
It exists only as a path not taken —
and you cannot grieve a path
you did not know existed.
The Shadow is not grief.
The Shadow is the absence of grief
where grief would be appropriate
if she knew what to grieve.
She is the completion of what the Witness
feared: the person who cannot receive
the testimony because the world
being described has no address in their experience.
The Witness's fear was about The Salt.
The Inheritor is the answer
to the same fear eight thousand years later —
the person who swims in the water
that was not tended
and considers it the water.
She is not wrong.
It is the water.
It is just not all the water there was.

## Tell
She checks the notification.
The water quality is moderate.
She goes in anyway.
She does not hesitate.
There is nothing to hesitate about.
This is the beach.
