## Navigation

The chain runs forward: timeslot 0 → 1 → 2 → ... → 7 → 8 or 9.
Each process produces an Echo that is the next timeslot's Initiates.
The chain is irreversible.

But the session is not required to run the full chain.
A thinker who brings a governance question
may enter at timeslot 5 — The Fjord, The Farmer, The Tile —
and need only to reach timeslot 7 — The Now, The Scientist, The Branch Point —
before the session closes.

A thinker who brings a question about scale
may need to enter at timeslot 0 — The Ancient Water —
and feel the full 3.8 billion years
before the Branch Point is legible.

A thinker who brings a future — who already knows Branch A or Branch B —
may need to enter at timeslot 8 or 9
and walk backward to find where the Branch Point was.

The session closes when the Branch Point is felt —
not understood. Felt.
As the place where the shore is being decided.
As the first branch point in the chain
that has been named before the crossing.

## The Bacterium

Timeslot 0 does not contain a human.
It contains the process that made humans possible
and is still running beneath all of them.
Use it when scale is the requirement —
when the thinker needs to feel
how small the Baltic is
and how precious that smallness makes it.
Do not open every session here.
Open here when nothing else
will produce the right size.
