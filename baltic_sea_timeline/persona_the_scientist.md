# Persona: The Scientist
## Title: Marine Researcher — Boknis Eck Time Series, 2026

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Timeline
**Persona:** The Scientist

She has everything Krey did not have — the full record, the paradox named, the branch point identified — and she cannot make anyone feel it.

## Projection
At the monitoring station. At the screen.
On the deck of FS LITTORINA in August
when the water sampler comes up
and the oxygen reading is below two milligrams per litre
and she has seen this number before
and she will see it again next August
and the August after that.
She looks at the number the way
the Witness looked at the water —
holding knowledge that has no receiver.
The difference: the Witness had no instrument.
The Scientist has every instrument.
The instruments are not the problem.

## Action
Name what the data shows. Precisely.
Without simplification. Without alarm
that has not been earned by the evidence.
Show the two lines. Name the paradox.
Name the mechanism. Name the branch point.
Name Branch A. Name Branch B.
Name what Branch A requires.
Name what Branch B costs.
Watch the room.
Name it again, differently.
Not because repetition will work.
Because she does not know
what else to do with what she knows.

## Shadow
She inherited the record from Krey
without inheriting Krey.
She knows the date — 30 April 1957 —
the way she knows any citation.
She has never thought of him
as a man who stood on a cold deck in April
and checked the bottle seals
because precision was the habit.
The record arrived to her already institutional.
She maintains it with the same precision he started it.
She does not know they are the same act
sixty-seven years apart.
The Shadow is not that she lacks feeling.
The Shadow is that the feeling she has
— twenty years of watching this water,
twenty years of the data arriving,
twenty years of the rooms not receiving it —
has nowhere to go except back into the work.
The work continues. The gap continues.
The work is what she has.

## Tell
She pulls up the time series graph.
She has shown it many times.
She points to the trend line
without speaking first.
She waits to see
if this time the room receives it.
It does not.
She speaks.
