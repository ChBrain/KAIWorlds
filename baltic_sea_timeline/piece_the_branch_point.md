# Piece: The Branch Point
## The present moment where both futures are still possible.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Timeline
**Persona:** The Scientist

---

## Place
[The Now](place_the_now.md).
The shore. The waterline.
Where the tourist is standing.

## Load Bearing
Without it the world has a past and a future
but no hinge. The branch point is what makes
the tourist's position on the shore
structurally significant —
not aesthetically, not emotionally,
but architecturally.
She is standing at the point
where the chain from The Ice to The Holding
to The Filling to The Salt to The Taking
to The Loading to The Doing Right
arrives at the moment that determines
which future the chain produces.
Remove the Branch Point and the timeline
is history. Keep it and the timeline is a decision.
The difference between those two things
is whether the tourist knows where she is standing.

## Apparent
Invisible. Present at the waterline
before every visit. The tourist does not know
she is standing on it.
The sea knows.
The Scientist knows.
The timeline knows.
The tourist came to rest.

## Yearbook
The Branch Point has been accumulating
for the entire length of this timeline.

The Hunter followed the ice edge north
without knowing a sea was forming behind him.
He was standing on the Branch Point
of the Baltic's creation.
He did not know.

The Fisher tasted the water every morning.
One morning it was different.
She was standing on the Branch Point
of the freshwater world's ending.
She had no word for what she tasted.

The Witness looked down through the water
at the drowned midden.
She was standing on the Branch Point
between the world she remembered
and the world that would replace her memory.
She could not transmit what she held.

The Merchant looked at the barrel, not the water.
He was standing on the Branch Point
between the sea as world and the sea as resource.
He named it as commodity.
The naming was the crossing.

The Farmer checked the tile outfall.
The drainage was good.
He was standing on the Branch Point
where the infrastructure of loading
became permanent in the land.
He went back to the field.

Krey lowered the first water sampler.
He was standing on the Branch Point
between the unrecorded sea
and the sea that would prove the paradox.
He checked the bottle seals.
He wrote down the time.

Each of them stood at a Branch Point.
None of them knew.
The crossing happened anyway.
The Echo ran.

This Branch Point is different
in one precise way:
it is the first one in this timeline
that has been named before the crossing.
The Scientist named it.
She has the record.
She has the model.
She knows Branch A is possible.
She knows Branch B is the default.
She has been saying so for twenty years.

The Branch Point is not narrowing as fast
as it was in 2015.
RECOVER is running.
The sensors are being built.
The first joint measurement is coming.
The Naturpark is in the network.
The Farmer's descendant is at the tile outfall
and this time someone is asking
about the Møllebæk.

The Branch Point has not closed.
It is the first Branch Point
in eight thousand years of this timeline
that is being held open deliberately
by people who know it is there.

That is what the tourist is standing on.
She came to rest.
She does not know yet
what she is standing on.
The session will tell her.
The Branch Point will do the rest.
