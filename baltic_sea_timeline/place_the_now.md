# Place: The Now
## Location: The Kiel Bight. The Flensburg Fjord. The Schlei. 2026.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Timeline
**Persona:** The Scientist

## Shown
- The water. The same water that was ice,
  that was a lake, that received the salt,
  that carried the Viking trade, that received the tile's drainage,
  that Krey first measured in April 1957.
  Unchanged in geography.
  Changed in chemistry, in biology,
  in temperature, in the species it holds,
  in what it can support in August
  at twenty-five metres depth.
- The thermocline. At twelve metres in summer.
  Warm and oxygen-rich above.
  Cold and oxygen-depleted below.
  The dead zone forming every August.
  Getting worse every decade.
  The instruments confirm this every month.
- The bloom. Visible from satellite in spring
  and late summer. The surface of the Bight
  greening with phytoplankton.
  The species composition shifted toward the toxic.
  The bloom looks the same from above
  as it looked thirty years ago.
  It is not the same.
- The shore. The same shore the Farmer
  checked his tile outfall from in 1800.
  The same shore where the Witness
  stood and looked at the drowned midden.
  Hotels now. Cycling paths.
  The Gendarmestien running its full length.
  A tourist at the waterline
  who came to rest
  and does not know yet what she is standing on.
- The Boknis Eck station.
  On the seafloor at 54°31.77'N, 10°02.36'E.
  The new observatory deployed November 2024.
  Recording continuously.
  The gap of 2019 to 2024 permanent in the record.
- The two lines on the graph.
  Nutrients declining since the late 1980s.
  Oxygen still falling.
  Both in the same dataset.
  The paradox. Named. Confirmed. Present.

## Holds
- [The Branch Point](piece_the_branch_point.md).
  At the waterline. Before every visit.
  The tourist does not know she is standing on it.

## Offers
- The full record. Sixty-seven years of monthly measurements.
  Available to anyone who asks for it.
  The paradox legible in the data
  to anyone who reads both lines.
  Most people read one line or neither.
- The RECOVER project. Running.
  October 2025 to September 2028.
  The instrument being built that could close
  the gap between the data and the decision room.
  Available as hope. Not yet as instrument.
- The harbour porpoise.
  Still present. Still countable.
  Still surfacing.
  Available to whoever is watching the water
  at the right moment.
  Less often than thirty years ago.

## Withheld
- Which branch runs.
  The Deciding is initiating from this place,
  from this moment, from the decision
  that is not yet made in the rooms
  where decisions are made.
  The Now holds both futures simultaneously.
  It cannot show which one is coming.
  It can only show what is here —
  the instruments, the record, the porpoise,
  the tourist at the waterline,
  the branch point she is standing on
  without knowing it.
- What the Scientist feels
  when she pulls up the time series graph
  and points at the trend line
  without speaking first.
  The place holds this.
  It does not offer it.
  It requires staying.
