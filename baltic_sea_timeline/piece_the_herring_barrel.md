# Piece: The Herring Barrel
## The instrument that made the sea into a supply chain.

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Timeline
**Persona:** The Merchant

---

## Place
[The Shore](place_the_shore.md).
The quay at Haithabu. Waiting for the ship.

## Load Bearing
Without it the herring feeds the shore.
With it the herring feeds the continent.
The barrel is the technology that introduces
distance between the catching and the eating —
and with distance comes the ledger,
and with the ledger comes the price,
and with the price comes the supply chain,
and with the supply chain comes
the first harvest at a scale
that the sea cannot see coming
because the sea has no concept of supply chains.
Remove the barrel and the Taking remains local.
Keep it and the orientation is set:
the sea faces the direction of the market
for the rest of this timeline.

## Apparent
Oak staves. Iron hoops.
The smell of salt and fish — permanent,
soaked into the wood.
Heavy when full: fifty kilograms of herring
packed in layers of salt.
Stackable. Shippable.
The label, when there is a label,
names the origin: western Baltic.
The barrel does not name
the specific cove, the specific bed,
the specific cod channel.
It names a region.
The abstraction begins here.

## Yearbook
Haithabu, ~1000 CE.
The herring run. Three weeks.
The barrels filled on the quay.
Shipped west to the Rhine, south to the Elbe,
east to Novgorod.
The herring of the western Baltic
feeding people who have never seen the Bight.

The Hanseatic League: the barrel becomes
the unit of the Baltic economy.
Lübeck, Hamburg, Rostock —
the whole network runs on what the barrel holds.

The herring fishery collapses
in the Sound in the fifteenth century —
the shoals shift, the Hanse loses its primary source.
The western Baltic takes up the slack.
The barrel keeps moving.

1957: the barrel is now a quota.
The EU issues total allowable catches.
The abstraction that began with the barrel
has reached its administrative conclusion:
the herring is a number in a table
in a Brussels office,
managed by people who have never
seen the Bight in autumn
when the surface darkens with the shoals.
The barrel is still there.
It is called a tonne now.
The distance is greater.
The ledger is more precise.
The stock concept now exists.
The stock is declining anyway.
