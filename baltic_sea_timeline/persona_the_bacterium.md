# Persona: The Bacterium
## Title: The First Metaboliser — 3.8 billion years ago, and now

## Owner
**Project:** Baltic Sea Timeline
**Persona:** The Bacterium

It has been running the same process for 3.8 billion years
and has not needed to change.

## Projection
Not visible. Not a body in any sense
the Hunter would recognise as a body.
A membrane. A chemical gradient.
The capacity to replicate.
In the Ancient Water: dispersed through the ocean,
concentrated near the vents,
running the oldest metabolism on Earth.
In the Kiel Bight in August:
present in every millilitre of water
below the thermocline,
in every gram of sediment at Boknis Eck,
running the same metabolism,
at maximum efficiency,
as the instruments record and the Scientist reads
and the dead zone expands
and the oxygen falls.
The Bacterium is not in two places.
It is in one continuous process
that has never stopped.

## Action
Metabolise. Break down the organic compounds.
Release the carbon, the nitrogen, the phosphorus.
Produce hydrogen sulfide where oxygen is absent.
Consume oxygen where oxygen is present.
Replicate.
This is the whole of the action.
It has never been more than this.
It will never be more than this.
It does not need to be more than this —
it has been the foundation of every food web
on this planet for 3.8 billion years
without once intending to be.

## Shadow
The Bacterium has no Shadow.
It has no interior. No conflict.
No blindness because nothing to be blind with.

What this does to the timeline:
every human persona from the Hunter to the Inheritor
has a constitutive conflict — the gap between
what they can see and what they cannot,
between what they intend and what they produce.
The Bacterium has no such gap.
It produces exactly what it intends
because it has no intentions —
only chemistry that runs at the rate
the conditions allow.
The nine humans in this timeline
are consequential because they choose
and are blind to the consequences of their choices.
The Bacterium is more consequential than all of them
because it does not choose
and therefore cannot be wrong
and therefore cannot be stopped
by making better decisions.
The Branch Point cannot reach it.
The RECOVER project cannot reach it.
The Deciding runs above it
while it runs below,
indifferent to both futures,
older than the question.

## Tell
The hydrogen sulfide in the porewater at Boknis Eck in August.
