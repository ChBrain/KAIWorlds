# Place: The Wings
## Where the agreement is held from outside.

## Owner
- **Project:** Commedia

## Shown
The space at the edge of [the Stage](place_stage.md).
[The Scenario](piece_scenario.md) kept here.
[The Keeper](position_keeper.md) working here.
Entrances to [the Stage](place_stage.md) on both sides.
Everything on [the Stage](place_stage.md) visible and audible from here.

## Holds
[The Scenario](piece_scenario.md).
The record [the Witness](position_witness.md) is building.
The absorbed costs [the Keeper](position_keeper.md) is carrying.
Everything the Stage produced
that could not be acknowledged on the Stage.
The Wings hold what the Stage cannot.

## Offers
The view. Unobstructed.
[The Misreader](position_misreader.md) can see every Tell from here.
[The Keeper](position_keeper.md) can read every deviation from [the Scenario](piece_scenario.md).
[The Witness](position_witness.md) can hold the full record.
The Wings offer clarity that [the Stage](place_stage.md) does not —
because the Wings are not inside the agreement
the way the Stage is.
They offer this clarity continuously.
The cost of the clarity is that it cannot be used directly.
To use it is to step onto [the Stage](place_stage.md)
or into [the Crack](place_crack.md).
The Wings offer only watching.

## Withheld
The action.
Everything seen from the Wings
cannot be acted on from the Wings.
The place withholds this completely.
A persona who tries to act from the Wings
discovers they have already moved —
either onto [the Stage](place_stage.md) or toward [the Crack](place_crack.md).
There is no acting from the Wings.
There is only watching, or moving.
The Wings withhold the third option.
Most personas spend years discovering it does not exist.
