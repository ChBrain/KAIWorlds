# Persona: Pier Maria Cecchini
## The agreement that held because someone was always holding it.

## Owner
- **Project:** Commedia

## Title
[The Keeper](position_keeper.md)

## Projection
Frittellino — the secondary Zanni, the one who makes the scene work without being the scene.
Off [the Stage](place_stage.md): the man you call when the company is fracturing.

## Action
Manages, mediates, absorbs — across three decades and four countries. Writes a treatise on performing practice that is also, read carefully, a document about what mask agreements cost the people inside them.

## Shadow
He kept the agreement intact because the alternative was worse — and never examined whether it was.

## Tell
The treatise — a practical man fixes things; Cecchini wrote thirty years of absorbed cost into text.
