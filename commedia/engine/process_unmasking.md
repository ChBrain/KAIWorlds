# Process: The Unmasking
## The decision or the collapse that ends the agreement.

## Owner
- **Project:** Commedia
- **Position:** [The Unmasker](position_unmasker.md)
- **Place:** [The Crack](place_crack.md)
- **Piece:** [The Face](piece_face.md)

## Initiated by
One of two conditions. Both produce the same process.

**The decision** — [the Unmasker](position_unmasker.md) chooses the moment.
The agreement is live. [The Stage](place_stage.md) is running.
The Unmasker has seen enough. Acts.

**The collapse** — [process_maintenance](process_maintenance.md) fails
under accumulated weight.
[The Crack](place_crack.md) surfaces without anyone choosing it.
[The Tell](piece_tell.md) breaks through at full force.
The agreement ends because it cannot continue.

## Direction
Toward [the Face](piece_face.md).
[The Mask](piece_mask.md) is removed — by choice or by force.
[The Stage](place_stage.md) cannot hold the moment.
The action moves to [the Crack](place_crack.md)
where the agreement does not survive.
[The Witness](position_witness.md) is the only position
that remains functional throughout.

## Lever
**Carrying force** — [the Face](piece_face.md) pressing against [the Mask](piece_mask.md) from underneath.
Everything [process_maintenance](process_maintenance.md) absorbed. Every Tell that was misread.
It has been building since [the Threshold](place_threshold.md).

**Resistance** — the cost of the agreement ending, distributed across every position that depended on [the Scenario](piece_scenario.md) continuing.
It does not stop the process.

## Echo
[The Mask](piece_mask.md) is off. [The Face](piece_face.md) visible.
[The Scenario](piece_scenario.md) no longer runs.
[The Witness](position_witness.md) holds the complete record.
A new [process_masking](process_masking.md) is now possible — the world resets, not to zero.
