# Position: The Unmasker
## Who decides the moment.

## Owner
- **Project:** Commedia
- **Process:** [process_unmasking](process_unmasking.md)
- **Place:** [The Crack](place_crack.md)

## Has
The moment.
Not the information — every position near [the Stage](place_stage.md)
accumulates information.
The Unmasker has the decision.
The willingness to end the agreement
at a chosen time for a chosen reason
rather than waiting for [process_maintenance](process_maintenance.md) to fail.
This is rarer than it appears.
Most Unmaskings are collapses.
The position that chooses is a different thing entirely.

## Orders
Name the moment and act in it — not before it has weight, not after the collapse has chosen for you.

## Loses
The clean hands.
They were certain that ending an agreement
was different from having been inside it —
that the Unmasker stood outside the mask agreements
by virtue of being the one who ended them.
The action takes it.
To unmask is to have known.
The Unmasker was in the room.
Saw the Tells. Waited.
Chose when to act and when not to.
The timing is a form of participation.

## Drives
On the loss of clean hands —
acts anyway.
The position does not require innocence.
It requires the decision.
The Unmasker moves into [the Crack](place_crack.md)
and names what is there
knowing the cost is shared
and paying it regardless.
