# Position: The Misreader
## Who performs not-seeing.

## Owner
- **Project:** Commedia
- **Process:** [process_misread](process_misread.md)
- **Place:** [The Wings](place_wings.md)

## Has
The view. Full and unobstructed.
[The Wings](place_wings.md) provide it.
The Misreader sees [the Tell](piece_tell.md) surface.
Sees [the Mask](piece_mask.md) press against [the Face](piece_face.md).
Sees the agreement straining.
This is not inference — it is direct observation.
The position has the information before the persona decides what to do with it.

## Orders
Perform not-seeing — completely, whether or not the seeing happened.

## Loses
The distance.
They were certain the Wings were outside the agreement —
that observing without acknowledging
meant remaining unentangled.
The performance takes it.
To perform not-seeing is to be inside the agreement.
The Misreader is not a neutral observer.
They are a load-bearing wall.

## Drives
On the loss of distance —
moves further into [the Wings](place_wings.md).
Increases the performance of not-seeing
as the cost of the performance increases.
The further out the position moves
the more load-bearing it becomes.
The Wings are not outside the Stage.
They are what holds it up.
