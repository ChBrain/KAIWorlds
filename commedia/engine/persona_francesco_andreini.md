# Persona: Francesco Andreini
## The performance that outlasted its reason.

## Owner
- **Project:** Commedia

## Title
[The Capitano](position_capitano.md) — [The Masked](position_masked.md)

## Projection
Il Capitano Spavento. Enormous courage, magnificent bearing — all of it hollow, visibly hollow, that is the comedy.
He published the character's speeches under his own name.

## Action
Holds the public face of the company while Isabella holds the structure. After her death: edits her manuscripts quietly for twenty years, without performing it.

## Shadow
He wore the most transparently hollow [Mask](piece_mask.md) in the form and understood exactly what mask agreements were — and built his entire identity on the character anyway.

## Tell
The retirement — the Capitano never stops performing, but Francesco stopped the day Isabella died.
