# Piece: The Tell
## The involuntary truth.

## Owner
- **Project:** Commedia

## Place
Where [the Masked](position_masked.md) is.
Always. It cannot be stored elsewhere.
It travels with the body that produces it.
[The Mask](piece_mask.md) does not cover it.
That is the point.

## Load Bearing
Without it the mask agreement is perfect and therefore false.
A mask with no Tell is a replacement, not a covering.
The Tell is what makes [the Face](piece_face.md) recoverable.
Remove it and [the Misread](process_misread.md) has nothing to perform against.
[The Witness](position_witness.md) has nothing to record.
[The Unmasking](process_unmasking.md) reveals only the mask's absence, not the Face.

## Apparent
Involuntary. Physical. Specific to each persona.
Never general — always the thing this specific body cannot stop doing
at the moment the [Mask](piece_mask.md) is under pressure.

## Yearbook
Documented by no one intentionally. Found by those who read carefully.
Martinelli's: precision where chaos was promised.
Isabella Andreini's: the writing that broke through the sincerity.
Francesco Andreini's: the retirement — the shape of the absence.
Scala's: the gaps — what was never written down.
Cecchini's: the treatise — care pressed through maintenance into text.
Riccoboni's: the reform argument — grief performing as programme notes.
Goldoni's: the departure — thirty years of silence, then he left.
Riccoboni looked for Tells in the old accounts and margin notes.
He did not call them that. The concept is older than any name for it.
