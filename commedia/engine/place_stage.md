# Place: The Stage
## Where the agreement runs.

## Owner
- **Project:** Commedia

## Shown
Open space. Boards underfoot.
Every mask visible to every other mask simultaneously.
[The Scenario](piece_scenario.md) is not here — it is in [the Wings](place_wings.md).
But its shape is present in how the space is organised.
Entrances on two sides.
The audience implied, always, even when absent.
[The Wings](place_wings.md) visible at the edges.
[The Crack](place_crack.md) not visible — but present.
It surfaces here when [process_maintenance](process_maintenance.md) fails.

## Holds
The active mask agreements.
Every [Mask](piece_mask.md) currently being worn.
Every [Tell](piece_tell.md) currently being produced.
The Stage holds all of this simultaneously
and does something specific with it:
it makes the mask feel like the face.
That is the Stage's particular pressure.
It is not neutral ground.

## Offers
Legibility. A persona on the Stage
is readable in a way they are not elsewhere.
The mask agreement is publicly visible and publicly maintained.
The Stage offers the full power of the agreed role —
access, authority, the weight of the character.
It offers this continuously, for as long as the mask holds.

## Withheld
The exit that costs nothing.
Every exit from the Stage
either goes to [the Wings](place_wings.md) — which is still inside the agreement —
or goes to [the Crack](place_crack.md) — which ends it.
The Stage does not offer a way out that preserves everything.
It withholds that.
The persona discovers this
only when they need it.
