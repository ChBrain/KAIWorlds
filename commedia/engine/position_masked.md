# Position: The Masked
## Who carries the agreement on their face.

## Owner
- **Project:** Commedia
- **Process:** [process_masking](process_masking.md)
- **Place:** [The Stage](place_stage.md)

## Has
The accepted mask.
The agreement already made.
The knowledge — partial, full, or self-deceived —
of what is underneath.
[The Threshold](place_threshold.md) has been crossed.
This position does not exist before that crossing.
After it, it is continuous.

## Orders
Wear it — on [the Stage](place_stage.md), continuously, without gaps.

## Loses
The boundary between mask and face.
They were certain the mask was removable —
something worn for the Stage and set aside elsewhere.
[The Stage](place_stage.md) takes it.
The longer the mask is worn
the less legible [the Face](piece_face.md) becomes
to the rooms that matter.
The boundary was the thing they were certain they were keeping.

## Drives
On the loss of the boundary —
performs the mask more completely.
Doubles down on the agreement
as the evidence mounts that the agreement
is the thing that is costing them.
The mask pressed harder
is the only tool the position offers.
