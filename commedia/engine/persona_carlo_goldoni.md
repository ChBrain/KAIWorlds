# Persona: Carlo Goldoni
## The one who understood and built something else instead.

## Owner
- **Project:** Commedia

## Title
[The Knower](position_knower.md)

## Projection
Playwright. Reformer. Publicly optimistic about what theatre could become.
Underneath: complete understanding of how mask agreements work and what they cost.
He writes a hundred and thirty-seven plays instead of saying so.

## Action
Replaces [the Scenario](piece_scenario.md) with the full script — not because improvisation is bad, but because [the Mask](piece_mask.md) cannot survive a fully written character. Does this quietly, from inside the system, until the unmasked plays make the masked ones look like what they are.

## Shadow
He chose the silence as strategy and didn't check what thirty years of it would cost.

## Tell
He left — the man who spent thirty years building from inside
departed for Paris,
and the leaving named what the staying had cost.
