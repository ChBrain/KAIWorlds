# Position: The Masker
## Who controls which masks exist.

## Owner
- **Project:** Commedia
- **Process:** [process_masking](process_masking.md)

## Has
The inventory of masks.
Which ones exist, which ones are available,
which ones open which doors.
The knowledge of what each agreement costs
and what it produces.
This exists before any persona arrives.
The Masker does not create the masks in the moment —
they arrive already knowing which ones are in the room.

## Orders
Decide which mask is offered and to whom.

## Loses
The neutrality of the offer.
They were certain the mask was a tool —
something passed between two parties
with no residue on the hand that held it.
The first offer takes it.
The Masker is inside the agreement
the moment they make it.
They chose the mask.
The choice is theirs.
That is not neutral.

## Drives
On the loss of neutrality —
performs the archivist.
The one who documents rather than decides.
The mask of the Masker is stewardship.
The agreement they are most inside
is the one they present as merely observed.
