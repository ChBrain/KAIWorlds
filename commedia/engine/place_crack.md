# Place: The Crack
## Where the agreement does not survive.

## Owner
- **Project:** Commedia

## Shown
A rupture in [the Stage](place_stage.md), not a built room.
[The Mask](piece_mask.md) off or coming off.
[The Face](piece_face.md) present.
[The Tell](piece_tell.md) no longer involuntary — it is everything.
[The Witness](position_witness.md) the only position fully functional here.

## Holds
[The Face](piece_face.md).
The accumulated Tells that [process_misread](process_misread.md) suspended
and [process_maintenance](process_maintenance.md) absorbed.
Everything the agreement covered
is present in the Crack simultaneously.
The Crack holds what the whole world
spent its energy keeping in separate places.

## Offers
The truth of the record.
The Crack is the only place in the world
where [the Face](piece_face.md) and [the Mask](piece_mask.md)
are present at the same moment
and the difference between them is visible.
It offers this to everyone in the room
whether they want it or not.
It also offers the beginning of the next agreement.
[The Threshold](place_threshold.md) opens again
on the far side of the Crack.
The world does not end here.
It resets.

## Withheld
The cost accounting.
Who paid what, and whether it was fair,
and whether the agreement should have run as long as it did —
the Crack does not produce this.
It produces the fact of the ending.
The accounting happens afterward,
in the conversations nobody writes down,
in [the Witness](position_witness.md)'s record
that may or may not be spoken aloud.
The Crack withholds the verdict.
The world must produce that elsewhere
or not at all.
