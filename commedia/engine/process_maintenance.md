# Process: The Maintenance
## The continuous work the agreement requires to exist.

## Owner
- **Project:** Commedia
- **Position:** [The Keeper](position_keeper.md)
- **Place:** [The Wings](place_wings.md)
- **Piece:** [The Scenario](piece_scenario.md)

## Initiated by
[The Masking](process_masking.md) has completed.
[The Masked](position_masked.md) is on [the Stage](place_stage.md).
The agreement is live.
[The Keeper](position_keeper.md) is present.
This process begins at the same moment as [process_masking](process_masking.md)
and does not end until [process_unmasking](process_unmasking.md) closes it.

## Direction
Toward the continuous integrity of [the Stage](place_stage.md).
[The Keeper](position_keeper.md) reads the room from [the Wings](place_wings.md),
absorbs the moments that would crack the agreement,
and holds [the Scenario](piece_scenario.md) present as the reference everyone improvises against.

## Lever
**Carrying force** — [the Scenario](piece_scenario.md) pressing against the continuous improvisation of everyone on [the Stage](place_stage.md).
The Keeper does not perform this force. The Keeper is this force.

**Resistance** — the accumulated cost of every absorbed moment, every Tell suspended, every crack held closed rather than examined.

## Echo
[The Stage](place_stage.md) holds.
The agreement continues.
[The Keeper](position_keeper.md) carries more than before.
[The Witness](position_witness.md) records that the holding happened.
When [process_maintenance](process_maintenance.md) fails —
not gradually but suddenly, under accumulated weight —
[process_unmasking](process_unmasking.md) is no longer a choice.
It is what happens next.
