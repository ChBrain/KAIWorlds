# Position: The Zanni
## The servant who sees everything and performs seeing nothing.

## Owner
- **Project:** Commedia
- **Place:** [The Wings](place_wings.md)

## Has
The view from below and the structural permission to be underestimated.
The position brings velocity — physical, verbal, cognitive —
and the gap between what the room expects and what the Zanni can do.
That gap is the mechanism.
The Zanni's intelligence is load-bearing precisely because no one has agreed to take it seriously.

## Orders
Be faster than the room expects.
Not faster than you are —
faster than the room has decided you are allowed to be.
The gap between those two speeds
is where the position operates.

## Loses
The exit.
They were certain the fool's [Mask](piece_mask.md) was protective —
that performing below the room's attention
meant remaining outside its consequences.
The first time the intelligence breaks through completely
takes it.
The Zanni discovers that the [Mask](piece_mask.md) that kept them safe
is also the [Mask](piece_mask.md) that keeps them unheard
at the moment they most need to be heard.
There is no removing it cleanly.
The position does not offer that.

## Drives
On the loss of the exit —
moves faster.
The only tool the position offers at the point of exposure
is more of what caused the exposure.
The Zanni doubles the performance
when the performance is failing.
This sometimes works.
It is always the wrong solution.
It is also the only one available from inside this position.
