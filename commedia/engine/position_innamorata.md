# Position: The Innamorata
## The one who performs without a mask — which is the most powerful mask of all.

## Owner
- **Project:** Commedia
- **Place:** [The Stage](place_stage.md)

## Has
The unmasked [Face](piece_face.md) — by convention.
The position brings the audience's belief that what they are seeing is real,
in a world where everything else is visibly constructed.
That belief is the most powerful thing on [the Stage](place_stage.md)
and is, structurally, a mask agreement that no one has named as one.

## Orders
Feel it. Visibly. Completely.
Not the performance of feeling —
the position requires that the distinction
become undetectable.
The Innamorata performed at half-commitment
is not the Innamorata.
The position only exists at full extension.

## Loses
The distinction.
They were certain that performing sincerity completely
was different from being sincere —
that the [Face](piece_face.md) shown on [the Stage](place_stage.md)
was a choice, not a disclosure.
The position takes it.
The audience cannot tell the difference
and neither, eventually, can the persona.
The [Face](piece_face.md) shown so completely and so continuously
becomes the only Face the room knows.
The persona discovers this
when they need to be seen as something other than feeling
and the room cannot reconfigure.

## Drives
On the loss of the distinction —
performs more completely.
Goes further into the feeling.
The position offers depth as the only direction.
The Innamorata under pressure
becomes more of what the [Mask](piece_mask.md) requires
because the position has no lateral movement.
You cannot be half-sincere.
You can only be more.
