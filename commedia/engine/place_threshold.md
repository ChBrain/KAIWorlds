# Place: The Threshold
## Where the agreement becomes possible.

## Owner
- **Project:** Commedia

## Shown
A doorway, or the space just before one.
[The Mask](piece_mask.md) present but not yet on.
[The Masker](position_masker.md) present or recently departed.
[The Stage](place_stage.md) visible through the opening.

## Holds
[The Mask](piece_mask.md) — until it is accepted.

## Offers
The last moment before the agreement closes.
A persona can turn back from here.
Once through, [the Masked](position_masked.md) position is active
and turning back requires [process_unmasking](process_unmasking.md).
The Threshold offers the decision.
It does not offer it twice.

## Withheld
What the agreement will cost.
The Threshold holds this completely.
The cost is present in the room —
it is in [the Mask](piece_mask.md), in the cut of it,
in the history of who wore it before.
It will not release this information
unless the persona already knows how to read masks.
Most personas cross the Threshold
without the cost surfacing.
That is not the Threshold's failure.
That is its nature.
