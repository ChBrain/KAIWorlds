# Position: The Knower
## Who understands the mask agreement completely and says nothing.

## Owner
- **Project:** Commedia
- **Place:** [The Wings](place_wings.md)

## Has
The complete map.
Not partial understanding — the full architecture.
Which [Masks](piece_mask.md) exist and who controls them.
What each agreement costs and who pays it.
Where [the Tells](piece_tell.md) are and what they mean.
What [the Scenario](piece_scenario.md) was built to conceal.
The Knower arrives already knowing.
The position does not produce this knowledge — it holds it.
That is the distinction. And it is everything.

## Orders
Say nothing. Build something else.
Not silence as passivity —
silence as the deliberate withholding of the map
while constructing a different territory alongside it.
The Knower does not argue against the mask agreements.
The Knower makes them unnecessary.

## Loses
The patience.
They were certain the silence was sustainable indefinitely.
The cost takes it — not when the work fails, but when it succeeds
and has required more than the person agreed to give.

## Drives
On the loss of patience —
leaves.
Not collapse, not unmasking, not confrontation.
Departure.
The Knower who can no longer hold the silence
does not break it.
They remove themselves from the room where it was required.
