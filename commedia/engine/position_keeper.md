# Position: The Keeper
## Who holds the agreement intact.

## Owner
- **Project:** Commedia
- **Process:** [process_maintenance](process_maintenance.md)
- **Place:** [The Wings](place_wings.md)

## Has
The Scenario.
Not as a document — as a living standard.
The shape of what the agreement is supposed to be
pressing against what it actually is at any given moment.
The Keeper has the gap.
That is the position's specific and continuous burden:
knowing the distance between
what [the Stage](place_stage.md) is producing
and what [the Scenario](piece_scenario.md) requires.

## Orders
Hold the agreement intact — continuously, invisibly, between the crises not just during them.

## Loses
The belief that maintenance is neutral.
They were certain that holding the agreement intact
was a service performed for everyone equally —
that the Keeper had no stake, only a function.
The first absorbed crack takes it.
To hold an agreement is to endorse it.
The Keeper discovers this
at the moment they absorb something
they know should not be absorbed.
They absorb it anyway.
That is when the position becomes a choice.

## Drives
On the loss of neutrality —
holds harder.
The alternative is the agreement ending
and the Keeper has already decided
that the alternative is worse.
Whether it actually is
is a question the position does not ask.
