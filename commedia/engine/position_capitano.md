# Position: The Capitano
## The performance of knowing — which is the most dangerous confusion in the world.

## Owner
- **Project:** Commedia
- **Place:** [The Stage](place_stage.md)

## Has
The loudest [Mask](piece_mask.md) on [the Stage](place_stage.md).
The position brings volume, scale, and total conviction —
not intelligence, but the performance of intelligence so complete
that rooms reorganise around it before anyone has checked the content.
That reorganisation is real. The authority it produces is real.
The gap between performance and intelligence underneath is the entire content of the role.

## Orders
Perform certainty.
About everything. At full volume.
The position does not permit hesitation —
not because hesitation is weakness
but because the [Mask](piece_mask.md) has no mechanism for it.
Uncertainty in the Capitano
is not uncertainty. It is malfunction.

## Loses
The performance's boundary.
They were certain the Capitano [Mask](piece_mask.md) was worn —
that the conviction was a tool
and the actual assessment of the room
was happening underneath it, privately, correctly.
The first real test takes it.
Real information arrives —
a threat, a fact, a person who does not reorganise —
and the performance stutters.
In the stutter the persona discovers
that the boundary between performed certainty
and actual certainty
dissolved some time ago
without announcement.

## Drives
On the loss of the boundary —
performs louder.
Volume is the only instrument the position offers
when the performance is failing.
The [Mask](piece_mask.md) that cannot read its own failure
doubles down on the failure with complete and genuine conviction.
