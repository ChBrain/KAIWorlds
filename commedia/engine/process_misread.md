# Process: The Misread
## The deliberate or innocent failure to see.

## Owner
- **Project:** Commedia
- **Position:** [The Misreader](position_misreader.md)
- **Place:** [The Wings](place_wings.md)
- **Piece:** [The Tell](piece_tell.md)

## Initiated by
[The Mask](piece_mask.md) is in use on [the Stage](place_stage.md).
[The Tell](piece_tell.md) has surfaced — involuntarily, visibly.
[The Misreader](position_misreader.md) is present and has seen it.
The agreement requires a response.
Two paths exist: acknowledge or perform not-seeing.
This process begins when the second path is taken.

## Direction
Toward the preserved agreement.
[The Misreader](position_misreader.md) moves to [the Wings](place_wings.md)
or performs distance while remaining on [the Stage](place_stage.md).
The Tell is absorbed without acknowledgment.
[The Scenario](piece_scenario.md) continues unbroken.

## Lever
**Carrying force** — the agreement pressing against
the moment that would crack it.
The Misreader holds the crack closed
by performing not-seeing.
The performance may be deliberate or innocent.
The mechanism does not distinguish.
The result is identical.

**Resistance** — [the Tell](piece_tell.md) itself.
It has already happened.
The Misread does not erase it.
It suspends it.
The Tell remains in the room.
[The Witness](position_witness.md) holds it
whether or not the Misread succeeded.

## Echo
The agreement holds — for now.
[The Stage](place_stage.md) continues.
[The Tell](piece_tell.md) is suspended, not resolved.
[The Witness](position_witness.md) has added to the record.
[process_maintenance](process_maintenance.md) absorbs the cost.
The pressure on [the Mask](piece_mask.md) increases.
[process_unmasking](process_unmasking.md) moves closer.
