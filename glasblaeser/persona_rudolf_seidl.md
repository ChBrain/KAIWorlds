# Persona: Rudolf Seidl
## Title: [der Glashändler](position_der_glasshaendler.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** Glasbläser im Bayerischen Wald

Rudolf Seidl.
He runs a Werksverkauf
on [die Glasstraße](place_die_glasstrasse.md).
The shop is where the glass is made.
That used to mean something different
than it means now.
2024.

## Projection
A man in his fifties
behind the counter
of a Werksverkauf in Zwiesel.
Handblown pieces on the left side
of the display.
A second range on the right -
also glass, also functional,
not made here.
He stocks both.
The tourist moves between them.

## Action
Watches which range
the tourist moves toward first.
Does not intervene.

## Shadow
The Glasstraße was built
on the idea that the place
where the glass is made
is part of what is being sold.
Rudolf believes this.
He has stocked the second range
for eleven years
because the margin requires it.
He tells himself
the two ranges serve different customers.
He has not resolved
what it means that he stocks both.

## Tell
When a tourist asks
which range he recommends,
Rudolf names the handblown piece.
He does this every time.
He does not mention
that he also sells the other one.
