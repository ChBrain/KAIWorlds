# Process: The Gathering
## The calculation that determines whether the season runs.

## Owner
- **Project:** Glasbläser im Bayerischen Wald
- **Position:** [der Waldmeister](position_der_waldmeister.md)
- **Place:** [der Wald](place_der_wald.md)
- **Place:** [die Glashütte](place_die_glashuette.md)

## Initiated by
Winter. The furnaces are cold.
[Die Glashütte](place_die_glashuette.md) is between seasons -
ovens under repair, last season's stock moved.
[Der Waldmeister](position_der_waldmeister.md) is present.
The question is not whether to gather.
The question is whether enough remains within reach
to run the season at all.

## Direction
Toward the furnace loaded before Easter.
Wood cut and stacked. Quartz broken from the ground.
Potash rendered from beech ash in the Aschehaus.
All three present simultaneously before the fires are lit.

## Lever
Carrying force: the season opens at Easter
and runs without pause until November.
The furnace does not wait.
Resistance: [der Wald](place_der_wald.md) depletes in one direction only.
What was taken last season is not there this season.
[Der Waldmeister](position_der_waldmeister.md) calculates the distance
to viable timber. At some point the distance
exceeds what this process can sustain.
That is not this process. That is [The Move](process_the_move.md).
This process runs until the calculation fails.

## Echo
The furnace is loaded.
The season can begin.
[Der Wald](place_der_wald.md) is diminished.
[The Melt](process_the_melt.md) can begin.
