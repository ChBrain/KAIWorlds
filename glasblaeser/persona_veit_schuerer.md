# Persona: Veit Schürer
## Title: [der Einträger](position_der_eintraeger.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** Glasbläser im Bayerischen Wald

Veit Schürer. Einträger on the Schönauer Hütte.
Fourteen years old.
The sources say children carried the glass to the Kühlofen.
He is one of them.
1638.

## Projection
A boy, thin, moving fast
across the floor of [die Glashütte](place_die_glashuette.md).
He carries the finished piece
from the Glasmeister's bench
to the Kühlofen
at something between a walk and a run.
The piece is hot.
He holds it with the carrying iron,
not his hands.
His face is turned away from the heat.

## Action
Takes the piece from the bench.
Carries it to the Kühlofen.
Places it.
Returns.

## Shadow
Veit has carried hundreds of pieces
to the Kühlofen.
He has never seen one come out broken
as a result of his carry.
He is certain this means
he carries correctly.
He has never seen
a piece come out of the Kühlofen.
By the time it opens,
he is already carrying the next one.

## Tell
He slows slightly
in the last three steps
before the Kühlofen.
He does this every time.
