# Persona: Georg Hilz
## Title: [der Waldmeister](position_der_waldmeister.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** Glasbläser im Bayerischen Wald

Georg Hilz. Waldmeister on the Schönauer Hütte.
Winter, 1637.
He married the widowed Hüttenherrin Anna Maria Wirdinger
and with her the calculation he cannot yet solve.

## Projection
A broad man who moves through
[der Wald](place_der_wald.md) the way a man moves
through something he has read three times.
He counts as he walks -
not cattle, not coins.
Trees.
Specifically: the distance to uncut beech
that will make the Pottasche the season requires.
He carries nothing visible.

## Action
Walks the perimeter of what remains
before anyone has asked him to.
Names the number to himself
before he names it to anyone else.

## Shadow
He married into the Hütte
and with it the knowledge
that the previous Waldmeister -
the husband who died -
had already used the most accessible stands.
What Georg has is what was left.
He tells himself this is enough.
He has not yet told himself
when it stops being enough.
He is still certain
the marriage was the right decision.

## Tell
He stops walking.
Looks back at the way he came.
Measures the distance with his eyes.
Turns and continues.
He does not write anything down.
