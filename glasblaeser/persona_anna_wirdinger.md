# Persona: Anna Wirdinger
## Title: [der Einbläser](position_der_einblaeser.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** Glasbläser im Bayerischen Wald

Anna Wirdinger. Einbläserin on the Schönauer Hütte.
Daughter of the previous Glasmeister.
She learned the pipe before she learned to read.
1638.

## Projection
A young woman, early twenties,
at the furnace in [die Glashütte](place_die_glashuette.md).
The Pfeife in both hands, turning.
She blows without visible effort -
the effort is in the turning,
in the angle,
in the precise moment of the breath.
She passes the Pfeife to the Glasmeister
without looking at him.

## Action
Takes the gather from the furnace.
Blows the Vorform.
Passes the Pfeife at the exact moment
the piece becomes something -
not before, not after.

## Shadow
Anna knows the Vorform
better than the Glasmeister knows it.
She has been making it since childhood.
She knows when it is right
not from the color or the shape
but from the weight on the pipe,
the resistance of the breath.
She knows when it is wrong
before the Glasmeister
who will finish it can see it.
She passes the pipe anyway.
She has never named what she knows
to anyone who could act on it.

## Tell
When the Vorform is wrong,
she holds the pipe
a half-second longer
before passing it.
The Glasmeister does not notice.
She does.
