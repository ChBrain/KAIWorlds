# Process: The Finishing
## The glass receives what the blow cannot give it.

## Owner
- **Project:** Glasbläser im Bayerischen Wald
- **Position:** [der Veredler](position_der_veredler.md)
- **Place:** [die Werkstatt](place_die_werkstatt.md)
- **Piece:** [das Glas](piece_das_glas.md)
- **Piece:** [das Veredelte Glas](piece_das_veredelte_glas.md)

## Initiated by
[Das Glas](piece_das_glas.md) has cooled.
It holds.
[Der Veredler](position_der_veredler.md) receives it -
grinder, engraver, painter, or cutter,
depending on what the piece requires
and what was commissioned.
[Die Werkstatt](place_die_werkstatt.md) is a separate place
from [die Glashütte](place_die_glashuette.md).
It always was.

## Direction
Toward [das Veredelte Glas](piece_das_veredelte_glas.md).
The surface opened, cut, engraved, or painted -
the piece made into something
that could not have come directly from the blow.
[Der Veredler](position_der_veredler.md) works cold, or at the wheel,
or with the brush.
None of these require the furnace.
None of them require the Glasmeister.
The two crafts share a material and nothing else.

## Lever
Carrying force: the commission -
what the buyer asked for that the blown form alone
cannot deliver.
Resistance: [das Glas](piece_das_glas.md) itself.
[Der Veredler](position_der_veredler.md) works against a surface
that will not forgive a wrong cut.
The wheel removes material.
It does not return it.

## Echo
[Das Veredelte Glas](piece_das_veredelte_glas.md) exists.
The blow is inside it, invisible.
The finishing is on its surface, permanent.
[The Packing](process_the_packing.md) can begin.
