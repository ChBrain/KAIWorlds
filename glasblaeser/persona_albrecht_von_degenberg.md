# Persona: Albrecht von Degenberg
## Title: [der Grundherr](position_der_grundherr.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** Glasbläser im Bayerischen Wald

Albrecht von Degenberg.
The Degenberg family held the Herrschaft Zwiesel
and the forest rights across the Zwieseler Winkel
from at least 1434.
Their Salbuch of 1434/37
records the first Glashütte in Unterzwieselau.
Albrecht is reading a Konzession request.
He has not been to the Glashütte.
1540.

## Projection
A man at a writing desk
in the Herrschaft seat.
The Konzession request is in front of him -
the Glasmeister's petition for continued rights
at the current Standort.
Albrecht has a ledger open beside it.
The ledger records the Steuern
from the last three seasons.
The numbers are good.
He reads the Holzverbrauch section
of the Konzession request
and then reads the ledger.
He does not read the forest.

## Action
Calculates the tax yield
against the stated Holzverbrauch.
Signs the renewal.

## Shadow
Albrecht is certain
the Konzession terms protect the forest -
six years, limited simultaneous Hütten,
accountability for Waldschäden.
The Glasmeister signed the same terms last time.
The Hütte needed to move
eighteen months before that Konzession expired.
Albrecht approved the move
and issued the new Konzession for the next site.
He has done this three times.
He has not mapped the pattern.
The ledger shows three seasons of good Steuern.
The forest shows something else.
Albrecht reads the ledger.

## Tell
He looks at the ledger
a second time before signing.
The numbers are still good.
He signs.
