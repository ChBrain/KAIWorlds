# Persona: Maria Wirdinger
## Title: [die Fasserin](position_die_fasserin.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** Glasbläser im Bayerischen Wald

Maria Wirdinger. Fasserin on the Schönauer Hütte.
Wife of the Einbläser.
The sources say women and daughters of the glassmakers
packed the finished pieces.
They are never named in the sources.
This is her name.
1638.

## Projection
A woman sitting beside a mound of damp straw
in [die Glashütte](place_die_glashuette.md),
loading [die Kraxe](piece_die_kraxe.md).
[Das Glas](piece_das_glas.md) arrives from the Kühlofen -
cooled, checked, ready.
She takes each piece.
Turns it once in her hands.
Begins the packing.

## Action
Turns the piece once
before packing it.
Feeling for the rim,
the stem, the base -
where it will fail
if the straw is wrong.

## Shadow
Maria knows every shape
the Hütte makes.
She has packed them all
more times than she has counted.
She is certain her knowledge
is complete.
Last winter a stem glass
broke on the road to Zwiesel.
The Glasträger brought back the pieces.
The break was at the stem,
exactly where the straw
was thinner than she thought.
She has repacked the stem glasses since.
She has not told anyone
that the break was hers.
She is the only one who knows.

## Tell
She turns each piece twice now.
She turned them once before last winter.
Nobody has noticed the change.
