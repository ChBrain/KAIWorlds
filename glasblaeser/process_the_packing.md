# Process: The Packing
## The glass is fragile. The road is not.

## Owner
- **Project:** Glasbläser im Bayerischen Wald
- **Position:** [die Fasserin](position_die_fasserin.md)
- **Place:** [die Glashütte](place_die_glashuette.md)
- **Piece:** [das Glas](piece_das_glas.md)
- **Piece:** [das Veredelte Glas](piece_das_veredelte_glas.md)
- **Piece:** [die Kraxe](piece_die_kraxe.md)

## Initiated by
[Das Glas](piece_das_glas.md) or [das Veredelte Glas](piece_das_veredelte_glas.md)
has passed [der Veredler](position_der_veredler.md) or come directly from the Kühlofen.
It is ready to leave [die Glashütte](place_die_glashuette.md).
[Die Fasserin](position_die_fasserin.md) is present.
[Die Kraxe](piece_die_kraxe.md) is waiting.

## Direction
Toward the piece protected for the distance it must travel.
Damp straw. Wooden frames. [Die Kraxe](piece_die_kraxe.md).
The packing is built around the specific piece -
its weight, its shape, its fragility at the rim or the stem.
No two pieces pack identically.

## Lever
Carrying force: the distance between
[die Glashütte](place_die_glashuette.md) in the forest
and the markets, the cities, the buyers
who have never seen the forest.
Resistance: the piece itself -
glass that survived the Kühlofen intact
will not survive a poorly packed cart
on a mountain road.
A broken load is not a loss of objects.
It is a loss of a season's wood, a season's fire,
a season's breath.

## Echo
The piece is packed.
[Die Kraxe](piece_die_kraxe.md) is loaded.
[The Trade](process_the_trade.md) can begin.
