# Process: The Move
## When the forest is gone, the Hütte moves.

## Owner
- **Project:** Glasbläser im Bayerischen Wald
- **Position:** [der Waldmeister](position_der_waldmeister.md)
- **Place:** [der Wald](place_der_wald.md)
- **Place:** [die Glashütte](place_die_glashuette.md)

## Initiated by
[Der Wald](place_der_wald.md) cannot sustain [The Gathering](process_the_gathering.md).
The wood within reach of [die Glashütte](place_die_glashuette.md) is exhausted.
The distance to viable timber
has exceeded what [The Gathering](process_the_gathering.md) can sustain.
[Der Waldmeister](position_der_waldmeister.md) makes the call.

## Direction
Toward a new site.
Deeper into [der Wald](place_der_wald.md).
Where the trees are untouched
and the quartz is in the ground
and the process can begin again.
The move happens approximately every ten to twenty years.
No record marks the previous site as used.
The forest grows back over it.

## Lever
Carrying force: the craft -
the knowledge carried in the hands of [der Glasmeister](position_der_glasmeister.md)
moves with the Hütte.
It does not require the place. Only the materials.
Resistance: everything built -
the furnace, the Kühlofen, the settlement
that grew around the Hütte - stays behind
or is dismantled and carried.
The community moves because the craft requires it.
The craft does not move for the community.

## Echo
[Die Glashütte](place_die_glashuette.md) is in a new place.
[Der Wald](place_der_wald.md) at the old site begins to recover.
[The Gathering](process_the_gathering.md) can begin again.
The glass made at the previous site
is now somewhere else in the world.
It does not know it moved.
