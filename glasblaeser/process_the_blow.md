# Process: The Blow
## Three hands. One piece.

## Owner
- **Project:** Glasbläser im Bayerischen Wald
- **Position:** [der Einbläser](position_der_einblaeser.md)
- **Position:** [der Glasmeister](position_der_glasmeister.md)
- **Position:** [der Einträger](position_der_eintraeger.md)
- **Place:** [die Glashütte](place_die_glashuette.md)

## Initiated by
The melt runs clear.
[Der Einbläser](position_der_einblaeser.md) is at the furnace with the Pfeife.
[Der Glasmeister](position_der_glasmeister.md) is at the bench.
[Der Einträger](position_der_eintraeger.md) is present.
The Kühlofen has space.

## Direction
Toward the finished form in the Kühlofen.
Three steps, three hands, no pause between them.
[Der Einbläser](position_der_einblaeser.md) gathers the Batzen from the furnace
on the end of the Pfeife and blows the Vorform -
the pre-form, the rough shape, the beginning.
He passes the Pfeife to [der Glasmeister](position_der_glasmeister.md).
[Der Glasmeister](position_der_glasmeister.md) finishes it -
the final shaping, the decision of what the piece becomes.
The last step always belongs to [der Glasmeister](position_der_glasmeister.md).
[Der Einträger](position_der_eintraeger.md) carries the finished piece
to the Kühlofen before it cools unevenly.

## Lever
Carrying force: the gather cooling from the moment it leaves the furnace.
The Pfeife must be kept turning or the glass sags.
The handover from [der Einbläser](position_der_einblaeser.md)
to [der Glasmeister](position_der_glasmeister.md) must be immediate.
Resistance: the form cannot be corrected after the handover.
What [der Einbläser](position_der_einblaeser.md) gives
is what [der Glasmeister](position_der_glasmeister.md) finishes.
If the Vorform is wrong the finished piece is wrong.
[Der Glasmeister](position_der_glasmeister.md) will not say this afterward.
The piece will.

## Echo
The finished piece is in the Kühlofen.
[Der Einbläser](position_der_einblaeser.md) is back at the furnace.
[Der Glasmeister](position_der_glasmeister.md) is at the bench.
[Der Einträger](position_der_eintraeger.md) has returned.
The chain runs again.
[The Cooling](process_the_cooling.md) has begun.
