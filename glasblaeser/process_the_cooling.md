# Process: The Cooling
## The form holds, or it does not.

## Owner
- **Project:** Glasbläser im Bayerischen Wald
- **Position:** [der Einträger](position_der_eintraeger.md)
- **Place:** [die Glashütte](place_die_glashuette.md)
- **Piece:** [das Glas](piece_das_glas.md)

## Initiated by
The form exists.
[The Blow](process_the_blow.md) is complete.
[Das Glas](piece_das_glas.md) is placed in the Kühlofen -
where the temperature descends slowly, by controlled degrees.

## Direction
Toward [das Glas](piece_das_glas.md) stable at room temperature.
No cracks. No internal stress visible.
The form the breath made, held.

## Lever
Carrying force: controlled descent -
temperature reduced slowly enough
that the glass does not fracture under its own internal pressure.
Resistance: [das Glas](piece_das_glas.md) itself.
Cooled too fast: it shatters.
Cooled unevenly: the stress is invisible in the piece
and emerges later - in transit, in use,
in the first winter of a window
in a house the Hütte will never see.

## Echo
[Das Glas](piece_das_glas.md) exists and holds.
Or it does not - and the process has produced a failure
that only the next process will discover.
If it holds: [das Glas](piece_das_glas.md) enters the world.
