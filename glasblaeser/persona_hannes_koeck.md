# Persona: Hannes Köck
## Title: [der Glasträger](position_der_glastraeger.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** Glasbläser im Bayerischen Wald

Hannes Köck. Glasträger.
He carries [die Kraxe](piece_die_kraxe.md)
on the Handelswege from the Hütte
to Zwiesel and beyond.
He knows the price from last season.
He does not know the price he will get.
1680.

## Projection
A lean man on a forest track,
bending forward slightly
under the weight of [die Kraxe](piece_die_kraxe.md).
Moving at the pace of someone
who has learned that pace
by arriving with broken glass
and then arriving without it.
He does not stop to rest on hills -
stopping on a slope shifts the load.

## Action
Walks.
Counts the hours, not the distance.
At each inn: listens before he speaks.

## Shadow
Hannes has been walking this route
for eleven years.
He is certain he knows
what the glass will fetch -
that his knowledge of the route,
the merchants, the seasonal demand
gives him an advantage
at the point of sale.
The Bohemian traders
who arrived at the Zwiesel market
two weeks ago
came with carts, not Kraxen,
and prices Hannes cannot match on a good day.
He heard about this at the last inn.
He is still certain
his knowledge is the advantage.

## Tell
At the last inn before Zwiesel,
he asks what the glass is fetching
this week.
He has already decided
what he will say
if the answer is lower
than what he needs.
