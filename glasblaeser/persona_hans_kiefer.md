# Persona: Hans Kiefer
## Title: [der Schürer](position_der_schuerer.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** Glasbläser im Bayerischen Wald

Hans Kiefer. Tagschürer on the Schönauer Hütte.
He works the day shift.
The Nachtschürer takes over when he sleeps.
Except when the melt clears at night.
Then nobody is sleeping.
1638.

## Projection
A man in front of [die Glashütte](place_die_glashuette.md)'s furnace.
Blackened around the eyes from the heat.
He holds a long iron poker
in his right hand.
His left arm is raised
to shield his face
from the radiant heat
while he looks at the color of the fire.
Orange is not ready.
Yellow-white is close.
He knows this without thinking about it.

## Action
Reads the color of the fire.
Adjusts the wood feed.
Holds the temperature
at the line between too hot and not enough.

## Shadow
Hans has read this fire
every day for seven years.
He is certain he reads it accurately -
that his certainty comes from the years,
not from the fire itself.
The fire changes every session.
The wood this week
is not the wood of last week.
Wet wood burns differently.
Green wood burns differently.
What Hans reads is the fire he has learned.
The fire in front of him
is also the fire it actually is.
He cannot always tell the difference.
He does not know he cannot always tell the difference.

## Tell
When the melt is close,
he moves one step back
from the furnace
and looks at the fire
from slightly further away.
He was taught this gives a cleaner read.
It gives a different read.
He has not examined whether different
is the same as cleaner.
