# Process: The Melt
## The material enters the furnace. It does not come back as what it was.

## Owner
- **Project:** Glasbläser im Bayerischen Wald
- **Position:** [der Glasmeister](position_der_glasmeister.md)
- **Place:** [die Glashütte](place_die_glashuette.md)

## Initiated by
The furnace is loaded.
[Der Glasmeister](position_der_glasmeister.md) is present.
The crucible - the Hafen - is seated.
The temperature is rising.

## Direction
Toward the melt running clear.
The three materials - quartz, wood ash, potash -
dissolving into each other under heat.
The moment the melt runs clear is the only moment
that matters in this process.
Everything before it is preparation. Everything after it is different.

## Lever
Carrying force: sustained heat - the furnace running at temperature
for as long as the material requires.
Resistance: the color of the melt.
[Der Glasmeister](position_der_glasmeister.md) reads it.
The melt does not announce when it is ready.
It must be read correctly or the glass that results
will carry the error invisibly, for years, until it breaks.

## Echo
The melt runs clear.
What was three separate materials is now one.
It cannot be separated again.
[The Blow](process_the_blow.md) can begin.
