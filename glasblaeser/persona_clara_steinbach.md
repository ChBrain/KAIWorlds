# Persona: Clara Steinbach
## Title: [der Lehrmeister](position_der_lehrmeister.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** Glasbläser im Bayerischen Wald

Clara Steinbach. Twenty-six.
Graduate of [die Glasfachschule](place_die_glasfachschule.md).
One of fourteen Ausbildungsrichtungen.
She chose Glasveredlung - engraving and cutting.
She is now at a bench
in the Gründerwerkstatt in Zwiesel -
a former Glashütte,
ten subsidised studio places,
low rent, ministry funding, donors.
She teaches two evenings a week.
The rest of the time
she works on commissions
that come in slowly
and repairs that come in faster.
2024.

## Projection
At a bench in the Gründerwerkstatt.
Showing a student
how to hold the engraving tool.
The angle of the wrist.
The pressure - how much,
and where it stops.
[Das Zeugnis](piece_das_zeugnis.md) is on the wall.
She knows it is there.
She does not look at it
while she is teaching.

## Action
Corrects the angle
of the student's hand.
Does not explain why.
Shows it again.

## Shadow
Clara stayed in Zwiesel
because the Gründerwerkstatt existed -
the subsidised bench,
the low rent,
the structure that made staying possible.
She is certain she stayed because she chose to.
She has not examined
how much of the choice
was the structure
and how much was her.
She teaches the craft completely.
She does not teach
the part she knows about
[das Zeugnis](piece_das_zeugnis.md) -
what it opens and what it does not.
She tells herself
her students will find this out
in their own time.
She found it out in her own time.

## Tell
When a student asks
what they will do after the Zeugnis,
Clara names three paths.
All three are real.
She names them in the order
of likelihood she does not believe
but cannot bring herself to reverse.
Some days more convincingly than others.
