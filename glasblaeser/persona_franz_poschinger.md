# Persona: Franz Poschinger
## Title: [der Glasmeister](position_der_glasmeister.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** Glasbläser im Bayerischen Wald

Franz Poschinger. Hüttenherr auf Frauenau.
He took over in 1662.
Seven years later, in 1669, the Hütte burned.
He rebuilt it.
His records of the operation survive.

## Projection
A man in his early thirties
standing at the Hafenofen
in [die Glashütte](place_die_glashuette.md).
The fire is in his face.
He does not shield his eyes.
He is reading the melt -
the color of it, the way it moves
when he draws the sample thread.
He holds [das Betriebsgeheimnis](piece_das_betriebsgeheimnis.md)
in the way he holds the Pfeife:
without thinking about holding it.

## Action
Draws the Fadenprobe - the thread test.
Holds the thread up.
Reads it.
Names the moment the melt is ready
to the men waiting at the bench.

## Shadow
Franz rebuilt after the fire of 1669.
He knows what it costs to start again from ash.
He is certain that what he rebuilt
is what was there before -
the same Hütte, the same knowledge, the same glass.
He is wrong in one specific way:
what the fire took was not the building.
It was three years of accumulated knowing -
which wood that season,
which sand from which part of the Bach,
which temperature for which product.
He has replaced all of it.
His records, which survive,
do not record what was lost.
They record what he rebuilt.

## Tell
He draws a second Fadenprobe
when the first is clear.
It is always clear.
He draws it anyway.
