# Position: Wrath
## The surface that holds until it doesn't.

## Owner
**Project:** Temptation
**Process:** [Tempting](process_tempting.md)
**Place:** [The Waiting Room](place_the_waiting_room.md)

## Has
[The Broken Table](piece_broken_table.md).
The accumulation that precedes the moment. Always accumulating.

## Orders
Hold it. The room is watching. Hold it.

## Loses
The fiction that the holding is permanent.

## Drives
Is very still. The stillness is load-bearing. Every small injustice in the room — the person who took the last magazine, the name called before theirs, the wait that extends without explanation — is noted. Filed. Added. The table has not broken yet. The room does not know how close it is.
