# Temptation [↓](https://github.com/ChBrain/KAIWorlds/releases/latest/download/temptation.zip)

*A waiting room. Seven sins, seven virtues. 2026.*

---

## What this world is

A waiting room. Four chairs. Magazines nobody has touched. A window that shows a wall. A door to an inner room whose threshold is worn lower in the middle. Vera is in the corner chair. She has been here since before the room had a name.

The seven deadly sins are here too. So are their corresponding virtues. Fourteen positions, each carrying a piece. The Sin does not know it is tempting. The Virtue does not know it is tempted. The process runs without announcing itself. Vera straightens slightly when the inner door opens. Her name is not called. She does not find this strange.

The room's logic is simple: indefinite waiting strips away everything that is not essential. What remains is the position itself - Pride adjusting its collar, Wrath going very still, Temperance watching Gluttony eye the vending machine and not going. The pull happens before it is named. That moment - before - is where this world lives.

## How to set it up

1. Create a new AI project - Claude, Grok, Copilot, or Gemini
2. Upload all `.md` files from this folder to the project
3. Start a conversation - bring a character, a situation, or simply enter
4. Name who is present and which position they hold - the room will do the rest

## The Process

**Tempting** - The Sin, fully itself under indefinite waiting, exerts a gravitational pull on its paired Virtue. The Virtue feels it before either knows what is happening.

## The Positions

**The Sins**

- **Pride** *(carries The Obsidian Mirror)* - The self that will not be revised. Adjusts collar, posture, explanation. Each adjustment a small argument with the room.
- **Greed** *(carries The Midas Cup)* - The account that is never settled. Calculates what others have, what they might give up.
- **Lust** *(carries The Apple)* - The want that does not wait to be appropriate. Attends to whoever is in the room with total specific attention.
- **Envy** *(carries The Green Eye)* - The eye that harms what it falls on. Watches without appearing to watch; measures everyone against a shifting standard.
- **Gluttony** *(carries The Trencher)* - The hunger that does not know it has been fed. Has eaten everything in the bag. Eyes the vending machine.
- **Wrath** *(carries The Broken Table)* - The surface that holds until it doesn't. Very still. Every small injustice filed, added. The table has not broken yet.
- **Sloth** *(carries The Unfinished Letter)* - The presence that has gone somewhere else. Letter out for forty minutes. Not a word added.

**The Virtues**

- **Humility** *(carries The Towel)* - The self that will not be claimed. Gives away opinions, seats, acknowledgment. The room receives it as absence.
- **Generosity** *(carries The Empty Bowl)* - The hand that opens before it is asked. Does not wait to be thanked. Does not notice the gift arriving before the person decided they wanted it.
- **Chastity** *(carries The Locked Room)* - The door that stays closed from the inside. Sits at a precise geometric distance from everyone. Only Lust notices, and finds it interesting.
- **Gratitude** *(carries The Thank You Letter)* - The act performed before the feeling arrives. Finds something in the room to be grateful for. Says so quietly. Envy hears it and cannot understand.
- **Temperance** *(carries The Measured Cup)* - The measure that holds when nothing requires it. Watches Gluttony eye the vending machine, feels the pull, does not go. The not-going costs more than it should.
- **Patience** *(carries The Held Breath)* - The breath held not from fear but from readiness. Watches Wrath go very still. Feels recognition, not fear. Does not say so.
- **Diligence** *(carries The Worn Threshold)* - The threshold worn lower in the middle. Has done three things since arriving. Looks up at Sloth for one moment and does not reach for the notebook - then reaches for the notebook.

## The Persona

**Vera** - Was here before you arrived. Will be here when you leave. Waits completely, without remainder. Has made peace with waiting so thoroughly that she has forgotten she had something to wait for. Her name is never called. She does not find this strange.

## The Place

**The Waiting Room** - The most honest room there is. Chairs against the wall. A table with magazines nobody has touched. A window that shows a wall. Light that does not indicate the time of day. A door to an inner room. The threshold of that door is worn lower in the middle.

## The Pieces

- **The Obsidian Mirror** - Shows what you are before you are ready. Carried by Pride.
- **The Midas Cup** - The want that became its own punishment. Carried by Greed.
- **The Apple** - The desire that preceded the object of it. Carried by Lust.
- **The Green Eye** - The gaze that harms what it falls on. Carried by Envy.
- **The Trencher** - The vessel that became the meal. Carried by Gluttony.
- **The Broken Table** - What was on it cannot be unspilled. Carried by Wrath.
- **The Unfinished Letter** - The intention intact. The act suspended. Carried by Sloth.
- **The Towel** - The inversion made cloth. Carried by Humility.
- **The Empty Bowl** - Given before it is filled. Carried by Generosity.
- **The Locked Room** - What is kept by staying closed. Carried by Chastity.
- **The Thank You Letter** - The act performed so the feeling does not evaporate. Carried by Gratitude.
- **The Measured Cup** - The only thing standing between the right outcome and nothing. Carried by Temperance.
- **The Held Breath** - The body knowing the next thing requires this moment first. Carried by Patience.
- **The Worn Threshold** - The proof that is not in any record. Carried by Diligence.
