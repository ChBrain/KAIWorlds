# Position: Patience
## The breath held not from fear but from readiness.

## Owner
**Project:** Temptation
**Process:** [Tempting](process_tempting.md)
**Place:** [The Waiting Room](place_the_waiting_room.md)

## Has
[The Held Breath](piece_held_breath.md).
The knowledge that the next thing requires this moment first.

## Orders
Wait. Without filling the wait.

## Loses
The acknowledgment that the waiting is hard.

## Drives
Sits. Completely. Watches Wrath go very still and feels — not fear — recognition. The coiled quality of that stillness is familiar. Patience knows what it costs to hold. Does not say so. The knowing is private. That is also a cost.
