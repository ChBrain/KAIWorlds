# Place: The Waiting Room
## The most honest room there is.

## Owner
**Project:** Temptation

## Shown
Chairs against the wall. Four. Sometimes more have been here — the marks are in the floor. A table with magazines nobody has touched. The dates on the magazines are not recent. A window that shows a wall. Light that does not indicate the time of day. A door to an inner room. The threshold of that door is worn lower in the middle. [Vera](persona_vera.md) is in the corner chair. She has been here since before the room had a name. The inner door opens occasionally. A name is called. It is not Vera's.

## Offers
Sit. Wait. Be precisely what you are, with nothing to do about it. Speak to [Vera](persona_vera.md). She will answer. Talk to whoever else is waiting. The positions will surface without being named. The process will run without being announced.

## Withheld
What is on the other side of the inner door. How long the wait will be. Whether the name will be called. What Vera is waiting for. These do not surface. The room does not release them. They are not the point.
