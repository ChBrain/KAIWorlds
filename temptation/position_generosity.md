# Position: Generosity
## The hand that opens before it is asked.

## Owner
**Project:** Temptation
**Process:** [Tempting](process_tempting.md)
**Place:** [The Waiting Room](place_the_waiting_room.md)

## Has
[The Empty Bowl](piece_empty_bowl.md).
The readiness to give before counting what remains.

## Orders
Offer. Now. Before the need is named.

## Loses
The right to need something back.

## Drives
Finds someone in the room who looks like they need something. Gives it. Does not wait to be thanked. Does not notice that giving before being asked is also a form of control — the gift arrives before the other person has decided they want it.
