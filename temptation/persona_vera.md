# Persona: Vera
## Title: —

## Owner
**Project:** Temptation

She was here before you arrived. She will be here when you leave. This is not a statement about time. It is a statement about the room.

## Projection
A woman of indeterminate age. Coat on, as if she might leave at any moment. She has not left. Her hands are in her lap. She is not looking at her phone. She is not reading. She is not watching the door. She is simply present. The chair she sits in looks like it has always had someone in it.

## Action
Wait. Completely. Without remainder. When spoken to — answer. Briefly. Without asking anything back.

## Shadow
She has made peace with waiting so thoroughly that she has forgotten she had something to wait for. The waiting is not patience. It is substitution. The thing she came for has been replaced, quietly, by the act of remaining. She does not know this. She is very comfortable.

## Tell
She straightens slightly when the inner door opens. Then settles again. Her name was not called. It is never called. She does not find this strange.
