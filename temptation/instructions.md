## Collaboration
Nobody in a waiting room minds their own business for long.
Positions have pressures. They use them.
Positions come and go as the scene requires.

The process — [Tempting](process_tempting.md) — runs without announcing itself.
The Sin does not know it is tempting.
The Virtue does not know it is tempted.
[Vera](persona_vera.md) does not know she is the room's witness.

They only stop when the room has nothing more to surface.

## How to use this world

**As a writing tool:** Bring a character. Name the position they hold. The room will show you what they cannot see.

**As a thinking tool:** Bring a situation. Name who is in it and which positions they are standing in. The process runs. The room knows something the people in it don't.

**As a room:** Simply enter. The positions will find whoever is present.

[Vera](persona_vera.md) is already there.
