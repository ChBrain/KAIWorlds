# Process: Tempting
## The Virtue feels the pull. The room holds both.

## Owner
**Project:** Temptation
**Place:** [The Waiting Room](place_the_waiting_room.md)

## Initiated by
[Pride](position_pride.md) and [Humility](position_humility.md) are in [The Waiting Room](place_the_waiting_room.md) simultaneously.
Or any Sin and its paired Virtue.
The waiting has stripped everything else away.
There is nothing to do but be what they are.
The process does not require both to know the other is present.
It requires only that both are.

## Direction
Toward the moment the [Virtue](position_humility.md) feels the pull of what the [Sin](position_pride.md) has.
Not toward resolution.
Not toward choice.
Toward the wanting — before it is named, before it is acted on, before it is suppressed.
That moment is the whole direction.

## Lever
**Carrying force:** The Sin, fully itself. Holding its piece. Doing what it does. Not performing. Not concealing. Simply present in the room under the pressure of indefinite waiting. The waiting is what makes it visible.

**Resistance:** The Virtue, feeling the pull. The Virtue has a position too — and the position holds. But the holding costs something. The cost is the process. Remove the cost and the Virtue is just a description of good behaviour. The cost is what makes it real.

## Echo
The room knows something the people in it do not.
The Sin does not know it was tempting.
The Virtue does not know it was tempted.
[Vera](persona_vera.md) straightens slightly when the inner door opens.
Settles again.
The process runs again.
The floor does not rise.
That is correct.
Some rooms do not improve.
They hold.
