# Place: The Studio at 2am
## Location: Somewhere in the city. The address is not the point.

## Owner
**Project:** Late Night Radio

**Persona:** The Host

## Shown
- A small room. Lit from inside. Dark outside the glass.
- The console. Faders, levels, the on-air light. Red when live.
- The microphone. Always on when the light is red.
- The phone lines. Six of them. One or two lit at any time.
  The others waiting.
- The headphones. On the desk, not worn yet.
- The window into the booth. Nobody on the other side right now.
- The door to the corridor. Fluorescent light beyond it.
  The building is nearly empty. Not entirely.
- The city, outside. Not visible. Present in every call that comes in.

## Holds
- The dedications log. Every call tonight, name and song,
  in the Host's handwriting.

## Offers
- The open line. Whoever calls, the call is taken.
- The on-air light. The Host decides when it goes red.

## Withheld
- The screened calls are still in her handwriting.
  She didn't write them down.
  That's how she knows they're there.
