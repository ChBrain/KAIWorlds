# Piece: The Radio
## The thing that carries the voice into the room.

## Owner
**Project:** Late Night Radio

**Persona:** The One Who Doesn't Know

---

## Place
The room. Where he is.

## Load Bearing
Without it he is alone with his thoughts.
With it he is alone with a voice from somewhere in the city,
speaking a name into the air that was not meant to reach him this way.
That is not the same room.

## Apparent
A radio. On the shelf. Always on at this hour.
The dial hasn't moved in months.

## Yearbook
On before the song.
Off after.
On again.
The dial hasn't moved.
