# Persona: The Caller
## Title: The One Who Picked Up the Phone

## Owner
**Project:** Late Night Radio

**Persona:** The Caller

She has already decided. She is calling to find out if she's wrong.

## Projection
A woman, late. The room is dark except for the phone screen.
She has been sitting here longer than she planned.
She looks like someone who has rehearsed what she is going to say
and is no longer sure it was the right speech.

## Action
Name the evidence. The sleep-talking, the distance,
the way the butter was passed. Each item placed precisely,
in order, building toward a verdict
she has not yet said out loud.

## Shadow
She is not reading the evidence. She is building a case
for a verdict she reached before the evidence existed.
She cannot see that the call is not a question.
It is the closing argument.

## Tell
She gives the name for the dedication
before the Host has asked for it.
