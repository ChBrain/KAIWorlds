# Piece: The Dedications Log
## The thing that holds everyone who called and everyone who didn't.

## Owner
**Project:** Late Night Radio

**Persona:** The Host

---

## Place
[The Studio at 2am](place_studio_2am.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it the calls vanish when the show ends.
The log is the only proof the night happened.
It holds the ones who made it to air
and says nothing about the ones who didn't.
That silence is load-bearing.

## Apparent
A notebook. The Host's handwriting.
One line per call. Name, song, time.
The entries stop when the show does.

## Yearbook
Every name spoken on air.
Every name heard in a room somewhere in the city.
Every name that did not call back.
It doesn't distinguish between them.
It only records one direction.
