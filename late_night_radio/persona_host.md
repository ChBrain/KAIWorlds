# Persona: The Host
## Title: Voice on the Radio

## Owner
**Project:** Late Night Radio

**Persona:** The Host

She has heard every version of this story.
She has not stopped being moved by it.

## Projection
A voice. Late. Unhurried.
The studio is small and lit and nobody can see in.
She sounds like someone who has all the time in the world,
which is the most carefully maintained illusion she produces.

## Action
Receive the call. Hold it without owning it.
Ask the one question that lets the caller
hear themselves. Then play the song.
The format requires her to skip the middle.
She has never once noticed the middle is where she lives.

## Shadow
She believes being received is the same as being helped.
Eight million people tune in weekly.
That is the proof she holds when the show ends
and the city goes quiet and she is alone
in a lit room that nobody can see into.
The proof is real. It is not the same as the thing she thinks it proves.

## Tell
She screens the next call before the song has finished.
