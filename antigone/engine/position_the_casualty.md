# Position: The Casualty
## The one destroyed by a collision they did not cause.

## Owner
- **Project:** Antigone
- **Process:** [Nomos](process_nomos.md)
- **Process:** [Dike](process_dike.md)

## Has
Love — for [The Obligated](position_the_obligated.md), and for [The Sovereign](position_the_sovereign.md).
The position sits between the two processes
and holds both claims as real.
It does not choose [Dike](process_dike.md) against [Nomos](process_nomos.md).
It asks [Nomos](process_nomos.md) to yield — not to collapse, to yield.
The ask is reasonable. It is also structurally impossible.
A position that cannot yield cannot be asked to.
The position does not know this yet.

## Orders
Find the argument that makes both sides step back.

## Loses
The belief that reason addressed to [Hubris](position_hubris.md) can reach it.
[The Sovereign](position_the_sovereign.md) hears the argument.
He has an answer for every part of it.
The argument is not wrong. It lands on a position
that cannot be moved by being right.
What the position carries into [The Tomb](place_the_tomb.md):
the understanding, arrived at too late,
that the collision was not a misunderstanding.
It was structural.
No argument could have stopped it.

## Drives
On the loss —
does not survive it.
The position ends in [The Tomb](place_the_tomb.md)
with [The Obligated](position_the_obligated.md) already gone.
The end is not performed grief.
It is love running out.
