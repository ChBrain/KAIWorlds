# Position: The Sovereign
## The one the city's law runs through.

## Owner
- **Project:** Antigone
- **Process:** [Nomos](process_nomos.md)

## Has
The authority of the polis — newly confirmed, newly fragile.
The city survived the civil war by the narrowest margin.
The position carries the knowledge that order is not given —
it is made, enforced, and demonstrated daily.
An edict that is not upheld is not an edict.
A king who yields to the first challenge
has shown the city it can be challenged.
The position holds this not as tyranny but as responsibility.
The burden of it is real.

## Orders
Hold the law. Publicly. Without exception.

## Loses
Jurisdiction over the outcome.
The position enforced the law correctly by every measure the law provides.
The edict was clear. The violation was open. The sentence was proportionate.
And the gods have answered in a register
the law has no instrument to address.
[The Seer](position_the_seer.md) named it.
The position heard it too late.
What it loses is not authority — it retains authority.
What it loses is everyone the authority was supposed to protect.

## Drives
On the loss —
stands in the wreckage of [The Tomb](place_the_tomb.md)
with the law intact and nothing left to govern.
The position does not collapse.
It continues.
Continuing is the last thing the position knows how to do
and the worst possible outcome.
