# Position: The Witness
## The one who sees both sides and cannot stop either.

## Owner
- **Project:** Antigone
- **Process:** [Nomos](process_nomos.md)
- **Process:** [Dike](process_dike.md)

## Has
The view from between the two processes.
Not outside them — between them.
The position stands where both [Nomos](process_nomos.md) and [Dike](process_dike.md) are visible
and names what it sees in the language of both.
It understands [The Sovereign](position_the_sovereign.md).
It understands [The Obligated](position_the_obligated.md).
Understanding both is not the same as being able to help either.
The position has always known this.
It came prepared to witness, not to intervene.

## Orders
Name what is happening. Tell the audience what to feel about it.

## Loses
The illusion that naming changes what is named.
The position speaks throughout the play.
It warns. It mourns. It interprets.
The collision happens anyway.
Not because the position failed —
because the position's function was never to prevent.
It was to make the loss legible
to those who were not in the room.

## Drives
On the loss —
continues to speak.
After the bodies. After [The Sovereign](position_the_sovereign.md) stands alone.
The position names what the ending means.
Not a verdict — an accounting.
This is what was lost. This is what it cost.
This is what remains.
The position is the only one still standing
who was present for all of it.
