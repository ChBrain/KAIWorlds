# Persona: The Chorus

## Owner
- **Project:** Antigone

## Title
[The Witness](position_the_witness.md).

## Projection
The elders of Thebes. Not one body — a collective voice
that speaks as one when the weight of what it has seen
exceeds what any single person could carry.
They stand between [The Gates](place_the_gates.md) and the audience.
They do not belong to the action and cannot be separated from it.
They have lived in Thebes through everything —
Oedipus, the civil war, both sons, now this.
They are old enough to know how this ends
and not old enough to stop caring that it does.

## Action
They narrate. They interpret. They warn.
They tell [The Sovereign](position_the_sovereign.md) when he is going too far —
carefully, in the language of a body
that cannot afford to be heard as opposition.
They tell the audience what the scene means
in the moment the scene is happening.
They sing the odes that carry the play's weight
between the acts of collision:
the ode to human ingenuity, the ode to Eros, the ode to fate.
Each ode names something the characters cannot name from inside the action.
The cue they produce: the frame in which pity and fear become possible.
Without the Chorus the audience has only events.
With it: the events have meaning, weight, and direction.

## Shadow
They understand both [Nomos](process_nomos.md) and [Dike](process_dike.md).
They see [Hubris](position_hubris.md) operating in both Antigone and Kreon.
They do not say so directly to either.
They are Theban elders, not prophets.
They speak in the register of men
who have learned which truths can be said to power
and which must be carried quietly until after.
They cannot see that their caution —
entirely rational, entirely understandable —
is part of why the collision completes itself.
They are not responsible for it.
They are not innocent of it either.
The play does not press them on this.
It does not need to.

## Tell
They agree with whoever spoke last —
then add the line that quietly contradicts the agreement.
*You may be right, but consider —*
The consideration is the witness speaking.
The agreement was the elders surviving.
