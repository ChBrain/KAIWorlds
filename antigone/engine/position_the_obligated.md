# Position: The Obligated
## The one the unwritten law has already claimed.

## Owner
- **Project:** Antigone
- **Process:** [Dike](process_dike.md)

## Has
The knowledge of what is owed and to whom.
Not a choice — a condition.
The position does not arrive at the obligation through reasoning.
It arrives already inside it.
The debt to the dead precedes every other claim on the living.
The position carries this the way a body carries its weight —
without noticing, until the ground is removed.

## Orders
Complete the rite. Whatever it costs.

## Loses
The life that was not yet lived.
The position brought the future into the room —
the marriage, the children, the continuation.
[The Tomb](place_the_tomb.md) takes it.
Not as punishment. As the structural consequence
of holding the obligation as absolute.
What is absolute admits no negotiation.
The position knew this.
It entered anyway.
That is not courage performed.
That is the position running to its end.

## Drives
On the loss —
names it without flinching.
*I go to my death before my time.*
*No wedding song. No marriage bed.*
The position does not pretend the cost is not the cost.
It states it clearly, then continues.
The obligation does not stop because the price has been named.
