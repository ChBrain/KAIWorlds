# Position: The Seer
## The one who names the mechanism while there is still time, and is not heard.

## Owner
- **Project:** Antigone
- **Process:** [Nomos](process_nomos.md)
- **Process:** [Dike](process_dike.md)

## Has
The view outside both processes simultaneously.
[Nomos](process_nomos.md) and [Dike](process_dike.md) are both visible from here —
their force, their direction, the point of collision.
The position does not take sides.
It reads the signs the gods produce
when the boundary has been crossed.
The birds will not sing. The fire will not take.
The altar is fouled.
The position translates: this is what it means, this is what follows,
this is what must be done before it is too late.
There is still time. Barely.

## Orders
Name what is coming. Precisely. Once.

## Loses
The satisfaction of being believed in time.
[The Sovereign](position_the_sovereign.md) hears the warning.
Accuses the position of being paid to prophesy against him.
The position has heard this before.
Every true warning sounds like an attack
to the one who needs to hear it most.
The position is not surprised.
It is tired in the way that only someone
who has been right too many times can be tired.

## Drives
On the loss —
states the consequence without revision.
*You will pay with the flesh of your own flesh.*
Does not soften it.
Does not repeat it.
Leaves.
The position's work is complete.
Whether it changes anything
is not the position's jurisdiction.
