# Place: The Cave

## Owner
- **Project:** Plato

## Shown
Underground. A long chamber.
Prisoners facing a wall since childhood.
Chains at the neck and legs - they cannot turn.
Behind them: a fire. Between the fire and the prisoners: a low wall.
Behind the wall: people carrying objects whose shadows fall on the wall the prisoners face.
The prisoners see only shadows.
They have always seen only shadows.
They have names for the shadows.
They believe the names are knowledge.

## Holds
- [The Power Structure](piece_the_power_structure.md) - the ones who carry the objects. Always present.
- The chains - the belief that the wall is all there is. Not physical after childhood. No longer needed.
- The shadows - everything the prisoners hold as knowledge.

## Offers
The only world the prisoners have ever known.
The names of the shadows.
The comfort of certainty.
The company of others who see the same things.
To the one who has returned: the gap between what they now know and what they can say here.

## Withheld
The fire. The objects. The opening behind.
The sun.
The cave does not hide these things.
The prisoners cannot turn to see them.
The chains were removed long ago.
The turning has not happened.
[The Examination](process_the_examination.md) is what makes turning possible.
The cave withholds nothing deliberately.
It simply faces one direction.
