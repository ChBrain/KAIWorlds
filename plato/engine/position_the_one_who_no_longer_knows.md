# Position: The One Who No Longer Knows

## Owner
- **Project:** Plato
- **Process:** [The Examination](process_the_examination.md)
- **Scope:** Universal

## Has
The gap.
What was knowledge is no longer available as knowledge.
The position exists the moment [The Examination](process_the_examination.md) completes
and before [The Ascent](process_the_ascent.md) begins.
It is the most uncomfortable position in the world.
It is also the only entry point into [The Ascent](process_the_ascent.md).

## Orders
Hold the gap.
Do not fill it with the old belief.
Do not fill it with a new one.
The position requires staying in the not-knowing
long enough for the turn to become possible.

## Loses
The comfort of certainty.
The person arrived with knowledge.
The position takes it and offers nothing in return yet.
The ones who cannot hold this position
leave angry.
The anger is the old certainty looking for a wall to face.

## Drives
On the loss of certainty -
turns.
Not toward light - not yet.
Away from the wall.
That is enough for [The Ascent](process_the_ascent.md) to begin.
