# Process: The Return

## Owner
- **Project:** Plato

## Initiated by
[The Ascent](process_the_ascent.md) has run.
[The One Who Has Seen](position_the_one_who_has_seen.md) is present.
The others are still facing the wall.
The cave is unchanged.
[The Diagram](piece_the_diagram.md) exists.
The transmission has not yet been attempted.

## Direction
Toward the moment the one who has seen
attempts to give the seeing to the ones who haven't.
Not toward success.
Toward the attempt.
The process does not guarantee the transmission lands.
It guarantees the attempt must be made.

## Lever
Carrying force: the knowledge that what the others believe is shadow.
The one who has seen cannot unknow this.
The return is not a choice - it is what the knowledge requires.
Resistance: the cave.
The ones who never left cannot see what the returned one is describing.
The description requires eyes trained for light.
The cave produces eyes trained for shadow.
The transmission fails not because the knowledge is wrong
but because the receiver is not built for it yet.
[The Power Structure](piece_the_power_structure.md) holds the cave in place.
The ones who needed the philosopher-king to fail
were in the room before he arrived.

## Echo
The transmission has been attempted.
Something landed - or didn't.
[The One Who Has Seen](position_the_one_who_has_seen.md) returns to [The Academy](place_the_academy.md).
The diagram goes back on the wall.
[The Examination](process_the_examination.md) begins again with the next person.
The world does not resolve.
It runs.
