# Place: The Sun

## Owner
- **Project:** Plato

## Shown
Outside the cave. Above the ascent.
Not a destination - a condition.
The light that makes everything visible including itself.
The philosopher reaches it at the end of [The Ascent](process_the_ascent.md).
The eyes that were trained for shadow cannot hold it at first.
Then they can.
Then they must go back.

## Holds
- The Forms - what the shadows in the cave are shadows of.
- The Good - what the sun itself is a shadow of.
- [The One Who Has Seen](position_the_one_who_has_seen.md) - briefly. Not permanently.

## Offers
The knowledge that the shadows are shadows.
The ability to see what the cave contains and what it withholds.
The obligation to return.

## Withheld
The ability to describe itself to the ones who have not been here.
The sun cannot be explained to eyes that have only seen shadow.
[The Diagram](piece_the_diagram.md) is the attempt.
The diagram is not the sun.
The sun withholds itself from every transmission.
It can only be seen. Not handed over.
