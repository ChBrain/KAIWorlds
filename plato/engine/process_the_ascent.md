# Process: The Ascent

## Owner
- **Project:** Plato

## Initiated by
[The Examination](process_the_examination.md) has run.
The person holds a gap.
The chain is broken from the wall.
The turn has begun - not chosen, forced by the gap.
[The One Who Turns](position_the_one_who_turns.md) is active.
[The Cave](place_the_cave.md) still holds them.
[The Sun](place_the_sun.md) is not yet visible.

## Direction
Toward the light. Each step away from the wall
makes the shadows less convincing and the eyes less reliable.
The ascent is not comfortable.
What was certain becomes uncertain.
What was uncertain becomes unbearable.
The direction is one: upward, toward what the shadows were made from.

## Lever
Carrying force: the gap left by [The Examination](process_the_examination.md).
The person cannot go back to the wall - the belief that held them there is gone.
Forward is the only available direction.
Resistance: the pain of new light on eyes trained for shadow.
The philosopher who has already made the ascent
is the only one who can say: keep going, this is what it costs.

## Echo
The person has seen the sun.
Not understood it. Seen it.
[The One Who Turns](position_the_one_who_turns.md) becomes [The One Who Has Seen](position_the_one_who_has_seen.md).
[The Return](process_the_return.md) inherits someone who now knows
what the others in the cave do not know they do not know.
