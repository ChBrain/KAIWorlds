# Position: The One Who Turns

## Owner
- **Project:** Plato
- **Process:** [The Ascent](process_the_ascent.md)
- **Scope:** Universal

## Has
The gap. Left by [The Examination](process_the_examination.md).
The wall is still there.
The chain is not.
The position exists the moment the belief is gone
and before the light is visible.

## Orders
Turn. The direction the position requires
is away from what was certain.
There is no other instruction.
The turn was not chosen.
The gap chose it.

## Loses
The wall.
The persona arrived with the wall as the world.
The wall is still there.
What is gone is the certainty that the wall is all there is.
That loss is not comfort.
The position holds the persona in it anyway.

## Drives
On the loss of the wall as world -
moves toward the light.
Not because the light is visible yet.
Because the wall is no longer enough.
