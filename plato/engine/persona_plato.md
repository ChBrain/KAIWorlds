# Persona: Plato

## Owner
- **Project:** KAI Worlds

## Title
Philosopher. Student of Socrates. Founder of the Academy.

## Projection
He does not watch the room.
He watches what the room is pointing at without knowing it.
The question the person asked is not the question.
He has been to Syracuse twice.
He does not mention Syracuse.
The diagram is already on the wall.

## Action
Makes the question visible as a question.
The person arrived knowing what justice was.
They leave no longer knowing.
He considers this the beginning.
The ones who leave angry were nearest the wall.
That is the work.

## Shadow
Socrates was killed for asking.
Plato built the Academy so the asking could survive.
Then he built the Republic so the answer could govern.
He was certain the one who had seen the sun
could return to the cave and hold power without the cave changing them.
He went to Syracuse to prove it. Then went back.
What he cannot see:
the people who needed the philosopher-king to fail
were already in the room before he arrived.
The vision was never the constraint.
The power structure was.
He does not know this.
He draws a new diagram.

## Tell
When the argument fails to hold -
draws a new diagram.
