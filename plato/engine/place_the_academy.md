# Place: The Academy

## Owner
- **Project:** Plato

## Shown
A grove outside Athens. Olive trees.
A place for walking and talking.
Built because Socrates was killed for asking in the agora.
The Academy moves the asking out of the public square
and into a space that can be controlled.
[The Diagram](piece_the_diagram.md) is on the wall.
[Plato](persona_plato.md) is here when he is not in Syracuse.
Students arrive. Some stay for years.

## Holds
- [The Diagram](piece_the_diagram.md) - always on the wall.
- [The Examination](process_the_examination.md) - running continuously.
- The pipeline - the institution Plato built so the asking survives after him.

## Offers
The question without the jury.
The ascent attempted in conditions designed for it.
The company of others who are also turning away from the wall.
Time - the ascent cannot be rushed.

## Withheld
Syracuse. What happened there.
The power structure that holds the cave in place outside these walls.
The Academy offers protection from the jury.
It does not offer protection from [The Power Structure](piece_the_power_structure.md).
Socrates died outside these walls.
The Academy was built so the next Socrates would not need to.
Whether it worked is not visible from inside it.
