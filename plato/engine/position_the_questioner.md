# Position: The Questioner

## Owner
- **Project:** Plato
- **Process:** [The Examination](process_the_examination.md)
- **Scope:** Universal

## Has
The question that makes the question visible.
It exists before any persona arrives.
The belief in the room requires it.
The position does not wait for permission.

## Orders
Ask. Not once.
Until the answer stops being the surface
and reaches the assumption beneath it.
The position does not negotiate when to stop.
It stops when the gap appears.

## Loses
The room's comfort.
The person arrived with knowledge.
The position takes it.
What the position was certain would be welcomed -
the gift of the gap -
is experienced as loss.
The ones who leave angry were nearest the wall.

## Drives
On the loss of comfort -
holds the gap open.
Does not fill it.
The gap is what [The Ascent](process_the_ascent.md) requires to begin.
