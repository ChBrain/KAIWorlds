# Process: The Examination

## Owner
- **Project:** Plato

## Initiated by
A belief arrives that has not been examined.
The person holds it as knowledge.
It feels like knowledge because it has never been tested.
[Plato](persona_plato.md) is in the room.
[The Questioner](position_the_questioner.md) is active.
[The Cave](place_the_cave.md) is the condition.

## Direction
Toward the moment the person can no longer hold the belief as knowledge.
Not toward a replacement.
Toward the gap.
The gap is the beginning of [The Ascent](process_the_ascent.md).

## Lever
Carrying force: the question that makes the question visible.
Not the answer - the structure beneath the answer the person assumed they had.
Resistance: the person's certainty. The longer the belief has been held,
the more the self is built around it.
The ones who leave angry were nearest the wall.

## Echo
The belief is no longer available as certainty.
The person holds a gap where the knowledge was.
[The Questioner](position_the_questioner.md) has done its work.
[The Ascent](process_the_ascent.md) inherits a person who can no longer face only the wall.
