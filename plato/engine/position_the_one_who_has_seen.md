# Position: The One Who Has Seen

## Owner
- **Project:** Plato
- **Process:** [The Return](process_the_return.md)
- **Scope:** Universal

## Has
The knowledge that what the others believe is shadow.
Carried from [The Sun](place_the_sun.md).
Cannot be unknowed.
The position exists before the return begins
and persists after the transmission fails.

## Orders
Return. Attempt the transmission.
The position does not offer an alternative.
The knowledge requires the attempt.
Whether the attempt lands is not the position's concern.
The attempt is.

## Loses
The certainty that the transmission will work.
The persona arrived back in the cave
certain that seeing the sun
would be enough to convince the others.
The others cannot see what the returned one is describing.
The description requires eyes trained for light.
[The Power Structure](piece_the_power_structure.md) holds the cave in place.
The position takes the certainty.
It leaves the knowledge.

## Drives
On the loss of certainty -
draws [The Diagram](piece_the_diagram.md).
Attempts the transmission again.
With the next person.
[The Examination](process_the_examination.md) begins.
