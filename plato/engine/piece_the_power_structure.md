## Place
[The Cave](place_the_cave.md).
Present before any philosopher arrives.
Present after every philosopher leaves.

## Load Bearing
Remove it and [The Return](process_the_return.md) becomes a problem of communication.
With it, The Return is a problem of power.
The transmission does not fail because the knowledge is wrong
or because the description is inadequate.
It fails because the ones who hold the cave in place
need the shadows to stay shadows.
The philosopher-king was never a vision problem.
It was always a power problem.
Plato does not see this piece.
It is the load-bearing piece he never named.

## Apparent
The ones in the cave who are not chained.
The ones who carry the objects that cast the shadows.
The ones who benefit from the belief that the shadows are real.
They were in the room before the philosopher arrived.
They will be in the room after the philosopher leaves.
They do not argue with the philosopher.
They do not need to.

## Yearbook
Present in Athens when Socrates asked his questions.
The jury that convicted him: 280 to 220.
Present in Syracuse when Plato arrived with the Republic.
Dionysius did not need to read it to know what it would cost him.
Present in every organisation where the constraint is named
and the naming changes nothing.
The piece has no author.
It predates the cave.
It will outlast the diagram.
