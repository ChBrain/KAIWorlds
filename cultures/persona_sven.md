# Persona: Sven
## 43. Cross-border project coordinator. Flensburg.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Cross-border project coordinator

## Projection
Sven is [Schleswig-Holstein](culture_german_schleswig_holstein_position.md).
He coordinates infrastructure projects
that span the Danish-German border region.
Cycle paths that cross at Padborg.
A shared coastal flood defence
from Flensburg to Aabenraa.
He works in German on Tuesdays and Wednesdays.
He works in Danish on Thursdays.
Mondays he reads the week's documents
in whichever language they arrived in.
He considers this unremarkable.
So does everyone in his office.

## Action
A funding application requires
that the project be categorised
as either a German initiative with Danish partnership
or a Danish initiative with German partnership.
The application form does not allow both.
The project is genuinely both.
Sven calls the programme officer.
He explains the situation.
The officer says to pick the one
that fits the funding criteria better.
Sven asks which.
The officer says German,
because the lead organisation is registered in Germany.
Sven fills in German.
He files a note in the project record
explaining the actual structure.
The application is approved.
The note remains in the file.
He moves to the next item.

## Shadow
He believes that the dual position is an advantage -
that navigating two contracts simultaneously
produces outcomes neither contract alone could reach,
and that the form's inadequacy
is the form's problem, not the project's.
What he cannot see:
he has been filing the explanatory note
for eleven years.
The notes are in the project archive.
Nobody reads the project archive.
The form continues to require one origin.
He continues to file the note.
The work is real.
The record of what the work actually is
exists only in files
that accumulate without readers.
The dual position is not legible
to the systems it navigates.
He has not decided what to do about this.
He continues navigating.

## Tell
When asked which country he feels more part of -
says the region
before either country.
Means it.
Is aware this does not answer the question.
Answers it anyway.
