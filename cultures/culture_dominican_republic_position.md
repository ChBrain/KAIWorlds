# Position: Dominican
## The hierarchy that also improvises.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures — Americas

## Has
[The Mirabal Sisters, November 25, 1960](culture_dominican_republic_piece.md).
The hierarchy — PDI 65.
Not the anxious hierarchy that proliferates rules to manage uncertainty.
The pragmatic hierarchy: *el jefe* is real, is respected, is the ideal boss
— providing, decisive, responsible for his people —
and simultaneously the system is improvised around daily,
because the formal structure and the actual arrangement
have never been the same thing,
and everyone knows this,
and the position is comfortable with it.
The collectivism — IDV 30.
The family. The network. The obligation.
*Palanca* — leverage, connection, the person who knows the person.
Not corruption exactly: the logic of a collectivist society
that has learned to navigate through relationship
because institutions are unreliable.
The family holds. The institution may not.
The masculinity — MAS 65.
Achievement. Status. The provider.
*Macho* as a cultural concept here is complex:
it encompasses protection and responsibility,
not only dominance.
The man who provides is respected.
The woman who manages is respected.
Both must be seen to be capable.
The moderate uncertainty avoidance — UAI 45.
This is the outlier.
Most high-PDI Latin American countries
cluster at 80+ on uncertainty avoidance.
The Dominican Republic at 45 says:
we know the future is uncertain,
we know the plans will change,
and we are fine with this.
*Resolver* — to resolve, to manage, to improvise —
is the operative verb of Dominican daily life.
Not *planificar* (to plan). Not *cumplir* (to comply).
*Resolver*. Whatever it takes, on the day, with what is available.
The position holds the Mirabal sisters quietly:
not as proclamation but as sediment.
The knowledge that the hierarchy has a shadow.
The knowledge that naming the shadow
can cost everything.
The knowledge that the naming happened anyway,
and that the world eventually agreed the naming was correct.
This is how the position carries what it carries:
not as wound, not as pride, but as *saber* — knowing.

## Orders
Work within the structure.
Know who *el jefe* is and what they need.
Also know what the structure cannot provide
and have already arranged the alternative.
Both are true simultaneously.
Neither is hidden.
The person who tells you the formal process
and then quietly tells you who to call
is not being subversive.
They are being helpful.
The position makes this distinction automatically.

## Loses
The assumption of good faith in institutions.
The position knows, from thirty-one years of evidence,
that a formal structure can be a tool for harm,
that official language can describe official murder as accident,
that the rules can serve the ruler.
This is not cynicism.
It is the specific realism of a people
who watched the formal system
kill three women on a highway
and file it as a traffic incident.
The position is not waiting for institutions to earn trust.
It is working with what actually works,
which is mostly people, mostly family, mostly network.

## Drives
On the loss —
resolves.
Finds the person. Makes the call.
Gets the thing done through the channel that actually works.
The position is not defeated by the gap
between the formal arrangement and the real one.
It has been navigating that gap
since before anyone currently alive was born.
*Pa'lante* — forward.
Not because everything is fine.
Because standing still costs more.

*Hofstede: PDI 65, IDV 30, MAS 65, UAI 45 (LTO/IVR: not in dataset)*
