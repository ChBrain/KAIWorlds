# Place: Santiago
## Location: Chile

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures — Americas

## Shown
- The Plaza de la Constitución and La Moneda. The presidential palace. The hole in the facade from the 1973 bombing was repaired. The building is intact. The hole is in the historical record, not in the wall.
- The Andes to the east, visible on clear days, which are not as frequent as they used to be. The smog basin. The mountains were always there; the smog is recent.
- Barrio Italia and Barrio Lastarria. Cafés. Bookshops. Murals. The urban middle class on weekends. The city that looks, in certain lights, like a European city that arrived in South America with its bags already packed.
- Villa Grimaldi. The detention and torture centre, now a Peace Park. Roses. Trees. Plaques. The names of those who did not come back. School groups visit.
- The Mercado Central. The fish market near the river. Families on Sundays. Conger eel stew. The oldest restaurants in the city. The things that remain.
