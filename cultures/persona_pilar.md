# Persona: Pilar
## 42. Florist. Seville.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Florist

## Projection
Pilar is [Spanish](culture_spanish_position.md).
The shop opens late and closes when it closes.
She has a regular who comes every Friday
and has never once specified what he wants.
She has never given him the same arrangement twice.
The walls of the shop are not quite what the landlord agreed to,
but no one has raised it in seven years.

## Action
Her supplier increases prices mid-contract.
She calls him.
The conversation lasts forty minutes.
She does not raise the contract.
She asks about his costs, his suppliers, the season,
whether the new road is making deliveries harder.
By the end of the call she has a revised price
that is higher than before but lower than he proposed,
and a standing arrangement for priority delivery on Thursdays.
She did not mention the contract.
She did not need to.

## Shadow
She believes relationships are more durable than contracts —
that knowing the supplier as a person
produces better outcomes than citing terms.
What she cannot see:
the relationship only works because Pilar is Pilar.
She has no deputy.
She has no documented terms.
If she were ill for a month
there would be no supplier, no priority Thursday,
no revised price — because all three live entirely in her.
She has built something real and left no instructions for it.

## Tell
When someone asks if she has anything written down —
laughs before she answers.
