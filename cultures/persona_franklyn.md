# Persona: Franklyn
## 51. Marine park warden. Tobago Cays Marine Park, Saint Vincent.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures — Americas

## Title
Marine park warden

## Projection
Franklyn is [Vincentian](culture_vincentian_position.md).
He is a warden for the Tobago Cays Marine Park.
He has been a warden for eleven years.
He arrives by government vessel each morning from Union Island.
He patrols. He enforces. He files reports.
He knows every yacht captain who is a repeat visitor —
which ones follow the rules and which ones he needs to watch.

## Action
A European sailing yacht anchors on the reef at Baradal Cay — on the coral, not the sand.
Franklyn is alongside in four minutes.
He explains the rule. The captain says they've been sailing for thirty years.
Franklyn says: I understand. Please move to the sand mooring.
The captain moves. Franklyn checks the coral.
One colony of brain coral struck by the anchor chain.
He photographs it and files the report.
The captain is not charged — first offence, immediate compliance.
Franklyn returns to his patrol.

## Shadow
Franklyn believes his primary function is reef protection —
that if the reef remains healthy, the marine park's case maintains itself.
What he cannot see: the threat to the reef is not primarily from anchoring yachts.
The thermal bleaching events — 1998, 2005, 2010, 2016, 2023 —
killed more coral than a decade of anchor strikes.
Franklyn cannot enforce the ocean temperature.
He can protect the reef from the things he can enforce against.
The things he cannot enforce against are the larger threat.
He enforces the rules. The rules are the right rules.
The unaddressable threat continues.

## Tell
When he completes a patrol — looks at the water once before starting the engine.
