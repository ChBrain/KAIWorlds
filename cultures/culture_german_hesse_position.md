# Position: Hesse
## The state that the world passes through on the way to somewhere else.

## Owner
- *
## Has
[The Airport](culture_german_hesse_piece_airport.md).
The financial centre - Frankfurt as Europe's bank.
The European Central Bank.
The Deutsche Börse.
The publishing industry.
The trade fair infrastructure.
The position is neither north nor south,
neither Protestant heartland nor Catholic south -
it is the crossing point,
the place where the trajectories of the republic
intersect in transit.
The Hessians themselves: direct, occasionally brisk,
a political culture noted for
a certain no-nonsense practicality.
Not the precision of the German position.
Not the thrift of the Swabian.
Practical, without drama, oriented toward outcome.
The apple wine - Ebbelwoi -
sour, local, proudly unexported.
The state that connects everything
has something it keeps for itself.
It exists before the persona arrives.
The room is a transit point.
The room is also someone's home.
Both things are true simultaneously.

## Orders
Connect.
The position does not hoard the connections.
It facilitates them.
The airport, the rails, the financial infrastructure -
all of it is about moving things through
rather than accumulating them here.
The position profits from the transit
without requiring the things to stay.
This is different from the Hanseatic model:
Hamburg builds the reputation from the trade.
Hesse builds the infrastructure for the trade
and lets the goods go.

## Loses
The identity.
They were certain that being the hub -
the most connected state,
the financial capital,
the publishing capital -
would produce a clear and celebrated identity.
The identity of a transit point
is transit.
People know Frankfurt Airport.
They know less about Frankfurt.
They know less about Hesse.
The position is famous for facilitating
what others do.
Its own character is harder to name.

## Drives
On the loss of identity -
keeps the infrastructure running.
The flights connect.
The books are traded.
The euros are managed.
The Ebbelwoi is drunk in Sachsenhausen.
The position does not need the world
to know its name.
It needs the connections to hold.
They hold.
