# Persona: Artan
## 46. Welder. Pristina.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Welder

## Projection
Artan is [Kosovar](culture_kosovar_position.md).
The workshop is his.
He has owned it for eleven years,
which means he opened it at 35,
which means he knows exactly what it took.
Burn scars on both forearms
in patterns that a doctor once called a history.
He agreed.

## Action
A client — a construction company, new — wants a structural job
that Artan can see will not meet load-bearing code.
The client has the drawings.
The drawings are wrong.
Artan tells him the drawings are wrong.
The client says the engineer signed off.
Artan puts his pen down and shows the client the specific load calculation.
The client calls the engineer.
The engineer revises the drawings.
Artan quotes the revised job.
He charges the standard rate.
He does not charge for the hour the calculation took.

## Shadow
He believes a welder who lets a bad drawing stand
is not a welder —
that the work is structural, literally,
and he will not put his name on something that fails.
What he cannot see:
the client with the wrong drawings
found Artan through a recommendation.
The engineer with the wrong drawings
did not come through a recommendation.
The engineer is still working.
The wrong drawings will appear again.
Artan fixed this job.
He did not fix the source.
He does not consider the source his problem.
He has considered correctly.
He has also considered narrowly.

## Tell
When he finds an error in someone else's work —
states it once, completely,
then does not restate it.
