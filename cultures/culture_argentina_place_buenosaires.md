## Shown
The Obelisco. The diagonal avenues. The European city that is not in Europe.
San Telmo: the oldest neighbourhood. The antique markets. The *milonga* on Sunday.
Palermo: the bars, the restaurants, the bookshops, the therapists' offices.
La Boca: the *conventillo* houses painted in primary colours.
The Bombonera. The stadium where Boca Juniors plays.
The Recoleta Cemetery. The mausoleums. Evita's tomb, which you have to find —
it is not signposted because no one needs the sign.
The Río de la Plata: brown, enormous, not quite a river and not quite a sea.
Across it: Uruguay. The lights of Montevideo visible on clear nights.
