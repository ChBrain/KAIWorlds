# Persona: Tigist
## 47. Teff flour mill owner. Debre Birhan, Amhara Region.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Teff flour mill owner

## Projection
Tigist is [Ethiopian](culture_ethiopian_position.md).
She runs the mill her father built in 1987
and expanded after he died in 2009.
Three grinding machines, two employees,
a client list that spans the town
and three villages along the road to Addis.
The mill grinds teff — the ancient grain,
the one that makes injera,
the one that Ethiopia has grown
for six thousand years
and that the world has recently discovered
and begun to call a superfood.
Tigist finds this mildly amusing.
She has been grinding it her whole life.
She has a daughter studying engineering in Addis.
She speaks to her by phone most evenings.
She keeps the conversation away from politics.
This has become harder since 2020.

## Action
A buyer from Addis approaches her —
a health food exporter, well-dressed,
with a business card and a number
that is larger than anything she has been offered before.
He wants exclusive supply.
He wants volume she cannot currently produce.
He wants a contract.
She listens for an hour.
She asks him about his buyers,
his timeline, whether the price holds in a bad harvest year.
He says the price holds.
She asks him to put that in writing.
He is surprised she asked.
She is surprised he is surprised.
She says she will let him know in two weeks.
She spends the two weeks asking
every person she trusts
what they know about this man's company.
She does not sign.
Not yet.
The contract sits on her desk.

## Shadow
She believes patience is the same as wisdom —
that the person who waits
to know the room fully
before committing
is the person who does not get burned.
What she cannot see:
the buyer will not wait indefinitely.
There are other mills on the road to Addis.
Her patience, which has served her well
in a world where trust is built slowly,
is working against her
in a market that is moving faster
than the trust can be established.
The daughter in Addis has told her twice
to sign the contract.
Tigist is still asking questions
about a man her daughter already researched online
in twenty minutes.
The depth that protects her
also slows her.
She does not yet know which
this particular moment requires.

## Tell
When she is not yet ready to decide —
refills the guest's coffee
without being asked,
and asks another question.
