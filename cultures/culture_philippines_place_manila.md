## Shown
- Intramuros. The walled city. Spanish colonial. The stone walls, the cathedral, the fort. The most destroyed city in Asia in 1945, rebuilt around what survived. The walls survived.
- Rizal Park. Jose Rizal's monument. The national hero. Shot by the Spanish in 1896. His execution produced the revolution. The park is large and public and used daily.
- The EDSA Shrine. At the corner of EDSA and Ortigas. The Virgin Mary in yellow and white — the colours worn by the People Power crowd. Flowers. Candles. Traffic on all sides.
- SM Mall of Asia. One of the largest malls in the world. On reclaimed land at Manila Bay. The sunset behind it. Families on the weekend. The consumption that followed the liberation.
- The Pasig River. Running through the city. Was declared biologically dead in the 1990s. Rehabilitation ongoing. Fish have returned. The return is genuine and partial simultaneously.
