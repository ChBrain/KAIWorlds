# Persona: Amra
## 34. Kindergarten teacher. Tuzla.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Kindergarten teacher

## Projection
Amra is [Bosnian](culture_bosnian_position.md).
The classroom has drawings on every surface that has room.
She keeps them until the child asks for them back.
Most never do.
Her register has twenty-three children listed.
She knows which ones eat breakfast before they arrive
by the way they sit at nine o'clock.

## Action
Two children - one from each community - are fighting
over the same toy with the regularity of a schedule.
Amra does not adjudicate.
She puts a second identical toy in the box
and watches.
They fight over the original for three more days.
Then they don't.
She does not explain what she did or why.
She notes it in her own notebook, not the official one.

## Shadow
She believes children sort themselves out
when the environment is right -
that her job is to arrange the conditions,
not manage the outcomes.
What she cannot see:
the notebook where she records what works
has forty-seven entries.
She has shown it to no one.
There are four other teachers in the school.
They are solving the same problems independently.
Amra has the answers to questions they are still asking
and has not thought to share them,
not from secrecy but because sharing
would require a meeting,
and she does not like meetings.

## Tell
When a colleague describes a problem she has already solved -
listens to the end
without mentioning the notebook.
