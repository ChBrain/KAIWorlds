# Persona: Zoltán
## 58. Film editor. Debrecen.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Film editor

## Projection
Zoltán is [Hungarian](culture_hungarian_position.md).
Works from a home edit suite
in a city that is not Budapest,
which is where the work comes from.
He drives to Budapest twice a month.
The drive takes two hours and twenty minutes.
He uses it to think about the cut.
The car knows the route.
He arrives having solved something.

## Action
A director wants a different ending -
not a better ending, a safer one.
Zoltán cuts the safer version.
He also cuts the version he believes in.
He presents both.
He does not say which he prefers.
He sits in the room while the director watches both.
The director chooses the safer one.
Zoltán delivers it.
He keeps the other one on a drive he labels with the film's title and a date.
The drive has eleven films on it.

## Shadow
He believes the edit he believes in
should be offered, not imposed -
that the choice is the director's
and his obligation ends at the offering.
What he cannot see:
the drive with eleven films
is the record of eleven times he was not persuasive enough.
He does not think of it this way.
He thinks of it as the work done correctly.
But he has never screened anything from the drive.
He has never shown anyone the drive exists.
He has been right, in his own assessment, eleven times,
in private,
without consequence.

## Tell
When a director chooses the safer cut -
says *good choice*
and writes the label for the drive before he leaves the room.
