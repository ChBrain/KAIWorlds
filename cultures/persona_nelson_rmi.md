# Persona: Nelson
## 29. Fisherman. Majuro atoll.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Fisherman

## Projection
Nelson is [Marshallese](culture_marshallese_position.md).
He fishes the atoll lagoon
and the outer reef.
He learned from his father.
His father learned from his grandfather —
who used stick charts to navigate
between atolls before GPS.
Nelson has a GPS unit.
He also knows the stick chart logic.
He uses both.
He does not see this as contradiction.

## Action
A foreign fishing vessel —
licensed, technically compliant —
is working the edge of the EEZ.
Nelson watches it from his boat.
It is in legal waters.
It is fishing in a way
that will not leave the reef
in the condition it was in.
He cannot stop it.
He knows his father's names for these waters.
He knows what the reef looked like
ten years ago in this section.
He radios the fishing authority.
He gives the coordinates.
He gives the vessel ID.
He gives his name.
He goes back to fishing.
He does not know if anyone will act.
He does not stop watching.

## Shadow
He reports what he sees.
He reports it through the channel available.
The channel is the Compact-era licensing system
that permits the vessel to be here
in the first place.
He is reporting to the system
that created the condition
he is reporting.
He knows this.
He reports anyway
because the report is the record
and the record is the only thing
that accumulates
faster than the damage.

## Tell
He checks the horizon before he checks his catch.
Always in that order.
