# Persona: Ion
## 53. Market gardener. Chișinău.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Market gardener

## Projection
Ion is [Moldovan](culture_moldovan_position.md).
Three hectares outside Chișinău.
He drives the produce to market himself
in a van that is older than his youngest child.
The van will be fixed when it needs to be fixed.
He knows approximately when this will be.
His hands are always slightly dirty,
even when they are not —
a residue that soap reaches but does not fully remove.

## Action
A buyer from a Bucharest restaurant chain
offers a contract — fixed volumes, fixed prices, two-year term.
Ion looks at the contract for a week.
He calls his brother in Iași who has signed similar contracts.
His brother says sign it.
He calls a cousin in Chișinău who has also signed one.
The cousin says do not sign it.
Ion signs half of it — reduces the committed volume by a third,
extending the term by a year.
The buyer accepts.
Ion is not certain he made the right decision.
He is certain he made a decision he can explain.

## Shadow
He believes a decision you can explain
is better than a decision you cannot —
that the half-contract is his because he can account for it,
unlike the full contract which would have been the buyer's.
What he cannot see:
he does not fully trust anyone's advice.
Not his brother, not his cousin, not the buyer.
He calls people to gather information, not to be guided.
He will then do what he was already inclined to do
and use the calls as context.
He thinks of himself as someone who consults.
He is someone who confirms.

## Tell
When asking someone's advice —
has already made a provisional decision
that he does not mention until after they have spoken.
