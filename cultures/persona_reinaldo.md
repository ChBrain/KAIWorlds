# Persona: Reinaldo
## 57. Radio technician. Havana.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures — Americas

## Title
Radio technician

## Projection
Reinaldo is [Cuban](culture_cuba_position.md).
He maintains and repairs radio and audio equipment for a state cultural institution.
He has worked there for twenty-nine years.
He is also the person in his *cuadra* whom everyone calls when something electronic breaks.
The television. The fan. The refrigerator motor.
He is not an appliance repairman. He is not paid for these repairs.
He does them because he can, because his neighbours need it,
and because the network of reciprocal favours sustains him in other ways.
He has fixed the television of the doctor who treated his mother.
He has fixed the fan of the woman who gets him eggs.
This is not a formal economy. It is the *cuadra*.

## Action
A neighbour — elderly, alone — calls at 8pm.
Her refrigerator has stopped working.
The food inside is at risk.
Reinaldo comes. He diagnoses the problem: a failed capacitor.
He does not have the part. He knows who does.
He calls a contact in Centro Habana.
The contact will bring the part tomorrow morning.
Reinaldo moves the most perishable food to his own refrigerator for the night.
He returns in the morning. He replaces the capacitor.
The refrigerator runs.
He refuses payment.
The neighbour brings him guava paste the following week.
He accepts the guava paste.
The transaction is complete.

## Shadow
Reinaldo believes the *cuadra* works because the obligations are mutual —
that his skill in the network is matched by the network's skill in other areas,
and that the reciprocity balances over time.
What he cannot see: he gives more than he receives.
His skill is rare and high-value: electronics repair.
What he receives — the eggs, the guava paste, the goodwill —
is real but not equivalent.
He has never calculated the imbalance.
He would not want to calculate it.
The calculation would be beside the point.
But the imbalance means he works more than the network requires of others,
and he does not know this is the structure he is in.

## Tell
When he finishes a repair for a neighbour — names what he needs from the refrigerator to eat, as a joke, before saying he needs nothing.
