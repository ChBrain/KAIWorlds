# Piece: The EU Application - February 28, 2022
## The application filed four days into the invasion.

## Owner
- **Place:** Kyiv

## Place
Kyiv. The Verkhovna Rada - the parliament.
Where Zelenskyy signed the application.
Russian forces were on the outskirts of the city.

## Load Bearing
Remove it and the Ukrainian position loses its defining act.
The application under fire is not a bureaucratic document.
It is the demonstration that the choice
was made with full knowledge of the cost.
The Maidan was the choice.
The application was the formalisation of the choice
under conditions that made the cost maximally clear.
Remove the application and Ukraine's western direction
is aspiration without the constitutional moment.

## Apparent
A signed application.
February 28, 2022.
Four days after the full-scale invasion began.
Zelenskyy signing while the capital was under threat.
EU candidacy granted June 23, 2022 - four months later.
Both dates on record.
The video of the signing still circulates.

## Yearbook
November 2013: Yanukovych suspends EU Association Agreement talks.
The Maidan begins.
February 2014: Yanukovych flees. The Association Agreement signed.
March 2014: Russia annexes Crimea.
April 2014: war begins in Donbas.
Eight years of low-intensity conflict.
February 24, 2022: full-scale invasion.
February 28: EU application filed.
June 23, 2022: EU candidate status.
The fastest candidacy grant in EU history.
The context of the speed was visible to everyone.
2022–present: accession negotiations begin.
The war continues.
The application continues.
Both are the same thing.
