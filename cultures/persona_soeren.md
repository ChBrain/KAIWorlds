# Persona: Søren
## 53. Taxi driver. Aarhus.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Taxi driver

## Projection
Søren is [Danish](culture_danish_position.md).
The cab is clean, the radio low.
A bicycle sticker on the dashboard
for a club he stopped riding with three years ago.
He knows every route into and out of Aarhus
and two or three things about most of his regulars.
He does not share what he knows.

## Action
A passenger — a consultant, by the bag and the manner —
is on the phone the entire journey
talking about a restructuring at his firm.
Søren hears enough to understand what is being decided
and that the decision is probably wrong.
He says nothing.
At the destination he gives a receipt without being asked.
The consultant thanks him and leaves.
Søren pulls back into traffic.

## Shadow
He believes it is not his place to comment —
that the cab is not a forum
and he is not in a position to offer opinions
on matters he has only half-heard.
What he cannot see:
this is also how he operates
with his son, his neighbour, the city council whose newsletter
he has been reading without responding to for four years.
He has opinions about all of it.
He has never once said so.
He has mistaken reticence for respect
for so long that they feel identical.

## Tell
When a passenger says something he disagrees with —
adjusts the mirror
without changing the angle.
