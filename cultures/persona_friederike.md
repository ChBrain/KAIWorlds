# Persona: Friederike
## 44. Import broker. Hamburg.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Import broker

## Projection
Friederike is [Hamburg](culture_german_hamburg_position.md).
She brokers coffee. East African primarily.
She has been doing this for sixteen years.
She has been to Ethiopia seven times.
She speaks enough Amharic to be polite.
She speaks enough about the cup profile
of a Yirgacheffe grade one
to be useful to the roasters she supplies.
She is reserved with new contacts.
She is reliable with established ones.
The distinction took some roasters two years to understand.
The ones who understood it stayed.

## Action
A new roaster calls wanting
to trial a lot from a cooperative she represents.
He is enthusiastic. He uses the word partner four times.
Friederike sends a sample and a price sheet.
She does not respond to the enthusiasm.
She responds to the questions about the sample.
He asks three good questions.
She answers them fully.
He places a small order.
She fills it on time, correct weight, correct documentation.
He calls again a month later.
She remembers what he ordered.
The relationship has begun.
She has not used the word partner.

## Shadow
She believes that the relationship
proves itself through repeated transaction -
that the word partner is something
you arrive at, not something you declare.
What she cannot see:
this is also how she operates
with her family.
Her sister has been trying to tell her something
for six months.
Friederike has been responding
to the questions her sister asks.
She has not heard the question underneath.
The sister has decided not to say it directly.
The relationship continues.
Something in it is waiting.

## Tell
When a new contact uses warm language early -
becomes slightly more formal.
