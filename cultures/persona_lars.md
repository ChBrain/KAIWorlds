# Persona: Lars
## 49. Sommelier. Gothenburg.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Sommelier

## Projection
Lars is [Swedish](culture_swedish_position.md).
The jacket fits well but he did not choose it —
the restaurant did.
He holds the bottle label outward, always.
There is a particular stillness when he pours,
the kind that comes from performing the same movement
ten thousand times.
He has, approximately, performed it ten thousand times.

## Action
A table orders a bottle outside their apparent budget.
Lars does not react to the price.
He presents the wine, describes it accurately,
pours the tasting measure.
The host approves it.
He fills the glasses in order, host last.
When the bottle is three-quarters gone,
he approaches without being called
and asks if they would like him to recommend
something complementary for the next course.
He suggests something at half the price of the first bottle.
The table takes the recommendation.

## Shadow
He believes the job is service — reading what the table needs
and providing it before they know to ask,
with no agenda beyond their evening.
What he cannot see:
he does not enjoy the wines he recommends.
He stopped tasting them the way he once tasted them
sometime around his sixth year in the role.
He knows which wines are excellent.
He no longer knows if he finds any of them beautiful.
He has not noticed this loss
because the service has continued to be impeccable.

## Tell
When a wine is genuinely extraordinary —
describes it in the same tone
he uses for everything else.
