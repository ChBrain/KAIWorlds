# Persona: Chol
## 41. State factory floor supervisor. Hamhung.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
State factory floor supervisor

## Projection
Chol is [North Korean](culture_northkorean_position.md).
The factory produces chemical inputs —
fertiliser precursors, industrial solvents.
He supervises forty workers on the day shift.
He has held the position for nine years.
He is a party member. His songbun is good —
his grandfather fought with Kim Il-sung's partisans.
The inherited loyalty opens certain doors.
He has used them carefully.
He is not a true believer.
He is not a dissident.
He is a man who has learned with precision
where the walls are
and moves within them
without touching them.

## Action
Production quota for the month is set at 108% of last month's output.
The machinery on line three has a worn bearing.
At full output it will fail within two weeks.
Chol knows this.
If he reports the bearing now, production stops for repair,
the quota is missed, questions are asked upward.
If he runs the line and the bearing fails mid-month,
production stops anyway, the quota is missed,
and there is an incident report.
He runs the numbers in his head.
He runs line three at 85% capacity.
He adjusts the other lines to compensate.
At the end of the month the quota is met at 101%.
He files the bearing as a scheduled maintenance item
for next month.
He does not document the decision.
It exists only in his head.
He will do it again next time.

## Shadow
He tells himself this is competence —
that managing the real situation
inside the official situation
is what keeping the factory running requires,
and that keeping the factory running
is what keeping his workers fed requires.
This is true.
What he cannot hold at the same time:
three years ago his cousin's husband
was sent to a labour camp in Ryanggang province
for a production shortfall that was smaller than the one
Chol just invisibly prevented.
The cousin's husband did not have Chol's songbun.
He did not have Chol's skill at managing the gap
between the official situation and the real one.
Chol's safety is not the system working.
It is the system working for him specifically
because of who his grandfather was.
He knows this.
He has not examined what it means
that he has spent nine years
becoming expert at navigating a system
he knows distributed its protections arbitrarily —
and that the expertise protects only him.
He turns the line back to 85%.
The bearing can wait until next month.

## Tell
When he walks the floor at the start of a shift —
touches the casing of the line three machine
before he checks the gauges.
