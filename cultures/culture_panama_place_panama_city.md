# Place: Panama City, Panama

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures — Americas

## Shown
- The Canal Zone. The locks at Miraflores — the visitor centre, the observation deck,
  the ships moving through in slow procession every hour of every day.
  The thing that Panama is because of.
- Casco Viejo. The old quarter on a peninsula. UNESCO listed.
  Colonial churches, crumbling mansions, renovated boutique hotels.
  The gentrification visible in the gap between restored and unrestored blocks.
- The Amador Causeway. The breakwater built from canal excavation spoil.
  Connected to the former US military bases — Fort Amador.
  The Bridge of the Americas visible. The Pacific visible.
- Punta Pacifica and Punta Paitilla. The glass towers.
  The banking district. The international money at rest.
  Panama as offshore financial centre, banking secrecy laws, flag of convenience.
  All of this visible in the skyline.
- The Emberá villages in the Darién. Accessible by dugout canoe.
  The community that holds what the canal economy does not.
  The two Panamas: skyline and river, simultaneously.
