# Persona: Milica
## 36. Hotel receptionist. Budva.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Hotel receptionist

## Projection
Milica is [Montenegrin](culture_montenegrin_position.md).
The hotel faces the Adriatic.
She has worked at this desk for six seasons.
The desk faces the door and the sea simultaneously,
a fact she registered in her first week
and has not thought about since.
She handles four languages without announcing she does.

## Action
A guest is furious about a booking error —
not Milica's error, the system's,
or the manager's, or the season's.
She listens without interrupting.
She does not apologise for things she did not do.
She fixes what can be fixed: the room, the rate, the dinner reservation.
She offers what she can offer.
The guest is not fully satisfied.
Milica notes in the log what was offered and accepted.
She does not log what the guest said during the complaint.
She has heard most of it before, differently.

## Shadow
She believes her job is to solve what is solvable
and hold what is not —
that some guests will leave unhappy
and that is also an outcome, not a failure.
What she cannot see:
she has become very efficient at this calibration.
She decides very quickly what is solvable.
The decision is made in the first thirty seconds.
The guest who needed only to be heard for longer
gets the efficient version.
She does not know she has a threshold.
She would describe herself as patient.

## Tell
When a complaint extends past three minutes —
her hand moves to the keyboard
before the guest has finished speaking.
