# Persona: Fernand
## 60. Tax inspector. Esch-sur-Alzette.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Tax inspector

## Projection
Fernand is [Luxembourgish](culture_luxembourgish_position.md).
The office is in the south of the country,
which is not Luxembourg City.
He chose this posting thirty years ago
and has been offered transfers twice.
He declined both.
Esch is where the steel was.
He grew up knowing people who worked the steel.
The steel is gone.
He is still here.

## Action
A corporate filing arrives with a structure
that is, as far as Fernand can determine,
legal but designed to minimise liability
in a way that uses Luxembourg's treaty network
in a manner he considers technically valid
and practically contrary to the intent of the treaties.
He processes the filing.
He also writes an internal note
flagging the structure for policy review.
The note goes to his supervisor.
His supervisor files it.
Fernand does not know what happens to it after that.
He wrote it anyway.

## Shadow
He believes his job is the law as written —
that he applies it correctly
and flags where the law may need to change.
What he cannot see:
he has been writing these notes for fourteen years.
He does not know if any of them have produced a policy change.
He has not followed up on any of them.
He files the note and considers the responsibility discharged.
He believes the system processes his concerns.
He has never verified this.

## Tell
Dates the internal note precisely — day, month, year, time —
before filing it in the folder
he has never been asked about.
