# Persona: Branko
## 60. Port worker. Rijeka.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Port worker

## Projection
Branko is [Croatian](culture_croatian_position.md).
Shift starts at five.
The safety vest is his own, not the port's —
he bought it three years ago when the port's supply ran low
and he did not raise the issue, just solved it.
Works the container cranes.
Has done since 1992.
The year matters to him in a way he does not explain.

## Action
A new shipping line is routing through Rijeka —
expanded contracts, changed procedures, German management.
Branko learns the new procedures in full.
He also keeps the old ones in his head.
When the new procedures produce a bottleneck
in the third week — a sequencing problem the Germans designed around their home port —
he uses the old procedure to clear it,
tells his supervisor after, not before.
The bottleneck does not recur.
The procedure is formally revised four months later.
Branko's name is not in the revision.

## Shadow
He believes the work is what matters
and credit is what people who don't do the work care about.
What he cannot see:
the people who revised the procedure
did not know what created the bottleneck.
They fixed the symptom.
The underlying logic that Branko understood —
the reason his old procedure worked better —
was not transferred.
He cleared the problem and kept the knowledge to himself.
The next bottleneck will be different.
He will solve that one too.
Nothing will accumulate.

## Tell
When asked how he solved a problem —
says *just experience*
and picks up the next task.
