# Position: Finnish
## The silence that was already the answer.

## Owner
- *
## Has
[The Winter War](culture_finnish_piece_winter_war.md).
The silence that communicates.
Not absence - precision.
The room does not fill silence with words.
It fills silence with presence.
The decision has often been made
before the meeting begins.
The work has often been done
before it is announced.
The position does not explain this.
It exists before the persona arrives.
The room is already communicating.

## Orders
Mean what you say.
Say only what you mean.
The position does not allocate words
for relationship maintenance,
for transition,
for the management of awkwardness.
These are overhead.
If something needs saying, say it.
If it does not - the silence is the message.
The position trusts the other person
to read it.

## Loses
The filler.
They were certain that words were the work -
that speaking was showing up,
that filling the silence
was a form of respect.
The Finnish room does not receive the filler.
It receives it as noise.
Worse: as uncertainty.
As someone who has not yet
formed a view.
The persona fills the silence.
The room waits for them to finish
and then continues
from where it was
before the words arrived.

## Drives
On the loss of the filler -
sits in the silence.
Finds it is not empty.
The position does not offer warmth.
It offers precision and trust:
the trust that the other person
will understand what is not said,
and the precision of meaning
only what is spoken.
