# Position: Iranian
## The civilisation that predates Islam — and the state that governs in its name.

## Owner
- *
## Has
[The Revolution and the Republic](culture_iran_piece_revolution.md).
The Persian inheritance.
Four thousand years of continuous civilisation —
Cyrus, Darius, the Achaemenids,
the Sassanids, the poets:
Rumi, Hafez, Ferdowsi.
The position carries all of this
underneath the Islamic Republic
that has governed since 1979.
The *ta'arof* — the elaborate system of
ritual politeness, the offer refused,
the insistence, the acceptance —
is the social technology of a civilisation
that has navigated courts and conquerors
for four millennia
by managing the surface with precision.
The *ta'arof* is not insincerity.
It is the intelligence of a people
who know the difference between
what is said and what is meant
and have built an entire social system
around the management of that gap.
The hierarchy is real: age, learning, the *mullah*,
the position in the revolutionary establishment.
The individualism is also real — the Persian self
is literary, interior, proud.
The bazaar and the mosque
and the university
are three different rooms
with three different rules
and the Iranian position navigates all three
before lunch.
It exists before the persona arrives.
The room is already calculating
which of the three rooms this is.

## Orders
Navigate the gap.
Between what the Islamic Republic requires
and what the Persian civilisation produces —
the poetry, the music, the mixed-gender parties
behind closed doors, the satellite dishes
banned and present on every roof —
the gap is the daily life of most Iranians.
The position navigates the gap
with the sophistication of four thousand years of practice.
The surface: correct.
The interior: the actual life.
Both maintained simultaneously.
This is not hypocrisy.
It is survival as a form of culture.

## Loses
The future.
They were certain the revolution would produce
the just society the revolutionaries imagined.
The society produced is the Islamic Republic —
the clerical state, the Revolutionary Guards,
the economy under sanctions,
the 40% inflation, the brain drain.
The young who were born into the republic
and did not choose it
and watched Mahsa Amini die in 2022
for the wrong hijab
and went into the streets
and were shot and jailed
and went back inside
carrying the knowledge of what the state will do.
The position loses the future
when the young see it leaving —
the engineers, the doctors, the artists —
and go.

## Drives
On the loss of the future —
endures and creates.
The Iranian position does not stop creating.
The cinema, the literature, the music
that are made despite the restrictions
or smuggled out
or made underground
are the evidence that the position
cannot be fully enclosed.
Ferdowsi wrote the *Shahnameh*
under the Ghaznavids who tried to suppress Persian.
The Persian survived.
The position has done this before.
It does it again.
Whether this time is different —
whether the Islamic Republic
has damaged something that will not recover —
is the question the position
is living inside.
It does not have the answer yet.
It creates anyway.
