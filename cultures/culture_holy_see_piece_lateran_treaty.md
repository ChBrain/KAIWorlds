## Apparent
A document. February 11, 1929.
Signed by Cardinal Gasparri for the Holy See
and Mussolini for Italy.
Three treaties: the political treaty (creating Vatican City),
the concordat (governing Church-state relations in Italy),
the financial convention (compensation for lost Papal States).
Vatican City: 44 hectares, 0.44 square kilometres.
The smallest internationally recognised state in the world.
The document in the Vatican archives.
