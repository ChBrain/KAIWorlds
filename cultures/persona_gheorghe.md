# Persona: Gheorghe
## 67. Retired farmer. Iași.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Retired farmer

## Projection
Gheorghe is [Romanian](culture_romanian_position.md).
The land is his son's now, legally.
In practice the handover is incomplete.
He visits the fields most mornings
in a capacity that neither of them has named.
His hands still know the soil by texture —
he picks it up without thinking when he walks the rows.
He washed his hands before dinner for fifty years.
He still does, even though the reason has changed.

## Action
His son wants to shift to a different crop rotation —
EU subsidy driven, agronomist-recommended.
Gheorghe listens to the full explanation.
He does not object.
He asks about the third summer — the dry one that comes every seven years —
and whether the new rotation will hold in that summer.
The agronomist does not have this data.
The son does not know about the third summer.
Gheorghe does not say more.
He returns to the field the next morning.

## Shadow
He believes the land teaches on its own timeline —
that the third summer will arrive
and be its own answer.
What he cannot see:
the third summer will come after he is gone.
He has not written down the third summer.
He has not described it to his son.
He holds the knowledge like the soil in his hand —
present, textured, not transferred.
The land will teach his son.
But his son will learn it alone,
without Gheorghe's name on the lesson.

## Tell
When his son makes a decision he would not have made —
says nothing
and visits the affected field the following morning.
