# Persona: Eleni
## 48. Psychiatrist. Thessaloniki.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Psychiatrist

## Projection
Eleni is [Greek](culture_greek_position.md).
The office has two chairs of equal height.
She chose this specifically.
A degree on the wall and nothing else -
she removed two certificates because they felt competitive with the patient.
The window faces a courtyard.
She keeps it open regardless of the season
because the sound of the city is preferable to silence.

## Action
A patient challenges the diagnosis -
has read extensively, has an alternative framework,
wants to debate it.
Eleni engages the debate.
She takes his framework seriously, names what is useful in it,
then names what it cannot account for.
She does not conclude the debate.
She says: let's both sit with both frameworks this week.
The following session the patient has moved closer to her position
without being told to.
She considers this the correct order of things.

## Shadow
She believes the debate is the therapy -
that letting a patient argue their way to understanding
is more durable than directing them to it.
What she cannot see:
she enjoys the debate.
The patients who do not want to debate -
who want to be told, guided, reassured -
get less of her.
They get competent, correct, careful Eleni.
They do not get the Eleni who is fully present.
She has not noticed the pattern.
She believes she gives equally.

## Tell
When a patient stops arguing and simply agrees -
shifts her weight slightly in the chair,
just before she speaks.
