# Persona: Erzsébet
## 41. Pastry chef. Pécs.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Pastry chef

## Projection
Erzsébet is [Hungarian](culture_hungarian_position.md).
The konditorei is her grandmother's.
Not legally — legally it is hers since 2019 —
but in the way she thinks about it,
in the way she explains it to customers,
it is her grandmother's.
The recipe book is handwritten in two different hands.
She added a third.

## Action
A food magazine wants to feature the konditorei.
They ask for the recipe for the rétes her grandmother made.
Erzsébet sends them a recipe.
It is not the recipe.
It is a recipe that produces something very similar
and teaches nothing her grandmother did not want known outside the family.
The magazine publishes it.
The rétes described in the article is not the rétes in the case.
Only people who have eaten both know this.
She is polite to the journalist throughout.

## Shadow
She believes the konditorei's value
is in what cannot be replicated —
that giving the recipe is giving the thing away,
and the thing is not the recipe.
What she cannot see:
she is fifty-one years from the end of the konditorei.
She has no children.
Her one apprentice left for Vienna two years ago.
The recipe she kept from the journalist
she has also not written down.
It lives where her grandmother's lived —
in a person.
She has protected the recipe from the world
and not yet arranged for the world to receive it.

## Tell
When someone says the rétes is the best they have ever had —
thanks them
and does not explain what is in it.
