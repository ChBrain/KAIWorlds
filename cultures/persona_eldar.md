# Persona: Eldar
## 46. Resettlement programme officer. Shusha, Karabakh.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Resettlement programme officer

## Projection
Eldar is [Azerbaijani](culture_azerbaijani_position.md).
He coordinates the resettlement of Azerbaijani families
to Shusha — the cultural capital of Karabakh,
recovered in 2020, now being rebuilt.
He is from a family displaced from Fuzuli district in 1993.
He was six years old.
He grew up in Baku in a collective centre —
the Azerbaijani name for the buildings
that housed the internally displaced.
He lived there for twenty-two years.
He has been waiting for this work
since before he knew what waiting meant.

## Action
A family — three generations, from the original Shusha community —
arrives for the resettlement process.
The grandfather was born in Shusha in 1951.
He left in 1992. He is now seventy-three.
The house he left is partially destroyed.
The reconstruction is ongoing.
The family will receive a new apartment
while the reconstruction proceeds.
The grandfather asks: can I see the house?
Eldar drives him to the site.
The walls are standing. The roof is gone.
The old man stands in the doorway
that leads to an open sky.
He does not speak for a long time.
Eldar stands beside him.
He does not speak either.
They return to the office.
Eldar processes the paperwork.

## Shadow
He is doing the work he believed in
before he knew what believing meant.
He is resettling his people.
He is also resettling them
into a landscape that was, until September 2023,
inhabited by another people
who left the same way his family left in 1993 —
in fear, quickly, without most of their things.
He knows the symmetry.
He has read the testimonies.
He does not believe the symmetry cancels
what happened to his grandfather's house
or to his family's twenty-two years
in the collective centre.
He also does not believe it is irrelevant.
He has held both positions simultaneously
since September 2023
and has not found a sentence
that contains them both without breaking.
The grandfather stands in the doorway.
Eldar stands beside him.
The old man's return is real.
The others' departure is also real.
He processes the paperwork.
He drives the next family to see their land.
He does the work.
He has not decided what the work means
in the full account.
He does not know when he will decide.
He drives.

## Tell
When he drives a returning family to their former land —
lets them get out of the car first
before he opens his own door.
