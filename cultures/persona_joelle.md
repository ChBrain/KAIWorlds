# Persona: Joëlle
## 43. Civil servant. Luxembourg City.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Civil servant

## Projection
Joëlle is [Luxembourgish](culture_luxembourgish_position.md).
The office has three working languages.
She switches between them within meetings,
sometimes within sentences,
without noting that she has done so.
Her colleagues do the same.
This is not a skill here. It is the condition of working here.

## Action
A European Commission consultation requires input
from Luxembourg's ministry within six weeks.
Joëlle drafts the response.
It is sixteen pages.
She circulates it to four colleagues
for comment before submission.
The comments arrive in three languages.
She incorporates them in one.
She translates two herself.
The response is submitted on time.
The cover letter is in French.
The body is in English.
The annexes are in German.
She chose these without being asked.
They were the correct choices.

## Shadow
She believes her job is to make the process work —
that a civil servant who removes friction
is serving the institution correctly.
What she cannot see:
she has become so efficient at removing friction
that she has accumulated a reputation
she did not seek and cannot fully see.
Three commission officials now contact her directly
for guidance before formal processes begin.
She helps them because it is the right thing to do.
She is also now doing two jobs
and billing for one.
She has not named this as a problem.

## Tell
Switches language mid-explanation
when she is working out the answer
as she speaks it.
