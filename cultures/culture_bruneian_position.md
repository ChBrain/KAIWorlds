# Position: Bruneian
## The sultanate that found oil and stopped needing to become anything else.

## Owner
- *
## Has
[The Melayu Islam Beraja](culture_brunei_piece_mib.md).
The petroleum state.
The position was built on an accident of geology —
oil discovered in 1929 —
and on the decision of a sultan
who negotiated independence from Britain in 1984
while retaining absolute authority
and distributing enough of the oil revenue
to make the authority comfortable.
No income tax. Subsidised housing, healthcare, education.
The social contract is material and specific:
the state provides, the subject is loyal,
the questions that might destabilise the provision
are not the questions that are asked.
The Malay identity is the frame,
the Islam is the law,
the Raja is the apex.
These three things — *Melayu Islam Beraja* —
are the national ideology
and the national theology simultaneously.
The position is not restless.
A position that is materially comfortable
and ideologically complete
does not produce restlessness.
It produces a specific kind of stillness
that outsiders mistake for contentment
and insiders know is something more complicated.
It exists before the persona arrives.
The room already knows
what is provided and what is not asked.

## Orders
Accept the provision.
The position does not require the citizen to build —
the state builds.
It requires the citizen to be Bruneian,
which means Malay, Muslim, and loyal
in that order.
The non-Malay Chinese community —
15% of the population —
holds the economy and holds no citizenship.
The position does not address this directly.
It addresses it by not addressing it.

## Loses
The project.
They were certain the provision was the answer —
that a state that gives you housing, healthcare, education,
and asks only loyalty and conformity in return
had solved the problem other states struggle with.
What the provision cannot give:
the sense of having built something.
The young Bruneian who has everything provided
discovers that having everything provided
is not the same as having done anything.
The restlessness is not hunger.
It is purposelessness with a full stomach.
The position has not resolved this.
The oil is projected to run out.
The date changes. The question does not go away.

## Drives
On the loss of the project —
leaves, or performs.
The ones who leave: to the UK, to Australia, to Singapore.
They build things there.
They sometimes come back with the building.
The ones who perform: the halal tourism initiative,
the Brunei Vision 2035,
the economic diversification that has been announced
and has not yet arrived.
The position waits for the project to materialise.
The oil funds the waiting.
