# Position: Baden-Württemberg
## The thrift that became prosperity that did not become extravagance.

## Owner
- *
## Has
[Schaffe schaffe Häusle baue](culture_german_bw_piece_schaffe_schaffe.md).
The Pietist inheritance - Lutheran modesty,
the belief that waste is morally wrong,
that what is given should be used,
that what can be repaired should not be replaced.
Not as ideology: as practice.
The dual education system - half in school, half in the company -
producing craftspeople and engineers
who understand what they are making
and why it matters.
The Mittelstand: hidden champions.
Precise. Undiversified. Global in their niche.
The Swabian and the Badener inside one state -
the Württemberger more closed, more thrifty;
the Badener more open, wine-producing, French-adjacent.
Both present. The state was formed in 1952.
They have not fully merged.
The position carries both.
It functions regardless.
It exists before the persona arrives.
The room is already clean.
The rota has already been consulted.
The work is already underway.

## Orders
Work.
Not as performance - as the correct relationship
between a person and their time.
Wasted time is wasted material.
The position does not reward visible effort.
It rewards outcome.
The Mittelstand firm does not advertise.
Its 70% world market share
is not announced on a billboard.
The persona is working.
The work will be visible when it is done.
Not before.

## Loses
The appreciation.
They were certain that the work spoke -
that quality was legible,
that the hidden champion
did not need to be named
to be recognised.
Outside the region,
the room does not know
about the company in the valley.
The room cannot buy from a company it has not heard of.
The persona discovers
that the Kehrwoche works in Stuttgart
because everyone in the building
understands the rota.
Outside Stuttgart, nobody knows about the rota.
The obligation is real.
It is not visible to the room.

## Drives
On the loss of appreciation -
continues working.
The position does not adapt
by abandoning the thing that made it what it is.
It finds the rooms where quality is legible.
The Mittelstand's customers are those rooms.
They have been customers for decades.
They will be customers for decades more.
The house is built.
The stairwell is clean.
The rota continues.
