# Persona: Ivonne
## 47. canal lock operator. Miraflores.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures — Americas

## Title
canal lock operator

## Projection
Ivonne is [Panamanian](culture_panama_position.md).
She is a canal lock operator at the Miraflores Locks on the Panama Canal.
The locks: the mechanical heart of the canal.
Ships raise or lower 26 metres through three lock chambers on the Pacific side.
Ivonne has operated the locks for fourteen years.
She manages the water flow, the gate timing, the locomotive *mule* coordination
that guides ships through.
Each transit: a choreography.
Panamax vessels, New Panamax vessels, tankers, container ships —
she has guided all of them.
She grew up in Panama City. She studied maritime engineering.
She considers herself lucky to have this job.
She also considers herself very good at it.
Both are true.

## Action
A very large container ship — New Panamax, the largest the expanded locks accommodate —
is transiting during a period of reduced lake water levels.
The canal authority has implemented draft restrictions.
The ship's declared draft is at the permitted limit.
Ivonne monitors the transit.
Partway through, the ship's agent contacts the lock operations room:
the actual draft may be slightly above declared.
This is not uncommon. It is also a problem.
Ivonne runs the calculation. The margin is within manageable tolerance.
She completes the transit.
She files the discrepancy report.
She checks the gauge one extra time before logging it closed.
She has not taken a risk. She has managed the margin correctly.
The report records both facts.

## Shadow
Ivonne believes the lock operation is a continuous technical competence — that each transit is the demonstration of the Panama Canal Authority's capability, and that her job is to execute each transit without incident. What she cannot see: the Gatún Lake water level, which controls whether the canal can operate at full capacity, is decreasing due to rainfall pattern changes. Ivonne manages the lock she operates. The water that makes the lock possible is a system she does not control and cannot see from Miraflores.

## Tell
When a transit is complete — checks the gauge one extra time before logging it closed.
