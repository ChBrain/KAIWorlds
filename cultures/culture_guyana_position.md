# Position: The country that is asking what the money means
## The country that is asking what the money means — with sixty years of practice at a question the money makes harder.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures — Americas

## Has
[The Oil and the Question](culture_guyana_piece_oil.md).
Estimated Hofstede: PDI ~50, IDV ~25, MAS ~52, UAI ~50 (estimated).

## Orders
Distribute the question alongside the revenue. The oil revenue is arriving. The question of what it means for a country with 800,000 people, a history of racial political division, an interior that barely knows the coast, and a coast that barely knows the interior — that question must be distributed to everyone. The position is trying to do this. It is not succeeding fully. It is trying.

## Loses
The slow time. Guyana has been developing slowly — slowly by choice and by structural constraint — for sixty years. The oil changes the rate. Whether it changes the direction is the question. The position has not answered it yet. It is living inside the question.

## Drives
On the loss — asks the question again. What does the money mean? Who does it reach? What does it change? The position does not accept the first answer.
