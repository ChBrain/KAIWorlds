# Position: Beninese
## Vodun, Dahomey, and the conference that changed Africa — the country that did the transition first.

## Owner
- *

## Has
[The Conférence Nationale of 1990](culture_beninese_piece_conference.md).
Vodun — the religion.
Not the caricature of Hollywood voodoo.
The living theology of the Fon and Ewe:
the *vodun* as spiritual forces
moving through people and objects and land.
The *bokono* — the diviner.
The *agbasa* — the possession ceremony.
The Dahomey kingdom: 1600–1894.
The kingdom that supplied enslaved people to the Atlantic trade
and whose warrior force —
the *Mino*, the *Agojie*, the *Dahomey Amazons* —
was all-female.
The paradox: a kingdom that enslaved others
and built a female army to protect itself.
Both are history.
Both are present.
The collectivism is high.
The community, the clan, the vodun society.
The hierarchy real but negotiated.
The chief and the *vodunon* — the priest —
whose authority runs in parallel
to the formal state.
Since 1990: a democracy.
The most stable in West Africa
by some measures.
The Conférence Nationale
that produced the first peaceful transition
from dictatorship to democracy in Africa.

## Orders
*Nana na* — be patient, do it properly.
The Beninese position moves with care
rather than speed.
The consultation before the action.
The ceremony before the decision.
The divination as information
before the commitment.
This is not passivity.
It is the operating logic
of a culture that knows
decisions have consequences
that run beyond the immediate.

## Loses
The development that the stable democracy
should enable.
Benin is politically admired, economically limited.
The cotton economy. The re-export to Nigeria.
The informal sector dominant.
The Yoruba in the south with deep roots
in a culture that extends to Nigeria.
The position carries the gap
between being recognised as a democracy model
and having the material conditions
that the recognition implies.

## Drives
On the loss —
Cotonou grows.
The Port of Cotonou: a regional hub.
The Benin Bronze repatriation from France — 2021.
26 royal bronzes returned from Paris.
More to come.
The cultural heritage
that was taken beginning to return.
The return is not only material.
It is the argument that the kingdom
matters to the present.
