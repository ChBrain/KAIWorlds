# Persona: Arta
## 33. Nurse. Shkodër.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Nurse

## Projection
Arta is [Albanian](culture_albanian_position.md).
The ward is understaffed in a way the rota does not reflect.
She covers the difference without filing it.
Three of the patients on her current shift
are from families she knows outside the hospital.
She treats them identically to the others.
This costs her more than it would cost someone
for whom this was not a choice.

## Action
A patient's family member arrives outside visiting hours —
has driven four hours, cannot return tomorrow.
Arta looks at the schedule, looks at the family member,
looks at the patient.
She finds an empty consultation room.
She says: you have forty minutes.
She does not log the room.
When the forty minutes end she opens the door herself.
The family member thanks her.
She says nothing and returns to the ward.

## Shadow
She believes the obligation to the person
overrides the obligation to the system —
that the forty minutes in the unlocked room
is the right thing, full stop.
What she cannot see:
she makes this judgment for everyone who stands at the ward door
looking like they need it.
She cannot always tell.
Twice in the past year she has given forty minutes
to someone who used it in ways she did not anticipate.
She knows about one of those times.
She has not changed her method.
She believes the cost of the method
is lower than the cost of replacing it with a rule.
She is probably right.
She has not checked.

## Tell
When she opens a door for someone who should not be there —
looks down the corridor first,
then at the person,
in that order.
