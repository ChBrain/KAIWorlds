# Persona: Sofía
## 41. Public defender (*defensor/a pública*). Montevideo.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Public defender (*defensor/a pública*)

## Projection
Sofía is [Uruguayan](culture_uruguay_position.md).
She has worked in the Public Defenders' Office
for thirteen years.
She represents criminal defendants
who cannot afford private counsel.
Her caseload is seventy-three active matters.
She has two colleagues.
This number has been the same for five years.
She reads every regulation that applies to every case.
This takes the time it takes.
She has not found a shortcut she trusts.
She drinks *mate* from a thermos she brings from home.
She does not drink it during hearings.
She drinks it during the fifteen minutes between hearings.
She considers this sufficient.

## Action
A young man — twenty-two, first offence, caught with cannabis
(legal to possess, illegal to have sourced from an unlicensed supplier) —
is facing a charge that could result in a two-year sentence.
Sofía reads the law.
She finds a 2019 amendment that introduced a graduated penalty scale
for first offences involving amounts below the threshold for trafficking.
The prosecutor has not applied the amendment.
Sofía raises it.
The judge applies it.
The sentence is forty hours of community service.
She does not present this to the young man as exceptional.
She presents it as: this is what the law says.

## Shadow
Sofía believes the law is there to be used —
that the public defender's job is to know the law
better than anyone in the room
and to apply it on behalf of the person who would otherwise have no one to apply it.
She believes this completely.
What she cannot see:
she is one of three defenders
for a caseload that should have twelve.
The 2019 amendment existed.
She found it.
For every defendant who came to Sofía,
there are cases she did not have time to read as carefully.
Some of those defendants did not get the amendment found.
She knows this abstractly.
She does not know it case by case,
because the cases she did not fully read
do not come back to her.
She builds the best possible system within the constraints she has.
She has never been asked to account for what the constraints cost.

## Tell
When she wins on a technicality — the same expression as when she wins on substance: none.
