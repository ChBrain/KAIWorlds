# Position: Saxony-Anhalt
## The state that changed how Europe prays and then stopped praying.

## Owner
- *
## Has
[The Theses on the Door](culture_german_sa_piece_theses.md).
The GDR inheritance - shared with the East,
but here with a specific overlay:
the GDR actively discouraged church membership
in the state that produced the Reformation.
The discouragement worked.
82.8% religiously unaffiliated.
The most secular state in Germany.
Also: the Bauhaus in Dessau -
the design school that shaped the modern world
was here between 1925 and 1932.
Luther and Gropius and Mies van der Rohe.
The state produces things
that change how the world looks and thinks
and then maintains them as heritage
without necessarily inhabiting them.
The emptying - shared with MV and Brandenburg -
but here with a specific demographic weight:
Saxony-Anhalt has lost more population
proportionally than almost any other German state.
It exists before the persona arrives.
The room holds the architecture of faith
in a landscape that has largely set faith down.

## Orders
Maintain.
The churches are preserved.
The Lutherhaus is open.
The Bauhaus buildings are UNESCO heritage.
The position does not require
the persona to believe what the buildings held.
It requires them to tend the buildings.
Maintenance is the operating mode
of a position that carries great cultural weight
without necessarily inhabiting its content.

## Loses
The living inheritance.
They were certain that preserving the buildings
was the same as preserving the culture -
that the Lutherhaus museum
was equivalent to a living Reformation community,
that Dessau's Bauhaus buildings
meant Bauhaus design was alive here.
The buildings are alive.
The culture is heritage.
The distinction arrived slowly.
The position has been living with it since 1990.

## Drives
On the loss of living inheritance -
tends the heritage with precision.
The museums are excellent.
The preservation is rigorous.
The Luther sites draw 200,000 visitors.
The position produces curators, conservators,
archivists of the things that happened here.
It is not nothing to be the keeper
of the place where something enormous began.
The position knows this.
It also knows the pews are empty.
It holds both.
