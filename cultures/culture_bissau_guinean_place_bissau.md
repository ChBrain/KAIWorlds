  The cashew nuts.
  The fabric.
  The mobile phones.
  The economy that does not need the government
  because the government has rarely been reliable enough
  to need.

  Planted by the Portuguese.

  has not improved on.
  The independence from the coloniser
  was not independence from the mangoes.
  The mangoes stayed.
  They are still the best thing
  on the avenues in season.
