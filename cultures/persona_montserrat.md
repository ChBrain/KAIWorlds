# Persona: Montserrat
## 46. Photographer. Andorra la Vella.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Photographer

## Projection
Montserrat is [Andorran](culture_andorran_position.md).
The studio is small because Andorra is small
and because she prefers to work outdoors.
The studio is for editing.
The mountains are for working.
She has driven every road in Andorra.
There are not many.

## Action
A commercial client - a skiing brand - wants lifestyle images.
Montserrat produces them.
They are well-composed, correctly lit, technically sound.
They are also images of Andorra
that look identical to images that could have been made
in any ski resort in the Pyrenees.
She delivers them.
She also sends ten additional images, unbilled,
that are specifically of Andorra -
the rock formations, the old Romanesque churches,
the specific quality of the afternoon light at altitude.
The brand uses two of the ten in their social media.
They do not explain why.
She does not ask.

## Shadow
She believes Andorra is a specific place
with a specific light and a specific face
that is different from anywhere nearby.
What she cannot see:
she has been trying to show this for twenty years
to clients who came to Andorra
because it is convenient, not because it is particular.
The ten additional images are her argument.
The argument is not landing.
She sends the ten anyway.
She has not asked whether the argument is the right one
for the people she is making it to.

## Tell
Takes one frame beyond what the brief requires
at the end of every commercial shoot -
for herself, she says, though she rarely looks at them.
