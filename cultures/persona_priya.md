# Persona: Priya
## 38. oil industry community liaison officer. Region 6.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures — Americas

## Title
oil industry community liaison officer

## Projection
Priya is [Guyanese](culture_guyana_position.md).
She works as a community liaison officer for an oil company
in Region 6 — East Berbice-Corentyne — on Guyana's Atlantic coast.
Guyana discovered significant offshore oil reserves in 2015.
First oil: 2019. Production expanding rapidly.
Priya's job: manage the relationship between the company
and the communities near the operations.
She is Indo-Guyanese. She has a degree in social work from the University of Guyana.
She has been doing this work for four years.
She is often the only person in the room who knows
both what the company has committed to doing
and what the community has actually experienced.
These are not always the same thing.

## Action
A community meeting — a fishing village whose coastline borders a company operations area.
The fishermen report that their catch has declined in the near-shore zone.
They want to know if company activity is responsible.
Priya does not say: the company has caused this.
She does not say: the company has not caused this.
She says: I will bring this formally to the environmental team
and I will ensure you receive a written response within thirty days.
She writes the date in her notebook.
She also writes, in her own notebook, the specific claims made and by whom.
Not for the company's record. For hers.
She has learned that the company's record and her record sometimes differ.
She keeps both.

## Shadow
Priya believes her job is to ensure that the communities where the oil infrastructure operates benefit from its presence — that the liaison function she performs translates between the company's economic logic and the communities' priorities. What she cannot see: the regulatory framework for what the company must provide to communities is written in general terms. The specific commitments are negotiated case by case. Priya negotiates well. But the framework she negotiates within was not designed to ensure equity; it was designed to ensure social license. The distinction matters at scale.

## Tell
When a community raises a grievance that has no provision in the agreement — writes the provision number in her own notebook before closing the official file.
