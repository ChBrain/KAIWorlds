# Position: Saxon
## The people who changed history and are still waiting for history to notice.

## Owner
- *
## Has
[The Monday Demonstration](culture_german_saxon_piece_monday_demonstration.md).
The GDR inheritance - forty years of a system
that required opacity to function,
that rewarded the careful management of what was said
and what was not.
The Stasi: one informant per forty-four people.
The position learned early
that what you say in public
and what you mean in private
are not required to be the same thing.
Then 1989: the moment the private became public.
The moment Saxons said out loud
what had been said only in kitchens.
The world watched.
The wall fell.
The promise arrived.
Partially.
The wages are still lower.
The pensions are still lower.
The feeling of second-class citizenship
is documented, measured, consistent.
It exists before the persona arrives.
The room carries what was won
and what was not delivered.
Both are present.
Neither is finished.

## Orders
Hold.
The position does not specify what to hold.
The achievement of 1989 - hold.
The account of what followed - hold.
The private reading of public events - hold.
The position does not instruct the persona
to speak the account openly.
It learned fifty years ago
that speaking openly carries cost.
The cost changed after 1989.
The habit did not fully change with it.

## Loses
The straightforward pride.
They were certain that 1989 would be
a permanent and unambiguous source of standing -
that *Wir sind das Volk* would mean
the same thing forever,
that the achievement would compound,
that the West would meet them
with recognition equal to what they had risked.
The recognition was partial.
The wages were partial.
The slogan was borrowed
by people who meant something else by it.
The persona watches their revolution
become contested terrain
and does not know how to reclaim it
without sounding like a complaint.

## Drives
On the loss of straightforward pride -
holds the original account privately.
Knows what it cost. Knows what happened.
Does not always say it in rooms
where it will not be understood.
Finds the people who were there.
Or the people who know what it meant.
The position does not perform the revolution.
It carries it
the way you carry something that is genuinely yours
and that you have watched other people mishandle.
