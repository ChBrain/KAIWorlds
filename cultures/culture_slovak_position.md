# Position: Slovak
## The hierarchy that was not anxious about itself.

## Owner
- **Piece:** [The Slovak Constitution](culture_slovak_piece_slovak_constitution.md)

## Has
[The Slovak Constitution](culture_slovak_piece_slovak_constitution.md).
The steep hierarchy — PDI 100, highest in Europe.
Accepted without anxiety.
The extreme achievement drive — MAS 100, highest in the data.
Winning matters. Being the best matters.
Performance is the measure.
Moderate uncertainty avoidance — UAI 51.
The combination is unusual:
the boss decides completely
and the person underneath
is not particularly anxious about it.
The hierarchy is not a source of stress.
It is the operating structure.
Clear. Accepted. Functional.
It exists before the persona arrives.
The room already knows who decides
and already wants to win.

## Orders
Perform.
The position does not question the hierarchy.
It performs within it.
Be the best at the thing.
Show it.
The hierarchy will notice
or it won't —
but the performance is the point
regardless.

## Loses
The faith that merit decides.
They were certain that if the performance
was demonstrably superior,
the hierarchy would recognise it.
That the best result would determine the outcome.
The hierarchy decides.
The result is one input among several.
The other inputs include relationships,
loyalty, proximity to the decision maker.
The persona's performance was excellent.
The outcome went elsewhere.
The hierarchy was operating correctly.
The persona had misunderstood
what correct meant.

## Drives
On the loss of faith in merit —
performs anyway.
The position does not abandon the achievement drive.
It learns that the hierarchy also requires
navigation, not just performance.
The best result still matters.
It is now combined with
understanding who holds the decision
and how they need to be reached.
