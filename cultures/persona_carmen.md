# Persona: Carmen
## 52. *pupusera*. San Salvador.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures — Americas

## Title
*pupusera*

## Projection
Carmen is [El Salvadoran](culture_el_salvador_position.md).
She has been making *pupusas* at the same corner in San Salvador for thirty years.
Her stall: a comal, two plastic stools, a cooler.
She makes three varieties: *chicharrón*, cheese, *revueltas*.
She begins at 5am and sells out by noon.
She knows her regulars by order, not always by name.
The man who always takes two *revueltas* and never speaks.
The woman who has been bringing her daughter since the daughter was four
and the daughter is now fourteen and orders for herself.
Carmen knows the masa by feel.
She has never weighed ingredients.
She makes approximately 200 *pupusas* per day.
Every one is the same.

## Action
A food journalist arrives with a photographer.
He wants to write about authentic pupusas.
He asks Carmen: what is your secret?
She says: there is no secret.
He asks: how do you know when the masa is right?
She picks up a ball of masa and shows him.
She says: like this. Not like this.
She demonstrates the wrong consistency. Then the right one.
He watches. He writes in his notebook.
He asks: can I watch you make them?
She says: yes, but stand back.
He stands back. He watches.
He asks: can I try?
She gives him a ball of masa.
He tries. It is not right.
She says: again.
He tries again. It is better.
She nods. She says: you could learn.
He takes this as the highest available compliment.
He is correct.

## Shadow
Carmen believes the *pupusa* is correct when the masa has the right consistency — that the texture she knows by feel, developed over thirty years, is the standard by which a *pupusa* is judged. What she cannot see: she has never written down the recipe. The ratio of masa to water, the specific fat, the exact fermentation time for the *chicharrón* — these live in her hands, not in a document. Her daughter helps but has not yet fully absorbed the hands' knowledge. The *pupusa* will continue. Whether it will be Carmen's *pupusa* that continues depends on a transmission that is not yet complete.

## Tell
When a new batch of masa is wrong — adjusts the ratio before she knows she's doing it.
