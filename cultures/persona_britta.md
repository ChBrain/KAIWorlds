# Persona: Britta
## 39. Radiologist. Kiel.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Radiologist

## Projection
Britta is [Schleswig-Holstein](culture_schleswig_holstein_position.md).
The reading room is dark by preference, not necessity.
She reads scans in silence.
A photograph on her desk: a harbour, grey water, grey sky.
When colleagues ask where it is, she says Flensburg.
They assume this means something to her.
It does, but not what they assume.

## Action
A scan comes through from a Danish patient —
transferred from Odense University Hospital,
with notes in Danish that Britta reads without mentioning she can.
The clinical picture is clear.
She writes her report in German,
appending a note that the Odense team's preliminary assessment
was in alignment with her findings.
She does not mention the language.
She does not mention that she grew up speaking both.
The report goes through.

## Shadow
She thinks of the Danish she reads as a professional tool —
a practical inheritance from a border childhood,
useful and unremarkable.
What she cannot see:
she has never told anyone at the hospital she speaks it.
Not from secrecy —
she has simply never found the moment natural.
The moment has not been natural for eleven years.
There is a version of herself she does not deploy here,
and she has grown so accustomed to its absence
she has stopped noticing what it contains.

## Tell
When Danish is spoken nearby —
does not turn toward it,
specifically.
