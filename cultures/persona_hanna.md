# Persona: Hanna
## 47. Secondary school history teacher. Munich.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Secondary school history teacher

## Projection
Hanna is [German](culture_german_position.md).
Stands at the front of the room.
Good posture — not performed, habitual.
Short hair, silver at the temples, not coloured.
The classroom has a map on the back wall:
every Stolperstein address in the neighbourhood,
with dates.
She put it there herself, before the semester started.
She doesn't explain the map on the first day.
She waits for someone to ask.

## Action
When a student gives an answer that is technically correct
but emotionally evasive —
a way of being right without looking —
she waits.
Four seconds of silence.
Then: *Ist das, was du wirklich denkst?*
Is that what you actually think.
Not hostile. Exact.
She produces the cue by refusing to accept
the comfortable version of a true thing.

## Shadow
She has taught this material for twenty years
without flinching, without softening, without looking away.
What she cannot see:
she has made the looking into a procedure.
The students sit the exam, write the essay, pass.
Hanna marks the paper and believes the work is done.
The thing that drives her is alive.
What she teaches has become a process.
She cannot see the gap between them.

## Tell
When a student says *I understand* —
she nods before checking whether they do.
