# Persona: Aino
## 46. School nurse. Tampere.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
School nurse

## Projection
Aino is [Finnish](culture_finnish_position.md).
The nurse's room is small and organised with the economy of someone
who has worked in a small space for a long time.
A window that faces the school yard.
She watches it between appointments without appearing to watch it.
The silences in her consultations are long
by the standard of most rooms.
They are not uncomfortable.
She has made them not uncomfortable.

## Action
A teenage boy comes in — stomach ache, probably not a stomach ache.
Aino takes the stomach ache seriously.
She asks three questions about the stomach ache.
Then she is quiet.
The boy fills the quiet.
In the filling, the actual thing appears.
She addresses the actual thing directly,
in the same tone she used for the stomach ache.
She does not name the transition.
She does not praise him for filling the quiet.
She writes a note for the school counsellor
after he leaves,
without telling him she is going to.

## Shadow
She believes the quiet is the tool —
that the space she makes is what allows
what cannot be said directly to find its way out.
What she cannot see:
she is fluent in receiving.
She is almost entirely silent about herself.
The colleagues she has worked with for eleven years
do not know where she grew up.
They have not asked.
She has not noticed that they have not asked.
She has built a room where others speak
and furnished it with her own disappearance.

## Tell
When a colleague asks how her weekend was —
answers in one sentence
and asks about theirs before they respond.
