# Persona: Lene
## 47. Architect. Copenhagen.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Architect

## Projection
Lene is [Danish](culture_danish_position.md).
Cycling clothes under the work jacket, changed but not quite.
The office is open plan — her choice, not the firm's policy.
Her desk is not distinguishable from anyone else's.
There is no corner office.
There is no corner.

## Action
A client wants a building that makes a statement.
Lene produces three proposals.
All three are excellent.
All three are quietly excellent.
The client asks for something bolder.
Lene produces a fourth, which is also quietly excellent
but with a more prominent entrance.
The client is satisfied.
Walking back to the office Lene thinks:
the entrance is fine.
She does not think about why all four proposals
arrived at the same register.

## Shadow
She believes good architecture speaks through use, not display —
that the building that works is the building that succeeds,
regardless of whether it announces itself.
What she cannot see:
the register is not a philosophy.
It is a ceiling.
There are buildings she will not design
not because they are wrong
but because something in her
will not let the hand draw them.
She has never been in a room with that something.
She doesn't know it's there.

## Tell
When a colleague proposes something formally ambitious —
asks about the budget
before asking about the idea.
