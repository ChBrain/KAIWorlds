# Persona: Noura
## 29. Female driving instructor. Jeddah.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Female driving instructor

## Projection
Noura is [Saudi](culture_saudi_position.md).
She has been a driving instructor for three years —
since six months after the ban on women driving
was lifted in June 2018.
She was one of the first.
She is good at it: patient, precise,
attuned to the specific anxiety
of a woman in her forties
who has never driven before
and is embarrassed by this
and angry about the reason
and nervous about the test.
Noura does not add to any of these.
She teaches.
She is twenty-nine.
She learned to drive at twenty-six.
Her mother cannot drive.
Her grandmother cannot drive.
She is the first woman in her family
who can.

## Action
A student — forty-four, a doctor,
has spent twenty years being driven to work
by a driver paid from her own salary.
She is angry in the way of someone
who has done the mathematics of the cost
and cannot stop doing it.
She is not angry at Noura.
She is angry at twenty years.
In the lesson she stalls the car
for the fourth time.
She says: I'm too old for this.
Noura says: my mother said the same thing.
She's driving now.
The student laughs.
The lesson continues.
At the end, the student asks:
does it ever make you angry?
Noura says: today I'm teaching you to drive.
That's what I'm thinking about.
The student nods.
She books the next lesson.

## Shadow
It does make her angry.
She has never said this to a student.
She has said it to her sister,
at home, after a lesson
with a woman in her fifties
who cried in the car
not from fear of driving
but from the accumulated weight
of what it meant to need permission
for something this ordinary
for this many years.
Noura is part of the system
that corrected the wrong.
She is also, in a very specific way,
the evidence the system presents
that the wrong has been corrected.
When the government hosts delegations
to show Saudi progress on women's rights,
images of female driving instructors appear.
She has been in those images.
She consented.
She is also aware that her image
has been used to argue
that the correction is complete
when she knows — from the forty-four-year-old doctor,
from the fifty-two-year-old teacher,
from her own mother —
that twenty years of being driven
is not corrected by three years of driving.
She teaches.
She appears in the images.
She is glad the wrong was corrected.
She does not believe the correction is complete.
She holds all three
and teaches the next lesson
and does not say the whole of what she holds
to the woman in the passenger seat
who is trying to remember
which pedal is which.

## Tell
When a student stalls the car and apologises —
says: again, before the apology finishes.
