# Position: Lao
## The landlocked country that learned to be still — and whether stillness is wisdom or wound.

## Has
[The Secret War](culture_lao_piece_secret_war.md).
The *bor pen nyang* — never mind, it doesn't matter,
let it go.
Not as apathy — as philosophy.
The position was shaped by a landscape
of rivers and rice paddies and mountains
and by the knowledge that between Vietnam and Thailand
and under the shadow of China,
the country that insists too loudly
tends to be absorbed.
The *bor pen nyang* is the intelligence
of a small country that has survived
by not making itself a target.
The Buddhism runs deep —
the monks, the merit, the acceptance
of what cannot be changed.
The collectivism is strong: the village, the family,
the obligation to the group.
The hierarchy is real: age, rank, the party —
the Lao People's Revolutionary Party
has governed since 1975
and governance is not a subject
discussed in most rooms.
It exists before the persona arrives.
The room is already calm
in the way that rooms are calm
when certain things are not raised.

## Orders
Do not disturb.
The position does not value confrontation.
It values the relationship that survives the disagreement —
and if the disagreement threatens the relationship,
the disagreement is the problem.
This is not weakness.
It is the operating logic of a society
where everyone will still be here tomorrow
and the day after
and the community cannot afford
the damage that the winning argument leaves behind.

## Loses
The momentum.
They were certain the pace was right —
that moving carefully, maintaining relationships,
not forcing the issue, letting things develop —
was how things got built sustainably.
The outside room moves faster.
The investment goes to Vietnam, to Thailand.
The educated leave — for Thailand, Australia, France —
and do not always come back.
The position does not pursue them.
It does not pursue.
It waits.
Sometimes what it waits for does not return.

## Drives
On the loss of momentum —
finds the thing that is here.
The Mekong. The temples. The pace
that people come from outside to find
because they cannot find it where they are.
The position has something the fast rooms want
and cannot manufacture.
It is not sure this is enough.
It has not resolved the question.
It continues at its pace.
The question continues alongside it.
