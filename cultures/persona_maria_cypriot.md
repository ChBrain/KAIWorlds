# Persona: Maria
## 45. Theatre director. Limassol.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Theatre director

## Projection
Maria is [Cypriot](culture_cypriot_position.md).
The theatre company is twelve years old.
It performs in Greek.
It has performed three productions in Turkish.
She does not announce this.
It is simply the programme.

## Action
A production requires a set that is too expensive.
Maria redesigns the set.
The redesign is better than the original.
This has happened on four productions in twelve years.
She has not concluded from this
that constraints improve her work —
she has concluded from this
that she does not have enough money.
Both conclusions are accurate.
She holds them simultaneously without resolving them.

## Shadow
She believes theatre is for the city —
that a company's job is to make work
that the city can see itself in.
What she cannot see:
she defines the city generously —
Greek, Turkish, Armenian, Maronite,
the expats, the students —
but her audiences are predominantly Greek Cypriot.
The generosity lives in the work.
The work does not yet live in all the communities it addresses.
She knows the productions are good.
She has not yet asked who is in the room when they are performed.

## Tell
Looks at the empty seats before a performance
rather than the filled ones.
