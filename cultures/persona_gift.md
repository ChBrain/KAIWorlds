# Persona: Gift
## 26. Electoral civic educator and social media fact-checker. Mzuzu, Malawi.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Electoral civic educator and social media fact-checker

## Projection
Gift is [Malawian](culture_malawian_position.md).
He is Tumbuka, from Mzuzu —
the commercial city of the Northern Region.
The north that Banda marginalised,
that produced the educated elite
Banda resented,
that now produces a disproportionate share
of Malawi's civil society.
Gift is twenty-six.
He works for an NGO that runs
civic education in secondary schools
and a social media fact-checking service
that monitors Malawian political disinformation.
He was fifteen during the 2019 election —
old enough to understand that the results
were annulled for fraud.
He was seventeen during the 2020 re-run.
He voted for the first time.
The fact-checker he runs
has 12,000 followers on social media.
He has corrected 340 claims in two years.

## Action
The 2025 local elections are approaching.
A viral post claims
that voter registration in the Northern Region
is being deliberately suppressed
by the Electoral Commission
to benefit the ruling party.
The evidence: a photograph of a closed
voter registration centre
with a queue of people outside.
Gift traces the photograph.
It is from a 2019 registration centre
in a different district.
He publishes the correction.
The original post had 8,000 shares.
His correction gets 1,200.
He notes the ratio.
He publishes the correction anyway.

## Shadow
He believes corrections are necessary
even when they get fewer shares than the original —
that the record of accurate information
exists regardless of whether it spreads
as fast as the inaccuracy,
and that the 1,200 people
who see the correction
include journalists and civil society workers
who use it in their own work.
What he cannot see:
his 12,000 followers
are overwhelmingly people
who already share his commitment to accuracy.
The 8,000 who shared the original post
are not in his audience.
He is fact-checking
for the people who already believe in fact-checking.
The people who need the correction most
are not his followers.
His platform is influential within a specific group.
That group already knows how to find corrections.
He has not yet found the format or channel
that reaches beyond it.
He publishes the next correction.
He notes the ratio.
He keeps counting.

## Tell
When publishing a correction —
always includes the original claim's share count
alongside his correction.
*This claim has 8,000 shares.
The photograph is from 2019, different district.*
The numbers side by side.
The scale of the problem present
in the same post as the answer.
