# Piece: The Declaration of the Rights of Man and of the Citizen
## La Déclaration des droits de l'homme et du citoyen - the argument that France makes about itself.

## Owner
- **Place:** [Paris](culture_french_place_paris.md)

## Place
[Paris](culture_french_place_paris.md).
The Archives nationales. Rue des Quatre-Fils.
In a humidity-controlled case.
The parchment is brown. The ink has faded.
The argument has not.

## Load Bearing
Without it the French position loses its ground.
The intellectual frame - every claim provisional until pressed,
the argument as form of care -
is not merely cultural habit.
It is the operating mode
of a culture that declared universal principles
in 1789 and has been arguing about what they mean
every day since.
Remove the Declaration and the French position
loses the shared text the argument returns to.
The argument would continue.
But it would lose its address.

## Apparent
A parchment document. Seventeen articles.
Written August 1789 by the National Constituent Assembly.
The original in the Archives nationales.
The text reproduced on the walls of the Assemblée nationale.
Article 1: *Les hommes naissent et demeurent libres et égaux en droits.*
Men are born and remain free and equal in rights.
Followed immediately by sixteen more articles
that argue about what this means in practice.
The arguing was built into the structure.

## Yearbook
Adopted 26 August 1789.
The revolution was already in progress.
The Bastille had fallen six weeks earlier.
The Declaration preceded the Constitution by two years.
The principles came first. The institutions followed.
1793: a second Declaration, longer, more radical.
Also superseded.
1948: the Universal Declaration of Human Rights at the UN.
The 1789 text was the explicit model.
France has referenced it in legal challenges
in its own courts, in European courts,
and in every political argument about what France is
for two hundred and thirty-five years.
The text is load-bearing not because it is followed precisely
but because it is the shared address
that every disagreement returns to.
