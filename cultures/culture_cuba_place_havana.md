## Shown
The Malecón: the seawall. The couples in the evening. The sea spray.
The 1950s American cars — maintained because they have been maintained,
not because they are aesthetically nostalgic.
Old Havana: the UNESCO-listed colonial city. The restoration, partially complete.
The buildings that are restored and the buildings beside them that are not.
The Capitol building: modelled on Washington's. Now the National Assembly.
The Plaza de la Revolución: the Che Guevara image on the Ministry of the Interior.
The José Martí memorial.
The *bodega*: the ration store. The *libreta* — the ration book.
Still in use. The monthly allocation of rice, beans, cooking oil.
The neighbourhood: the *cuadra*. The life that happens on the street.
