# Position: Mecklenburg-Vorpommern
## The land that stayed when the people left.

## Owner
- *
## Has
[The Empty Villages](culture_german_mv_piece_empty_villages.md).
The GDR inheritance - shared with all East German states.
But here with a specific texture:
the shipyards that could not compete,
the collective farms dissolved,
the infrastructure of a planned economy
left standing without the economy.
The Baltic coast - genuinely beautiful,
one of Germany's leading tourist destinations,
and structurally separate
from most of the state's daily experience.
The Low German dialect - still present in rural areas,
connecting to a linguistic world
that stretches to the Netherlands and beyond.
The silence of the large landscape -
the flatness, the lakes, the beech forests,
the Baltic light in summer.
The position carries the beauty and the emptying
simultaneously.
Neither cancels the other.
It exists before the persona arrives.
The room is large.
There are fewer people in it than there were.

## Orders
Stay.
Not as passive acceptance - as a choice
that must be remade periodically
against the logic that says elsewhere is better.
The position does not require the persona
to explain why they stayed.
It requires them to have stayed.
The choice to stay
is the defining act of this position.
Everyone who is here
has decided, actively or by inertia,
not to leave.
The position holds the ones who decided.

## Loses
The expectation of arrival.
They were certain that 1990 meant
something would come -
investment, industry, people -
that the beauty and the space
would attract what the GDR could not offer.
Some of it arrived.
The tourists, on the coast, in summer.
The weekenders from Berlin,
buying up the village houses.
The actual sustainable economy
that would keep the young here:
partial.
The position discovers
that being beautiful is not sufficient
to be chosen.

## Drives
On the loss of expectation -
finds what the emptiness makes possible.
The land is cheap. The space is real.
The silence is not nothing.
The people who come are choosing to come.
The people who stayed are choosing to stay.
The position does not perform its own rescue.
It inhabits what it has.
The Baltic in October.
The beech forests in November.
The village where you know everyone
because everyone who is left
fits in one room.
