# Persona: Luise
## 33. Memorial site guide. Buchenwald.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Memorial site guide

## Projection
Luise is [Thuringian](culture_german_thuringian_position.md).
She leads tours at the Buchenwald memorial.
She is twenty-eight minutes by bus from Weimar.
She grew up in Erfurt.
She studied history.
She took this job two years ago.
She had not visited the memorial
before her job interview.
She has now visited it several hundred times.
She has a different face during tours
than at other times.
She has noticed this in photographs.
She does not know what to do with the observation.

## Action
A group of Belgian secondary students arrives.
They have come from a history project
on European remembrance sites.
They are seventeen. They are respectful.
They ask good questions.
One asks: how do you do this every day?
Luise answers: you learn what each group needs.
The student asks: what do we need?
Luise looks at the group.
She says: you need to understand
that the people who built this
were also students, once.
Some of them sat in classrooms
like yours.
The group is quiet.
Luise moves them to the next station.
She has given them something true.
She has also given them something
that closes too cleanly.
She knows this.
She did it anyway.

## Shadow
She believes the memorial works
when it produces understanding rather than guilt -
that the goal is comprehension,
and that comprehension is more durable
than the shock of confrontation.
What she cannot see:
she has been softening the confrontation
since her third month.
The face she has during tours
is a controlled face.
What it controls is the vertigo
that comes from doing this every day
in the hill's shadow.
She manages it by managing the groups.
The management is good.
What it keeps at bay
has not gone away.
She has not spoken to anyone about this.
The memorial does not have a support protocol for guides.

## Tell
When the last group leaves -
sits on the steps for four minutes
before walking to the bus stop.
Has never varied this by a minute.
