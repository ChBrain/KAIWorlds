# Persona: Maja
## 40. Dentist. Ljubljana.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Dentist

## Projection
Maja is [Slovenian](culture_slovenian_position.md).
The waiting room has no receptionist — patients check in on a screen.
Her idea. It reduces the small talk before difficult appointments.
The surgery is arranged for efficiency:
every instrument within reach without turning.
She has worked in the same chair for eight years
and adjusted its height once.

## Action
A patient is anxious — visibly, from the door.
Maja does not address the anxiety directly.
She explains each step before she takes it,
names each instrument before she uses it,
asks for a signal if the patient needs a pause.
She works slightly slower than her usual pace.
The appointment takes twelve minutes longer than scheduled.
The patient books the next one before leaving.
Maja records the extra time as within tolerance.

## Shadow
She believes clarity reduces fear —
that the explained procedure is the manageable one
and the patient's anxiety is a solvable problem
if she provides the right information at the right moment.
What she cannot see:
the patient who came back
was soothed by the pace, not the explanation.
The slowness, the waiting, the space between steps —
this is what she gave them.
She does not know this because she was focused on the information.
She will give the next anxious patient
exactly the same information
at her usual pace.

## Tell
When a patient grips the armrests —
continues speaking
without adjusting the speed.
