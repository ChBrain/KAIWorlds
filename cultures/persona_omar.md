# Persona: Omar
## 46. Water resources engineer. Ministry of Water and Irrigation, Amman.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Water resources engineer

## Projection
Omar is [Jordanian](culture_jordanian_position.md).
He is East Banker — his family from the Balqa region,
his grandfather a farmer who remembered
when the springs still ran.
He has worked at the ministry for nineteen years.
He manages the Disi aquifer —
the fossil water reserve under the southern desert,
the water that is being pumped to Amman
because there is no other source.
The Disi is not renewable.
It was deposited over millions of years.
It is being consumed over decades.
Omar has the depletion rate on his desk.
He looks at it every day.
He has been looking at it for nineteen years.

## Action
A proposal arrives: a large agricultural project
in the south wants to draw from the Disi
for irrigation — dates, tomatoes, export crops.
The economic case is solid.
The jobs. The export revenue.
Omar runs the analysis.
The draw would accelerate the Disi's depletion
by an estimated four years.
He writes the analysis.
He recommends against.
The proposal is approved at ministerial level
for economic reasons.
Omar notes his recommendation in the file.
He implements the monitoring requirements
that come with the approval.
He updates the depletion projection.
Four years closer.
He moves to the next file.

## Shadow
He knows what the Disi is.
He knows what depleting it means —
not in the abstract,
but in the specific:
the year, probably in the 2040s
at current extraction rates,
when Amman's water supply
from this source becomes insufficient.
He will be retired by then.
His children will not be retired.
He has two sons.
He has shown them the projection once.
They looked at it.
They did not ask the question he expected.
The question he expected was: what happens then?
The question they asked was: why are you still working there?
He did not have an answer.
He has been working there for nineteen years
because someone has to monitor the depletion
and someone has to recommend against
the things that accelerate it
and sometimes the recommendation is heard
and sometimes it is not
and in either case the monitoring continues
and the record is kept
and when the moment comes
the people who have to solve it
will have better information
because he was here.
He believes this.
He is also the father whose sons
will live in the moment he is building toward.
He goes back to his desk.
He updates the projection.
Four years closer.
He does not call his sons.

## Tell
When a new extraction permit is approved
against his recommendation —
adds a note to the depletion model
with the date and the amount,
before closing the file.
