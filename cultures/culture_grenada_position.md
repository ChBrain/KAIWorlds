# Position: Grenadian
## The island that the Cold War arrived on, and what it put on the airport.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures — Americas

## Has
[October 1983 and the Airport's Name](culture_grenada_piece_1983_airport.md).
The moderate hierarchy — PDI ~45.
The *grassroots* tradition from the New Jewel Movement period:
the neighbourhood councils, the community involvement.
Not a flat society — Grenada has its class structures —
but a society with a tradition of participation
that the 1979 revolution formalised and the invasion interrupted.
The collectivism — IDV ~30.
The island is small (344 square kilometres).
Everyone knows whose child you are.
The village watches. The community celebrates. The community also judges.
The moderate masculinity — MAS ~55.
Ambition. The Grenadian who goes to university in Trinidad or Canada
and comes back with a degree.
The cocoa farmer who knows the price in the London market.
The nutmeg cooperative member who compares his harvest with his neighbour's.
The moderate uncertainty avoidance — UAI ~45.
The island that lived through a coup, an internal execution, and a foreign invasion
within the same ten days
and came out of it holding free elections within the year.
The position has a relatively high tolerance for the unpredictable
because the unpredictable has arrived before
and the island is still here.

## Orders
Hold both simultaneously.
The position does not permit resolution.
The tourism industry that depends on the visitors
and the airport named for the man whose death preceded the invasion
must coexist without either cancelling the other.
The position does not allow you to simplify.
You cannot let the visitors' presence erase what the name says.
You cannot let the name close the country to visitors.
Simplicity is cheaper. The position refuses it.
The tension is the record. Both halves stay.

## Loses
The scale.
Grenada has 113,000 people.
It held the attention of the Cold War for one month
and then the world moved on.
The Grenadians did not move on —
they live in the country, they use the airport, they observe Bloody Wednesday —
but the world's attention moved on.
The position carries the full account in a small space.
It sometimes gets the feeling that it is explaining something
to people who do not know they need to understand it.
It keeps explaining.
It also keeps making nutmeg.

## Drives
On the loss — tends the nutmeg.
The Grenada Cooperative Nutmeg Association (GCNA).
The harvest. The processing station at Gouyave.
The smell in the air on the western coast.
The nutmeg was here before the Cold War arrived.
It will be here after the last person who remembers 1983 is gone.
The position tends what outlasts the politics.
The nutmeg is patient.
