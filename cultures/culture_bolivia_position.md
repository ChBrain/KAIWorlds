# Position: Bolivian
## The country that wrote the earth into its constitution, and is still learning what that means.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures — Americas

## Has
[The 2009 Constitution](culture_bolivia_piece.md).
The *ayllu* not just as survival mechanism
but as cosmological framework:
the community is not separate from the land;
the land is a member of the community.
*Pachamama* is not a deity above the earth.
She is the earth.
The *ch'alla* — the ritual offering to her before any significant act.
Before planting. Before building. Before beginning.
The position does not distinguish
between what it does to the earth
and what it does to itself.
The high hierarchy — PDI 80.
The authority of the *apu* — the community elder, also the mountain spirit.
In Bolivia, the hierarchy of the community
and the hierarchy of the landscape
are the same hierarchy.
The *jilaqata* — the community leader, rotating annually —
serves the community and the *Pachamama* simultaneously.
The high uncertainty avoidance — UAI 87.
The ritual as the certainty.
The *ch'alla* performed before the uncertain event
not to prevent uncertainty
but to establish the correct relationship
with the forces that govern outcomes.
The short-term normative orientation — LTO 21.
Tradition. The ancestors. The mountain.
The *Pachamama* does not change.
The community renews its relationship with her annually.
The position is oriented toward the renewal, not the future.
The future will be what the earth and the community make together.

## Orders
Do the *ch'alla* — and find the ceremony for what is new.
The *ch'alla* was written for a stable earth.
The earth is changing under conditions the ceremony did not anticipate.
Esperanza performs the correct relationship with an entity
that is under external assault.
The order demands: hold the ceremony.
And also: ask what the ceremony should say
to the forces that are changing the earth.
Zenón files the recommendation.
The recommendation cites Article 33.
The order demands: also ask
whether Article 33 has ever been enforced
against a state-owned mining joint venture,
and if not, what tool has.
The *ch'alla* is the relationship.
The law is a different relationship.
Both must be performed.
One is not enough.

## Loses
The credit for what it built.
The *ayllu* system is one of the most durable forms of community organisation ever devised.
It survived the Inca, the Spanish, the republic, and structural adjustment.
The *aynuqa* — the communal land rotation system —
maintains soil fertility without modern agronomy.
The *ch'uno* — freeze-dried potatoes — is a food preservation technology
that functions at 4,000 metres with no refrigeration.
All of this exists.
None of it is called civilisation by the institutions that measure civilisation.
The 2009 constitution named it.
The world did not significantly revise its definitions.
The position holds this with the patience
of something that has been waiting 500 years
and is not in a hurry.

## Drives
On the loss — *ch'alla* and begin again.
The *Pachamama* is not discouraged.
The position takes its orientation from her.
The work continues.
The offering continues.
The *ayllu* continues.
The constitution is the most recent layer
of something that has been continuous
since before the first layer anyone outside Bolivia counts.
