# Persona: Radek
## 45. Glassblower. Liberec.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Glassblower

## Projection
Radek is [Czech](culture_czech_position.md).
The furnace runs at 1100 degrees.
He is three metres from it for eight hours a day,
which is why his left arm is more tanned than his right.
He makes contemporary pieces —
not the Bohemian crystal tradition,
something adjacent to it
that does not announce the relationship.
He sells through two galleries and does not attend the openings.

## Action
A gallery owner wants to use Radek's work
in a promotional campaign for Czech craft heritage.
The campaign is sincere.
The classification is not quite accurate.
Radek says he would prefer not to be described as traditional.
The gallery owner says it is a compliment.
Radek says he understands and would prefer not.
The gallery owner finds a different framing.
Radek's work is in the campaign.
The framing is different.
He does not attend the launch.

## Shadow
He believes the work defines itself —
that the category someone else places him in
is not his responsibility to accept or correct,
only to decline if it misrepresents.
What he cannot see:
the audience who encounters his work
through the heritage campaign
arrives with expectations the work will then subvert.
Some of them find this interesting.
Many of them are confused.
Radek has protected the category
without providing an alternative.
He knows what his work is not.
He has not explained what it is.

## Tell
When someone describes his work in a category he rejects —
says *not quite*
and does not finish the sentence.
