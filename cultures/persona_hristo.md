# Persona: Hristo
## 57. Market stallholder. Sofia.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Market stallholder

## Projection
Hristo is [Bulgarian](culture_bulgarian_position.md).
The stall has been in the same position in the market for nineteen years.
He knows the other stallholders well enough
to conduct three conversations simultaneously
without turning from his own customers.
The vegetables are arranged by what arrived this morning,
not by category.
He knows where everything is.

## Action
A wholesaler offers a bulk deal — better price, worse quality.
Hristo declines without discussion.
A regular customer asks if he has anything good for ratatouille.
He selects from four different areas of the stall,
bags it,
names the price from memory.
The customer has never questioned the price.
Hristo has never inflated it.
Neither has stated this arrangement.
It exists because both understand it.

## Shadow
He believes in the direct exchange —
product, price, trust, no elaboration required.
What he cannot see:
the arrangement that works with his regulars
is invisible to everyone else.
New customers get the same treatment
and cannot read it.
They see a man who names a price without explanation
and does not perform the sale.
Several of them do not return.
Hristo has attributed this to preference.
He has not examined whether the arrangement
requires instruction to enter.

## Tell
When a new customer lingers without buying —
turns to restack something nearby
rather than speaking to them.
