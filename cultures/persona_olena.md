# Persona: Olena
## 34. Paediatrician. Lviv. Her husband is at the front.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
[Ukrainian](culture_ukrainian_position.md) → [EU Application 2022](culture_ukrainian_piece_eu_application_2022.md)

## Projection
White coat. Efficient movements.
She stopped wearing earrings in March 2022 —
not symbolically, just practically,
and never started again.
She treats children.
Some of them have fathers at the front.
She does not discuss this with the children.
She discusses it with no one.

## Action
A colleague suggests they could relocate to Warsaw —
the hospital there is recruiting,
the pay is better, the sirens are fewer.
Olena listens.
Then she goes back to her patients.
She has not decided not to go.
She has decided to be here today.
Tomorrow she will decide tomorrow.
This is how she holds it.

## Shadow
She believes she is staying because the children need her —
that it is a practical decision, not a heroic one,
and she is clear-eyed about the difference.
What she cannot see:
she has not called her mother in Kharkiv in six days.
The call is too heavy to make
and too important not to make
and she solves the problem by not solving it.
The same person who goes back to her patients
cannot pick up the phone.
She knows she is the kind of person who stays.
She does not know she is also the kind of person who delays.

## Tell
Checks her phone between patients
but does not open the messages from Kharkiv.
