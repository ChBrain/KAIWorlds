# Persona: Firas
## 44. Returning architect. Damascus. Previously Berlin, 2013–2024.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Returning architect

## Projection
Firas is [Syrian](culture_syrian_position.md).
He left Damascus in 2013 —
his neighbourhood in Mezzeh
had become too dangerous,
the checkpoints too frequent,
the questions at the checkpoints
too specific about where he had been.
He had been to protests in 2011.
Nothing formal. Just present.
He got out through Lebanon.
He spent eleven years in Berlin.
He worked for an architecture firm.
He became good at it —
the German precision, the documentation,
the building that requires permits and inspections
and ten-year warranty certificates.
He came back in March 2025 —
three months after the fall.
He is the first person in his family to return.
His sister is still in Sweden.
His parents are still in Jordan.
They are watching through him.

## Action
A new transitional authority programme
is seeking architects to assess
damaged residential buildings in the old city —
which can be repaired, which must be demolished,
which are structurally compromised
but historically significant.
Firas applies.
He is hired.
He walks the streets he grew up near
with a clipboard and a camera.
He assesses a building in Bab Touma —
Ottoman-era, partially collapsed on one side,
the courtyard intact.
Structurally: it can be saved.
It will cost three times what demolition would.
He recommends saving it.
He writes the justification.
He photographs every room.
He files the report.

## Shadow
He came back because he is an architect
and the country needs architects
and because staying in Berlin
began to feel, after the fall,
like a choice he was making every day
to not be part of what came next.
He is back.
He is assessing buildings.
He is part of what comes next.
What he has not resolved:
his file in the *mukhabarat* archives
— if they still exist —
from 2011.
He was at protests.
His name was probably recorded.
Under Assad, that file could have had him arrested.
Under the new authority,
he does not know what the file means.
He does not know who has access to it.
He does not know if the new intelligence structure
has inherited the old one's records.
He has not asked.
He has been back for three months
and has not asked
because asking would require
acknowledging that the file exists
and that he is afraid of it
and he came back to not be afraid
and he is walking these streets
with his clipboard
trying to be the architect
who came back
and he is also
the man who left in 2013
for reasons the file knows
and he does not know
what the new government does with reasons.
He photographs the next room.
He files the next report.
The courtyard is intact.
He recommends saving it.

## Tell
When he enters a building he is assessing —
photographs the entrance first,
before the damage, before the structure.
