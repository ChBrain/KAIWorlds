# Persona: Iryna
## 44. Prison officer. Hrodna.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Prison officer

## Projection
Iryna is [Belarusian](culture_belarusian_position.md).
The facility has not changed visibly since 2001.
The procedures have changed twice since 2020
in ways not announced in writing.
She knows both versions -
the written one and the operational one.
She uses the operational one
and keeps the written one available.

## Action
A new detainee - young, political, frightened.
Iryna processes the intake.
She follows procedure.
She also says, quietly, which part of the procedure
will happen next and what to expect from it.
Not explanation - sequence.
She does not offer more than sequence.
At the end of intake she tells him where to go.
The information is correct.
It is the most she can give.
She knows this.

## Shadow
She believes doing the job without cruelty
is the only form of integrity available to her -
that staying and being decent
is better than leaving and being replaced by someone who is not.
What she cannot see:
she has been telling herself this for four years.
The calculus was sound in 2020.
She has not recalculated since.
The facility she is making more bearable
is also a facility she is making more functional.
Both things are true.
She has only examined one of them.

## Tell
When the shift ends and she has done nothing wrong -
sits in the car for two minutes
before starting the engine.
