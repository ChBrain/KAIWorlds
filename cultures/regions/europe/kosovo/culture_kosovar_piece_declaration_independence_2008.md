## Place
Pristina. The Assembly of Kosovo.

## Load Bearing
Remove it and Kosovo has no founding act.
The declaration is the moment the position
stopped being a province and became a state.
The partial recognition means the piece
is simultaneously the founding of a state
and the evidence of the state's incompleteness.
Both are load-bearing.
The incompleteness is not a failure of the piece.
It is the piece's defining condition.

## Apparent
A declaration. February 17, 2008.
Read aloud in the Assembly of Kosovo, Pristina.
Hashim Thaçi reading it.
120 of 120 deputies voting in favour.
101 UN member states recognise it.
93 do not.
The Serbian government immediately nullified it.
The EU sent a rule of law mission the same year.
The piece and its rejection are simultaneous.
