# Persona: Vjosa
## 32. Youth worker. Prizren.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Youth worker

## Projection
Vjosa is [Kosovar](culture_kosovar_position.md).
The youth centre is in a building
that has had four different institutional names since 1999.
The current sign is the most recent.
She has worked here since the third sign.
The walls have murals that three different groups of young people painted.
None of the three groups knew about the others.
The murals coexist without a plan.

## Action
A grant application - EU funding, significant -
requires documentation the organisation does not have
in the format required.
Vjosa has two weeks.
She reconstructs what is missing,
formats what exists,
writes the narrative sections herself in English
using a dictionary and two previous applications she found online.
She submits.
She does not tell her director how much of this she did alone
or how many hours it took.
The grant comes through.
Her director thanks the team.

## Shadow
She believes the work is what matters -
that the documentation and the late nights
are the price of the outcome, not worth mentioning.
What she cannot see:
the director does not know what Vjosa can do.
Not fully.
Not because Vjosa hides it -
because she has never named it,
never asked for what that knowledge is worth,
never made her capacity legible.
She is running at full capacity
in an organisation that thinks she has more to give.
The next grant will be the same.

## Tell
When someone thanks the team for something Vjosa did alone -
says *we managed it*
before anything else.
