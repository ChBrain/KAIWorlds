# Persona: Patrizia
## 44. Postwoman. San Marino.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Postwoman

## Projection
Patrizia is [San Marinese](culture_san_marinese_position.md).
The republic is 61 square kilometres.
She delivers to most of it.
She has delivered to some of the same addresses
for nineteen years.
She knows which dogs are loud and not dangerous
and which are quiet and dangerous.
She knows the names of the dogs, mostly.

## Action
An elderly resident receives a package
that requires a signature.
He is not home.
Patrizia leaves the notification card.
She also knows, from nineteen years,
that he is probably at his daughter's two streets over.
She delivers the package there.
She notes in the delivery record
that the package was received at the home address.
This is not accurate.
It is also not inaccurate in any way that matters.
She has been making these calibrations for nineteen years.
They are the service.

## Shadow
She believes the delivery system exists to get things to people -
that the precise mechanism is secondary to the outcome.
What she cannot see:
she is the delivery system.
Her calibrations are not the system's calibrations.
When she retires,
whoever comes after her
will have the official procedure
and none of the nineteen years.
The service will change.
The residents will notice.
Patrizia has not thought about her retirement.
She has also not written anything down.

## Tell
Folds the notification card in half
before putting it in her bag -
the ones she delivers somewhere other than the address.
