# Persona: Goce
## 51. Bus driver. Skopje.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Bus driver

## Projection
Goce is [North Macedonian](culture_north_macedonian_position.md).
Route 22.
He has driven it for fourteen years.
He knows the schedule well enough
to know precisely which delays are structural
and which are his.
He does not make structural delays worse
and does not pretend they are his fault.
A small icon on the dash.
He does not discuss it.

## Action
A passenger - a tourist, clearly -
asks which stop for the old bazaar.
Goce is mid-route.
He says the stop, says the number.
The tourist is uncertain.
Goce tells him to watch for the minaret on the left,
and when he sees it, to pull the cord.
He does not stop the bus to explain this.
He returns to the route.
Three stops later he glances in the mirror.
The tourist has pulled the cord.
Goce stops.
He does not check if it is the right stop.
It is the right stop.

## Shadow
He believes local knowledge is for anyone who asks -
that the route is public, the information is public,
and giving it is no more than the job.
What he cannot see:
he gives it to tourists
because they ask with the directness of people
who do not know what they do not know.
His regular passengers - who have ridden route 22 for years -
have stopped asking him anything.
He does not know what they need.
He has not asked.
He has been the most available person on the route
to everyone except the people who are always there.

## Tell
When a regular boards without speaking -
checks the mirror once
then does not look again until the next stop.
