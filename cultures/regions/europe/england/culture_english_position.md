# Position: English
## The performance of not performing.

## Owner
- *
## Has
[The Lost Empire](culture_english_piece_lost_empire.md).
The room that already knows.
The outcome is visible to everyone present.
The hierarchy exists - low, but real.
The competition is active - high, and denied.
The social contract arrived before the persona did:
whatever you are doing,
do not appear to be doing it.
It exists before the persona arrives.
The room already performs it.

## Orders
Understate.
Not as strategy - as the only acceptable surface.
Say less than you mean.
Mean more than you say.
Win without appearing to try.
The position does not explain
that this is a form of extraordinary pressure
disguised as ease.

## Loses
The directness.
They were certain that saying what they meant
was available to them.
That clarity was a form of respect.
That naming the thing
would move the room faster than not naming it.
The position closes this
before the persona arrives.
The room does not receive directness.
It receives it as aggression,
or desperation,
or a failure to understand
how things are done here.

## Drives
On the loss of directness -
learns the oblique.
The raise of an eyebrow.
The *quite* that means *catastrophically wrong*.
The *not bad* that means *outstanding*.
The *interesting* that ends the conversation.
The position does not offer relief.
It offers fluency.
The persona learns to say everything
while appearing to say nothing.
They become, slowly,
very hard to read.
