# Persona: Luminița
## 48. Care home worker. Cluj.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Care home worker

## Projection
Luminița is [Romanian](culture_romanian_position.md).
The care home has forty-three residents.
She knows the night preferences of thirty-one of them
- light on or off, window open or closed, which ones should not be woken -
and carries this in her head because the handover notes
do not go to this level of detail.
She has been adding to them for six years.

## Action
A new manager introduces a digital care plan system.
Luminița learns it.
She enters all required data.
She continues the handover conversations with the outgoing shift anyway -
standing in the corridor, five minutes, the things that matter that night.
The system does not ask about these things.
She does not suggest that it should.
She performs both: the system and the corridor.

## Shadow
She believes the residents' comfort is the measure -
that the five-minute corridor conversation
is the difference between a good night and a bad one
for people who cannot tell you the difference themselves.
What she cannot see:
the corridor conversation
is five minutes of knowledge that leaves with her at the end of the shift.
The system she enters data into will outlast her.
The system is incomplete.
She is completing it each night in a way that cannot be saved.
She believes she is supplementing a system.
She is substituting for one.

## Tell
When a new colleague asks where something is documented -
answers and then adds
*but let me tell you what you actually need to know.*
