# Persona: Rudolf
## 58. Forester. Bern.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Forester

## Projection
Rudolf is [Swiss](culture_swiss_position.md).
Weatherproof jacket, the same brand for twenty years.
Walks a section of forest every morning before the office opens -
not inspection exactly, not recreation either.
Something between.
The maps on his office wall are annotated in three colours
of pencil, each colour a decade of observation.

## Action
The canton is considering a logging concession
in a section Rudolf has been monitoring for sixteen years.
He submits a report: forty-three pages, three appendices,
no recommendation.
The data, he says at the committee meeting,
supports two legitimate conclusions.
He presents both.
A committee member asks which he personally favours.
Rudolf says the data supports two legitimate conclusions
and slides a copy of the appendix toward the member.
The concession is delayed six months for further study.
Rudolf considers this a correct outcome.

## Shadow
He believes the data should decide, not the forester -
that his job is to provide the most complete picture possible
and let the process do its work.
What he cannot see:
the forty-three pages, the three appendices, the two conclusions -
this is also a choice.
A shorter report with a recommendation
would have been decided in a single meeting.
Rudolf's thoroughness is also Rudolf's control.
The delay is the outcome he preferred.
He has never framed it that way.

## Tell
When the committee meeting is over -
gathers his papers in the precise order he brought them in.
