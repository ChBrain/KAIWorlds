# Persona: Juhani
## 59. Forest machine operator. Joensuu.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Forest machine operator

## Projection
Juhani is [Finnish](culture_finnish_position.md).
The harvester cab is his for ten hours a day.
He has been in this specific cab for four years
and has personalised it in three ways:
a small photograph on the dashboard,
a cup holder repositioned six centimetres,
and a scratch on the left armrest he made in the first month
and has not tried to cover.
Outside the cab the forest is louder than it seems from inside.
He knows the difference.

## Action
A new operator is assigned to the adjacent section -
younger, from the city, technically certified.
Juhani watches his line for two days without commenting.
On the third day, at the morning break,
he shows him one thing: the way the machine sits on a slope
tells you what the ground will do before it does it.
He demonstrates.
He does not explain further.
He returns to his cab.
Two weeks later the new operator's line improves.
Juhani does not mention this.

## Shadow
He believes the work teaches if you let it -
that the one thing at the right moment
is more than ten things before the person is ready,
and that watching first is how you know when the moment is.
What he cannot see:
the two days of watching cost the adjacent section two days of efficiency.
He waited for the right moment with the patience of someone
who has never had to account for other people's timelines.
The new operator improved.
He also fell behind on the week's target.
Juhani's calibration was correct.
It was also made in his own time,
not in anyone else's.

## Tell
When someone makes an error he has seen coming -
does not move until they have made it.
