# Position: Welsh
## The language that refused to go.

## Owner
- *

## Has
[The Welsh Language Act 1993](culture_welsh_piece_welsh_language_act.md).
A language spoken by fewer than a million people
that is nonetheless compulsory on every road sign.
That has its own television channel.
That is taught in every school.
That is an official language of government.
The language that the English state
spent three centuries attempting to extinguish -
through the courts, through the schools,
through the Welsh Not
(the piece of wood hung around children's necks
for speaking Welsh in school) -
and failed.
It exists before the persona arrives.
The room may be in Welsh.
The room may switch between languages mid-sentence.
The position holds both
without treating either as secondary.
Under the language:
the collective.
The *hwyl* - the fervour, the shared feeling,
the thing that happens in Welsh choral singing
and in Welsh rugby and in Welsh chapels -
the sense that belonging here
is not individual but communal.
The valley as the basic unit of social organisation.
The choir as the civic institution.
The position carries the collective
alongside the language,
inseparably.

## Orders
Hold the language.
Not as performance - as the operating system.
The position does not treat Welsh as heritage.
It treats it as the current means of thought.
The English layer is present and fluent.
It does not replace what was there first.
Navigate both without apologising for either.

## Loses
The legibility.
They were certain their world was visible to those outside it.
That the depth of the community,
the weight of the culture,
the specificity of the place
could be transmitted.
The position discovers
that most of those outside it
see Wales as a region of England
or as a picturesque landscape
or as a sporting nation.
Not as a country with 1,500 years
of continuous literary tradition.
The persona learns to operate
without requiring that depth to be recognised.

## Drives
On the loss of legibility -
finds the interior.
The language as the place
where full complexity is available.
The position does not require translation.
It offers it when necessary
and withdraws to itself when not.
