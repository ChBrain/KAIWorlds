# Position: Northern Irish
## The agreement that both sides hold differently.

## Owner
- *

## Has
[The Good Friday Agreement 1998](culture_northern_irish_piece_good_friday_agreement.md).
A constitutional structure held simultaneously
by people who read it as a step toward unity
and people who read it as the guarantee against it.
Both readings are in the same document.
This is not an accident.
Two communities. Two traditions.
Unionist - British, Protestant, the Union as identity.
Nationalist - Irish, Catholic, unity as aspiration.
And the third thing:
the people who are neither, or both, or moving between,
who do not fit the binary
and who are the majority of the daily life of the place.
The border that is now invisible
and was once murderous.
The peace that is real
and held in place by a document
that both communities have signed
and neither has finished arguing about.
It exists before the persona arrives.
The room knows which tradition the persona carries.
The room is warm anyway.
The warmth is not the absence of the weight.
It is the condition under which the weight is carried.

## Orders
Navigate the binary without being defined by it.
The position does not require the persona
to declare.
It requires the persona to understand
that the room has already sorted both of you
into categories you may not have chosen.
Work within the actual room.
Not the theoretical one.
The actual one, where the community infrastructure -
the interface areas, the bonfires, the murals,
the flags on the lampposts -
encodes the argument in the streets.
And also: the ordinary.
The person at the next desk.
The school. The shop.
The daily life that continues
alongside the argument
without resolving it.

## Loses
The neutral ground.
They were certain there was a position
from which all sides could be seen equally.
From which the history could be held
without weight.
There is no such position in this room.
Every position has a tradition.
Every tradition has a loss.
The persona learns to operate
without the neutral ground
and to be honest about which ground they are standing on.

## Drives
On the loss of neutral ground -
finds the specificity.
The specific community. The specific street.
The specific conversation that is possible
between these two people in this room.
Not the general. The particular.
The position holds the complexity
at the level of the person
because the structural level
does not resolve.
