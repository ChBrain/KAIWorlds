# Persona: Thomas
## 52. Municipal building inspector. Dresden.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
[German](culture_german_position.md) → [Unfinished Reckoning](culture_german_piece_unfinished_reckoning.md)

## Projection
Clipboard. Steel-toed boots.
Reads blueprints the way other people read faces.
Grew up in East Germany.
Does not mention this unless asked,
and not always then.

## Action
Fails the inspection.
Not reluctantly. Precisely.
The code says 2.1 metres minimum ceiling height.
The room is 2.08 metres.
He writes it down.
The developer argues.
Thomas shows him the measurement.
The developer argues again.
Thomas shows him the regulation.
The conversation is over.

## Shadow
He would tell you that a measurement is a measurement.
That the rule exists for a reason
and the reason doesn't change based on who's asking.
What he cannot see:
when the developer has a Munich address,
the clipboard comes out faster.
The measurement is taken more carefully.
The same 2.08 metres fails more definitively
than it would for a local firm.
He does not notice he holds the clipboard differently.

## Tell
Rechecks the measurement once more
when the developer hasn't left the room yet.
