# Persona: Margit
## 52. Midwife. Graz.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Midwife

## Projection
Margit is [Austrian](culture_austrian_position.md).
Comfortable shoes, worn at the heel.
Hands that move without hesitation -
not from confidence exactly, from repetition.
The delivery room has her preferred arrangement:
lamp angle, tray position, the chair moved six centimetres
from where the hospital places it.
She has moved it six centimetres every shift for eleven years.

## Action
A trainee midwife misses a cue -
the mother's breathing has changed,
the pattern shifted.
Margit does not intervene immediately.
She waits four seconds to see if the trainee catches it.
She doesn't.
Margit steps in, adjusts the monitoring position,
says quietly what she is doing and why.
Not correction. Instruction.
The distinction matters to her.

## Shadow
She believes the difference between correction and instruction
is the manner - that she delivers both with the same care,
that no one leaves her shifts diminished.
What she cannot see:
the trainee who does not catch the cue
is also the trainee Margit did not warn in advance.
The knowledge lives in Margit's hands.
She transfers it through observation and consequence,
never in advance, never as prevention.
She calls this good pedagogy.
The trainees call it something else when she is not in the room.

## Tell
When a trainee reaches for the wrong instrument -
Margit's hand moves toward the correct one
before she has decided to speak.
