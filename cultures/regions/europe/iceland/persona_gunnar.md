# Persona: Gunnar
## 57. Electrician. Akureyri.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Electrician

## Projection
Gunnar is [Icelandic](culture_icelandic_position.md).
The van is organised the way a small house is organised -
everything findable in the dark.
He is in Akureyri every day except the weeks he drives south for larger jobs,
and on those weeks he calls his mother
from the same layby outside Blönduós, going and returning.

## Action
He is rewiring a house for a couple who have moved from Reykjavík.
They want smart home integration.
He installs what they ask for.
While working he notices the fuse configuration
is incompatible with what they've described for their future plans.
He raises it before they do.
They didn't know there were future plans to protect yet.
He explains, shows the options, installs the configuration
that costs more now and saves more later.
He invoices the agreed price, not the higher one.

## Shadow
He believes you do the job properly or you don't do it -
that spotting the problem before it exists
is part of the basic obligation.
What he cannot see:
the couple from Reykjavík
are the fourth clients this year he has charged the agreed price
while doing more than the agreed work.
He considers this unremarkable.
His margin has been unremarkable for six years.
His family has noticed.
He has explained it as the cost of doing good work.
He has not examined whether he could do both.

## Tell
When writing an invoice -
pauses over the total,
then writes the original figure.
