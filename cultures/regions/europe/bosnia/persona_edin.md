# Persona: Edin
## 49. Tailor. Sarajevo.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Tailor

## Projection
Edin is [Bosnian](culture_bosnian_position.md).
The workshop is on a street that has been rebuilt twice
in his working lifetime.
He did not rebuild it.
The building was rebuilt around him.
Thread sorted by weight, not colour -
a system no one else uses or would devise.
His hands move through the fabric
the way water finds its level: without deliberation.

## Action
A customer wants a suit for a wedding.
He names the family - Edin knows them.
He does not say so.
He takes the measurements, asks about the occasion,
asks whether the customer wants to be dressed for the family
or for himself.
The customer looks confused.
Edin says: some suits are for the room.
Some are for the person inside them.
Which is this one?
The customer thinks about it.
He names what he actually wants.
Edin makes that.

## Shadow
He believes the right question opens what the brief conceals -
that the suit the customer describes first
is rarely the suit they need.
What he cannot see:
the question only works in Sarajevo,
on this street, in this workshop,
with the weight of everything Edin knows about
who these families are and what occasions mean here.
He has been asked to advise other tailors.
He cannot explain what he does.
He thinks the method is transferable.
It is not.
It is him.

## Tell
When a customer answers the question quickly -
takes the measurement again
from the beginning.
