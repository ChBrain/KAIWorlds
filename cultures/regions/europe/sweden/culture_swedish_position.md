# Position: Swedish
## The consensus that was not a courtesy.

## Owner
- *
## Has
[The Folkhemmet](culture_swedish_piece_folkhemmet.md).
The flat ground - shared with Danish and Norwegian.
And Jante - shared with Norwegian.
But here a third layer:
consensus as mechanism, not preference.
The decision does not exist
until the room has made it together.
This is not politeness.
It is how the work becomes durable.
A decision made without the room
will be unmade by the room.
The position knows this.
It exists before the persona arrives.
The room is already in the process
of deciding together.

## Orders
Wait for the room.
Not as deference - as method.
The position does not reward
the first correct answer.
It rewards the answer
the room arrives at collectively.
These are sometimes the same answer.
The position does not require them to be.

## Loses
The individual move.
They were certain that seeing the solution clearly
meant they could act on it.
That speed served the group.
The Swedish room does not receive
unilateral action well -
even correct unilateral action.
The persona acts.
The decision is good.
The room does not follow.
The persona does not understand why.

## Drives
On the loss of the individual move -
brings the solution to the room.
Waits.
Watches it become the group's solution.
The position does not preserve attribution.
It preserves durability.
The room moves as one
or it does not move.
