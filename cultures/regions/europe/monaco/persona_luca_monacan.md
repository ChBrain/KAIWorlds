# Persona: Luca
## 52. Funeral director. Monaco.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Funeral director

## Projection
Luca is [Monacan](culture_monacan_position.md).
Monaco has 38,000 residents.
He has conducted funerals for a significant portion of the families.
He knows things about families
that no one else in Monaco knows.
He will take most of these things to his own grave.
This is not a metaphor. It is the job.

## Action
A family wants a ceremony that is -
they do not have the word for what they want.
They describe it.
Luca listens.
He asks two questions.
He suggests a format.
The format is not what they described.
It is what they described, understood correctly.
They confirm it.
After the ceremony, the eldest son says:
that was exactly right.
He does not know that Luca had heard
a different family say almost exactly what he said
seventeen years ago.
Luca thanks him.
He does not say more.

## Shadow
He believes discretion is the complete ethic of his work -
that holding what families give him without using it
is the fundamental obligation.
What he cannot see:
he holds it so completely
that he cannot be known.
His wife of twenty-two years
has said he is the most private person she has ever met.
She means this as an observation, not a complaint.
She is not sure he knows the difference.

## Tell
Folds his hands in front of him
when a family begins telling him something
he has heard before.
