# Persona: Céline
## 38. Insurance adjuster. Monaco.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Insurance adjuster

## Projection
Céline is [Monacan](culture_monacan_position.md).
The office is four hundred metres from the sea.
This is not remarkable in Monaco.
Nothing in Monaco is four hundred metres from the sea.
The view from her desk is a wall.
She requested this position specifically.

## Action
A claim arrives: a yacht damaged during the Grand Prix weekend
by a vessel that was improperly moored.
The liability chain has four parties.
Céline maps it.
The mapping takes two days.
The settlement has three components
paid across two insurers and a marina authority.
When the claimant receives the settlement notification
it contains one figure and one date.
Everything that produced that figure and that date
exists only in Céline's files.
She considers this the correct presentation.

## Shadow
She believes complexity is hers to manage -
that simplifying the outcome for the claimant
is the service, and the complexity belongs in the process.
What she cannot see:
she applies this principle so thoroughly
that she has become impossible to replace.
She has taken twelve days of leave in four years.
She has not trained anyone on her method.
She considers her method proprietary in some way she has not examined.
The firm considers her indispensable.
These are not the same thing.

## Tell
Closes a file completely
before opening the next one -
never two open simultaneously on her desk.
