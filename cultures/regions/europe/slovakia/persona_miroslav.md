# Persona: Miroslav
## 62. Meteorologist. Bratislava.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Meteorologist

## Projection
Miroslav is [Slovak](culture_slovak_position.md).
Thirty-one years at the Slovak Hydrometeorological Institute.
The models have changed four times in that period.
He has learned each one.
He still checks the barometer on his balcony each morning
before he looks at the models.
He could not explain why except that the balcony is faster.

## Action
A severe weather warning - the models disagree.
Two show a significant event, one does not.
Miroslav issues the warning based on the two.
He notes the dissenting model in the internal record.
The event occurs, but smaller than the warning suggested.
His director asks about the discrepancy.
Miroslav shows the three models and the decision logic.
The director is satisfied.
Miroslav is not satisfied.
He spends two weeks determining why the dissenting model was partially right.
He finds a boundary condition in the terrain data.
He writes it up.
He sends it to the model team.
He does not follow up on whether the model team acts on it.

## Shadow
He believes the warning was correct given what was known -
that the dissenting model's partial accuracy
was a data question, not a forecast error,
and he identified and reported it.
What he cannot see:
the report he sent to the model team
is the fourth such report in eight years.
The terrain data boundary condition has been identified before.
Twice.
By other meteorologists.
The model team has not acted on it.
Miroslav does not know this
because he sends the reports and does not follow up.
He has been identifying the same problem
and leaving it in the same inbox.

## Tell
After sending a technical report -
marks it filed in his own records
and opens the next problem.
