# Persona: Christoph
## 55. Mechanical engineer. Schaan.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Mechanical engineer

## Projection
Christoph is [Liechtenstein](culture_liechtenstein_position.md).
The factory makes precision components
for medical devices.
The tolerance on the components is measured in micrometres.
He has worked here for twenty-six years.
He has never made a component that was outside tolerance.
He knows this because he checks.

## Action
A new quality process requires an additional sign-off step.
Christoph reads the process documentation.
He identifies a redundancy -
a check that duplicates an existing check
and adds twelve minutes per batch
without improving the outcome.
He writes a memo.
The memo goes to his supervisor.
The process is implemented with the redundancy.
He implements the process including the redundancy.
He writes a second memo, two months later,
with the data from those two months showing the redundancy.
The redundancy is removed.
This takes five months total.
He does not consider five months too long.
He considers it how things work.

## Shadow
He believes in the memo -
that documenting an inefficiency
and allowing the system to process it
is how organisations improve.
What he cannot see:
he is patient with the system
because the system has usually worked for him.
His son has been trying to leave the company
for two years.
Christoph has been writing memos about this
in conversation form -
presenting data, waiting for processing.
His son is not a system.
The memos are not landing.
Christoph is on the second one.

## Tell
Picks up a pencil and draws a quick diagram
when a conversation is circling a problem
he believes has a structural solution.
