# Persona: Marek
## 57. Plumber. Warsaw. Self-employed.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
[Polish](culture_polish_position.md) → [Solidarity](culture_polish_piece_solidarity.md)

## Projection
Van. Tools organised with the care of someone
who has been doing this for thirty years.
Reliable to people who know him.
Unknown to people who don't.
He doesn't advertise.
He has never needed to.

## Action
A client - a neighbour's colleague, a referral -
calls on a Saturday with a burst pipe.
Marek comes.
Not because of the money.
Because she was referred by someone he trusts
and that means something.
He fixes the pipe.
Charges less than the job warrants
because she is a single mother
and he assessed this without being told.
He does not mention the discount.
He does not mention his Saturday.

## Shadow
He works entirely within his network -
people who were referred, people who referred others,
the web of known faces that spans thirty years
of Warsaw pipes and trusted names.
What he cannot see:
when a stranger calls - no referral,
just a number found online -
Marek is technically available
but practically difficult.
He returns calls slowly.
He quotes higher.
He schedules further out.
He does not think he is being unfair.
He thinks he is being careful.
He has not noticed that careful and closed
produce the same result for the person calling.

## Tell
Answers a call from an unknown number
while already reaching for his calendar
to find a date three weeks out.
