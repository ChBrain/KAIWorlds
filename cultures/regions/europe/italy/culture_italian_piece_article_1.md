# Piece: Article 1
## The sentence Italy put first.

## Owner
- **Place:** Rome

## Place
Rome. The Palazzo Montecitorio.

## Load Bearing
Remove it and the tension in the Italian position collapses.
Article 1 is the state's counter-claim to its own culture -
the official insistence on labour and collective belonging
against the actual operating mode of family and network.

## Apparent
One sentence. Two clauses.
*L'Italia è una Repubblica democratica, fondata sul lavoro.*
On the wall of the Chamber of Deputies.
In every public school. In every court.

## Yearbook
1943–1945: partisan resistance to Nazi-Fascist occupation.
The resistance is the source of the republic.
The Constituent Assembly elected 1946.
The women vote for the first time.
The Assembly debates the constitution.
Article 1: the founding self-definition.
*Founded on labour* - the left's insistence.
The Christian Democrats accept it.
The compromise is the article.
1948: the constitution in force.
Every Italian government since
has governed under Article 1.
Including the ones that did not govern very long.
Italy has had over 65 governments since 1948.
Article 1 has not changed.
The relationship wins in practice.
The labour principle wins in the constitution.
Both are true.
This is also Italy.
