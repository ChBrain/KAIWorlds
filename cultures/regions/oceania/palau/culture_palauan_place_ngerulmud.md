- Jellyfish Lake.
  Ongeim'l Tketau.
  The landlocked marine lake.
  Two million mastigias jellyfish.
  No stinging capacity — evolved without predators.
  Snorkelling through them: the experience
  that made Palau's reputation.
  The lake closed periodically for restoration.
  The position that closes its most famous tourist site
