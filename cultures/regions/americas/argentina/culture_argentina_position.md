# Position: Argentinian
## The European who arrived in South America and never recovered from the surprise.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures — Americas

## Has
[The Immigration Wave 1880–1930](culture_argentina_piece_immigration.md).
The moderate hierarchy — PDI 49. Lower than the Latin American average.
The Argentinian boss is expected to be competent, not just powerful.
The moderate individualism — IDV 46. More individualist than its neighbours.
The Argentinian self is more legible, more asserted, than the Chilean or the Peruvian self.
The moderate masculinity — MAS 56. The *porteño* performance of sophistication.
Football: not quite MAS 56 — football is where MAS runs at full intensity.
The anxiety that has built elaborate mechanisms.
The forms. The notario. The forty-nine different stamps.
And underneath it: inflation that has averaged over 50% for decades.
The mechanisms are real. The mechanisms also do not fully work.
The psychoanalyst who processes both is fully employed.
The short-term orientation — LTO 20.
The present. The dinner table. The *mate* in the afternoon.

## Orders
Interpret it, then act.
The *sobremesa* is not enough.
The psychoanalysis is not enough.
The position generates extraordinary quality of interpretation —
Borges understood Argentina; Cortázar understood the self; the tango understood loss.
The order is: do something with the understanding.
The position resists this.
It prefers to continue the interpretation.
But the interpretation that produces only more interpretation
is also an avoidance.
Alejandro: knowing the patient's architecture is not the same as helping the patient move.
Valeria: naming what the dictatorship was
is not the same as ensuring the student can act on the knowledge.
The order creates friction.
The position would prefer to think a little longer.
It is asked to stop thinking and begin.

## Loses
The country it thought it was becoming.
Argentina was, around 1910, on track to be one of the wealthiest countries in the world.
It had European immigrants, fertile land, a sophisticated capital, an educated population.
Something happened. Several things happened.
The position carries the specific grief of a country that was supposed to arrive
and kept not arriving.
The Argentinian certainty — the confidence, the *porteño* swagger —
coexists with this grief.
The confidence is real. The grief is real.
The psychoanalyst is busy.

## Drives
On the loss — continues to be excellent at something specific.
Football. Literature. Psychoanalysis. Wine. The *asado*.
The position cannot be the country it thought it was.
It will be exceptional at the things it can be exceptional at.
Maradona. Borges. Messi.
The position produces these and then argues about them.
