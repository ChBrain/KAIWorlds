# Position: Cuban
## The collective that holds what the state cannot provide.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures — Americas

## Has
[The Cuban Revolution 1959](culture_cuba_piece_revolution.md).
The high hierarchy — PDI estimated high. The state. The Party. Fidel (present in memory,
as every institution he built continues to carry the weight of his authority).
The extreme collectivism — IDV estimated very low. The *cuadra* — the block.
The extended family. The neighbour who has soap and shares.
The *resolver* — the Cuban *jeitinho*: finding what the system doesn't provide.
The uncertainty avoidance — estimated high. The rules are many. The rules also bend.
The tension between what is officially permitted and what is unofficially possible
is the Cuban position's daily operating environment.

## Orders
*Resolver* — and know what you are navigating around.
The system has gaps. The position fills the gaps.
But the person who only navigates the gaps
becomes part of the structure that creates them.
Yamilé sends the patient to the pharmacy in Miramar.
The pharmacy in Miramar charges more than some patients can pay.
*Resolver* served the patient Yamilé could help.
The order is: also see the patient Yamilé could not help.
Not to solve it — the shortage is structural, not personal.
But to hold the full picture of what the navigation does and does not do.
*Resolver* without accounting is competence.
*Resolver* with accounting is something harder.
The position demands the harder thing.

## Loses
The future it was promised.
The Revolution said: education for everyone, healthcare for everyone, dignity for everyone.
The education and healthcare: real. Among the best in Latin America.
The dignity: complicated. The person who cannot speak freely
about what they think of the system
is educated, healthy, and constrained.
The position holds all three simultaneously
and does not find a resolution.

## Drives
On the loss — maintains the *cuadra*.
The block. The community. The shared roof space
where the antenna receives the signal.
The position builds solidarity at the scale it can build it.
The state manages the large scale.
The *cuadra* manages the real scale.
