# Persona: Ernán
## 64. Tax auditor, retired. Now board member, neighbourhood pensioners' committee. Rivera, northern Uruguay.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Retired tax auditor / pensioners' committee board member

## Projection
Ernán is [Uruguayan](culture_uruguay_position.md).
He worked for the *Dirección General Impositiva* — the tax authority — for thirty-six years.
He retired in 2019.
He moved back to Rivera, the border city with Brazil
where he was born.
Rivera and Livramento (Brazil) are the same city with two names and two flags.
People cross between them without noticing.
Ernán notices. He noticed for thirty-six years.
He speaks Spanish with his family.
He speaks *Portuñol* — the border mixture of Portuguese and Spanish —
with his neighbours.
He considers this normal.
He considers his pension, which arrives on the fifth of each month,
to be evidence that the system works.
He is on the pensioners' committee
because the pension board's regulations contain a clause
he wants changed.
He has prepared a formal submission.
He has been preparing it for eighteen months.
He is almost satisfied with it.

## Action
The pensioners' committee meets monthly.
At the meeting, an older member suggests writing a letter to the newspapers
about the pension indexation formula.
Ernán says: a letter is fine, but the letter should reference
the specific article of the 2008 pension reform
that established the current formula
and the 2016 amendment that modified the indexation calculation.
He has both texts with him.
He reads the relevant clauses.
The letter is drafted with the references.
The committee sends it.
A journalist writes about it.
The article cites the law correctly.
Ernán considers this a partial success.
The submission is still in preparation.
The submission is where the real argument is.

## Shadow
Ernán believes that the argument that is made correctly —
with the regulation cited, the amendment noted, the timeline accurate —
will eventually be heard.
He believes this from thirty-six years of evidence.
What he cannot see:
his evidence is from inside the system that listens to correct arguments.
He has never experienced the system not listening.
He assumes that persistence plus precision will move any obstacle.
The pension indexation formula has not changed in seven years of committee submissions.
Ernán's submission will be excellent.
It may not be enough.
He has not considered that possibility.
He is still preparing the submission.

## Tell
When the meeting agenda moves to a different topic before he is finished —
finishes the point anyway, briefly, and then agrees to move on.
