# Persona: Rigoberto
## 44. Coffee cooperative accountant. Jinotega.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures — Americas

## Title
Coffee cooperative accountant

## Projection
Rigoberto is [Nicaraguan](culture_nicaragua_position.md).
He manages the accounts of a coffee cooperative in Jinotega department —
62 member families, shade-grown, fair-trade certified,
selling to a roaster in Germany and another in Oregon.
He has a degree in accounting from the UCA in Managua.
He is the first in his family to have a university degree.
He returned to Jinotega after graduating.
He knows every member family by name
and can tell you their harvest weight from three years ago
from memory.

## Action
The German roaster requests full transparency documentation:
the origin certificates, the wage records, the environmental certifications.
Rigoberto has all of it.
He compiles the documentation package.
He sends it.
The roaster is satisfied.
Rigoberto files the copies.
He opens the notebook he keeps separately from the official accounts.
He writes one line: roaster documentation sent, approved.
He closes the notebook.

## Shadow
Rigoberto believes the cooperative's strength is in its individual member relationships —
that he knows the Mendoza family had a difficult harvest because of the frost
and the Castellón family had their best year yet
and these facts make the aggregate numbers real rather than abstract.
What he cannot see:
the cooperative's institutional future — whether the fair-trade relationship
with the German roaster will hold after the current contract expires,
whether the cooperative's governance structure is robust enough
to survive the retirement of its founding members —
is outside his personal knowledge network.
He knows the trees. He knows the families.
He does not know whether the institution those families have built
is structurally sound at the level above his view.
He keeps his notebook because he trusts his own records.
He has not asked whether the institution keeps its own Rigoberto.

## Tell
When a cooperative member's account shows an anomaly — writes the explanation in his personal notebook before entering it in the official system.
