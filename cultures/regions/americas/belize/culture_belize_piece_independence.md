## Load Bearing
Remove independence and Belize is British Honduras, a small British Caribbean colony with a Guatemalan territorial claim. With it: Belize is the only Central American country to have achieved independence through the British Westminster system, in English, while simultaneously being claimed by Guatemala, receiving its independence with a British garrison present to deter invasion. The position has been managing this complexity since September 21, 1981.

## Apparent
1798: The Battle of St. George's Caye. British settlers and enslaved Africans defeat a Spanish fleet. The founding myth of Belize. 1862: British Honduras declared a Crown Colony. 1821: Guatemala, on independence from Spain, claims all of Belize. 1954: universal suffrage. George Price and the People's United Party lead the independence movement. 1975: the UN recognises Belize's right to self-determination. 1981: September 21. Independence. The Guatemalan claim: suspended but not withdrawn. A British garrison remains until 1994. The ICJ referral: 2019. Guatemala and Belize agree to submit the territorial dispute to the International Court of Justice. Still pending.
