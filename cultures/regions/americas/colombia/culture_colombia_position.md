# Position: Colombian
## The warmth that holds what the country cannot yet resolve.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures — Americas

## Has
[The 2016 Peace Accord](culture_colombia_piece_paz.md).
The high hierarchy — PDI 67. The authority is present and is also relational.
The *cacique*, the *patrón*, the political boss who provides and expects loyalty.
The extreme collectivism — IDV 13. One of the most collectivist in the Americas.
The group is the survival unit. The family holds. The barrio holds.
The masculinity — MAS 64. Achievement. Status. But also: the warmth that accompanies it.
The Colombian who succeeds brings the family; the Colombian who fails is not abandoned.
The network as the certainty.
You know who to trust because you have built the knowing over years.
The people who do not have this network
do not have certainty.
The present: the *fiesta* on Saturday, the *sancocho* on Sunday.
The warmth is not decoration. It is the primary infrastructure.

## Orders
Trust the warmth even after what happened.
The position has fifty-two years of armed conflict in it.
The relationship survived the conflict.
It must now survive the peace —
which is harder in a different way:
the conflict gave the warmth a clear purpose.
*We stay together because if we do not, what happened will happen again.*
Without that threat, the warmth must hold on its own.
The position demands this: stay warm when there is no emergency requiring it.
Build the *parche* when no one is in danger.
That is the harder order.

## Loses
The certainty that peace holds.
The 2016 accord was the moment Colombia chose differently.
The FARC disarmed. The government signed.
The position had not dared to fully believe this was possible.
Now it is possible and the position does not know how to hold a future
that is not organised around the conflict.
The conflict was terrible. It was also the frame.
Without the frame, the position is learning a different kind of uncertainty.

## Drives
On the loss — builds community. The neighbourhood association.
The church *bazar*. The *sancocho* made for the whole block.
The Colombian response to the unresolvable is to feed people.
The food is not displacement. The food is the position's answer
to what it cannot fix with a policy.
The food is warm. The food is real.

*Hofstede: PDI 67, IDV 13, MAS 64, UAI 80, LTO 13, IVR 83*
