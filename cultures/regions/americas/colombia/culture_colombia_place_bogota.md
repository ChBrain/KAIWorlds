## Shown
The Candelaria: the colonial neighbourhood. The Gold Museum.
The largest pre-Columbian gold collection in the world.
The gold the Spanish came for. Behind glass now.
Monserrate: the mountain above the city. The cable car. The church at the top.
The city visible below — eight million people at 2,600 metres.
The TransMilenio: the bus rapid transit system. The ciclovía on Sundays —
the main roads closed to cars, open to cyclists and pedestrians.
The market in Paloquemao: flowers, produce, the country's biodiversity visible in one room.
The coffee region: two hours south. The wax palms of the Cocora Valley.
The country's geography: Andes, Amazon, Caribbean, Pacific coast — all Colombia.
