# Position: Brazilian
## The warmth that holds the inequality together.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures — Americas

## Has
[Lei Áurea 1888](culture_brazil_piece_lei_aurea.md).
The high hierarchy — PDI 69. The *chefe*. The authority accepted with warmth rather than deference.
The moderate collectivism — IDV 38. The family, the group, the *jeitinho brasileiro* —
the creative workaround that keeps the collective functioning.
The moderate masculinity — MAS 49. Achievement matters; so does relationship.
The *ginga* — the flexible, improvisational quality that applies equally to football and negotiation.
The high uncertainty avoidance — UAI 76. Rules proliferate. They are also navigated around.
The long-term orientation — LTO 65. Patience. The future is being built.
The high indulgence — IVR 59. The *samba*. The *carnaval*. The *churrasco*.

## Orders
Give before you need.
The Brazilian position requires generosity as investment, not charity.
The *compadre* who will help you in April
must have been fed in November.
The relationship that holds is the one that was built when nothing was needed.
The position demands this from Beatriz:
she goes to the neighbourhood before the student disappears, not after.
It demands this from Carlos:
the supervisor who trusts him on Thursday
is the supervisor who has been told the truth on Wednesday.
The warmth is the infrastructure.
Infrastructure must be maintained before failure, not during.

## Loses
The belief that the warmth resolves the inequality.
Brazil is one of the most unequal societies in the world.
The Gini coefficient. The *favela* and the *condomínio*.
The warmth moves across the inequality without dissolving it.
The position knows this and does not know what to do with it.
The *jeitinho* navigates. The navigation does not change the landscape it navigates.

## Drives
On the loss — holds the relationship. The *boteco*. The table.
The shared meal that crosses the class line for one evening.
The position believes the relationship is real even when the structure beneath it is unjust.
It may be right. The relationship may be the only thing preventing a different kind of rupture.
