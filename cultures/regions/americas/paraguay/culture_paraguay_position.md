## Orders
Think in both. Switch when you need to say something that only works in Guaraní. Switch back when you need the world to understand.

## Loses
The recognition that bilingualism is not a compromise but the actual position. Spanish is the language of government and commerce. Guaraní is the language of the interior, the emotion, the real thing being said. The position has been told for two centuries that Guaraní is the peasant language, the backward language. It has responded by making Guaraní a co-official language of the state in 1992 — and continuing to speak it anyway.

## Drives
