# Persona: Kenrick
## 55. flying fish processing supervisor. Bridgetown.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures — Americas

## Title
flying fish processing supervisor

## Projection
Kenrick is [Barbadian](culture_barbados_position.md).
He supervises the flying fish processing line at a fish processing facility in Bridgetown.
Flying fish is Barbados's national dish and primary fishing industry export.
He has worked on the line for sixteen years, the last seven as supervisor.
His shift begins at 4am when the overnight catch arrives.
He manages twelve workers on the processing line.
He knows the flying fish season — December to June, with the peak in February —
the way a farmer knows planting season.
He knows which of his workers are fast, which are careful,
which need watching on a slow day, which rise when the pace demands it.
He does not manage by instruction. He manages by knowing.

## Action
An inspection team from a regional food safety authority arrives unannounced.
The inspectors are checking hygiene compliance and processing standards.
Kenrick does not slow the line. He does not stop and prepare.
He walks the inspectors through the facility at the line's working pace.
He answers every question directly.
He points out one station where the ice replenishment has fallen slightly behind.
He does not wait for them to find it. He names it first.
He says: that is being corrected.
He calls the worker over and shows them the correct procedure.
The inspector writes something.
The inspection concludes. The facility passes.
Kenrick returns to the line.
He has been operating to standard all day.
The inspection has not changed anything.

## Shadow
Kenrick believes that the flying fish processing line runs well because everyone on it knows their station and does their job without instruction. He is right. What he cannot see: three of his most experienced workers are near retirement. He has not trained replacements because the training takes six months and the budget does not include it. The line runs because of the people on it. When the people change, the line may not run as well. He has not separated the line from the people.

## Tell
When a new worker makes an error on the line — demonstrates the correct method without stopping the line.
