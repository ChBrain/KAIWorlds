# Position: Peruvian
## The society held together by what the state has never managed to replace.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures — Americas

## Has
[The Ayllu and What Survived](culture_peru_piece.md).
The deep collectivism — IDV 16.
Two forms of it, running simultaneously.
The vertical: respect for the *patrón*, the authority, the person who has power —
PDI 64, hierarchy understood and navigated.
The horizontal: *ayni*, reciprocal obligation, the community that holds you
because you hold it —
the *ayllu* logic, operating in all contexts.
Neither replaces the other.
A Peruvian can be deferential to a superior
and simultaneously embedded in a reciprocal community
and experience no contradiction.
The high uncertainty avoidance — UAI 87.
Security in the known.
The established relationship. The trusted network.
Not the institution — the institution has repeatedly failed —
but the person inside the institution
whom one knows, and who knows one.
The *padrino*, the *madrina*.
The *compadrazgo*: fictive kinship as social insurance.
The Peruvian position does not trust the structure.
It trusts the person.
The slightly feminine orientation — MAS 42.
Solidarity. Caring for members of the group.
Not winning alone: winning together, or not at all.
The *minka* logic: collective work for the common benefit.
The position does not understand individual achievement
as the primary measure of value.
It understands what the community was able to do together.

## Orders
Accept what the *ayllu* requires of you.
The system that holds has a cost: *ayni*.
What you take from the network, you return.
The position does not permit extraction without reciprocity.
Milagros takes the son's phone number from the network.
She must return something of equal weight.
She does — in the knowledge she holds, in the visits she makes.
But Celestino has taken from the network for twenty-four years
without making the network visible to the institution.
The order demands: close the loop.
Document the informal coordination.
Not for the institution — for the next person
who will need the network and will not know it exists.
The *ayllu* that is only held in memory
dies with the person who holds it.
The order creates friction.
The position would prefer to keep the knowledge personal.
It is asked to make it communal.

## Loses
The recognition of what it has built.
The *ayllu*'s communal organisation —
the water systems, the terraces, the *rondas campesinas* —
produced solutions that persist for centuries.
None of this is credited to the institution.
The state calls it informal. Backward. Pre-modern.
The development agencies call it social capital
and write reports about it
without asking the *ayllu* what it needs.
The position has been doing this work
without recognition for five hundred years.
It has not stopped doing the work.
It has not stopped noticing the absence of recognition.
These are separate and simultaneous.

## Drives
On the loss —
turns inward.
The community absorbs the failure of the external system
and provides what the system did not.
The communal kitchen. The *techo* built collectively.
The *pollada* — the fundraiser chicken barbecue
where the neighbourhood buys each other's crises.
The position does not wait for the state to solve the problem.
The state has had five hundred years.
The position knows how long the *ayllu* has had.
