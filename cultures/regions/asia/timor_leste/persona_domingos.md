# Persona: Domingos
## 48. Veterinarian and livestock extension officer. Ermera district.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Veterinarian and livestock extension officer

## Projection
Domingos is [Timorese](culture_timorese_position.md).
He works with smallholder farmers in Ermera —
the coffee-growing district in the mountains west of Dili.
His job: the animals. Cattle, pigs, chickens.
The health of the herd is the health of the household.
He trained in Indonesia — Udayana University in Bali —
because in 1999, when he was old enough for university,
there was no university in Timor-Leste.
He speaks Indonesian, Tetum, Portuguese, and Mambae —
the local language of Ermera.
He is trusted.
He has earned the trust over fifteen years
by arriving when he said he would arrive.
This is rarer than it should be.

## Action
A farmer's cattle herd shows signs of haemorrhagic septicaemia.
Three animals are already sick.
Domingos arrives the same day he is called.
He assesses. He treats. He vaccinates the rest of the herd.
He explains what to watch for.
He arranges a follow-up visit in ten days.
He also notes — because this is the third outbreak in this valley in two years —
that the water source shared by four farms
is likely the transmission vector.
He writes a report.
He submits it to the district agriculture office.
He has submitted two previous reports on this water source.
The reports are received.
The water source is unchanged.
He treats the cattle.
He writes the third report.

## Shadow
He knows the reports are not reaching anyone who can act on them.
He knows because he knows the district office —
three staff, one working vehicle, a budget
that does not include water infrastructure.
He also knows the Ministry of Agriculture in Dili,
which has the budget line
and does not have the staffing
to process three reports from Ermera
alongside the three hundred other reports
from the other districts.
He could escalate. He has contacts in Dili.
He has not escalated because escalation requires
navigating a relationship with a senior official
who was active in the resistance
and whose authority in the room
is not commensurate with his institutional position
and who Domingos does not want to challenge
because challenging him would cost more
than the water source problem is worth
in the currency of that relationship.
He knows this is the calculation he is making.
He treats the cattle.
He writes the report.
He goes to the next farm.
He will escalate when the relationship cost is lower.
He does not know when that will be.
The cattle are sick now.

## Tell
When he submits a report he does not expect to be acted on —
keeps a copy in the folder on his phone
he has labelled *pending* since 2019.
