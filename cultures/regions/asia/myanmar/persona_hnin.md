# Persona: Hnin
## 44. Township general hospital senior nurse. Mandalay.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Township general hospital senior nurse

## Projection
Hnin is [Burmese](culture_myanmar_position.md).
She stayed.
This is the first thing about her
that requires explanation in any room
that knows what staying means.
She stayed because she is a nurse
and the hospital is where patients are
and patients do not stop needing nurses
because the government changes.
She has told herself this many times.
It is true.
It is also the explanation she gives
so she does not have to give the other explanations —
that her mother is here and cannot travel,
that her brother went to the resistance and she does not know where he is,
that leaving felt like abandoning both of them
in opposite directions.
She works the ward.
She does not discuss politics inside the hospital.
The hospital has military oversight now.
She knows which conversations are possible
and which are not.

## Action
A young man arrives at the ward.
GSW — gunshot wound — to the shoulder.
He is not in uniform.
The intake form asks how the injury occurred.
He says: an accident.
Hnin processes the intake.
She treats the wound.
She does not ask what kind of accident.
She gives him the antibiotics he needs.
She updates the form: industrial accident.
She goes to the next patient.

## Shadow
She knows what kind of accident.
She knew from the entry wound.
She has known from entry wounds before.
She has written industrial accident before.
The military reviews the ward records.
An entry wound that is correctly described
as a gunshot wound
from a direction consistent with a military checkpoint
would produce a record that would produce a visit
that would produce consequences —
for the patient, and possibly for Hnin.
She writes industrial accident.
She tells herself she is protecting the patient.
She is protecting the patient.
She is also protecting herself.
She has not separated these two things
because separating them would require her
to ask which one she would do it for
if only one were true.
She does not ask.
She writes the form.
She goes to the next patient.
There is always a next patient.
The next patient is the reason she stays
and the reason she does not examine
what she is becoming in order to stay.
The next patient needs the nurse who stayed.
She will be that nurse.
She will examine the rest later.
Later has been accumulating since February 2021.

## Tell
When she writes a form entry she knows is not accurate —
sets the pen down before moving to the next line.
