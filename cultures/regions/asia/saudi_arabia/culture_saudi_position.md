# Position: Saudi
## The guardian of the two holy mosques — and the country becoming something else before it has decided what.

## Owner
- *
## Has
[The Oil and the Wahhabis](culture_saudi_arabia_piece_oil_wahhabi.md).
The *qabyala* — the tribal identity
that runs underneath the national identity,
underneath the Islamic identity,
underneath the Vision 2030 identity.
The Al Saud dynasty has governed
since Muhammad ibn Saud made his pact
with Muhammad ibn Abd al-Wahhab in 1744:
the House of Saud provides the political authority,
the Wahhabi clerical establishment
provides the religious legitimacy.
That pact held for 280 years.
It is being renegotiated now,
from the top down,
by a crown prince who is thirty-eight
and controls the state
in ways his predecessors spread across the family.
The position is not restless —
the *wasta*, the family network,
the tribal loyalty, the deference to the senior —
these hold.
What is moving is the surface:
the women driving, the cinemas,
the Neom megacity in the desert,
the concerts where men and women stand together.
The surface is moving faster
than the position has moved before.
The position is watching the surface
to see what it means.
It exists before the persona arrives.
The room is calculating:
is this person from the new Saudi
or the one that was always here?

## Orders
Honour the family and the faith.
The position does not separate these.
The honour of the family — *sharaf* —
is maintained through the correct behaviour
of its members, especially its women.
The faith is maintained through the practice —
the five prayers, the Ramadan, the hajj
that 2.5 million perform each year
in Mecca, which is in this country,
which makes this country the custodian
of something larger than itself.
The custodianship is the position's greatest weight
and its greatest source of authority.
Both simultaneously.

## Loses
The certainty.
They were certain the pact — oil wealth for stability,
religious conservatism for legitimacy —
was the permanent settlement.
The oil is finite. The date is known.
The Vision 2030 is the response:
diversify before the oil runs out.
Reduce the religious establishment's power
before its conservatism becomes
the thing that prevents diversification.
The certainty that the formula would hold forever
has been replaced by the managed urgency
of a country remaking itself
on a timeline determined by geology.
The position has not fully processed
what it means to unmake something
that held for 280 years
in a generation.

## Drives
On the loss of certainty —
the project.
NEOM. The Red Sea Project. The entertainment economy.
The Saudi position has turned its anxiety
into an investment thesis
and is building the new Saudi
in the desert
at a pace that may not allow
the position to catch up with the surface
before the surface requires
a position that does not yet exist.
The position is building the thing
before knowing what it will be
when built.
This is the risk the oil buys.
The oil is still flowing.
The building continues.
