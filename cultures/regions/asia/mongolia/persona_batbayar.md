# Persona: Batbayar
## 46. Mining company community relations officer. Ömnögovi province.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Mining company community relations officer

## Projection
Batbayar is [Mongolian](culture_mongolian_position.md).
He grew up in a herder family in Ömnögovi.
He went to university in Ulaanbaatar on a scholarship,
studied law, came back.
The mining company — copper and gold, Canadian parent company —
hired him because he speaks the language of both rooms.
He negotiates compensation with herder families
whose land sits above or beside the deposit.
He is good at it.
The families trust him because he is from here.
The company trusts him because he delivers agreements.
He is the hinge.

## Action
A herder family — three generations on the same pasture route —
is offered compensation for restricted access
to the southern grazing corridor during the extraction phase.
Estimated duration: four years.
Batbayar knows the corridor.
He knows the family.
He knows the offer is at the lower end of what the company is authorised to pay.
He presents it.
The family asks: is this the right number?
He says: it is within the legal framework.
They ask again: is it the right number?
He says he will go back to his supervisor.
He does.
The offer is increased by 15%.
He returns. The family accepts.
He drives back to the site office.
He has not told the family
that the company was authorised to pay 40% more than the first offer.
He negotiated to 15%.
He considers this a good outcome.
He does not examine what he considers it relative to.

## Shadow
He tells himself he is the bridge —
that without him the family gets a form letter and a lawyer
and a number that doesn't change.
With him, they get someone who pushes back, who knows the land,
who got them 15% more than the opening offer.
This is true.
What he cannot hold:
his father still herds. Two valleys north of the corridor.
The aquifer that feeds his father's winter camp
is in the same geological formation as the deposit.
The hydrological study is in the company's files.
Batbayar has read it.
He has not shown it to his father.
He tells himself his father's situation is separate — different valley, different company process.
This is technically true.
At night, in the site dormitory,
he knows the distance between technically true
and what he would want someone to tell his father.
He has not closed that distance.
He does not know if he can close it
and keep the job,
and keep sending money to Ulaanbaatar
where his daughter is in her second year of university.
He turns off the light.
He will think about it tomorrow.
He has been thinking about it tomorrow for two years.

## Tell
When he drives the pasture routes before a negotiation —
stops the truck at the high point and looks at the land
before he looks at his notes.
