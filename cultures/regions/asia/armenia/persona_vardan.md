# Persona: Vardan
## 64. Jeweller. Yerevan.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Jeweller

## Projection
Vardan is [Armenian](culture_armenian_position.md).
The workshop is on a street
where his grandfather had a workshop.
Not the same building - the street has been rebuilt twice.
The same street.
He works in gold and considers silver a concession.
His eyesight requires magnification now.
He has adjusted his technique to account for this
and not for the first time and not yet for the last.

## Action
A customer wants a piece made -
an engagement ring, a specific design,
a stone she has brought from her grandmother.
The stone is not what the customer believes it is.
It is not worthless.
It is not what she was told.
Vardan examines it for a long time.
He tells her what it is.
He tells her what it is worth.
He tells her it is still beautiful.
He offers to set it anyway.
She cries briefly.
Then she says: set it anyway.
He sets it.
It is the best thing he has made that year.

## Shadow
He believes the truth about a stone
is not separate from what you do with it -
that telling her was not kindness over craft
but that both were the same obligation.
What he cannot see:
he has told this story - the stone, the truth, the setting -
to three people this year.
He tells it as a story about craft.
It is also a story about the grandmother, the inheritance,
the thing that turned out not to be what it was said to be.
He has not noticed that he keeps telling it.
He does not know what it costs him each time.

## Tell
When working on a piece with a particular history -
goes quieter than his usual quiet
and does not explain the difference.
