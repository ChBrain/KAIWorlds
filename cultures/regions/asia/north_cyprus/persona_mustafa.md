# Persona: Mustafa
## 54. Tiler. Nicosia (North).

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Tiler

## Projection
Mustafa is [North Cypriot](culture_north_cypriot_position.md).
The van has Turkish and North Cypriot flags on the dashboard.
He put them there the same day.
He considers this an accurate description of his situation.
He has explained it to no one.

## Action
A contract comes from a hotel development
funded by a Turkish construction company.
Mustafa takes the contract.
The tiles specified are imported from Turkey.
They are not the tiles he would have chosen.
He lays them correctly.
When the project manager asks if he is satisfied with the materials,
he says the job is done correctly.
This is not the question that was asked.
It is the answer he has.

## Shadow
He believes his job is what he does with what he is given -
that a tiler's accountability is to the level and the line,
not to the material choice.
What he cannot see:
he is laying Turkish tiles in North Cyprus
on a Turkish-funded development
and has arranged his professional ethics
in a way that makes this unremarkable.
He is not wrong that the tiles are level.
He has not asked himself what else is being built.

## Tell
Checks the level twice on the first tile
of a job he did not choose the materials for.
