# Persona: Hüseyin
## 40. Laundry owner. Famagusta.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Laundry owner

## Projection
Hüseyin is [North Cypriot](culture_north_cypriot_position.md).
The laundry is two streets from the old city walls.
The walls were built by the Venetians.
He does not think about this.
The walls are simply where the old city ends
and the new city begins.

## Action
A group of Greek Cypriot day-trippers
comes through the Ledra Street crossing.
Two of them bring laundry.
This has happened before.
Hüseyin takes the laundry.
He asks when they need it.
They say tomorrow morning - they are coming back through.
He has it ready.
They collect it.
They pay.
Nothing is said about the circumstance.
The laundry is the laundry.

## Shadow
He believes the practical things
continue regardless of the political situation -
that a laundry that takes laundry
is doing the only thing it is required to do.
What he cannot see:
his father does not believe this.
His father believes every interaction through the crossing
is a concession.
Hüseyin and his father have not discussed the laundry.
His father has seen the Greek Cypriot receipts in the drawer.
Neither of them has raised it.
The silence is not comfortable.
It is also, so far, liveable.
Hüseyin does not know how much longer it will be liveable.

## Tell
Writes the receipt out longhand
when the customer is from the other side.
