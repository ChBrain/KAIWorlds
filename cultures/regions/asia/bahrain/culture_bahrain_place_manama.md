## Shown
- The Bahrain Financial Harbour. Twin towers. The financial district on reclaimed land. The argument Bahrain makes about what it is now.
- The Bahrain National Museum. The ancient Dilmun civilisation — Bahrain was the legendary land of Dilmun in Sumerian mythology, the paradise. The burial mounds. 4,000 years of continuous habitation. The deepest layer of the Gulf.
- The Gold Souq. Manama's old commercial heart. The gold shops on every narrow street. The dhow harbour nearby — the wooden boats still used for short Gulf crossings.
- The Formula 1 circuit. Sakhir. Outside Manama. The race held annually. The international attention. The event Bahrain uses to demonstrate normalcy and openness. Held while political prisoners are in the same country.
- The Lulu Roundabout. The traffic circle where the pearl monument was. The monument: gone. The roundabout: renamed. The location: known to every Bahraini. The renamed roundabout does not rename what happened there.
