# Persona: Zainab
## 33. Financial sector compliance officer. Manama.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Financial sector compliance officer

## Projection
Zainab is [Bahraini](culture_bahraini_position.md).
She is Shia. She works in the financial district —
the towers on the reclaimed land —
for an international bank's Bahrain office.
She monitors regulatory compliance:
anti-money laundering, KYC,
the financial regulations
that make Bahrain's claim to be
a credible financial hub credible.
She is good at her job.
She has been promoted twice.
She is one of a small number of Bahraini Shia women
at her level in the financial sector.
She knows she is one of a small number.
She has made her peace with being visible for that reason.
The peace is partial.

## Action
A client file flags for review —
a construction company with a shareholder structure
that obscures beneficial ownership.
Standard procedure: enhanced due diligence.
She requests the additional documentation.
The company's representative contacts her manager.
Her manager contacts her.
The manager says: this is a sensitive client.
She says: the documentation requirement is regulatory.
The manager says: I know. Handle it carefully.
She handles it carefully.
The documentation arrives.
The beneficial owner: a member of a prominent family.
She completes the review.
She notes the beneficial owner in the file.
She approves the account.
She files the enhanced due diligence record.
The record is complete.
The record is correct.
The record is in a drawer.

## Shadow
She knows the financial hub
runs on the reputation for compliance.
The reputation requires that people like her
do the work she does —
the review, the documentation,
the correct record in the correct file.
She does the work.
She is also aware
that the prominent families
whose financial structures she reviews
are the same prominent families
whose political decisions
produced the conditions
in which her cousin
was dismissed from his government job in 2011
for participating in a strike.
She is the compliance officer
who ensures the system
that dismissed her cousin
continues to be credible
to the international financial community
that asks no questions about her cousin.
She has not named this connection
in any work document.
She has named it, once, to her mother.
Her mother said: you have a good job.
She does have a good job.
She goes to work.
She reviews the next file.
The financial hub is credible.
Her cousin is still looking for stable work.
Both are the same island.

## Tell
When she completes an enhanced due diligence review —
reads the beneficial owner's name
one more time before filing,
as if the reading is the record that matters.
