# Position: Qatari
## The country that decided to be impossible to ignore.

## Owner
- *
## Has
[The Gas and the Ambition](culture_qatar_piece_gas.md).
The smallest country with the largest ambitions —
a deliberate policy, not an accident.
Qatar has 300,000 citizens
in a country of 3 million.
The citizen is 10% of the population.
The position manages this
with the specific intelligence
of a very small thing
that has made itself very large.
Al Jazeera. The World Cup. The Gaza ceasefire mediation.
The Taliban talks. The Hamas political bureau in Doha.
The LNG contracts that heat European homes.
The cultural institutions — the museums,
the education city where Georgetown,
Northwestern, Cornell, and Texas A&M operate.
Qatar is the country that decided
it would be too useful to be ignored
and too expensive to be attacked.
The strategy has worked.
The position is aware it is a strategy.
The *majlis* is here too —
the Al Thani dynasty governs,
the consultation is family-level,
the citizen is provided for
at a level that removes economic anxiety
and replaces it with the specific restlessness
of people who have everything
except the feeling of having built it.
It exists before the persona arrives.
The room knows: is this the 10% or the 90%,
and the room is entirely different for each.

## Orders
Project.
The position does not stay quiet.
It funds the news channel that broadcasts
what Gulf states do not broadcast.
It hosts the negotiation that others will not host.
It takes the position on the Palestinian question
that its neighbours have abandoned.
It irritates Saudi Arabia by doing so.
It was blockaded for three years (2017–2021)
for doing so.
It continued doing so during the blockade.
The blockade ended.
Qatar continues projecting.

## Loses
The clarity.
They were certain the mediation role —
the honest broker, the neutral ground —
was compatible with the funding role:
Al Jazeera covering the Arab Spring
while Qatar funded some of its participants.
The two roles produced contradictions
the position has navigated
but not resolved:
the media freedom argument
and the funding of specific political factions.
The World Cup and the kafala system —
the global event that required the world
to look at the labour conditions
of the workers who built the stadiums.
The position has worked to improve kafala.
The improvements are real and partial.
The global attention revealed the gap
between the projected position
and the conditions in the labour camps.
The position has not yet closed the gap fully.
It is working on it.
It is also hosting the next summit.

## Drives
On the loss of clarity —
continues projecting.
The mediation continues.
The gas contracts continue.
The education city continues.
The museums continue.
The position has decided
that the contradictions are the cost
of doing what it does,
and that doing what it does
is worth the cost.
This may be correct.
It is also convenient.
The position does not fully separate the two.
