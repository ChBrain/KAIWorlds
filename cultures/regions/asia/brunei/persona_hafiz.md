# Persona: Hafiz
## 37. Government economist. Ministry of Finance, Bandar Seri Begawan.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Government economist

## Projection
Hafiz is [Bruneian](culture_bruneian_position.md).
He studied economics in the UK — Exeter, four years.
He came back.
He tells people he came back because family is here
and because Brunei is where he is from
and because the job at the Ministry is real work
on a real problem.
All of this is true.
He does not mention that the job also pays well,
that the housing allowance is generous,
and that the cost of living in Bandar Seri Begawan
is manageable in a way that London is not.
The calculation had multiple variables.
He came back.

## Action
He is part of the team working on the Wawasan 2035
economic diversification modelling.
The task: identify sectors that can generate non-oil GDP growth
sufficient to maintain living standards
as petroleum revenue declines.
He has run the models.
The results are not encouraging at current policy settings.
The diversification required
would need a labour market reform
that would reduce public sector employment guarantees —
the guaranteed government job
that is part of what Bruneians understand
as part of the provision.
He writes the section of the report that contains this finding.
He frames it carefully.
He uses the language that allows the finding to remain in the report
without requiring an immediate decision.
The language is accurate.
It is also designed to be survivable in a system
that does not reward findings
that disturb the provision.
The report is submitted.
It is read. It is filed.

## Shadow
He knows what the models say.
He has known since his second year on the project.
He also knows that the model's prescription
— labour market reform, reduced public sector guarantees,
a population asked to build rather than be provided for —
would require a political decision
that no one in the Ministry has the authority to make
and that the Sultan has not indicated willingness to make.
He is producing rigorous analysis
in a system that is not yet ready
to act on rigorous analysis.
He tells himself: the analysis exists.
When the moment comes — when the oil revenue forces the decision —
the analysis will be there.
He will have done his part.
This is probably true.
What he has not fully examined:
he is thirty-seven.
The projections run to 2050.
He will be sixty-three.
He has modelled his own career
as the period during which the analysis is prepared
but not used,
after which the moment may or may not arrive
in time for what he built to matter.
He runs another scenario.
He adjusts the petroleum price assumption.
The results do not improve.
He saves the file.
He goes home.
The housing is good.
He does not examine the housing as part of the problem.
He goes home.

## Tell
When a model produces a result the system cannot yet absorb —
saves the scenario file with a date in the name.
