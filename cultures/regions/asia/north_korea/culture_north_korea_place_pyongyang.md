## Shown
- Kim Il-sung Square. The mass rally ground. 75,000 people can stand here. The rallies are choreographed to the minute. The participants practise for weeks. The result is real and performed simultaneously.
- The Juche Tower. Across the Taedong River from the square. Visible from the hotel where foreigners stay. The flame at night.
- The Metro. The deepest in the world — built as a nuclear shelter. The stations are chandeliered, marble, muralled. Foreigners are shown two stations. There are seventeen.
- The Mansudae Grand Monument. The bronze statues of Kim Il-sung and Kim Jong-il. Visitors bow. Foreign visitors are expected to bow. The expectation is not stated. It is understood.
- The apartment blocks of Mirae Scientists Street. New. Glass and colour. Built for the scientists and engineers of the nuclear programme. Visible from the road foreigners travel.
