# Piece: The Juche Idea
## The ideology of self-reliance that made dependence total.

## Owner
- **Place:** Pyongyang

## Place
Pyongyang. The Juche Tower.
170 metres. One torch flame for each year of Kim Il-sung's life at the time of construction.
The flame is lit at night.
It is visible from the hotel where foreigners stay.
That is part of the design.

## Load Bearing
Remove Juche and North Korea is a Soviet satellite state
that lost its patron in 1991 and collapsed.
With it: North Korea is a state with an indigenous ideological framework —
self-reliance, independence from foreign influence —
that survived the Soviet collapse by defining dependence as the enemy
even as it became dependent on China.
The contradiction is the point.
Juche says: we stand alone.
The practice says: we accept Chinese fuel, food, and diplomatic cover.
Both are true. The ideology resolves the contradiction
by making the ideology itself the measure —
any dependence that serves Juche is not dependence.
Remove the piece: the regime loses its legitimating framework.
With it: a population that has been given a total explanation
for why the world is the way it is,
why they sacrifice,
why the enemy is the enemy,
why the suffering has meaning.
The explanation is false in important ways.
It is also complete.
A complete false explanation
is more stable than an incomplete true one.
This is what makes it load-bearing.

## Apparent
1945: Korea liberated from Japan. Divided at the 38th parallel.
Kim Il-sung installed in the north by the Soviets.
1950–1953: the Korean War. Three million dead.
The armistice. No peace treaty. The war technically continues.
1955: Kim Il-sung first articulates Juche — self-reliance — in a speech.
1972: Juche enshrined in the constitution as state ideology.
The Juche Tower built in 1982 for Kim Il-sung's 70th birthday.
1994: Kim Il-sung dies. The Arduous March — the famine.
Estimates: 240,000–3.5 million dead. The range reflects the information control.
Kim Jong-il inherits. The system holds.
2011: Kim Jong-il dies. Kim Jong-un inherits at 27.
The system holds.
The nuclear programme: first test 2006. Ongoing.
The logic: Juche requires that the country defend itself.
The nuclear weapon is the Juche argument made material.
2024: North Korea sends troops to support Russia in Ukraine.
The world outside notices.
Inside: the frame explains it or does not mention it.
Both are available to the system.
