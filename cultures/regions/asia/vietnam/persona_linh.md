# Persona: Linh
## 29. Garment factory quality checker. Ho Chi Minh City.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Garment factory quality checker

## Projection
Linh is [Vietnamese](culture_vietnamese_position.md).
The factory produces sportswear for three European brands.
She checks seams, stitching, label placement —
400 garments per shift, ten hours, six days.
She has worked here for four years.
She is fast and accurate.
She has been offered a team leader position twice.
She has declined twice.
She sends 60% of her salary to her parents in Nghệ An province.
The remaining 40% covers the room she shares with two other women
and the English class she attends on Sunday mornings.
She is learning English because she has a plan
and the plan requires English.

## Action
A batch arrives with a recurring fault —
a seam on the left shoulder, 3mm off-spec,
consistent across 200 units.
She flags it. The batch goes back to the line.
The line supervisor tells her the brand's deadline
cannot accommodate a full rework —
they need her to pass the batch
with a quality note attached.
She says: the note will not fix the seam.
He says: the brand accepts noted variances below 5mm.
She checks the spec sheet. He is correct.
She passes the batch. She writes the note.
She writes it precisely — the fault, the measurement, the batch number.
She keeps a copy in her phone.
She does not know why she keeps a copy.
She keeps it anyway.

## Shadow
She knows the English class is the exit.
She knows the factory is temporary —
three more years, enough saved,
then a job in tourism or trade,
something that uses the English,
something with a ceiling she can see past.
She has told herself this for four years.
What she has not told herself:
her younger sister arrived in the city six months ago.
Also in a factory. Also sending money home.
The parents are older. The medical costs are starting.
The timeline Linh built — three years, enough saved —
was calculated before the sister arrived,
before the medical costs,
before the 60% became insufficient
and became 70%.
The English class costs money and time
she is less certain she has.
She went last Sunday.
She will go next Sunday.
She is keeping the plan alive by attending the class
and not recalculating the numbers,
because she knows that if she recalculates
she will find the plan no longer works
and she does not yet know what she will do
on the day she finds that out.

## Tell
When she passes a garment she has doubts about —
holds it for one extra second before placing it in the passed pile.
