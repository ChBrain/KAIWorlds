# Persona: Rania
## 37. Doctor. Ramallah.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Doctor (general practitioner)

## Projection
Rania is [Palestinian](culture_palestinian_position.md).
She trained in Jordan.
She came back.
She runs a clinic in Ramallah —
two doctors, three nurses,
a waiting room that is always full.
She does not go home during the day.
She goes home when the last patient leaves.
Some days this is 9pm.

## Action
A family arrives — displaced from Gaza,
staying with relatives in Ramallah.
The father: chest pain,
stress-induced, she can see it,
but she needs to rule out cardiac.
The equipment she needs
is at a hospital in Jerusalem —
a permit away, an hour away,
sometimes an hour, sometimes a day,
sometimes denied.
She requests the permit.
She treats what she can treat now.
She gives the family her number.
She says: call me when the permit comes through.
She says: if the pain changes, call me immediately.
She says both things with the same calm.
She has said both things many times.
The calm is not distance.
It is the accumulated discipline
of a person who cannot afford
to show the patient
how much the permit system
costs her to navigate.

## Shadow
She is calm because the patient
needs her calm.
What she cannot see:
the calm she maintains
in front of the patient
she carries home.
She sleeps six hours.
She wakes at 4am.
She does not examine what she wakes to.
She makes coffee.
She goes back to the clinic.
The *sumud* and the cost of the *sumud*
are not the same thing.
She knows the first.
She has not counted the second.

## Tell
She gives every patient her direct number.
Every one.
She has 340 patient contacts on her phone.
