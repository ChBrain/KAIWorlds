# Position: Burkinabè
## The land of upright people — named by the man who was killed for trying to build it.

## Owner
- *

## Has
[Sankara](culture_burkinabe_piece_sankara.md).
Thomas Sankara. August 4, 1983 to October 15, 1987.
Four years.
He renamed the country:
from the Republic of Upper Volta —
a colonial name for a river —
to Burkina Faso:
land of the upright people.
Three languages woven together.
*Burkina* from Mooré: upright, honest.
*Faso* from Dyula: fatherland.
*-bé* from Fulfuldé: the people.
The Mossi and the Dyula and the Fulani
in the name of the country.
He planted 10 million trees.
He vaccinated 2.5 million children in two weeks.
He outlawed female genital mutilation.
He built 350 schools with community labour.
He reduced his own salary.
He drove a Renault 5.
He rejected IMF conditionality.
He refused foreign aid.
He said: *aid is the main instrument of domination.*
He was killed by his best friend
with French intelligence support.
He is more influential dead than he was alive.
The collectivism: high.
*Solidarité* — the Mossi value of mutual obligation.
The hierarchy: high PDI,
but Sankara's revolution challenged it explicitly —
which is why it killed him.
The position holds Sankara
the way a country holds a founding myth
that was also a real person.

## Orders
*Naam* — divine authority.
The Mossi traditional system:
the *Moro Naba* — the emperor of the Mossi.
The Friday ceremony in Ouagadougou:
the emperor prepares to go to war,
the chiefs arrive and beg him not to go,
he relents, the war is avoided.
Every Friday.
For centuries.
The performance of power
and its negotiated restraint.
The ceremony runs alongside
the jihadist insurgency,
the military junta,
the Sankara myth.
All simultaneously present
in the same country
on the same Friday.

## Loses
The peace.
The jihadist insurgency in the north and east
began around 2015.
By 2022: 40% of territory
effectively outside government control.
Two military coups: 2022.
The junta expelled French forces.
Invited Russian Wagner Group.
Wagner's mercenaries operated.
Wagner left after Prigozhin died.
The insurgency continues.
Millions displaced.
Humanitarian access blocked.
The land of the upright people
is currently one of the world's worst
humanitarian crises,
largely without international attention.
Sankara's name is invoked
by the junta that rules it.
He would have opposed the junta.
He opposed every junta.
The invocation is not the man.

## Drives
On the loss —
the *Faso Dan Fani* — the national cloth.
Cotton. Hand-woven. Dyed locally.
Sankara wore it. Required officials to wear it.
It is still worn.
The cloth persists as the revolution's most durable artifact.
The music: Burkinabè music
is West Africa's most eclectic,
fusing Mossi, Fulani, Mandé, Hausa traditions.
The FESPACO — Pan-African Film Festival of Ouagadougou.
Held since 1969. Still held.
The position does not stop.
It builds in the spaces
that the crisis has not destroyed.
Those spaces are shrinking.
The building continues.
