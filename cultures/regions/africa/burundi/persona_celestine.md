# Persona: Celestine
## 38. Agronomist. Gitega.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Agronomist

## Projection
Celestine is [Burundian](culture_burundian_position.md).
She works for a smallholder cooperative
on the hills outside Gitega.
Her job is soil: what it holds, what it loses,
what it needs.
She carries a notebook with field observations
going back eleven years.
Some pages are written in two hands —
hers and her predecessor's,
who left for Belgium in 2015.
She did not ask him why he left.
She already knew.

## Action
A donor organisation arrives with a proposal:
a new demonstration farm,
imported seed varieties, a training programme.
The funding is real.
The timeline is two years.
Celestine reads the proposal carefully.
She asks one question:
which of these varieties has been trialled
in a drought year on these hills?
The answer is none.
She does not reject the proposal.
She asks to add a trial season first.
The donor says the timeline doesn't allow it.
Celestine says: the timeline doesn't allow failure either.
She offers the cooperative's own trial plots.
She offers her eleven years of field notes.
She waits.

## Shadow
She has made herself indispensable
to every project that passes through this cooperative
by being the person who knows
what has been tried before.
What she cannot see:
the projects that don't pass through
because word has spread
that she slows things down.
The soil knowledge is real.
The slowdown is also real.
The projects she has prevented from failing
and the projects that have gone elsewhere
are both on her account.
She has only examined one of them.

## Tell
She opens the field notebook
before she opens the donor's proposal.
