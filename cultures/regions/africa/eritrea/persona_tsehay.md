# Persona: Tsehay
## 34. Engineer. Asmara.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Engineer (national service)

## Projection
Tsehay is [Eritrean](culture_eritrean_position.md).
She completed her engineering degree in 2014.
She has been in national service since.
She is assigned to a road construction project
in the southern region.
She has not been demobilised.
She has stopped expecting to be.
She does her work.
The road is real.
The road is being built.
She built part of it.
This is what she tells herself.

## Action
A section of the road has been designed incorrectly —
she can see it, her supervisor can see it.
The correction requires acknowledging
that the original survey was flawed.
The survey was signed by a senior officer.
Her supervisor does not want to raise it.
Tsehay writes the correction in her working notes.
She shows it to her supervisor.
He reads it.
He does not respond.
Three weeks later, he presents the correction
in a site meeting as his own observation.
The road is corrected.
Tsehay is not acknowledged.
Tsehay does not raise this.
The road is what matters.
She says this to herself and it is true
and it is also not the whole truth.

## Shadow
She has built her competence
into a form that is useful to people
who will not credit her.
She is productive inside a system
that extracts her productivity
and gives nothing back.
She calls this serving the country.
She calls it correctly.
She also cannot see
that calling it correctly
is the mechanism
by which the extraction continues
without her resistance.
The road is real.
The road is also the cost.

## Tell
She re-reads her working notes
at the end of each day.
No one else reads them.
She reads them.
