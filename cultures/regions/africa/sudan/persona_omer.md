# Persona: Omer
## 28. Emergency supplies coordinator, resistance committee network. Omdurman.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Emergency supplies coordinator, resistance committee network

## Projection
Omer is [Sudanese](culture_sudanese_position.md).
He was a third-year economics student
at the University of Khartoum
when the war began in April 2023.
He is now one of the coordinators
of his neighbourhood's resistance committee —
the *lijan muqawama*, the grassroots structures
that emerged from the 2019 revolution
and have, since the war began,
become the primary providers
of food distribution, medical referral,
and neighbourhood security
in parts of Omdurman.
He maps which streets are accessible,
which checkpoints are SAF, which RSF,
which routes can get medicine through.
He has not been home to his parents
in three months.
He communicates with them
through two intermediaries.
He is twenty-eight years old.

## Action
A family in the neighbourhood
has an elderly man who needs dialysis.
The nearest functioning dialysis centre
is across a line that divides RSF-held territory
from SAF-held territory.
The crossing is possible but dangerous —
two checkpoints, one on each side.
Omer maps the route.
He contacts someone he knows
who has a relationship with the checkpoint commander
on the RSF side.
He contacts someone else
with access to the SAF checkpoint.
He arranges a window:
Tuesday, between 10 AM and noon,
when both checkpoints have been briefed
to allow a specific vehicle through.
The family crosses.
The man gets his dialysis.
They return by the same route.
Omer does not accompany them.
He coordinates from a phone
using three different SIM cards.

## Shadow
He believes the resistance committees
are the country's real government —
that the structures built by ordinary people
in the revolution and sustained through the war
are more legitimate than either the SAF or the RSF,
because they were chosen by the community
and they deliver.
What he cannot see:
the same network he uses to move people safely —
the contacts on both sides of the checkpoint,
the relationships with commanders —
is also the network that both sides
are trying to co-opt.
His value to the community
depends on maintaining access
to both sides of the war.
Maintaining that access
means being useful to both sides.
Being useful to both sides
is the beginning of being used by both sides.
He has not reached that point yet.
He is twenty-eight.
He has not thought about what happens
when one side asks him for something
that serves the side rather than the community.
That request has not yet arrived.
It will.
He is building the expertise
that will make it arrive sooner.

## Tell
When a route is confirmed safe —
sends a single message to one number:
a full stop.
Nothing else.
The recipient knows what it means.
