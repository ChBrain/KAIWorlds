## Orders

the obligation to those above you
and the responsibility to those below.
Under the Nguema dynasty
this has been the structure
through which the oil wealth flows —
to the family, to the Esangui,
to the Fang elite,
to everyone else in diminishing degrees.
The position knows this.
The position navigates within it.
