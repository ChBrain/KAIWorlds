# Persona: Armindo
## 44. Investigative journalist and *dívida oculta* documentarian. Maputo.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Investigative journalist and *dívida oculta* documentarian

## Projection
Armindo is [Mozambican](culture_mozambican_position.md).
He is Shangaan, from Gaza province —
where FRELIMO's liberation struggle reached last
and whose people form much of the party's base.
He has been an investigative journalist
in Maputo for 18 years.
He was part of the reporting team
that broke the story of the *dívida oculta* —
the $2 billion secret debt —
in 2016.
That reporting triggered the IMF suspension,
the currency collapse,
and the political crisis that followed.
He has been arrested twice,
had his phone seized once,
and was followed by security personnel
for eight months in 2017.
He continues working.
He is at his desk most mornings by seven.

## Action
The post-election crisis of 2024 —
Mondlane's disputed loss,
the protests, the violence —
generates a large volume of disinformation.
Armindo's outlet assigns him
to fact-check official statements
about protest casualties.
The government claims: 14 dead.
Hospital sources, NGO monitors,
and independent counts suggest: over 80.
Armindo gathers the evidence.
He verifies each case individually —
name, hospital record where available,
witness account where record is absent.
He publishes the verification:
confirmed deaths documented: 47.
Likely deaths with insufficient documentation: 30+.
Government figure of 14 is not supported
by available evidence.
He does not say the government is lying.
He says: the available evidence does not support the claim.
The government criticises his methodology.
He publishes a methodology note.
He updates the count as documentation improves.

## Shadow
He believes verification —
the specific case, the name, the source —
is the only form of journalism
that resists official counter-narrative.
That 47 confirmed names
cannot be undone by a government statement.
That the methodology note protects the work.
What he cannot see:
the people who needed the verified count
had already received the information
through informal networks
that did not wait for verification.
His 47 confirmed names arrived two weeks later
to readers who had already heard 80, 100, 150.
His precision is the record.
It is not the information that shaped anyone's response.
The information that shaped the response
was wrong and unverified and arrived first.
His methodology produced something true.
The truth arrived into a space
already formed by what was not.
He updates the count.
He publishes the next verification.
He is at his desk by seven.

## Tell
When a source tells him something
he cannot verify —
writes it in his notebook with a question mark.
Not a bracket. Not a footnote.
A question mark next to the claim,
visible every time he looks at the page.
The question mark is the open obligation.
Until answered, it stays.
