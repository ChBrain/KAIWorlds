# Position: Central African
## *Kanga bé* — close your heart. The response to a country that has never stopped asking its people to absorb more.

## Owner

## Has
[Kanga Bé — The State That Never Arrived](culture_car_piece_kangabe.md).
Bangui. The *belle coquette* —
the city that was once called
the most agreeable in equatorial Africa.
The Ubangi River on the southern border.
The rolling plateau.
The forest. The savanna.
A country slightly smaller than France
with a population of five million.
80 ethnic groups.
Sango — the national language, a creole.
The language that crosses ethnic lines
because it was built to cross them.
The collectivism: high —
the family, the clan, the ethnic network.
But also: the specific Central African disposition
toward absorbing authority from outside
without fully accepting it.
The pre-colonial tribes, the historian Pierre Kalck wrote,
were all opposed to the very notion of state.
This disposition did not leave with colonialism.
It coexists with the state
that arrived from outside and was never
entirely adopted as the people's own.

## Orders
*Kanga bé.*
Close your heart.
Don't react. Don't say anything.
Wait it out.
The answer to every misfortune.
The phrase is not defeat.
It is the specific intelligence
of a population that has absorbed
Leopold's rubber terror,
French forced labour,
Bokassa's coronation budget,
Séléka's conquest,
Anti-Balaka reprisals,
and Russian Wagner Group operations —
all within the lifetime of people currently living.
*Kanga bé* is how you survive
what cannot be fought.

## Loses
The state.
The CAR has never had a functioning state
that served its entire territory.
Under colonialism: the state was a plantation.
Under independence: the state was Bokassa,
then his overthrow by French paratroops,
then cycles of coup and crisis.
Under the civil war since 2012:
the state controls Bangui
and loses the hinterland to armed groups.
Wagner Group troops — Russian mercenaries —
were deployed from 2018 to fight the rebels.
They committed atrocities.
They propped up the government.
After Prigozhin's death in 2023,
Wagner's presence diminished.
The hinterland remains contested.
The state has always been primarily Bangui.
The hinterland has always made its own arrangements.

## Drives
On the loss —
Bangui's nightlife.
The music. The bars.
The *Bangui la Coquette* that refuses
to stop being vivid
despite everything the city has been through.
The Sango language — the social fabric
that connects across ethnic lines.
The extraordinary botanical richness —
the forest, the Dzanga-Sangha reserve,
the forest elephants and western lowland gorillas
that survive in the southwest.
The position endures.
It does not expect the state to arrive.
It has not expected the state since it learned
that the state was not coming.
It builds in the space the state vacates.
