# Persona: Augustin
## 43. Constitutional law lecturer and civil society monitor. Porto-Novo.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Constitutional law lecturer and civil society monitor

## Projection
Augustin is [Beninese](culture_beninese_position.md).
He teaches constitutional law
at the Université d'Abomey-Calavi.
He writes a monthly analysis
of Beninese constitutional developments
for a civil society platform
read primarily by lawyers, NGO workers,
and international observers.
He was a student during the 1990 conference.
He was fifteen years old in his memory of it:
the father coming home, saying
something has changed.
That memory is the foundation
of his professional life.
He has spent twenty years
building the analytical tools
to document what is happening to what 1990 built.

## Action
The 2021 elections are held.
Major opposition parties have been excluded
through new eligibility requirements
critics argue were designed for exclusion.
Augustin writes his analysis.
It documents: the changed electoral code,
the sequence of legal changes since 2018,
the comparison with the 1990 framework.
He names what he sees precisely —
neither overstating nor softening.
He calls it: *a significant regression
from the 1990 democratic framework
in the direction of competitive authoritarianism.*
He does not call it a coup.
He does not call it a dictatorship.
He calls it what the evidence shows.
A government official contacts the university
to express concern
about the platform's framing.
The university dean calls Augustin.
Augustin reads the dean the text.
He asks: what specifically is inaccurate?
The dean does not answer.
Augustin says: I wrote what the evidence shows.
The dean says: be careful.
Augustin thanks him.
He publishes the next analysis.

## Shadow
He believes precise language
is the only tool that cannot be easily dismissed —
that calling things exactly what they are,
no more no less,
creates a record that political actors
cannot claim was partisan.
What he cannot see:
his insistence on precision
is also a form of restraint
that political actors have learned to work with.
They know Augustin will not call it a coup.
There is a vocabulary that is safe.
They operate within that vocabulary.
The document that says *regression*
does not produce the same pressure
as the document that says
what the regression adds up to.
He is precise because precision is honest.
Precision is also what allows
the situation to continue being named
without triggering the response
that would stop the naming.
He has not resolved whether
his precision is the last honest act available
or the last comfortable one.
He continues writing.
The analysis is in the record.

## Tell
When reading a constitutional text —
marks the original provision in one colour,
the amendment in another.
Side by side.
The comparison visible before he writes a word.
