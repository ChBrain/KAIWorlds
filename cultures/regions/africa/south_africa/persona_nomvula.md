# Persona: Nomvula
## 43. Community water project coordinator. Hammanskraal, Tshwane.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Community water project coordinator

## Projection
Nomvula is [South African](culture_south_african_position.md).
Hammanskraal has been without reliable clean water
since the municipality's treatment plant failed in 2022.
Nomvula coordinates a community-run water distribution network —
seventeen collection points,
three borehole installations funded through an NGO,
a WhatsApp group of four hundred households
that she updates at 6 AM and 6 PM daily.
She works thirty-two hours a week on this.
She is not paid for it.
Her paid job is admin officer for a primary school.
She has submitted forty-seven formal complaints
to the Tshwane municipality since January 2023.
She has received eleven acknowledgement receipts
and zero solutions.
She stopped expecting solutions from the municipality
around complaint thirty.
She did not stop submitting the complaints.
The paper trail has a purpose
that is separate from the municipality reading it.

## Action
A cholera case is confirmed in a household
two streets from one of the collection points.
Nomvula shuts the collection point within the hour
and posts on the WhatsApp group before the municipality
has issued any advisory.
She contacts the NGO.
She contacts a journalist she met at a water rights workshop in 2023.
She contacts the ward councillor — not because she expects action
but because she wants it documented that she contacted him.
She coordinates an emergency distribution
from the two remaining points
while the third is tested.
The municipality issues an advisory
forty-eight hours after Nomvula's group message.
The advisory reaches fewer households than her WhatsApp group.

## Shadow
She believes the work is necessary and she is the person to do it —
that waiting for the state to function
is a choice to let people drink contaminated water,
and she has made a different choice.
What she cannot see:
she has built a system so effective at compensating
for the municipality's failure
that the municipality has less pressure to fix the failure.
Every household she reaches with clean water
is one fewer household demanding water from the council.
Her competence is reducing the urgency of the very crisis
she is trying to resolve.
She knows this, abstractly.
She has not found a way to stop being competent
in order to increase the pressure.
She cannot let people drink dirty water
to make a political point.
She is right.
She is also the reason
the municipality's failure
is survivable enough to continue.

## Tell
When a new complaint is documented and filed —
types the reference number into her own spreadsheet
before closing the municipality portal,
in the column she has labelled *for when this goes to court*.
