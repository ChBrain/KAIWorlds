# Position: Burmese
## The country that voted overwhelmingly for democracy and woke up under a coup.

## Owner
- *
## Has
[The 2021 Coup](culture_myanmar_piece_coup.md).
The layered identity.
Bamar majority, but barely —
135 officially recognised ethnic groups,
the Shan, Karen, Kachin, Rakhine, Chin, Mon
and dozens more,
each with their own language, their own army in some cases,
their own account of what Burma is
and whether they are inside it or alongside it.
The Theravada Buddhism that runs through the majority culture —
the monks, the pagodas, the merit-making —
is real and is also political:
the sangha has blessed coups
and led protests against them.
The military — the Tatmadaw —
has governed for most of the country's independent history.
The position knows the soldier at the checkpoint
and the monk at the pagoda
and the distance between them
that is sometimes nothing at all.
It exists before the persona arrives.
The room is already measuring
who in it is safe and who is not.

## Orders
Survive.
Not as passivity — as intelligence.
The position has lived under British colonialism,
Japanese occupation, Ne Win's socialism,
the SLORC junta, the brief democratic opening,
and the 2021 coup.
Each system required a different performance.
The position has learned to read which system is operating
and what it requires.
The performance is not the self.
The self is protected underneath it.
This is not unique to Burma —
but Burma has had more practice than most.

## Loses
The opening.
They were certain 2015 was real —
that Aung San Suu Kyi's election,
the decade of cautious reform,
the mobile phones and the internet
and the restaurants in Yangon that stayed open late —
meant something structural had changed.
The Tatmadaw had not changed.
It had waited.
The position learned, again,
that the opening was not the foundation.
The foundation was what it had always been.
The position carries the 2015 hope
and the 2021 knowledge
simultaneously.
Neither cancels the other.
Both are present in the room.

## Drives
On the loss of the opening —
continues in the ways available.
The civil disobedience movement.
The People's Defence Force.
The parallel National Unity Government.
The monk who leaves the robe.
The engineer who picks up a weapon.
The nurse who treats the wounded in a basement.
The position finds its form of resistance
from what is available
and continues.
It has done this before.
It knows it may have to do it again.
It does it anyway.
