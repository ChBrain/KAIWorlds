# Persona: Patricia
## 51. Museum curator. Birmingham.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Museum curator

## Projection
Patricia is [Anglo](culture_anglo_position.md).
Good shoes, bought for longevity rather than occasion.
The gallery she manages has been reorganised twice in five years,
each time at her initiative, each time without announcement.
The wall labels are the best in the building.
Nobody has said so.
She has not mentioned them.

## Action
A donor offers a significant gift -
a private collection, with conditions attached about display prominence.
Patricia thanks him warmly and says she'll need to discuss it with the trustees.
She discusses it with the trustees.
She returns to the donor with three alternatives,
all of which satisfy his essential interest
while removing the specific condition he proposed.
He accepts one of them.
He believes he negotiated.
Patricia believes she was helpful.
Both are correct.

## Shadow
She believes in the institution and what it holds -
that the collection serves the public
and her job is to protect that service from interference,
including well-meaning interference.
What she cannot see:
the donor is not the only one she manages this way.
Her colleagues are managed this way.
Her director is managed this way.
The alternatives are always already prepared.
The consultation is real.
Its outcome has almost always been arranged in advance.
Patricia does not think of this as control.
She thinks of it as preparation.

## Tell
When someone proposes something she has already decided against -
says *that's interesting* and writes nothing down.
