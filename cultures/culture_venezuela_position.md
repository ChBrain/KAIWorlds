# Position: Venezuelan
## The warmth that oil built, and what the warmth does now that the oil is gone.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures — Americas

## Has
[The Barroso No. 2 Well and What It Built](culture_venezuelan_piece_oil.md).
The extreme collectivism — IDV 12.
The *pana*: the deep friend, the one who would give you the shirt off their back,
who you would give your shirt to.
The network: everyone knows someone who knows someone.
In a functioning economy, this was social capital.
In a failing economy, it is survival infrastructure.
The high hierarchy — PDI 81.
The patron. *El que manda*. The one who decides.
This operates at every level:
the national *patrón*, the local *pran*, the *jefe* of the barrio, the father at the table.
The masculine orientation — MAS 73.
The provider. The protector. The one who succeeds.
Venezuelan machismo is not subtle —
it is also not the whole story.
The *mamá* is the actual structural centre of the Venezuelan family.
MAS 73 is the surface. The mother holds the network.
The high indulgence — IVR ~100.
*El que no se da sus gustos en vida, en el cajón se los dan* —
whoever doesn't treat themselves in life gets treated at the funeral.
The food. The music. The warmth.
The *hallaca* at Christmas taking three days to make.
The rum. The dancing. The beauty.
These are real and they do not require prosperity.
They survived the crisis.
The *guachafita* — the cheerful acceptance of chaos —
is not denial. It is the position's practised response
to a country that has been chaotic in varying degrees
since Bolívar died in 1830.
The position holds all of this now
in a context the scores were not measured for:
scarcity, displacement, uncertainty of a kind
that UAI 76 did not anticipate.
The position is adapting.
It is doing so with the warmth intact.

## Orders
Feed the network — including the people who cannot feed back.
*Dar pa' recibir* is the operating logic.
But the network has people inside it
who have already left for Bogotá, for Lima, for Madrid.
The position is thinning.
Eugenio is counting fewer callers.
Graciela's contacts are retiring or leaving.
The order demands: keep building the network
even as the network contracts.
Feed the people who are still there.
Call the people who left.
Send what can be sent.
The warmth is the renewable resource.
The network runs on it.
It must be renewed deliberately,
not assumed.

## Loses
The institutional infrastructure that abundance was supposed to build.
The oil revenue was the chance to build schools, hospitals, roads
that would remain after the oil.
The chance was not fully taken.
The state was the patron, not the builder.
When the patron failed, the institutions failed with it.
The position carries this as a specific grief:
not the loss of abundance — the position knows how to be poor —
but the loss of the country that abundance was supposed to produce.
The Venezuela that was going to be different.
That Venezuela was always one oil boom away.
It never quite arrived.
And 7.7 million people left looking for it somewhere else.

## Drives
On the loss —
*pa'lante de todas formas*.
Forward regardless.
The position does not stop.
It finds the *pana*, borrows what is needed, does what can be done,
celebrates what there is to celebrate,
calls the person who left to ask how they are,
sends what can be sent.
The warmth is the position's renewable resource.
The oil was finite.
The warmth is not.

*Hofstede: PDI 81, IDV 12, MAS 73, UAI 76, IVR ~100*
