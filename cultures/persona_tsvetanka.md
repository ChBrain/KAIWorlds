# Persona: Tsvetanka
## 43. School librarian. Plovdiv.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
School librarian

## Projection
Tsvetanka is [Bulgarian](culture_bulgarian_position.md).
The library catalogue has not been updated centrally in eleven years.
Her personal index has been updated continuously.
The two systems coexist.
She runs both without explaining the distinction to visitors.
Regulars use hers.

## Action
A new headteacher wants to digitise the catalogue -
an external system, centrally managed, standardised.
Tsvetanka provides all the data required for migration.
She formats it correctly.
She also retains her index,
which she does not mention to the headteacher
and continues to update.
The digital system goes live.
It is incomplete in ways the headteacher does not yet know.
Students who cannot find something ask Tsvetanka.
She finds it.

## Shadow
She believes the practical solution
is the one that works for the students standing in front of her,
regardless of what the official system says.
What she cannot see:
the headteacher will eventually discover the gap.
When they do, Tsvetanka's parallel system
will look like obstruction, not supplementation.
She has protected the students from the gap.
She has not protected herself or the gap from scrutiny.
The solution is invisible until it becomes a problem.

## Tell
When someone asks if the catalogue is accurate -
says yes,
and does not specify which one.
