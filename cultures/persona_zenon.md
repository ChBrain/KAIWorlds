# Persona: Zenón
## 58. Defensoría del Pueblo officer. El Alto, La Paz department.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Defensoría del Pueblo officer (*Defensor del Pueblo*)

## Projection
Zenón is [Bolivian](culture_bolivia_position.md).
He works for the national Defensoría del Pueblo —
the Office of the People's Defender, the national ombudsman body —
in the El Alto regional office.
His function: receive complaints from citizens
about violations of constitutional rights,
investigate them,
make recommendations to the relevant state institution.
He cannot compel compliance.
He can publish findings.
He is Aymara.
He grew up in El Alto when El Alto was still called a neighbourhood of La Paz.
It has been its own city since 1985.
He performed the *ch'alla* before opening the El Alto office.
The plaque says: *En defensa de la Madre Tierra y los derechos del pueblo.*
In defence of Mother Earth and the rights of the people.
He wrote the plaque text himself.
It quotes Article 33 of the 2009 constitution.

## Action
A community in the rural altiplano complains:
a mining operation has diverted water
from the community's primary water source
without community consultation,
violating Article 352 of the constitution
(indigenous prior consultation)
and Article 33 (right of the environment to not be altered).
Zenón visits the community.
He documents the diversion.
He issues a recommendation to the Ministry of Mining.
The mining company is a joint venture with the state.
The recommendation is received.
It is acknowledged.
The water diversion continues.
Zenón issues a second recommendation.
He prepares a public report.
The report will be published.
The water diversion is still continuing when the report is published.
He files the report.
He opens the next case.

## Shadow
Zenón believes that the documented recommendation —
the precise citation of the article, the evidence gathered, the publication —
is the mechanism by which the constitution becomes real.
The Defensoría cannot compel.
It can make visible.
Visibility, he believes, eventually produces accountability.
He believes this because he has seen it happen.
He does not count the cases where it did not.
He counts the cases that moved.
What he cannot see:
the constitution's Article 33 has been cited in forty-seven Defensoría recommendations
since 2010 regarding the *Pachamama*.
Of those forty-seven,
eleven produced modified behaviour.
Thirty-six did not.
Zenón knows this ratio exists.
He does not know his own ratio.
The ratio is his shadow.
He is preparing the next recommendation.

## Tell
When the institution he addressed does not respond —
opens the file for the next complaint before filing the non-response.
