# Piece: The 1987 EU Application
## The door that defined the wall.

## Owner
- **Place:** Ankara

## Place
Ankara. The Grand National Assembly.
Where the application was formally decided.
Also: Brussels. Where it has been waiting since 1987.

## Load Bearing
Remove it and the Turkish position loses its
38-year-old organising principle.
Turkey is not defined by EU membership.
It is defined by the pursuit of EU membership
that has not concluded.
The candidacy shaped Turkish law, Turkish institutions,
Turkish foreign policy, and Turkish domestic politics
for four decades.
The door that does not open
still organises the room around it.

## Apparent
An application letter. April 14, 1987.
The European Commission opinion: 1989 — not yet ready.
The Helsinki European Council: 1999 — candidate status officially granted.
Accession negotiations: 2005 — opened.
Accession negotiations: 2018 — effectively suspended.
The application still formally active.
38 years of documentation.
Filed in Ankara. Processed in Brussels.
Still open.

## Yearbook
1963: the Ankara Agreement. Associate membership.
The relationship begins.
1987: formal application for full membership.
1999: official candidate status — Helsinki.
The Turks wait.
2004: EU enlargement. Ten new members.
None of them Turkey.
2005: accession negotiations open.
2013: negotiations effectively stall over Cyprus chapter.
2016: the coup attempt. Democratic backsliding.
The EU freezes most chapters.
2018: EU Parliament votes to suspend negotiations.
The application remains formally active.
Erdoğan calls EU membership still a strategic goal.
The EU calls Turkish behaviour incompatible with membership.
Both positions are maintained simultaneously.
2024: Turkey still a candidate.
Still waiting.
The wait is now older than many EU member states' membership.
