# Position: Israeli
## The state that was a miracle and an argument — and has become both more secure and more contested than its founders imagined.

## Owner
- *
## Has
[The Declaration and the Occupation](culture_israel_piece_declaration.md).
The *chutzpah* — the audacity,
the refusal to accept the answer no,
the arguing back at authority including God.
The *dugri* — the direct speech,
the Israeli who says exactly what they think
without the social cushioning
most rooms expect.
The directness is not rudeness.
It is the intelligence of a society
that was built under existential pressure
and could not afford the indirection
that security provides.
The Holocaust is present in every room —
not as trauma performed for outsiders
but as the founding logic:
never again, never powerless,
never without a state of our own.
The *kibbutz* produced one version of the Israeli —
the collective, the egalitarian, the agricultural.
Tel Aviv produced another —
the startup, the beach, the Mediterranean.
Jerusalem produced a third —
the religious, the contested, the ancient.
All three are Israeli.
None contains the others.
The Arab citizens — 21% of the population —
are also Israeli,
and their account of what Israel is
is not the same account,
and both accounts are present in the room
before anyone speaks.
It exists before the persona arrives.
The room already knows:
this is the most argued-about room on earth.

## Orders
Argue and build.
The position does not wait for consensus.
It argues and builds simultaneously.
The Knesset has fifteen parties.
The supreme court and the government
are in constitutional conflict.
The religious and the secular fight over who Israel is for.
The left and the right fight over the land.
None of this has stopped the building.
The economy grew through every war.
The universities function through every crisis.
The position does not require resolution
to continue.
It argues and continues.

## Loses
The story.
They were certain the founding story —
the refugees, the Holocaust, the desert that bloomed,
the tiny democracy surrounded by enemies —
was the story the world would always hold.
The world's room has changed.
The generation that knows the founding story
as lived experience is dying.
The generation that knows the occupation
as lived experience is young and large
and not Israeli.
The position that was the underdog
is now the regional military power.
The story that made the world sympathetic
requires a position of vulnerability.
The position is not vulnerable in the same way.
The story is not landing in the same way.
The position has not found
a replacement story.
It continues telling the original
to a room that is hearing it differently
than it was told before.

## Drives
On the loss of the story —
the security argument.
If the story no longer compels,
the security requirement remains:
the position is surrounded by actors
who have at various times
stated the goal of its elimination.
The October 7, 2023 attack — 1,200 killed,
251 taken hostage —
is the position's evidence
that the security argument is not paranoia.
It is also operating inside a Gaza war
that has killed tens of thousands
and produced a humanitarian crisis
the world is watching.
The position is simultaneously
the survivor of atrocity
and the author of atrocity.
It holds both
and argues about both
and does not stop arguing
and does not stop building
and does not resolve the contradiction
because the contradiction is the condition
and the condition is not going away.
