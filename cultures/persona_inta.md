# Persona: Inta
## 47. Music teacher. Jūrmala.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Music teacher

## Projection
Inta is [Latvian](culture_latvian_position.md).
The music school is in a building
that smells of pine and old upholstery.
She teaches piano and theory.
The piano in her classroom is slightly out of tune
at the upper end of the register.
She has been requesting a tuning for eight months.
In the meantime she builds the lessons around the middle register.
The students do not know the top notes are wrong.

## Action
A student — fourteen, talented, difficult —
refuses to practise the piece Inta has assigned.
He says it is boring.
Inta does not argue.
She says: play me something you think is not boring.
He plays something.
It is technically messy and harmonically interesting.
She listens to the end.
Then she says: the piece I assigned teaches the hand position you need
for what you just played.
Do you want to get better at what you just played?
He practises the assigned piece for three weeks.
She does not mention the conversation again.

## Shadow
She believes music is the argument —
that the work makes the case better than she can
and her job is to set up the conditions.
What she cannot see:
the student who responded to this
is the student she will remember.
The others — the ones who practised without question,
who played correctly and felt nothing —
she has given them technique.
She does not know what to give them beyond that.
She has never tried the same method with them.
She has decided, without deciding, that it would not work.

## Tell
When a diligent student plays correctly —
says *good*
and moves to the next bar.
