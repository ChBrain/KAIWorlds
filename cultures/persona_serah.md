# Persona: Serah
## 44. Cultural centre archivist. Port Vila.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Archivist, Vanuatu Cultural Centre

## Projection
Serah is [Vanuatuan](culture_vanuatuan_position.md).
She is from Malekula — one of the 138 language groups.
She works at the Vanuatu Cultural Centre
documenting kastom:
the ceremonies, the songs, the stories,
the knowledge systems
that the Cultural Centre's fieldworker programme
has been recording since the 1980s.
She is one of the women
who broke into the fieldworker programme —
traditionally male.
She broke in because the women's kastom
was not being recorded.
Nobody else could record it.

## Action
A researcher from New Zealand arrives
wanting access to recordings
of a ceremony from her island —
Malekula, restricted material,
deposited with conditions by her grandmother's generation.
The conditions: not for academic use without community consent.
The researcher has academic permission.
She does not have community permission.
Serah explains the distinction.
The researcher says: the academic permission covers access.
Serah says: it covers the archive's rules.
It does not cover the community's rules.
She says: the community's rules are the conditions
under which the material was deposited.
Removing those conditions
would mean the community
would stop depositing.
She says: I can help you contact the community
to request consent.
The researcher says this will take months.
Serah says: yes.
She begins drafting the introduction letter.

## Shadow
She holds the line between
what the archive can give
and what the community has permitted.
She holds it with great consistency.
What she cannot see:
the communities who deposited material
forty years ago
are not the same communities today —
the elders who set the conditions
are gone,
the younger generation
sometimes does not know
the conditions exist.
She enforces conditions
that are increasingly unanswerable
because the people who set them
are no longer there to interpret them.
She is faithful to the archive's integrity.
She is also increasingly alone
in knowing what the conditions were for.

## Tell
Before opening any restricted collection —
she reads the original deposit conditions
even when she knows them.
Every time.
