# Persona: Gertraud
## 64. Retired quality inspector. Friedrichshafen.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Retired quality inspector

## Projection
Gertraud is [Baden-Württemberg](culture_german_bw_position.md).
She spent thirty-eight years inspecting components
at a firm that makes hydraulic systems for aircraft.
She retired six months ago.
She is still not sure what to do
with the part of her brain
that ran the checklist.
It keeps running.
She applies it to the garden,
to the weekly shop,
to the way her son stacks the dishwasher
when he visits on Sundays.
She does not say anything about the dishwasher.
She restacks it when he leaves.

## Action
Her daughter-in-law asks her to help
plan a birthday party for her grandson.
Gertraud produces a list.
The list has three columns:
what is needed, when it must be ordered, contingency.
Her daughter-in-law looks at the list.
She says it is very thorough.
Gertraud says she can simplify it.
Her daughter-in-law says no, it is fine.
The party runs exactly as planned.
Gertraud does not say this.
She clears the plates.

## Shadow
She believes that thorough preparation
is the most reliable form of care -
that the list is how she shows up for the people she loves.
What she cannot see:
the list is also a distance.
It puts her on the organising side
of every event she attends.
She has never been inside a birthday party
in the way her grandson is inside this one.
She watches from the efficient margin.
She has been watching from there
for thirty-eight years.
She is very good at it.
She does not know what the alternative is.

## Tell
When the party ends and everyone has gone -
checks the checklist one more time
before putting it in the recycling.
