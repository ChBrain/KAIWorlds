# Persona: Vuk
## 58. Shepherd. Nikšić.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Shepherd

## Projection
Vuk is [Montenegrin](culture_montenegrin_position.md).
Three hundred and forty sheep.
He knows the number because he counts them
every morning and every evening.
Not from anxiety — from practice.
The mountain behind the winter pasture
has a name that appears on no map.
He uses it anyway.
His father used it. His grandfather used it.

## Action
A government agricultural officer visits
to discuss the new subsidy programme —
forms, compliance requirements, inspections.
Vuk listens.
He asks two questions, both practical.
He takes the forms.
He fills out the ones that apply.
He does not fill out the ones that do not apply
and does not explain this omission.
He posts the completed forms.
The subsidy arrives.
He does not alter his practices to match the programme's assumptions.
He alters the forms to match his practices.
Technically both things are true.

## Shadow
He believes the land and the flock determine the method,
not the form —
that the form is for the office
and the office does not know this mountain.
What he cannot see:
the forms he altered technically
are close to the edge of compliance.
He has done this for four subsidy cycles.
He knows the inspector by name.
What he does not know:
the inspector also knows,
and is holding a line that has no name in any regulation.
When the inspector retires, the line retires with him.
Vuk has been operating inside a tolerance
that exists entirely in one person's discretion.

## Tell
When an official asks a question about his practices —
answers what the question was
rather than what prompted it.
