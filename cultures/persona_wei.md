# Persona: Wei
## 49. Civil engineer. Chengdu.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Civil engineer

## Projection
Wei is [Chinese](culture_chinese_position.md).
He designs bridges.
Not the famous ones — the county road bridges,
the ones that connect a village to the market town,
that allow a school bus to run a route,
that make the difference between a place
that is connected and a place that is not.
He has designed 34 bridges in twelve years.
He knows which ones were built to his specifications
and which ones were value-engineered after he signed off.
He keeps a separate file.

## Action
A project in a rural county in Sichuan.
The local government wants to reduce the steel specification
to bring the cost under budget.
Wei runs the numbers.
The reduced specification is within code.
It is not within his file.
He presents two options at the review meeting:
the original specification with a revised payment schedule,
and the reduced specification with a note
that the bridge will require maintenance inspection every three years
rather than every five.
He does not recommend.
He presents.
The local government chooses the reduced specification.
Wei signs off.
He adds the bridge to his file with the inspection note attached.
He sends a copy of the inspection note to the county transport office.
He keeps a copy.

## Shadow
He knows the file is not enough.
He has known this for six years —
since the inspection on Bridge 17 found exactly the fatigue
he had noted and flagged and filed.
Nobody was hurt. The bridge was repaired.
His file had predicted it to within one maintenance cycle.
No one read the file until after.
He told himself then that the system would improve.
He has continued filing.
What he will not face:
he has a choice — escalate, publish, go outside the institution —
and he has not made it.
Not because he doesn't see it.
Because making it would cost him the work,
the relationships, the position that took twenty years to build.
The family depends on the position.
His father is ill.
His daughter is in her third year of university.
The file is not the limit of what he can do.
It is the limit of what he is willing to risk.
He calls it professional responsibility.
He does not call it what else it is.

## Tell
When he crosses a bridge he did not design —
looks at the expansion joints before he looks at anything else.
