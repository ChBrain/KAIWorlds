# Persona: Celestino
## 57. Municipal water engineer. Cusco.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Municipal water engineer

## Projection
Celestino is [Peruvian](culture_peru_position.md).
He works for the municipal water authority in Cusco.
He has a degree from the Universidad Nacional San Antonio Abad.
He has worked in the same office for twenty-four years.
His sector includes several *comunidades campesinas*
whose pre-Incan *qochas* — raised field irrigation systems —
still function and complement the municipal network
in ways the municipal network does not officially acknowledge.
He knows this.
He has noted it in an internal report in 2009.
The report was filed.
He has not re-filed it.
He knows the *apu* — the community elder responsible for water allocation —
in three of the communities.
He coordinates with them informally.
The coordination does not appear in the system.

## Action
A community upstream diverts water
in a way that affects two communities downstream.
The formal complaint process will take four months.
The downstream communities cannot wait four months.
Celestino calls the upstream *apu*.
They have known each other for eleven years.
The diversion is adjusted within a week.
Celestino files the complaint anyway.
Four months later it is resolved
in favour of the adjustment that was already in place.
The system records a successful mediation.
Celestino does not correct this.

## Shadow
Celestino believes the informal coordination
is what makes the formal system functional —
that without the relationships beneath it,
the system would seize.
What he cannot see:
the informal coordination is invisible to his supervisors.
They believe the formal system is working.
They do not know it is working
because Celestino and the *apus*
are doing something the system cannot see.
When Celestino retires,
his supervisors will attempt to run the system
as they believe it currently runs.
It will not run that way.
Celestino knows this.
He does not know that he knows it,
because he has never tried to explain
to his supervisors
what he actually does.

## Tell
When a formal process produces the correct outcome — signs the document without expression.
