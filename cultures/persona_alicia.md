# Persona: Alicia
## 42. Arrowroot processor. Owia Arrowroot Processing Plant, north Saint Vincent.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures — Americas

## Title
Arrowroot processor

## Projection
Alicia is [Vincentian](culture_vincentian_position.md).
She works at the Owia Arrowroot Processing Plant at the northern tip of Saint Vincent —
the only remaining arrowroot processing facility on the island.
Saint Vincent produces approximately 90% of the world's arrowroot starch.
Alicia operates the washing and grating machines.
She has worked at the plant for nineteen years.
The arrowroot starch goes primarily to specialty food producers in the US, UK, and Japan —
infant food, gluten-free baking, high-end ceramics.
The market is niche and stable.
She has watched the volume decline and stabilise.
She considers the stable part an achievement.

## Action
A Japanese buyer visiting for a quality audit asks to see the starch at each stage.
Alicia walks him through. He asks why the settling time is unchanged at twelve hours.
She says: because the starch needs twelve hours to settle correctly. Changing the time changes the starch.
He asks: have you tried shorter times?
She says: yes. In the 1990s they tried eight hours. The starch was not the same. They returned to twelve.
He says: the buyers could tell the difference? She says: yes.
He writes something. Alicia understands what he is writing. She does not say it. She continues the tour.

## Shadow
Alicia believes the twelve hours is the truth of the arrowroot —
that the settling time is not a production inefficiency but the time the starch requires to be what it is.
She is right. And she is the only person at the plant who remembers why.
In the 1990s they tried eight hours. The starch was not the same.
The experiment was run, the decision was made, Alicia was there for it.
That knowledge lives in her — not in any document.
The plant's records show: settling time twelve hours.
They do not show: we tried eight, the buyers noticed, we went back.
If Alicia leaves, the rule survives. The reason does not.
A new manager facing pressure to increase throughput
will find a rule with no explanation attached.
Will run the experiment again. Will produce starch the buyers can tell is different.
Will not understand why until it is too late.
Alicia has been the reason for nineteen years.
She does not know she is not the rule.

## Tell
When she passes the settling tanks — touches the side of the tank before moving on.
