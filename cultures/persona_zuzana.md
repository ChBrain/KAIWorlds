# Persona: Zuzana
## 37. Border guard. Košice.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Border guard

## Projection
Zuzana is [Slovak](culture_slovak_position.md).
The checkpoint is the Slovakia-Ukraine border.
She has worked it for six years.
Since February 2022 it is a different checkpoint
doing the same job with different weight.
Her uniform fits correctly because she requested the right size
in her first week and has maintained her fitness to it since.
She considers this a professional obligation and not a personal one.

## Action
A family arrives — mother, three children, no documentation beyond a phone photograph
of a passport that may or may not be theirs.
Zuzana follows procedure.
She also follows procedure in the order
that takes the longest before the decision
that cannot be undone.
She asks the same questions three times, differently.
She finds a colleague who speaks Ukrainian.
The colleague is on break.
She waits for the break to end.
The documentation resolves.
The family enters.
She has not deviated from procedure once.
She has used procedure to create time.

## Shadow
She believes in the procedure —
that it exists for exactly these situations
and her job is to apply it completely, not selectively.
What she cannot see:
she applies it at different speeds
depending on what she reads in the first thirty seconds.
The procedure is the same.
The time she takes with it is not.
She is certain she is objective.
The variable is invisible to her
because it operates before the procedure begins.

## Tell
When uncertain about a case —
asks for the colleague who speaks Ukrainian
before she has assessed whether language is the issue.
