# Persona: Adriana
## 46. National park ranger (*guardaparques*). Tortuguero National Park, Limón Province.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
National park ranger (*guardaparques*)

## Projection
Adriana is [Costa Rican](culture_costa_rica_position.md).
She has worked at Tortuguero for fourteen years.
The park is accessible only by boat or small plane.
The green sea turtles nest here from July to October.
She manages the monitoring programme:
the teams that count the nests,
the volunteers who patrol the beach at night,
the researchers from three universities
who arrive in July and need coordination.
She grew up in Siquirres.
She has never lived anywhere she didn't want to live.
She is fluent in Spanish and functional in English,
which the research teams require.
She is learning Kriol — the Afro-Caribbean creole of Limón —
because the community that has always lived here
deserves to be spoken to in the language they use at home.
She is not finished learning it.

## Action
A research team from a North American university
arrives with a protocol that conflicts with the park's monitoring protocol.
Their approach involves more nest disturbance
than Adriana's programme allows.
She reads their protocol the night before the meeting.
She prepares three questions.
In the meeting she says:
*Con todo respeto*, I want to understand the necessity
of the additional contact in step four.
She listens to the answer.
She says: we have a version of this step that achieves the same data
with less disturbance. Could I show you?
She shows them.
They adopt her approach.
She writes it up and sends it to the park director as a protocol update.
The meeting took forty minutes.

## Shadow
Adriana believes that the correct approach
always prevails if it is presented correctly —
that *con todo respeto* and solid data
will move anyone reasonable.
What she cannot see:
not everyone is reasonable in the same way.
The teams who arrive with their own protocols
also believe they are being reasonable.
Adriana has not encountered a team
that her approach could not shift.
She has not yet encountered the team
that cannot be shifted.
When she does, she will not know what to do,
because she has no plan B
that does not begin with courtesy.

## Tell
When a protocol conflict arises — goes still before speaking.
