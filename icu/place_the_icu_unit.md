# Place: The ICU Unit
## Location: The intensive care unit. Three rooms. One architecture.

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU

## Shown
- Rooms running simultaneously.
  Each one is [The ICU Room](place_the_icu_room.md).
  Each one is different.
  The walls between them are thin.
  Sound travels.
- The nurses' station. Centre of the unit.
  Glass on three sides.
  Every room visible from here.
  The nurses move between rooms
  with a purposefulness that looks calm
  and is not always calm.
- [The Corridor](place_the_corridor.md).
  Runs the length of the unit.
  Doors on one side, one per room.
  The families of different beds
  use the same corridor.
  They do not always know each other.
  They learn each other's schedules.
- [The Relatives' Room](place_the_relatives_room.md).
  Off the corridor. One room.
  All three families use it.
- The unit door. Locked.
  A buzzer to enter.
  The sound of the buzzer
  is the sound of the world outside
  arriving into this one.
- The clock on the wall above the nurses' station.
  The only clock in the unit.
  Everyone in every room
  learns to read it
  from wherever they are sitting.

## Holds
- The beds assigned to this unit
  each one an instance of [The Bed](piece_the_bed.md),
  each one the entire world of one patient.
- The [Monitors](piece_the_monitor.md)
  their sounds distinct enough
  that the nurses can tell them apart.
  The families cannot, at first.
  They learn.

## Offers
- [The Corridor](place_the_corridor.md).
  To whoever needs the distance from any room.
- [The Relatives' Room](place_the_relatives_room.md).
  To all three families simultaneously.
  It does not offer privacy between them.
  It offers only the closed door
  and the kettle.
- The nurses' station.
  To whoever needs to ask something
  they cannot ask inside the room.

## Withheld
- What is happening in the other rooms.
  The families of three beds
  are adjacent to three different situations.
  They hear sounds through walls.
  They read the nurses' faces
  when the nurses move quickly.
  They do not know what they are reading.
  Sometimes they are reading correctly.
  Sometimes they are not.
  The unit does not clarify this.
  It runs all three rooms
  without commentary.
