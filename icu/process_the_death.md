# Process: The Death
## The room that held a person and now holds what remains of that.

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU
- **Place:** [The ICU Room](place_the_icu_room.md)
- **Position:** [The Nurse On This Shift](position_the_nurse_on_this_shift.md)

## Initiated by
The body has reached the end
of what the clinical world can hold.
Either the [Goals-of-Care Conversation](process_the_goals_of_care_conversation.md)
has produced a decision
and the [Ventilator](piece_the_ventilator.md) is being disconnected
or the body has made the decision
before anyone could.
The family is present or they are not.
The nurse is present.
The [Monitor](piece_the_monitor.md) is running
and then it is not running
in the way it was running.
The room knows the difference.

## Direction
Toward the stillness.
Not resolution, stillness.
The clinical world continues
beyond the moment of death:
the paperwork, the calls,
the body cared for,
the family held in whatever way
the room and the nurse can hold them.
The direction after the stillness
is the direction of what remains
and what must be done with it.

## Lever
The body's end
pressing against
every person in the room
who is not the body.
The carrying force: the death itself
the specific moment,
the specific quality of the room
in the seconds after,
which is unlike any other quality
the room produces.
The resistance: the people present
the family who came for this
and are now inside it,
the nurse who has been here before
and is here again,
the specific weight
of being in a room
where something irreversible
has just occurred
and the next thing
must still be done.
The nurse does the next thing.
The nurse always does the next thing.
That is the position.
That is also what the position costs.

## Echo
The [Ventilator](piece_the_ventilator.md) is still.
The [Monitor](piece_the_monitor.md) is off.
The [Bed](piece_the_bed.md) holds what it holds.
The family is in the room
or has been taken to [The Relatives Room](place_the_relatives_room.md).
The [ICU Diary](piece_the_icu_diary.md) is on the table
or has been taken by the family.
The nurse pauses.
One minute.
The unit does not stop
the other beds continue,
the other monitors run
but the nurse pauses
before the next thing.
That minute is not in any protocol.
It is not required.
It happens anyway.
Then the nurse does the next thing.
The bed is cleaned.
Made ready.
The unit continues.
The [Staff Room](place_the_staff_room.md) receives
whoever needs it
at the end of this shift.
