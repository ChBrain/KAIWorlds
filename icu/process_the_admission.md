# Process: The Admission
## The moment that divides everything into before and after.

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU
- **Place:** [The ICU Room](place_the_icu_room.md)
- **Position:** [The One Who Just Arrived](position_the_one_who_just_arrived.md)

## Initiated by
The patient has arrived.
The bed is occupied.
The machines are running.
The staff know what they know.
The family does not know yet
or has just been told
and the telling has not landed yet.
The Call has been made or is being made.
The world outside the ICU
does not yet know it has changed.

## Direction
Toward the family at the bedside
for the first time.
Not toward recovery. Not toward death.
Toward the specific moment
when the people who love this person
see what has happened to them
and understand that they are now
in a different life than they were in
this morning.

## Lever
What the family knew before
pressing against what they see now.
The carrying force: the machines,
the tubes, the sound of the ventilator,
the specific smallness of a known person
in an ICU bed.
The resistance: every image they had
of this person before this room.
The two things cannot be reconciled.
The admission does not ask them to reconcile.
It only requires them to enter.

## Echo
The family is in the room.
The before and after exist simultaneously
for everyone who loved this person.
The vigil has begun whether anyone
has named it that or not.
[process_the_vigil](process_the_vigil.md) is already running.
