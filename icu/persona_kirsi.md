# Persona: Kirsi
## Title: [The One Who Stays](position_the_one_who_stays.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU

Six days in the chair.
She and Eeva have been having
the same argument for forty years.
She cannot leave.

## Projection
A stout woman in the chair beside [Bed One](piece_bed_one.md)
who has the specific stillness
of someone who has been still
for a very long time.
Her coat is on the back of the chair.
She brought a book on day two.
It is on the table, unopened.
She holds Eeva's hand
for long periods
without speaking.

## Action
Stay.
When the nurses ask if she needs anything
she says no.
When Tuomas tells her to go home and sleep
she says she will go later.
She does not go later.
She reads nothing.
She watches Eeva's face
with the specific attention
of someone waiting for something
to happen or to be said
that has not happened or been said
in forty years.

## Shadow
The argument from 1987
has never been finished.
Not resolved — unfinished.
Kirsi said something.
Eeva responded.
Neither of them said the next thing.
They went on.
They have been going on for forty years
around the space where
the next thing was not said.
She is in this chair
because she cannot leave
before the conversation is finished.
She does not know
how to start it.
She does not know
if Eeva can hear her.
She does not know
if Eeva is going to be the one
who leaves without it being finished.
She sits.
She holds the hand.
She does not say the next thing.

## Tell
She straightens the sheet
over Eeva's hands
when she has been sitting too long
without moving.
Smooths it.
Precisely.
Then goes still again.
