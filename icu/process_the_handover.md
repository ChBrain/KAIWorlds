# Process: The Handover
## The ten minutes that carry everything the next shift inherits.

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU
- **Process:** [process_the_morning_shift](process_the_morning_shift.md)
- **Process:** [process_the_evening_shift](process_the_evening_shift.md)
- **Process:** [process_the_night_shift](process_the_night_shift.md)
- **Place:** [The ICU Unit](place_the_icu_unit.md)
- **Position:** [The Nurse On This Shift](position_the_nurse_on_this_shift.md)

## Initiated by
A shift is ending.
The [Nurse On This Shift](position_the_nurse_on_this_shift.md)
has eight to twelve hours of knowledge
about the patients,
the families,
the beds,
and what the night did or did not do.
The incoming nurse is at the station.
The ten minutes have begun.
The handover does not wait
for the outgoing nurse to be ready.

## Direction
Toward the incoming nurse
holding what the outgoing nurse holds
the clinical picture,
the family dynamics,
the thing that almost happened at three-thirty,
the result that came back different,
the name the patient responds to
that is not the name on the chart.
As much of this as ten minutes allows.

## Lever
What was known pressing against
what ten minutes can carry.
The carrying force: eight to twelve hours
of continuous presence
the outgoing nurse knows these rooms
in a way the chart cannot record.
The resistance: ten minutes.
The format. The clinical priority
of what gets said first.
The gap between what was observed
and what gets written down.
The night nurse writing what she knows
and not what she doesn't know.
What she doesn't know
does not transfer.
It stays in the room
without a name.
The incoming nurse begins
without knowing what was lost
in the handover.
This is always true.
It is never acknowledged as a problem.
It is the mechanism the world runs on.

## Echo
The incoming nurse holds the shift.
The outgoing nurse leaves.
What transferred: the clinical picture,
the named concerns,
the legible information.
What did not transfer:
the specific quality of a room at three-thirty,
the way the patient in the far bed
looked at the ceiling when the blood was drawn,
the daughter in Room One
who has been in the chair for six days
and whose name the outgoing nurse
finally learned last night.
The incoming nurse will learn these things again
from the beginning.
The floor does not lower.
The knowledge does not accumulate
the way it should.
Nobody has named this as a failure.
It is not a failure.
It is what happens when the thing
that cannot be written down
is also the thing that matters most.
The system runs on it.
The system does not see it.
[process_the_morning_shift](process_the_morning_shift.md) begins.
