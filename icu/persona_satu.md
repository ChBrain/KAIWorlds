# Persona: Satu
## Title: [The One Whose Place Here Is Unresolved](position_the_one_whose_place_here_is_unresolved.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU

She is Mirja's daughter.
Her place here is not in question.
What is unresolved
is the conversation she came to have
before she knew this room existed.

## Projection
A young woman who visits
every Saturday and Wednesday.
She sits beside [Bed Three](piece_bed_three.md)
and talks about ordinary things —
her work, her flatmate,
what is on television,
the neighbourhood where she lives.
She is warm. She is present.
She asks her mother questions
that have simple answers
and waits for the opening
that never quite comes
to ask the one that doesn't.

## Action
Tend the visit.
Fill it with the small things
that make the room feel
like a version of normal life —
the news from outside,
the stories that require nothing back,
the presence that does not demand.
She is good at this.
She learned it from Mirja.

## Shadow
Before her mother was admitted
Satu had decided to tell her something.
Not a secret — a direction.
A decision she had made
about her own life
that she had been not-telling
for eight months
because she did not know
how Mirja would receive it
and she needed Mirja to receive it well.
She came to that conversation
and found this room instead.
She visits twice a week.
She talks about ordinary things.
She waits for her mother
to be well enough
to have the conversation she came for.
She does not know
if she is waiting to protect her mother
or to protect herself.
Mirja, who spent twenty-six years
reading patients' faces,
knows something is being carried.
She has not asked.
She is waiting for Satu to be ready.
Neither of them knows
the other is waiting.

## Tell
Before she leaves she says:
*I'll see you Wednesday.*
Or: *I'll see you Saturday.*
Always specific.
Never *I'll see you soon.*
She is making a promise
she can keep
to stand in for the one
she cannot make yet.
