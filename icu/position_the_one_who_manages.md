# Position: The One Who Manages
## The information held so no one else has to hold it.

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU
- **Process:** [process_the_vigil](process_the_vigil.md)
- **Place:** [The ICU Room](place_the_icu_room.md)

## Has
The information.
What the doctors said. What the numbers mean.
What happens next and in what order
and what each option costs.
It arrived the moment someone
had to be the contact person
and someone always has to be
the contact person.
The position does not ask
whether this persona wanted it.

## Orders
Hold the information
so the others don't have to.
Make the calls. Speak to the doctors.
Translate the clinical language
into something the family can carry.
The position does not specify
what this costs the one who does it.

## Loses
The permission to not know.
They were certain they could manage the information
and also grieve.
The managing takes everything.
There is no room left
for the thing they came here for
to be someone who loves this person,
not someone who administers their crisis.
They look at the [Monitor](piece_the_monitor.md)
and see numbers.
They wanted to see their person.
The position gave them the numbers instead.
They cannot put the numbers down.
Nobody else in the room can pick them up.

## Drives
On the loss of permission to not know
checks the [Phone](piece_the_phone.md).
There are messages.
There are always messages.
Someone needs to know something.
They are the someone who knows it.
They type the update.
They do not put the phone down.
