# Process: The Transfer
## The exit that looks like resolution and is also a loss.

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU
- **Place:** [The ICU Unit](place_the_icu_unit.md)
- **Position:** [The Nurse On This Shift](position_the_nurse_on_this_shift.md)
- **Position:** [The Waking Patient](position_the_waking_patient.md)

## Initiated by
The clinical picture has reached the threshold
where the ICU is no longer the right place.
The [Doctor On This Round](position_the_doctor_on_this_round.md)
has made the decision.
The ward has a bed.
The [Nurse On This Shift](position_the_nurse_on_this_shift.md)
has begun the paperwork.
The family has been told.
The patient has been told.
The [Bed](piece_the_bed.md) is still occupied.
Not for much longer.

## Direction
Toward the door.
The patient leaves [The ICU Unit](place_the_icu_unit.md)
through the corridor they have never walked.
The world they have learned
every sound, every nurse,
the ceiling, the [Monitor](piece_the_monitor.md)'s specific register,
the chair with the family's shape in it
exits with them only in the form of memory.
The ward receives them.
The ward does not know what they are carrying.

## Lever
The clinical readiness to leave
pressing against
the world the patient has built inside the bed.
The carrying force: the clinical threshold
the numbers sufficient,
the ventilator gone or never needed,
the body capable of a different level of watching.
The resistance:
what six days or seventeen days or six weeks
in one bed produces in a person
the specific calibration to this room,
this noise register, this quality of being watched
continuously by people who know
exactly what the sounds mean.
The ward is quieter.
The ward is also less watched.
The patient will not know
which of those is the relief
and which is the exposure
until they have been there long enough
to find out.
The family carries its own resistance:
the chair in the corridor is not available on the ward.
The nurses' station nod does not exist there.
The family must learn a new room's language
at the moment they believed the learning was over.

## Echo
The [Bed](piece_the_bed.md) is empty.
The patient is on the ward.
The [Chart](piece_the_chart.md) has traveled with them
everything it recorded, everything it couldn't.
The [ICU Diary](piece_the_icu_diary.md) has traveled with them.
The nurses who sat with them at three in the morning
did not travel with them.
The ward team receives the chart.
They do not receive the three in the morning.
The bed is cleaned.
Made ready.
The unit continues.
