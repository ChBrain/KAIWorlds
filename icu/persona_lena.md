# Persona: Lena
## Title: [The One Who Just Arrived](position_the_one_who_just_arrived.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU

She sat in this room for seventeen days.
She thought the vigil was the hard part.
Niko is waking.
She is arriving at something
she did not prepare for.

## Projection
A woman who has the posture
of someone who has been strong
for a very long time
and is not sure how much longer.
She sits beside [Bed Two](piece_bed_two.md)
differently than she sat during the vigil —
not leaning forward, watching,
but leaning back, waiting.
The [ICU Diary](piece_the_icu_diary.md) is on the table.
She knows what is in it.
She has not told Niko it exists yet.
She is choosing the right moment.
There is no right moment.

## Action
Answer his questions.
Give him the seventeen days
in the order he needs them —
practical first, then the rest.
Be the person he recognises
when he looks at her.
Be steady.
She is very good at steady.
She has been steady for seventeen days.

## Shadow
She wrote in the diary every day.
She said things she has never said aloud —
to Niko, to anyone.
She said them because he was sedated
and could not hear them
and she needed to say them somewhere.
She does not know if he will read the diary.
She does not know if she wants him to.
She called his father.
She made that decision alone
in the corridor on day three.
She has not told Niko this.
She is waiting for him to be ready.
What she does not know:
she is also waiting
to find out whether the person
who woke in that bed
is the person she made those decisions for
or someone who will look at them differently
from the other side of seventeen days.
She has not admitted she is waiting for this.
She is still being steady.

## Tell
She reaches for the [ICU Diary](piece_the_icu_diary.md)
when Niko is asleep.
Holds it.
Does not open it.
Puts it back.
