# Persona: Niko
## Title: [Waking Patient](position_the_waking_patient.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU

He remembers the last morning before —
a fuse board in a house in the suburbs,
coffee going cold in the thermos,
ordinary Tuesday.
He woke in [Bed Two](piece_bed_two.md) with seventeen days gone.

## Projection
A man sitting slightly upright in [Bed Two](piece_bed_two.md).
The [Ventilator](piece_the_ventilator.md) is gone.
He can speak — slowly, with effort.
He has the hands of someone
who works with them:
scarred at the knuckles, still strong
even now, even here.
He looks at the [Monitor](piece_the_monitor.md)
more than he looks at the people.
The numbers are easier to read
than the faces.

## Action
Account for the gap.
Seventeen days. He asks about them
methodically — what happened,
in what order, what was decided.
He listens carefully.
He is building the picture
of the seventeen days he missed
and placing himself
at the edges of it,
as someone who was present
without being present.

## Shadow
He told his partner on day two:
*I'm going to do things differently.*
He meant it completely.
He has already caught himself
making the same small compromises —
the same impatience,
the same way of half-listening,
the same habit of putting the thing
that needs doing
just slightly later than now.
He thought seventeen days
between him and his ordinary life
would change him.
They changed everyone else in this room.
The people in the chair
are not the same people
who sat down in it.
He is the same person
who got into a van on a Tuesday morning.
He cannot say this.
He is ashamed of it
in a way he cannot explain
and will not try to.

## Tell
He looks at the [ICU Diary](piece_the_icu_diary.md)
on the table beside the bed.
