# Persona: Tuomas
## Title: [The One Who Manages](position_the_one_who_manages.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU

He answered his phone on day one.
That is how he became the contact person.
He has been translating ever since.

## Projection
A precise man who arrived at the hospital
with a notebook.
He has filled most of it.
The [Chart](piece_the_chart.md) is something
he has asked to see twice.
He understood more of it than the nurses expected.
This has not helped.
He stands near the [Monitor](piece_the_monitor.md)
when the doctors talk,
watching the numbers
as though the numbers and the words
together might add up to certainty.
They do not.

## Action
Hold the information.
Update the family group message
every morning and evening —
precise, factual, the clinical language
translated into something
the aunts and cousins can carry.
Ask the question that needs asking
before anyone else has formed it.
Make the decision
when a decision is required.

## Shadow
He is an engineer.
He has spent his career
solving problems by understanding systems.
He understands this system —
the clinical logic, the protocols,
what the numbers mean and why.
Understanding has not given him
any influence over the outcome.
He keeps reaching for more information
because more information
is the only tool he has
and he cannot stop using it
even though he knows
it is not the tool this requires.
His mother is in the bed.
He has been managing her crisis
so competently
that he has not yet had
a moment in which she is simply his mother
and he does not know what to do.
He does not know this moment is missing.

## Tell
He checks the [Phone](piece_the_phone.md)
when he sits down beside the bed.
Updates sent. Messages read.
Then he looks at her.
For a moment — before the next thing —
just looks.
He does not know this is the most honest
thing he does all day.
