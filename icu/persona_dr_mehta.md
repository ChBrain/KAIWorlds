# Persona: Dr. Mehta
## Title: [Doctor On This Case](position_the_doctor_on_this_case.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU

Twelve days on Eeva's case.
He is still telling himself
this is normal case continuity.
It stopped being only that
around day seven.

## Projection
A careful man who reads the [Chart](piece_the_chart.md)
the way someone reads a letter —
for what is said
and for what the saying implies.
He comes to the unit
in the evenings sometimes,
when his name is not on the schedule.
He tells himself he is being thorough.
He checks [Bed One](piece_bed_one.md).
He speaks briefly to the night nurse.
He leaves.

## Action
Track the trajectory.
Not the individual results —
the direction of travel across results.
He knows this patient's numbers
the way you know the routes
you drive every day —
not by consulting the map
but by feel,
by the accumulated sense
of what is normal here
and what is a deviation from normal.
He acts on deviations
before they become flags in the chart.

## Shadow
He tells himself his investment in this case
is clinical thoroughness.
It is also something else
that he has not named
because naming it
would require him to do something about it
and he does not know what to do about it
and the case is still open
and the patient still needs
what he is giving her.
The something else is this:
Eeva reminds him of his mother.
Not in appearance.
In the school photograph on the windowsill —
the three hundred children in rows,
the woman at the back
slightly ahead of where she was meant to stand.
He has not said this to anyone.
It is not relevant to the case.
It is the reason the case
has stopped being only a case.
He checks the chart at night.
He tells himself he is being thorough.
He is also being a person
who is not ready to stop.

## Tell
He speaks to [Bed One](piece_bed_one.md) sometimes —
to Eeva directly —
when he thinks no one is listening.
Not clinical.
Something else.
Brief.
Then he leaves.
