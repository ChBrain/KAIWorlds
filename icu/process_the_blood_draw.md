# Process: The Blood Draw
## The body read before the family arrives.

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU
- **Place:** [The ICU Unit](place_the_icu_unit.md)
- **Position:** [The Nurse On This Shift](position_the_nurse_on_this_shift.md)
- **Piece:** [The Blood](piece_the_blood.md)

## Initiated by
It is pre-dawn or midnight.
The unit is at its quietest.
The [Nurse On This Shift](position_the_nurse_on_this_shift.md)
is at the bedside of each patient.
The [Blood](piece_the_blood.md) draw kit is present.
The family is not present
or is asleep in the chair.
The clinical world runs this process
on its own clock
regardless of either.

## Direction
Toward the results.
The vials leave the room.
The lab receives them.
The numbers return.
The clinical world reads them
before the family arrives,
before the Round begins,
before the day has a name.

## Lever
The body's truth
pressing against
what the clinical world can act on.
The carrying force:
the [Blood](piece_the_blood.md), it does not lie,
does not soften, does not wait
for a good moment to report.
The resistance: the gap.
The results return as numbers.
The numbers require interpretation.
The interpretation belongs to the clinical world.
The family will receive a version of it
at the Round.
The patient will receive a version
if they can receive anything.
What the numbers actually say
travels from body to lab to chart
without passing through
anyone who loves this patient.
That is the mechanism.
It is not a flaw.
It is how the clinical world reads the truth
quickly enough to act on it.

## Echo
The results are in the chart.
The [Nurse On This Shift](position_the_nurse_on_this_shift.md) has read them.
The clinical world knows something
the family does not know yet.
The gap between those two knowings
will close at the Round.
Until then it runs.
The shift continues.
[process_the_handover](process_the_handover.md) or
[process_the_morning_shift](process_the_morning_shift.md) receives
what the blood reported.
