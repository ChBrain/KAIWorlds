# Process: The Threshold
## The body that leaves before the mind has decided to.

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU

## Initiated by
The vigil has been running.
The accumulation has reached
whatever each person's body can carry.
The [Corridor](place_the_corridor.md) is outside the door.
The door is always there.
It does not announce itself.
It is simply available
which is the whole of its pressure.

## Direction
Toward the corridor or the staying.
Not a decision. A discovery.
The body already knows.
The mind finds out after.

## Lever
The accumulated vigil pressing against
the specific reason each person is here.
The carrying force: everything
the hours have done to this person
the hunger they haven't named,
the phone calls they haven't made,
the life outside that has been running
without them.
The resistance: the specific fear
of being absent if something changes.
Not leaving the person.
Leaving the moment.
The corridor does not promise anything.
It only offers the absence of the room.
That is enough.

## Echo
Someone has left the room.
Someone has stayed.
Both know something the other doesn't.
The room is different for both.
What the corridor does to the one
who steps into it
depends entirely on what they were carrying
when they left.
[process_the_vigil](process_the_vigil.md) continues
for those who remain.
