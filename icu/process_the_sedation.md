# Process: The Sedation
## The parallel world running inside the room.

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU
- **Place:** [The ICU Room](place_the_icu_room.md)
- **Position:** [The Sedated Patient](position_the_sedated_patient.md)
- **Piece:** [The Ventilator](piece_the_ventilator.md)

## Initiated by
The [Sedated Patient](position_the_sedated_patient.md) is in the [Bed](piece_the_bed.md).
The [Ventilator](piece_the_ventilator.md) is breathing for them.
Sedation is running.
The room is present around them
and they cannot locate themselves inside it.
The family is at the bedside or not.
The nurses are working or not.
None of this is available to the patient
in the form the room believes it is.

## Direction
Toward whatever the sedated body makes
of what it receives.
Not absence, translation.
Voices arrive without faces.
Touch arrives without context.
The [Monitor](piece_the_monitor.md) sounds without meaning.
The body is running its own version
of the room.

## Lever
What the room is doing
pressing against
what the sedated body can receive.
The carrying force: the room continues
the family speaks, the nurses work,
the monitor runs, the light changes
from day to night and back.
The resistance: the sedation.
It does not block everything.
It translates everything
into something the brain
can almost but not quite
assemble into sense.
Familiar voices sometimes reach through.
The body sometimes responds
to what the mind cannot process.
The nurses know this.
They speak to the patient anyway.
The family is told to speak to the patient.
Not all of them can.

## Echo
The sedation continues or is interrupted.
If interrupted: [process_the_awakening_trial](process_the_awakening_trial.md).
If continued: the parallel world runs on.
What the patient receives in this state
is not retrievable afterward
in any reliable form.
Some of it stays anyway.
The body holds what the mind cannot file.
