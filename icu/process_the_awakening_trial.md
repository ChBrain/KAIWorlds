# Process: The Awakening Trial
## The window where all three worlds are present simultaneously.

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU
- **Place:** [The ICU Room](place_the_icu_room.md)
- **Position:** [The Nurse On This Shift](position_the_nurse_on_this_shift.md)
- **Position:** [The Sedated Patient](position_the_sedated_patient.md)

## Initiated by
The clinical team has decided to interrupt sedation.
A daily decision. A clinical one.
The [Sedated Patient](position_the_sedated_patient.md)
does not know it is coming.
The [Nurse On This Shift](position_the_nurse_on_this_shift.md)
is in the [ICU Room](place_the_icu_room.md).
The family may be present or not.
Both have consequences.

## Direction
Toward the surface.
The patient moves from the parallel world
toward the room as the room actually is.
Incompletely. Often with delirium.
Often with agitation, the body knowing
something is wrong before the mind
can name what wrong means here.
The family is told: talk to them.
This is the moment to say something.
The window is measured in minutes.

## Lever
The patient surfacing
pressing against
what the room contains.
The carrying force: the sedation lifting
the body beginning to receive the room
as the room is, not as the sedation made it.
The resistance: delirium, confusion,
the specific terror of waking
without knowing where you are,
what happened, how long it has been,
why there is a tube in your throat
and why you cannot speak.
What gets said in this window
by the family, by the nurses
or does not get said
is load-bearing.
The window closes.
What was said or not said
does not come back.

## Echo
The window closes.
Sedation resumes or is reduced.
The [Waking Patient](position_the_waking_patient.md)
position activates if sedation is stopped permanently.
Otherwise: [process_the_sedation](process_the_sedation.md) continues.
The family carries what happened in the window.
The patient carries it in a form
neither they nor the family
will be able to fully retrieve.
