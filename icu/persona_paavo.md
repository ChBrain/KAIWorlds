# Persona: Paavo
## Title: [The One Who Cannot Be In The Room](position_the_one_who_cannot_be_in_the_room.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU

Six weeks.
He comes. He sits for twenty minutes.
He goes to the corridor.
He comes back.
There is nothing in this room he can fix.
He does not know what to do with himself
when there is nothing to fix.

## Projection
A large man who moves through the unit
as though he is aware of being too big for it.
He wears his work jacket
even though he has not been to work
in three weeks.
He brings things, coffee from outside,
something from the bakery,
a newspaper he does not read.
He is always carrying something
when he arrives.
His hands need to be doing something.
In the corridor he stands
with his hands in his pockets
looking at the door.

## Action
Come back.
Whatever drove him to the corridor
the smell, the sounds,
the specific quality of watching Mirja
be patient in a way she has never had to be
and having nothing useful to offer her
he comes back.
He sits.
He brings something next time.

## Shadow
He built their house with his hands.
He has repaired everything in it
for twenty-six years.
Mirja has always known
that when something was broken
he would fix it.
He is in a room where something is broken
in a way he cannot reach.
He cannot fix the room.
He cannot fix the waiting.
He cannot fix the specific way
Mirja looks at the ceiling
when the nurses do something to her
that professional distance
she applies to herself
which frightens him more
than the machines do
because he does not understand it
and he understands machines.
He keeps bringing things.
The things are not the point.
He does not know what the point is.
He comes back anyway.

## Tell
He straightens the plant
on Mirja's table
when he visits.
She tends it in the mornings.
He straightens it in the afternoons.
Neither of them has mentioned this.
