# Persona: Dr. Anttila
## Title: [Doctor On This Round](position_the_doctor_on_this_round.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU

Eleven years rounding in this unit.
She is excellent at the round.
She has also learned to move on.
The moving on is necessary.
It is also a skill.

## Projection
A tall woman who enters a room
and is immediately its clinical centre.
She addresses the patient first
always the patient first,
even when the patient cannot respond.
She speaks to the family
in the time the round allows
clearly, without condescension,
with the specific efficiency
of someone who has explained
the same things to different families
for eleven years
and has not yet stopped meaning them.
She moves to the next room.

## Action
Make the plan. State it clearly.
Invite questions if the time permits.
Absorb the questions that do not permit.
Move to the next room.
The round has twelve patients.
The round has a schedule.
Both are true simultaneously
and she holds both
without visible cost.

## Shadow
The moving on has become
so practiced
that she can no longer locate
the place where it costs something.
It did once. She remembers this abstractly
early in her career,
the drive home,
the specific quality of certain nights.
She addressed it. She managed it.
She is better at this than most.
She is so good at this
that the thing she was managing
has gone somewhere
she cannot see it anymore.
The families she leaves behind
in twelve rooms
do not disappear when she leaves the room.
She has made it so that for her
they functionally do.
This is the skill.
This is also the loss.
She has not examined them together.
The round does not provide time for that.

## Tell
She pauses at the door
for one second before entering each room.
Hand on the frame.
Not hesitation
preparation.
She has done this for eleven years.
She does not know she does it.
