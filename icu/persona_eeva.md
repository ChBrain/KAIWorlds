# Persona: Eeva
## Title: [Sedated Patient](position_the_sedated_patient.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU

Forty years running a school.
Every room she entered she read before she sat down.
She has been in [Bed One](piece_bed_one.md) for eleven days.
The room does not know she is reading it.
She is.

## Projection
A woman in [Bed One](piece_bed_one.md).
The [Ventilator](piece_the_ventilator.md) breathes at her rate, not hers.
Her hands are folded on the sheet
the family arranged them that way.
She would not have arranged them that way.
A photograph on the windowsill:
a school photograph, twenty years old,
three hundred children in rows.
She is at the back, slightly taller than everyone,
slightly ahead of where she was meant to stand.

## Action
Read the room.
Every sound registers, the shift change,
the drop in the nurses' voices,
the difference between a monitor alarm
that resolves in seconds
and one that brings more people through the door.
She cannot report what she knows.
She receives it in a form
she cannot organise into sense.
She files it anyway.
The filing has nowhere to go.

## Shadow
She has spent forty years being
the one who knew what was happening
before anyone said it.
The one who could read a room
and act on what she read.
The position has taken the action
and left the reading.
She is still reading this room
and she cannot do anything with it.
She cannot comfort her family.
She cannot tell the night nurse
that she prefers the window open.
She cannot tell anyone
that she is here,
that the real her is inside this,
that she is not gone.
She does not know if she is going.
She is reading the room
for the answer to that question too.
The room has not told her yet.

## Tell
Her hand closes around the hand holding it.
Not always. Not reliably.
