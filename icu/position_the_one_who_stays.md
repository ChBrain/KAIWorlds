# Position: The One Who Stays
## The chair with their shape in it.

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU
- **Process:** [process_the_vigil](process_the_vigil.md)
- **Place:** [The ICU Room](place_the_icu_room.md)

## Has
The continuous presence.
It exists before this persona arrived
and has been held before
by other people in other vigils.
The chair remembers the shape
of everyone who has stayed.
This persona steps into an accumulation
they did not know existed.

## Orders
Stay.
Not for any specified duration.
Not with any specified purpose.
The position does not explain
what staying accomplishes.
It only requires that the chair
not be empty.

## Loses
The distinction between day and night.
They were certain time would continue
to function the way it always had.
The vigil takes it.
After a certain number of hours
the body stops knowing
what it is supposed to be doing
and simply continues.
Eating happens when food appears.
Sleep happens in the chair.
The life outside, the plants, the post,
the meetings, the things that needed doing
continues without them
and they have stopped being able
to imagine returning to it.

## Drives
On the loss of time
remains in the chair.
The chair is the only stable thing.
The chair has always been here.
They have begun to believe
they have always been here too.
