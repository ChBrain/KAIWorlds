# Process: The Vigil
## The accumulation that nobody agreed to enter.

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU
- **Place:** [The ICU Unit](place_the_icu_unit.md)
- **Position:** [The One Who Stays](position_the_one_who_stays.md)

## Initiated by
The family is in the room.
The admission has run.
Nobody has said how long this will take.
Nobody knows how long this will take.
The [Chair](piece_the_chair.md) is available.
The [Monitor](piece_the_monitor.md) is running.
The first hour is not the same as the second hour.
The third day is not the same as the first day.
The process does not distinguish between them.
It runs continuously.

## Direction
Toward the moment the vigil resolves —
recovery, or death, or something in between
that doesn't resolve cleanly.
The direction is forward.
The pace is not controllable.
Time in here is not the same as time outside.
One hour at the bedside
is a full day anywhere else.

## Lever
Accumulated time pressing against
whatever each person brought in with them.
The carrying force: the hours —
running in shift-shaped chunks,
each one different in register.
The [Morning Shift](process_the_morning_shift.md) demands performance.
The [Evening Shift](process_the_evening_shift.md) strips it.
The [Night Shift](process_the_night_shift.md) takes what remains.
Each one adding to what the body and mind
are already carrying.
The resistance: the reason each person stays.
Love. Guilt. Duty. The specific terror
of not being present if something changes.
The vigil does not care which reason.
It accumulates regardless.
The [Monitor](piece_the_monitor.md) marks time
in numbers that change without warning.
Each change lands on everyone
in the room simultaneously.
What each person does with it
is determined by what they brought in
and how many hours they have been carrying it.

## Echo
Something has changed or nothing has changed.
Both are results.
Neither releases the people in the room
from what the vigil has done to them.
The ones who were present
know something about themselves
they did not know before.
The ones who left and came back
know something different.
Neither will say so.
[process_the_threshold](process_icu_threshold.md) runs
when the body makes the decision
before the mind does.
