# Process: The Night Shift
## The shift where what happens happens because it had to.

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU
- **Place:** [The ICU Unit](place_the_icu_unit.md)
- **Position:** [The Nurse On This Shift](position_the_nurse_on_this_shift.md)

## Initiated by
[process_the_handover](process_the_handover.md) has run.
The evening nurse has left.
The night nurse holds the unit.
The corridor is empty.
The relatives' room has one or two people in it
or none.
The unit is at its quietest.
The [Blood Draw](process_the_blood_draw.md)
will run before dawn.
The night has begun.

## Direction
Toward dawn.
That is the only direction available.
The night shift does not resolve anything.
It holds what the day produced
through the hours
when the clinical world
is at its most reduced
and the family is at its most exposed.

## Lever
The stripped hour
pressing against
whatever was not resolved by day.
The carrying force:
the quiet, the dark, the reduced staff,
the monitor sounds that are louder
because everything else has gone quiet,
the specific quality of three in the morning
that belongs to no shift
but the night shift.
The resistance:
what the people in the chairs are carrying —
the vigil accumulated over days,
the questions not asked,
the things not said at the Awakening Trial,
the conversation that needs to happen
and cannot happen in daylight
in front of other people.
The night shift is when
the goals-of-care conversation happens.
The night shift is when
the family member still in the chair
at three in the morning
asks the question
they have been not-asking for a week.
Not because the night nurse
is better at this than the day nurse.
Because the night has taken
everything else away
and left only the question.

## Echo
The [Blood Draw](process_the_blood_draw.md) has run.
The results are in the chart.
Something was said
in one of the rooms tonight
that will not be in any chart.
It is in the people who were present.
The night is almost over.
[process_the_handover](process_the_handover.md) runs at dawn.
The morning shift inherits
what the night produced —
and what the night produced
will not all transfer.
