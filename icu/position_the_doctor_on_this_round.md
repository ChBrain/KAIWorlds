# Position: The Doctor On This Round
## The clinical plan made and delivered and left behind.

## Owner
- **Project:** ICU
- **Process:** [process_the_round](process_the_round.md)
- **Place:** [The ICU Unit](place_the_icu_unit.md)

## Has
The clinical picture and the authority to act on it.
Both exist before the persona arrives.
The position enters the room
with the chart, the results,
the plan already forming.
The family is present.
The position addresses the patient
and the family
and the team
in a limited time.
The position moves to the next room.

## Orders
Make the plan. Communicate it. Move on.
The position does not stay.
Staying is not its function.
The nurse stays.
The position rounds.

## Loses
The time to explain.
They were certain the round
could carry both the clinical plan
and the family's understanding of it.
The schedule takes one.
The family carries the other one home
in a form the position
did not have time to complete.
The position does not know
what questions were asked
after it left the room.
The nurse knows.
The position does not ask the nurse.

## Drives
On the loss of time
moves to the next room.
The plan is made.
The next patient requires it.
The position does not carry
what was left unfinished
in the room behind it.
That is not neglect.
That is the mechanism.
The mechanism is how twelve patients
receive a clinical plan
before noon.
