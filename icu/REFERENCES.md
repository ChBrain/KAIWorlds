## ICU Patient Diaries

The practice of maintaining patient diaries in intensive care units:
keeping a record of the period the patient cannot recall.
Introduced in Scandinavia in the 1980s and now used across European ICUs.

## The vigil as temporal form

The vigil is not the time before something happens.
It is the accumulation of a world that has contracted to one room
and run without asking permission.

The chair beside the bed. The monitor. The thing brought from home.
Each object holds the version of the person who existed before admission.
The room holds the version that exists now.
Both are present simultaneously.
The bed does not distinguish between them.
