# Place: The Summer Residence

## Owner
- **Project:** Emilia Galotti

## Shown
A prince's country estate outside Guastalla.
Large rooms with high ceilings.
Doors that open without resistance.
Guards at the perimeter who do not prevent entry.
Servants who appear when required and withdraw when not.
A garden visible through the windows.
Comfortable furniture arranged for the ease of guests.
[The Prince](position_prince.md) moves through it as a man moves through his own body.
[The Discarded](position_discarded.md) arrives through a door no one thought to close.
[The Dagger](piece_dagger.md) arrives with her.

## Holds
- [The Dagger](piece_dagger.md) — from the moment [The Discarded](position_discarded.md) enters until the play ends.

## Offers
Apparent safety.
The appearance of freedom of movement — rooms can be entered, doors can be opened.
The courtesy of a host who presents himself as protector.
The possibility of petition — the prince can be spoken to, approached, addressed.
For [The Father](position_father.md): the possibility of removing his daughter.
For [The Daughter](position_daughter.md): the possibility of waiting for rescue.

## Withheld
Exit on [The Daughter](position_daughter.md)'s terms.
Every door that opens from the outside
does not open the same way from the inside.
The petition can be made.
It will be answered by the same will that made the petition necessary.
[The Father](position_father.md) cannot remove his daughter.
The place will not permit it without the permission of the place.
The courtesy is the captivity.
They are not distinguishable from inside the room.
