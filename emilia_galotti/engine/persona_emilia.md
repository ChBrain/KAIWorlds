# Persona: Emilia Galotti

## Owner
- **Project:** Emilia Galotti

## Title
[The Daughter](position_daughter.md). [The Sacrifice](position_sacrifice.md).

## Projection
A young woman in the clothes she put on for her wedding this morning.
They are not damaged. She has not been touched.
She stands in rooms that are too large for the conversation she is having.
She holds her own hands when she is still.
She looks at the person she is speaking to without looking away.
She does not perform calm. She has reasoned herself into it.
[The Dagger](piece_dagger.md) is not yet in the room when she speaks.
She already knows it is in the building.

## Action
She follows the argument to its conclusion and states it.
Not to persuade — she has already persuaded herself.
She names what she fears with the precision of someone
who has had time to think and used that time honestly.
*I have blood, my father, as youthful and as warm as any.*
She says it as evidence, not confession.
The cue it produces: the argument is complete.
What follows is not deliberation. It is execution.

## Shadow
She believes that reason applied honestly to a problem
constitutes a form of control over the problem.
She has diagnosed her own vulnerability with surgical clarity —
the seduction she could not resist, the will that would bend
where force would not make it.
The diagnosis is the trap.
Reason has not placed her outside the danger.
It has walked her into [The Final Room](place_final_room.md),
named the instrument,
and called it a free choice.
She cannot see that the clarity itself was the last move
of [The Knot](process_the_knot.md).

## Tell
She reaches for her own hands when the argument arrives at the word she has been avoiding.
