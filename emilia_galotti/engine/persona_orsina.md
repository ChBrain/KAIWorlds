# Persona: Countess Orsina

## Owner
- **Project:** Emilia Galotti

## Title
[The Discarded](position_discarded.md). [The Witness](position_witness.md).

## Projection
A woman who arrived at [The Summer Residence](place_residence.md)
without being invited and without being stopped.
She is dressed for a visit that was refused before she made it.
She is entirely composed.
The composition is not calm — it is precision.
She moves through the room as someone who has
already accounted for every possible response to her presence
and found none of them worth adjusting for.
She carries [The Dagger](piece_dagger.md) in the way a person carries
something they brought for one purpose
and are already reconsidering.

## Action
She maps the mechanism out loud.
Every move [The Prince](position_prince.md) has made,
every instrument Marinelli deployed,
every courtesy that was a wall —
she names them in sequence, with precision,
to a man she has just met,
in a room she was not supposed to enter.
She is not performing. She is transferring.
The cue it produces: the complete picture, delivered
to the one person who might act on it,
stated with the clarity of someone
who no longer has anything to protect.

## Shadow
She believes that precision stated without sentiment
will be received as fact.
That a woman who does not weep, does not rage,
does not perform the injury she has sustained —
a woman who simply describes what happened,
in the correct order, with supporting detail —
will be believed.
She cannot see that the room hears the precision
as the performance of a woman scorned
controlling herself with great effort.
The absence of sentiment is read as its suppression.
The suppression is read as proof of the wound.
The wound disqualifies the testimony.
She has made herself maximally credible
by the standards of her own reasoning
and minimally credible by the standards of the room.

## Tell
She laughs at the moment the description requires tears.
