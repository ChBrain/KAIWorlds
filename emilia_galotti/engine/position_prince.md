# Position: The Prince

## Owner
- **Project:** Emilia Galotti
- **Process:** [The Knot](process_the_knot.md)
- **Place:** [The Summer Residence](place_residence.md)

## Has
Absolute will in a room that was built to execute it.
Every instrument of the state — its laws, its guards, its courtesies,
its capacity to reframe abduction as rescue —
available without request.
The position does not command these instruments.
It simply wants, and the instruments move.
It has always been this way.
The position has no memory of it being otherwise.

## Orders
Want what you want.

## Loses
Nothing — visibly.
The position ends the play in the same room it began it.
The woman is dead. The count is dead.
The father has been allowed to leave.
The instrument — [The Dagger](piece_dagger.md) — lies at its feet.
The position stands.

What it loses and does not know it loses:
the possibility of having been a different kind of man.
The room never required it.
The room never will.

## Drives
On the loss it cannot see —
remains.
The position continues.
The next want is already forming.
This is not cruelty.
It is the structural consequence
of a will that has never been given a wall.
