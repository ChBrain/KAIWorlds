# Position: The Daughter

## Owner
- **Project:** Emilia Galotti
- **Process:** [The Knot](process_the_knot.md)

## Has
A fortune that belongs to her by right of birth, virtue, and the choices of others made on her behalf.
A name that has been kept clean.
A future arranged with care — a count, a marriage, a life outside the reach of the court.
The position carries all of this into the room.
It has never been tested.

## Orders
Remain who you are.

## Loses
The belief that remaining who you are is something a person can choose.
The position brought virtue as a possession — held, maintained, defended by will.
What the room reveals: virtue held by will
can be taken by a will that is stronger,
or surrendered by a will that is human.
The position cannot survive the distinction.

## Drives
On the loss —
names what is coming before it arrives.
Asks for the instrument.
Meets the end of the position
as the only act of will the position has left.
