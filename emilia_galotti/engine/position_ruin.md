# Position: The Ruin

## Owner
- **Project:** Emilia Galotti
- **Process:** [The Knot](process_the_knot.md)
- **Place:** [The Summer Residence](place_residence.md)

## Has
The intact survival of a will that has destroyed everything around it
without being destroyed itself.
The position carries this not as triumph
but as simple fact — the way a storm carries the fact of the calm it leaves.
It does not survey the damage.
It does not know there is damage to survey.
What it has is the unbroken continuity
of a self that was never required to account for itself.

## Orders
Continue.

## Loses
The recognition that continuation is the evidence of the catastrophe.
Every other position in the room has been transformed —
broken, completed, spent.
The position stands unchanged.
It was certain its survival meant strength.
What the room has demonstrated:
its survival means only that the room was built for it,
and the room is still standing,
and the room will require another occupant soon enough.

## Drives
On the loss it cannot name —
turns from the body.
The position does not linger.
There is nothing here that requires it.
The next room is already waiting.
