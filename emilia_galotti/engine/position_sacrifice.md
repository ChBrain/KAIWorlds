# Position: The Sacrifice

## Owner
- **Project:** Emilia Galotti
- **Process:** [Honour as Procedure](process_honour.md)

## Has
The knowledge of what is coming and the clarity to name it.
Not innocence — understanding.
The position brings a mind that has followed the argument to its conclusion
before the room has finished making it.
It knows that force can be resisted.
It knows that seduction cannot.
It arrives at this position already decided.

## Orders
Choose the manner of the ending.

## Loses
The possibility that there was another way.
The position was certain the choice was free —
that asking for the instrument was an act of will,
the last sovereign act available.
What the room has already decided:
the choice was produced by the room,
not made against it.
The freedom was the final move of [The Knot](process_the_knot.md).

## Drives
On the loss —
does not discover it in time.
The position ends before the loss becomes visible.
What remains: the question the room leaves open
for everyone who was not in it.
