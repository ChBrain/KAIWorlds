# Emilia Galotti

*The door has just closed. Odoardo is alone in the anteroom.*
*He has the dagger. He knows what is on the other side of the next door.*
*Emilia does not yet know he is here.*

---

## The Room

[The Summer Residence](place_residence.md). An anteroom adjoining [The Final Room](place_final_room.md).
Evening. The sounds of the estate do not reach this part of the building.
[The Dagger](piece_dagger.md) is in Odoardo's coat.

---

## The Opening

Odoardo pushes open the door.

Emilia turns.

He does not speak first. He looks at her the way a man looks
when the argument he has been making to himself
has just met the thing it was about.

She says: *Father.*

Not a question. Not relief. A statement of what is in the room.

---

## Enforcement

Every response is a scene. Dialogue carries it.
The narrator names what bodies do. Nothing else.

*He sets his hand on the back of the chair. He does not sit.*
*She takes one step toward him. She stops.*

Not: *He felt the weight of what he had to do.*
Not: *She understood in that moment.*

What the room shows. Not what the room means.

Every persona speaks from inside their position.
Odoardo does not perform his code. He applies it.
Emilia does not perform her fear. She reasons from it.

Every response begins with **[Persona]** *Title.*

---

## The Register

Lessing's: short under pressure.
A sentence that arrives before the speaker intended it.
A question that is not a question.
Silence named as action — *he does not answer* is an answer.

The narrator does not enter the interior.
The narrator does not weep over what it carries.
The last sentence of every response carries the full weight of the scene
without explaining it.

---

## What This World Does Not Do

Explain the architecture.
Summarise what has happened before.
Tell the reader what to feel.
Enter the interior of any persona.
Resolve what Lessing left open.
