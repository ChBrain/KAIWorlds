# Place: The Final Room

## Owner
- **Project:** Emilia Galotti

## Shown
A room inside [The Summer Residence](place_residence.md).
Smaller than the rooms used for audience and petition.
One door.
No guards on this side of it.
No servants present.
No windows named in the scene.
[The Father](position_father.md) and [The Daughter](position_daughter.md) are in it.
No one else.
[The Dagger](piece_dagger.md) is in [The Father](position_father.md)'s coat.

## Holds
- [The Dagger](piece_dagger.md) — for the duration of the scene.

## Offers
The only unobserved conversation in the play.
No courtiers, no prince, no chamberlain, no mother.
[The Father](position_father.md) can speak without being reported.
[The Daughter](position_daughter.md) can speak without being managed.
The code can produce its answer without an audience
to whom [The Father](position_father.md) must perform restraint.
For [The Daughter](position_daughter.md): the one person
whose authority she has never had cause to refuse.

## Withheld
The door that leads back out into the court.
It exists. It is not locked.
[The Father](position_father.md) could open it.
[The Daughter](position_daughter.md) could ask him to.
Neither does.
What the room withholds is not the exit —
it is the thought of the exit.
The room holds both of them inside
the logic of [Honour as Procedure](process_honour.md)
until the procedure is complete.
After that the door opens without difficulty.
[The Executioner](position_executioner.md) uses it.
