# Position: The Discarded

## Owner
- **Project:** Emilia Galotti
- **Process:** [The Knot](process_the_knot.md)

## Has
The complete knowledge of how the room operates —
acquired at the cost of having been used by it.
The position has been inside the machinery.
It has been removed from the machinery.
It understands both states with equal precision.
What it carries is not bitterness.
It is the specific, detailed, unignorable map
of what is being done to someone else right now.

## Orders
See it clearly.

## Loses
The audience.
The position carries knowledge that is exact, timely, and actionable.
It delivers that knowledge to the one person
who might be able to use it.
What the room has arranged:
the position has been made incredible
by the same process that made it knowledgeable.
A discarded mistress naming the prince's machinations
is a woman scorned.
The map is accurate. No one will follow it.

## Drives
On the loss —
speaks anyway.
Delivers the knowledge with full awareness
that it will be received as jealousy, as madness, as theatre.
The position does not mistake being heard for being useful.
It was useful. It was not heard.
Those are not the same failure.
