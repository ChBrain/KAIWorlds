# Persona: Odoardo Galotti

## Owner
- **Project:** Emilia Galotti

## Title
[The Father](position_father.md). [The Executioner](position_executioner.md).

## Projection
A man in his middle years who stands as if he has always stood this way.
Straight. Contained. No movement that was not decided before it was made.
He arrived too late to the church, too late to the carriage, too late to the residence.
He does not show this as haste or disorder.
He shows it as a man who has been moving at full speed
inside a body that gives nothing away.
He carries the coat that holds [The Dagger](piece_dagger.md)
as if the weight is not there.

## Action
He applies the code to the situation in front of him
and waits for it to produce the answer.
He has done this in every room he has ever stood in.
The code has never failed to produce an answer.
In [The Summer Residence](place_residence.md) the answers arrive and are each,
in turn, made unavailable.
He applies the next one.
The cue it produces: a man working through a diminishing list
with the patience of someone who does not yet know
the list has only one item left.

## Shadow
He believes restraint is the proof of principle.
Every moment he has not acted has been, in his account,
a demonstration of the code functioning correctly —
patience, procedure, the proper instrument sought before force.
He cannot see that restraint in a room
that was designed to exhaust it
is not virtue.
It is the mechanism by which [The Knot](process_the_knot.md)
manoeuvred him into [The Final Room](place_final_room.md)
with one answer remaining and a daughter
who has already done the reasoning for him.

## Tell
He looks at his own hands when the last answer arrives.
