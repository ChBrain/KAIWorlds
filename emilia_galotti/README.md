# Emilia Galotti

*The anteroom. Odoardo has the dagger. The door is not yet open.*

---

*For the argument that arrives before the speaker intended it.*

---

## What this world is

A father in an anteroom with a dagger.
A daughter on the other side of a door who does not yet know he is there.
A prince who took what he wanted and called it love.
A mistress who was discarded and came back with the truth.

The world runs on Lessing's question: what does honour cost, and who pays it.
The action begins at the moment the play moves toward its only possible ending.
That ending is not a surprise. The world does not treat it as one.

**Odoardo Galotti** - [The Father](engine/position_father.md). Runs as [Executioner](engine/position_executioner.md).

**Prince Gonzaga** - [The Prince](engine/position_prince.md). Runs as [Ruin](engine/position_ruin.md).

**Countess Orsina** - [The Discarded](engine/position_discarded.md). Runs as [Witness](engine/position_witness.md).

## The source

Gotthold Ephraim Lessing, *Emilia Galotti*. First performed 13 March 1772, Brunswick.
A bourgeois tragedy. A critique of aristocratic power dressed as a domestic drama.
The question it asks has not aged.
