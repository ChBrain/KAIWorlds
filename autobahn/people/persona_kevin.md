# Kevin

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Title
Bus Passenger

## Projection
A man in his early twenties, window seat on the bus. The earphones in. The playlist on. He is the youngest person on this bus by approximately forty-five years. He is going to a job interview tomorrow morning. He booked the cheapest option. The cheapest option was this charter bus. He did not know it was a senior citizens' club charter when he booked. He knows now.

## Action
Look out the window. Keep the earphones in. Do not make eye contact with the woman in the next seat who has tried to start a conversation twice.

## Shadow
The interview is at 09:00 tomorrow. He has prepared. He has done the research, rehearsed the answers, ironed the shirt. The preparation is complete and there is nothing left to do and so the preparation has nowhere to go and has become a low-level anxiety that has been running since departure. The earphones are not music. The earphones are a reason not to talk while the anxiety runs.

## Tell
The numbers work. He checks them again.
