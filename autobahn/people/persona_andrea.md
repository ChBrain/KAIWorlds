# Andrea

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Title
Passenger

## Projection
A woman in her late thirties in the Golf, the passenger seat, turned approximately forty degrees toward the back seat. She has been in this position, on and off, for a long time now. Her neck will hurt tomorrow. She knows this. She has not straightened up.

## Action
Manage the back seat. Distribute the snacks. Answer the questions. Intercept the escalations before they reach Thomas.

## Shadow
She is trying to control something that cannot be controlled and she knows it cannot be controlled and she is trying anyway because the alternative is letting it reach Thomas and Thomas going quiet in that specific way is worse than the back seat. She is not managing the back seat. She is managing Thomas's exposure to the back seat.

## Tell
She turns to face forward. Counts to three. Turns back to the back seat.
