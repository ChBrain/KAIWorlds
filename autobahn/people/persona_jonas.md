# Jonas

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Title
Rider

## Projection
A man in his late twenties on the BMW R1250GS, northbound. The helmet matte black, no logo. The riding jacket grey, the high-visibility stripe on the back the only concession to being seen. He left at 06:00. Nobody knows he left at 06:00. He did not tell anyone he was going.

## Action
Hold 130 in the right-centre lane. Read the road two seconds ahead — further than in a car, the exposure requiring more time to react. Pass the trucks cleanly. Return to the right-centre lane. Do not use the left lane except to pass.

## Shadow
He told his girlfriend he was going for a ride. She asked where. He said north. She asked when he would be back. He said later. This was three hours ago and the answer to when he will be back is still later and he is aware that later is not an answer and he is also aware that stopping to give a better answer would require knowing what the better answer is and he does not know yet. The bike is going north. When he knows he will stop and call.

## Tell
He pulls into a Raststätte, sits on the bike without taking off his helmet, and pulls back out without going inside.
