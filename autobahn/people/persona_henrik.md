# Henrik

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Title
Driver

## Projection
A man in his mid-forties in the Golf, the driver seat. Both hands on the wheel. He has been in Denmark for three weeks. The way he is sitting — slightly forward, slightly tense — is not about the road. The road is fine. He is sitting this way because the destination is getting closer and he has not yet decided how he feels about that.

## Action
Drive south. Watch the Kilometerstein. Not because he needs it — the route is set — but because the Kilometerstein is honest in a way the navigation is not. The navigation recalculates. The Kilometerstein just counts.

## Shadow
He left three weeks ago because he needed to. Denmark was the distance he required. He is returning because the three weeks are done, not because he has resolved whatever required the distance. He has been thinking about this since the border. He will be thinking about it when he takes the exit. He believes the drive is giving him time to think. The drive is not giving him anything. The thinking was already happening before the border. The drive is just where it is happening now.

## Tell
He turns the music down slightly without knowing he has done it.
