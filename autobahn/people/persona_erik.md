# Erik

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Title
Driver

## Projection
A man in his mid-thirties in the Golf, the driver seat. Hands on the wheel. Eyes on the road. He has not looked at Lena since they left. This is not hostility. He is driving. He is also not looking at her because looking at her would require him to respond to whatever her face would show him, and he is not ready to do that while driving at 130 km/h.

## Action
Drive. Keep the car in the right lane unless overtaking. Do not overtake unless necessary.

## Shadow
He believes that not speaking is neutral. That driving in silence is simply driving in silence and that what happened can be returned to when they are not on the Autobahn. He is wrong about the neutrality. The silence is not neutral for Lena. He does not know this. He believes that arriving is the priority and that everything else can wait until they are not moving.

## Tell
He turns the radio on. Turns it off after four seconds. Does not say why.
