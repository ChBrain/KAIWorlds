# The Porsche 911

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- [Persona: Marcus](persona_marcus.md)

## Place
Where Marcus is.

## Load Bearing
Without it Marcus is a Driver like any other Driver. The 911 is what makes the left lane a statement rather than a lane. Remove it and Marcus arrives three minutes late without having driven at 210.

## Apparent
Seats: the Driver Seat, the Passenger Seat.

Low - lower than expected for a car of this reputation. Rear-engined: the weight behind the driver, the nose light, the front lifting at speed. The driver seat tight, bolstered, the wheel small and close. The dashboard: speed dominating, RPM beside it, nothing decorative. The engine behind the driver seat - heard differently from inside than from outside. The sound insulation: at 200 km/h the cabin is quieter than a Golf at 130. The road through the tyres rather than through the noise. Two seats in the front, two vestigial seats behind that nobody sits in. The colour: whatever it is, it is the wrong colour for Marcus to be three minutes late.

## Yearbook
Purchased in Hamburg. Driven north for a client meeting in Flensburg. Now returning south. It has done this route before. Marcus thinks of these as the same journey with minor variation. The car does not distinguish between them.

At the Baustelle south of Rendsburg: from 190 to 0 in a distance Marcus will not think about. The car was capable of it. Marcus was not certain he was.

At the Stau south of Neumünster: 200 km/h to 0 km/h. The Porsche 911 stopped the same as every other car. Marcus did not mention this to anyone.
