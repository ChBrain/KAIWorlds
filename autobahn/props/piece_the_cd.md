# The CD

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Place
In the slot. Or in the case in the door pocket. Or in a wallet of sleeves on the sun visor.

## Load Bearing
Without it the head unit has no physical source. The CD is what the Driver chose before the journey — at home, the night before, or years ago when the case was assembled. Remove it and the Driver has UKW or silence. The CD is load-bearing because the choice was made in advance, away from the road, with time to decide.

## Apparent
A disc. 74 or 80 minutes. An album, a mixtape burned from a computer, a greatest hits. Loaded into the slot before or after the Auffahrt. The display showing track number or title depending on whether the disc has text data. Playing from track one unless the Driver pressed a button. When it ends: silence, or repeat, or the Driver reaching for the case.

## Yearbook
The same album since before the last stop. The Driver stopped hearing it before the halfway point. The music is playing. The Driver is not listening. This is not a failure of the CD. This is what the CD is for — to fill the cab with something known, so the Driver does not have to decide again.

A mixtape made in 2003. Still in the case. Played on long journeys only. The Driver knows every transition between tracks. The transitions are part of the journey now.
