# UKW

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Place
In whatever head unit or infotainment system is receiving it.

## Load Bearing
Without it the Driver has no external signal. UKW is the only source that arrives without being chosen — the Driver selects a frequency, not a programme. What plays on that frequency is decided elsewhere. Remove it and the cab is sealed from broadcast. The traffic report does not arrive. The news does not arrive unless the Driver brought it.

## Apparent
A frequency. 88 to 108 MHz. A station name on the display. Music that gives way to news without asking permission. The traffic report at the half hour — or not at the half hour, depending on the station. The signal strong near transmitters, weakening between them, recovering. Static in tunnels. Four minutes of no signal, then the station again.

## Yearbook
Selected once and left. The Driver does not think of it as a choice after the first minute — it becomes the background against which the journey runs. The station plays what it plays. The news arrives when it arrives. The Driver registers it or does not. Nobody curated this. That is both its limitation and its character.
