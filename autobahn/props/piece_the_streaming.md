# Streaming

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Place
In the infotainment system. Via the phone. Via the car's own connection.

## Load Bearing
Without it the Driver with no physical media and no UKW preference has silence. Streaming is the source that requires no preparation — no disc loaded, no frequency selected, no choice made before the journey. Remove it and the Driver must have decided in advance what to play. Streaming is load-bearing because it removed that requirement and the Driver has not noticed the removal.

## Apparent
A playlist. An algorithm. A song chosen by the Driver or chosen for them based on what they played last time. The queue visible on the screen or not visible. The next track arriving without announcement. A song the Driver did not choose appearing and being left to play, or skipped, or left to play after being almost skipped. Offline downloads for the tunnel. The connection dropping between transmitters and recovering — or not recovering, and the silence that follows.

## Yearbook
Running in cars where the Driver connected their phone before the Auffahrt. Or did not connect and the car's own account is playing something the Driver set up months ago and has not changed. The algorithm knows the Driver's patterns. The Driver does not know what the algorithm knows. This is the arrangement.

A playlist called something generic — Driving, Road Trip, Focus. Made for a different journey. Still the playlist for this one because the Driver did not change it and the infotainment remembered.
