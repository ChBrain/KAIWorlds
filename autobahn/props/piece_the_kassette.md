# The Kassette

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Place
In the slot. Or in a box under the seat.

## Load Bearing
Without it the head unit that has only a cassette slot has nothing to play. The Kassette is the oldest physical source in this world — older than the CD, older than digital. Remove it and a specific kind of Driver has silence or UKW. No other option. The Kassette is load-bearing in the vehicles where nothing else fits.

## Apparent
A plastic housing. Two reels. 45 or 60 minutes per side. Side A ends and the Driver reaches forward to flip it or the autoreverse engages. The hiss beneath the music — always present, ignored after the first seconds. Recorded at home from vinyl or another cassette or the radio. Or bought. The label handwritten or printed on a sticker. The tape inside: fragile, degrading with each play, the sound slightly different from the last time.

## Yearbook
In a vehicle old enough to still have the slot. Played on journeys where the Driver does not want to choose — where having already chosen, years ago, is enough. The tape has been in the box under the seat since before the last service. It still plays. The Driver did not expect it to still play. They are glad it does.
