# the tank

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Place
In every vehicle. Always.

## Load Bearing
Without it no process runs past the point where the tank empties. The tank is load-bearing precisely because it is always declining and nobody thinks about it until they have to.

## Apparent
A number on the dashboard. Or a gauge. Or the orange warning light that has been on for forty kilometres.

## Yearbook
Full at the start of every journey. Never full at the end. Dieter calculates it before he starts. Marcus does not calculate it - the Porsche has range and he has time. Henrik filled it in Flensburg and has been watching the number decline since the Auffahrt. Sabine did not check it before leaving. The orange light came on south of Neumünster. She is now watching for her exit and watching the gauge simultaneously.
