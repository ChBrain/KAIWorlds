# The Raststätte Stop

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Initiated by
[the tank](piece_the_tank.md) is low, or [the coffee](piece_the_coffee.md) is empty, or the Driver has been driving long enough that the body requires a stop. The Raststätte sign is visible.

## Direction
Toward the parking bay, the building, the return to the car.

## Lever
Carrying force: the need - [the tank](piece_the_tank.md) low, the body requiring a stop, [the coffee](piece_the_coffee.md) empty.
Resistance: the time - the destination not getting closer while the car is still.

## Echo
[the tank](piece_the_tank.md) [is fuller. A new Coffee](piece_the_coffee.md) [is in the cupholder or the hand. The Driver is back in the car. Auffahrt](process_the_auffahrt.md) runs again from the Raststätte slip road.
