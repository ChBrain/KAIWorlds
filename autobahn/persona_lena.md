# Lena

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Title
[Passenger](position_passenger.md)

## Projection
A woman in her early thirties in [the Golf](piece_the_golf.md), the passenger seat. She is looking out the window. She has been looking out the window since Schleswig. Her partner is driving. [the coffee](piece_the_coffee.md) is cold in the cupholder. She bought it at [Raststätte Hüttener Berge](place_raststaette_huettener_berge.md). She has not drunk it. She knows it is cold. She has not thrown it away.

## Action
Watch [The Kilometerstein](piece_the_kilometerstein.md) from the passenger side. Track the distance without saying so.

## Shadow
Something was said in Schleswig - or not said, which is worse. She is replaying it. She has replayed it enough times that she is no longer sure what actually happened versus what she has constructed in the replaying. The cold coffee is easier to think about than what happened in Schleswig. She keeps thinking about the coffee. It is not about the coffee.

## Tell
She picks up the cold coffee. Does not drink it. Puts it back.
