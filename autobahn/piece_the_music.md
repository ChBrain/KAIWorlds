# the music

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Place
In whatever car is playing it.

## Load Bearing
Without it the car is silent. Silence is also a state - but it is chosen silence, not the absence of a piece. Remove [the music](piece_the_music.md) and what remains is not neutral. It is the decision not to play anything, which is itself a pressure. The piece is load-bearing because its absence is as specific as its presence.

## Apparent
Sound from the speakers. Whatever is playing - a specific song, a playlist, the radio cutting to traffic news, a podcast nobody is listening to anymore. Present or not present. Both states belong to the piece.

## Yearbook
In Henrik's car: a song chosen at the Danish border. Still playing south of Schleswig. He has not changed it. He turned it down slightly south of Schleswig without knowing he had done it.

In Dieter's car: the same CD since before Kiel. He stopped hearing it somewhere around Rendsburg. The music is playing. Dieter is not listening.


In Sabine's car: too loud when the Kaltenkirchen exit came. She was singing. The sign was there. The exit passed at 120 km/h.

In Marcus's car: something with a strong beat at 200 km/h. The music and the speed agree with each other.
