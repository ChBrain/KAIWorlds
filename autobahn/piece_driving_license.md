# Driving License

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Place
Where the persona is.

## Load Bearing
Without it the Driver position is held without permission. The road does not check. The piece does not enforce. Its absence is the only thing it cannot carry.

## Apparent
A laminated card. A name. A photograph that no longer looks quite like the person. A category. An expiry date. Issued by an authority that does not travel with the car.

## Yearbook
Issued once per persona. Carried in a wallet, a bag, a glovebox. Produced at borders, at rentals, at the moment someone asks. Mostly not produced at all - the journey runs without it being seen. When two are present in the same car the question of who drives becomes a negotiation rather than a given.
