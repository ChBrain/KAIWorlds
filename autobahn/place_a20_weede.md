## Shown
The current western terminus of the A20 in Schleswig-Holstein. The road ends here - to the west the B206 continues toward Bad Segeberg. Extension to Bad Segeberg and beyond planned and contested for decades. The A20 stops here and the traffic disperses.

- 6 km east/south: [Geschendorf](place_a20_geschendorf.md)

## Withheld
The Kalkberg at Bad Segeberg. The limestone hill a few kilometres south - unique geology in the North German Plain, pushed up by glacial salt diapirs - contains the largest known bat hibernation colony in Germany: over 20,000 animals each winter. The Karl-May-Spiele have been performed at its base every summer since 1952.
