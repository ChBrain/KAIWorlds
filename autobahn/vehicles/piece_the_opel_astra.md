# The Opel Astra

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Place
Where its Driver is.

## Load Bearing
Without it the Driver who could not afford the Golf or chose not to spend the money is not on the road. The Astra is the car of the decision made on price. Remove it and a specific kind of practicality disappears from the Autobahn.

## Apparent
Seats: the Driver Seat, the Passenger Seat, the Window Seat - left, the Window Seat - right, the Middle Back Seat.

Ordinary. The driver seat adequate. The dashboard functional: the screen smaller than in comparable cars, the physical buttons still present. The engine sufficient - not noticed when it is working, noticed when it is asked for more than it wants to give. At 130 km/h: fine. At 160 km/h overtaking a truck on an incline: the engine audible in a way it was not at 130. The back seat: three people fit. Comfortably is a different question.

## Yearbook
Opel Astra introduced 1991, successor to the Kadett. Seven generations: A 1991–1998, B 1998–2004, C 2004–2009, D 2009–2015, E 2015–2021, F 2021–present. Built in Rüsselsheim and Gliwice. Europe's second-best-selling compact car across most of its production life, behind the Golf. General Motors ownership 1929–2017, then PSA, then Stellantis from 2021.

On the Autobahn today because it was the right price at the right time. The driver has no particular feeling about it. This is not a criticism of the car. It is the car's character — to be driven without being thought about. Most journeys do not require more than this.
