# The Audi A6

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Place
Where its Driver is.

## Load Bearing
Without it the Driver arrives at the same destination by different means. The A6 is the car that does not call attention to itself. Remove it and nobody notices it is gone - including, eventually, the person who drove it.

## Apparent
Seats: the Driver Seat, the Passenger Seat, the Window Seat - left, the Window Seat - right, the Middle Back Seat.

Understated. The lines clean, the proportions resolved. The quattro badge on the back: all-wheel drive, the road held in all conditions. The interior: the virtual cockpit displays speed in the instrument cluster, the centre screen for navigation. The driver seat: supportive without announcing it. The lane assist active - the wheel resisting gently when the car drifts. At 180 km/h: stable, silent, the Autobahn processed rather than experienced.

## Yearbook
Successor to the Audi 100, renamed A6 in 1994 with the C4 generation. Built in Neckarsulm. Five generations: C4 1994–1997, C5 1997–2004 (first turbo), C6 2004–2011 (MMI infotainment, single-frame grille), C7 2011–2018 (Virtual Cockpit, quattro throughout, drag coefficient 0.25), C8 2018–present (48V mild-hybrid, digital instrument cluster). A car built to be unremarkable to its owner and unmistakable to everyone else.

The car that sales directors drive. That consultants lease. Arrives at the airport car park next to three other A6s. Different colours. The drivers do not acknowledge each other.
