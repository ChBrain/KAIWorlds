## Shown
Exit 9. The sign: Itzehoe-Mitte. The road here crossing the Störbrücke section - the 1,160-metre bridge over the Stör river. Two lanes in each direction. The road is motorway standard here despite what the gap in the original numbering implied. Itzehoe visible to the east.

- 3 km north: [Itzehoe-Nord](place_a23_08_itzehoe_nord.md)

## Withheld
The Stör. The river running under the bridge connects the Holstein interior to the Elbe and ultimately the North Sea. It passes through Itzehoe and reaches the tidal estuary near Glückstadt. The bridge crosses it in seconds. The river takes hours to reach the sea.
