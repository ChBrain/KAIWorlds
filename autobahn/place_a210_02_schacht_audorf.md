## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Shown
The western terminus of the A210. The sign: Schacht-Audorf. The road beginning here - west of this point no motorway. The Nord-Ostsee-Kanal a few kilometres north: the Rader Hochbrücke on the A7 carries the road over it.

- 2 km east/south: [Kreuz Rendsburg](place_a210_03_kreuz_rendsburg.md)

## Withheld
The Nord-Ostsee-Kanal proximity. The canal runs a few kilometres north - the world's busiest artificial waterway, connecting the North Sea to the Baltic. Here at Schacht-Audorf the canal bends south toward Rendsburg before straightening east. The canal lock at Kiel-Holtenau is 98 kilometres east.
