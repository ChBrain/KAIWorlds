## Withheld
The Kieler Förde ice age origin. The fjord is not a true fjord but a glacially carved channel - the ice sheet covering this area 15,000 years ago cut the Förde into the bedrock, then retreated, and the Baltic filled it. The landscape here - the rolling hills, the lakes - is the same glacial deposit.
