# the coffee

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Place
In the cupholder. In every vehicle that has stopped at a Raststätte.

## Load Bearing
Without it the Raststätte stop has no object. The stop happens for the coffee - or the coffee is the form the stop takes when the real reason is harder to name.

## Apparent
Paper cup with a plastic lid. Or a thermos from home. Hot when bought. Temperature declining. In the cupholder, or in the Driver's hand at 130 km/h, or forgotten on the roof of the car at the Raststätte and remembered too late.

## Yearbook
Bought at a Raststätte stop. Drunk between exits without tasting it. The cup still in the cupholder. Not thrown away until the destination.

A passenger bought one at the same stop. Her companion declined when she offered. It has been in the cupholder since the last stop.

One driver brought a thermos from home. He refills it each morning. He does not think of it as a piece of the journey. It is part of the cab.
