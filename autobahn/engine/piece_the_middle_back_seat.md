# The Middle Back Seat

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Place
The centre of the rear row. Between the two window seats.

## Load Bearing
Without it the rear row cannot carry three. It exists to make the car hold one more person than it would otherwise hold.

## Apparent
A seat, narrower than the others. No window. No door. A seatbelt that rises from the floor or the ceiling. The seatbelt buckles on both sides belong to the window seats. A raised floor section in many vehicles - the transmission tunnel below.

## Yearbook
Assigned. Rarely chosen. Occupied by whoever was youngest, smallest, last in, least able to refuse. Statistically the safest seat in the vehicle. This is not why anyone sits here.
