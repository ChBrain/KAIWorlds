# The Driver Seat

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Place
The seat behind the wheel.

## Load Bearing
Without it the vehicle does not move. Every other seat depends on this one being occupied.

## Apparent
A seat. Adjusted to the body that last occupied it. The wheel within reach. The pedals below. The mirrors set to someone's eyes. The seatbelt. The headrest.

## Yearbook
Occupied by whoever held the driving license and the decision. Adjusted and readjusted. When two licenses are in the car, this seat is negotiated before the Auffahrt. When one license is in the car, it is not.
