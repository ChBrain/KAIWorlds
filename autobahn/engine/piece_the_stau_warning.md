# the Stau warning

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Place
In the radio. On the dashboard. In the brake lights of the car ahead.

## Load Bearing
Without it the Stau arrives without announcement. Remove it and every Stau is brake lights at 130 km/h with no prior information.

## Apparent
A triangle on the dashboard. Or the radio cutting from music to traffic news. Or the sudden red of brake lights two hundred metres ahead that is not normal braking. Sometimes all three at once. Sometimes none of them and then the Stau anyway.

## Yearbook
Warned a driver of a Stau ahead. He was at 200. He slowed to 0 in a distance that left marks on the road.

Did not warn a driver of the construction zone ahead. She was watching it. She was also watching the exit sign. She looked at the wrong one at the wrong moment.

One driver does not need it. He knew the Stau was there. He reduced speed early. The cars behind him did not understand why. Then they understood.
