# Construction Worker

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- Process: [the Baustelle](process_the_baustelle.md)

## Has
The cones. The road surface under repair. The section of Autobahn that belongs to the work rather than to the traffic.

## Orders
Hold the zone. The position does not stop for the cars. The cars stop for the position.

## Loses
The road. The finished section is immediately absorbed by the traffic. The position moves to the next section. The road does not remember the work.

## Drives
On the loss of the road - places the next cone.
