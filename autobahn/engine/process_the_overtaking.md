# Overtaking

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- Position: Driver

## Initiated by
A slower vehicle is ahead in the right lane. The left lane is clear or clearable. The Driver makes the decision.

## Direction
Toward the left lane, past the slower vehicle, back to the right lane.

## Lever
Carrying force: the Driver's speed.
Resistance: the left lane - what is in it, what is approaching in it, what the mirror shows and what it doesn't.

## Echo
The slower vehicle is behind. The right lane is resumed. [Cruising](process_the_cruising.md) continues or the next vehicle ahead initiates [Overtaking](process_the_overtaking.md) again.
