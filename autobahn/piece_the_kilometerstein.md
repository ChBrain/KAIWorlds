# the Kilometerstein

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- Place: [the A7](place_the_a7.md)

## Place
On the right verge of the road. Every hundred metres.

## Load Bearing
Without it the journey has no fixed external measure. The Navi gives a number but the number is calculated, predicted, subject to recalculation. The Kilometerstein does not recalculate. Remove it and the only measures of progress are internal to the vehicle.

## Apparent
White post. Blue number. On the right verge. Appearing every hundred metres. The number: not the distance to the destination but the road's own measure of itself.

## Yearbook
Read by Henrik between Schleswig and Rendsburg when he realised he had been driving without thinking for twenty minutes. The Kilometerstein told him where he was on the road.

Read by Lena every few minutes since Schleswig. She is watching the number change. It is the only thing in the car she can measure that is not inside the car.

Not read by Dieter. He knows where he is.
Not read by Marcus. He knows how fast he is getting there.
