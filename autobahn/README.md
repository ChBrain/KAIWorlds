*A7, A23, A1, A20, A21, A210, A215, A24, A25 - Schleswig-Holstein*

---

## What this world is

A world built from the particular of a motorway journey.

The Kilometerstein counts every hundred metres. It does not recalculate. The road does not know which journey it is on. It is always on one.

The world holds the journeys that happen in parallel on the same road without knowing about each other. Henrik returning from three weeks away, nothing resolved. Lena and her partner in a silence that started before the last stop. Dieter on his 381st run. Marcus making up the eleven minutes. Ruth managing the margin she has not mentioned to her passengers. Jonas, who said "north" and "later" and is not back yet.

---

## How to set it up

1. Create a new AI project - [Claude](https://claude.ai), Grok, Copilot, and Gemini all work
2. Upload folders to your AI project based on what you need:
   - **Always**: `/engine`
   - **For a specific road**: `/roads/a7`, `/roads/a1`, etc.
   - **For people**: `/people`
   - **For vehicles**: `/vehicles`
   - **For props**: `/props`
3. Start a conversation - bring what you have

Direction is in the Driver, not the place. The road goes both ways. The world picks the persona and place with the strongest pull on what you bring.

## The Personas

**Henrik** - returning from three weeks away. He is returning because the three weeks are done, not because anything is resolved.

**Lena** - passenger in the Golf. The coffee is cold in the cupholder. It is not about the coffee.

**Dieter** - MAN truck driver, 381 runs. His wife called. He let it go to voicemail.

**Marcus** - Porsche 911, meeting at 14:00. He made up eleven minutes. He will be three minutes late. He believes arriving is the same as arriving on time.

**Ruth** - bus driver, 41 passengers, 40 minutes late. She recalculated the schedule and has not mentioned it.

**Jonas** - BMW R1250GS, left at 06:00 without telling anyone where he was going. Said "north." Has not gone inside at the Raststätte.

**Sabine** - missed her exit because the music was too loud and she was singing. Has already constructed the plausible version she will tell.

**Thomas** - three children, Andrea managing the back seat. Has answered *bald* eleven times.

**Markus** - construction worker at the Baustelle. Daughter born six weeks ago. Wife said fine. He is aware that "fine" is not the same as "fine."

And others: Andrea, Elif, Erik, Kevin, Lena's partner.

## The Roads

**A7** - Flensburg to Hamburg, 31 exits plus junctions and Raststätten. The Elbtunnel between exits 27 and 28.

**A23** - Heide to Dreieck Hamburg-Nordwest, 22 exits. Westküstenautobahn.

**A1** - Vogelfluglinie, Heiligenhafen to Lübeck, Scandinavian ferry connection.

**A21** - Nettelsee to Kreuz Bargteheide, 52 km, incomplete both ends.

**A210, A215, A20, A24, A25** - connecting roads.

## What to bring

A journey. Something that started before the Auffahrt. Something that will not be resolved before the Abfahrt.

The world holds it. It does not resolve.
