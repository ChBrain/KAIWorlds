# The Window Seat

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Place
A seat beside a window.

## Load Bearing
Without it the back seat has no window. The rear of the car carries passengers with no view out.

## Apparent
A seat. Less adjustable than the front. A window beside it. A seatbelt. The back of the front seat ahead of it. A door handle within reach.

## Yearbook
Claimed before the others could. Called from the driveway, settled by unspoken agreement, defended across many journeys. The view it offers has been pressed against by many faces. The exits it has shown have not been taken.
