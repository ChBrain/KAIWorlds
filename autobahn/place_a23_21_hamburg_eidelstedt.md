# Hamburg-Eidelstedt

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- Place: [the A23](place_the_a23.md)

## Shown
Hamburg. The first Hamburg exit on the A23. The sign: Hamburg-Eidelstedt. This exit serves the missing relation at the Dreieck - drivers from the A7 northbound who cannot take the direct A23 ramp come here instead.

- 2 km north: [Halstenbek-Krupunder](place_a23_20_halstenbek_krupunder.md)
- 2 km south: [Dreieck Hamburg-Nordwest](place_a23_22_dreieck_hamburg_nordwest.md)

## Holds

## Offers

## Withheld
The Eidelstedt Moor. Before Hamburg expanded westward, Eidelstedt was surrounded by heath and bog. The peat was cut for fuel. The names of surrounding streets still reference the old landscape. Marginal land a hundred years ago.
