# the Navi

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Place
In every vehicle. Mounted or running on a phone in the cupholder.

## Load Bearing
Without it the Driver knows or does not know the way. The Navi replaces that uncertainty with a different one - the certainty of the route and the silence about the reason for it. Remove it and the Driver must hold the route in their head alongside everything else the head is holding.

## Apparent
A screen. The road from above as a line. The current position moving along it. The voice that speaks slightly before the action is required - or slightly after. The ETA in the corner. The ETA. It changes when conditions change.

## Yearbook
Guided every Driver in this world toward their destination. Never asked why they were going. Recalculated for Sabine when she missed Kaltenkirchen without comment. Recalculated for Henrik when he slowed below 100 south of Schleswig without comment. It does not remember the previous recalculations.
