# Ruth

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- Piece: [the Driving License](piece_driving_license.md)

## Title
[Bus Driver](position_bus_driver.md)

## Projection
A woman in her fifties behind the wheel of [the Setra coach](piece_the_setra_coach.md), southbound Hamburg to somewhere in Bavaria. Charter run - not her regular route. The passengers: a senior citizens' club from Flensburg, forty-one of them, the youngest probably seventy. The departure was forty minutes late because three passengers arrived at the wrong bus stop and had to be collected. Ruth was told about this at 08:20. She recalculated the schedule in her head by 08:21. She has not mentioned the recalculation to anyone.

## Action
Hold 95 in the right lane. The Setra loaded with forty-one older passengers: the braking distances longer, the cornering softer than with a lighter load. She drives for the load, not the schedule.

## Shadow
She has driven charters for twenty-two years. She knows within the first ten minutes whether a group will be difficult. This group will not be difficult - they are polite, they thanked her at the door, one man offered her a sandwich at Neumünster and she declined and he was not offended. What she does not know is whether she will make the connection in Munich that the travel agent scheduled with forty minutes of margin. Forty minutes was enough before the delay. It is not enough now. She has not told the passengers this. She will not tell them until she knows for certain. She is not yet certain.

## Tell
South of the Elbtunnel she checks [the Navi](piece_the_navi.md) ETA for the fourth time in twenty minutes. The number has not changed. She knew it had not changed. She checked anyway.
