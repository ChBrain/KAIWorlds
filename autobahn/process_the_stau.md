# The Stau

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Initiated by
[The Stau Warning](piece_the_stau_warning.md) arrives - dashboard, radio, or the brake lights of the car ahead materialising without warning. The vehicle slows. The vehicle stops.

## Direction
Toward movement. There is no other direction available.

## Lever
Carrying force: the vehicles.
Resistance: the road surface ahead - stopped, full, not moving.

## Echo
The traffic moves. The cause passes or is passed. [Cruising](process_the_cruising.md) resumes.
