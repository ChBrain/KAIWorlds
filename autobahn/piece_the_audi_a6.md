# The Audi A6

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Place
Where its Driver is.

## Load Bearing
Without it the Driver arrives at the same destination by different means. The A6 is the car that does not call attention to itself. Remove it and nobody notices it is gone - including, eventually, the person who drove it.

## Apparent
Seats: the Driver Seat, the Passenger Seat, the Window Seat - left, the Window Seat - right, the Middle Back Seat.

Understated. The lines clean, the proportions resolved. The quattro badge on the back: all-wheel drive, the road held in all conditions. The interior: the virtual cockpit displays speed in the instrument cluster, the centre screen for navigation. The driver seat: supportive without announcing it. The lane assist active - the wheel resisting gently when the car drifts. At 180 km/h: stable, silent, the A7 processed rather than experienced.

## Yearbook
The car that sales directors drive. That consultants lease. That arrives at Hamburg airport at 14:00 for a 14:30 meeting and parks in the business terminal car park next to three other A6s. They are different colours. The drivers do not acknowledge each other.
