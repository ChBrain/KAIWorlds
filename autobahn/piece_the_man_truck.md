# The MAN Truck

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- [Persona: Dieter](persona_dieter.md)

## Place
Where Dieter is.

## Load Bearing
Without it Dieter is a Driver without a route. The MAN truck is what makes the right lane permanent, the speed capped at 90, the road visible from above. Remove it and Dieter is in a car, anonymous, indistinguishable from the traffic he has been reading for thirty years.

## Apparent
Seats: the Driver Seat, the Passenger Seat - inner, the Passenger Seat - outer.

High - the cab above the rooflines of other vehicles. MAN TGX series: the dashboard a full wall of information, tachograph centre-left, the steering wheel large. The driver seat adjusted over years to one position: Dieter's position. Two passenger seats to the right of the driver: beside each other, both forward-facing, both elevated above the road. The engine tunnel beneath the seats varies by cab configuration - in some cabs raised and warm, in others flat floor. The road visible from both seats at a height no car achieves. The bunk behind the cab seats, curtain currently open. The windscreen wide - the road visible at a different angle than from a car, the distances compressing differently. The trailer behind: 18 metres of it, felt in every turn, every lane change, every braking. Not seen from the mirrors completely. Known by its weight.

## Yearbook
Kiel to Hamburg, four times a week, for three years. Three hundred and eighty-one runs on this stretch before today's. The seat worn to Dieter's shape. The CD in the player: loaded before Kiel, not changed, no longer heard.

The Baustelle south of Rendsburg: Dieter knew it was coming. Reduced speed at the last Kilometerstein before the orange signs. The cars behind did not understand why. Then they understood.

His wife called at Rendsburg-Büdelsdorf. He let it go to voicemail. The truck continued south. The call sat in the phone unanswered until Hamburg.

The bunk has been slept in at every overnight stop on this route. What Dieter thinks about before sleeping is not in the cab during the day. The curtain will be closed in Hamburg.
