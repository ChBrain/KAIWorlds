## Withheld
The Hüttener Berge. The hills west of here are a glacial moraine landscape - the highest terrain between the Danish border and Hamburg. The Raststätte Hüttener Berge is named for them. From the summit of the Hüttener Berg the Schlei, the Kieler Förde, and the North Sea coast are all visible on a clear day.
