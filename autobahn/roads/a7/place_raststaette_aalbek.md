# Raststätte Aalbek

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- Place: [the A7](place_the_a7.md)
- Position: [Raststätte Worker](position_raststaette_worker.md)

## Shown
Between [Neumünster-Nord](place_a7_13_neumuenster_nord.md) and [Neumünster-Mitte](place_a7_14_neumuenster_mitte.md). No exit number. Small. The building modest. [The coffee](piece_the_coffee.md) available. Toilets. The Autobahn close - the traffic sound constant.

## Holds
[The coffee](piece_the_coffee.md). [The tank](piece_the_tank.md).

## Offers
A stop between the two northern Neumünster exits. For drivers who need to stop but have not decided where: this Raststätte arrives before [Raststätte Neumünster](place_raststaette_neumuenster.md) further south.

## Withheld
Whether stopping here or at Neumünster makes more sense. The Raststätte does not advise. It offers [the coffee](piece_the_coffee.md) regardless.
