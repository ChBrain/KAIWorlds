# Gudow

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- Place: [the A24](place_the_a24.md)

## Shown
Exit 8b. The sign: Gudow. The last Schleswig-Holstein exit on the A24. The state border is 1.4 kilometres east. The road approaching the end of Schleswig-Holstein and the beginning of Mecklenburg-Vorpommern.

- 6 km west: [Hornbek](place_a24_hornbek.md)

## Holds
[The Kilometerstein](piece_the_kilometerstein.md).

## Offers

## Withheld
The Schaalsee. Germany's deepest lake lies a few kilometres south - a glacial lake that formed the border between Schleswig-Holstein and Mecklenburg-Vorpommern during the division. The lake and its shores were a restricted zone during the GDR period. Now a UNESCO biosphere reserve.
