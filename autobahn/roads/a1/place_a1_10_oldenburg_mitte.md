# Oldenburg in Holstein-Mitte

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- Place: [the A1](place_the_a1.md)

## Shown
The central Oldenburg exit. The sign: Oldenburg in Holstein-Mitte. The town accessible from here. The road continues its bypass of the town - three exits, none of them quite right for reaching the centre.

- 2 km north: [Oldenburg in Holstein-Nord](place_a1_09_oldenburg_nord.md)
- 2 km south: [Oldenburg in Holstein-Süd](place_a1_11_oldenburg_sued.md)

## Holds
[The Kilometerstein](piece_the_kilometerstein.md).

## Offers
Ausfahrt 10 - the exit ramp. The ordinary road below.

## Withheld
The Wallanlage. The medieval fortification earthworks on the Oldenburger Wall are still visible in the town - the remains of a Slav fortification from the 9th century. Two kilometres from this exit. Not signposted.
