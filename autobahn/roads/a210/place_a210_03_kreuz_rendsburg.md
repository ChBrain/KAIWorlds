# Kreuz Rendsburg

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- Place: [the A210](place_the_a210.md)

## Shown
Exit 3. The junction of the A210 and the A7. The A7 runs north to Flensburg and south to Hamburg. The A210 continues east toward Kiel. On the A7 this is exit 9.

- 2 km west/north: [Schacht-Audorf](place_a210_02_schacht_audorf.md)
- 5 km east/south: [Bredenbek](place_a210_04_bredenbek.md)

## Holds
[The Kilometerstein](piece_the_kilometerstein.md).

## Offers
Ausfahrt 3 - the exit ramp.

## Withheld
The Rader Hochbrücke two kilometres north. The bridge carries a carriageway over the Kiel Canal at 42 metres. Visible on the approach - the bridge deck above the tree line. The Schwebefähre hangs below the railway high bridge beside it: a transporter ferry carrying cars across the canal on a suspended gondola, operating since 1913.
