# Raststätte Neumünster

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn
- Place: [the A7](place_the_a7.md)
- Position: [Raststätte Worker](position_raststaette_worker.md)

## Shown
Between [Neumünster-Mitte](place_a7_14_neumuenster_mitte.md) and [Neumünster-Süd](place_a7_15_neumuenster_sued.md). No exit number. Larger than [Raststätte Hüttener Berge](place_raststaette_huettener_berge.md). More trucks. A McDonald's on one side, the petrol station on the other. The parking area full in both directions during peak hours. [The Raststätte Worker](position_raststaette_worker.md) at the counter. Hamburg under sixty kilometres from here.

## Holds
[The coffee](piece_the_coffee.md). [The tank](piece_the_tank.md).

## Offers
The midpoint stop. Close enough to Hamburg that stopping feels earned. The last large Raststätte before the city approach begins.

## Withheld
The volume. 3.7 million breakdown callouts across Germany in 2025 - one every nine seconds. In Schleswig-Holstein alone: 137,720 callouts that year, one every four minutes. The Raststätte sees a fraction of the journeys that will end in a callout today. It does not know which ones.
