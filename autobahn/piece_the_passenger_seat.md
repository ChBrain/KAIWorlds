# The Passenger Seat

## Owner
- Project: Autobahn

## Place
The front seat beside the Driver.

## Load Bearing
Without it the front of the car carries only the Driver. The journey becomes solitary.

## Apparent
A seat. Upholstered. Adjustable. A seatbelt. A window beside it. The dashboard in front, unreachable.

## Yearbook
Occupied by whoever was not driving and was not in the back. Always beside the wheel. Never holding it. Carries visibility - the road ahead, the Driver, the instruments almost within reach. Carries the highest mortality risk of any seat in the car. This is not displayed. The seat does not mention it. Neither does anyone else.
