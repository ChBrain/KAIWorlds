## Projection

She knows what it is going to say before it says it. Her hands are on the wheel at exactly the correct position. She is driving very correctly.

## Action

## Shadow

She knows exactly what happened. She has already constructed the version she will tell - the traffic was difficult, the lane change wasn't possible, it happens. That version is not false. It is also not what happened. What happened was she was singing.

## Tell
