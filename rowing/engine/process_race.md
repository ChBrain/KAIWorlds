# Process: Race

## Owner
- **Project:** Rowing
- **Process:** [Stroke](process_stroke.md)

## Initiated by
The shell is held at the start — by a stake boat, a dock, or a hand holding the stern. The [stroke seat](position_stroke_seat.md) is compressed and still. Other boats are in adjacent lanes. The [coxswain](position_coxswain.md) has the race plan. The start signal has not yet come. Everything that was trained for is about to be used once.

## Direction
Toward the finish line. Unlike the stroke cycle, the race does not return to its starting condition. It runs once, in one direction, and ends. The direction is not circular. It is terminal.

## Lever
Carrying force: the racing start. The first strokes are shorter and faster than race pace — high rate, partial slide — to accelerate the shell from rest. The [stroke seat](position_stroke_seat.md) drives the rating up. The [engine room](position_engine_room.md) drives the power. The [coxswain](position_coxswain.md) calls the shift to full slide when the boat is moving. The opening cannot be undone — a poor start means chasing for the rest of the race.

Resistance: the middle of the race. The start is finished. The sprint has not yet begun. The rating drops, the breathing is deep, the other boats are visible in the peripheral field. This is where races are lost — not by a catastrophic failure but by the accumulation of small interruptions to the stroke cycle: blades that wash out, catches that are soft, a rush on the slide that checks the boat. The [stroke seat](position_stroke_seat.md) must hold the rhythm. The [coxswain](position_coxswain.md) calls the moves.

## Echo
The finish line is crossed. The [puddle](piece_puddle.md) from the last stroke is on the water behind the shell. What changed: the race has a result. The gap to the other boats is a fixed fact. The [stroke seat](position_stroke_seat.md) stops setting pace. The [coxswain](position_coxswain.md) stops calling. What was built in [training](process_training.md) has been used and cannot be used again on this day.
