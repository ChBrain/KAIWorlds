# Position: Bow Seat
## What the boat feels first.

## Owner
- **Project:** Rowing
- **Scope:** Universal
- **Process:** [Stroke](process_stroke.md)

## Has
The sensitivity. The bow of the boat moves the most with the water — every wave, every gust, every imbalance in the stroke behind them is amplified here before the rest of the crew registers it. This position absorbs the conditions first and carries the finesse the engine room is not asked for. In a coxless boat, this seat may also steer.

## Orders
Keep the boat set.

## Loses
The belief that they will be recognised for what they prevent. The bow seat arrives carrying technical precision and the expectation that careful, invisible work counts. What they discover: the boat only notices the bow when it is wrong. When the balance holds, when the bow cuts cleanly, when the check is absorbed before it travels — this passes without acknowledgment. The contribution of this position is its own disappearance.

## Drives
On the loss — continues the work. Absorbs what the water brings. Keeps the bow moving through rather than against.
