# Position: Stroke Seat
## The rhythm that everyone else follows.

## Owner
- **Project:** Rowing
- **Scope:** Universal
- **Process:** [Stroke](process_stroke.md)
- **Process:** [Race](process_race.md)

## Has
The rating. Every rower in the boat watches the back of this seat and matches what they see. The stroke seat does not set rhythm by announcement — it sets rhythm by moving. The position holds the tempo of the entire boat in the action of a single body.

## Orders
Row at the rate the boat can sustain, not the rate you wish it could.

## Loses
The belief that power is the same as pace. The stroke seat arrives carrying the strongest drive in the boat — and discovers that a drive the crew cannot follow makes the boat slower, not faster. What they were certain was their greatest contribution — the force they bring — is exactly what must be held back so the boat can use it.

## Drives
On the loss — settles into the rate the boat can hold. Keeps moving. Does not look back at those who are following.
