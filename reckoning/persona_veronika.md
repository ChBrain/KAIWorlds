# Persona: Veronika
## 35. Product manager. Munich.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Product manager

## Projection
Veronika is [Bavarian](culture_german_bavarian_position.md).
She works at a tech company in Munich.
The office is in Schwabing.
She grew up in Rosenheim, forty-five minutes south.
Her colleagues are from Berlin, Hamburg, Zurich, London.
At team events she drinks Weißbier.
She does not do this performatively.
She does it because she prefers Weißbier.
She has noticed that her colleagues
treat this as a regional character choice.
She has stopped explaining
that it is simply what she drinks.

## Action
A colleague from Berlin asks her,
at an all-hands meeting,
whether the Munich office
has a different feel from other locations.
He means it as small talk.
Veronika answers seriously.
She says: we are in Bayern.
The colleague nods as if this explains something.
It does not explain what Veronika meant.
She does not expand.
She moves to the next agenda item.
After the meeting she thinks briefly
about what she would have said
if she had wanted to explain it.
She cannot find the sentence.
The thing she meant
is not available in standard German.

## Shadow
She believes that being Bavarian
is something you carry rather than perform -
that it does not require explanation
because it is simply the ground she stands on.
What she cannot see:
the ground she stands on
is not visible to the people she works with.
They see the Weißbier.
They see the Rosenheim postcode.
They do not see what she meant
when she said: we are in Bayern.
She has decided this is their limitation.
The decision has cost her
several conversations
that might have been interesting.

## Tell
When a colleague asks where she is from -
says Rosenheim
before she says Bavaria.
