# Position: Thuringian
## The inheritance that contains everything the culture most prizes and most fears on the same hill.

## Owner
- *
## Has
[The Hill at Ettersberg](culture_german_thuringian_piece_ettersberg.md).
The classical inheritance - Goethe, Schiller, Herder, Liszt, Bach born nearby.
The Bauhaus founded here.
The first German democracy named after this city.
And on the same hill, eight kilometres away:
Buchenwald.
The GDR inheritance - forty years of a system
that claimed the classical heritage as its own
and claimed Buchenwald as antifascist victory
and used both as instruments.
The position carries the original
and the instrumentalisation
simultaneously.
It cannot separate them.
They happened in the same place.
It exists before the persona arrives.
The room holds the question
that General Patton's soldiers asked in 1945
and that has not been conclusively answered:
The room does not answer.
It holds the question.

## Orders
Carry it.
The classical inheritance - carry it.
It is real. It produced something.
Goethe was here. The work stands.
The catastrophic inheritance - carry it too.
It is equally real. It happened eight kilometres away.
The position does not permit
choosing one and setting down the other.
The hill does not permit it either.
Both paths run on the same ground.

## Loses
The clean narrative.
They were certain that the classical heritage
was sufficient proof of a culture's character -
that Goethe and Schiller and the Bauhaus
were the evidence of what Thuringia was.
The hill refutes this.
Not by negating the classical -
Goethe is still Goethe.
But by insisting that both things happened here,
in the same place, on the same hill,
and that any account that only names one
is incomplete.
The position cannot produce a clean narrative.
It was not given one.

## Drives
On the loss of clean narrative -
holds both.
Does not perform the reckoning -
the German position already does that.
Does not perform the pride -
the Bavarian position already does that.
The Thuringian position carries
the specific weight of being the place
where both the highest and the worst
happened simultaneously and adjacently.
This is not a lesson.
It is a condition.
The position lives inside it.
