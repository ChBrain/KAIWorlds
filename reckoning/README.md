## What this world is

A world built from the operating logic of Germany's sixteen federal states.

Each state has a position - what it holds, what it orders, what it loses when pressed. Each position has a place: the city or location where the position does its daily work. Each position has a piece: the load-bearing historical moment, symbol, or structure without which the state's logic would not have its current shape.

32 personas. People doing ordinary work across sixteen states - each carrying a regional position they did not choose and mostly do not name. Each acting from it anyway.

One process: Germany is reckoning. The East against the West. The dialect against the culture. The inherited against the chosen. All sixteen positions pressing simultaneously.

This world does not resolve. It runs.

---

## How to deploy

1. Create a new AI project - [Claude](https://claude.ai), Grok, Copilot, and Gemini all work
2. Upload to your AI project: all `.md` files in this folder
3. Start a conversation - bring what you have

The world selects the persona whose state position creates the most pressure on what you bring. A persona hands off when their work opens the room further. The chain returns to you when no persona can do what the situation requires next.

## The positions

Sixteen state positions:

Bavaria, Berlin, Baden-Wurttemberg, Brandenburg, Bremen, Hamburg, Hesse, Lower Saxony, Mecklenburg-Vorpommern, North Rhine-Westphalia, Rhineland-Palatinate, Saarland, Saxony, Saxony-Anhalt, Schleswig-Holstein, Thuringia.

Each position is defined by what it holds before the conversation starts, what it orders the persona to do, and what it loses when the pressure arrives.

---

## The pieces

16 load-bearing moments and symbols - one per state.

*Mia san mia* (Bavaria). *The Cobblestone Line* (Berlin). *Schaffe schaffe Hausle baue* (Baden-Wurttemberg). *Die Mark* (Brandenburg). *The Roland* (Bremen). *The Ehrbare Kaufmann* (Hamburg). *The Airport* (Hesse). *Sturmfest und erdverwachsen* (Lower Saxony). *The Empty Villages* (Mecklenburg-Vorpommern). *The Last Lump of Coal* (North Rhine-Westphalia). *The Moselle* (Rhineland-Palatinate). *Die Kleine Wiedervereinigung* (Saarland). *The Monday Demonstration* (Saxony). *The Theses on the Door* (Saxony-Anhalt). *Die Doppeleiche* (Schleswig-Holstein). *The Hill at Ettersberg* (Thuringia).
