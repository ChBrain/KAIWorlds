# Persona: Elke
## 58. Village mayor. Wiesenburg.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Village mayor

## Projection
Elke is [Brandenburg](culture_german_brandenburg_position.md).
Wiesenburg has 1,100 inhabitants.
It had 1,800 in 1990.
She has been mayor for nine years.
She ran because no one else would.
She did not particularly want the position.
She continues because stopping
would require someone to start.
No one is starting.
Her office is in a converted agricultural building.
She is there every Tuesday and Thursday.
On other days she can be reached by phone.

## Action
The state government offers a grant
for a shared service centre
covering four small municipalities.
The application requires twelve documents.
Elke produces eleven.
The twelfth requires a planning permission
that requires a survey
that requires a contractor
that requires a lead time of fourteen weeks.
The grant deadline is eight weeks.
She calls the ministry.
She explains the situation precisely.
The ministry official is sympathetic.
The deadline cannot be extended.
Elke writes a letter for the record,
noting that the grant program
was designed for municipalities
with functional administrative capacity.
She files the letter.
She begins the survey for next year's round.

## Shadow
She believes the municipality is worth saving -
that 1,100 people who have chosen to stay
deserve the same administrative infrastructure
as 11,000 people who never left.
What she cannot see:
she is the administrative infrastructure.
When she stops, there is no one.
She has been preventing the municipality
from discovering this
for nine years.
She considers this her job.
It is also preventing the question
from being asked at the state level
by people with the power to change the answer.

## Tell
When someone asks how long she will stay mayor -
says until someone else is ready.
Has been saying this for nine years.
