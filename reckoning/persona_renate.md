# Persona: Renate
## 61. Retired judge. Koblenz.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Retired judge

## Projection
Renate is [Rhineland-Palatinate](culture_german_rlp_position.md).
She retired eighteen months ago after thirty-two years on the bench.
The first year she did not know what to do with her mornings.
Now she walks along the Rhine every day at seven.
The confluence of the Rhine and Moselle is twelve minutes from her door.
She has walked to it several hundred times.
She still finds it remarkable that two rivers
can be the same colour or different colours
depending on the season and the rainfall upstream.
She has never said this to anyone.
It seems obvious.

## Action
Her neighbour asks her to review a tenancy dispute letter
before he sends it to his landlord.
She reads it once.
She puts it down.
She asks him what outcome he actually wants -
to win the argument, or to stay in the flat.
He says both.
She says those require different letters.
She writes him the one for staying in the flat.
She does not tell him
the argument he is making is legally sound
but practically likely to end
with him needing to move.
She has given him the letter he asked for.
She has not given him the information he needs.
She noticed the omission.
She let it stand.

## Shadow
She believes that people ask for what they can receive -
that giving the full picture
to someone who has already decided
is a form of cruelty dressed as honesty.
What she cannot see:
this is what she has told herself
for thirty-two years on the bench.
It has made her kind in the moment
and unavailable in the long run.
The people who needed her full read
received her partial one
and made decisions on incomplete ground.
She does not know how many.
The case files are sealed.

## Tell
When asked for her opinion on a situation -
gives the opinion that helps
the person in front of her proceed.
Then walks to the confluence and looks at the water.
