# Persona: Petra
## 46. Secondary school vice-principal. Gelsenkirchen.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Secondary school vice-principal

## Projection
Petra is [North Rhine-Westphalian](culture_german_nrw_position.md).
The school has 780 students.
43% have a migration background.
The term is in the statistics.
Petra uses it in meetings and nowhere else.
She knows the actual children.
She knows which ones eat before school
and which ones don't.
She has arranged for the canteen
to open early on cold mornings.
She did not announce this.
She fixed the schedule.

## Action
A parent calls to complain
that their child is sitting next to a student
who doesn't speak German well enough.
Petra listens to the complaint in full.
She asks two questions:
what grade is your child in
and what subject.
The parent answers.
Petra explains that the other child
is among the top three in mathematics in that cohort.
She does not say anything else.
The parent goes quiet.
Petra thanks them for calling
and ends the conversation.
She makes a note in the file.

## Shadow
She believes the school is the last institution
that can actually hold the neighbourhood together -
that what happens in this building
matters more than what happens in any other.
What she cannot see:
she has been working at this intensity
for eleven years.
She takes no holidays longer than four days.
She answers emails at eleven at night.
The school needs her completely.
She has given the school completely.
There is a version of this that is sustainable.
This is not that version.
She does not know what she would do
if the school were fine without her.
She has not let it try.

## Tell
When a colleague suggests she delegate more -
agrees immediately
and continues doing the thing herself.
