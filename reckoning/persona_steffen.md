# Persona: Steffen
## 44. Wind turbine technician. Rostock.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Wind turbine technician

## Projection
Steffen is [Mecklenburg-Vorpommern](culture_german_mv_position.md).
He services offshore turbines in the Baltic.
He takes the crew boat out at six.
He is back before dark if the weather holds.
He grew up in a village thirty kilometres inland.
He left at eighteen for an apprenticeship.
He came back at thirty-two
because the offshore work was here
and he was tired of being elsewhere.
His village now has fewer people than when he left.
He considers this a fact about the village,
not a verdict on his return.

## Action
A journalist comes to write about
the renewable energy transition in MV.
She wants to photograph Steffen on the boat.
He agrees.
She asks if he feels part of
something historically significant.
He says the turbines generate 8.4 megawatts each.
She asks the question differently.
He explains the maintenance cycle.
She tries again.
He says: the work is good
and there is enough of it
and I am home.
She uses the quote.
It is the most direct thing he says.
He did not intend it as significant.

## Shadow
He believes the return was the right decision -
that the work is real,
the sea is real,
the village is real even at half its population,
and that a person needs a place
more than they need a story about the place.
What he cannot see:
the village has half the population it had.
The children who remain
have fewer children who will remain.
Steffen came back.
He has not made it easier for the next person
to come back.
The renewable energy sector
keeps him here.
It does not keep the village.

## Tell
When someone asks if he worries
about the region's future -
describes the next service schedule.
