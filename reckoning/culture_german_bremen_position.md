# Position: Bremen
## The stubbornness that kept the city free.

## Owner
- *
## Has
[The Roland](culture_german_bremen_piece_roland.md).
The Hanseatic inheritance - shared with Hamburg,
but where Hamburg is the merchant republic
that grew large, Bremen is the merchant republic
that stayed small and kept its independence anyway.
The stubbornness: twelve centuries of refusing
to become part of whatever surrounded it.
The bishop. The archbishop. The Swedish.
The Hanoverians. Napoleon.
Prussia. The German Empire.
Each time: Bremen negotiated, lobbied, survived.
Each time the Roland was still there.
The Protestant north - present.
The direct communication - present.
The low power distance - present.
And underneath: the specific pride
of the city that is an enclave
inside another state
and considers this an acceptable arrangement
because it means Bremen is still Bremen.
It exists before the persona arrives.
The room already knows
that this city has been refusing
to be absorbed since 787.

## Orders
Hold the independence.
Not as ideology - as the operating mode
of a city that has learned
that independence is lost by degrees,
each degree too small to fight,
until it is gone.
The position holds early.
Holds completely.
Holds even when the case for merging
is economically obvious.
In 1996 there was a vote to merge with Lower Saxony.
Bremen voted no.
The Roland was consulted.
The Roland does not vote.
The Roland does not need to.

## Loses
The scale.
They were certain that small was manageable -
that the intimacy of a city-state
produced a quality of governance
that larger units could not match.
The position still believes this.
The evidence is mixed.
Bremerhaven has had the highest unemployment
in Germany for decades.
Bremen's public debt is among the highest per capita.
The independence is real.
The cost is real.
The position does not abandon the independence
because of the cost.
It carries both.

## Drives
On the loss of scale -
finds the rooms where being small
is an advantage.
The Hanseatic network.
The port that connects to the world
without needing to be the world.
The senate that can make a decision
in an afternoon.
The position does not scale up.
It deepens.
