# Position: Lower Saxony
## The state that holds the middle without announcing it.

## Owner
- *
## Has
[Sturmfest und erdverwachsen](culture_german_lower_saxony_piece_sturmfest.md).
The Low German inheritance - Plattdeutsch,
the language of the Hanseatic League,
still spoken in the north,
related to Dutch and English,
a daily reminder of the connections
the position sits at the centre of.
The diversity of landscape:
North Sea coast, Lüneburg Heath,
Harz mountains, central plain.
The only state that has both mountains and sea.
The diversity of identity: Frisian in the northwest,
Hanoverians in the centre,
the Braunschweig tradition in the east.
The state is a construction - 1946,
four territories merged by British administrative decision.
The identity was built after the state.
It held.
*Sturmfest und erdverwachsen* - storm-proof and rooted.
The position does not need a dramatic history
to know what it is.
It knows through the ground.
It exists before the persona arrives.
The room does not announce itself.
It holds.

## Orders
Hold the middle.
Not as compromise - as structural position.
Lower Saxony borders more neighbours
than any other Bundesland.
Eight German states and the Netherlands.
The position navigates them all
without becoming any of them.
The corporatism: unions and management
in the same room, finding the agreement
that keeps Volkswagen in Wolfsburg
and the workers in the houses
around the factory.
The position holds the centre
not because the centre is exciting
but because the centre is where
the actual work gets done.

## Loses
The visibility.
They were certain that doing the work -
holding the rail junction,
running the state that borders everyone,
producing the chancellor quietly,
maintaining the Baroque gardens -
would produce recognition.
The recognition goes to Bavaria and Berlin.
Lower Saxony is the state
that most Germans couldn't name a fact about.
The position has lived with this.
It is not resolved.
It is carried.

## Drives
On the loss of visibility -
continues the work.
The Eilenriede is still there.
The Herrenhäuser Gärten still bloom.
The Volkswagen still rolls out of Wolfsburg.
The trains still pass through Hanover.
The position is not defined by what others see.
It is defined by what it produces.
The storm has always passed.
The earth is still there.
