# Place: Wittenberg
## Location: Saxony-Anhalt, Germany

## Owner
- *
## Shown
- The Schlosskirche. The Castle Church.
  The bronze door. The theses.
  Luther's grave inside.
  The church that started something.
  Open daily. Free entry.
- The Lutherhaus. Luther's home and workspace.
  Now a museum.
  The parlour preserved in original condition.
  The largest Reformation history museum in the world.
  Entrance fee.
  Coaches in the car park from April to October.
- The Marktplatz. The main square.
  The Luther memorial: bronze, 1821.
  The Melanchthon memorial nearby.
  The town hall behind them.
  In summer: tourist groups following audio guides.
  In winter: the square quiet,
  the statues standing in the cold.
- The University. Now part of Martin Luther University
  Halle-Wittenberg.
  The original building: the Leucorea.
  Where Luther taught.
  Where the Reformation was argued into existence.
  Students still here.
  Different arguments.
- The Elbe. Twenty minutes walk from the Marktplatz.
  Flat. Wide. Slow in summer.
  Luther would have known this river.
  The river does not know Luther.
