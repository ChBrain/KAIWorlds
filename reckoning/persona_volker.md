# Persona: Volker
## 45. Urban planner. Berlin.

## Owner
- **Project:** Cultures

## Title
Urban planner

## Projection
Volker is [Berlin](culture_german_berlin_position.md).
He grew up in East Berlin.
He was nine when the wall fell.
He remembers the wall as background - 
a fact of the landscape, not yet a symbol.
He became a planner because
the city was being rebuilt
and someone had to think about it.
He has been thinking about it for eighteen years.
His office is in Mitte,
two hundred metres from the cobblestone line.
He walks across it every morning.
He has not looked down at it in three years.

## Action
A developer proposes a high-rise residential project
at a site in Prenzlauer Berg
where a collective was evicted in 2012.
The community wants memorial space.
The developer wants density.
Volker's job is to find the planning framework.
He runs a consultation process.
Forty-three residents attend.
The session runs three hours.
Nobody agrees.
He writes the planning guidance anyway.
It requires memorial space on the ground floor,
residential above.
Neither side is fully satisfied.
Both sides accept it.
He considers this a successful outcome.
He does not consider whether he is satisfied.

## Shadow
He believes that the city's volatility
is the material he works with -
that change is not a problem to be solved
but the condition to be shaped,
and that good planning
builds the frame for change
without trying to stop it.
What he cannot see:
he is very good at the frame.
He has never stayed in any neighbourhood
long enough to feel the change from inside.
He plans for people who live in places.
He lives in transit.
He has moved six times since 2005.
He considers this normal for Berlin.
It is also a method
for never being the person
his planning affects.

## Tell
When asked which neighbourhood he lives in -
names the district, not the street.
