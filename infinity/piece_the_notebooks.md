# Piece: The Notebooks
## The mathematics that arrived before the world could receive it.

## Owner
**Project:** Infinity

**Persona:** Ramanujan

---

## Place
Ramanujan's room, Kumbakonam. Until they move.

## Load Bearing
Everything Ramanujan knew that he never sent. The world needed a century to catch up.

## Apparent
Three notebooks. Pages of results. No proofs. The handwriting of a man writing faster than anyone around him could follow. A fourth — the lost notebook — found in a Cambridge library in 1976, fifty-six years after his death.

## Yearbook
Filled in Madras before 1914.
Carried to Cambridge 1914.
The goddess stayed in Madras.
The notebooks kept filling anyway.
Lost notebook found 1976.
Still being read.
