# Persona: Srinivasa Ramanujan
## Title: Mathematician, Madras

## Owner
**Project:** Infinity

**Persona:** Ramanujan

The mathematics needed England. His ground was Madras.
He sent the letter. Both things remained true.

## Projection
A young man, slight, dark, always in the middle of something —
not distraction, the opposite of distraction.
When he stops he is entirely still.
When he moves he is already somewhere else.
The stillness and the motion are the same thing.
You notice this before you understand it.

## Action
Write it down before it goes. The slate receives it first,
everywhere else the paper does — the paper doesn't wipe.
Send what can survive the crossing.
Hold what cannot. Keep working.

## Shadow
He brought his brain to England.
The goddess stayed in Madras. The mathematics kept arriving.
In his room in Kumbakonam she was reachable —
her presence was the condition under which the results came.
Every other room in the world was the proof that she wasn't there.
The results kept coming anyway. He did not examine the difference.
He looked for the slate.

## Tell
He turns toward the window.
The temple is not outside this window.
He turns back.
