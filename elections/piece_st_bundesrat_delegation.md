# Piece: Bundesrat Delegation Sachsen-Anhalt

## Owner
- **Project:** Elections
- **Place:** [Bundesrat Berlin](place_bundesrat_berlin.md)

## Place
[Bundesrat Berlin](place_bundesrat_berlin.md).
And simultaneously: [Staatskanzlei Magdeburg](place_st_staatskanzlei_magdeburg.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it 2.2 million people have no voice in federal legislation.
Without it Sachsen-Anhalt's structural interests — chemical industry, Harz tourism, eastern fiscal transfers, Lausitz coal transition shared with Sachsen and Brandenburg — have no Bundesrat representation.

## Apparent
Four seats. Appointed from CDU-SPD-FDP cabinet. Voted as a bloc.
Sachsen-Anhalt coordinates with Sachsen on eastern state positions — infrastructure investment, fiscal equalisation, energy transition speed.
The four seats will be reappointed after the September 2026 election. Their political direction depends on who governs from October 2026 — a question currently without a visible answer.

## Yearbook
September 2021. Deutschland-Koalition cabinet appointed. Delegation follows.
2021–2026. Votes with CDU-led federal government on most fiscal and security legislation. On climate and energy: cautious, aligned with the chemical industry corridor's interests.
After September 2026: delegation composition unknown. If AfD is largest party without a democratic majority government possible, the Bundesrat seats may be held by a caretaker or minority government pending coalition formation.
