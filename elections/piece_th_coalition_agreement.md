## Owner

## Place
[Staatskanzlei Erfurt](place_th_staatskanzlei_erfurt.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it the Brombeer coalition has no programme.
Without it the three parties have no common ground to negotiate from.
Without it the Linke has nothing to hold the coalition to in the Pflichtenheft discussions.
Remove it and each vote requires a new negotiation from zero.

## Apparent
A bound document. Signed November 2024.
CDU, BSW, SPD. Three parties that had never governed together in any German state.
Streitpunkt during negotiations: a clause on Friedenspolitik — peace policy, specifically the question of US intermediate-range missiles stationed in Germany. BSW demanded a position. CDU resisted. Wagenknecht intervened from Berlin. Voigt travelled to Berlin personally. The clause was agreed in compromise language.
44 seats sign this document. The chamber has 88 seats.
The coalition knows, as it signs, that every contested vote requires either Linke support or produces failure.

## Yearbook
November 2024. CDU, BSW, SPD sign after weeks of negotiations repeatedly threatened by BSW's national leadership intervening in local talks. Steffen Schütz (BSW) to his partners after signing: "Wir waren eine Zumutung für euch" — we were a burden for you.
December 2024. The Brombeer coalition signs a "Pflichtenheft" with the Linke — a set of commitments for cooperation without formal toleration. The CDU's incompatibility resolution prevents a formal agreement. The Linke provides investiture votes for Voigt.
March 2025. First budget passed. Required Linke votes. Campaign promise on deportation detention places dropped — Linke condition. "An diesem Beispiel zeigt sich die Kompliziertheit dieses Bündnisses ohne Mehrheit."
April 2025 — 100 days in. Voigt declares the government is delivering. Opposition and analysts note the gap between programme and delivery. The Linke calls the coalition "Ankündigungsweltmeister" — announcement world champions.
Current polls: AfD 38%. CDU 24%. The governing coalition polling below its election result after four months.
