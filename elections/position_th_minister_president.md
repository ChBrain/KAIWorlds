# Position: Ministerpräsident Thüringen
## The mandate held without a majority. The coalition maintained without a formal partner.

## Owner
- **Project:** Elections
- **Process:** [the election process](process_election.md)
- **Process:** [the governing process](process_governing.md)
- **Place:** [Staatskanzlei Erfurt](place_th_staatskanzlei_erfurt.md)

## Has
The mandate from the [Thüringer Landtag Erfurt](place_th_landtag_erfurt.md) — obtained with votes from a party the position is formally forbidden to cooperate with.
The coalition agreement — programme, promise, and the knowledge that it requires external support to deliver any of it.
The Bundesrat delegation — four seats in Berlin.
The Pflichtenheft — a "parliamentary duties document" with the Linke that is not a coalition, not a toleration, and has no legal standing.
The Sperrminorität against the position in every constitutional vote — held by the largest party in the chamber.
All of this exists before the persona arrives.

## Orders
Govern without a majority.
Maintain the firewall against the largest party in the chamber.
Deliver the coalition programme using votes from a party the federal CDU forbids you to work with.
Do not call it a coalition. Do not call it toleration. Call it a Pflichtenheft.

## Loses
The clean mandate.
The position was certain it was bringing a governing mandate from the election.
The election produced a result in which the position's coalition holds exactly half the seats minus one.
Every bill is a negotiation with parties outside the coalition.
Every vote is a calculation about who can be brought in without breaking the rules that define what the position is allowed to do.
The position governs by managing the gap between what the result requires and what the rules permit.

## Drives
On the loss of the clean mandate —
invents new procedural categories.
The Pflichtenheft. The prelegislative consultation. The three-plus-one format.
Governs by naming the arrangement differently each time until the arrangement holds.
Whether it holds until 2029 is the question the position cannot answer.
