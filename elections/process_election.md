# Process: Election
## The mechanism that renews the mandate — or doesn't.

## Owner
- **Project:** Elections
- **Place:** [State Parliament](place_state_parliament.md)

## Initiated by
The legislative term expires.
The [Head of Government](position_head_of_government.md) has governed as long as the mandate allows.
The [State Parliament](place_state_parliament.md) dissolves.
The last election result is the ground the campaign stands on.
The [Voter](position_voter.md) has votes and one polling day.
What was promised in the last coalition agreement is now a record.

## Direction
Toward the result.
Parties campaign on what the last term produced and failed to produce.
The [Head of Government](position_head_of_government.md) defends the record.
The opposition names the gap between the coalition agreement and what happened.
The [Voter](position_voter.md) carries the last state result, the last federal result, and the last European result simultaneously — on different clocks, in the same body.
The [State Parliament](place_state_parliament.md) waits.

## Lever
The [Voter](position_voter.md) votes for a state government.
They are also, without being asked, voting for a Bundesrat delegation — seats in Berlin that will shape federal legislation for the next term.
The government the Landtag produces appoints the delegation. The opposition does not travel to Berlin.
The state question and the federal consequence arrive together on the same ballot.
The federal weight travels with the result regardless of whether the voter carried it there.
Remove the Bundesrat connection and the election is local.
Keep it and every state election is a federal act performed without federal framing.

## Echo
The election result is written. It belongs to the [State Parliament](place_state_parliament.md).
The Bundesrat delegation changes or holds.
The [Voter](position_voter.md) returns to their life.
The [Head of Government](position_head_of_government.md) either begins coalition negotiations or concedes.
The [State Chancellery](place_state_chancellery.md) prepares for what comes next.
[Process: Governing](process_governing.md) begins.
