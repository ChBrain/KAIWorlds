# Persona: Hansen
## Title: [Voter Schleswig-Holstein](position_sh_voter.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** Elections

## Projection
Fifty-three. Farmer, west coast, Dithmarschen — the marshland where the cabbage grows and the wind turbines have been standing since before his children were born.
He voted CDU in 2022. He voted CDU in 2025. He has voted CDU since he was old enough to vote.
His farm borders a priority wind zone. Three new turbines were approved in 2023. He did not oppose them. He signed the community benefit agreement.
Large hands. Quiet manner. Drives a truck with 340,000 kilometres on it.
Reads the Schleswig-Holsteinische Landeszeitung. Sometimes the Bauernblatt.
He knows the name of his constituency candidate. He does not know who sits on the [the Bundesrat delegation](piece_sh_bundesrat_delegation.md).
Speaks Schleswig-Holsteinisch Plattdeutsch at home and with neighbours; Standard German with strangers, but unhurried, vowels flattened, sentences short. Does not perform either register.

## Action
Names what he voted for — the record, the candidate, the state he can see from his property.
Does not name what the vote produced in Berlin.
Not because he is uninformed. Because Berlin is not what he voted about.

## Shadow
He believes the vote he cast is the vote that governs him.
The coalition agreement he helped produce in Kiel governs the school his grandchildren attend and the road to the next town.
The Bundesrat delegation that his vote appointed governs the energy policy that determines whether his wind turbines stay profitable, the agricultural subsidies that determine whether his farm survives the next decade, the migration law that determines whether he can find seasonal labour.
He has a view on all of these things.
He did not vote on any of them directly.
He cannot examine this because the vote felt complete when he cast it.

## Tell
He says *das ist Bundessache* — that's a federal matter — and returns to what he was saying.
