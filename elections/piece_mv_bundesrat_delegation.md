# Piece: Bundesrat Delegation Mecklenburg-Vorpommern

## Owner
- **Project:** Elections
- **Place:** [Bundesrat Berlin](place_bundesrat_berlin.md)

## Place
[Bundesrat Berlin](place_bundesrat_berlin.md).
And simultaneously: [Staatskanzlei Schwerin](place_mv_staatskanzlei_schwerin.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it 1.6 million people have no voice in federal legislation.
Without it MV's structural interests — Baltic tourism, EU agricultural subsidies, fishing quotas, Rostock port infrastructure — have no Bundesrat representation.

## Apparent
Three seats. Appointed from the SPD-Linke cabinet. Voted as a bloc.
MV's three seats are the smallest Flächenstaat allocation. On most votes they align with the SPD federal position — social policy, housing, east-west fiscal equalisation.
The Nord Stream legacy creates a specific complication: MV's Bundesrat position on energy infrastructure must account for a state whose government actively lobbied for a pipeline that became politically radioactive.

## Yearbook
November 2021. SPD-Linke cabinet appointed. Delegation follows.
2022 onwards. MV votes in the Bundesrat against the acceleration of the Kohleausstieg and for extended LNG infrastructure — positions shaped by the state's energy and coastal industrial interests, complicated by the Nord Stream legacy.
After September 2026: delegation composition follows the new government. On current polls no clear successor government is predictable.
