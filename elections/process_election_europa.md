# Process: Election Europaparlament
## The mechanism that renews the European mandate — in the institution that cannot propose its own laws.

## Owner
- **Project:** Elections
- **Place:** [Europaparlament Strassburg](place_europaparlament_strassburg.md)

## Initiated by
The five-year term expires.
The last European election result is the ground the campaign stands on.
The [Voter](position_voter.md) has one vote — a party list, no constituency candidate.
Voting age is 16 in Germany since 2024.
The SSW does not stand — the minority exemption that protects it in German elections does not apply here.
What was done in the outgoing Parliament is rarely the campaign.

## Direction
Toward the result.
Campaigns run nationally — each country uses its own parties, its own media, its own framing.
The European Parliament has no government to defend or oppose in the way national parliaments do.
The Commission is not accountable to the Parliament in the way a Chancellor is accountable to the Bundestag.
The [Voter](position_voter.md) votes for a European outcome through a national party list whose MEPs will join a transnational political group whose priorities the voter did not negotiate.
The [Europaparlament Strassburg](place_europaparlament_strassburg.md) and [Europaparlament Brüssel](place_europaparlament_bruessel.md) wait.

## Lever
The [Voter](position_voter.md) votes for MEPs who can co-decide, amend, and block legislation.
They cannot vote for MEPs who can initiate legislation — that right belongs to the Commission.
The gap between what a voter wants the EU to do and what the Parliament can make the EU do is structural. It is not a failure of the current Parliament. It is the design.
The Parliament can request. The Commission decides whether to respond.
Remove this constraint and the Parliament is a legislature.
Keep it and every European election produces a Parliament that governs reactively — shaping what arrives, unable to set the agenda.

## Echo
The European election result is written.
MEPs are distributed to political groups. The groups negotiate committee chairs and the legislative agenda.
The Commission President is proposed by the European Council and approved — or rejected — by the Parliament.
The [Voter](position_voter.md) returns to their life.
[Process: Governing Europaparlament](process_governing_europa.md) begins.
