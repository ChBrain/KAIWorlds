# Position: Voter Mecklenburg-Vorpommern

## Owner
- **Project:** Elections
- **Process:** [the election process](process_election.md)

## Has
Two votes — Erststimme and Zweitstimme. Voting age: 18.
One vote in the federal election. One vote in the European election.
The [election record](piece_mv_election_record.md) as the accumulated history.
Geography as political identity: Rostock, Stralsund, Greifswald — university cities, tourism, the Hanseatic legacy — vote differently from the rural interior, the Mecklenburgische Seenplatte, the western districts. The same ballot, a 37% AfD state polling average that sits unevenly across this geography.
The knowledge that September 2026 is five months away and the polls show no clear path to a democratic majority government.

## Orders
Vote or don't.
In 2021: 70.8% voted — highest since 2002. Schwesig's personal standing mobilised the electorate. In 2026 the same position produces a different number. The mobilisation dynamic is gone.
In September 2026: the position votes without a Schwesig-equivalent to vote for or against. The choice between parties must carry the weight that personal trust carried in 2021.

## Loses
The 2021 certainty.
Voting SPD in 2021 at 39.6% meant being part of a clear majority. Voting SPD in September 2026 at 23% means being part of a minority that may not reach government.
The CDU voter of 1990 — when CDU won 38.3% and governed — voting CDU in 2026 at 13% in the polls. The same type of vote, a completely different structural position.
The voter who stayed home in 2006 when turnout was 59.1% and the NPD entered the Landtag. The NPD came and went. The AfD came and is at 37%.

## Drives
On the loss of the 2021 certainty —
votes in September 2026 without Schwesig's personal authority pulling the SPD above its structural floor.
The position that turned out for Schwesig at 70.8% must decide whether the coalition, the party, or the principle is what it was actually voting for.
The ones who return do so for the party or the coalition. The ones who don't become the AfD's 37%.
