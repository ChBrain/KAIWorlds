# Position: Voter Sachsen

## Owner
- **Project:** Elections
- **Process:** [the election process](process_election.md)

## Has
Two votes — Erststimme and Zweitstimme. Voting age: 18.
One vote in the federal election. One vote in the European election.
The [election record](piece_sn_election_record.md) as the accumulated history — eight elections, eight CDU victories.
A regional identity that is distinct and consciously held. The Freistaat — the name reclaimed in 1990 — carries meaning. Sachsen is not just a German state; it is a territory with its own cultural weight: Luther's Bible German, Bach, the Sorbian minority in the Lausitz, the Erzgebirge craftwork, Dresden baroque, Leipzig trade fairs.
The memory of the DDR as lived experience — for the over-fifties — or as inherited condition for those who grew up after. Both are political.

## Orders
Vote or don't.
In 2024: 74.4% did. The second-highest turnout in any German state election that year, after Brandenburg. The choice between CDU and AfD — framed by Kretschmer as a question about whether Sachsen remains governable — mobilised both sides simultaneously.

## Loses
The assumption that CDU dominance is stable.
In 1994 the CDU had 58.1%. In 2024 it had 31.9%. The same party won both times — but the condition of winning changed beyond recognition.
The voter who cast the same CDU vote in 1994 as in 2024 is now one of 32% rather than one of 58%. Their vote still decided the government. But the state they are voting to govern is no longer the state where CDU dominance felt permanent.
The Dresden voter who voted CDU in every election and watched the AfD grow from 0% in 2009 to 30.6% in 2024 in their own city. The AfD voters are their neighbours. This is not an abstraction.

## Drives
On the loss of CDU dominance as permanent —
votes knowing the CDU has won every Sachsen election since 1990 and is now at 31.9%, governing as a minority.
The position in Leipzig and the position in Annaberg-Buchholz cast the same ballot and inhabit different Sachsen.
The position votes for the version it inhabits, knowing the other version also exists and also voted.
