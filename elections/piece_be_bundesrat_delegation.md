## Place
[Bundesrat Berlin](place_bundesrat_berlin.md).
And simultaneously: [Rotes Rathaus Berlin](place_be_rotes_rathaus.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it Berlin has no formal voice in federal legislation.
Without it 3.7 million people in the federal capital — including those who live 500 metres from the Bundesrat building — have no representation in it.
Remove it and the city that hosts the Bundesrat sends nobody to sit in it.

## Apparent
Four seats. Middle tier — more than the smallest states (3 seats), fewer than the largest (6).
Appointed from the CDU-SPD Senat. Voted as a bloc.
The Bundesrat building is on Leipziger Straße — in Berlin-Mitte, within the city the Senat governs. The delegation walks to work, in a sense.
Berlin's four votes are cast on federal legislation from a city that receives more from the federal fiscal system than it contributes. The delegation votes on laws that shape that system.
The state that hosts the federal capital does not have special weight in the chamber that meets in its territory.

## Yearbook
April 2023. CDU-SPD Senat appointed. Bundesrat delegation follows from the new government.
The delegation votes on housing law — politically central in Berlin. On migration — the AfD's rise in Berlin makes this visible. On transport infrastructure — Berlin's S-Bahn, U-Bahn, and regional rail are partially federal questions.
Berlin's four votes, aligned with a centre-right Senat since 2023, shifted the city's Bundesrat position from the R2G-era alignment with left-majority states.
