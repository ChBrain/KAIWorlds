# Position: Ministerpräsident Sachsen

## Owner
- **Project:** Elections
- **Process:** [the election process](process_election.md)
- **Process:** [the governing process](process_governing.md)
- **Place:** [Staatskanzlei Dresden](place_sn_staatskanzlei_dresden.md)

## Has
The mandate from the [Landtag Dresden](place_sn_landtag_dresden.md) — won in second ballot with Linke votes.
A CDU-SPD minority government with 50 of 120 seats — eleven short of majority.
The CDU's unbroken governing record since 1990 — the position has always been CDU, always Dresden, always the Freistaat.
Four Bundesrat seats.
The TSMC semiconductor fab near Dresden — a €10 billion investment that is the most significant industrial commitment in Sachsen since reunification.
The AfD's 40 seats — identical to the CDU's — as permanent structural constraint.
The Kretschmer brand: a Sachsen-first CDU that distances itself from the federal party on migration and Russia, governs pragmatically, and maintains personal popularity above CDU vote share.

## Orders
Govern as a minority — assembling majorities vote by vote, bill by bill, from a chamber where the largest opposition bloc is classified as gesichert rechtsextrem.
Hold the CDU's winning streak in a state where the AfD is closing the gap election by election.
Deliver the semiconductor investment as proof that Sachsen's economic transformation can produce jobs that replace what the DDR industries lost.
Not govern with the AfD. Not in Sachsen. Not while he is Ministerpräsident. The position has stated this as an absolute.

## Loses
The governing majority.
For thirty-four years the CDU in Sachsen governed with majorities — alone, or with a comfortable partner. The position now governs at the sufferance of parties it cannot formally rely on.
The Biedenkopf inheritance — the Landesvater model where CDU dominance was taken as the natural condition — is no longer available. The position holds the title but not the structure that made it stable.
Every legislative success requires revealing which parties provided the votes. If the Linke provides the margin repeatedly, the CDU's eastern conservative brand takes damage. If the AfD abstains to let something pass, the no-cooperation pledge is tested from the other side.

## Drives
On the loss of majority —
governs through personal authority rather than coalition arithmetic.
Kretschmer's approval rating substantially exceeds the CDU's vote share — the position is the CDU's most durable asset in Sachsen.
Frames every policy delivery as proof that Sachsen can function without extremism on either side. The semiconductor fab, the A4 corridor, the flood defences on the Elbe — these are the argument.
