## Load Bearing
There is no coalition agreement. The SPD governs alone with 29 of 51 seats — absolute majority. This is the piece that records the absence of a coalition as the governing condition.

## Apparent
SPD absolute majority government. No coalition document exists. No coalition partner.
29 of 51 seats — absolute majority of 3.
Rehlinger elected Ministerpräsidentin 25 April 2022 with 32 votes (SPD 29 + 3 from CDU or AfD in support). The investiture produced more votes than the SPD held — the absolute majority was confirmed by others' willingness to support it.
The Greens, who would have been the natural coalition partner, missed by 23 votes. If they had entered the Landtag, this would be a different document.

## Yearbook
27 March 2022. SPD wins absolute majority.
25 April 2022. Rehlinger elected Ministerpräsidentin.
2022–2027. SPD governs alone. No coalition negotiations. No coalition constraints. The government is the party programme.
The industrial transformation — Ford Saarlouis, Saarstahl hydrogen transition — is managed without a coalition partner's intervention or input.
Next election: 2027. Whether the SPD absolute majority is renewed, reduced, or reversed will determine whether this entry remains a record of successful solo governance or a peak that preceded decline.
