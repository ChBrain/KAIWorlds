# Position: Voter Berlin

## Owner
- **Project:** Elections
- **Process:** [the election process](process_election.md)

## Has
Two votes on Abgeordnetenhaus polling day — Erststimme for a constituency candidate (78 Wahlkreise), Zweitstimme for a party list.
Minimum voting age: 16 — one of six German states where 16-year-olds vote in state elections.
One vote in the federal election. One vote in the European election.
The [the election record](piece_be_election_record.md) as the accumulated history.
The particular experience of living in the federal capital and voting in a state election — the Bundestag is visible from some polling stations; the vote is for the Abgeordnetenhaus.
In East Berlin: the additional layer. The Scheinwahlen memory. Parents or grandparents who voted in 1946 before the split, or not at all from 1948 onwards.

## Orders
Vote or don't.
In Berlin the consequence of the vote is complicated by geography: Prenzlauer Berg votes differently from Spandau, Neukölln differently from Zehlendorf. The city's electoral map still carries the shadow of the wall — eastern districts trend left, western districts trend right.

## Loses
The expectation that the result produces the government the vote implied.
In 2023: the R2G coalition retained a majority. The SPD chose a different coalition. The Greens and Linke voters who voted for R2G did not get R2G.
The election that was declared invalid. In 2021, voters cast ballots that were subsequently annulled by constitutional court. The vote they cast governed nothing. The rerun produced a different result.
The vote that is cast in the city where all other votes are counted — but is not the vote that shapes federal policy.

## Drives
On the loss of the clear eastern/western divide —
votes in a city that has been reunified for 35 years and is still negotiating what that means precinct by precinct.
The Lichtenberg voter and the Zehlendorf voter cast ballots in the same election and describe different cities.
The position that notices this votes for its own Berlin, not for the composite.
