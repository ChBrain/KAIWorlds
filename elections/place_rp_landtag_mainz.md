## Shown
Deutschhausplatz 12, Mainz. A baroque building — the former Deutsche Haus of the Teutonic Knights — on the Rhein bank. Mainz: cathedral city, Gutenberg city, wine country on the Rhine.
105 seats currently — expanded by two CDU Überhangmandate and two Ausgleichsmandate. CDU 38, SPD 32, AfD 24, Greens 10. Majority: 53.
CDU-SPD coalition forming — together 70 seats, a large majority.
4 Bundesrat seats.
Voting age: 18.
Next election: 2031.
The 2026 election — held twelve days ago — ended 35 years of SPD-led government. The Landtag that forms in coming weeks will be the first CDU-led in RLP since 1987.

## Withheld
The FDP's 2.1% — two terms in government as Ampel partner, then cut in half at the federal level and wiped out at state level simultaneously. The FDP voters who cast the same type of vote in 2021 and 2026 and produced opposite outcomes.
The AfD's 24 seats — 19.5%, best in any western state. In the Westerwaldkreis and the Eifel they exceeded 25%. This is not eastern Germany. This is the Rhineland and the Eifel and the Palatinate.
What happens to the SPD at 25.9% under a CDU Ministerpräsident for the first time in living political memory.
