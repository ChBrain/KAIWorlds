## Shown
Regierungsstraße 73, Erfurt.
The working house of the Minister-President and the Thüringen state government.
Voigt governs from here. The Brombeer coalition runs from here.
Three parties in the coalition. One party formally excluded from cooperation. One party holding veto power over constitutional decisions. A budget that required Linke votes to pass.
The Bundesrat delegation — four seats in Berlin. Appointed from this coalition. SPD-governed states and CDU-governed states now share Thuringia's four votes in unpredictable combination.
The state that in 1999 was governed by a CDU with an absolute majority is now governed by a minority of a minority, sustained by a party the governing party is forbidden to work with formally.
