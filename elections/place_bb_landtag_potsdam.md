## Shown
Am Havelblick 8, Potsdam. On the Havel waterfront. The current building opened 2014 — a reconstruction of the baroque Stadtschloss that was demolished by the SED in 1960. The Landtag sits in a rebuilt palace on the site of a demolished palace. The SED demolished it. Brandenburg rebuilt it as a parliament.
88 seats currently. Maximum: 110 — capped by law, unique in Germany. Majority: 45.
SPD 32, AfD 30, BSW 14, CDU 12.
The AfD holds 30 seats — just above a third. Sperrminorität: constitutional decisions requiring two-thirds can be blocked. Woidke's coalition of SPD and BSW holds 46 — one above majority, two above the AfD's blocking threshold combined with any defection.
Voting age: 16.
Four Bundesrat seats.
