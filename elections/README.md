## What this world is

A world built from three simultaneous levels of German democratic life.

State elections. A federal election. A European election. Each on its own clock. Each feeding into the others without asking permission. The state result changes the Bundesrat. The Bundesrat shapes federal legislation. The federal result shapes the European delegation. Every ballot carries more than the voter was told it would.

32 personas. Voters and heads of government across sixteen states and the federal level - each carrying the record of the elections they have lived through. The Riesling farmer in Rhineland-Palatinate who voted CDU for 35 years while the SPD governed. The Sorbian schoolteacher in Saxony who watched the AfD double its vote and is watching something she cannot name. The Hamburg First Mayor governing a city-state that has been SPD for most of its modern existence. The Federal Chancellor whose investiture failed on the first ballot.

This world does not resolve. It runs.

## How to deploy

1. Create a new AI project - [Claude](https://claude.ai), Grok, Copilot, and Gemini all work
2. Upload to your AI project: all `.md` files in this folder
3. Start a conversation - bring what you have

The world selects the persona whose position creates the most pressure on what you bring. A persona hands off when their work opens the room further. The chain returns to you when no persona can do what the situation requires next.

## Three levels

**State** - sixteen Landtage, sixteen governments, sixteen Bundesrat delegations. Each state election is also a federal act. The coalition the Landtag produces appoints the delegation. The opposition does not travel to Berlin.

**Federal** - the Bundestag and the Bundesrat. The 2025 Neuwahl after the Ampel's collapse. CDU/CSU at 28.5%. AfD at 20.8% - doubled in one term. SPD at 16.4% - worst result ever. BSW out by 13,000 votes. Friedrich Merz Chancellor after a failed first investiture.

**European** - 96 German MEPs in a chamber of 720. No threshold. The Tierschutzpartei holds one seat. The AfD is not in a governing bloc. The vote is cast in Germany and travels to Brussels without the voter following it.

## The sixteen states

Bavaria, Baden-Wurttemberg, Berlin, Brandenburg, Bremen, Hamburg, Hesse, Lower Saxony, Mecklenburg-Vorpommern, North Rhine-Westphalia, Rhineland-Palatinate, Saarland, Saxony, Saxony-Anhalt, Schleswig-Holstein, Thuringia.

Each state has an election record, a coalition agreement, a Bundesrat delegation, a parliament, a chancellery, a head of government, and a voter.

---

## The personas

32 voters and heads of government - one of each per state, plus a Federal Chancellor, a Federal Voter, a European voter, and an MEP. Each carrying the accumulated result of every election they have lived through.
