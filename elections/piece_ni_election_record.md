## Load Bearing
Without it the SPD's western Stammland status has no evidence — Niedersachsen is the largest western state where the SPD reliably governs.
Without it the VW connection has no political history — the state holds 20% of Volkswagen, its votes on the supervisory board shape labour policy, and this has been true since the VW-Gesetz of 1960.
Without it the 2022 result — SPD holding 33% against the federal Ampel headwind, CDU at its worst since 1955 — has no context.
Without it Weil's three terms and their different coalition partners tell no story.

## Apparent
Niedersachsen: 8.1 million inhabitants. 6 Bundesrat seats — maximum, shared with Bayern and NRW. Capital: Hannover. Landtag in the Leineschloss.
Current: SPD-Green coalition. Weil Ministerpräsident, third term. SPD 57 seats, Greens 24, together 81 of 146. Majority: 74.
Next regular election: 2027.
Voting age: 18. 6 Bundesrat seats.

## Yearbook
**2027** — Next regular Landtag election.

**2022** — 9 October. SPD 33.4% — down 3.5 points. CDU **28.1%** — worst since 1955. Greens 14.5% — best ever. AfD 10.9% — up 4.7 points. FDP 4.7% — outside after one term. Linke 2.7% — outside. Turnout 60.3%. SPD-Green coalition. Weil third term. CDU Spitzenkandidat Althusmann resigned the party leadership on election night.

**2017** — 15 October. SPD 36.9%. CDU 33.6%. Greens 8.7%. FDP 7.5%. AfD 6.2% — first entry. Turnout 63.1%. Snap election — the Green Elke Twesten switched to CDU mid-term, the red-green majority collapsed by one seat. SPD-CDU coalition. Weil second term.

**2013** — 20 January. SPD 32.6%. CDU 36.0%. Greens 13.7%. FDP 9.9%. Turnout 59.4%. SPD-Green coalition by one seat majority. Weil first term. David McAllister (CDU) outgoing.

**2008** — 27 January. CDU 42.5%. SPD 30.3%. FDP 8.2%. Greens 8.0%. Turnout 57.1%. CDU-FDP coalition. Christian Wulff Ministerpräsident until 2010 (becomes Bundespräsident), then McAllister.

**2003** — 2 February. CDU **48.3%** — absolute majority. SPD 33.4%. Greens 7.6%. FDP 8.1%. Turnout 67.3%. CDU governs alone. Wulff.

**1998** — 1 March. SPD 47.9% — near-absolute majority. CDU 35.9%. Greens 7.0%. FDP 4.9% — outside. Turnout 73.8%. SPD governs with absolute majority. Gerhard Schröder briefly — leaves for federal chancellery in September 1998. Sigmar Gabriel succeeds.

**1994** — 13 March. SPD 44.3%. CDU 36.4%. Greens 7.4%. FDP 4.4% — outside. Turnout 73.8%. SPD absolute majority. Gerhard Schröder Ministerpräsident.

**1990** — 13 October. SPD 44.2%. CDU 42.0%. Greens 5.5%. FDP 6.0%. Turnout 74.8%. SPD-Green coalition. Gerhard Schröder. The 1990 election took place on the same day as the first all-German Bundestagswahl — the SPD held Niedersachsen against the national CDU wave.

**1986** — 15 June. SPD 42.1%. CDU 44.3% — CDU largest but SPD-Green coalition governs. Greens 7.1%. Turnout 75.4%. Ernst Albrecht (CDU) had governed since 1976 — now displaced despite being largest party.

**1978** — 4 June. CDU **48.7%** — absolute majority. SPD 42.2%. Turnout 80.3%. Ernst Albrecht Ministerpräsident — begins eight years of CDU dominance.

**1974** — 9 June. CDU **48.8%** — first CDU victory in Niedersachsen since 1959. SPD 43.1%. Turnout 83.2%. Alfred Kubel (SPD) outgoing, Albrecht incoming. Niedersachsen had been SPD-governed since 1959. The CDU breakthrough came with the national CDU recovery after the Brandt years.

**1970** — 14 June. SPD 46.1%. CDU 44.8%. Turnout 81.0%. SPD continues. Georg Diederichs.

**1967** — 4 June. SPD 43.1%. CDU 41.7%. FDP 6.9%. Turnout 76.3%. SPD-FDP coalition — breaks CDU-SPD Große Koalition. Diederichs.

**1963** — 19 May. SPD 44.9%. CDU 37.7%. FDP 8.9%. Turnout 75.5%. SPD-CDU Große Koalition, then SPD-FDP.

**1959** — 19 April. SPD 43.1%. CDU 35.9%. FDP 13.8%. Turnout 76.5%. SPD-FDP coalition — first SPD government in Niedersachsen. Hinrich Wilhelm Kopf Ministerpräsident, then Georg Diederichs.

**1955** — 24 April. CDU 38.5%. SPD 35.2%. FDP 12.4%. DP 9.5%. Turnout 68.2%. CDU-DP coalition. Heinrich Hellwege (DP) Ministerpräsident — Niedersachsen's unusual postwar phase with the conservative Deutsche Partei as a regional force.

**1951** — 6 May. SPD 33.4%. CDU 23.7%. DP 23.7%. FDP 11.7%. KPD 5.8%. Turnout 67.8%. SPD-DP-FDP coalition. Kopf Ministerpräsident.

**1947** — 20 April. SPD 43.4%. CDU 19.9%. DP 18.4%. FDP 8.8%. Turnout 65.5%. SPD governs. Kopf.
