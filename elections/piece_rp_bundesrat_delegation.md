## Owner
- **Project:** Elections

## Place
[Bundesrat Berlin](place_bundesrat_berlin.md).
And simultaneously: [Staatskanzlei Mainz](place_rp_staatskanzlei_mainz.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it 4.1 million people have no Bundesrat voice.
Without it RLP's structural interests — BASF's chemical industry, US military installations, wine sector regulation, Moselle and Rhine infrastructure — have no federal representation.

## Apparent
Four seats. Currently transitioning — the Ampel-Koalition SPD-Green-FDP delegation from 2021 is being replaced by the CDU-SPD coalition forming in April 2026.
For 35 years RLP's Bundesrat delegation voted with SPD-led positions. From spring 2026 it will vote with a CDU-led position. The four votes shift direction.
BASF Ludwigshafen — one of the largest chemical sites in the world — is on the RLP side of the Rhine. Federal chemical regulation, energy prices for industrial production, and EU environmental legislation all run through BASF's interests, which the Bundesrat delegation has always tracked.

## Yearbook
2021–2026. SPD-Green-FDP Ampel delegation. Voted with left-centre position on climate, energy transition, social policy.
Spring 2026. Transition to CDU-SPD coalition delegation. Four votes shift from Ampel alignment to CDU-led alignment. The direction change is significant on energy and migration policy in particular.
BASF's energy cost sensitivity — industrial electricity prices, the hydrogen transition, chemical regulation — will likely become more prominent in the new delegation's Bundesrat priorities.
