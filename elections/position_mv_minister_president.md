# Position: Ministerpräsidentin Mecklenburg-Vorpommern

## Owner
- **Project:** Elections
- **Process:** [the election process](process_election.md)
- **Process:** [the governing process](process_governing.md)
- **Place:** [Staatskanzlei Schwerin](place_mv_staatskanzlei_schwerin.md)

## Has
The mandate from the [Landtag Schwerin](place_mv_landtag_schwerin.md) — 41 votes, 35 against, 3 abstentions in November 2021.
A SPD-Linke coalition with 43 of 79 seats — narrow majority, three above threshold.
Three Bundesrat seats.
The SPD's unbroken governing record in MV since 1998 — twenty-eight years, four Ministerpräsidenten.
The Schweriner Schloss as the seat of government — the most photogenic executive building in Germany, on a lake island, facing the parliament across the water.
The Nord Stream Klimastiftung as the defining liability — a foundation the position established, wound up under international pressure, and carries as a political legacy that has not closed.

## Orders
Govern MV through its final months before the September 2026 election.
Manage the Nord Stream legacy — a decision made before the invasion, defended, then abandoned, not fully resolved.
Hold the SPD-Linke coalition's narrow majority on a budget and legislative agenda while polling at 23%.
Attempt to construct a path through which the SPD can remain the governing party after September 2026 — without the personal authority that won 39.6% in 2021.

## Loses
The 2021 mandate.
The 39.6% was built substantially on personal trust — Schwesig's approval ratings significantly exceeded the SPD's vote share. The Nord Stream affair eroded that trust without destroying the position. But trust and approval are not the same — the position still holds office; the mandate has contracted.
The CDU as a coalition option — choosing the Linke in 2021 over the CDU was deliberate. If the September 2026 arithmetic requires CDU, the position's 2021 choice becomes a constraint.
What would have happened if she had not established the Klimastiftung. The position cannot examine this. The counterfactual is the permanent background of everything the position does.

## Drives
On the loss of the 2021 mandate —
governs on delivery rather than trust — pointing to what the coalition built rather than to personal standing.
Uses the Schloss as a resource — international visitors, cultural events, the aesthetic of governing from a remarkable place.
Holds the North — MV as a distinct region with its own character, Hanseatic, Baltic, low-density — as the frame for every policy argument. Not eastern, not peripheral — coastal, particular.
