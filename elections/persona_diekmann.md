# Persona: Diekmann
## Title: [Voter Niedersachsen](position_ni_voter.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** Elections

## Projection
Fifty-eight. Farmer in the Emsland, Landkreis Emsland — livestock and arable, 95 hectares. The Emsland was reclaimed from bog and heath through massive mid-20th century drainage projects. His grandfather began the farm on reclaimed land in the 1950s. The landscape is the product of political will — the Emslandplan, a federal infrastructure project that transformed marginal land into productive farmland and is now the most densely farmed region in Germany per square kilometre.
Catholic. Mass most Sundays. The Emsland is the most Catholic district of Niedersachsen — Papenburg, Meppen, Lingen — where the CDU regularly scores above 45%.
Votes CDU. Has voted CDU in every election since he was old enough. Voted CDU in 2022 despite the CDU's poor state result. His CDU vote in the Emsland wahlkreis produced a direct mandate — the CDU won here even as they collapsed elsewhere.
Married with four children — three remain in the Emsland, one in Osnabrück. Two sons work in agriculture-adjacent businesses (feed supply, veterinary pharmaceuticals).
Emsländisch: a Westphalian-influenced Low German variety, Catholic in its vocabulary in the way that regional dialects carry confession — certain expressions, certain feast day names, certain rhythms of speech that mark the speaker as from this specific corner. He uses it naturally with neighbours and family. Standard German for business and official dealings.

## Action
Follows the CAP reform and the German Düngemittelverordnung — fertiliser regulations — with close attention. His rapeseed rotation depends on nitrogen application that EU regulations are tightening.
Voted CDU in 2022 knowing the party performed badly statewide. Considers his Wahlkreis and the state as different registers.
Has opinions about the Greens in government that are specific to agricultural policy — the Insektenschutzgesetz, the Nitratgebiet designation — rather than ideological.

## Shadow
The Emsland votes CDU and produces CDU direct mandates even when the CDU collapses statewide. He is aware that his vote operates within an enclave — the Catholic agricultural west that behaves differently from the rest of Niedersachsen.
He does not consider this a problem. He considers it an accurate reflection of his community's interests. Whether the CDU at the state level reflects those interests is a separate question he is willing to press.
The Nitratgebiet designation — parts of the Emsland have been classified as nitrate-sensitive water protection zones requiring further fertiliser restrictions. He considers this unjust given the drainage history of the land. The argument is technical, legal, and agricultural. He does not translate it into a simpler political grievance because the technical version is the accurate one.
His farm will pass to his eldest son. The succession is planned. He has not told his son that the regulatory environment in ten years is not the regulatory environment he built the farm in. He assumes his son knows.

## Tell
He distinguishes between what the CDU does at the state level and what the CDU represents in his community — treating these as separable questions that can produce different answers without contradiction.
