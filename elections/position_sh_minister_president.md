# Position: Minister-President Schleswig-Holstein
## The mandate held between elections.

## Owner
- **Project:** Elections
- **Process:** [the election process](process_election.md)
- **Process:** [the governing process](process_governing.md)
- **Place:** [Staatskanzlei Kiel](place_sh_staatskanzlei_kiel.md)

## Has
The mandate from the [Landeshaus Kiel](place_sh_landeshaus_kiel.md).
The [the coalition agreement](piece_sh_coalition_agreement.md) — a programme and a promise simultaneously.
The [the Bundesrat delegation](piece_sh_bundesrat_delegation.md) — four votes in Berlin, appointed from this position.
The [Staatskanzlei Kiel](place_sh_staatskanzlei_kiel.md) as working house.
All of this exists before the persona arrives.
A different person would hold the same things.

## Orders
Govern the state within its domains.
Represent the state in Berlin without the state watching.
Hold the coalition together for five years.

## Loses
The belief that governing and representing are the same act.
In Kiel the position is accountable — the Landtag watches, the press reports, the voter forms a view.
In Berlin the Bundesrat delegation votes on federal bills that Kiel did not frame, did not campaign on, and will not be held accountable for.
The Minister-President was certain they were bringing one mandate.
There are two. They do not always point the same direction.

## Drives
On the loss of the unified mandate —
manages the distance between the two rooms.
What is said in Berlin does not always return to Kiel.
What is decided in Kiel does not always travel to Berlin.
The position holds both and does not name the gap.
