## Place
[Bundesrat Berlin](place_bundesrat_berlin.md).
And simultaneously: [Staatskanzlei Potsdam](place_bb_staatskanzlei_potsdam.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it 2.5 million people have no voice in federal legislation.
Without it the Lausitz coal transition — which depends on federal law and federal money — has no formal Brandenburg representation in the chamber that shapes it.

## Apparent
Four seats. Appointed from the SPD-BSW cabinet. Voted as a bloc.
Brandenburg's four seats are cast from the state that surrounds Berlin. The federal government's decisions on energy, migration, and infrastructure shape Brandenburg more directly than most states — the Tesla Gigafactory in Grünheide, the Lausitz lignite fields, the Berlin-Brandenburg airport, the A10 motorway ring.

## Yearbook
November 2024. SPD-BSW cabinet appointed. Bundesrat delegation follows.
Brandenburg votes on federal legislation from a coalition whose positions on Friedenspolitik and coal transition sometimes align imperfectly with the federal SPD's line.
The four seats align with the SPD-led federal government's general direction on economic policy but introduce BSW positions on security and foreign policy that create occasional internal coalition tension before the Bundesrat vote.
