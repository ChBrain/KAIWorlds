## Yearbook
**2025** — 23 February. Snap election. Turnout 82.5%. CDU ~26% — 8 seats. AfD ~20% — 5 seats. SPD ~18% — 5 seats. Greens ~18% — 4 seats. Linke ~9% — 2 seats. SSW 0.15% — 1 seat. Total 25 seats from SH. CDU/CSU-SPD coalition forms nationally. Merz Chancellor from May. Eight days before Hamburg state election — federal coalition talks held back.

**2021** — 26 September. SPD 26.5%, CDU 22.0%, Greens 19.3%, FDP 14.4%, AfD 9.5%, SSW 0.2% — 1 seat. Traffic light coalition nationally. Scholz Chancellor.

**2017** — 24 September. CDU 34.0%, SPD 23.8%, AfD 10.9% — first entry into Bundestag nationally. FDP 12.8%, Greens 11.3%. Grand coalition nationally. Merkel continues.

**2013** — 22 September. CDU 38.6%, SPD 31.3%, Greens 10.0%, FDP 8.3% — nationally FDP falls below 5%, exits Bundestag. Grand coalition. Merkel third term.
