# Process: Governing Bundestag
## The period between the federal mandate and the federal reckoning.

## Owner
- **Project:** Elections
- **Place:** [Reichstag Berlin](place_reichstag_berlin.md)
- **Place:** [Bundesrat Berlin](place_bundesrat_berlin.md)

## Initiated by
The coalition agreement is signed.
The Chancellor is elected by the Bundestag.
The federal government takes office.
The Bundesrat composition is inherited from the state elections already run — the federal government did not produce it.
The gap between what was promised and what the Bundesrat will permit opens immediately.

## Direction
Toward the next federal election result.
The federal government legislates. The Bundestag debates and votes.
Every bill travels to the [Bundesrat Berlin](place_bundesrat_berlin.md).
Consent bills require Bundesrat approval. Objection bills can be overridden — but the override costs political capital.
State elections run throughout the term. Each one potentially shifts the Bundesrat composition.
A federal government can lose its Bundesrat majority mid-term without losing a federal vote.
The coalition agreement is the measure. The Bundesrat is the terrain.

## Lever
The Bundesrat sits between the federal government and its legislation.
Its composition is determined by state elections on sixteen different clocks.
A state election in Thuringia or Brandenburg shifts the federal room.
The federal government that won its mandate in February may face a different Bundesrat by October — because Bavaria or NRW or Berlin voted.
Remove the Bundesrat and the federal government governs alone.
Keep it and every state election is also a federal event, and every federal government governs on ground it only partially controls.

## Echo
The term has run.
The coalition agreement is now a record — what was promised, what the Bundesrat permitted, what was quietly abandoned.
The Bundesrat composition at the end of the term reflects four years of state elections the federal government watched but did not control.
The [Voter](position_voter.md) has lived in the result.
[Process: Election Bundestag](process_election_bundestag.md) begins again.
