## Place
[Bundesrat Berlin](place_bundesrat_berlin.md).
And simultaneously: [Staatsministerium Stuttgart](place_bw_staatsministerium.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it 11 million people in Germany's most productive state have no formal voice in federal legislation.
Without it BW's fiscal position — one of the two largest net contributors to the Länderfinanzausgleich — has no representation in the chamber that shapes the rules it pays into.
Remove it and Germany's most important industrial economy outside NRW has no seat in Berlin.

## Apparent
Five seats. Appointed from the Landesregierung — currently in transition as the coalition agreement is finalised.
Voted as a bloc. Coalition must agree a position before each vote.
Five votes on federal legislation from the state that produces Mercedes-Benz, Bosch, Porsche, SAP, Carl Zeiss — companies whose regulatory environment is shaped in part by what the Bundesrat approves.
BW and Bayern together contribute the largest share of Länderfinanzausgleich payments. This creates alignment on fiscal federalism votes but not always on social policy.

## Yearbook
2021–2026. Grün-Schwarz delegation under Kretschmann. Voted as a moderate centre-right coalition — supporting some federal climate legislation, resisting others, opposing federal overreach on state competences.
March 2026 onwards. Delegation composition follows the new coalition. Under negotiation. Grün-Schwarz continues — five seats align between two parties that just received identical vote shares and are bargaining hard over everything else.
