# Piece: Bundesrat Delegation Saarland

## Owner
- **Project:** Elections
- **Place:** [Bundesrat Berlin](place_bundesrat_berlin.md)

## Place
[Bundesrat Berlin](place_bundesrat_berlin.md).
And simultaneously: [Staatskanzlei Saarbrücken](place_sl_staatskanzlei_saarbruecken.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it 1 million people have no Bundesrat representation.
Without it Saarland's structural interests — steel transition, cross-border employment, the Ford transformation, EU regional funding — have no federal voice.

## Apparent
Three seats — the minimum for any Flächenstaat. Appointed from the SPD cabinet. Voted as a bloc.
Saarland's three votes align with SPD positions on social policy, industrial transformation, and Länderfinanzausgleich.
The cross-border dimension creates specific Bundesrat interests: German-French-Luxembourgish labour law harmonisation, EU frontier worker regulations, cross-border transport infrastructure. These are Saarland-specific positions that rarely align neatly with any standard coalition bloc.

## Yearbook
April 2022. SPD cabinet appointed. Delegation follows.
Three votes aligned with SPD federal positions. On industrial transformation, EU cohesion funds, and the hydrogen transition for steel: Saarland often leads Bundesrat initiatives or supports federal programmes that directly fund the Völklingen and Saarlouis sites.
