## Loses
The distinction between local and federal.
In Hamburg the position governs the port. The port operates under federal and EU trade law. The position is accountable locally for consequences it cannot fully control federally.
The Bundesrat delegation carries three votes on bills that shape port operations, shipping standards, trade tariffs. The position leads that delegation.
Three votes. Out of 69. Hamburg's weight in the Bundesrat is the same as Mecklenburg-Vorpommern's — a state with a third of Hamburg's population and none of its economic weight.
The position governs the second city of Germany with the Bundesrat weight of a small rural state.
It was certain it was bringing Hamburg's full consequence to Berlin.
It brings three votes.

builds coalitions with other SPD-led Länder before the session.
