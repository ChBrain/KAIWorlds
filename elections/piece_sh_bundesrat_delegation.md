# Piece: Bundesrat Delegation Schleswig-Holstein

## Owner
- **Project:** Elections
- **Place:** [Bundesrat Berlin](place_bundesrat_berlin.md)

## Place
[Bundesrat Berlin](place_bundesrat_berlin.md).
Leipziger Straße 3-4.
And simultaneously: [Staatskanzlei Kiel](place_sh_staatskanzlei_kiel.md).
The delegation lives in both places. The votes land in Berlin. The mandate comes from Kiel.

## Load Bearing
Without it Schleswig-Holstein has no voice in federal legislation.
Without it the state election produces only a state government.
Without it the connection between state mandate and federal power is severed.
Remove it and 2.9 million people have no representation in the chamber that can block or consent to federal law.

## Apparent
Four seats in the Bundesrat chamber.
Occupied by members of the state government — the Minister-President and three cabinet ministers, or their designated deputies.
The four votes are cast as a bloc. No splitting.
If the coalition cannot agree, the four votes become an abstention.
An abstention counts as opposition in a chamber that requires 35 for a majority.
The delegation changes composition when the state government changes.
No election. No campaign. The state election produces the government. The government produces the delegation.

## Yearbook
May 2022. The election result produces a CDU-Green coalition. The delegation composition shifts accordingly.
The Minister-President takes the presidency of the Bundesrat by rotation — each state holds it for one year in turn, alphabetically.
The delegation votes on federal legislation: infrastructure bills, tax reform, migration law, climate packages. Each vote is cast on behalf of 2.9 million people by four members of the state cabinet.
Most of those 2.9 million do not follow what the delegation votes on in Berlin.
The delegation knows this. It governs accordingly — locally accountable, federally consequential, the gap between the two managed quietly.
February 2025. The federal election produces a new Bundestag and eventually a new federal government. The Bundesrat composition, including SH's four seats, is unchanged — it does not respond to federal elections, only to state ones.
The delegation continues. The mandate from 2022 still holds. 2027 will either renew it or replace it.
