# Process: Governing
## The period between the mandate and the reckoning.

## Owner
- **Project:** Elections
- **Place:** [State Chancellery](place_state_chancellery.md)

## Initiated by
The coalition agreement is signed.
The [Head of Government](position_head_of_government.md) is elected by the [State Parliament](place_state_parliament.md).
The Bundesrat delegation is appointed from the state cabinet — not elected, appointed.
The opposition holds seats in the [State Parliament](place_state_parliament.md). It holds no seat in [Bundesrat Berlin](place_bundesrat_berlin.md).
The [State Chancellery](place_state_chancellery.md) becomes the working house of the state.
The gap between what was promised and what is possible opens immediately.

## Direction
Toward the next election result.
The [Head of Government](position_head_of_government.md) governs in the limited domain of the state — education, culture, police, administration.
The [Bundesrat Delegate](position_bundesrat_delegate.md) travels to [Bundesrat Berlin](place_bundesrat_berlin.md) and votes on federal legislation that the state electorate did not vote on directly.
Federal bills arrive. The delegation consents, objects, or abstains.
Abstention counts as opposition.
The coalition agreement is the measure.
Every year the distance between the agreement and the record grows.

## Lever
The Bundesrat delegation carries the state's votes into a chamber that requires 35 for a majority.
The delegation votes on bills that affect 84 million people.
They were appointed by a coalition elected by the state's population alone.
The Bundesrat composition shifts every time any state holds an election — mid-term, without a federal vote being cast.
A federal government can lose its Bundesrat majority between federal elections.
The state that just voted changed the federal room without the federal room voting.
Remove this mechanism and the states are administrative units.
Keep it and every state election is a federal event wearing local clothes.

## Echo
The term has run.
The coalition agreement is now a record — what was promised, what was delivered, what was quietly dropped.
The Bundesrat delegation has voted in Berlin. Some of those votes shaped federal law. Most went unnoticed at home.
The [Head of Government](position_head_of_government.md) carries the record into the next campaign — or doesn't run.
The [Voter](position_voter.md) has watched, lived in, and mostly not followed what the delegation did in Berlin.
The last election result is now history. The new result will be its own piece.
[Process: Election](process_election.md) begins again.
