## Owner
- **Project:** Elections

## Place
[Bundesrat Berlin](place_bundesrat_berlin.md).
And simultaneously: [Rathaus Bremen](place_hb_rathaus_bremen.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it 0.7 million people in Germany's smallest state have no federal voice.
Without it Bremen's specific interests — port infrastructure, Länderfinanzausgleich recipient status, ArcelorMittal steel transition, North Sea wind energy — have no Bundesrat representation.

## Apparent
Three seats — the minimum for any state. Appointed from the SPD-Green-Linke Senate. Voted as a bloc.
Bremen's three votes align consistently with the left-SPD Bundesrat position — for fiscal redistribution toward weaker states, for housing policy, for strong social insurance. Bremen is among the most reliable left Bundesrat blocs.
The port dimension: Bremerhaven is one of the world's largest car import terminals. Federal shipping and trade regulation matters directly to this state. Bremen's Bundesrat position on maritime infrastructure and trade law is specific and tracked by the port authority.

## Yearbook
July 2023. SPD-Green-Linke Senate appointed. Delegation follows.
Three votes aligned with SPD positions in a Bundesrat now dominated by CDU-led state governments after the Ampel's collapse.
Bremen's three votes are a consistent left anchor — not enough to determine outcomes alone, but part of the SPD-led bloc that includes NI (6 seats), MV (3 seats), BB (4 seats), SL (3 seats), HH (3 seats) and occasionally others.
