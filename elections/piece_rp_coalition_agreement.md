## Place
[Staatskanzlei Mainz](place_rp_staatskanzlei_mainz.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it CDU has no governing partner — 38 seats, majority 53.
Without it the end of 35 years of SPD government has no institutional successor.
Without it the first CDU-led government in RLP since 1991 has no record.

## Apparent
CDU and SPD. Not yet signed — negotiations ongoing as of April 2026.
Together: 70 of 105 seats — a large majority, 17 above threshold. The arithmetic is stable; the political relationship is new. CDU and SPD in RLP have been governing as opponents for 35 years. Coalition negotiations are also the first working relationship.
Gordon Schnieder (CDU) incoming Ministerpräsident — CDU Landeschef, first from the Eifel region.
Alexander Schweitzer (SPD) will lead the party in opposition or as junior partner — his tenure as Ministerpräsident lasted eight months.

## Yearbook
22 March 2026. Election. CDU 31.0%, SPD 25.9%. AfD 19.5%.
23 March 2026 onwards. CDU announces intent to negotiate with SPD. Schweitzer designated SPD negotiator.
April 2026. Coalition negotiations ongoing. Agreement not yet signed as of 03.04.2026.
The coalition agreement will be the first governing document of a CDU-led RLP government since 1991. It will reorient the state's Bundesrat position, its migration policy, its agricultural and wine sector support, and its fiscal approach.
