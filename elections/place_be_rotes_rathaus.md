## Runs: [the governing process](process_governing.md)

## Owner
- **Project:** Elections

## Shown
Rathausstraße 15, Berlin-Mitte. Named for its red brick facade, built 1861–1869. Located between Alexanderplatz and the Spree. The TV Tower is behind it. The Neptune Fountain is in front.
The seat of the Senat von Berlin — the state government — and the office of the Regierender Bürgermeister.
Not a Staatskanzlei. The Senat governs from the Rotes Rathaus. The Abgeordnetenhaus legislates from Niederkirchnerstraße. The federal government governs from the Bundeskanzleramt 3 kilometres to the west on the Spree bend.
Three governments in the same city. None of them the same government.
The Bundesrat sends 4 seats from here — appointed from the Senat.
Kai Wegner (CDU) has governed from here since 27 April 2023.

## Offers
Executive governance of a city-state of 3.7 million — the largest German city, the third largest in the EU after Paris and Madrid.
The Senat: Regierende Bürgermeister plus ten Senators — five from each coalition partner.
The four Bundesrat seats voted as a bloc from this building.
Housing policy, public transport (BVG), the Berliner Philharmoniker, the state universities, the police.
The running negotiation between Berlin as a broke city-state — chronically underfunded, dependent on the Länderfinanzausgleich — and Berlin as the showcase of German democracy and culture.

## Withheld
The Bundeskanzleramt is not here. The Bundestag is not here. The embassies answer to the federal government, not to the Senat.
Berlin is the federal capital but does not govern federally. The Regierende Bürgermeister is not more powerful than the Ministerpräsident of NRW. In the Bundesrat, Berlin has 4 votes. NRW has 6.
The fiscal reality: Berlin spends considerably more than it collects. The Länderfinanzausgleich transfers roughly €4 billion annually into Berlin from net contributor states, primarily Bayern and Baden-Württemberg.
What it costs to govern the capital of a country that made you a divided ruin for four decades and then reunified you at speed.
