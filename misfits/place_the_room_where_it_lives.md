# Place: The Room Where It Lives
## Location: Wherever the Body is.

## Owner
**Project:** MISFITS

**Persona:** The Bearer

## Shown
- A room. Any room.
  The chain did not design it.
  The chain does not know its dimensions.
  The chain has never been in it.
- The Product. Installed. Running.
  The green lights the Specification required.
  The Manual on the shelf.
  The shelf the Body put the Manual on
  because there was nowhere else to put it.
- The workarounds. Not visible as workarounds.
  Visible as how things are.
- The body of the Bearer.
  Moving through the room in the way the room requires.
  Not the way the Specification intended.
- No door back to the chain.
  The [Field Service Engineer](position_field_service_engineer.md)'s door closed.
  It did not reopen.

## Holds
- [The Misfits](piece_the_misfits.md).
  All of them.
  The room holds what the chain delivered
  and what the Body built to make the delivery liveable.

## Offers
- The threshold.
  The moment the Body reaches it
  [Service Management](process_service_management.md) becomes possible.
  The room does not announce when the threshold is crossed.
  The Body knows.

## Withheld
- What the Body has adapted to
  that it no longer knows is an adaptation.
  The room knows.
  The [Witness](persona_the_witness.md) will see it.
  If the Witness arrives.
