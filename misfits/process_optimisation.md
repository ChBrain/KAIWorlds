# Process: Optimisation
## The loop that closes or doesn't.

## Owner
**Project:** MISFITS

**Persona:** The Architect

## Initiates
[The Finding Misfit](piece_the_finding_misfit.md) exists.
The [Advocate](position_the_advocate.md) has carried it to the [Design Office](place_the_design_office.md).
The Architect has it on the desk.
[The Decision Travels](piece_the_decision_travels.md) runs here. In reverse. For the first time.

## Direction
Toward [The Specification Misfit](piece_the_specification_misfit.md).
The same one that started the chain.
This time it knows something it didn't know before.

## Lever
The recognition.
The [Design Engineer](position_design_engineer.md) reads the Finding
and recognises their own Specification Misfit in it.
Or does not.
It cannot be mandated.
It cannot be filed.
It has to happen in a person.

## Echo
The Specification is revised. Or it is not.
The loop closes. Or it doesn't.
A new Bearer will receive a smaller Misfit. Or the same one.
[R&D](process_rd.md) can begin again.
