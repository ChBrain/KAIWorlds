# Place: The Design Office
## Location: Wherever R&D works.

## Owner
**Project:** MISFITS

**Persona:** The Architect

## Shown
- A desk. A screen. Tools for making decisions precise.
  CAD software. Specifications. Standards documents.
  The brief on the desk.
- The category. Named. Documented.
  Standing in for the Body
  that is not in the room.
- No Body in the room.
  There has never been a Body in this room.
  The research is here instead —
  surveys, personas, use cases.
  The research is not a Body.
- The door to the rest of the organisation.
  [Procurement](process_procurement.md) is through it.
  The Body is not through it.
- A second door.
  It has been here the whole time.
  It opens onto the same room
  after the loop has run.
  The Architect does not always see it.

## Holds
- [The Specification Misfit](piece_the_specification_misfit.md).
  Before it travels.
- [The Finding Misfit](piece_the_finding_misfit.md).
  If it arrives.
  On the desk. Waiting to be read.

## Offers
- The Specification.
  To whoever the chain sends next.
- The recognition.
  To the Architect who reads the Finding
  and sees their own work in it.
  The room offers it.
  The Architect takes it or doesn't.

## Withheld
- What the Body's room looks like.
  The Design Office has never held that image.
  The Finding carries a fragment of it.
  The rest remains withheld
  until someone makes the journey
  the chain was never designed to make.
