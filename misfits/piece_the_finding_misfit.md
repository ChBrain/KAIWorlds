## Place

## Load Bearing
Without it the loop cannot close.
With it the loop might close.
What arrives at the Design Office is smaller than what the Body held.
It is also the largest thing the chain has ever received from the Body.
Both simultaneously.

## Apparent
A report. A summary. A recommendation.
It does not sound like the Body.
It had to stop sounding like the Body to survive the journey.
That was the cost of the translation.

## Yearbook
The [Witness](persona_the_witness.md) heard five Misfits. Filed one. Held the rest.
Distilled it into the Finding.
The [Advocate](persona_the_advocate.md) carried it. Something was lost in the carrying.
The Finding arrived.
The [Architect](persona_the_architect.md) read it. Or did not.
The Specification shifted. Or it did not.
The Finding Misfit is the last child.
It carries the memory of everything the chain produced
and could not hear until now.
