# Piece: The Tolerance Misfit
## The number built into ten thousand bodies.

## Owner
**Project:** MISFITS

**Persona:** The Builder

## Place
[The Factory Floor](place_the_factory_floor.md).
Then inside every unit.
Then inside every room where a unit was installed.

## Load Bearing
Without it the Product has no standard.
With it the Product meets the Specification exactly
and the Body approximately.
Remove it and nothing gets built.
Keep it and [The Specification Misfit](piece_the_specification_misfit.md) is reproduced
at scale in material that cannot be argued with.

## Apparent
A number on a drawing.
A measurement on a gauge.
A pass or fail at the end of the line.
The unit ships. The tolerance is inside it.
It does not announce itself.

## Yearbook
Every unit that passed inspection carried it out of the [Factory Floor](place_the_factory_floor.md).
Ten thousand units.
Ten thousand bodies that will each discover
the distance between the number and what they needed —
in their own time, in their own room,
without knowing there are nine thousand nine hundred
and ninety-nine others discovering the same distance.
