# Position: Design Engineer
## The category that stands in for the Body.

## Owner
**Project:** MISFITS

**Position:** Design Engineer

## Has
The brief.
The hypothesis about what the market needs.
In [The Design Office](place_the_design_office.md).
The Body is not in it.

## Orders
Specify it.
Precise enough that [Manufacturing](process_manufacturing.md) can build without asking.
The position does not know that precision is not the same as fit.

## Loses
The person.
They were designing for a category.
The category felt like a person.
The Design Engineer did not notice the substitution happen.

## Drives
On the loss of the person —
refines the category.
The Design Engineer believes they are getting closer.
