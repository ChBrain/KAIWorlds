# Position: Field Service Engineer
## The completion that mistakes departure for done.

## Owner
**Project:** MISFITS

**Position:** Field Service Engineer

## Has
The Product. The Manual. The checklist.
Six more installations after this one.
At [The Installation Site](place_the_installation_site.md).
The Body is present.
For the first time in the chain
the position and the Body are in the same room.

## Orders
Install it. Commission it. Get the sign-off.
Sign-off means the Product meets the Specification.
Not that it meets the Body.
The Field Service Engineer did not design the checklist.

## Loses
The moment.
The Body is standing in the room watching them pack up.
The moment the door closes
the Body begins a relationship with [The Misfits](piece_the_misfits.md)
that will run longer than the warranty.
The Field Service Engineer is in the van
before they know the moment existed.

## Drives
On the loss of the moment —
moves to the next installation.
[The Installation Misfit](piece_the_installation_misfit.md) is live in the room.
The Field Service Engineer is already on the road.
