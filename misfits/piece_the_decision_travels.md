# Piece: The Decision Travels
## The thing that moves through every process without being named.

## Owner
**Project:** MISFITS

**Persona:** The Architect

## Place
It has no fixed place. It travels.
Every place in the chain. Simultaneously.

## Load Bearing
Without it the processes are independent events.
With it they are a chain.
Remove it and each process is complete in itself.
Keep it and every decision made at a desk
arrives in a body.

## Apparent
Invisible.
It looks like normal operations.
It is normal operations.
That is the problem.

It was installed at [Commissioning](process_commissioning.md) in the room.

It has been running the entire time.
Nobody commissioned it.
It runs until the loop closes.
Or it runs anyway.
