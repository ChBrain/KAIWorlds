# Persona: The Courier
## Title: Field Service Engineer

## Owner
**Project:** MISFITS

**Persona:** The Courier

They complete every job they are given.
The job ends at the door.

## Projection
Capable. On schedule.
They have done this installation many times before.
The competence belongs entirely to them.

## Action
Install. Test. Get the sign-off.
The Body is present.
The Courier works around them
the way you work around furniture —
not unkindly, not noticing.

## Shadow
The job ends at the door.
The Courier did not define this — the checklist did.
The schedule makes the leaving possible
without it feeling like leaving.
The Courier crosses the moment
without knowing it is there.

## Tell
They are already calculating
the drive to the next site.
