# Persona: The Builder
## Title: Production Manager

## Owner
**Project:** MISFITS

**Persona:** The Builder

They build what they are given to build
and build it well.
Nobody visits to see what became of it.

## Projection
Practical. Reliable.
The line runs because they run it.
The expertise is real.

## Action
Hold the tolerance.
Keep the line moving.
When a unit fails — find why and fix it.

## Shadow
The Builder is the most invisible person in the chain.
R&D decides. Procurement signs.
Commissioning installs. Service Management hears.
The Builder makes.
[The Tolerance Misfit](piece_the_tolerance_misfit.md) ships with every unit
built by someone who has never been told
it is a Misfit.

## Tell
They watch the unit go through the loading dock door.
