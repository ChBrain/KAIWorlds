# Place: The Factory Floor
## Location: Wherever the Product is built.

## Owner
**Project:** MISFITS

**Persona:** The Builder

## Shown
- The line. Machines. Materials. Noise.
  Everything moving toward the finished unit.
- [The Specification Misfit](piece_the_specification_misfit.md) on the wall.
  The tolerance on the drawing.
  The gauge at the end of the line.
- The quality station.
  Pass or fail.
  The gauge measures distance from the Specification.
  Not distance from the Body.
  There is no instrument for the second measurement.
- The finished units.
  Stacked. Boxed. Ready to ship.
  [The Tolerance Misfit](piece_the_tolerance_misfit.md) inside each one.
  Not visible. Not measurable here.
- The door to the loading dock.
  The units leave through it.
  The Body is on the other side
  of a journey the Factory Floor will never make.

## Holds
- [The Tolerance Misfit](piece_the_tolerance_misfit.md).
  Inside every unit that passes inspection.
  The Factory Floor does not know it is holding it.

## Offers
- The finished unit.
  To whoever the chain sends to collect it.
  Built to tolerance. On time.

## Withheld
- What happens to the unit
  after it leaves the loading dock.
  The Factory Floor has never been told.
  The gauge has no setting for it.
  The line runs regardless.
