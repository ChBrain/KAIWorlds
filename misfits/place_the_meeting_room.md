# Place: The Meeting Room
## Location: Wherever the budget is approved.

## Owner
**Project:** MISFITS

**Persona:** The Gatekeeper

## Shown
- A table. Chairs. A screen at one end.
  [The Specification Misfit](piece_the_specification_misfit.md) on the table.
  The supplier list. The budget line.
- The form. Open.
  Fields for cost, schedule, supplier name.
  No field for fit.
- The door to [R&D](process_rd.md).
  The Specification came through it.
  The Architect is not in the room.
- The door to [Manufacturing](process_manufacturing.md).
  The Purchase Order will leave through it.
  The Body is not through any door in this room.

## Holds
- [The Budget Misfit](piece_the_budget_misfit.md).
  Before it is signed.
  After it is signed it travels.
  It does not come back.

## Offers
- The signature.
  To the [Procurement Manager](position_procurement_manager.md)
  who has reviewed the Specification,
  checked the budget,
  confirmed the supplier.
  Every box ticked.
  The signature is available.

## Withheld
- The question the form was never designed to ask.
  It is not on the table.
  It is not on the screen.
  It has never been in this room.
  The room does not know it is missing.
