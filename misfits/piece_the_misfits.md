# Piece: The Misfits
## The accumulated whole that only the Body holds.

## Owner
**Project:** MISFITS

**Persona:** The Bearer

## Place
The Body.
The chain has fragments —
[The Specification Misfit](piece_the_specification_misfit.md),
[The Budget Misfit](piece_the_budget_misfit.md),
[The Tolerance Misfit](piece_the_tolerance_misfit.md),
[The Installation Misfit](piece_the_installation_misfit.md),
[The Adaptation Misfit](piece_the_adaptation_misfit.md),
[The Ticket Misfit](piece_the_ticket_misfit.md),
[The Finding Misfit](piece_the_finding_misfit.md).
None of those are The Misfits.
The Misfits is what the Body holds
that the chain cannot see from any of its desks.

## Load Bearing
Without it the chain is a sequence of correct decisions
that produced a successful deployment.
With it the chain is a sequence of correct decisions
that produced a person
who absorbs, adapts, compensates, endures
and calls it Tuesday.

## Apparent
Invisible to the chain.
It looks like a person doing their job.
It is a person doing their job
and six other jobs the chain never commissioned
and will never pay for.

## Yearbook
The Specification Misfit was designed in.
The Budget Misfit made it official.
The Tolerance Misfit built it into the material.
The Installation Misfit transferred custody to the Body.
The Adaptation Misfit was built by the Body to make the others liveable.
The Ticket Misfit named one symptom of the accumulated whole.
The Finding Misfit carried part of what remained back toward the chain.

The chain held fragments — one per process, filed separately, never read together.
The Body held all of it. Before the Ticket. Through the Ticket. After the Ticket.

The Misfits is what you get when you read them together.
Nobody in the chain has ever done this.
The Body has always known.
It just didn't have a word for it.

Now it does.
