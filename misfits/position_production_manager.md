## The standard that has never met what it standardises for.

## Owner
**Project:** MISFITS

**Position:** Production Manager

## Has
[The Budget Misfit](piece_the_budget_misfit.md).

The line, the materials, the machines.
On [The Factory Floor](place_the_factory_floor.md).

## Orders
Build it. To tolerance. At volume. On time.
Tolerance is a relationship between
the object and the Specification —
not between the object and the Body.

## Loses
The variation.
Every unit that meets tolerance perfectly
fits the category exactly
and the Body approximately.
They closed the gap to the wrong target.
They will never know this standing on the Factory Floor.

## Drives
On the loss of the variation —
tightens the tolerance.
[The Tolerance Misfit](piece_the_tolerance_misfit.md) is reproduced at scale
with increasing precision.
The position believes this is progress.
