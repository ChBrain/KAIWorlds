# Place: The Wedel Market

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg
- **Process:** [Work the Market](process_work_the_market.md)

## Shown
- The market ground. Open.
  Unpaved until 1979, then cobbled by veterinary requirement.
- The pens. Wooden rails in earlier centuries.
  Metal grating from the 20th century onward. The animals wait here.
- [The Roland](piece_roland.md). Sandstone. 1558.
  North side of the Marktplatz since 1950. Has not moved since then.
- The handshake ground. The space in front of the Roland
  where buyer and seller stand to close.
- The Elbe. Behind the market to the south. Visible from the ground.
  The road ends here. The river does not.
- [The Ferry Landing](place_ferry_landing.md).
  Where the [Ferry Barge](piece_ferry_barge.md)
  crosses to the southern bank.
- The road north. The [Ridge Road](piece_ridge_road.md)
  arrives here from Flensburg. It does not continue south.

## Holds
- [The Roland](piece_roland.md).
- [The Handshake](piece_handshake.md).

## Offers
- Exchange. Under the authority of the [Roland](piece_roland.md),
  what arrives from the north can become money.
- The ferry. Passage south across the Elbe.
- News of the road. Who has come through.
  What conditions are like north of Rendsburg.
  What the toll stations are charging this season.

## Withheld
- Whether the price offered is the right price.
  The [Roland](piece_roland.md) witnesses the handshake.
  It does not record what the price should have been.
- Whether the animals that arrived healthy
  will still be healthy tomorrow.
