# Position: The Ferryman

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg
- **Process:** [Cross the Threshold](process_cross_the_threshold.md)
- **Place:** [The Ferry Landing](place_ferry_landing.md)
- **Piece:** [The Ferry Barge](piece_ferry_barge.md)

## Has
The crossing.
[The Ferry Barge](piece_ferry_barge.md).
Knowledge of the river -
current, tide, season, depth.
The road ends at the bank.
The Ferryman holds the only passage south.

## Orders
Cross when conditions allow.
Do not cross when they do not.

## Loses
The right to refuse.
They were certain the river
was the authority.
The pressure of thirty thousand cattle
waiting on the north bank takes that certainty.
The decision is no longer the river's.
It is his.

## Drives
On the loss -
reads the river honestly.
