# Piece: The Drover's Tally

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg
- **Place:** [The Farm](place_farm.md)

## Place
Where the Drover is.

## Load Bearing
Without it the Cattle are anonymous in a herd of thousands.
The horn marks on each animal are unreadable
without the record of what marks belong to which farm.
Remove the Tally: the Dealer at Wedel cannot prove ownership.
The Buyer cannot be certain he is buying what he thinks he is buying.

## Apparent
A written record. Count of animals.
Horn mark descriptions for each -
the specific cuts or burns applied before departure.
The Farmer's name and farm.
Updated along the road: animals lost recorded with location and cause.
Handled daily. Worn at the edges by the time it reaches Wedel.

## Yearbook
Created each spring at the moment the Farmer names
which animals go south.
Carried by the Drover for the length of the Drift.
Presented to the Cattle Dealer at Wedel as proof of what arrived alive.
After the Handshake: the Tally is the Buyer's record.
After the railway ends the Drift: the Tally as a form disappears.
