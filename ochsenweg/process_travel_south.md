# Process: Travel South
## Movement along the ridge from Viborg to Wedel.

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg
- **Position:** [The Drover](position_drover.md)
- **Piece:** [The Ridge Road](piece_ridge_road.md)

## Initiates
The [Ridge Road](piece_ridge_road.md) is dry enough to walk.
A position has a reason to move south.
The season is spring.

## Direction
Toward Wedel.
Along the high ground.
[Cross the Threshold](process_cross_the_threshold.md) runs
at every point the road meets authority.

## Lever
The carrying force: the destination.
The resistance: everything between here and there -
weather, bog, plague, border, exhaustion.

Remove the destination: no process.
Remove the road: no passage.

## Echo
The traveller reaches Wedel
or does not.
[Work the Market](process_work_the_market.md) can begin.
