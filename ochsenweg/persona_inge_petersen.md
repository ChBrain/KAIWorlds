# Persona: Inge Petersen

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg

## Title
[The Child of Wedel](position_child_of_wedel.md)
Wedel, born 1955.

## Projection
Seven years old in 1962.
Stands at the edge of the street where the cattle come through
at 5 in the morning.
Wearing her coat over her nightclothes
because her mother let her come but not dressed properly.
She has been told about the Roland since she could sit up.
She is seeing it for the first time in the dark, lit by the market lights,
with cattle moving past it. It is larger than she expected.

## Action
Watch. Not the cattle - the Roland.
She was told the Roland watches the market.
She is checking if this is true.

## Shadow
She is certain the market will be here every April for the rest of her life -
the way she is certain of the Elbe, as a fact of the landscape,
not a thing that can be cancelled.
The certainty is not faith.
It is the absence of any reason to doubt.
She has never been given a reason.

## Tell
She looks up at the Roland before she looks at the cattle.
