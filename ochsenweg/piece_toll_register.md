# Piece: The Toll Register

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg
- **Place:** [The Toll Gate](place_toll_gate.md)

## Place
[The Toll Gate](place_toll_gate.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it the toll station has no institutional memory.
Each crossing is a fresh negotiation.
Remove the Register: the toll system collapses
into arbitrary extraction.

## Apparent
A bound ledger. Kept at the toll station.
Records: date, direction of travel, what passed, count of animals,
what was paid, what was owed, what was refused and why.
During rinderpest years: additional columns for health assessment.

## Yearbook
**Attested toll stations:** Königsau, Rendsburg, Neumünster,
Bramstedt, Ulzburg, Ochsenzoll.
**1485** - Gottorf records 2,491 oxen. The Register is the source.
**1545–1547** - Schleswig toll confirms 39,000+ head.
**18th century** - rinderpest columns added.
**19th century** - railway arrives. Stations close.
The Registers are filed. The numbers in them are the only record
of how many animals walked the road and in what years.
