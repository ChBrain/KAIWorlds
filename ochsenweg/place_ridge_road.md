# Place: The Ridge Road

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg
- **Process:** [Travel South](process_travel_south.md)
- **Piece:** [The Ridge Road](piece_ridge_road.md)

## Shown
- High ground. The watershed of the peninsula.
  Dry underfoot when everything below is bog.
- A worn path - compressed by weight and repetition.
  Not built. Walked into being.
- In spring: parallel tracks running south.
  Several drovers, several herds, the same ridge, the same direction.
  The corridor is wide.
- Burial mounds visible on clear days - to the east and west.
  Older than the road. Beside it because the road was already there.
- Sky larger than expected.
- The road ahead. The road behind. Both visible for a long distance.
  No shelter. No ambiguity about direction.

## Holds
- [The Ridge Road](piece_ridge_road.md).
  The piece that is also this place.
  The Yearbook of everyone who has walked it.

## Offers
- Passage south. The only dry passage north to south on the peninsula.
- The knowledge of other travellers. On the ridge everyone is visible.
- The toll stations and inns at intervals.

## Withheld
- How far it is to the next threshold. The road gives no distances.
- Whether the herd ahead has plague.
  The road carries everyone equally.
