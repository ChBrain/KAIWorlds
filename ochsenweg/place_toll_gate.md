# Place: The Toll Gate

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg
- **Process:** [Cross the Threshold](process_cross_the_threshold.md)
- **Position:** [The Toll Keeper](position_toll_keeper.md)
- **Piece:** [The Gate](piece_gate.md)

## Shown
- The road narrowed to a single point.
  Heath or marsh on both sides. No way around.
- [The Gate](piece_gate.md). Closed until opened.
- The toll keeper's booth or house beside it.
  [The Toll Register](piece_toll_register.md) inside.
- Pens or holding area where animals wait
  while documentation is read and payment assessed.
- Other travellers waiting. The road backs up here.
- In rinderpest years: an additional barrier further out
  where the health assessment begins before the gate is reached.

## Holds
- [The Gate](piece_gate.md).
- [The Toll Register](piece_toll_register.md).

## Offers
- Passage south. For payment, documentation,
  and the absence of plague signs.
- The Register entry - proof that this herd passed this point
  on this date.

## Withheld
- What the Toll Keeper suspects about the animals in front of him
  but cannot prove.
  The Register records what was decided.
  It does not record what was uncertain.
