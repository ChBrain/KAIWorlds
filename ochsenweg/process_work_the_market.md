# Process: Work the Market
## The traveller uses what the market offers.

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg
- **Position:** [The Trader](position_trader.md)
- **Place:** [The Wedel Market](place_wedel_market.md)
- **Piece:** [The Roland](piece_roland.md)

## Initiates
The traveller has reached [The Wedel Market](place_wedel_market.md).
The market is open.
The [Roland](piece_roland.md) is present.
The traveller has something to exchange
or something to obtain.

## Direction
Toward exchange.
What the traveller brought south
meets what the market holds.

## Lever
The carrying force: what the traveller needs from the market.
The resistance: the market does not need the traveller.

Remove the need: no pressure, no exchange.
Remove the market: nowhere to resolve the journey.

## Echo
Exchange occurs or it does not.
If exchange: [The Handshake](piece_handshake.md).
[Travel South](process_travel_south.md) closes for this traveller.
