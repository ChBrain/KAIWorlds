# Persona: Sigrid Magnusdóttir

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg

## Title
[The Pilgrim](position_pilgrim.md)
Norwegian, c. 1347.

## Projection
Tall. Moves with intention.
Carries [The Pilgrim's Credential](piece_pilgrims_credential.md)
in a leather pouch against her chest.
More stamped than most pilgrims at this stage -
she has been walking since Bergen.
Her boots are wrong for this landscape. They were right for Norway.

## Action
Walk south. At each threshold: present the credential,
receive the stamp, continue.
The destination is Santiago.
The distance remaining is not something she calculates.

## Shadow
She left Norway because her husband died
and the farm passed to his brother.
She told everyone - and herself - that the pilgrimage was faith.
The credential says: pilgrim. It does not say: displaced.
She is certain the destination will resolve what she has not named.

## Tell
She touches the credential pouch
when someone asks where she is going.
