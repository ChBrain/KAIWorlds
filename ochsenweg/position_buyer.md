# Position: The Buyer

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg
- **Process:** [Work the Market](process_work_the_market.md)
- **Place:** [The Wedel Market](place_wedel_market.md)

## Has
Money. Or credit.
Knowledge of what lean Jutland cattle
will be worth after fattening
in the Frisian or Flemish marshes.
The Buyer does not need to buy today.
The Dealer does.
This is the entire difference
between their positions.

## Orders
Assess the animals.
Name a price.
Wait.

## Loses
The advantage.
They were certain their patience
against the Dealer's urgency
meant the price was theirs to name.
The advantage held at dawn
is gone by midmorning
if the market fills with Buyers.

## Drives
On the loss -
decides whether the animals
are worth the price the Dealer will accept.
