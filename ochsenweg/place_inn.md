# Place: The Inn

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg
- **Process:** [Travel South](process_travel_south.md)
- **Position:** [The Innkeeper](position_innkeeper.md)

## Shown
- A building beside the road. Visible from the ridge before you reach it.
- The main room. A fire. Tables.
  Smell of food, smoke, wet animals.
- Sleeping space - a room, a loft, or a shared floor.
- The yard or paddock behind. Animals penned overnight.
  Hay available for purchase.
- The road visible through the window or the open door.
- Other travellers already present from both directions.

## Holds
- News of the road. North and south.
  Road conditions. Plague reports. Toll prices.
  Which drovers passed yesterday and in what condition.

## Offers
- Food. Shelter. Fodder for animals. All at a price.
- The road news - offered freely because it keeps travellers alive.
- The paddock. The animals can rest.

## Withheld
- Which of the travellers in the room should not be trusted.
  The Innkeeper knows this. It is not offered.
  It is available to whoever asks the right question in the right way.
