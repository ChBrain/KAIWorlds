# Process: Cross the Threshold
## Movement meets authority.

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg
- **Position:** [The Toll Keeper](position_toll_keeper.md)
- **Piece:** [The Gate](piece_gate.md)

## Initiates
[Travel South](process_travel_south.md) reaches a point
that has authority over what passes.
The [Toll Keeper](position_toll_keeper.md) is present.
The [Gate](piece_gate.md) is closed.

## Direction
Toward passage or refusal.
The [Toll Keeper](position_toll_keeper.md) reads what arrives
against what is permitted.
The process does not move
until the question is answered.

## Lever
The carrying force: the need to continue south.
The resistance: the [Toll Keeper](position_toll_keeper.md)
does not open the [Gate](piece_gate.md) for need.
He opens it for payment, documentation,
or the absence of plague signs.

Remove the Gate: no threshold, no authority.
Remove the need: no pressure, no process.

## Echo
The [Gate](piece_gate.md) opens or it does not.
The [Toll Register](piece_toll_register.md) records both.
[Travel South](process_travel_south.md) resumes or stops.
