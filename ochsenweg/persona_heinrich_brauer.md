# Persona: Heinrich Brauer

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg

## Title
[The Trader](position_trader.md)
Lübeck, c. 1510.

## Projection
Well-dressed for the road, which means less well-dressed than he would like.
Travels with two pack horses and a hired guard who says very little.
Carries a ledger in addition to goods.
The ledger is more important than the goods.

## Action
Read the road for information. Everything else follows.

## Shadow
He believes the information he collects is more accurate
than what others have because he collects it himself.
He does not account for the fact that everyone on the road
is also managing what they tell him.
The Drover who mentioned, casually, that he heard plague
was west of Rendsburg said it to slow Heinrich down.
Heinrich wrote it in the ledger as fact.

## Tell
He writes in the ledger while the other person is still speaking.
