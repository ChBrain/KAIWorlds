# Place: The Farm

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg
- **Process:** [Travel South](process_travel_south.md)

## Shown
- Land. Pasture and hay fields.
- The cattle. Wintered here. Lean from the cold months.
- The byre or enclosure where the animals that will go south
  are separated from those that stay.
- The farm buildings. A house. Storage.
- The road north or south - visible from the farm, or known to be nearby.
- In contracted years: the Buyer or his agent, arrived the previous autumn.
  Now gone. The Contracte is here in his place.

## Holds
- [The Contracte](piece_contracte.md).
  In contracted years: the agreement made the previous autumn.
- [The Cattle](piece_cattle.md).
  Until the Farmer decides which go south.

## Offers
- The animals that will become [The Cattle](piece_cattle.md)
  of this year's Drift.
- The horn marking - the act that makes individual animals
  legible to the road's institutions.
- [The Drover's Tally](piece_drovers_tally.md),
  created here before departure.

## Withheld
- Whether the animals wintered well enough to fulfil what was promised.
  The Contracte names a number.
  The winter does not read contracts.
  The gap between the promised count and the actual count
  is held here until the Drift departs.
