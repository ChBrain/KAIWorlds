# Persona: Georg Pfeiffer

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg

## Title
[The Toll Keeper](position_toll_keeper.md)
Rendsburg, 1744.

## Projection
A careful man. The booth is orderly.
[The Toll Register](piece_toll_register.md)
is open to the current page.
The rinderpest column was added two years ago.
He added it himself, without instruction,
because the instruction had not come
and the animals were arriving anyway.

## Action
Read what arrives against the Register.
Ask the questions the new column requires.
Record the answer. Open [The Gate](piece_gate.md) or do not.

## Shadow
He believes the Register makes the decision for him.
He has not told his wife that last Tuesday he turned back a herd
on suspicion alone - no visible symptoms,
just a way one animal was standing that he had seen once before, in 1741,
three days before an entire herd was destroyed.
The Register says: herd refused, suspected illness.
It does not say: I was not certain.

## Tell
He closes the Register before he gives his decision.
