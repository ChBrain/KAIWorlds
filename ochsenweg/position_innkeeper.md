# Position: The Innkeeper

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg
- **Process:** [Travel South](process_travel_south.md)
- **Place:** [The Inn](place_inn.md)

## Has
A fixed place beside the road.
Food, shelter, information.
Knowledge of everyone who has passed
in both directions.

## Orders
Keep the house.
Know the road conditions north and south.
Feed whoever arrives.

## Loses
Surprise.
They were certain each arrival
would bring something new.
After enough seasons the Innkeeper sees
the same journey walking through the door
in different bodies.
This shape of person has been here before.

## Drives
On the loss -
feeds them anyway.
