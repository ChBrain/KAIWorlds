## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg

## Place
[The Ferry Landing](place_ferry_landing.md).

## Load Bearing
Without it the road ends at the north bank of the Elbe.
Remove the Ferry Barge: the road is not a route to southern markets.
It is a route to a riverbank.

## Apparent
A flat-bottomed barge. Wide enough to carry cattle.
Poled or rowed across the tidal Elbe.
The barge does not hurry. The river decides the pace.

## Yearbook
**Medieval period** - the ferry crossing at Wedel established.
**c. 1450** - the ferry landing at Wedel replaces the earlier crossing
at Lichte. The Roland erected the same decade.
**Peak drift, c. 1600** - up to 30,000 cattle ferried across
each spring. Multiple barges operating across several weeks.
**19th century** - the railway bridge makes the ferry obsolete.
