# Position: The Trader

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg
- **Process:** [Travel South](process_travel_south.md)
- **Place:** [The Ridge Road](place_ridge_road.md)

## Has
Knowledge of differential value -
what is scarce at the destination
and plentiful at the origin.
Goods moving in one direction.
Money or goods returning in the other.

## Orders
Move goods along the price gradient.
Arrive at the destination before the margin closes.

## Loses
The margin.
They were certain the price difference
they left with would still exist on arrival.
The knowledge that made the journey worthwhile
expired on the road.

## Drives
On the loss -
reads the new conditions.
