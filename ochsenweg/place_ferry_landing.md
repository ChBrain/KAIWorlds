# Place: The Ferry Landing

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg
- **Process:** [Cross the Threshold](process_cross_the_threshold.md)
- **Position:** [The Ferryman](position_ferryman.md)
- **Piece:** [The Ferry Barge](piece_ferry_barge.md)

## Shown
- The north bank of the Elbe at Wedel.
  The river ahead. Wide. Tidal.
  The south bank visible but not close.
- [The Ferry Barge](piece_ferry_barge.md). Moored or crossing.
- The holding ground beside the landing.
  Where cattle wait before loading.
  The last ground on the road's side of the river.
- [The Wedel Market](place_wedel_market.md) behind -
  the market is already visible from here.
  The road has ended. The river is what remains.
- The Elbe. It does not wait.
  Tide, current, season - the river runs on its own schedule.

## Holds
- [The Ferry Barge](piece_ferry_barge.md).

## Offers
- The crossing. The only passage from the road
  to the markets south of the Elbe.
- The wait - which is also an offer.
  The Ferry Landing is the last place to turn back.

## Withheld
- Whether the river will hold today.
  The Ferryman knows. He does not always say.
