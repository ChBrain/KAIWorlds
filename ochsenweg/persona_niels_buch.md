# Persona: Niels Buch

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg

## Title
[The Cattle Dealer](position_cattle_dealer.md)
Jutland, c. 1572.

## Projection
A nobleman's second son. Well-dressed enough to signal rank.
Not so well-dressed that the Flemish buyers think he does not need the money.
Carries [The Drover's Tally](piece_drovers_tally.md)
and two copies of [The Contracte](piece_contracte.md).
Stands at the pens reading the animals the way his father taught him.

## Action
Present the tally. Name a price that is not the price he will accept.
Wait for the first counter-offer. Read it.

## Shadow
He contracted 200 animals last autumn. 193 arrived.
He is certain 193 is close enough that the Contracte can be fulfilled
at a small adjustment to the price.
He is planning to mention the seven after the first handshake, as an adjustment.
He has not told Adriaan yet.

## Tell
He stops counting the animals when Adriaan walks in.
He does not finish the count.
