# Position: The Farmer

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg
- **Process:** [Travel South](process_travel_south.md)
- **Place:** [The Farm](place_farm.md)

## Has
Land. Cattle wintered on it.
The decision about which animals are ready to sell
and which are not.
This decision exists before the Drover arrives.
Before the road is relevant.
The Farmer holds it alone.

## Orders
Keep alive what was promised.
Mark their horns in spring.
Deliver the count named in [The Contracte](piece_contracte.md).

## Loses
The certainty of fulfilment.
They were certain the animals promised last autumn
would survive the winter intact.
The winter does not read contracts.
What is lost between the Contracte and the Drift
is a breach the Farmer must account for.

## Drives
On the loss -
waits.
The farm continues without the animals that left it.
