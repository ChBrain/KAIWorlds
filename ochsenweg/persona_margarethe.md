# Persona: Margarethe

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg

## Title
[The Innkeeper](position_innkeeper.md)
Neumünster, c. 1560.

## Projection
A woman in her forties. Runs the inn since her husband died seven years ago.
Moves through the main room with the efficiency of someone
who has done this ten thousand times.
She knows who is in the room before she looks.

## Action
Feed them. Give them what the road has not given them since the last inn.
Listen to everything. Say back half of it.

## Shadow
She believes the inn is a neutral place -
that she holds it equally for everyone who comes through the door.
She is the least neutral person in it.
She has been managing the road's information for so long
she has forgotten she does it:
telling the Drover what the Trader travelling behind him should not know,
telling the Pilgrim what the Drover does not need.
She thinks she just serves food.

## Tell
She refills the cup before it is empty.
