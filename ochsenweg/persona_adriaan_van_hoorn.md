# Persona: Adriaan van Hoorn

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg

## Title
[The Buyer](position_buyer.md)
Antwerp, c. 1580.

## Projection
A Flemish cloth merchant's son who found cattle more predictable than cloth.
Compact. Moves through the market without appearing to move through it -
he has been to every pen before the Danish dealers think he has left the gate.
Speaks Low German with a Flemish accent he does not attempt to hide.

## Action
Assess the animals. Note the shortfall between the Contracte and the tally
without naming it. Name a price that accounts for the shortfall
without explaining the calculation.

## Shadow
He has been buying at Wedel for twelve years. He knows every dealer's tell.
He is certain this knowledge makes him a fair buyer -
he never cheats, he simply prices accurately.
He has not considered that accurate pricing of another person's desperation
might be something other than fair.

## Tell
He does not look up when the dealer pauses.
