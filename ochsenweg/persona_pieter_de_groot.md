# Persona: Pieter de Groot

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg

## Title
[The Ferryman](position_ferryman.md)
Wedel, c. 1595.

## Projection
A large man made larger by years of poling the Elbe.
Stands on the bank reading the water the way other men read faces.
[The Ferry Barge](piece_ferry_barge.md) is tied to his left hand
by habit - he touches the rope without looking.

## Action
Read the river. Cross when it allows. Do not cross when it does not.

## Shadow
It is April. Behind him on the north bank: cattle, drovers, dealers, buyers -
three weeks of accumulated traffic waiting for the crossing.
He is certain his reading of the river is honest and will be respected.
He is certain there is a difference between his judgement and the pressure.
He is no longer certain he has not already let the pressure decide.

## Tell
He looks at the crowd before he looks at the river.
He does not know he did it.
