# Persona: Jens

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg

## Title
[The Drover](position_drover.md)
Holstein, c. 1600.

## Projection
Lean. Weathered past his age.
Wears the same coat in April as in October.
Carries [The Drover's Tally](piece_drovers_tally.md)
folded inside the coat.
Moves at the same pace as the cattle.
Has learned to think at that pace.

## Action
Walk south. Count every morning.
Record every loss before nightfall.
Do not speculate about Wedel.

## Shadow
He has walked this road eleven times.
He knows the road better than anyone who has ever hired him.
He is certain this knowledge makes him irreplaceable.
The Cattle Dealer who hired him does not know his name.

## Tell
He checks the tally when there is no reason to check it.
