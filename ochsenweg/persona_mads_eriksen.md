# Persona: Mads Eriksen

## Owner
- **Project:** Ochsenweg

## Title
[The Farmer](position_farmer.md)
Jutland, 1581.

## Projection
A broad man. Work-worn. Moves through the farm
as if he built it, which he partly did.
Speaks slowly. Not because he is slow -
because he finished thinking before he started talking.
The farm around him is tidy.
He carries [The Contracte](piece_contracte.md)
in the same pocket as his knife.

## Action
Count the animals every morning.
The number must not change downward before spring.

## Shadow
The Contracte promised 40 animals. He has 38. Two died in January.
He is certain he can replace them by purchasing two from a neighbour
before the Drover arrives. He is certain this is not a breach
if the count is correct at departure.
He has not read the Contracte again since January.

## Tell
He touches the Contracte pocket
when someone asks how the winter went.
